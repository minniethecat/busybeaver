[008-001]As ADAM lay a-dreaming beneath the Apple Tree,

[008-002]The Angel of the Earth came down, and offered Earth in fee.

[008-003]But Adam did not need it,

[008-004]Nor the plough he would not speed it,

[008-005]Singing:--“Earth and Water, Air and Fire,

[008-006]What more can mortal man desire?”

[008-007](The Apple Tree's in bud.)

[008-008]As Adam lay a-dreaming beneath the Apple Tree,

[008-009]The Angel of the Waters offered all the Seas in fee.

[008-010]But Adam would not take 'em,

[008-011]Nor the ships he wouldn't make 'em,

[008-012]Singing:--“Water, Earth and Air and Fire,

[008-013]What more can mortal man desire?”

[008-014](The Apple Tree's in leaf.)

[008-015]As Adam lay a-dreaming beneath the Apple Tree,

[008-016]The Angel of the Air he offered all the Air in fee.

[008-017]But Adam did not crave it,

[008-018]Nor the flight he wouldn't brave it,

[008-019]Singing:--“Air and Water, Earth and Fire,

[008-020]What more can mortal man desire?”

[008-021](The Apple Tree's in bloom.)

[008-022]As Adam lay a-dreaming beneath the Apple Tree,

[008-023]The Angel of the Fire rose up and not a word said he.

[008-024]But he wished a fire and made it,

[008-025]And in Adam's heart he laid it,

[008-026]Singing.--“Fire, fire, burning Fire,

[008-027]Stand up and reach your heart's desire!”

[008-028](The Apple Blossom's set.)

[008-029]As Adam was a-working outside of Eden-Wall,

[008-030]He used the Earth, he used the Seas, he used the Air and all;

[008-031]And out of black disaster

[008-032]He arose to be the master

[008-033]Of Earth and Water, Air and Fire,

[008-034]But never reached his heart's desire!

[008-035](The Apple Tree's cut down!)
