[005-001]If the stock had not been old and overcrowded, the Wax-moth would never

[005-002]have entered; but where bees are too thick on the comb there must be

[005-003]sickness or parasites. The heat of the hive had risen with the June

[005-004]honey-flow, and though the farmers worked, until their wings ached, to

[005-005]keep people cool, everybody suffered.

[005-006]A young bee crawled up the greasy trampled alighting-board. “Excuse me,”

[005-007]she began, “but it's my first honey-flight. Could you kindly tell me if

[005-008]this is my--”

[005-010]you! Next!”

[005-011]“Shame!” cried half a dozen old workers with worn wings and nerves, and

[005-012]there was a scuffle and a hum.

[005-014]alighting-board, had waited this chance all day. She scuttled in like a

[005-015]ghost, and, knowing the senior bees would turn her out at once, dodged

[005-016]into a brood-frame, where youngsters who had not yet seen the winds blow

[005-017]or the flowers nod discussed life. Here she was safe, for young bees

[005-018]will tolerate any sort of stranger. Behind her came the bee who had been

[005-019]slanged by the Guard.

[005-021]in a full load of first-class stuff, and the Guard told me to go and be

[005-022]foul-brooded!” She sat down in the cool draught across the combs.

[005-024]the Guard's tone when she cursed our sister. It aroused the Entire

[005-025]Community.” She laid an egg. She had stolen in for that purpose.

[005-027]there, Miss?” She did not know how to address the slim stranger.

[005-029]working-sister. My heart bled for you beneath your burden.” The Wax-moth

[005-032]“My dear child, I give you my most solemn word of honour those aren't

[005-033]eggs. Those are my principles, and I am ready to die for them.” She

[005-034]raised her voice a little above the rustle and tramp round her. “If

[005-035]you'd like to kill me, pray do.”

[005-037]folds of the Wax-moth's wing, which hid her ceaseless egg-dropping.

[005-039]“Ah, don't let your conscience reproach you later, but when you've

[005-040]killed me, write me, at least, as one that loved her fellow-worker.”

[005-046]shoulder. “I'd been on the Gate for three hours, and one would

[005-047]foul-brood the Queen herself after that. No offence meant.”
