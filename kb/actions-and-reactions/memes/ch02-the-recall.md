[002-001]I am the land of their fathers,

[002-002]In me the virtue stays;

[002-003]I will bring back my children,

[002-004]After certain days.

[002-005]Under their feet in the grasses

[002-006]My clinging magic runs.

[002-007]They shall return as strangers,

[002-008]They shall remain as sons.

[002-009]Over their heads in the branches

[002-010]Of their new-bought, ancient trees,

[002-011]I weave an incantation,

[002-012]And draw them to my knees.

[002-013]Scent of smoke in the evening,

[002-014]Smell of rain in the night,

[002-015]The hours, the days and the seasons

[002-016]Order their souls aright;

[002-017]Till I make plain the meaning

[002-018]Of all my thousand years

[002-019]Till I fill their hearts with knowledge,

[002-020]While I fill their eyes with tears.
