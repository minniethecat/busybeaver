# Cramoisi

## Character Overview

Cramoisi is a servant in the household of the [[comte-de-mont-gobert|Comtesse de Mont-Gobert]], engaged to marry [[lisette|Lisette]], the Comtesse's attendant. His defining trait is [[jealousy|jealous vigilance]]: suspecting a rival for Lisette's affections, he prowls the estate by night, and his discoveries set in motion the events that lead to [[thibault|Thibault]]'s exposure and capture.

His name — *cramoisi*, meaning "crimson" in French — carries suggestions of passion, anger, and the flushed face of jealousy, all of which prove relevant to his role in the narrative.

## Role in the Story

Cramoisi is first mentioned in conversation between [[thibault|Thibault]] (inhabiting the body of [[baron-of-vez|Raoul de Vauparfond]]) and [[lisette|Lisette]], who identifies Cramoisi as the man who "always" tends to the Baron's horse:

> "Why Cramoisi, of course, the man who always does" (016-033).

[[thibault|Thibault]], uneasy, repeats the name as if to fix it in memory: "Ah, yes, to be sure, Cramoisi will look after it" (016-034). When a mysterious footstep is heard, [[lisette|Lisette]] immediately suspects Cramoisi — "no doubt Cramoisi's foot" (016-039) — and then reveals the crucial fact:

> "Do you not know that Cramoisi is the man I am engaged to?" (016-042).

This revelation transforms Cramoisi from a background servant into a personal threat. [[thibault|Thibault]]'s private reflection — "I am deceiving the Comtesse de Mont-Gobert and I am the rival of Monsieur Cramoisi!" (017-026) — captures the dangerous triangle in which he now moves.

Cramoisi's vigilance proves decisive. His [[jealousy|jealous]] night-watching leads him to observe a strange man entering the park through secluded paths:

> "Cramoisi, jealous with regard to Lisette, had been prowling about at nights on the watch, and had, only the evening before, noticed a strange man come in and go out of the park" (020-065).

He reports his findings to the head of the police. With Cramoisi as guide, the authorities ambush [[thibault|Thibault]] at the breach in the park wall, leading to his capture (020-065).

## Key Relationships

- **[[lisette|Lisette]]** — His fiancée, the Comtesse's attendant, whose flirtation with [[thibault|Thibault]] (as the Baron) provokes Cramoisi's [[jealousy|suspicion]] and his nightly patrols of the estate (016-042).
- **[[thibault|Thibault]]** — His unwitting rival for Lisette's affections; Cramoisi's jealousy-driven surveillance leads directly to [[thibault|Thibault]]'s exposure.
- **[[comte-de-mont-gobert|The Comtesse de Mont-Gobert]]** — His employer, in whose household the romantic rivalry and its consequences unfold.
