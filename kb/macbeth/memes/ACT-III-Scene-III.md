### SCENE III. The same. A Park or Lawn, with a gate leading to the Palace.

[014-001] Enter three Murderers.

[014-002] FIRST MURDERER.
But who did bid thee join with us?

[014-003] THIRD MURDERER.
Macbeth.

[014-004] SECOND MURDERER.
He needs not our mistrust; since he delivers
Our offices and what we have to do
To the direction just.

[014-005] FIRST MURDERER.
Then stand with us.
The west yet glimmers with some streaks of day.
Now spurs the lated traveller apace,
To gain the timely inn; and near approaches
The subject of our watch.

[014-006] THIRD MURDERER.
Hark! I hear horses.

[014-007] BANQUO.
[*Within.*] Give us a light there, ho!

[014-008] SECOND MURDERER.
Then ’tis he; the rest
That are within the note of expectation
Already are i’ th’ court.

[014-009] FIRST MURDERER.
His horses go about.

[014-010] THIRD MURDERER.
Almost a mile; but he does usually,
So all men do, from hence to the palace gate
Make it their walk.
Enter Banquo and Fleance with a torch.

[014-011] SECOND MURDERER.
A light, a light!

[014-012] THIRD MURDERER.
’Tis he.

[014-013] FIRST MURDERER.
Stand to’t.

[014-014] BANQUO.
It will be rain tonight.

[014-015] FIRST MURDERER.
Let it come down.

[014-016] [*Assaults Banquo.*]

[014-017] BANQUO.
O, treachery! Fly, good Fleance, fly, fly, fly!
Thou mayst revenge—O slave!

[014-018] [*Dies. Fleance escapes.*]

[014-019] THIRD MURDERER.
Who did strike out the light?

[014-020] FIRST MURDERER.
Was’t not the way?

[014-021] THIRD MURDERER.
There’s but one down: the son is fled.

[014-022] SECOND MURDERER.
We have lost best half of our affair.

[014-023] FIRST MURDERER.
Well, let’s away, and say how much is done.

[014-024] [*Exeunt.*]
