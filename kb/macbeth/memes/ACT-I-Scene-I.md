### SCENE I. An open Place.

[001-001] [001-001] Thunder and Lightning. Enter three Witches.

[001-002] [001-002] FIRST WITCH.
When shall we three meet again?
In thunder, lightning, or in rain?

[001-003] [001-003] SECOND WITCH.
When the hurlyburly’s done,
When the battle’s lost and won.

[001-004] [001-004] THIRD WITCH.
That will be ere the set of sun.

[001-005] [001-005] FIRST WITCH.
Where the place?

[001-006] [001-006] SECOND WITCH.
Upon the heath.

[001-007] [001-007] THIRD WITCH.
There to meet with Macbeth.

[001-008] [001-008] FIRST WITCH.
I come, Graymalkin!

[001-009] [001-009] SECOND WITCH.
Paddock calls.

[001-010] [001-010] THIRD WITCH.
Anon.

[001-011] [001-011] ALL.
Fair is foul, and foul is fair:
Hover through the fog and filthy air.

[001-012] [001-012] [*Exeunt.*]
