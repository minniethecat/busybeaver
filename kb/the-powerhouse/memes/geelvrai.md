# Mr. Geelvrai

Mr. Geelvrai is a restaurant manager who appears briefly in *The Powerhouse*. He manages the restaurant where [[edward-leithen|Leithen]] attends a private dinner that proves to be a trap set by [[andrew-lumley|Lumley]]'s network.

## Role in the Narrative

Mr. Geelvrai manages a restaurant where a private room has been engaged. When Leithen arrives, Geelvrai greets him and directs him to the room (006-009):

> "Mr. Geelvrai," he nodded. "He has engaged a private room. Vill you follow, sir?" (006-009)

The manager notes that the client in question comes often, though sometimes late, and assures Leithen that everything is prepared (006-011):

> "Mr. Geelvrai comes often here," said the manager. "He vill be late — sometimes. Everything is ready, sir. I hope you vill be pleased." (006-011)

## Character

Mr. Geelvrai is portrayed as a discreet and professional restaurateur, accustomed to serving clients who require private accommodations. His foreign accent and manner suggest he is not English-born.
