[015-001] Before the walls of [[athens|Athens]].

::: table pn=015-002

<!-- pn=015-002 -->|  | *Enter two **Senators** and a **Messenger**.* |

<!-- pn=015-003 -->| [[first-senator|First Senator]] | Thou hast painfully discover’d: are his files As full as thy report? |

<!-- pn=015-004 -->| Messenger | I have spoke the least: Besides, his expedition promises Present approach. |

<!-- pn=015-005 -->| [[second-senator|Second Senator]] | We stand much hazard, if they bring not [[timon|Timon]]. |

<!-- pn=015-006 -->| Messenger | I met a courier, one mine ancient friend; Whom, though in general part we were opposed, Yet our old love made a particular force, And made us speak like [[friendship|friends]]: this man was riding From [[alcibiades|Alcibiades]] to Timon’s cave, With letters of entreaty, which imported His fellowship i’ the cause against your city, In part for his sake moved. |

<!-- pn=015-007 -->| First Senator | Here come our brothers. |

<!-- pn=015-008 -->|  | *Enter the **Senators** from **Timon**.* |

<!-- pn=015-009 -->| Third Senator | No talk of Timon, nothing of him expect. The enemies’ drum is heard, and fearful scouring Doth choke the air with dust: in, and prepare: Ours is the fall, I fear; our foes the snare. *Exeunt.* |

:::
