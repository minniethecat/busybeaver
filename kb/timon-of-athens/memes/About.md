# About This Wiki

## Source Material

*Timon of Athens* by William Shakespeare, c. 1605–1608. First published in the First Folio, 1623. This wiki provides a structured knowledge base of the play's characters, themes, scenes, and scholarly commentary.

## Usage Notes

This wiki is designed for students, scholars, and enthusiasts of Shakespearean drama. Browse entries by type using the home page sections, search for specific characters or themes, or explore the structured knowledge graph.
