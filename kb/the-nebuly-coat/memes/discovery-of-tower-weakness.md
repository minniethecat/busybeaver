# Discovery of the Tower's Weakness

## Overview

Westray's discovery that the central tower of Cullerne Minster rests on inadequate foundations introduces the novel's central architectural crisis.

## Narrative Role

Westray's professional inspection reveals the tower's dangerous condition. "He had been specially cautioned" about the tower (002-027), and his own examination confirms the worst fears. This discovery transforms the restoration from routine preservation into urgent structural intervention.
