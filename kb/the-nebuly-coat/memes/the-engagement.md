# The Engagement of Lord Blandamer and Anastasia Joliffe

## Overview

The engagement between Lord Blandamer and Anastasia Joliffe reconfigures the novel's social landscape, transforming a lodging-house keeper's daughter into a future peeress.

## Narrative Role

Lord Blandamer's proposal to Anastasia and its subsequent announcement produce an immediate social transformation. The ring — an emerald surrounded by diamonds in green and white, the nebuly coat's colours (019-059) — symbolises the union of love and heraldry.
