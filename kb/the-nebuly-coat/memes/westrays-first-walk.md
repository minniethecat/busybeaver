# Westray's First Walk Through Cullerne

## Overview

Westray's nocturnal walk through old Cullerne with Sharnall establishes the novel's atmosphere of decay and introduces the town's landscape.

## Narrative Role

The walk through the deserted streets, past abandoned warehouses and along the wharf, is Westray's initiation into Cullerne's melancholy. Sharnall pilots him through the darkness, narrating the town's decline (003-008).
