# Standard Ebooks

## Overview

Standard Ebooks, the publisher of the digital edition used as the wiki's source text, represents the contemporary infrastructure of textual preservation that makes the novel accessible to modern readers.

## Role in the Novel

The Standard Ebooks edition of The Nebuly Coat, produced by volunteer editors, provides the clean, well-formatted text from which this wiki's chapter pages were derived. Their work exemplifies the modern equivalent of the antiquarian impulse that Falkner himself embodied.
