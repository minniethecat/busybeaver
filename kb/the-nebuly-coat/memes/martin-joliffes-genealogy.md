# Martin Joliffe's Genealogy

## Overview

Martin Joliffe's handwritten genealogy of the Blandamer family — elaborated and corrected through years of obsessive research — is the documentary heart of the novel's mystery.

## In the Novel

Martin's papers contain "a mass of information which bore on the subject" (008-032), taking "the published genealogy of the Blandamer family, and elaborated and corrected it by all kinds of investigation as to marriages and collaterals" (021-002). The manuscript represents a life's work — and a life wasted.

This work's role in the narrative and its symbolic resonance.
