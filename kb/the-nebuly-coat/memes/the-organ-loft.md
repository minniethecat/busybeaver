# The Organ Loft

## Overview

The organ loft is Sharnall's kingdom — a space where he can play to the bats of a full moon, where the nebuly coat in the window "would always look into the loft and haunt me" (010-072). It is a position of elevation, both physical and interpretive.

## Description

The organ loft of Cullerne Minster, Mr. Sharnall's domain, is a space of music, bitterness, and privileged observation — high above the congregation, looking out onto the nebuly coat in the transept window..
