# The Joliffe Family

## Overview

The Joliffe family — Martin the obsessed genealogist, Anastasia the future Lady Blandamer, and Miss Euphemia the guardian of family secrets — represents the novel's central domestic unit, bound by love, loyalty, and the spectre of the nebuly coat.

## Role in the Novel

The Joliffes occupy a precarious social position — respectably poor, connected to the Blandamers through Martin's obsessive belief, and transformed by Anastasia's engagement. Miss Euphemia's guardianship of Martin's papers and the hidden painting makes this modest family the keeper of the novel's central secrets.
