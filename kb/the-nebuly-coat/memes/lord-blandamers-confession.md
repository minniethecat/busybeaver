# Lord Blandamer's Confession

## Overview

Lord Blandamer's private revelations to Westray at Fording transform the architect from observer to reluctant keeper of dangerous secrets.

## Narrative Role

During Westray's visit to Fording, Lord Blandamer reveals information that implicates him in matters far graver than the inheritance dispute. Westray is burdened with "confidences being forced upon Westray that he could not forget, and could not reveal" (023-058).
