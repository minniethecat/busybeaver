# Cullerne Bay

## Overview

Cullerne Bay is where Martin Joliffe's parents were drowned — "drowned together in Cullerne Bay" (002-045) — a tragedy remembered decades later by the sexton. The bay represents the dangerous beauty of the natural world that surrounds Cullerne. Its waters have taken lives, yet its clean salt air provides the sensory counterpoint to the church's mouldering interior.
