# The Father Smith Organ

## Overview

The organ of Cullerne Minster, built by the celebrated organ-maker 'Father' Smith, is a fine instrument fallen into decay — its neglected state mirroring the condition of its player, Mr. Sharnall.

## In the Novel

The organ is described as "clear and sweet-voiced as a mountain brook" despite its decay (003-003). "The naturals are worn down to the quick, you can see the wood in the gutters of the keys, and the pedal-board's too short and all to pieces" (003-003). Its state embodies the novel's theme of beauty surviving amid neglect.
