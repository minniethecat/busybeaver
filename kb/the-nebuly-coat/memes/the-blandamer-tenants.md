# The Blandamer Tenantry

## Overview

The tenant farmers and estate workers who depend on the Blandamer family for their livelihoods represent the economic relationships that underpin aristocratic power.

## Role in the Novel

The tenantry appear at the margins of the novel — their labour sustains Fording, their deference confirms Blandamer status, and their lives are shaped by decisions made in the great house. They represent the economic foundation of the aristocratic order.
