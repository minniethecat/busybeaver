# The Bermondsey Chime Tune

## Overview

The hymn tune 'Bermondsey' in D major, scored in SATB for chimes, is the most prominent of the melodies played by Saint Sepulchre's chimes — a recurring musical motif whose notation appears in the novel's appendix.

## In the Novel

The tune is scored "in SATB for chimes in ³⁄₂ time" (024-034), and its notation appears as an illustration. It plays at critical moments in the narrative, its steady rhythm providing an auditory counterpoint to human drama.
