# Cullerne Parish

## Overview

The ecclesiastical parish of Cullerne, administered by Canon Parkyn, represents the institutional Church — its authority, its finances, and its complex relationship with the aristocratic patrons and working people who constitute its congregation.

## Role in the Novel

The parish is the administrative framework within which the restoration project operates. Canon Parkyn's fundraising, the management of the church fabric, and the maintenance of social order all fall within its remit. The parish represents institutional continuity — an organisation that predates and will outlast any individual incumbent.
