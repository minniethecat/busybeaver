# Cullerne Wharf

## Overview

Cullerne Wharf appears on the Ordnance maps (002-001) but its mapped existence belies its actual desolation. The abandoned warehouses stand "square-fronted, and huddled together like a row of gigantic packing-cases" (003-008). The river current gurgles among the mooring-posts, and the east wind drives wavelets up the quay. It is through this landscape of commercial decay that Westray first experiences Cullerne's atmosphere.

## Description

Cullerne Wharf, the abandoned port district where Westray first walks with Sharnall, embodies the town's commercial decay — its empty warehouses and rusting tramway lines testifying to a prosperity that has ebbed away..
