# The Secret of the Painting

## Overview

Westray's recognition that the painting in Miss Joliffe's house conceals the nebuly coat — the visual proof of the Blandamer inheritance claim — is the novel's crucial moment of revelation.

## Narrative Role

The hidden painting, concealed beneath decorative flowers, reveals its true nature when Westray understands "that the green wriggling caterpillar was but a bar of the nebuly coat" (023-058). This recognition transforms his understanding of the Blandamer mystery.
