# The Architectural Firm

## Overview

The London architectural firm that employs Westray and Sir George Farquhar represents the professional world from which Westray comes and to which he will return — the modern infrastructure of expertise that the restoration project requires.

## Role in the Novel

The firm sends Westray to Cullerne, setting the plot in motion. Its senior partner, Sir George Farquhar, provides the professional authority against which Westray's growing independence is measured. The firm represents the metropolitan centre to which Cullerne is peripheral.
