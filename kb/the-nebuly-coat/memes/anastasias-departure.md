# Anastasia's Departure for London

## Overview

Anastasia Joliffe's departure for London following her engagement marks her transition from Cullerne obscurity to her future as Lady Blandamer.

## Narrative Role

Her removal from Cullerne after the engagement announcement (019-059) signals the completion of her social transformation. She leaves the lodging house as a tradesman's daughter and will return as a peer's wife.
