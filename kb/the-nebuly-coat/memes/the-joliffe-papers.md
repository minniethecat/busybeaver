# The Examination of Martin Joliffe's Papers

## Overview

Westray's study of Martin Joliffe's genealogical papers reveals the depth of the dead man's obsession and provides crucial evidence for the Blandamer mystery.

## Narrative Role

The papers contain "a mass of information" — Martin's elaborate corrections to the published Blandamer genealogy (008-032). They represent the documentary legacy of a life consumed by the nebuly coat.
