# The Hand of God

## Overview

The Hand of God is where Cullerne's working men gather — where tales of the Blandamer family, the nebuly coat, and the church's secrets are exchanged over drink. It represents the oral culture that preserves local knowledge outside official channels.

## Description

The Hand of God inn, Cullerne's principal tavern, is the site of working-class conviviality and the circulation of local knowledge — a space where the Blandamer legend is told and retold among the town's ordinary inhabitants..
