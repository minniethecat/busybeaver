# Cullerne Road Station

## Overview

Cullerne Road is described as a "wayside station" (002-002), its modest scale reflecting the town's diminished importance. The arrival of trains is still attended by "Cullerne loungers as a daily ceremonial" (002-003), a ritual that measures the town's restricted horizon.

## Description

Cullerne Road, the wayside railway station that serves the town, is the threshold through which characters enter and leave Cullerne — a point of arrival and departure that marks the boundary between the town's enclosed world and the larger world beyond..
