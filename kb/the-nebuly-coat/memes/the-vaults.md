# The Church Vaults

## Overview

The vaults are the site of controversy early in the novel. The Rector's determination to cement them — "wasting money on cementing the vaults" in Sharnall's view (003-003) — represents the institutional Church's preference for sealing off the past rather than confronting it.

## Description

The vaults beneath Cullerne Minster, which the Rector insists on having cemented despite Sharnall's objections, represent the hidden spaces beneath the church — both physical and metaphorical — that the novel's plot uncovers..
