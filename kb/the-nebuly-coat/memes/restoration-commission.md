# The Restoration Commission

## Overview

The commission to restore Cullerne Minster brings architect Edward Westray to the town, setting the entire plot in motion.

## Narrative Role

The restoration commission originates with Canon Parkyn, who has been fundraising for the project. Sir George Farquhar's firm is engaged, and Westray is dispatched to superintend the work (001-001). The commission is both professional opportunity and narrative catalyst — it places Westray in Cullerne, where he will uncover the town's secrets.
