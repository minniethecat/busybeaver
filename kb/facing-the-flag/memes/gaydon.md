## Gaydon

"Gaydon" is the alias adopted by the French engineer **[[Simon Hart]]** during his assignment as the keeper and warder of [[Thomas Roch]] at Healthful House, near Charleston, South Carolina. Hart assumed this identity while working undercover for the French government to monitor Roch and protect the secret of the [[fulgurator]].

Under the name Gaydon, Hart was the constant companion of Thomas Roch, attending to the inventor's needs and observing his mental state. When [[count-dartigas]] (Ker Karraje) orchestrated the kidnapping of Roch, Gaydon was also abducted and transported to [[Back Cup]] aboard the schooner *Ebba*.

> "This guardian was known as Gaydon. Shortly after the sequestration of Thomas Roch at Healthful House, the director of the establishment had appointed him to this post." (002-001)

The Gaydon identity was maintained throughout Hart's captivity in Back Cup, where the pirates initially treated him merely as a harmless warder. Only later was it revealed that Gaydon was in fact Simon Hart, the French engineer.

> "Warder Gaydon is Engineer Simon Hart; and Ker Karraje will never forgive him for the destruction of Back Cup." (017-001)
