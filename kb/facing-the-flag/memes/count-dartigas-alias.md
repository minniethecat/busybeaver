The identity of Count d'Artigas was the aristocratic alias adopted by [[Ker Karraje]] to infiltrate [[Healthful House]] and mask his identity as a pirate leader. The persona of a wealthy European yachtsman — cultured, curious, and above suspicion — was a carefully constructed fiction that enabled Karraje to reconnoiter the sanatorium and plan the [[kidnapping-of-roch|kidnapping of Thomas Roch]].

## Construction of the Persona

The Count d'Artigas persona was supported by the [[Ebba Schooner|Ebba]], a schooner disguised as a pleasure yacht that anchored at [[New Berne]] during the Count's visit to [[Healthful House]]. The vessel's elegant appearance, the crew's disciplined demeanor, and the Count's own cultivated manner all contributed to an impression of wealthy leisure that disarmed suspicion.

## Penetration of the Facade

[[Simon Hart]], present at [[Healthful House]] under his own alias as Gaydon, was the first to sense that something was amiss. His observations of the Count's behavior — the intensity of his interest in [[Thomas Roch]], the significant glances exchanged with [[Captain Spade]] — planted the seeds of suspicion that would later be confirmed.
