The Helmsman of the [[Ebba Schooner|Ebba]] was one of the crew members who served under [[Captain Spade]] during the schooner's critical voyage from [[Pamlico Sound]] to [[Back Cup]]. As the sailor responsible for steering the vessel, he played an essential role in the [[ebba-voyage-to-back-cup|kidnapping voyage]] that transported [[Thomas Roch]] and [[Simon Hart]] to [[Ker Karraje]]'s hidden base.

## Role in the Voyage

The Helmsman's duties required precise navigation through the waters of [[Pamlico Sound]] and into the open [[Atlantic Ocean]]. Under [[Captain Spade]]'s command, he steered the schooner through channels that demanded local knowledge and steady seamanship, particularly during the night departure from [[North Carolina]].

## The Ebba's Crew

The [[Ebba Schooner|Ebba]] carried a crew of pirates disguised as a pleasure yacht's complement. Each member, from the helmsman to the deckhands, was part of [[Ker Karraje]]'s larger organization. The helmsman's competence was essential to the success of the kidnapping.
