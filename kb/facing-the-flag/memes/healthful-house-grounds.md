The Grounds of [[Healthful House]] were the extensive parkland surrounding the sanatorium in [[North Carolina]]. Designed to provide a therapeutic environment for patients like [[Thomas Roch]], the grounds also constituted the facility's outer security perimeter — the boundary that was breached during the [[kidnapping-of-roch|kidnapping]].

## Layout and Design

The grounds included gardens, walking paths, and open parkland that allowed patients to exercise and take the air under the supervision of staff such as the [[Warder of Healthful House]]. This open design, while therapeutically beneficial, made comprehensive security difficult — a vulnerability that [[Ker Karraje]] exploited.

## The Night of the Kidnapping

On the night of the kidnapping, [[Captain Spade]] and his raiding party crossed the grounds under cover of darkness, exploiting the gaps in surveillance that the open layout created. The breach of the grounds was the first phase of an operation that would end with [[Thomas Roch]] and [[Simon Hart]] aboard the [[Ebba Schooner|Ebba]], bound for [[Back Cup]].
