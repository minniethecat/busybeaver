The Visit of [[Ker Karraje|Count d'Artigas]] to [[Healthful House]] was the reconnaissance operation that preceded the [[kidnapping-of-roch|kidnapping of Thomas Roch]]. Disguised as a wealthy yachtsman, [[Ker Karraje]] toured the sanatorium, assessed its security, and confirmed the vulnerability that he would exploit.

## The Visit

[[Ker Karraje]], presenting himself as Count d'Artigas, arrived at [[Healthful House]] as a distinguished visitor — a cultured European aristocrat interested in the sanatorium's methods and patients. His yacht, the [[Ebba Schooner|Ebba]], was anchored at [[New Berne]], completing the impression of wealthy leisure.

The visit allowed Karraje to observe the facility's layout, the routines of its staff (including the [[Warder of Healthful House]] and the [[Director of Healthful House]]), and most importantly, the condition and custody arrangements for [[Thomas Roch]]. Every detail he gathered during this visit informed the planning of the kidnapping.

## Simon Hart's Observation

[[Simon Hart]], present at [[Healthful House]] in his alias as Gaydon, observed the Count's visit with growing unease. His narrative records the subtle discrepancies between d'Artigas's cultivated persona and the predatory attention he directed toward Roch:

> This observation was accompanied by such a significant glance at Captain Spade that I felt sure the evening would not pass without some decisive action. (004-042)

Hart's suspicion, though acute, came too late to prevent the kidnapping. The Count's reconnaissance had achieved its purpose.
