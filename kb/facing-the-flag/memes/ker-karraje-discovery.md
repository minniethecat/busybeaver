The Discovery of [[Ker Karraje]]'s Identity was the process by which [[Simon Hart]], imprisoned at [[Back Cup]], confirmed his suspicion that the urbane [[Count d'Artigas]] was in fact the notorious pirate leader sought by the navies of Europe. This realization transformed Hart's captivity from a personal misfortune into an intelligence mission.
