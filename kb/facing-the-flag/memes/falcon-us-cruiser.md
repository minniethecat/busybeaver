The **_Falcon_** is a United States Navy cruiser stationed in the waters off Bermuda in _[[facing-the-flag-novel|Facing the Flag]]_. It is the first naval vessel dispatched to investigate the disappearance of [[thomas-roch]] and to search for the schooner _[[ebba-schooner|Ebba]]_.

## Deployment to Bermuda

Following the kidnapping of Thomas Roch from [[healthful-house|Healthful House]], the U.S. authorities dispatch the _Falcon_ to coordinate the search:

> "Another dispatch ordered the cruiser _Falcon_, which was stationed in the port of Bermuda, to put to sea immediately." (006-001)

[[count-dartigas|Count d'Artigas]], the mastermind behind the abduction, observes the cruiser's movements from aboard the _Ebba_:

> "Count d'Artigas could see the _Falcon_, which was a couple of miles out at sea." (006-002)

## The Search Operation

The _Falcon_ conducts an extensive search of all vessels in the area, intercepting and inspecting ships suspected of involvement:

> "Not only the cruiser _Falcon_, but every available cutter and revenue boat was searching all vessels that passed." (006-010)

The cruiser's commander is specifically ordered to intercept the schooner _Ebba_, which is the prime suspect in the abduction:

> "Orders were given to the commander of the _Falcon_ to intercept the schooner at any cost." (006-032)

## Confrontation with the Ebba

When the _Falcon_ dispatches a boarding party to inspect the _Ebba_, [[count-dartigas|Count d'Artigas]] receives the lieutenant with cold formality. The inspection yields no incriminating evidence, as Roch and [[simon-hart|Simon Hart]] are concealed below deck:

> "Saluting the lieutenant of the _Falcon_ coldly, the Count d'Artigas stepped forward to meet him." (007-008)

The _Falcon_'s inability to discover the concealed prisoners allows the _Ebba_ to proceed to [[back-cup|Back Cup]], setting the stage for the remainder of the novel.
