Loyalty testing is a recurring motif in [[Simon Hart]]'s captivity at [[Back Cup]]. [[Engineer Serko]] and [[Ker Karraje]] repeatedly probe whether "Gaydon" can be persuaded or coerced to switch his allegiance — tests that Hart must navigate without revealing his true identity or loyalties.
