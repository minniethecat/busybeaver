The Sargasso Sea is a region of the North [[Atlantic Ocean]] near [[Bermuda]], characterized by floating Sargassum seaweed and calm waters. Its proximity to the [[Bermuda Archipelago]] places it within the operational area of [[Ker Karraje]]'s piracy and the search zone of the [[international-coalition-formation|international coalition]].
