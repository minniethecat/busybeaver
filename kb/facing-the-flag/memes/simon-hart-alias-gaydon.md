Gaydon was the alias adopted by [[Simon Hart]] to work as [[Thomas Roch]]'s keeper at [[Healthful House]]. The assumed identity was part of the French government's plan to place an agent in close proximity to the unstable inventor without alerting him — or potential adversaries — to the surveillance.

## Function of the Alias

The Gaydon identity served multiple purposes. It concealed Hart's true role as a French government agent from the staff of [[Healthful House]], who knew him only as Roch's attendant. It provided a layer of operational security in case [[Thomas Roch]]'s condition caused him to reveal information inadvertently. And it established a baseline identity that Hart would maintain even after his kidnapping to [[Back Cup]], where revealing his true identity would have been fatal.

## The Limits of Disguise

Hart's alias, like all the disguises in *Facing the Flag*, was imperfect. [[Engineer Serko]] and [[Ker Karraje]] suspected from the beginning that "Gaydon" was more than a simple keeper. The alias protected Hart from immediate exposure but could not withstand the sustained scrutiny of his captors.
