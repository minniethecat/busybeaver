The Ebba Deckhand was one of the unnamed crew members serving aboard the [[Ebba Schooner|Ebba]] during its [[ebba-voyage-to-back-cup|voyage to Back Cup]]. Like all the schooner's crew, he was a member of [[Ker Karraje]]'s [[pirate-crew]] disguised as the crew of a pleasure yacht.
