The Mouth of the [[Neuse River]] was the estuarine area where the river met [[Pamlico Sound]] and where the [[Ebba Schooner|Ebba]] was anchored before its [[ebba-voyage-to-back-cup|kidnapping voyage]]. This location, near [[New Berne]], provided the schooner with access to both the shelter of the river and the open waters of the sound.

## The Ebba's Anchorage

The [[Ebba Schooner|Ebba]]'s choice of anchorage at the river mouth was strategic. The position allowed the schooner to appear as a legitimate visiting yacht while remaining close enough to [[Healthful House]] for the kidnapping party to reach the sanatorium and return within a single night. The river mouth's tidal characteristics and navigable channel made it suitable for a vessel of the Ebba's draft.
