Bermuda occupies a significant position in the geography of *Facing the Flag*. Located in the North Atlantic, the archipelago serves as a navigational landmark and contextualizes the location of the hidden pirate base at [[Back Cup]].

## Geographic Context

The Bermuda group lies approximately on the thirty-fifth parallel in the North Atlantic, placing it within the operational radius of the pirate schooner [[Ebba Schooner|Ebba]]. The novel's maritime geography — spanning from [[Pamlico Sound]] to the open [[Atlantic Ocean]] — uses Bermuda as one of several real-world coordinates that ground the narrative in verifiable space.

> On the thirty-fifth parallel, is the Bermuda group, which belongs to England. (011-020)

## Strategic Relevance

Bermuda's significance in the novel is primarily navigational and strategic:

| Aspect | Relevance |
|--------|-----------|
| **Proximity to Back Cup** | The Bermuda group is one of the nearest known landmasses to the hidden pirate cavern |
| **Maritime Traffic** | Bermuda's location in a major shipping corridor means vessels passing near Back Cup may be identified as Bermuda-bound |
| **British Territory** | As a British possession, Bermuda represents colonial authority in waters contested by [[Ker Karraje]]'s pirates |

## Narrative Role

When the schooner [[Ebba Schooner|Ebba]] makes its voyages between the American coast and [[Back Cup]], the course regularly brings it into the vicinity of the Bermuda group. The narrator Simon Hart, observing from aboard the Ebba, notes the vessel's trajectory toward the Bermudas with increasing alarm — each nautical mile takes him further from [[Healthful House]] and deeper into the pirates' domain.

> I am convinced that it is the Bermudas that we are making for. (011-038)

Hart's deduction about their course toward Bermuda — later revealed to be toward the hidden [[Back Cup]] cavern nearby — marks a critical moment of geographical awareness in the novel. The reference to real Atlantic geography heightens the plausibility of the pirate operation's concealment.

## Connections to Other Locations

Bermuda forms part of a network of locations in the novel:

- **[[Atlantic Ocean]]** — the broader maritime theater encompassing Bermuda, Back Cup, and the American coast
- **[[Back Cup]]** — the hidden pirate cavern located in the same general region of the Atlantic
- **[[Pamlico Sound]]** — departure point for the schooner's Atlantic voyages
- **[[Healthful House]]** — the North Carolina sanatorium where the story begins
