The Night Departure of the [[Ebba Schooner|Ebba]] was the opening act of the [[ebba-voyage-to-back-cup|voyage to Back Cup]]. Under cover of darkness, with [[Thomas Roch]] and [[Simon Hart]] secured aboard, [[Captain Spade]] navigated the schooner from its anchorage at [[New Berne]] through [[Pamlico Sound]] toward the open [[Atlantic Ocean]].
