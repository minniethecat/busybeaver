Maritime isolation is a defining condition of *Facing the Flag*. [[Back Cup]]'s position in the remote [[Bermuda Archipelago]], accessible only through the [[submarine-tunnel]], creates a world apart — a sealed environment where the normal rules of law, society, and communication are suspended.
