## Captain Chapel

Captain Chapel is a minor figure mentioned in Jules Verne's *Facing the Flag* (1896). He is referenced as an artillery captain whose engines serve as a point of comparison.

> "No! the engines, after the manner of Artillery Captain Chapel's, were not intended to effect results through the intermediary of projectiles." (027-005)

The reference to Captain Chapel's engines occurs in the novel's final section, during the description of the [[fulgurator]]'s mechanism and its distinction from conventional artillery systems.
