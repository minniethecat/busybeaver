## Back Cup

Back Cup is an isolated islet at the extremity of the [[bermuda|bermuda]] archipelago that serves as the secret headquarters of [[count-dartigas|the pirate]] [[ker-karraje|ker karraje]] and his crew in [[jules-verne|jules verne]]'s *Facing the Flag* (1896). The island's name derives from its distinctive geological shape — a "reversed cup" — and its interior conceals a vast cavern accessible only by submarine, making it an impenetrable pirate fortress.

> "Well, I know and recognize this islet! It is situated at the extremity of the archipelago of the Bermudas. It is the 'reversed cup' that I had occasion to visit a few years ago — No, I am not mistaken." (013-003)

## In the Novel

Back Cup is the destination to which [[thomas-roch|thomas roch]] and [[simon-hart|simon hart]] are taken after their [[kidnapping-of-roch|abduction]] from [[healthful-house|healthful house]]. The island's exterior appears uninhabited — a barren calcareous rock formation rising from the sea. However, its interior has been transformed by Ker Karraje and [[engineer-serko|engineer serko]] into a sophisticated underground base.

The cavern within Back Cup is accessible only through an [[submarine-tunnel|underwater passage]] navigable by [[pirate-crew|the pirates]]' submarine boat. This natural fortress contains living quarters, workshops, and a laboratory where Roch is expected to work on his [[fulgurator|fulgurator]]. [[ebba-schooner|the schooner]] *Ebba* can be concealed within the reef's rocky channels, making the entire operation invisible from the sea.

> "The only thought that occurs to me now is that they will hoist me on board and lower me to the bottom of the hold and keep me there till the vessel is far out at sea." (008-001)

## Connections

Back Cup connects to the wider world of the novel through:

- **Ker Karraje (Count d'Artigas)**: The master of Back Cup, who rules this hidden domain with absolute authority.
- **Engineer Serko**: Designed and constructed the technological infrastructure within Back Cup.
- **The Submarine Boat**: Ker Karraje's most advanced asset, berthed within Back Cup's cavern.
- **Bermuda Archipelago**: Back Cup's location, approximately 750 miles from [[north-carolina|north carolina]], places it in British colonial waters but beyond the reach of ordinary maritime patrols.

## Key Passages

Simon Hart's recognition of Back Cup is a pivotal moment of narrative disclosure:

> "Yes, it is Back Cup, sure enough! Had I been less self-possessed I might have uttered an exclamation of surprise — and satisfaction — which, with good reason, would have excited the attention and suspicion of the Count d'Artigas." (013-004)

The island's geological description and the clever engineering that turned it into a habitable base represent Verne's characteristic fusion of natural science and technological imagination.
