The Healthful House Groundskeeper was a minor staff member at the sanatorium in [[North Carolina]] who maintained the facility's extensive grounds and gardens. His observations of unusual activity on the night of the [[kidnapping-of-roch|kidnapping]] contributed to the reconstruction of how [[Ker Karraje]]'s men penetrated the facility.

## Role in the Narrative

The Groundskeeper represents the outermost layer of [[Healthful House]]'s security — the grounds and perimeter that were the first to be breached on the night of the kidnapping. His testimony, like that of the [[Warder of Healthful House]] and the [[Healthful House Pharmacist|Pharmacist]], helped establish the timeline and method of the crime.
