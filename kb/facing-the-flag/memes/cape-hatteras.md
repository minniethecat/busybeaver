Cape Hatteras is the prominent cape on the coast of [[North Carolina]] that marks the transition from the sheltered waters of the American eastern seaboard to the open [[Atlantic Ocean]]. In *Facing the Flag*, it served as a navigational reference for vessels operating between [[Pamlico Sound]] and the Atlantic.
