The New Berne Wharf was the docking facility at [[New Berne]], [[North Carolina]], where visitors to [[Healthful House]] — including [[Ker Karraje|Count d'Artigas]] — arrived by water. It was near this wharf that the [[Ebba Schooner|Ebba]] was moored before its [[ebba-voyage-to-back-cup|departure for Back Cup]].
