The American Secretary of the Navy is an unnamed cabinet-level official in Jules Verne's *Facing the Flag* (1896) who coordinated the United States' naval response to the kidnapping of [[Thomas Roch]] and the search for [[Ker Karraje]]'s hidden base.

> The Secretary of the Navy at once ordered a squadron to cruise in the waters of the Bermudas. (006-008)

## Role in the Novel

After the [[kidnapping-of-roch|kidnapping of Thomas Roch]] from [[Healthful House]], the American government launched a comprehensive search. The Secretary ordered warships to patrol the waters around [[Bermuda]], the suspected location of [[Ker Karraje]]'s base, and coordinated with the [[US Navy]]'s Atlantic fleet. His decisions shaped the military backdrop against which the novel's climax unfolds.

## Significance

The Secretary embodies the institutional response of the American government — squadrons, patrols, and coordinated searches — that complements the personal heroism of [[Simon Hart]]. His offstage role represents the systematic, bureaucratic dimension of the crisis response.
