## Pamlico Sound

Pamlico Sound is a large body of water on the coast of [[north-carolina|north carolina]], separated from the [[atlantic-ocean|atlantic ocean]] by the Outer Banks. In [[jules-verne|jules verne]]'s *Facing the Flag* (1896), Pamlico Sound is the body of water near [[healthful-house|healthful house]] where [[ebba-schooner|the schooner]] *Ebba* anchors during the [[kidnapping-of-roch|kidnapping of thomas roch]] and his keeper [[simon-hart|simon hart]].

> "Obviously they will not allow either [[thomas-roch|thomas roch]] or his keeper to appear on deck as long as she remains in Pamlico Sound." (008-002)

## In the Novel

Pamlico Sound serves as the maritime escape route for the [[ker-karraje|count d'artigas]]'s operation. After [[captain-spade|captain spade]]'s raiding party breaches Healthful House and seizes Roch and Hart (Gaydon), the captives are carried through the park to the shoreline, where they are taken by boat to the waiting schooner *Ebba*, anchored offshore in the sound.

The sound's geography — sheltered waters between the mainland and the Outer Banks — makes it an ideal location for a vessel waiting to conduct a clandestine operation. The *Ebba* can remain hidden from routine [[surveillance-and-observation|observation]] while maintaining quick access to the Atlantic Ocean for a rapid escape.

Once both Roch and Hart are aboard, the *Ebba* sails out of Pamlico Sound into the open Atlantic, beginning the long voyage to [[pirate-crew|the pirates]]' hidden base at [[back-cup|back cup]] in the [[bermuda|bermuda]] archipelago.

> "My conjecture is correct. Still gagged and bound I am at last lifted by the legs and shoulders. My impression, however, is that I am not being raised over a ship's bulwark, but on the contrary am being lowered." (008-004)

## Connections

Pamlico Sound connects the novel's two key locations:

- **Healthful House**: The sanatorium from which Roch is abducted, located near the sound's shoreline.
- **The Ebba**: Count d'Artigas's schooner-yacht, anchored in the sound as the getaway vessel.

The sound represents the geographical bridge between the civilized world of Healthful House and the lawless domain of Ker Karraje's pirate operation. Once the *Ebba* clears the sound, Hart realizes that all hope of immediate rescue is lost.
