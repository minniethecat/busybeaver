The Outer Banks are the chain of barrier islands off the coast of [[North Carolina]] that separate [[Pamlico Sound]] from the [[Atlantic Ocean]]. In *Facing the Flag*, the Banks were the final American land visible to the [[Ebba Schooner|Ebba]] as it sailed from [[Pamlico Sound]] into the open Atlantic on its [[ebba-voyage-to-back-cup|voyage to Back Cup]].
