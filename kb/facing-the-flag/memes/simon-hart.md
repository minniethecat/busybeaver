## Simon Hart

Simon Hart is the first-person narrator and protagonist of [[jules-verne|jules verne]]'s *Facing the Flag* (1896). A French engineer by profession, Hart assumes the identity of "Gaydon" to serve as the keeper and guardian of [[thomas-roch|thomas roch]] at [[healthful-house|healthful house]], acting on behalf of the French government to protect both the inventor and the secret of his devastating weapon, [[roch-[[fulgurator|fulgurator]]-invention|the fulgurator]].

> "Where am I. — (Notes by Simon Hart, the Engineer.)" (005-001)

## Role in the Story

Hart's narrative voice drives the entire novel, providing intimate access to the unfolding events. Under the alias Gaydon, he is assigned as Roch's constant companion at Healthful House, monitoring the inventor's condition and preventing any attempt to exploit his knowledge.

When [[ker-karraje|count d'artigas]] and [[captain-spade|captain spade]] kidnap Roch, Hart is taken as well — bound, gagged, and transported aboard [[ebba-schooner|the schooner]] *Ebba*. Throughout his [[imprisonment-and-captivity|captivity]], Hart maintains a careful balance: outwardly compliant with his captors while secretly observing every detail of their operation and searching for an opportunity to alert the authorities.

> "There was no help for it, however. The essential was to get possession of Roch. If they could kidnap Gaydon, too, in conformity with the intentions of the Count d'Artigas, so much the better." (005-008)

## Relationships

- **Thomas Roch**: Hart's charge and, in time, the focus of his protective mission. Hart develops genuine concern for Roch's well-being despite the inventor's mental instability.

- **Count d'Artigas (Ker Karraje)**: Hart's captor and adversary. Hart studies the Count carefully, recognizing the danger he represents and ultimately working to thwart his plans.

- **[[engineer-serko|engineer serko]]**: Hart's immediate supervisor during captivity. Serko assigns Hart tasks related to Roch's care and technical work, creating an uneasy working relationship.

- **Captain Spade**: Commander of the *Ebba* who carried out Hart's [[kidnapping-of-roch|abduction]]. Hart's first interrogation occurs with Spade on the schooner.

## Key Passages

Hart's scientific mind and observational skills are on display throughout his captivity. His recognition of [[back-cup|back cup]] island demonstrates his knowledge:

> "Well, I know and recognize this islet! It is situated at the extremity of the archipelago of the [[bermuda|bermudas]]. It is the 'reversed cup' that I had occasion to visit a few years ago — No, I am not mistaken." (013-003)

His determination is captured in the novel's climactic moment, when he acts decisively to prevent the fulgurator from being used against innocent people.
