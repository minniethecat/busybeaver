The Pirate Boatswain was a member of the [[pirate-crew]] at [[Back Cup]] responsible for the maintenance and operation of the vessels moored in the [[Back Cup Lagoon|interior lagoon]], including the [[Ebba Schooner|Ebba]] and the submarine tug. His role placed him at the operational heart of [[Ker Karraje]]'s maritime enterprise.

## Duties at Back Cup

The Boatswain's responsibilities included the upkeep of the pirate vessels, the management of deck crew during operations, and the supervision of repairs and provisioning. His knowledge of the [[submarine-tunnel]]'s navigational characteristics — tides, clearances, timing — made him essential to the movement of vessels in and out of [[Back Cup]].
