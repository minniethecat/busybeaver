The **Healthful House Surveillance** was the period of observation and intelligence-gathering conducted at the Healthful House sanitarium, where Thomas Roch was undergoing treatment. This event marks the beginning of the efforts by various parties to gain access to the inventor and his revolutionary weapon, the Fulgurator.

## Setting

Healthful House was a celebrated sanitarium located in North Carolina, known for its advanced treatments and tranquil setting. The institution attracted wealthy and distinguished patients from across the United States and beyond (001-016). It was here that Thomas Roch was placed under medical care following the deterioration of his mental health after the French government commission's rejection of his Fulgurator.

## Count d'Artigas's Arrival

The arrival of Count d'Artigas — in reality the notorious pirate Ker Karraje — at Healthful House marked the beginning of the surveillance phase (001-007). D'Artigas, traveling aboard his schooner *Ebba*, took up residence at the sanitarium under the pretext of seeking treatment. His true purpose was to assess Roch's condition and determine how to acquire the Fulgurator (002-027).

## Observation and Planning

During this period, d'Artigas and his associates carefully observed Roch's routines, his mental state, and the security arrangements at the sanitarium. They gathered the intelligence necessary to plan the kidnapping that would follow. The surveillance was conducted with considerable skill, as d'Artigas maintained his cover as a wealthy foreign nobleman seeking medical care.

## Outcome

The surveillance culminated in the successful kidnapping of Thomas Roch and his caretaker, Simon Hart (a French engineer working undercover). The abduction was carried out so efficiently that it left authorities with few immediate clues, allowing Karraje to transport Roch to his secret base at Back Cup.
