Rejection and resentment form a psychological through-line in *Facing the Flag*. [[Thomas Roch]]'s experience of institutional rejection — by [[France]], [[Germany]], [[British Admiralty|Britain]], and other powers — transforms him from a patriotic inventor into a mercenary consumed by grievance.
