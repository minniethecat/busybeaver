## Pirate Crew of Back Cup

The pirate crew of Back Cup is the loyal band of outlaws serving under the Malay pirate chief [[Ker Karraje]] (alias Count d'Artigas). Operating from the hidden cavern of [[Back Cup]], this crew manned the schooner *Ebba* and carried out the kidnapping of [[Thomas Roch]] and his keeper [[Simon Hart|Gaydon]] from [[Healthful House]].

The crew demonstrated unwavering loyalty to Karraje, maintaining the secrecy of the Back Cup base and executing his orders with precision. Their operations included maritime interdiction, covert transport, and the maintenance of the sophisticated [[submarine-technology|submarine]] and weapons systems housed within Back Cup.

> "The crew of the Ebba, which numbered about thirty men, had been recruited by Ker Karraje from among the pirates of the China Seas and the Pacific." (011-001)
