The Bermuda Archipelago is the group of islands in the North [[Atlantic Ocean]] that includes [[Bermuda]] and the surrounding islets, among them the isolated rock of [[Back Cup]]. In *Facing the Flag*, the archipelago was the focus of the international naval search for [[Ker Karraje]]'s hidden base.

## Geographical Context

The archipelago's location — approximately 640 miles east-southeast of Cape Hatteras, [[North Carolina]] — placed it within sailing distance of the American coast while remaining sufficiently isolated to provide cover for a hidden pirate base. [[Back Cup]], at the extremity of the group, was particularly well suited to concealment: a barren volcanic islet whose unbroken exterior gave no hint of the [[back-cup-cavern|cavern]] within.

## Strategic Significance

The archipelago's position astride Atlantic shipping routes made it strategically valuable for piracy. [[Ker Karraje]]'s choice of [[Back Cup]] as his base allowed him to strike at vessels traversing the Atlantic while remaining hidden from the naval patrols that searched for him.
