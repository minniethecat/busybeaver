## Count d'Artigas

"Count d'Artigas" (with apostrophe) is an alternative spelling of **[[count-dartigas]]**, the alias adopted by the Malay pirate chief [[Ker Karraje]]. Throughout Jules Verne's *Facing the Flag*, the name appears both as "Count d'Artigas" and "Count dArtigas" in different editions and translations.

The Count d'Artigas persona presents as a wealthy, cultured foreign nobleman visiting the United States. In reality, this is a facade for Karraje's true purpose: to acquire control of [[Thomas Roch]]'s fulgurator and use it for extortion.

> "The Count d'Artigas had come to the United States with the intention of gaining possession of the fulgurator." (003-005)
