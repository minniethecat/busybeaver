The Raising of the French Flag was the climactic visual event of *Facing the Flag*'s conclusion. As the French warship approached [[Back Cup]] during the [[final-naval-battle|final battle]], the [[Tricolor Flag]] was raised at its peak — a signal of national identity that would prove to be the novel's decisive moment.
