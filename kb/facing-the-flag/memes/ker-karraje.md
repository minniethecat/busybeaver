## Ker Karraje

Ker Karraje is the true identity of the man who poses as the wealthy foreign nobleman **[[count-dartigas]]**. A Malay pirate chief of notorious reputation, Karraje operates from the hidden cavern of [[Back Cup]], using the schooner *Ebba* and its loyal crew to carry out his operations.

Karraje adopted the alias "Count d'Artigas" to conceal his identity while visiting Healthful House to assess [[Thomas Roch]] and his invention, the [[fulgurator]]. His ultimate goal was to obtain exclusive control of the devastating weapon and use it to hold the world's nations at ransom.

> "It was not the Count d'Artigas, it was Ker Karraje, the pirate, the former Malay chief whose ships had plied the China Seas and the Pacific." (017-002)

## Modus Operandi

Kerraje's plan involved:
1. Kidnapping Thomas Roch and his keeper Gaydon ([[Simon Hart]]) from Healthful House
2. Transporting them to the secret base at Back Cup
3. Exploiting Roch's genius to perfect the fulgurator
4. Using the weapon to extort unprecedented sums from the world's governments
