The stratified social hierarchy of the Potteries, from factory owners to operatives, and how class position determines characters' life chances.

## Analysis

The concept of Social Class in the Five Towns operates throughout *Anna of the Five Towns* as a structuring principle. Characters are defined by their relationship to this concept — whether they exemplify it, rebel against it, or are destroyed by it.
