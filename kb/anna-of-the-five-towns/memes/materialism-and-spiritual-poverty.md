# Materialism and Spiritual Poverty

The spiritual cost of industrial wealth; how the accumulation of money in the Five Towns is accompanied by moral and spiritual impoverishment.

## Analysis

The concept of Materialism and Spiritual Poverty operates throughout *Anna of the Five Towns* as a structuring principle. Characters are defined by their relationship to this concept — whether they exemplify it, rebel against it, or are destroyed by it.
