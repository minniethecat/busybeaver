# Religious Hypocrisy

The gap between Wesleyan piety and actual conduct, embodied in characters whose public religiosity masks private transgression.

## Analysis

The concept of Religious Hypocrisy operates throughout *Anna of the Five Towns* as a structuring principle. Characters are defined by their relationship to this concept — whether they exemplify it, rebel against it, or are destroyed by it.
