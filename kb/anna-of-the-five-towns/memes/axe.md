# Axe

Axe is an agricultural town situated near the Five Towns, distinct from the industrial character of Hanbridge and Bursley.

## Overview

Unlike the pottery-manufacturing centres of the Five Towns, Axe is described as "the agricultural town in the..." district[PN=008-012]. It represents a different economic world — one based on farming and land rather than industry and manufacture.

## Significance in the Novel

Ephraim Tellwright attends a sale of property at Axe, a trip that underlines his preoccupation with property dealing and financial matters[PN=008-012]. When he returns home from Axe, he appears to be in a bad temper, suggesting that the outcome of the sale did not go as he had hoped[PN=008-084]. Axe is a peripheral location, seen only through Tellwright's business dealings.
