Norris's Brewery is a commercial brewery in the Five Towns district, one of the enterprises in which Anna Tellwright holds shares through her inheritance.

## Holdings

The brewery stock forms part of Anna's diversified portfolio, listed alongside her shares in the North Staffordshire Railway, Five Towns Waterworks, and Toft End Colliery and Brickworks (003-043). Brewery investments were common in Victorian portfolios, as the brewing industry was a staple of the British economy with steady demand.

## Context

Local breweries were significant economic institutions in Victorian industrial towns. The presence of a brewery in the Five Towns reflects the social culture of the Potteries — where public houses and beer were central to working-class and middle-class social life. Norris's Brewery, as part of Anna's inheritance, ties her fortune to the everyday commerce and social fabric of the district.
