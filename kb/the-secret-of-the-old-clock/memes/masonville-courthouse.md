# Masonville Courthouse

The courthouse in **[[masonville|Masonville]]** is the seat of county legal records and proceedings. It plays an important administrative role in the resolution of the **[[the-lost-will|Crowley will mystery]]**.

## Connection to the Case

When **[[nancy-drew|Nancy Drew]]** seeks the names of the **[[crowley-relatives|Crowley relatives]]** who filed claims against the estate, her father directs her to the courthouse where such records "should be on file." Unfortunately, it is too late in the day for Nancy to visit.

**[[judge-hartgrave|Judge Hartgrave]]**, who presides at the courthouse, is a colleague of **[[carson-drew|Carson Drew]]**. Nancy delivers important legal documents to him on behalf of her father. Later, it is a judge at this courthouse who issues the court order that allows Carson Drew to access **[[josiah-crowley|Josiah Crowley]]**'s safety deposit box at the **[[masonville-national-bank|Masonville National Bank]]**.

The courthouse is also where the Tophams later file their unsuccessful legal challenge to the second will, which is ultimately "thrown out of court."
