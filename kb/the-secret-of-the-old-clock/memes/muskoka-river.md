# Muskoka River

A river running along the winding road between River Heights and Masonville. During periods of heavy rain it overflows its banks, creating hazardous driving conditions for travelers.

## Location

The Muskoka River parallels the road between River Heights and Masonville. The road winds in and out along the river's course through open country. (003-028)

## Description

The river flows alongside a winding country road. Under normal conditions it is a scenic feature of the drive to Masonville. However, with the arrival of heavy rains, the Muskoka River can overflow its banks and flood the surrounding roadway, making the journey treacherous. The road's proximity to the river means that drivers must exercise caution during storms. (003-028) (003-033)

> The road wound in and out along the Muskoka River. Nancy met few automobiles. (003-028)
