# Events by Chapter

A chronological catalog of the key events in *The Secret of the Old Clock*, organized by their primary chapter reference. This timeline traces Nancy Drew's investigation from the discovery of the missing will through to the climactic capture of the robbers.

::: query
type: event
sort: chapter_ref
display: table
fields: [label, chapter_ref, participants, quality]
:::

## Navigating the Timeline

Events are listed by their primary chapter occurrence. Many events span multiple chapters — the chapter reference indicates where the event is first introduced or most prominently featured. For a complete narrative sequence, cross-reference with the [Event Index](event-index).
