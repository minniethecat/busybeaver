# Camp Life at Moon Lake

**[[camp-avondale|Camp Avondale]]**, a girls' summer camp on **[[moon-lake|Moon Lake]]**, provides the setting for a key phase of **[[nancy-drew|Nancy Drew]]**'s investigation. She stays at the camp under the pretense of a vacation while her real purpose is to search the nearby **[[topham-bungalow|Topham bungalow]]** for the **[[crowley-clock|Crowley clock]]**.

## Daily Activities

Camp life is packed with activities: tennis games, swimming, water polo, hiking, and launch rides on the lake. Nancy's friend **[[helen-corning|Helen Corning]]** is an enthusiastic participant who keeps Nancy constantly occupied. The food is "plain but substantial," and the cabins are basic but comfortable.

## The Launch

The camp owns a small secondhand **[[the-launch|launch]]** with an unreliable engine. Nancy's attempt to use the launch to reach the Topham bungalow ends in disaster when the engine dies mid-lake, stranding her for eight hours. She eventually fixes the engine and returns to camp sunburned and covered with grease.

## Strategic Purpose

Nancy uses the camp as cover for her investigation. She tells Helen she needs "a rest" to secure a day alone, then takes the launch toward the Topham bungalow. After her launch mishap, she leaves camp early, telling Helen she has "something important" to attend to, and drives to the bungalow by road — where she discovers the **[[bungalow-robbery|robbery]]** in progress.
