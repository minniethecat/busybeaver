# West Lake Road

A rural road outside River Heights where several characters connected to the Crowley case reside. It is the location of Abigail Rowen's home, making it an important waypoint in Nancy's investigation.

## Location

West Lake Road lies outside the main River Heights area, reachable by automobile. It connects to rural properties and was accessible enough for Nancy to drive along while reviewing the facts of the case. (008-052) (010-032)

## Description

The road appears to be a country road lined with residential properties. Grace Horner gives directions to Abigail Rowen's home along this road. The area is rural enough that finding a specific house requires inquiry along the way. (009-003)

> "She lives over on the West Lake Road," Grace responded. (008-052)

## Role in the Mystery

West Lake Road serves as the access route to Abigail Rowen's home, where Nancy obtains critical information about Josiah Crowley's true wishes for his estate. Abigail's testimony — that Crowley intended to leave his money to relatives and friends who had been kind to him, not the Tophams — is a pivotal piece of evidence supporting the search for a later will. The road represents the path to one of the key witnesses whose account helps justify Nancy's conviction that a later, fairer will exists. (008-052) (009-003)
