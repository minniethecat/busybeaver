# Launch Engine Failure

During her stay at **[[camp-avondale|Camp Avondale]]** on **[[moon-lake|Moon Lake]]**, **[[nancy-drew|Nancy Drew]]** attempts to use the camp's **[[the-launch|launch]]** to reach the **[[topham-bungalow|Topham bungalow]]** on the opposite shore. Her plan is thwarted when the unreliable engine fails.

## The Breakdown

Nancy successfully starts the launch engine and heads across the lake. As she nears the opposite shore, "there was a miss in the steady throb of the engine. The motor gave one long slow wheeze and died." An examination reveals the tank is nearly full — the problem is mechanical.

## Stranded

For nearly an hour she works on the engine without success. The launch drifts "farther and farther from shore." She is within sight of the Topham bungalow, which appears deserted, but cannot reach it. Swimming is impractical — even if she reached shore, she would have no way to return to camp.

For eight hours she remains stranded, hungry and sunburned, watching the shadows lengthen. "Never had time seemed to pass so monotonously."

## Aftermath

When "the sun sank into the water several hours later," Nancy gives the engine wheel "a vicious swing" — and it mysteriously restarts. She returns to camp sunburned and covered with grease. The wasted day forces her to leave camp early and drive to the bungalow by road, where she discovers the **[[bungalow-robbery|robbery]]**.
