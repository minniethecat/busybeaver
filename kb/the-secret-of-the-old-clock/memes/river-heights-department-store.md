# River Heights Department Store

The department store in downtown **[[river-heights|River Heights]]** serves as a backdrop for two significant encounters between **[[nancy-drew|Nancy Drew]]** and the **[[topham-family|Topham sisters]]**.

## First Encounter

Nancy visits the store to purchase a party frock. While waiting, she observes **[[ada-topham|Ada]]** and **[[isabel-topham|Isabel Topham]]** loudly complaining to the floor manager about the wait for a saleswoman. Ada declares, "Perhaps you don't know who we are!" and threatens to have the manager discharged, claiming her father owns stock in the store.

Ada and Isabel are rude to the saleswoman, tossing aside expensive gowns and even stepping on one. When Ada brushes against Nancy without apologizing, she blames Nancy instead, snapping "Do watch where you are going!"

## Second Encounter

On a later visit, Nancy witnesses **[[ada-vase-incident|Ada breaking an imported vase]]** and falsely accusing the saleswoman. Nancy intervenes to tell the truth, deepening the Topham sisters' hatred of her.

## Significance

These encounters establish the Tophams' character — arrogant, dishonest, and abusive to those they consider beneath them — and strengthen Nancy's resolve to see justice done in the **[[the-lost-will|Crowley will case]]**.
