# Carson Drew's Law Office

The law office of **[[carson-drew|Carson Drew]]** is located in downtown **[[river-heights|River Heights]]**, within walking distance of the **[[royal-hotel|Royal Hotel]]**. It serves as a frequent meeting place for Nancy and her father to discuss the progress of the **[[the-lost-will|Crowley will mystery]]**.

## Role in the Investigation

Nancy visits the office multiple times during her investigation:

- After her **[[chance-meeting|chance meeting]]** with the **[[horner-sisters|Horner sisters]]**, she rushes to tell her father about their potential claim
- Following the **[[eavesdropping-in-park|eavesdropping incident]]**, she comes seeking the names of other **[[crowley-relatives|Crowley relatives]]**
- **[[carson-drew|Carson Drew]]** keeps **[[crowley-notebook|the Crowley notebook]]** in his office safe for security
- After recovering the **[[finding-the-missing-will|second will]]** from the **[[masonville-national-bank|Masonville National Bank]]**, Drew brings it here for careful legal analysis

The office features an inner private office where confidential conversations occur. An outer office is staffed by Drew's private secretary who manages appointments and visitors.
