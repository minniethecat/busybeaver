# The Will Mystery Timeline

The mystery of Josiah Crowley's missing will unfolds across all twenty-five chapters of *The Secret of the Old Clock*. This timeline traces the critical events — Nancy's growing suspicion, her systematic interviews with the relatives, the discovery of the secret notebook, and the final revelation of the true will — in chronological order with causal links between each development.

## Key Milestones

| Phase | Events | Chapters |
|-------|--------|----------|
| **The Problem** | Nancy learns of the Crowley will injustice; Carson Drew explains the legal obstacles | I |
| **First Investigation** | Nancy meets the Horner sisters; learns a second will may exist | II–IV |
| **Legal Research** | Nancy and her father consult Mr. Rolsted; confirmation that Crowley intended a new will | V |
| **Systematic Interviews** | Nancy visits the Turner sisters, Mathews brothers, and Abigail Rowen | IX–X |
| **The Topham Confrontation** | Nancy visits the Topham residence; discovers their hostility | XI |
| **Undercover Work** | Nancy goes undercover at Camp Avondale on Moon Lake to investigate the Topham bungalow | XII–XIII |
| **The Notebook** | The crowley-notebook is found, containing cryptic entries about the hidden will | XIV |
| **The Trapped Scene** | Nancy is locked in a closet in the Topham bungalow | XV |
| **The Rescue** | Nancy escapes and continues the investigation | XVI–XVII |
| **Police Involvement** | Marshal Melborne is notified; the robbers are pursued | XVIII–XIX |
| **The Discovery** | Nancy finds the crowley-clock containing the hidden will | XX |
| **The Chase** | Capturing the robbers who have been ransacking relatives' homes | XXI |
| **The Revelation** | The notebook reveals Crowley's final intentions | XXII |
| **The Search** | Looking for the will in the clock | XXIII |
| **The Reading** | The true will is read aloud; the deserving relatives receive their inheritance | XXIV |
| **The Reward** | Nancy receives recognition; the Horner sisters and other relatives are secure | XXV |
