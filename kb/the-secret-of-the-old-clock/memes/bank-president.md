# Bank President

The unnamed president of the [[first-national-bank|Masonville National Bank]], who is present when [[carson-drew|Carson Drew]] and [[nancy-drew|Nancy Drew]] access [[josiah-crowley|Josiah Crowley]]'s safety deposit box containing the long-lost [[crowley-will-document|second will]]. (023-026)

## Role in the Story

The bank president appears in a single scene at the [[first-national-bank|Masonville National Bank]] when [[carson-drew|Carson Drew]] and [[nancy-drew|Nancy]] open [[josiah-crowley|Josiah Crowley]]'s safety deposit box. He is present as a witness to the opening of the box, serving as the official bank authority. When [[doctor-nesbitt|Doctor Nesbitt]], one of the witnesses to the original [[will-legal-document|Crowley will]], attempts to tell his story about how [[josiah-crowley|Crowley]] came to make the [[crowley-will-document|second will]], "before he could finish the story, the bank president cut him short." (023-026)

## Character Traits

- Officious and businesslike: interrupts a witness's story to keep proceedings formal
- Institutional authority: serves as the representative of [[first-national-bank|the bank]] in the safety deposit box access

## Relationships

- [[carson-drew]] — The attorney who accesses the deposit box
- [[nancy-drew]] — Present with her father at [[first-national-bank|the bank]]
- [[doctor-nesbitt]] — The witness whose story the bank president cuts short
- [[josiah-crowley]] — The deceased whose safety deposit box is being accessed

## Role in the Mystery

The bank president facilitates the crucial discovery of the [[crowley-will-document|second will]]. His presence at the access of [[josiah-crowley|Josiah Crowley]]'s safety deposit box provides institutional legitimacy to the proceedings. His impatience with [[doctor-nesbitt|Doctor Nesbitt]]'s narrative underscores the tension between legal formality and the human story behind [[crowley-will-document|the will]].

## Narrative Significance

The bank president is a minor institutional figure who represents the official, procedural machinery that [[josiah-crowley|Josiah Crowley]] attempted to bypass by writing his own [[will-legal-document|will]]. His brief appearance grounds [[crowley-will-document|the will]]'s discovery in legitimate banking procedure, lending credibility to the document that [[will-legal-document|will]] ultimately redistribute [[josiah-crowley|Crowley]]'s fortune to the deserving relatives.
