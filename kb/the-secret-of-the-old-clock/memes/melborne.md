## Location

Melborne was the nearest town to the Topham family's summer bungalow, situated in the rural countryside [018-003]. It lay along the route that Nancy and her companions took when tracking the robbers who had stolen the Crowley clock.

## Significance in the Investigation

When Nancy, her father Carson Drew, and the authorities were pursuing the three robbers, they entered the outskirts of Melborne as the nearest settlement to the Topham property [018-003]. The roadhouse inn known as The Black Horse was located less than a mile from Melborne [018-049].

The town served as a key reference point for the search operations. The robbers' moving van was tracked on the roads around Melborne, and the police pursuit eventually passed through the Melborne area [021-017].
