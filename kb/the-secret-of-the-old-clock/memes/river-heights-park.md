# River Heights Public Park

The public park in **[[river-heights|River Heights]]** is a green space featuring benches, drinking fountains, and pathways lined with overhanging bushes. It serves as a key location in the investigation of the **[[the-lost-will|Crowley will mystery]]**.

## Role in the Mystery

After witnessing **[[ada-topham|Ada Topham]]** break an expensive vase at the **[[river-heights|River Heights]]** department store and falsely blame the saleswoman, **[[nancy-drew|Nancy Drew]]** walks through the park to clear her head. She spots **[[ada-topham|Ada]]** and **[[isabel-topham|Isabel Topham]]** seated on a bench engaged in earnest conversation.

Realizing they are likely discussing her, Nancy decides to avoid a confrontation by taking a path through the bushes behind their bench. As she passes, she overhears the words "the will" and stops to listen. Crouching behind the bench, Nancy learns that the Topham sisters are worried about the possibility of a second will surfacing — crucially, their conversation reveals they have not destroyed any such document and are uncertain of its whereabouts.

This **[[eavesdropping|eavesdropping]]** incident gives Nancy renewed hope and spurs her to visit the **[[crowley-relatives|Crowley relatives]]** to gather more information.

## Description

The park features a drinking fountain, benches, and gravel paths. The area behind the bench where the Tophams sit is thick with bushes, providing cover for Nancy's surveillance.
