# Characters by Affiliation

The characters of *The Secret of the Old Clock*, organized by their stated affiliation. This grouping reveals the social network of the novel — the households, families, and institutions that define each character's position in the story.

## Reading the Affiliations

- **Drew household** — Nancy Drew, Carson Drew, and Hannah Gruen: the moral center of the novel
- **Topham family** — Richard Topham and his daughters: the antagonists
- **Horner sisters** — Grace and Allie Horner: the deserving relatives
- **Moon Lake / Camp Avondale** — characters associated with the summer camp investigation
- Characters without a stated affiliation are listed separately
