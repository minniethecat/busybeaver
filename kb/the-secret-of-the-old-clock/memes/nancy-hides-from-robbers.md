# Nancy Hides from the Robbers

After tracking the **[[bungalow-robbery|robbers]]** to **[[the-black-horse|The Black Horse]]** roadhouse, **[[nancy-drew|Nancy Drew]]** locates their moving van in a barn behind the inn. She finds the keys under the driver's seat, unlocks the rear doors, and retrieves the **[[crowley-clock|Crowley clock]]** from inside.

## The Close Call

As Nancy secures the clock, she hears the robbers returning. She hastily locks the van doors and flings the keys onto the floor. With no time to escape, she scrambles into an empty manger and covers herself with a blanket just as the barn door swings open.

The three robbers, drunk and quarrelsome, enter. Nancy listens in terror as they search for the missing keys. One robber insists he left them under the seat, while the leader threatens him for losing them. A third robber discovers the keys on the floor, and after some argument, they dismiss the anomaly.

The leader checks the rear doors of the van, finds them still locked, and concludes, "No one has meddled with the truck." The robbers drive away, and Nancy emerges from the manger, clutching the **[[crowley-clock|precious clock]]**.

## Aftermath

Nancy escapes through the woods to her roadster. Inside the clock, she discovers **[[crowley-notebook|Josiah Crowley's notebook]]**, which contains directions to the **[[the-lost-will|missing will]]**. She then alerts the marshal at **[[melborne|Melborne]]**, leading to the **[[capturing-the-robbers|capture of the robbers]]**.
