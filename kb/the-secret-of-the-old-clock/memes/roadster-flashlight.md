# Nancy's Automobile Flashlight

**[[nancy-drew|Nancy Drew]]**'s automobile flashlight is an essential tool during her recovery of the **[[crowley-clock|Crowley clock]]** from the robbers' moving van at **[[the-black-horse|The Black Horse]]** roadhouse.

## The Search

As dusk falls, Nancy retrieves the flashlight from her **[[nancy-roadster|roadster]]** before approaching the inn through the woods. She uses it to examine the dark interior of the barn and the garage. When she opens the moving van doors, the flashlight beam reveals the loaded furniture: "chairs, tables, rugs and boxes."

The beam finally "rested for a moment upon an object in a far corner" — the Crowley clock, wrapped in a blanket. Without the flashlight, Nancy would have been searching blindly in the pitch-dark barn.

## Loss and Escape

In her hasty escape when the robbers return, Nancy leaves the flashlight behind — probably in the manger where she hid. She realizes this only after fleeing through the woods: "To her chagrin she discovered that she had left her automobile flashlight somewhere in the barn, probably in the manger."

She navigates the dark woods by "sense of direction" alone, plunging through bushes toward her hidden roadster. The lost flashlight becomes an acceptable sacrifice — the clock and **[[crowley-notebook|notebook]]** are far more valuable.
