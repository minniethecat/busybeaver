The physical world of *The Secret of the Old Clock*, organized by geographic location. This grouping maps the novel's spatial landscape — the towns, lakes, homes, and roads that form the setting of Nancy Drew's first investigation.

## The Geography of the Novel

- **River Heights** — Nancy's hometown and the moral center of the story
- **Moon Lake** — the resort area where key discoveries unfold
- **Masonville** — the nearby town with the courthouse and bank
- The surrounding countryside includes farms, cottages, and roads that connect the novel's key locations
