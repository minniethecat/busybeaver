# Crowley Family Relationships

Josiah Crowley's extended family forms the social backbone of *The Secret of the Old Clock*. This page maps every known relative, their relationship to Crowley, and their role in the story — from the scheming Tophams who manipulated their way into his will, to the deserving relatives who ultimately received their share.

## The Family Web

Josiah Crowley had no children of his own, and his wife died during the influenza epidemic following World War I. After her death, Crowley "made his home with various relatives" (001-025), moving from household to household. This peripatetic lifestyle created a complex web of dependents and claimants:

**Blood Relatives:**
- **The Horner Sisters** (Grace and Allie) — Crowley's nieces, or close enough to be treated as such; "great pets of Crowley's when they were children" (001-061)
- **The Turner Sisters** (Edna and Matilda) — Cousins living in Masonville
- **The Mathews Brothers** (William and Fred) — Nephews, farmers on the River Road

**By Marriage / Extended:**
- **The Topham Family** (Richard, Cora, Isabel, Ada) — Distant relatives who took Crowley in during his final years, feigning affection to secure the inheritance

**By Care and Loyalty:**
- **Abigail Rowen** — Not a blood relative, but she nursed Crowley through an illness and "he thought a lot of her" (009-053). She "knew more about his queer ways than any other living person" (009-055).

## The Inheritance Dispute

The family relationships were tested by the will controversy. Crowley had been "forced to live with kindly relatives who were scarcely able to have him with them" when the Tophams showed no interest. Later, the Tophams experienced a "sudden change of heart" (001-029) and took him in — only to treat him "unkindly" once the will was secured in their favor.

The true will, discovered by Nancy Drew, redistributed the fortune to the deserving relatives:
- The Horner sisters: the bulk of the estate
- The Turner sisters: a substantial bequest
- The Mathews brothers: $20,000
- Abigail Rowen: $75,000
