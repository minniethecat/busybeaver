# Imported Italian Vase

The imported Italian vase is an expensive decorative bowl displayed on a counter at the **[[river-heights-department-store|River Heights department store]]**. Its accidental destruction by **[[ada-topham|Ada Topham]]** triggers a confrontation that reveals the Topham sisters' dishonesty.

## The Accident

While walking through the store, Ada brushes carelessly against the counter, and her sleeve catches the vase. It falls to the floor and breaks into a dozen pieces. The floor manager informs her it is "an expensive imported bowl" from Italy, valued at fifty dollars.

## False Accusation

Rather than accept responsibility, Ada falsely claims the saleswoman broke the vase. She enlists her sister **[[isabel-topham|Isabel Topham]]** to corroborate the lie. The floor manager, caught between a wealthy customer and his employee, seems inclined to believe the Tophams.

## Nancy's Intervention

**[[nancy-drew|Nancy Drew]]**, who witnessed the accident, steps forward: "I am certain the saleswoman did not break the bowl, for I saw the accident myself." Her testimony forces Ada to pay.

This incident is a microcosm of the Tophams' broader pattern of dishonesty — a pattern that ultimately costs them the **[[fortune|Crowley fortune]]** when **[[justice|justice]]** prevails.
