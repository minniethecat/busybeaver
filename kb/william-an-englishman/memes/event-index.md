## About the Events

Hamilton structures her novel around a series of set-piece events that mark William Tully's transformation: the pre-war radical meeting where he meets Griselda, the outbreak of war and the rush to enlist, the battlefield experiences in Belgium, and the postwar reckoning. Each event page situates these fictional moments within their historical context — the suffrage campaign, the recruitment drives, the trench warfare, and the political realignments of 1918–1919.
