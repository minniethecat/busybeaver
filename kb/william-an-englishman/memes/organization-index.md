## About the Organizations

Hamilton, a lifelong activist in the women's suffrage and labour movements, writes institutions from the inside. Her portrayal of organizations — from the militant Women's Social and Political Union to the Fabian socialist circles to the British Army — is sharp, satirical, and informed by firsthand experience. Each organization page connects the novel's fictional representation to the historical record of these institutions in the early twentieth century.
