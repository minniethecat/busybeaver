# Fort Reliance

Fort Reliance was a remote [[hudsons-bay-company|Hudson's Bay Company]] trading post in the Canadian Arctic territories, serving as the administrative and commercial hub for [[hudsons-bay-company|the Company]]'s northernmost operations. In _The Fur Country_, it is the setting for the novel's opening chapters and the launching point for the expedition led by [[jaspar-hobson|Lieutenant Jaspar Hobson]] to establish a new fort on the shores of the [[arctic-ocean|Arctic Ocean]]. Fort Reliance served as the staging ground for Hobson's expedition, its storehouses and barracks providing the essential supplies and manpower needed for the ambitious northern venture.

### Overview

Fort Reliance was situated deep in the Arctic wilderness, one of [[hudsons-bay-company|the Company]]'s most isolated establishments. The fort's structures reflected the harsh conditions of its environment—walls constructed of roughly-hewn tree trunks, narrow windows encrusted with hoarfrost, and a great hall that served as both trading floor and social gathering place (001-001).

The post was managed by [[captain-craventy|Captain Craventy]], who hosted the soirée that opens the novel. Under his command, the fort maintained a small garrison of Company employees, native allies, and the essential personnel—traders, carpenters, and soldiers—who kept the fur trade operating through the brutal winters.

## The Great Hall

The fort's great hall was the center of its social and commercial life. [[corporal-joliffe|Corporal Joliffe]] had transformed the room for [[captain-craventy|Captain Craventy]]'s reception, its rough walls disguised by displays of weapons and furs—a living catalogue of the trade that sustained [[hudsons-bay-company|the Company]] (001-001).

The decorations told the story of [[hudsons-bay-company|the Company]]'s wealth: magnificent furs of wolves, [[polar-bear-encounter|polar bear]]s, otters, wolverines, beavers, muskrats, and silver foxes hung from the beams, while muskets, cutlasses, and boarding-pikes adorned the walls, reminders that the fur trade was conducted in a contested and often dangerous territory.

## The Expedition's Launch Point

From Fort Reliance, [[jaspar-hobson|Lieutenant Hobson]]'s expedition departed northward toward the Arctic coast, charged with establishing a new factory at the seventieth parallel. The fort thus serves as the novel's point of departure—the last outpost of civilization before the party ventures into the truly unknown polar wilderness (005-001).

The expedition's composition reflected the fort's role as a Company hub: Hobson himself, [[thomas-black|the astronomer]] [[thomas-black|Thomas Black]], the celebrated traveller [[paulina-barnett|Mrs. Paulina Barnett]], a company of soldiers, and a team of native guides and dog-sled drivers—all assembled at Fort Reliance for the journey north.
