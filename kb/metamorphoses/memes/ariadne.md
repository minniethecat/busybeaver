# Ariadne

Ariadne, daughter of King Minos of Crete, helped [[theseus|Theseus]] navigate the Labyrinth to destroy the [[minotaur|Minotaur]]. Her lover cruelly deserted her on the Isle of Dias, where [[bacchus|Bacchus]] discovered her, made her his wife, and gave her a crown that became a constellation (069-001).
