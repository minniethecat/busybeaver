[056-001] [[niobe|Niobe]], struck with horror at the magnitude of her calamities is changed into a stone.

[056-002] Widow’d, and childless, lamentable state!

[056-003] A doleful sight, among the dead she sate;

[056-004] Harden’d with woes, a statue of despair,

[056-005] To ev’ry breath of wind unmoved her hair;

[056-006] Her cheek still reddening, but its colour dead,

[056-007] Faded her eyes, and set within her head;

[056-008] No more her pliant tongue its motion keeps,

[056-009] But stands congeal’d within her frozen lips;

[056-010] Stagnate and dull, within her purple veins,

[056-011] Its current stopp’d, the lifeless blood remains;

[056-012] Her feet their usual offices refuse;

[056-013] Her arms and neck their graceful gestures lose:

[056-014] Action and life from ev’ry part are gone,

[056-015] And ev’n her entrails turn to solid stone:

[056-016] Yet still she weeps, and whirld by stormy winds,

[056-017] Borne through the air, her native country finds;

[056-018] There, fix’d, she stands upon a bleaky hill,

[056-019] There yet her marble cheeks eternal tears distil.
