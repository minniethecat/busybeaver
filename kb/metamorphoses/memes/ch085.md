[075-001] Achelous relates to his guest the various transformations of Proteus.

[075-002] He ceased in his relation to proceed,

[075-003] While all admired the author and the deed;

[075-004] But Theseus most, inquisitive to know

[075-005] From gods what wondrous alterations grow.

[075-006] Whom thus the Calydonian stream address’d,

[075-007] Raised high to speak, the couch his elbow press’d.

[075-008] “Some, when transform’d, fix in the lasting change;

[075-009] Some, with more right, through various figures range.

[075-010] Proteus, thus large thy privilege was found,

[075-011] Thou inmate of the seas, which earth surround.

[075-012] Sometimes a blooming youth you graced the shore;

[075-013] Oft a fierce lion or a furious boar:

[075-014] With glist’ring spires now seem’d a hissing snake.

[075-015] The bold would tremble in his hands to take:

[075-016] With horns assumed a bull; sometimes you proved

[075-017] A tree by roots, a stone by weight unmoved:

[075-018] Sometimes two wav’ring contraries became,

[075-019] Flow’d down in water, or aspired in flame.”
