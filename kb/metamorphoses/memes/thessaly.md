# Thessaly

Thessaly, in northern Greece, is the land of magic in the *Metamorphoses*. It is where Medea flies in her dragon-drawn chariot to gather herbs for her potions, and it is associated throughout classical literature with witchcraft and transformation. Thessaly thus represents the geographical concentration of transformative power — a place where the boundary between natural and supernatural is permeable.
