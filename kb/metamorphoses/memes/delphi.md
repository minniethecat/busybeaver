# Delphi

Delphi, on the slopes of Mount Parnassus, is Apollo's most sacred site in the *Metamorphoses*. It is where the god slew the serpent Python and established his oracle, and it is near Delphi that Deucalion and Pyrrha land after the Flood to consult the oracle of Themis.
