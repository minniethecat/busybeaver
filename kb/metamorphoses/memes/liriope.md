# Liriope

Liriope was a water-nymph and the mother of [[narcissus|Narcissus]]. She consulted the blind prophet [[tiresias|Tiresias]] about her son's future, and he gave the famous prophecy that Narcissus would live to old age "if he never knows himself" (022-004).
