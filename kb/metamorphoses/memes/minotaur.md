# Minotaur

The Minotaur, a hybrid monster half-man and half-bull, was imprisoned in the Labyrinth built by Daedalus in Crete. Theseus destroyed the Minotaur with the aid of Ariadne, who guided the hero through the winding passages of the Labyrinth (069-001). The creature is also referenced in a poetic context as dwelling within "private walls" (069-032).
