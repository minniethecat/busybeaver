[077-001] The goddess afflicts Erisichthon with continual hunger.

[077-002] “Where frozen Scythia’s utmost bound is placed,

[077-003] A desert lies, a melancholy waste:

[077-004] In yellow crops there Nature never smiled,

[077-005] No fruitful tree to shade the barren wild.

[077-006] There sluggish cold its icy station makes,

[077-007] There paleness frights, and anguish trembling shakes.

[077-008] Of pining Famine this the fated seat,

[077-009] To whom my orders in these words repeat:

[077-010] ‘Bid her this miscreant with her sharpest pains

[077-011] Chastise, and sheath herself into his veins;

[077-012] Be unsubdued by plenty’s baffled store,

[077-013] Reject my empire, and defeat my power;

[077-014] And lest the distance, and the tedious way,

[077-015] Should with the toil and long fatigue dismay,

[077-016] Ascend my chariot, and, convey’d on high,

[077-017] Guide the rein’d dragons through the parting sky.’

[077-018] The nymph, accepting of the granted car,

[077-019] Sprung to the seat, and posted through the air;

[077-020] Nor stopp’d till she to a bleak mountain came

[077-021] Of wondrous height, and Caucasus its name.

[077-022] There in a stony field the fiend she found,

[077-023] Herbs gnawing, and roots scratching from the ground.

[077-024] Her elf-lock hair in matted tresses grew,

[077-025] Sunk were her eyes, and pale her ghastly hue;

[077-026] Wan were her lips, and foul with clammy glue,

[077-027] Her throat was furr’d, her entrails seen within

[077-028] With snaky crawlings through her parchment skin.

[077-029] Her jutting hips seem’d starting from their place,

[077-030] And for a stomach’s was a belly’s space.

[077-031] Her joints protuberant by leanness grown,

[077-032] Consumption sunk the flesh, and raised the bone.

[077-033] Her knees’ large orbits bunch’d to monstrous size,

[077-034] And ankles to undue proportion rise.

[077-035] This plague the nymph, not daring to draw near,

[077-036] At distance hail’d, and greeted from afar;

[077-037] And though she told her charge without delay,

[077-038] Though her arrival late, and short her stay,

[077-039] She felt keen famine, or she seem’d to feel,

[077-040] Invade her blood, and on her vitals steal.

[077-041] She turn’d, from the infection to remove,

[077-042] And back to [[thessaly|Thessaly]] the serpents drove.

[077-043] The fiend obey’d the goddess’s command

[077-044] (Though their effects in opposition stand),

[077-045] She cut her way, supported by the wind,

[077-046] And reach’d the mansion by the nymph assign’d.

[077-047] ’Twas night, when, entering Erisichthon’s room,

[077-048] Dissolv’d in sleep, and thoughtless of his doom,

[077-049] She clasp’d his limbs, by impious labour tired,

[077-050] With battish wings, but her whole self inspired;

[077-051] Breathed on his throat and chest a tainting blast,

[077-052] And in his veins infused an endless fast.

[077-053] The task despatch’d, away the fury flies

[077-054] From plenteous regions, and from ripening skies;

[077-055] To her old barren north she wings her speed,

[077-056] And cottages distress’d with pinching need.

[077-057] Still slumbers Erisichthon’s senses drown,

[077-058] And sooth his fancy with their softest down.

[077-059] He dreams of viands delicate to eat,

[077-060] And revels on imaginary meat.

[077-061] Chews with his working mouth, but chews in vain,

[077-062] And tires his grinding teeth with fruitless pain;

[077-063] Deludes his throat with visionary fare,

[077-064] Feasts on the wind, and banquets on the air.

[077-065] The morning came, the night and slumbers pass’d,

[077-066] But still the furious pangs of hunger last;

[077-067] The cank’rous rage still gnaws with griping pains,

[077-068] Stings in his throat, and in his bowels reigns.

[077-069] Straight he requires, impatient in demand,

[077-070] Provisions from the air, the seas, the land.

[077-071] But though the land, air, seas, provisions grant,

[077-072] Starves at full tables, and complains of want.

[077-073] What to a people might in dole be paid,

[077-074] Or victual cities for a long blockade,

[077-075] Could not one wolfish appetite assuage;

[077-076] For glutting nourishment increased its rage.

[077-077] As rivers pour’d from every distant shore

[077-078] The sea insatiate drinks, and thirsts for more,

[077-079] Or as the fire, which all materials burns,

[077-080] And wasted forests into ashes turns,

[077-081] Grows more voracious as the more it preys,

[077-082] Recruits dilate the flame, and spread the blaze,

[077-083] So impious Erisichthon’s hunger raves,

[077-084] Receives refreshments, and refreshments craves.

[077-085] Food raises a desire for food, and meat

[077-086] Is but a new provocative to eat.

[077-087] He grows more empty, as the more supplied,

[077-088] And endless cramming but extends the void.
