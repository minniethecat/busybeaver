# Latium

Latium is the promised land of the *Metamorphoses*' final books: the destination of Aeneas's flight from Troy, the kingdom where he will found the line that produces Romulus, and the region that will become the heart of the Roman Empire. Ovid presents Latium not as a blank territory but as a landscape already dense with its own transformations.
