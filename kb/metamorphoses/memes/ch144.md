[128-001] Macareus relates the adventures of Ulysses and his companions during their voyage to Ithaca, with the enchantments of Circe, who detains the hero at her court twelve months.

[128-002] Then Macareus: “There reign’d a prince of fame

[128-003] O’er Tuscan seas, and Aeolus his name.

[128-004] A largess to Ulysses he consign’d,

[128-005] And in a steer’s tough hide enclosed a wind;

[128-006] Nine days before the swelling gale we ran,

[128-007] The tenth to make the meeting land began,

[128-008] When now the merry mariners, to find

[128-009] Imagined wealth within, the bag unbind.

[128-010] Forthwith outrush’d a gust, which backward bore

[128-011] Our galleys to the Laestrygonian shore,

[128-012] Whose crown Antiphates the tyrant wore.

[128-013] Some few commission’d were with speed to treat;

[128-014] We to his court repair; his guards we meet.

[128-015] Two, friendly flight preserved, the third was doom’d

[128-016] To be by those cursed cannibals consumed.

[128-017] Inhumanly our hapless friends they treat;

[128-018] Our men they murder, and destroy our fleet.

[128-019] In time the wise Ulysses bore away,

[128-020] And dropp’d his anchor in yon faithless bay.

[128-021] The thoughts of perils past we still retain,

[128-022] And fear to land, till lots appoint the men.

[128-023] Polites true, Elpenor given to wine,

[128-024] Eurylochus, myself, the lots assign.

[128-025] Design’d for dangers, and resolved to dare,

[128-026] To Circe’s fatal palace we repair.

[128-027] “Before the spacious front a herd we find

[128-028] Of beasts, the fiercest of the savage kind.

[128-029] Our trembling steps with blandishments they meet,

[128-030] And fawn, unlike their species, at our feet.

[128-031] Within, upon a sumptuous throne of state,

[128-032] On golden columns raised, the enchantress sate;

[128-033] Rich was her robe, and amiable her mien,

[128-034] Her aspect awful, and she look’d a queen;

[128-035] Her maids not mind the loom, nor household care,

[128-036] Nor wage in needlework a Scythian war,

[128-037] But cull, in canisters, disastrous flowers,

[128-038] And plants from haunted heaths, and fairy bowers,

[128-039] With brazen sickles reap’d at planetary hours.

[128-040] Each dose the goddess weighs with watchful eye,

[128-041] So nice her art in impious pharmacy.

[128-042] Entering, she greets us with a gracious look,

[128-043] And airs, that future amity bespoke.

[128-044] Her ready nymphs serve up a rich repast;

[128-045] The bowl she dashes first, then gives to taste.

[128-046] Quick, to our own undoing we comply;

[128-047] Her power we prove, and show the sorcery.

[128-048] “Soon, in a length of face, our head extends,

[128-049] Our chine stiff bristles bears, and forward bends,

[128-050] A breadth of brawn new burnishes our neck;

[128-051] Anon we grunt, as we begin to speak.

[128-052] Alone Eurylochus refused to taste,

[128-053] Nor to a beast obscene the man debased.

[128-054] Hither Ulysses hastes, (so fates command,

[128-055] And bears the powerful moly in his hand,

[128-056] Unsheaths his cimeter, assaults the dame,

[128-057] Preserves his species, and remains the same.

[128-058] The nuptial rite this outrage straight attends;

[128-059] The dower desired is his transfigured friends.

[128-060] The incantation backward she repeats,

[128-061] Inverts her rod, and what she did defeats.

[128-062] “And now our skin grows smooth, our shape upright,

[128-063] Our arms stretch up, our cloven feet unite;

[128-064] With tears our weeping general we embrace,

[128-065] Hang on his neck, and melt upon his face.

[128-066] Twelve silver moons in Circe’s court we stay,

[128-067] While there they waste the unwilling hours away.

[128-068] ’Twas here I spied a youth in Parian stone,

[128-069] His head a pecker bore, the cause unknown
