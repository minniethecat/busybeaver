[073-001] The nymph Perimele suffers violence from the river-god Achelous, and is cast into the sea by her enraged father—Neptune, in compassion, converts her into a rock.

[073-002] “But yonder far, lo! yonder does appear

[073-003] An isle, a part to me for ever dear;

[073-004] From that (it sailors Perimele name)

[073-005] I doting, forced, by strength, a virgin’s fame.

[073-006] Hippodamas’s passion grew so strong,

[073-007] Gall’d with the abuse, and fretted at the wrong,

[073-008] He cast his pregnant daughter from a rock;

[073-009] I spread my waves beneath and broke the shock;

[073-010] And, as her swimming weight my stream convey’d,

[073-011] I sued for help divine, and thus I pray’d:

[073-012] ‘O powerful thou! whose trident does command

[073-013] The realm of waters, which surround the land;

[073-014] We sacred rivers, wheresoe’er begun,

[073-015] End in thy lot, and to thy empire run;

[073-016] With favour hear, and help with present aid

[073-017] Her whom I bear, ’twas guilty I betray’d.

[073-018] Yet, if her father had been just or mild,

[073-019] He would have been less impious to his child;

[073-020] In her, have pitied force in the abuse;

[073-021] In me, admitted love for my excuse:

[073-022] O let relief for her hard case be found,

[073-023] Her, whom paternal rage expell’d from ground;

[073-024] Her, whom paternal rage relentless drown’d.

[073-025] Grant her some place, or change her to a place

[073-026] Which I may ever clasp with my embrace.’

[073-027] “His nodding head the sea’s great ruler bent,

[073-028] And all his waters shook with his assent:

[073-029] The nymph still swam, though with the fright distress’d;

[073-030] I felt her heart leap trembling in her breast;

[073-031] But, hard’ning soon, while I her pulse explore,

[073-032] A crusting cased her stiff body o’er;

[073-033] And, as accretions of new-cleaving soil

[073-034] Enlarged the mass, the nymph became an isle.”
