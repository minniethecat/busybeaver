[005-001] The Virtues, in despair, quit the earth; and the depravity of man becomes universal and complete.

[005-002] Hard steel succeeded then,

[005-003] And stubborn as the metal were the men.

[005-004] Truth, modesty, and shame, the world forsook;

[005-005] Fraud, avarice, and force, their places took.

[005-006] Then sails were spread to every wind that blew,

[005-007] Raw were the sailors and the depths were new;

[005-008] Trees, rudely hollow’d did the waves sustain,

[005-009] Ere ships in triumph plough’d the watery plain.

[005-010] Then landmarks limited to each his right;

[005-011] For all before was common as the light.

[005-012] Nor was the ground alone required to bear

[005-013] Her annual income to the crooked share,

[005-014] But greedy mortals, rummaging her store,

[005-015] Digg’d from her entrails first the precious ore

[005-016] (Which next to hell the prudent gods had laid),

[005-017] And that alluring ill to sight display’d.

[005-018] Thus cursed steel, and more accursed gold,

[005-019] Gave mischief birth, and made that mischief bold;

[005-020] And double death did wretched man invade,

[005-021] By steel assaulted, and by gold betray’d.

[005-022] Now (brandish’d weapons glittering in their hands)

[005-023] Mankind is broken loose from moral bands;

[005-024] No rights of hospitality remain;

[005-025] The guest, by him who harbour’d him, is slain;

[005-026] The son-in-law pursues the father’s life;

[005-027] The wife her husband murders, he the wife;

[005-028] The stepdame poison for the son prepares;

[005-029] The son inquires into his father’s years;

[005-030] Faith flies, and piety in exile mourns;

[005-031] And justice, here oppress’d, to heaven returns.
