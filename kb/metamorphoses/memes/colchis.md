# Colchis

Colchis, at the far eastern margin of the known world, is Medea's homeland. It is the source of the magical herbs, potions, and knowledge that enable her transformations — the rejuvenation of Aeson, the destruction of Pelias, the revenge on Jason. Colchis thus represents the geographic origin of mortal transformative power.
