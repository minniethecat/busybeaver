[032-001] The impiety of Alcithoe and her sisters is punished by their transformation into the shape of bats by the power of [[bacchus|Bacchus]].

[032-002] But Mineus’ daughters still their task pursue,

[032-003] To wickedness most obstinately true;

[032-004] At Bacchus still they laugh, when all around,

[032-005] Unseen, the timbrels hoarse were heard to sound.

[032-006] Saffron and myrrh their fragrant odours shed,

[032-007] And now the present deity they dread.

[032-008] Strange to relate! here ivy first was seen,

[032-009] Along the distaff crept the wondrous green;

[032-010] Then sudden, springing vines began to bloom,

[032-011] And the soft tendrils curl’d around the loom;

[032-012] While purple clusters, dangling from on high,

[032-013] Tinged the wrought purple with a second die.

[032-014] Now from the skies was shot a doubtful light,

[032-015] The day declining to the bounds of night.

[032-016] The fabric’s firm foundations shake all o’er,

[032-017] False tigers rage, and figured lions roar,

[032-018] Torches, aloft, seem blazing in the air,

[032-019] And angry flashes of red lightnings glare.

[032-020] To dark recesses, the dire sight to shun,

[032-021] Swift the pale sisters in confusion run:

[032-022] Their arms were lost in pinions as they fled,

[032-023] And subtle films each slender limb o’erspread.

[032-024] Their alter’d forms their senses soon reveal’d;

[032-025] Their forms, how alter’d, darkness still conceal’d.

[032-026] Close to the roof each, wond’ring, upwards springs,

[032-027] Borne on unknown, transparent, plumeless wings.

[032-028] They strove for words; their little bodies found

[032-029] No words, but murmur’d in a fainting sound.

[032-030] In towns, not woods, the sooty bats delight,

[032-031] And never till the dusk begin their flight;

[032-032] Till Vesper rises with his evening flame,

[032-033] From whom the Romans have derived their name.
