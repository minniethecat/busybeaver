# Mount Parnassus

Mount Parnassus is the only land visible above the waters of the Flood in the *Metamorphoses*. Deucalion and Pyrrha's ark comes to rest on its summit, and from here the repopulation of the earth begins. The mountain is also the home of the Muses, visited by Minerva in Book V.
