# Arcas

Arcas was the son of [[jupiter|Jupiter]] and the nymph [[callisto|Calisto]]. After Juno transformed Calisto into a bear, Jupiter placed both mother and son in the heavens as the constellation Ursa Major (the Great Bear) (013-001).
