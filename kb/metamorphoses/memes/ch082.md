[072-001] The river Achelous, displeased at the neglect of the Naiads converts them into the islands called Echinades.

[072-002] Theseus, meanwhile, acquitting well his share

[072-003] In the bold chase, confed’rate like a war,

[072-004] To Athens’ lofty towers his march ordain’d,

[072-005] By [[minerva|Pallas]] loved, and where Erectheus reign’d;

[072-006] But Achelous stopp’d him on the way,

[072-007] By rains a deluge, and constrain’d his stay.

[072-008] “O famed for glorious deeds, and great by blood,

[072-009] Rest here,” says he, “nor trust the rapid flood;

[072-010] It solid oaks has from its margin tore,

[072-011] And rocky fragments down its current bore,

[072-012] The murmur hoarse, and terrible the roar.

[072-013] Oft have I seen herds, with their shelt’ring fold,

[072-014] Forced from the banks, and in the torrent roll’d;

[072-015] Nor strength the bulky steer from ruin freed,

[072-016] Nor matchless swiftness saved the racing steed;

[072-017] In cataracts, when the dissolving snow

[072-018] Falls from the hills and floods the plains below,

[072-019] Tossed by the eddies, with a giddy round,

[072-020] Strong youths are in the sucking whirlpools drown’d:

[072-021] ’Tis best with me in safety to abide,

[072-022] Till usual bounds restrain the ebbing tide,

[072-023] And the low waters in their channel glide.”

[072-024] Theseus, persuaded, in compliance bow’d:

[072-025] “So kind an offer, and advice so good,

[072-026] O Achelous! cannot be refused;

[072-027] I’ll use them both,” said he; and both he used

[072-028] The grot he enter’d; pumice built the hall,

[072-029] And tophi made the rustic of the wall;

[072-030] The floor, soft moss a humid carpet spread,

[072-031] And various shells the checker’d roof inlaid:

[072-032] ’Twas now the hour when the declining sun

[072-033] Two thirds had of his daily journey run;

[072-034] At the spread table Theseus took his place,

[072-035] Next his companions in the daring chase;

[072-036] Pirithous here, there elder Lelex lay,

[072-037] His locks betraying age with sprinkled gray:

[072-038] Acharnia’s river-god disposed the rest,

[072-039] Graced with the equal honour of the feast,

[072-040] Elate with joy, and proud of such a guest.

[072-041] The nymphs were waiters, and, with naked feet,

[072-042] In order served the courses of the meat.

[072-043] The banquet done, delicious wine they brought,

[072-044] Of one transparent gem the cup was wrought.

[072-045] Then the great hero of this gallant train,

[072-046] Surveying far the prospect of the main,

[072-047] “What is that land,” says he, “the waves embrace?”

[072-048] (And with his finger pointed at the place:)

[072-049] “Is it one parted isle, which stands alone?

[072-050] How named? and yet, methinks, it seems not one.”

[072-051] To whom the watery god made this reply:

[072-052] “ ’Tis not one isle, but five; distinct they lie:

[072-053] ’Tis distance which deceives the cheated eye:

[072-054] But, that Diana’s act may seem less strange,

[072-055] These once proud Naiads were, before their change.

[072-056] ’Twas on a day more solemn than the rest,

[072-057] Ten bullocks slain, a sacrificial feast:

[072-058] The rural gods of all the regions near

[072-059] They bid to dance and taste the hallow’d cheer:

[072-060] Me they forgot; affronted with the slight,

[072-061] My rage and stream swell’d to the greatest height;

[072-062] And with the torrent of my flooding store,

[072-063] Large woods from woods, and fields from fields, I tore:

[072-064] The guilty nymphs, O, then rememb’ring me,

[072-065] I, with their country, wash’d into the sea;

[072-066] And joining waters with the social main,

[072-067] Rent the gross land, and split the firm champaign:

[072-068] Since, the Echinades, remote from shore,

[072-069] Are view’d as many isles as nymphs before.”
