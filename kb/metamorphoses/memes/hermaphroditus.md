# Hermaphroditus

Hermaphroditus was a youth of extraordinary beauty, the son of [[mercury|Mercury]] (Hermes) and [[venus|Venus]] (Aphrodite). The nymph Salmacis fell in love with him and, when he bathed in her spring, she embraced him and prayed to the gods that they never be parted. The gods granted her wish by fusing their bodies into a single being of both sexes (031-001).
