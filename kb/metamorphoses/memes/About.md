# About This Wiki

## Source Material

The *Metamorphoses* (Latin: *Metamorphōsēs*) is a Latin narrative poem by the Roman poet Ovid,

completed in 8 CE. It comprises 15 books and over 250 myths chronicling the history of the world

from creation to the deification of Julius Caesar, unified by the theme of physical transformation.

This wiki is based on the English translation of the poem, structured as a knowledge graph of

characters, places, events, and concepts from the mythological narrative.

## Usage Notes

This wiki is designed for readers, students, and researchers of classical mythology.

Each entry links to related characters, events, and source passages through paragraph number

(PN) references to the original poem.
