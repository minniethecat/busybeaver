## Colophon

[025-001]![The Standard Ebooks logo.](../../images/002.png)

[025-002]*Lady Rose’s Daughter*

[025-003]was published in 1903 by

[025-004][Mary Augusta Ward](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Mary_Augusta_Ward).

[025-005]This ebook was produced for

[025-006][Standard Ebooks](https://standardebooks.org/)

[025-007]by

[025-008]**An Anonymous Volunteer**,

[025-009]and is based on a transcription produced in 2004 by

[025-010]**Juliet Sutherland**, **Charlie Kirschner**, and [Distributed Proofreaders](https://www.pgdp.net/)

[025-011]for

[025-012][Project Gutenberg](https://www.gutenberg.org/ebooks/13782)

[025-013]and on digital scans from the

[025-014][Internet Archive](https://archive.org/details/ladyrosesdaughter00ward).

[025-015]The cover page is adapted from

[025-016]*Slender Woman with Vase*,

[025-017]a painting completed in 1894 by

[025-018][József Rippl-Rónai](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/József_Rippl-Rónai).

[025-019]The cover and title pages feature the

[025-020]**League Spartan** and **Sorts Mill Goudy**

[025-021]typefaces created in 2014 and 2009 by

[025-022][The League of Moveable Type](https://www.theleagueofmoveabletype.com/).

[025-023]This edition was released on

[025-024]**July 6, 2026, 9:33 p.m.**

[025-026]**revision 2734dbf**.

[025-028]April 17, 2026, 9:35 p.m.
