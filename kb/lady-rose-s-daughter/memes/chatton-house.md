# Chatton House

## Location

Chatton House is a London residence that serves as a venue for fashionable parties. Its exact location is not specified, but it is clearly within the social geography of the novel's West End society world, accessible to characters like the [[duchess-of-crowborough|Duchess of Crowborough]] and [[julie-le-breton|Julie Le Breton]].

## Significance

Chatton House appears in the novel as the setting for a "smart party" [016-092] that brings together several key characters. The party is notable for the social dynamics it reveals — [[duchess-of-crowborough|the Duchess]]'s maneuvering on [[julie-le-breton|Julie]]'s behalf, and [[jacob-delafield|Jacob Delafield]]'s observation of the social scene. As a gathering place, Chatton House is one of several London venues (alongside [[lady-hubert|Lady Hubert]]'s parties and [[lady-henry|Lady Henry]]'s receptions) that constitute the novel's social infrastructure.

## Description

The party at Chatton House is described as "smart" [016-092], indicating a gathering of fashionable society. The [[duchess-of-crowborough|Duchess of Crowborough]] takes an active role in managing [[julie-le-breton|Julie]]'s presentation there, telling another character: "The other night at Chatton House I was furious. I made [[julie-le-breton|Julie]] go" [016-018]. The Duchess's proprietary attitude toward [[julie-le-breton|Julie]]'s social appearances is on display, with Chatton House serving as the stage for her interventions. [[jacob-delafield|Jacob Delafield]] later references having seen [[duchess-of-crowborough|the Duchess]] there: "I saw you at Chatton House the other night" [017-117].

## Associated Characters

- **[[julie-le-breton|Julie Le Breton]]** — Attends the Chatton House party at [[duchess-of-crowborough|the Duchess]]'s insistence.
- **[[duchess-of-crowborough|Duchess of Crowborough]]** — Orchestrates [[julie-le-breton|Julie]]'s appearance at the party and is observed there by [[jacob-delafield|Jacob Delafield]].
- **[[jacob-delafield|Jacob Delafield]]** — Attends the Chatton House party and observes the social dynamics.
