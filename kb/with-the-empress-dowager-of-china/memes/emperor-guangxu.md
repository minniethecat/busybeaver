# Emperor Guangxu

**Redirect page.** The Emperor Guangxu (光绪帝) is the same person as the [[emperor-kwang-hsu|Emperor Kwang-Hsu]], using the modern Hanyu Pinyin romanization rather than the Wade-Giles system used in Katherine Carl's memoir. Carl describes him as "the twelfth Emperor, who has reigned over China, of the Dynasty of the 'Great Purity,' as the Manchu Dynasty is called" (008-003).
