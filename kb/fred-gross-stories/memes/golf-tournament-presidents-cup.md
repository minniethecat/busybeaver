# Golf Tournament and President's Cup

The **Golf Tournament and President's Cup** is a competitive event at the [[allison-country-club]] in which [[fred-gross]] participates after taking up golf. The tournament features a handicap system and the president's cup as its prize.

## Fred's Entry

Fred enters the tournament with confidence after his golf lessons:

> "There haveing some tournaments up to the club and I am going in to the 1 th…" (P03-042)

He acknowledges the handicap system but remains competitive: "believe me Charley I will…" (P03-043).

## The Competition

The tournament spans at least a week:

> "The tournament started a wk. ago today and I…" (P03-049)

Fred's goal is "winning that cup for the golf tournament" (P03-048), though he admits at the time of writing that he has not yet won it.
