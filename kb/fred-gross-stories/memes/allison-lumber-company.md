# Allison Lumber Company

The **Allison Lumber Company** (referred to as "the lumber Co.") is a building-materials supplier in [[allison]] that plays a role in [[fred-gross]]'s bungalow construction project. The company enforces strict credit policies that complicate the building process.

## Role in Fred's Bungalow

During the construction of Fred's house on lot number 29, the lumber company refuses to extend credit to the contractor, halting progress:

> "…the contrackter couldent do no more till he got some more lumber & the lumber Co. wouldent let…" (001-064)

The company's position is firm: "it dident make no diffrence to the lumber Co. because the contrackter a…" (001-065). The contractor is told he will receive "no credit & if he used more lumber then the contrack called for…" (001-066).

This credit constraint is one of several financial and logistical hurdles Fred faces during the house-building process, alongside bank loans, contractor negotiations, and the challenges described in [[own-your-own-home]].
