# The Piano Purchase

**The Piano Purchase** is an event in [[the-swift-six]] where [[fred-gross]] buys a piano, motivated by his desire to accompany himself singing.

## The Purchase

Fred encounters the piano while on Wabash Avenue in [[chicago]] with [[mrs-dutton]]:

> "…been down on Wabash ave. with Mrs. Dutton and walked passed a piano store and seen a piano in the windo…" (006-015)

Mrs. Dutton tries the instrument: "Mrs. Dutton set down and tride it" (006-016), and Fred decides to buy it.

## Motivation

Fred wants the piano primarily to accompany his singing. He notes elsewhere that he can "sing a couple songs and company myself with cords" (006-121), suggesting the piano is part of his musical aspirations.
