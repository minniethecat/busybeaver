# Standard Ebooks

Standard Ebooks is the volunteer-driven digital publishing project responsible for the edition of Ring Lardner's *Fred Gross Stories* that serves as the corpus for this wiki. Their edition provides the clean, well-formatted text from which all PN (paragraph-number) references are derived.

## The Project

Standard Ebooks produces high-quality, free, and open digital editions of public domain literature. Unlike automated conversions, each Standard Ebook is manually formatted, proofread, and designed to professional standards. Their editions are widely regarded as among the best free ebooks available.

## The Fred Gross Edition

The Standard Ebooks edition of *Fred Gross Stories*, produced by Jonathan Erdman with transcription by Scott Topping, draws on sources from Lardnermania and digital scans from the Internet Archive. Released under the project's standard open license, it makes Lardner's stories freely available in a format suitable for both reading and computational analysis.

## Relation to This Wiki

This wiki's sentence index, PN references, and chapter structure are all derived from the Standard Ebooks edition. Every `[PN-XXX-NNN]` reference in these pages points to a specific paragraph in that edition. Standard Ebooks' meticulous formatting makes this kind of structured analysis possible.
