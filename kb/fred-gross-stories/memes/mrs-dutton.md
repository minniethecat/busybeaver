# Mrs. Dutton

**Mrs. Dutton** is a minor acquaintance who appears in [[the-swift-six]], accompanying a shopping expedition to Wabash Avenue in [[chicago]].

## Appearance

Mrs. Dutton appears during a shopping trip on Wabash Avenue (006-015). She tries out a piano in a store window, which becomes a small anecdote:

> "…Mrs. Dutton set down and tride it. So I s…" (006-016)
