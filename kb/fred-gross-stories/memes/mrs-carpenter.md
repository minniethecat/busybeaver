# Mrs. Carpenter

**Mrs. Carpenter** is a social figure in [[allison-illinois|Allison]] who moves in the same circles as [[mrs-hamilton|Mrs. Hamilton]]. She appears in [[story-02-welcome-to-our-city|Welcome to Our City]] and [[story-03-the-last-laugh|The Last Laugh]].

## Social Role

Mrs. Carpenter is among the established village women whose social rituals shape Grace Gross's experience of Allison. She and her husband visit the Grosses early in their acquaintance:

> "…Mr. & Mrs. Carpenter come in & she set there talking…" (002-028)

During this visit, Mrs. Carpenter engages Grace in conversation about shopping and village life: "Mrs. Carpenter ast Grace dident she have some…" (002-029).

## Association with Mrs. Hamilton

Mrs. Carpenter appears in connection with the Hamilton social circle. Grace reports "passing by Mrs. Carpenter & Mrs. Hamilton" (003-019), suggesting the two women are frequently seen together in village social spaces. Mrs. Carpenter is also noted as a member of the country club (003-048).
