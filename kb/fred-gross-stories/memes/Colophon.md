[007-002]*[[Fred-Gross|Fred Gross]] Stories*  

[007-003]was compiled from short stories published between 1915 and 1917 by  

[007-004][[Ring-Lardner|Ring Lardner]]](<https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Ring_Lardner>).

[007-005]This ebook was produced for  

[007-006][Standard Ebooks](<https://standardebooks.org/>)  

[007-007]by  

[007-008]**Jonathan Erdman** ,  

[007-009]and is based on a transcription produced by  

[007-010]**Scott Topping**  

[007-011]for  

[007-012][Lardnermania](<https://web.archive.org/web/20041019234945/http://www.tridget.com/lardnermania/oyoh/index.htm>)  

[007-013]and on digital scans from  

[007-014][The Internet Archive](<https://standardebooks.org/ebooks/ring-lardner/fred-gross-stories#page-scans>).

[007-015]The cover page is adapted from  

[007-016]*The Birthplace of Herbert Hoover, West Branch, Iowa* ,  

[007-017]a painting completed in 1931 by  

[007-018][Grant Wood](<https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Grant_Wood>).  

[007-019]The cover and title pages feature the  

[007-020]**League Spartan** and **Sorts Mill Goudy**  

[007-021]typefaces created in 2014 and 2009 by  

[007-022][The League of Moveable Type](<https://www.theleagueofmoveabletype.com/>).
