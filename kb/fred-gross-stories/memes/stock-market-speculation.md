# Fred's Stock Market Speculation

**Fred's Stock Market Speculation** is a major plotline in [[war-bribes]] where [[fred-gross]] becomes involved in wartime stock trading, buying shares on margin based on insider tips about war production orders.

## Getting Started

Fred receives stock tips through his connection to "head quarters" of war production:

> "…I get tips on the stock markit strate from head quarters and…" (005-027)

He enters the market aggressively, buying multiple stocks, including one that "started a way down and n…" (005-033). He declares himself "a stock exchange man now" (005-040).

## The Mechanics

Fred trades on margin. His broker allows him to buy shares where "whenever the stock moved up a pt. I would make…" (005-043). He monitors stock prices in the morning paper (005-045) and follows the principle that "every body will want some of the stock and thats what makes it go up…" (005-046).

## The Crash

The market turns against him. Despite confident tips about war orders:

> "…this stock markit games got working beat to det…" (005-054)

The market collapses: "the stock markit was all shot to peaces" (005-062). Fred loses money and eventually concludes: "Im threw with this stock markit game and they can take there…" (005-098).

He ends with characteristic bitterness: "an that gives me a tip on the stock markit I will tip them hay" (005-102).
