Mr. Carry is the grocer of Allison, Illinois, appearing in Story 02 (Welcome to Our City). He runs the local grocery store where Fred Gross shops after moving to Allison, and his family features in the social fabric of the village.

## Role in Welcome to Our City

Fred and Grace patronize the Carry grocery store after their arrival in Allison. Mr. Carry extends credit to the Gross family — a standard small-town merchant practice that Fred, newly arrived from Chicago, regards with a mixture of gratitude and suspicion. The Carrys represent the established village merchant class into which Fred awkwardly attempts to integrate.

Grace Gross learns the identity of the local grocer:

> Grace forgot to find out from them what was the name of the peopl that lived in the 3rd house but it dident make no diffruns because the woman that come to the door at the next house told her the name and the name is Carry and Mr. Carrys the man that keeps the grosery store or 1 of them.
>
> (002-029)

## Distinction from Carpenter

The SCN29 scan (Phase 7, 2026-07-14) identified that an existing page (`carry-carpenter`) conflates the Carry and Carpenter families, treating them as a single entity. The source text distinguishes them: the Carrys run the grocery store, while the Carpenters are neighbors of the Hamiltons. The alias list incorrectly includes "Carpenters" under the Carry page. This separation requires a mislink fix.
