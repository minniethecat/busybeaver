# Rapworth Park

## Location & Setting

Rapworth Park is the country estate of Sir John Ruddimore, a local magnate and hunt figure who was "always fond of a joke" (028-016). The estate is situated within the Ringwell hunting country and features grounds suitable for hosting a lawn-meet, a formal gathering of the hunt on the lawn of a country house.

## Significance in the Memoir

Rapworth Park appears in Part 7 ("Denis Milden as Master") during a period of uncertainty for the Ringwell Hunt. A lawn-meet held at the park took on a somber atmosphere, "rendered positively funereal" by the news of the Master's impending resignation (030-017). The estate thus serves as the backdrop for a moment of institutional transition — the hunt gathering at a grand private estate while its leadership faces upheaval.
