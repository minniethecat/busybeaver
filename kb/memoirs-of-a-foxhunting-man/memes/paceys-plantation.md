# Pacey's Plantation

## Location & Setting

Pacey's Plantation was a covert in the Ringwell hunt country, accessed from the Downfield direction. It was drawn during a Tuesday meet when the hounds "trotted away to draw Pacey's Plantation" (028-004).

## Significance in the Memoir

The covert features in a single hunt episode during [[denis-milden]]'s mastership. A fox was found in the plantation and went away, providing a short run before going to ground in a drain. The local press hinted that "the hunt did not quite play the game by the Ringwell fox" (028-007), adding a note of gentle controversy to the proceedings.

## Associated Characters

- [[denis-milden]] — Master of the Ringwell Hounds, led the draw
- [[stephen-colwood]] — present at the meet

## Key Scenes

> "A fox was found in Pacey's Plantation (it was hinted that the hunt did not quite play the game by the Ringwell fox)." (028-007)

After the fox went to ground, "old Mr. Dearborn" took charge of the situation (028-004). The episode was brief but characteristic of the minor dramas of a day's hunting in the Ringwell country.
