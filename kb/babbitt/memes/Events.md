## About This Index

Events in this wiki capture the key narrative moments of the novel — from Babbitt's restless morning routine through his rebellion and eventual return to conformity. Each event page includes a summary, background context, key participants, and narrative consequences. The `event_type` field classifies events by their nature: social gatherings, public ceremonies, personal crises, and political incidents.

Quality levels reflect documentation depth: **featured** pages include full narrative analysis, **standard** pages provide comprehensive coverage with PN-anchored citations, and **basic** pages offer essential identification.
