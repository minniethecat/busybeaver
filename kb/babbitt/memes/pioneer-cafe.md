# Pioneer Cafe

A downtown Zenith cafe where businessmen gather for informal lunches. It represents the casual, masculine spaces of the business world — places where deals are discussed over sandwiches and the rituals of commercial friendship are performed.

The cafe appeared in the conversation of the Boosters' Club members as they planned their annual convention program. During the convention itself, a Sparta broker called out from the cafe crowd as the Zenith boosters performed their impromptu entertainment (013-026). Babbitt later took off his coat, hurled his hat on the ground, and climbed on the sundial to bellow a declaration of Zenith's superiority to the assembled delegates (013-039).
