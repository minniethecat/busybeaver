## About This Index

Commerce in *Babbitt* is not just a profession — it is an identity, a theology, and a language. Lewis uses the rhetoric of salesmanship to expose how thoroughly business values permeate every sphere of Zenith life: religion becomes "pep," civic reform becomes a "campaign," and friendship becomes a "proposition." The pages indexed here trace these connections from the property deeds of Floral Heights to the banquet speeches of the Boosters' Club.
