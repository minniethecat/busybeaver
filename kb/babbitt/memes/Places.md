## About This Index

Places in *Babbitt* are never merely backdrop — they are material expressions of the novel's themes. The standardized houses of Floral Heights embody conformity, the Athletic Club defines social hierarchy, and the contrasts between Zenith's commercial core and its serene outskirts trace the boundaries of Babbitt's world. Each place page includes description, narrative role, associated characters, and key scenes set there.

Quality levels reflect documentation depth: **featured** pages offer full thematic and narrative analysis, **standard** pages provide comprehensive coverage, and **basic** pages provide essential identification.
