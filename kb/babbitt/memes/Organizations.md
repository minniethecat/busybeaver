## About This Index

Organizations in *Babbitt* are never neutral — each one embodies a facet of the novel's critique of American civic and commercial life. The Boosters' Club promotes aggressive boosterism, the Sunday School functions as a social proving ground, and real-estate firms measure all value in square footage. This index collects them all, from the prominent institutions that shape the plot to the minor businesses and clubs that fill Zenith's commercial landscape.

Quality levels reflect documentation depth: **featured** pages include comprehensive analysis of the organization's satirical function, **standard** pages provide full coverage of key members and scenes, and **basic** pages offer essential identification awaiting further enrichment.
