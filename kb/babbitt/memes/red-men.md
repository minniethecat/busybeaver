# Improved Order of Red Men

A fraternal organization with a Zenith chapter, listed among the clubs to which the ideal American businessman must belong. The Red Men represent the ritualistic, pseudo-ethnic dimension of American fraternalism — the performance of identity through invented tradition.

The Red Men were enumerated alongside the Maccabees, the Elks, the Moose, and the Knights of Columbus as Zenith's fraternal organizations during the Boosters' Club convention (014-034). They also appeared among the civic bodies represented in the mayor's "Good Citizens' League," which sought to unify the city's respectable organizations behind a program of social control (016-004). The lodge was mentioned among the affiliations expected of a solid Zenith citizen (021-023).
