# Atlantic City
The New Jersey resort that Zilla Riesling dreams of visiting — "a nice expensive vacation in New York and Atlantic City, with the bright lights and the bootlegged cocktails and a bunch of lounge-lizards to dance with" (005-036). It represents the aspirational leisure that Zenith's middle class can glimpse but never quite grasp.
