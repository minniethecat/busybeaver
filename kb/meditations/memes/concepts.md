## Concepts

The *Meditations* engages with a wide range of Stoic philosophical concepts — from virtue and reason to the nature of the universe and the discipline of desire. This page provides a dynamic index of all concept pages in the wiki, automatically sorted by label.

::: query
type: concept
display: table
fields: [label, quality, total_refs, total_chapters]
field_labels:
  label: Concept
  quality: Quality
  total_refs: References
  total_chapters: Chapters
sort: label
order: asc
:::

## About This Index

This index is dynamically generated from the wiki's frontmatter metadata. The **Quality** column reflects the current assessment of each page (featured → standard → basic). **References** (`total_refs`) counts the number of other wiki pages that link to the concept. **Chapters** (`total_chapters`) reflects the number of distinct chapters of the *Meditations* in which the concept is referenced.

Pages are listed in alphabetical order by label. To browse concepts by topic cluster, see the individual concept pages and their **Related** sections.
