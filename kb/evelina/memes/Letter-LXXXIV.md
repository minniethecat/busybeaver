[084-001] Evelina to the Rev. Mr. Villars

[084-002] All is over, my dearest Sir; and the fate of your Evelina is decided! This morning, with fearful joy and trembling gratitude, she united herself forever with the object of her dearest, her eternal affection.

[084-003] I have time for no more; the chaise now waits which is to conduct me to dear Berry Hill, and to the arms of the best of men.

[084-004] Evelina.
