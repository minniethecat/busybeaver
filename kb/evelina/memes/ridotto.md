# The Ridotto

The ridotto (a private [[assembly-rooms|assembly]] or masked ball) is a critical setting in *[[evelina|Evelina]]*, representing both the allure and the danger of fashionable [[london|London]] society. At such events, the boundaries of propriety are tested under the guise of entertainment.

## Narrative Context

[[evelina|Evelina]] attends the ridotto early in her [[london|London]] sojourn, still naive to the ways of [[fashion|fashionable society]]. The event becomes a trial by fire — her beauty attracts attention, but her inexperience leaves her vulnerable to impertinence.

## Social Significance

The ridotto strips away the protective formalities of ordinary social interaction. Behind masks and in the press of the crowd, the usual rules of conduct are suspended — revealing the true [[reputation|character]]s of those present.
