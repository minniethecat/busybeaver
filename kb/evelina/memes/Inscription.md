[P03-001] To Dr. Burney

[P03-002] Oh, Author of my being!—far more dear

[P03-003] To me than light, than nourishment, or rest,

[P03-004] Hygeia’s blessings, Rapture’s burning tear,

[P03-005] Or the lifeblood that mantles in my breast!

[P03-006] If in my heart the love of Virtue glows,

[P03-007] ’Twas planted there by an unerring rule;

[P03-008] From thy example the pure flame arose,

[P03-009] Thy life, my precept—thy good works, my school.

[P03-010] Could my weak pow’rs thy num’rous virtues trace,

[P03-011] By filial love each fear should be repress’d,

[P03-012] The blush of Incapacity I’d chase,

[P03-013] And stand, Recorder of thy worth, confess’d:

[P03-014] But since my niggard stars that gift refuse,

[P03-015] Concealment is the only boon I claim;

[P03-016] Obscure be still the unsuccessful Muse,

[P03-017] Who cannot raise, but would not sink, thy fame.

[P03-018] Oh! of my life at once the source and joy!

[P03-019] If e’er thy eyes these feeble lines survey,

[P03-020] Let not their folly their intent destroy;

[P03-021] Accept the tribute—but forget the lay.
