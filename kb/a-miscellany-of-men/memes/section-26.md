# *** End Of The Project Gutenberg Ebook A Miscellany Of Men ***

[025-001](5) Don't (if any one calls your doctrine mad, which is likely enough),

[025-002]don't answer that madmen are only the minority and the sane only the

[025-003]majority. The sane are sane because they are the corporate substance of

[025-004]mankind; the insane are not a minority because they are not a mob. The

[025-005]man who thinks himself a man thinks the next man a man; he reckons his

[025-006]neighbour as himself. But the man who thinks he is a chicken does not

[025-007]try to look through the man who thinks he is glass. The man who thinks

[025-008]himself Jesus Christ does not quarrel with the man who thinks himself

[025-009]Rockefeller; as would certainly happen if the two had ever met. But

[025-010]madmen never meet. It is the only thing they cannot do. They can talk,

[025-011]they can inspire, they can fight, they can found religions; but they

[025-012]cannot meet. Maniacs can never be the majority; for the simple reason

[025-013]that they can never be even a minority. If two madmen had ever agreed

[025-014]they might have conquered the world.

[025-015](6) Don't say that the idea of human equality is absurd, because some

[025-016]men are tall and some short, some clever and some stupid. At the height

[025-017]of the French Revolution it was noticed that Danton was tall and Murat

[025-018]short. In the wildest popular excitement of America it is known that

[025-019]Rockefeller is stupid and that Bryan is clever. The doctrine of human

[025-020]equality reposes upon this: That there is no man really clever who has

[025-021]not found that he is stupid. That there is no big man who has not felt

[025-022]small. Some men never feel small; but these are the few men who are.

[025-023](7) Don't say (O don't say) that Primitive Man knocked down a woman

[025-024]with a club and carried her away. Why on earth should he? Does the male

[025-025]sparrow knock down the female sparrow with a twig? Does the male giraffe

[025-026]knock down the female giraffe with a palm tree? Why should the male

[025-027]have had to use any violence at any time in order to make the female a

[025-028]female? Why should the woman roll herself in the mire lower than the

[025-029]sow or the she-bear; and profess to have been a slave where all these

[025-030]creatures were creators; where all these beasts were gods? Do not

[025-031]talk such bosh. I implore you, I supplicate you not to talk such bosh.

[025-032]Utterly and absolutely abolish all such bosh--and we may yet

[025-033]begin to discuss these public questions properly. But I fear my list of

[025-034]protests grows too long; and I know it could grow longer for ever. The

[025-035]reader must forgive my elongations and elaborations. I fancied for the

[025-036]moment that I was writing a book.
