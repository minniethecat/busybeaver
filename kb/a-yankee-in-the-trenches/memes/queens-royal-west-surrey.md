# Queen's Royal West Surrey Regiment

## Overview

The Queen's Royal West Surrey Regiment was one of the oldest line infantry regiments in the [[british-army|British Army]], with origins dating back to 1661. During World War I, the regiment raised numerous [[battalion|battalions]] that served on the [[france|Western Front]], in Italy, and in other theaters. [[r-derby-holmes|R. Derby Holmes]] served in this regiment as an American volunteer.

## In the Memoir

Holmes does not dwell on regimental history — he was a soldier, not a historian — but his account is saturated with the regiment's presence. The men he fought alongside, the officers he served under, the traditions he observed: all were products of the Queen's Royal West Surrey's institutional culture. The regiment was the framework within which Holmes's entire war experience unfolded.
