# Knuckle Knife

The **knuckle knife** — also called a trench knife or knuckle duster — was a vicious close-[[billets|quarters]] weapon combining a short stabbing blade with a weighted brass-knuckle grip. Carried by British [[raiding-party|raiding parties]] alongside persuaders and [[pistol|revolver]]s, it was designed for the brutal, silent [[battle|combat]] of [[raid|trench raid]]s.

## In the Narrative

Holmes describes the knuckle knife alongside the persuader as standard [[raid|raiding]] [[equipment|kit]]:

> "served with persuader sticks or knuckle knives, and…" [003-031]

He provides a precise description:

> "The knuckle knife is a short dagger with a heavy…" [003-035]

The knife was issued for extreme emergencies:

> "trench knife, which he may use if he is near…" [007-043]

The glossary provides the technical entry:

> "Knuckle knife — A short dagger with a studded…" [016-224]

## Design and Purpose

The knuckle knife was designed for the specific conditions of [[raid|trench raid]]s:

- **Short blade** (6–8 inches): Long enough to reach vital organs, short enough to wield in confined trench spaces
- **Knuckle-duster grip**: The brass-knuckle handle served double duty — it protected the wielder's hand and could be used for blunt-force strikes
- **Silent operation**: Unlike a [[revolver|pistol]] shot, a knife thrust made no noise to raise the alarm
- **Psychological impact**: The weapon's brutal appearance was itself a form of intimidation

These knives were typically issued to assault troops going on [[raid|trench raid]]s, where stealth was paramount and the [[battle|fight]]ing would be at arm's length in narrow, dark [[trench|trenches]]. Along with the persuader and the [[pistol|revolver]], the knuckle knife completed the "holy trinity" of [[raid|trench raid]]ing weapons.
