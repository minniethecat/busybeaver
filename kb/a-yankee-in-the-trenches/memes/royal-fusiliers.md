# Royal Fusiliers

## Overview

The Royal Fusiliers (City of [[london|London]] Regiment) was a line infantry regiment of the [[british-army|British Army]]. During World War I, the regiment expanded to over 50 [[battalion|battalions]] and served in every major theater. Holmes encountered the Royal Fusiliers in the course of his service, and they appear in his memoir as one of the neighboring units in the trench system.

## In the Memoir

Holmes mentions the Royal Fusiliers in passing, as soldiers at the front would — regiments were the functional units of military identity, and the presence of another regiment in an adjacent sector was a matter of practical note. The Fusiliers' appearance in the memoir reflects the interlocking patchwork of British regiments that held the [[france|Western Front]].
