## Key Themes

The novel's thematic core revolves around the tension between genuine art and social performance — "cakes and ale" itself being a Shakespearean reference to simple earthly pleasures, set against the pretensions of the literary establishment. Each concept page explores one strand of this rich thematic fabric with supporting passages from the text.
