# High Cross Police

The local constabulary tasked with investigating [[murder|Kitely's murder]] — a force that proves competent but unimaginative, settling too quickly on the obvious [[suspicion|suspect]].

## Significance

The police represent official investigation in the novel, and their limitations create the space for [[Brereton]]'s amateur detection. They pursue [[harborough|Harborough]] as the [[suspicion|suspect]] based on circumstantial evidence (010-015) and are slow to consider the possibility that [[High-Cross|the borough]]'s most respected citizens might be involved. Their conventional approach — competent within its limits but unable to see beyond them — contrasts with [[Brereton]]'s willingness to follow evidence wherever it leads.
