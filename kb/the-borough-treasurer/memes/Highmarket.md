# Highmarket

The nearby market town that serves as the legal center for the district — where magistrates' hearings are held and suspects are brought for examination.

## Significance

Highmarket is the setting for the novel's legal proceedings. Harborough is "brought up before the Highmarket magistrates" (013-002) for examination, and the town's court becomes the arena where evidence is publicly tested.
