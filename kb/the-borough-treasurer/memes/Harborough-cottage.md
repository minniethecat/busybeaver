# Harborough's Cottage

The isolated dwelling on the outskirts of High Cross that becomes a focus of the murder investigation — its occupant the first suspect pursued by the police (010-015).

## Significance

The cottage's isolation makes it both a refuge and a trap. Its location — near the pine wood where Kitely's body was found — fuels police suspicions of Harborough, who lives there alone.
