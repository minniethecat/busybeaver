# Norcaster

A town referenced in connection with the investigation — a potential destination for suspects seeking to escape the scrutiny of High Cross (024-068).

## Significance

Norcaster represents the world beyond High Cross — the possibility of flight and the limits of local authority. References to Norcaster in the novel carry the implication of escape, of suspects "conveyed to Norcaster without suspicion" (024-068).
