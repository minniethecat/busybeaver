# Wilchester

The town whose name haunts the narrative — the scene of Mallalieu and Cotherstone's original crime, the building society fraud that gives Kitely his leverage years later (030-063).

## Significance

Wilchester is never visited in the novel's present action, yet it shapes every event. It was here that the conspirators served as treasurer and secretary of the building society (001-050), and here that they "had full control" of its funds (015-019). The Wilchester affair is the original sin from which all subsequent crimes flow.
