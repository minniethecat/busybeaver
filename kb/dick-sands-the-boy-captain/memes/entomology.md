# Entomology

The scientific study of insects that serves as the defining characteristic and comic obsession of [[benedict|Cousin Benedict]] in *[[dick-sands|Dick Sands]]: The Boy [[captain-hull|Captain]]*.

## Definition

Entomology is the branch of zoo[[ships-log|log]]y concerned with the study of insects. In Verne's novel, it is represented through the character of [[benedict|Cousin Benedict]], a devoted amateur entomo[[ships-log|log]]ist whose passion for beetles and other insects borders on monomania, often placing him in absurd and dangerous situations throughout the narrative.

## Role in the Novel

[[benedict|Cousin Benedict]]'s entomo[[ships-log|log]]ical pursuits provide much of the novel's comic relief while also serving plot functions. His single-minded focus on insects makes him oblivious to danger, and his habit of wandering off in pursuit of specimens repeatedly complicates the party's journey. Yet his expertise occasionally proves valuable—his knowledge of [[africa|Africa]]n insects helps confirm their location on the [[africa|Dark Continent]].

[[benedict|Benedict]] traveled with an "extensive collection of [[stag-beetle]]s, tiger-beetles and lady-birds" (007-019–020), and even converted the gentle giant [[hercules|Hercules]] into an assistant, teaching him to handle delicate specimens despite his "huge grasp" (007-020–022).

## Cousin Benedict's Character

[[benedict|Benedict]] represents the Verne archetype of the absent-minded scientist—brilliant in his narrow field but comically incompetent in practical matters. "The science of entomo[[ships-log|log]]y was thus occupying its two votaries"—[[benedict|Benedict]] and [[hercules|Hercules]]—while [[mrs-weldon|Mrs. Weldon]] attended to the education of young Jack (007-024–025). The contrast between [[benedict|Benedict]]'s obsessive pursuit of beetles and the life-threatening dangers surrounding the party creates dramatic irony throughout the novel.

[[captain-hull|Captain Hull]] observes dryly that he "would willingly give up all the [[right-whale|whale]]s in the ocean for one rare" insect specimen, characterizing [[benedict|Benedict]]'s inverted priorities (004-063).
