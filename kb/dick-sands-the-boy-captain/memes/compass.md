The magnetic navigational instrument aboard the [[the-pilgrim|Pilgrim]] that [[negoro|Negoro]] secretly sabotages by placing a piece of iron near the binnacle, causing the vessel to sail to [[africa|Africa]] instead of [[san-francisco|South America]]. The compass is one of the two instruments [[dick-sands|Dick Sands]] relies upon for [[navigation|navigation]], and its corruption is the central act of treachery in the novel's first half.

## Role in the Novel

The ship's compass is one of the two primary navigational instruments [[dick-sands|Dick Sands]] uses to steer the [[the-pilgrim|Pilgrim]] (the other being [[ships-log|the log]]). His understanding of navigation encompasses "the use of [[ships-log|the log]] and compass" (028-118).

[[negoro|Negoro]]'s sabotage of the compass is the central act of treachery in the maritime portion of the narrative. [[harris|Harris]] reveals that "one night the compass was tampered with, so that the *[[the-pilgrim|Pilgrim]]*, scudding along before a [[storm-at-sea|tempest]], was carried altogether out of her course" (028-120–121). By placing a piece of iron near the compass, [[negoro|Negoro]] caused it to give false readings, steering the ship toward the coast of [[angola|Angola]] instead of [[san-francisco|South America]].

Without the compass providing accurate heading, [[dick-sands|Dick Sands]] navigated in ignorance. The sabotage was undetectable to an inexperienced navigator, especially during a [[storm-at-sea|storm]]. The compass's [[treachery|betrayal]] symbolizes the larger theme of trust violated — an instrument designed to provide truth was weaponized to deceive.

## Significance

The compass represents the vulnerability of knowledge. [[dick-sands|Dick Sands]]' competence in using instruments cannot protect him when those instruments are deliberately corrupted. The sabotage also establishes [[negoro|Negoro]]'s cunning: rather than confronting the young captain directly, he subverts the very tools of command. The compass, meant to guide sailors safely home, becomes the instrument that delivers the castaways into the [[slavery|slave-trading]] heart of [[africa|Africa]].
