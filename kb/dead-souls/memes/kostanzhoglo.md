# Kostanzhoglo

## Character Profile

Constantine Thedorovitch Kostanzhoglo is the [[landownership|landowner]] [[chichikov|Chichikov]] meets
in Part II who represents Gogol's ideal of the **practical [[russian-empire|Russia]]n
agrarian**. Unlike every [[landownership|landowner]] in Part I — who are variously
sentimental, suspicious, blustering, materialistic, or miserly —
Kostanzhoglo is genuinely competent. His [[landownership|estate]] flourishes because he
works it himself, rising early, supervising every detail, and
dismissing imported economic theories with contempt.

He is first mentioned almost casually: "Kostanzhoglo" — a name
[[chichikov|Chichikov]] hears and immediately wants to visit [014-121]. When they
meet, Kostanzhoglo appears at closer quarters as a man of about forty,
with a quick, impatient manner and a face that suggests constant mental
activity [014-157]. He speaks in torrents — long, passionate
monologues about the folly of those who neglect their land, the
stupidity of factory-building as a path to wealth, and the simple
virtue of understanding what one's soil can produce.

Kostanzhoglo's key principle is that **wealth comes from the land**,
not from speculation, schemes, or [[dead-souls|dead soul]]s. "I myself was guided more
by experience than by any book," he tells [[chichikov|Chichikov]] [014-164]. His
contempt for the [[russian-empire|Russia]]n gentry's indolence is absolute — he spits at
the mention of those who mortgage their [[landownership|estate]]s to build factories
rather than learning to farm [014-215].

## Role in Dead Souls

Kostanzhoglo is Gogol's **positive counter-example** to the moral
grotesques of Part I. Where [[purchase-of-dead-souls|Chichikov's scheme]] is parasitic (extracting
value from the dead), Kostanzhoglo's life is productive (creating value
from the living land). He represents the path not taken — the
possibility of a [[russian-empire|Russia]]n gentry that works rather than merely owns.

He also serves a narrative function: redirecting [[chichikov|Chichikov]]'s journey.
When [[chichikov|Chichikov]] asks where he should go next, Kostanzhoglo directs him
to [[koshkarev|Colonel Koshkarev]], setting up the next stage of [[chichikov|Chichikov]]'s Part II
odyssey [014-172].
