# Iola Morton

Iola Morton is [[chet-morton|Chet Morton]]'s sister, a high-school girl in [[bayport|Bayport]] who is the only girl known to have won [[joe-hardy|Joe Hardy]]'s bashful admiration.

## Role in the Story

Iola appears only briefly in the novel, encountered by [[frank-hardy|Frank]] and [[joe-hardy|Joe Hardy]] during a morning errand in downtown [[bayport|Bayport]]. She is present when the brothers discuss their plans for the upcoming [[cabin-island|Cabin Island]] expedition (004-021). Her role is that of a supporting character — [[chet-morton|Chet]]'s sister and a member of the Morton family, which is close to the Hardys.

## Character Traits

- **Appearance**: Iola is described as "plump and dark" (004-021), contrasting with [[callie-shaw|Callie Shaw]], who is "brown-eyed" and "brown-haired."
- **Personality**: Cheerful and enthusiastic — she "chimed in" to conversations and expressed admiration for [[frank-hardy|Frank]]'s iceboat handling skills (004-023) (004-028).

## Key Appearances

- **Meeting the Hardy boys downtown**: While [[frank-hardy|Frank]] and [[joe-hardy|Joe]] are on an errand for their mother, they meet [[callie-shaw|Callie Shaw]] and Iola Morton in downtown [[bayport|Bayport]]. Iola is described and introduced as [[chet-morton|Chet]]'s sister (004-021).
- **Discussing the iceboat**: Iola joins the conversation about the expedition, noting that [[chet-morton|Chet]] has told her "all about it" (004-023).
- **Admiring [[frank-hardy|Frank]]'s skill**: Iola exclaims that [[chet-morton|Chet]] told her [[frank-hardy|Frank]]'s expert handling of the iceboat prevented "a terrible smash-up" (004-028).

## Relationships

- [[chet-morton]]: Iola is [[chet-morton|Chet]]'s sister. [[chet-morton|Chet]] has been keeping her informed about the [[cabin-island|Cabin Island]] plans.
- [[joe-hardy]]: [[joe-hardy|Joe]] is bashfully fond of Iola, admitting she is "all right — as a girl," which the narrator notes is "high praise" from [[joe-hardy|Joe]] (004-021).
- [[callie-shaw]]: Callie is Iola's friend and companion during the downtown encounter; both are girls from the boys' social circle at school.
- [[frank-hardy]]: Iola admires [[frank-hardy|Frank]]'s iceboat handling, relaying [[chet-morton|Chet]]'s praise of his skill (004-028).

## In the Hardy Boys Series

Iola Morton is a recurring character throughout the Hardy Boys series, appearing more prominently in other volumes. In this novel, her appearance is brief — a single scene in [[bayport|Bayport]] before the boys depart for [[cabin-island|Cabin Island]].
