# Iceboat Collision on Barmet Bay

A hostile encounter in which a large, unidentified iceboat deliberately rams the Hardy boys' craft while they are exploring near [[cabin-island|Cabin Island]], establishing the presence of dangerous adversaries.

## Summary

While iceboating near [[cabin-island|Cabin Island]], the Hardy boys find themselves confronted by "two large iceboats racing out of one of [[cabin-cove|the cove]]s" (001-020). The helmsman of the largest boat "became excited" and deliberately steered into the Hardys' path (001-023). [[frank-hardy|Frank]]'s "coolness and presence of mind" (003-001) prevents disaster, as he maneuvers the boat out of the way at the last moment. The bigger iceboat "did not change course" (002-046), confirming the hostility was intentional. The boys later review "the various iceboats he had seen on the bay" (002-035), trying to identify their attacker.

## Key Moments
- Two large iceboats appear racing from a cove (001-020)
