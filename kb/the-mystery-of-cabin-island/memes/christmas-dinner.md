# Christmas Dinner at the Hardys

The [[family|Hardy family]]'s [[christmas-holidays|Christmas]] dinner, held three days before the actual holiday, represents a pivotal moment of family unity that enables the boys' adventure to proceed.

## Summary

[[fenton-hardy|Fenton Hardy]] arranges for the [[christmas-holidays|Christmas]] dinner to be held early — "the [[christmas-holidays|Christmas]] dinner had been set ahead" (006-038) — so that [[frank-hardy|Frank]] and [[joe-hardy|Joe]] can celebrate with the family before departing for [[cabin-island|Cabin Island]]. The dinner surpasses all expectations: it "surpassed all previous [[christmas-holidays|Christmas]] dinners" (006-040) with a turkey that "tasted better than ever." [[fenton-hardy|Mr. Hardy]] went so far as to order a [[christmas-holidays|Christmas]] tree (006-041), and the celebration captures the warmth of the [[family|Hardy family]].

## Key Moments

- [[laura-hardy|Mrs. Hardy]]'s initial concern about the boys missing [[christmas-holidays|Christmas]] with the family (006-010)
- [[fenton-hardy|Mr. Hardy]]'s arranging the early celebration (006-038)
- The dinner itself — "surpassed all previous [[christmas-holidays|Christmas]] dinners" (006-040)
- The [[christmas-holidays|Christmas]] tree ordered by [[fenton-hardy|Mr. Hardy]] (006-041)
- [[joe-hardy|Joe]]'s exclamation: "We shan't miss our [[christmas-holidays|Christmas]] after all!" (006-039)
