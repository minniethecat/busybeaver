# Stolen Supplies

The theft of the Hardy boys' entire food supply on [[cabin-island|Cabin Island]] represents a direct attempt by their adversary to drive them away from the island and shut down their [[detective-work|investigation]].

## The Discovery

After a full afternoon snowshoeing on the mainland hills, the boys return to [[cabin-island|Cabin Island]] just as a blizzard descends. [[frank-hardy|Frank]] unlocks the cabin door and [[chet-morton|Chet]], declaring himself "as hungry as a bear" (010-024), heads to the kitchen "to look over the food supplies with a hungry eye" (010-026). Moments later he emerges, "his eyes almost popping out of his head" (010-027). "It's gone!" he gasps (010-028). "Every speck!" [[chet-morton|Chet]] is almost tearful. "There isn't a bit of food in the kitchen" (010-032).

The boys rush to confirm the loss: "All their food supplies had disappeared. The shelves had been swept clear. The lads gazed at the empty kitchen in consternation" (010-036). [[frank-hardy|Frank]] immediately connects the theft to [[joe-hardy|Joe]]'s earlier observation: "I guess you were right, [[joe-hardy|Joe]], when you said you saw someone on the island. Some thief has been here while we were away" (010-040).

## Suspicions

[[frank-hardy|Frank]] suspects [[hanleigh|Hanleigh]]: "Looks like some of [[hanleigh|Hanleigh]]'s work" (010-047). [[joe-hardy|Joe]] reasons that "he figured that if he stole our food, we'd have to clear out" (010-050), identifying the motive — to force the boys off the island. [[frank-hardy|Frank]] later reinforces this theory: "It was [[hanleigh|Hanleigh]], I'm sure of that. I'd give a lot to know why he's so anxious to get us away from this island!" (010-077).

## Survival

The situation is dire. "We can't get back to town in this storm" (010-041), [[biff-hooper|Biff]] notes. But [[chet-morton|Chet]] remembers an overlooked packsack containing emergency rations: half a loaf of bread, sardines, salmon, and tea. Though [[frank-hardy|Frank]] suspects [[chet-morton|Chet]] had "cached that grub away in case you might get hungry" (010-064), they all agree "this time it worked out all right" (010-066). The boys resolve to stay: "He won't freeze us out now. We'll stay here to the last minute" (010-078).

## Recovery

The next day, the boys systematically search the island and find the hidden supplies — concealed beneath a large rock on the shore. The discovery reveals the sabotage for what it was: an attempt to make them leave. In the supplies they also find [[hanleigh-notebook|a small notebook]] that provides the crucial link to the larger mystery.
