# Biff's Christmas Iceboat

The new iceboat given to [[biff-hooper|Biff Hooper]] as a [[christmas-holidays|Christmas]] gift, representing both the spirit of the holiday season and a practical addition to the expedition's resources.

## Description

Among the gifts [[biff-hooper|Biff Hooper]] received at [[christmas-holidays|Christmas]] was "an iceboat from his father" (007-001). The boat was presumably of higher quality than the Hardy boys' homemade craft, being a purchased rather than constructed vessel. Like the Hardy iceboat, it was capable of carrying passengers and supplies across the frozen bay. The gift reflects both [[biff-hooper|Biff]]'s enthusiasm for the sport and his family's comfortable economic circumstances.

## In the Novel

[[biff-hooper|Biff]]'s iceboat is practically significant — it provides a second craft for the [[cabin-island|Cabin Island]] expedition, enabling the group to transport more people and supplies than would be possible with the Hardy boat alone. The timing of the gift, at [[christmas-holidays|Christmas]], ties the iceboat to the holiday theme that runs through the early chapters. [[biff-hooper|Biff]]'s new boat also represents the broader iceboating culture of [[bayport|Bayport]], where multiple families own and enjoy these winter craft.
