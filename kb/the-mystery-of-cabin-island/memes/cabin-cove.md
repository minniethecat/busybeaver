Cabin Cove is the largest of the many coves indenting the shoreline of [[barmet-bay|Barmet Bay]], located beyond a high, gloomy cliff that rises sheer from the ice (001-031). It is the body of water in which [[cabin-island|Cabin Island]] sits, making it a key geographical landmark in the boys' winter adventure.

## Location

Cabin Cove lies on [[barmet-bay|Barmet Bay]], accessible by iceboat from [[bayport|Bayport]] in winter. It is situated beyond a prominent cliff that serves as a natural boundary marker; once past this point, the cove opens into a broad inlet (001-031, 001-038). A [[shore-road|little village]] nestles in a cove on the southern shore of the bay, some distance from Cabin Cove (010-007).

## Geographic Features

The cove is ringed by high cliffs that rise almost directly from the water, making access difficult. [[cabin-island|Cabin Island]] occupies the center of the cove, itself surrounded by rocks that make navigation treacherous for larger vessels — [[frank-hardy|Frank Hardy]] notes that their motorboat, the *Sleuth*, was too large to maneuver among the rocks (001-035). The island within the cove is heavily timbered and once inhabited, as evidenced by a large log cabin built on an eminence overlooking the bay (001-037).

> The iceboat swung around the point, the cliffs lowering bleakly overhead, and they sped down into the great cove. (001-038)

## Role in the Mystery

Cabin Cove serves as the aquatic gateway to [[cabin-island|Cabin Island]], where much of the boys' [[detective-work|investigation]] unfolds. On their first approach, the Hardy boys swept into the cove after a race across the bay, drawn by curiosity about the mysterious island (001-031–001-038). The cove's sheltered waters provide a natural harbor for the iceboats during their multiple expeditions to the island (007-012, 015-006–015-009).

## Key Events at This Location

- The Hardy boys and [[chet-morton|Chet Morton]] first enter Cabin Cove after winning an iceboat race, leading to their exploration of [[cabin-island|Cabin Island]] (001-020–001-038)
- Later expeditions to and from the island pass through the cove, which shelters the iceboats from open-bay winds (007-012)
- The boys depart from the cove on their final trip back to [[bayport|Bayport]] (015-009)

## Access & Travel

Access to Cabin Cove is by iceboat in winter or by motorboat in summer, though summer access is hazardous due to rocks skirting the shore (001-035). In winter, the frozen bay allows iceboats to ride right up to the base of the cliffs, making landing possible at points that would be inaccessible by water (001-036). A little village on the southern shore provides the nearest supply point, reachable by iceboat from the cove (010-007, 010-053).
