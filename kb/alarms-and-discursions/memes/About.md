# About This Wiki

## Source Material

This wiki is built upon **Alarms and Discursions** (1910), a collection of 39 essays by G.K. Chesterton (1874–1936). The essays originally appeared in the *Daily News* and cover a wide range of topics including society, politics, philosophy, and theology, all delivered in Chesterton's characteristic paradoxical and witty style.

- **Title**: Alarms and Discursions

- **Author**: G.K. Chesterton

- **Year**: 1910

- **Status**: Public domain

## Usage Notes

This wiki provides structured, interlinked entries for concepts, persons, events, and the original essay chapters found in the book. Each chapter page is annotated with paragraph numbers (`[NNN-PPP]`) for precise citation.

- Browse by **chapter** to follow the original essay sequence

- Explore **concept** pages for key ideas and themes

- Use **full-text search** to find specific passages
