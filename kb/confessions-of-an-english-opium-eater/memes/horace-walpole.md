# Horace Walpole (Lord Orford)

## Biography

Horace Walpole (Lord Orford) (1717–1797) was English. The English writer and art historian (1717–1797), referred to as Lord Orford in the text — a figure from the preceding generation of English letters whose works De Quincey's readers would have recognised.

Horace Walpole (Lord Orford) is referenced in the *Confessions*. Their connection to De Quincey's narrative and intellectual world is documented in the text.
