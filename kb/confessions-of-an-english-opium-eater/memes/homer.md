# Homer

**Homer** (c. 8th century BCE) is the legendary author of the *Iliad* and the *Odyssey*, the two foundational epic poems of ancient Greek literature. Whether Homer was a single historical individual or a name attached to an oral tradition is a matter of scholarly debate.

## Reference in the *Confessions*

De Quincey invokes Homer in [Section 13](Section-13) within the context of his opium dreams, where classical forms and images — including Homeric battles and landscapes — mingle with his personal memories and architectural fantasies. The Homeric epic provides a template for the grandeur and terror of De Quincey's visionary experiences.
