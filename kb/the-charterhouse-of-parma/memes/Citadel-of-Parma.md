# Citadel of Parma

## Location and Description

The iconic prison-fortress visible from all parts of the city — the setting of Fabrizio's imprisonment and the birthplace of his transcendent love for [[Clelia-Conti|Clelia Conti]].

## In the Novel

The setting plays a crucial role in the narrative. Stendhal's precise geography is not accidental — the novel's settings mirror its emotional and political landscapes. The enclosed world of Citadel of Parma shapes the actions and possibilities of its characters, and Stendhal's descriptions are always in service of psychological and political observation.
