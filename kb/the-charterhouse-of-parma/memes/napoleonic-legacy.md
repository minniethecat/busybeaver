# Napoleonic Legacy

The Napoleonic era forms the crucial historical backdrop of Stendhal's novel. The story opens with the French entry into Milan in 1796 and Fabrizio's youthful enthusiasm for Napoleon culminates in his quixotic journey to Waterloo. The Napoleonic legacy — its ideals, its military culture, and the reaction it provoked — pervades the political and social landscape of the Parma court.
