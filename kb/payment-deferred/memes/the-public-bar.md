The public bar in Payment Deferred is [[william-marble|William Marble]]'s second social world — a space of masculine camaraderie, financial scheming, and the kind of relaxed performance that his [[domestic-life-at-53-malcolm-road|domestic life]] does not permit. It is here that he recruits [[saunders|Saunders]] into the Franc speculation scheme, and it is here that his behaviour is observed and later recalled by witnesses.

## In the Novel

The bar scenes establish an alternative geography to [[53-malcolm-road|53 Malcolm Road]]. Where [[dulwich|the suburb]]an home is a space of constraint and performance, [[the-public-house|the bar]] allows a different kind of performance — the [[william-marble|Marble]] who drinks Scotch, greets acquaintances, and discusses [[franc-speculation|currency speculation]] with an air of professional authority [004-012]. The "girl at [[the-public-house|the bar]]" knows him [004-012], and the regular patrons — "those men at [[the-public-house|the bar]] whom he knew" [004-015] — form a community of witnesses. The bar is also the scene of [[william-marble|Marble]]'s recruitment of [[saunders|Saunders]] [004-059], the moment that transforms speculation into conspiracy. In retrospect, every detail of these bar scenes becomes potential evidence.
