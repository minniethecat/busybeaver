# France

France is the country through which [[jonathan-mansel|Jonathan Mansel]]'s rescue party travels en route to Austria. The party crosses from England to Dieppe and drives across the French countryside in their [[rolls-royce|Rolls-Royce]].

## Role in the Story

[[bell|Bell]] is dispatched ahead of the main party to cross to France by a morning boat. His mission: "to buy food and petrol against our coming and to promise ten thousand francs to the officials concerned if the Rolls was passed through the customs without any delay" (001-134).

The French leg of the journey is conducted at speed. [[george-hanbury|Hanbury]] takes the wheel for part of the crossing, maintaining the relentless pace that [[jonathan-mansel|Mansel]] demands.

After the main events in Austria, the party's escape route returns through France. [[rowley|Rowley]] remarks: "I guess we'll give one of the willies a lift to France" (008-222), plotting the journey home with a stolen car.

The French customs present an obstacle on both legs of the journey — the party must navigate the border crossing with discretion, relying on [[bell|Bell]]'s preparations and bribery to ensure smooth passage.


> Bell was to cross to France by a morning boat, there to buy food and petrol against our coming and to promise ten thousand francs to the officials concerned if we were clear of the Customs in half an hour.

> (001-134)
