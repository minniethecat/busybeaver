# The Willies

The Willies — Willys-Knight motorcars — are the vehicles used by [[rose-noble|Rose Noble]]'s criminal network for transport and pursuit throughout the Carinthian mountains. They represent the gang's resources and mobility, and become a prize of war for the rescue party.

> "I guess we'll give one of the Willies a lift to France. They'll have to go back to London to raise the wind. An' he'll put us through the Customs—if we remember to ask him while Mansel's up on the wall."
>
> (008-222)

## Role in the Story

After the escape from the [[castle-of-gath|Castle of Gath]], the party discusses their transport options. [[rowley|Rowley]] provides the intelligence: "I guess we'll give one of the willies a lift to France" (008-222), planning to commandeer one of Noble's cars for the return journey.

The Willys-Knight was an American automobile known for its sleeve-valve engine, offering quiet operation and good performance on rough roads — ideal for the mountainous terrain of Carinthia. That Noble's gang uses American cars while Mansel's party drives British [[rolls-royce|Rolls-Royces]] is a small but telling detail, contrasting the gang's New World pragmatism with the party's Old World elegance.
