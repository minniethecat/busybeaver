# Folkestone

Folkestone is the English Channel port from which [[jonathan-mansel|Jonathan Mansel]]'s rescue party embarks for continental Europe. The party plans to "meet at Folkestone and cross by the morning boat" (001-063):

> We were to meet at Folkestone and cross by the morning boat.
>
> (001-063)

The port was the point of departure from England — the last piece of home soil before the dangerous mission in Austria.

## Role in the Story

Folkestone's role is brief but symbolically significant. It is the threshold between the familiar English world of [[cleveland-row|Cleveland Row]] and [[maintenance|Maintenance]] and the unknown dangers of [[carinthia|Carinthia]]. The "morning boat" represents the irreversible commitment to action — once crossed, there is no turning back.

The logistical planning around Folkestone reflects [[jonathan-mansel|Mansel]]'s meticulous approach. The party coordinates their arrival at the port, arranges for the [[rolls-royce|Rolls-Royces]] to be transported, and plans the route from Folkestone through France to [[salzburg|Salzburg]] — all calculated to minimize delay and maximize speed.
