# The Spiral Staircase

The spiral staircase is a stone stair within the [[castle-of-gath|Castle of Gath]] that provides a hidden route from the archway beneath the cascade up to the King's Closet. It is the critical vertical axis of the rescue party's infiltration of the fortress.

> "In the side of the archway I found a flight of steps — a very steep spiral staircase, that comes to a sudden end. I'm certain it serves a trapdoor. If the bookseller's guide is sound, that trapdoor should be in the King's Closet."

> (005-206)

## Role in the Story

[[jonathan-mansel|Mansel]] identifies the spiral staircase during his reconnaissance of the castle. Rising from the archway door, it ascends within the thickness of the castle walls to a trapdoor that opens into [[the-closet|the King's Closet]]. This route allows the party to bypass the guarded main entrance and enter the royal apartments directly.

The staircase proves vital at multiple points in the operation. After the abortive nighttime assault, the party retreats down the stair to the relative safety of the archway. When [[adele-pleydell|Adèle]] is rescued from the terrace, she is brought up the spiral stair to change into dry clothes in the bedchamber. The stair is narrow, dark, and ancient — a passage built for medieval defenders that now serves modern rescuers.
