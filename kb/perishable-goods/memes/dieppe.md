# Dieppe

Dieppe is a French Channel port that serves as the continental entry and exit point for [[jonathan-mansel|Jonathan Mansel]]'s rescue party. The party lands here after crossing from England and passes through again on their return journey.

## Role

The crossing to Dieppe marks the party's passage from England into the unknown:

> At a quarter to seven we passed out of Cleveland Row, and at eight o'clock we were clear of London. At eleven o'clock we reached Newhaven, and at half past one we were in Dieppe.
>
> (001-141) in the Story

With [[rowley|Rowley]] and [[bell|Bell]] already dispatched ahead, the main party is reduced to four as they travel to Dieppe — "yet in a way we were five, for [[tester|Tester]], Mansel's Sealyham, went with us, and he could not have made a braver or a better companion" (001-139).

[[george-hanbury|Hanbury]] takes [[william-chandos|Chandos]]'s place at the wheel for the final leg, and [[jonathan-mansel|Mansel]] is "plainly pleased with the pace at which we had come" (002-013).

Later, when [[bell|Bell]]'s whereabouts are discussed, [[william-chandos|Chandos]] recalls: "He was on the quay at Dieppe" (003-178), confirming that Bell remained at the port to facilitate the party's passage.
