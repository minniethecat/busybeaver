# The Station

The railway station in the Austrian countryside serves as a key location in Chapter III, where [[jonathan-mansel|Jonathan Mansel]]'s party transitions from road pursuit to rail pursuit of [[rouse|Rouse]].

## Role in the Story

After disabling [[rouse|Rouse]]'s car, the party makes for the station. [[william-chandos|William Chandos]] records: "By now we had come to the station, and ten minutes later we had our tickets for Innsbruck and were aboard the train" (003-117):

> By now we had come to the station, and ten minutes later we had our tickets for Innsbruck and were aboard the train.
>
> (003-117)

The station represents a tactical pivot — the shift from the immediacy of the road chase to the longer game of shadowing [[rouse|Rouse]] toward the [[castle-of-gath|Castle of Gath]].

The station also serves as a site of deception. [[bell|Bell]] sabotages [[rouse|Rouse]]'s car at the station, ensuring the criminal cannot simply drive away. The station thus becomes a trap — a place where [[rouse|Rouse]] is contained and observed until the party can follow him to his destination.
