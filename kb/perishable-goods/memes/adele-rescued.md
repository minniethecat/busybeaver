# Adèle Rescued

Adèle Rescued marks the turning point of the [[castle-of-gath-operation|Castle of Gath Operation]]: the moment when [[jonathan-mansel|Jonathan Mansel]], hidden in [[the-archway|the archway]] beneath the cascade with [[william-chandos|William Chandos]], seized a split-second opportunity.

The opportunity was born of [[casemate|Casemate]]'s carelessness. [[jonathan-mansel|Mansel]] pulled [[adele-pleydell|Adèle Pleydell]] out of [[rose-noble|Rose Noble]]'s grasp in an act of such swift, audacious improvisation that even [[william-chandos|Chandos]] could "hardly believe that our attempt at rescue was fairly begun" (006-068).

## Context / Lead-up

After the abortive nighttime assault and the bitter retreat to [[the-archway|the archway]], [[jonathan-mansel|Mansel]] and [[william-chandos|Chandos]] had spent a cold, wet night hidden beneath the cascade door.

They had re-entered the King's Closet via the [[spiral-staircase|spiral stair]]case and the trapdoor, received food and dry clothes hauled up the cliff by [[george-hanbury|Hanbury]], and slept fitfully as dawn broke over the [[castle-of-gath|Castle of Gath]] (006-047).

The following morning, [[william-chandos|Chandos]] was at the keyhole of [[the-archway|the archway]] door, listening, when he heard a footstep on [[castle-of-gath-terrace|the terrace]] beyond (006-057). [[jonathan-mansel|Mansel]] was at his side in an instant, peering under the door through the channel of flowing water.

What they saw made their hearts leap: [[adele-pleydell|Adèle]], walking on [[castle-of-gath-terrace|the terrace]] under guard. But her beautiful hair had been "rudely shorn to the shape of her head, and about her shoulders she wore a rag of a shawl" (006-061).

## Sequence of Events

1. **[[casemate|Casemate]]'s Carelessness** — [[casemate|Casemate]], [[adele-pleydell|Adèle]]'s warder, complains about the sunshine on his head: "Don't you want a hat on your curls?" (006-063). [[adele-pleydell|Adèle]] refuses the taunt. Irritated, [[casemate|Casemate]] leaves her to fetch his hat: "You carry on, an' move. You're not here to wave to Charlie: you're here for exercise. An' [[rose-noble|Rose]] is red-hot this morning, so I shouldn't tread on his toes" (006-065).
2. **[[jonathan-mansel|Mansel]]'s Decision** — Without hesitation, [[jonathan-mansel|Mansel]] orders [[william-chandos|Chandos]]: "Pull up [[the-cord|the rope]]." Before [[william-chandos|Chandos]] can think, [[jonathan-mansel|Mansel]] is gone, vanished beneath the door (006-067).
3. **The Rescue** — In an astonishingly brief span of time, [[adele-pleydell|Adèle]]'s face appears in the channel almost between [[william-chandos|Chandos]]'s legs. He has her under the arms, draws her clear of the door, and lifts her up. [[jonathan-mansel|Mansel]] follows at once (006-068)(006-069).
4. **The Plan** — [[jonathan-mansel|Mansel]] speaks rapidly: "If we can do it in time, we shall let you down the face of the cliff. The moment you're down, unfasten [[the-cord|the rope]] and run for your life to [[the-beechwoods|the beechwoods]] that lie northeast... [[george-hanbury|George]] or [[carson|Carson]] will be there with one of [[rolls-royce|the Rolls]]. Tell them to drive you—" (006-070).
5. **[[rose-noble|Rose Noble]] Discovers the Escape** — [[jonathan-mansel|Mansel]] stops short, listening. [[rose-noble|Noble]]'s voice rings out from [[castle-of-gath-terrace|the terrace]]: "Hat be damned. I said 'See that she moves.' By ———, that's clean enough talking. D'you think—" And then the terrible realization: "By ———, she's gone. Look at that ——— cord" (006-074)(006-079).
6. **The Car Dispatched** — [[rose-noble|Noble]] roars orders: "[[bunch|Bunch]]! [[punter|Punter]]! Turn out the ——— car. Go and help them, you ———. And if we don't take her at the foot of this blasted rock I'll twist your block off your body with my bare hands" (006-081). [[casemate|Casemate]] flees, shouting for [[bunch|Bunch]] "like a man possessed" (006-082).
7. **The [[rope|Rope]] Withdrawn** — The rescue rope, which had been extended to the base of the cliff, is pulled back into the channel and lies "as snug and as taut as though it had never been touched" (006-083).
8. **Calm Amid Chaos** — [[jonathan-mansel|Mansel]] speaks low: "Sit down on the step, dear, and let me pull off your boots" (006-085). [[adele-pleydell|Adèle]], drenched to the skin, obeys without question. He sends her up the [[spiral-staircase|spiral stair]] to change into dry clothes from her dressing-case (006-087).
9. **The Gate Opens** — From [[the-archway|the archway]], [[william-chandos|Chandos]] watches the courtyard. The car engine sputters and roars; [[the-main-gate|the great gate]] swings open; [[rolls-royce|the car]] shoots out in pursuit (006-090).
10. **[[jonathan-mansel|Mansel]]'s Assessment** — [[jonathan-mansel|Mansel]] tells [[william-chandos|Chandos]] what he believes has happened: "I hope and believe it means that it's gone for good, that [[george-hanbury|George]] and the servants have got it and all or some of its crew... unless and until they return, I believe he's alone" (006-265).

## Participants

- **[[adele-pleydell|Adèle Pleydell]]** — the rescue target; her shorn hair and ragged shawl testify to weeks of brutal captivity, but her composure under fire is immaculate
- **[[jonathan-mansel|Jonathan Mansel]]** — the rescuer; his split-second decision to act when [[casemate|Casemate]] left [[castle-of-gath-terrace|the terrace]] was a gamble that paid off—though he would later reflect "I ought to have led out Clubs" (006-149), acknowledging that [[rose-noble|Noble]] had outplayed him in the longer game
- **[[william-chandos|William Chandos]]** — the anchor; his role in hauling [[adele-pleydell|Adèle]] through the channel and managing [[the-cord|the rope]] was physically and emotionally crucial
- **[[casemate|Casemate]]** — [[rolls-royce|the car]]eless warder; his decision to leave [[adele-pleydell|Adèle]] unattended to fetch his hat was the crack through which the rescue was achieved
- **[[rose-noble|Rose Noble]]** — the thwarted captor; his reaction—"Hat be damned... Look at that ——— cord"—reveals the suddenness of his loss

## Outcome / Consequences

The rescue of [[adele-pleydell|Adèle]] transformed the operational situation. Though the party was still physically trapped inside the [[castle-of-gath|Castle of Gath]]—pinned in the Closet and Bedchamber with doors wedged—their objective had been achieved.

[[adele-pleydell|Adèle]] was with them, dry, and in possession of her dressing-case. The moral calculus had shifted: [[rose-noble|Noble]] had lost his hostage, and [[jonathan-mansel|Mansel]] now had only to wait for [[george-hanbury|Hanbury]]'s attack on the following night.

But the rescue also provoked [[rose-noble|Noble]]'s final gambit—the cutting of [[the-cord|the waterfall rope]] and the ambush in the Dining-room that would follow.

And in the intimacy of the siege that followed, [[adele-pleydell|Adèle]] would at last speak the words that had been building since the [[carinthia-kidnapping|Carinthia Kidnapping]]: "I'm in love with [[jonah|Jonah]]. I think I've loved him for years, but I never knew" (006-251).
