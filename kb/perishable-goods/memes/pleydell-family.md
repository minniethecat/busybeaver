# Pleydell Family

The [[daphne-pleydell|Pleydell]] family, consisting of [[major-pleydell|Major Pleydell]], his sisters [[daphne-pleydell|Daphne]] and [[adele-pleydell|Adèle]], and [[adele-pleydell|Adèle]]'s husband "[[captain-pleydell|Boy]]" [[daphne-pleydell|Pleydell]], are cousins of [[jonathan-mansel|Jonathan Mansel]] and central figures in the novel. Their ancestral seat is [[white-ladies|White Ladies]] in the [[new-forest|New Forest]], and they own a farm in [[carinthia|Carinthia]] that becomes a crucial staging ground for the mission to rescue [[adele-pleydell|Adèle]] from the [[castle-of-gath|Castle of Gath]]. (001-031)


> It was early in the following April that Hanbury and I spent three fine days at White Ladies in the New Forest, that is to say, at Mansel's country home. This he shared with his cousins, whose name was Pleydell. It was not our first visit, but the Pleydells were absent when we had been there before, and, though the four Sargents in the gallery had told us what to expect, I do not think that either of us believed that all five members of one family could prove so charming.

> (001-031)

## Nature & Operations

The [[daphne-pleydell|Pleydell]]s are a landed English family of means and social standing. They maintain two residences — their English estate, [[white-ladies|White Ladies]] in the [[new-forest|New Forest]], and a farm in the village of [[poganec|Poganec]], near St. Martin in [[carinthia|Carinthia]]. The [[carinthia|Carinthia]]n property, served by the nearby village of [[sava|Sava]], functions not merely as a country retreat but as a strategic operational base for [[jonathan-mansel|Mansel]]'s party. (001-031) (001-109) (001-135)

The family operates with a clear chain of authority. [[major-pleydell|Major Pleydell]] serves as the de facto head, making key decisions and representing the family in action. [[daphne-pleydell|Daphne Pleydell]] manages the household and extends hospitality to [[jonathan-mansel|Mansel]]'s associates, while [[adele-pleydell|Adèle]]'s centrality to the rescue mission defines the family's role in the narrative. (002-044) (002-106)

She is described as the youngest and most striking of the [[daphne-pleydell|Pleydell]] siblings — [[william-chandos|Chandos]] remarks that "[[adele-pleydell|Adèle Pleydell]] would have diminished anyone." (001-034) (001-032)

## Activities in the Story

The [[daphne-pleydell|Pleydell]]s first appear when [[jonathan-mansel|Mansel]] invites [[william-chandos|Chandos]] and [[george-hanbury|Hanbury]] to stay at [[white-ladies|White Ladies]] while he introduces them to a proposed adventure. [[major-pleydell|Major Pleydell]] proposes the [[carinthia|Carinthia]]n expedition, and the family's farm becomes the operational headquarters for the party. (001-037) (001-058)

When [[adele-pleydell|Adèle]] is abducted by the criminal network led by [[rose-noble]], the Pleydells are thrust into crisis. [[major-pleydell|Major Pleydell]] takes an active role in the search and rescue, at one point forcing open a locked door and later driving [[jonathan-mansel|Mansel]]'s party to [[sava|Sava]] to investigate the scene of the kidnapping. (002-032) (002-044) (002-093) (002-108)

The family's discipline is evidenced when "it was then decided that the Pleydells should take no action, whatever happened, except such as came from them but [[major-pleydell|Major Pleydell]] or one of the servants we knew" — a protocol established to prevent infiltration by the enemy. (002-106)

## Relationship with the Protagonists

The [[daphne-pleydell|Pleydell]]s are cousins of [[jonathan-mansel|Jonathan Mansel]], and through him are connected to [[william-chandos|William Chandos]] and [[george-hanbury|George Hanbury]]. The relationship is one of mutual trust and shared purpose — the Pleydells provide safe houses, local knowledge, and material support for the protagonists' operations. (001-031)

Their [[carinthia|Carinthia]]n farm serves the party whenever they come or go, providing cover and logistical support. The family's servants and local connections in the [[sava|Sava]]–[[poganec|Poganec]] region are indispensable to the mission's success. (001-150)
