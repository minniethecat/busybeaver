# London

London is the capital of England and the home city of several key characters in *Perishable Goods*. [[jonathan-mansel|Jonathan Mansel]]'s flat is in [[cleveland-row|Cleveland Row]], situated in the heart of the city, and it is from London that the rescue party departs for Austria.

## Role in the Story

The story begins with [[william-chandos|William Chandos]] travelling to London for a cousin's wedding (001-002). He and [[george-hanbury|George Hanbury]] would never dream of visiting London without calling on [[jonathan-mansel|Jonathan Mansel]], whose flat was in [[cleveland-row|Cleveland Row]] (001-003). The bond that drew them there was forged in shared fortune and proven friendship:

> Neither Hanbury nor I would have dreamed of visiting London without calling on Jonathan Mansel, whose flat was in Cleveland Row; for we three had made our fortune together and together had proved the stuff of which friendship is made.
>
> (001-003)

[[william-chandos|Chandos]] started betimes and travelled to London by road, and "the clock of St. James's Palace was striking eleven" when he passed [[marlborough-gate|Marlborough Gate]] (001-004).

London serves as the departure point for the rescue expedition to Austria. The party gathers at [[cleveland-row|Cleveland Row]] before setting out on their cross-continental journey.
