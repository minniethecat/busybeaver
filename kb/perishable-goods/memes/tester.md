# Tester

Tester is [[jonathan-mansel|Jonathan Mansel]]'s Sealyham terrier, who accompanies the party throughout the entire course of the novel — from the departure at [[cleveland-row|Cleveland Row]] through the pursuit across Austria to the final confrontation at the [[castle-of-gath|Castle of Gath]]. Though a dog, Tester functions as a full member of [[jonathan-mansel|Mansel]]'s company: guarding the camp, barking warnings at intruders, and offering silent comfort to the rescued [[adele-pleydell|Adèle Pleydell]].

## Role in the Story

Tester is introduced as one of the party at the very outset: "in a way we were five, for Tester, [[jonathan-mansel|Mansel]]'s Sealyham, went with us" (001-139). He serves as an instinctive judge of character — barking at [[rouse|Rouse]] during the confrontation (003-033) and nearly flying at [[rose-noble|Rose Noble]] himself (009-064) — and as a guardian of the camp while the men are on operations (005-163), (007-097). After [[adele-pleydell|Adèle Pleydell]]'s rescue, Tester's quiet presence beside her becomes a symbol of the safety she has regained (009-010), (009-046).

## Key Appearances

- **Chapter I** — Drives off the quay with [[jonathan-mansel|Mansel]] and [[carson|Carson]] as the party departs (001-160); given his freedom during a rest stop (002-012); called for by [[jonathan-mansel|Mansel]] as they set off again (002-035).
- **Chapter III** — Speaks for the party by leaving [[rouse|Rouse]]'s room during the tense encounter at [[villach|Villach]] (003-003); barks as [[george-hanbury|George]] and [[rowley|Rowley]] confront the fake clergyman (003-033); wakes [[william-chandos|Chandos]] in the night by putting his nose against his hand (003-050).
- **Chapter IV** — Fed by [[carson|Carson]] during a reconnaissance pause (004-104); left guarding [[rolls-royce|the Rolls]] by the roadside (004-161).
- **Chapter V** — Stationed back in the wood, guarding the signal cord (005-163).
- **Chapter VII** — On guard within sound of the cliff (007-052); [[william-chandos|Chandos]] means to take him but forgets — a lapse he regrets (007-061); left to feed the party and guard [[rolls-royce|the car]]s with [[carson|Carson]], a dangerous post for a man alone except for the dog (007-097), (007-111).
- **Chapter VIII** — Licks [[william-chandos|Chandos]]'s face upon his return (008-273); scrambles before [[william-chandos|Chandos]], agog to join the action (008-293).
- **Chapter IX** — Lies close against [[jonathan-mansel|Mansel]]'s side as the chapter opens (009-010); [[adele-pleydell|Adèle]] asks quietly if Tester will let her tend to [[jonathan-mansel|Mansel]] — and he does (009-046); lifts his head and looks at her (009-057); [[william-chandos|Chandos]] catches him just in time as he would have flown at [[rose-noble|Rose Noble]] (009-064); wags his tail as the confrontation concludes (009-103); rolls over and puts his paws up (009-105); goes the rounds with [[carson|Carson]] and [[william-chandos|Chandos]] for the last time (009-162); sent down with [[carson|Carson]] to finish loading [[rolls-royce|the Rolls]] (009-165).

## Quotations

Tester's actions speak louder than any speech. The narrator records his presence in small, telling details:

> "in a way we were five, for Tester, Mansel's Sealyham, went with us" (001-139)

> "Tester spoke for us all, by leaving the room" (003-003)

> "Tester was licking my face" (008-273)

> "If Tester will let me, I'll do what I can" — Adèle Pleydell (009-046)

## Relationships

- **[[jonathan-mansel|Jonathan Mansel]]** — Tester's master; the dog lies close against his lord and would defend him against any threat (009-010).
- **[[carson|Carson]]** — [[jonathan-mansel|Mansel]]'s body-servant, who feeds Tester during operations (004-104) and takes him on the final rounds (009-162).
- **[[adele-pleydell|Adèle Pleydell]]** — The rescued woman whom Tester permits to tend [[jonathan-mansel|Mansel]]'s wounds, a gesture of trust that speaks volumes (009-046).
- **[[william-chandos|William Chandos]]** — The narrator, who catches Tester before he can attack [[rose-noble|Rose Noble]] (009-064) and whose face Tester licks in welcome (008-273).
- **[[rouse|Hannibal Rouse]]** — The fake clergyman at whom Tester barks, instinctively recognising a threat (003-033).
- **[[rose-noble|Rose Noble]]** — The antagonist at whom Tester would fly, requiring [[william-chandos|Chandos]] to restrain him (009-064).
