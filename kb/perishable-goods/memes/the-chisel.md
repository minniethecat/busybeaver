# The Chisel

The chisel is a heavy carpenter's tool carried by [[jonathan-mansel|Mansel]] as part of the rescue party's equipment. It is the key to forcing the massive locked doors that guard the approach to [[adele-pleydell|Adèle Pleydell]]'s prison in the [[castle-of-gath|Castle of Gath]].

> I held the torch, while Mansel played with the chisel and presently forced the lock.

> (005-261)

## Role in the Story

When the party reaches the locked doors of the castle, Mansel deploys the chisel with methodical precision: "With that, he set Hanbury to watch the dining-room door and hold the torch, while I took the stoutest chisel and laid its edge where the lock met the door" (005-261).

The chisel embodies the practical, physical nature of the rescue operation. For all Mansel's strategic genius, the mission ultimately depends on simple tools wielded with skill and courage — a chisel to force a lock, a rope to lower a captive, a torch to light the darkness. The choice of "the stoutest chisel" from the equipment bag reflects Mansel's preference for the right tool for the right job.
