# The Lift

The lift is a mechanical passenger lift installed in the [[castle-of-gath|Castle of Gath]] by [[rose-noble|Rose Noble]]'s gang. It is a modern intrusion into the medieval fortress, rigged to transport people between floors — and it becomes a dangerous trap for the rescue party.

## Role in the Story

During the escape from the [[dining-room|Royal Dining-Room]], the party encounters the lift. [[william-chandos|Chandos]] describes the mechanism: "The floor was but eight feet away, but, if one of us was to descend, the lift would instantly rise and, taking the other up, lock him once more into the room above" (007-036):

> The floor was but eight feet away, but, if one of us was to descend, the lift would instantly rise and, taking the other up, lock him once more into the room above.
>
> (007-036)

The lift is designed as a counterweight system: when one person steps onto it, the weight differential causes it to rise, trapping the other person. This ingenious but cruel mechanism forces the party to find an alternative escape route, complicating their flight from [[rose-noble|Noble]]'s men.
