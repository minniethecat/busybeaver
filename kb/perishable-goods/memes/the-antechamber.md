# The Antechamber

The antechamber of the [[castle-of-gath|Castle of Gath]] is a passage room identified by the bookseller's guide — a local informant whose knowledge of the castle's interior proves invaluable to [[jonathan-mansel|Jonathan Mansel]]'s rescue operation.

> A moment later we stood in the antechamber.

> (006-119)

## Role in the Story

The antechamber is reached after the rescue party passes through [[queens-rooms|the Queen's Rooms]]: "we came back to the Closet and, passing through the Queen's rooms, entered the other antechamber which the bookseller's guide had shown us" (006-119). The room serves as a navigational waypoint, one of several interior spaces the party must traverse to reach [[adele-pleydell|Adèle Pleydell]] in the [[castle-of-gath-tower|tower]].

The antechamber's strategic value lies in its position within the castle's internal geography. It provides access to multiple routes while offering cover from detection — a transitional space that the rescue party can use to regroup and confirm their bearings before advancing.
