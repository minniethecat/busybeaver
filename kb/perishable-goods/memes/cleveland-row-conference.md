# Cleveland Row Conference

The Cleveland Row Conference is the strategic council held in [[jonathan-mansel|Jonathan Mansel]]'s flat at [[cleveland-row|Cleveland Row]] after news of [[adele-pleydell|Adèle Pleydell]]'s kidnapping arrives. It is the moment when the scattered facts cohere into a plan of action.

> On the thirtieth day of August we dined in Cleveland Row, to settle the hour of departure and other things.

> (001-097)

## Lead-up

The telegram reporting Adèle's disappearance (001-097) shatters the calm of the flat. [[jonathan-mansel|Mansel]] absorbs the news in silence, then speaks the chilling phrase "perishable goods" (001-107). After a night of contemplation, he summons the party — [[william-chandos|Chandos]], [[george-hanbury|Hanbury]], [[rowley|Rowley]], and [[bell|Bell]] — to formulate a response.

## The Conference

Mansel lays out the situation with characteristic clarity. [[rose-noble|Rose Noble]] holds Adèle somewhere in Austria, and the police cannot be called. The party must operate as a private military expedition — self-contained, self-sufficient, and entirely outside the law.

The conference establishes the division of labour: [[bell|Bell]] to prepare the French passage, [[rowley|Rowley]] to manage logistics, [[george-hanbury|Hanbury]] to serve as second-in-command, and [[william-chandos|Chandos]] as [[jonathan-mansel|Mansel]]'s right hand. The Rolls-Royces will be the transport; the destination is Austria; the objective is Adèle, alive.
