# The Clerestory

The clerestory is a concealed upper passage within the [[castle-of-gath|Castle of Gath]], running above the level of the royal apartments. It serves as a hidden route of movement for the rescue party after they have secured [[adele-pleydell|Adèle Pleydell]].

> For us to break out would be extremely hard: we had long ago perceived that the two "galleries of stone" were the keys to the royal suite, and that these were joined by a passage meant that we were surrounded by a guardroom which two men could hold against twenty, the inside of which we had not so much as seen.
>
> (006-236)

## Role in the Story

After the successful rescue, [[jonathan-mansel|Mansel]] leads the party through the castle's hidden architecture — passages and chambers unknown to [[rose-noble|Rose Noble]]'s gang (005-196). The clerestory, positioned high above the main rooms, allows the party to observe and move without being detected by the guards below.

The discovery of the clerestory is typical of [[jonathan-mansel|Mansel]]'s tactical genius: he has studied the castle's medieval architecture and understood that its builders would have included such features for defense and surveillance. What was built for kings becomes a tool for rescuers.
