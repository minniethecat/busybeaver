# The Bridge

The bridge near [[poganec|Poganec]] is identified by [[jonathan-mansel|Jonathan Mansel]] as a critical tactical position during the surveillance phase of the rescue operation. It represents [[jonathan-mansel|Mansel]]'s characteristic attention to terrain and chokepoints.

## Role in the Story

[[jonathan-mansel|Mansel]] explains the bridge's significance with military precision: "A post in the woods would be useless, for the trees would get in the way; but that torrent commands Poganec; and a man sitting there with a glass could see anyone come and go" (002-121):

> A post in the woods would be useless, for the trees would get in the way; but that torrent commands Poganec; and a man sitting there with a glass could see anyone come and go.
>
> (002-121)

The bridge is a natural observation point — a position that controls visibility over the entire valley approach to [[poganec|Poganec]].

The bridge also serves as a reference point for tracking movement between villages. The road from [[sava|Sava]] to [[st-martin|St. Martin]] crosses the bridge, making it a natural chokepoint for anyone traveling between the operational area and the outside world.
