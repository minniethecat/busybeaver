# The Main Gate

The main gate of the [[castle-of-gath|Castle of Gath]] is the principal entrance to the fortress, a massive portal set in the courtyard wall through which vehicles pass. It plays a brief but dramatic role during the aftermath of Adèle's rescue.

## Role in the Story

After [[adele-pleydell|Adèle Pleydell]] is pulled from the terrace to safety, [[rose-noble|Rose Noble]] discovers her escape and roars orders to pursue. From the archway, [[william-chandos|Chandos]] watches the courtyard: "The car engine sputters and roars; the great gate swings open; the car shoots out in pursuit" (006-090).

> Suddenly the gate was opened, and the car shot out.
>
> (006-090)

The gate's opening is a moment of high tension. The rescue party has lowered Adèle down the cliff face by rope. If the pursuing car reaches the base of the cliff before she is away, the entire operation will be undone. The swinging open of the great gate signals the beginning of the race.

The gate's massive construction — designed to repel medieval armies — is ironically turned against its current occupants, as [[rose-noble|Noble]]'s men must open it to give chase, consuming precious moments.
