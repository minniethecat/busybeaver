# The Steps

The stone steps of the [[castle-of-gath|Castle of Gath]] are a critical element of the castle's defensive architecture. Each flight terminates in a massive door, and each door is locked — creating a series of barriers between the rescue party and [[adele-pleydell|Adèle Pleydell]].

## Role in the Story

[[jonathan-mansel|Jonathan Mansel]]'s reconnaissance reveals the challenge: "At the head of each flight of steps there's a massive door: both doors are fast" (005-201):

> At the head of each flight of steps there's a massive door: both doors are fast.
>
> (005-201)

The steps thus represent not merely a physical route but a gauntlet — a sequence of locked portals that must be forced or bypassed.

The steps' defensive design reflects the castle's original purpose as a fortress. What was built to repel medieval armies now serves [[rose-noble|Rose Noble]]'s purposes, turning the castle's historic architecture into a modern prison. The rescue party must overcome obstacles designed centuries before their time.
