# About This Wiki

This wiki is a comprehensive reference for Thornton Wilder's novel *The Bridge of San Luis Rey* (1927), winner of the Pulitzer Prize. It documents the novel's characters, places, concepts, events, and themes, drawing on the full text of the novel.

## Scope

- **Characters**: Major and minor figures in the novel
- **Places**: Locations in Peru, Spain, and beyond
- **Concepts**: Theological, philosophical, and literary themes
- **Events**: Key plot points and historical context
- **Organizations**: Institutions depicted or referenced

## Structure

Each page includes a description, relevant passages with paragraph-number (PN) citations, and cross-references to related pages through wikilinks.
