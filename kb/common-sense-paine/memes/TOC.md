## About This Work

*Common Sense* is a political pamphlet by [[thomas-paine|Thomas Paine]], published in [[philadelphia|Philadelphia]] on February 14, 1776. It argued for [[america|America]]n independence from [[england|Great Britain]] at a moment when the question remained sharply contested, and became one of the most influential documents of the [[american-revolution|American Revolution]]. (P01-003)

[[thomas-paine|Paine]] wrote in plain, forceful prose aimed at ordinary readers, deliberately avoiding the Latin quotations and legal precedents favored by 18th-century political writers. The pamphlet sold over 100,000 copies within months and helped shift colonial opinion decisively toward [[independence|independence]].
