# House of Lancaster

## Overview
The House of Lancaster was the cadet branch of the Plantagenet dynasty that held the English throne from 1399 to 1461 and again briefly in 1470–1471. Their red rose emblem gave the [[wars-of-the-roses|Wars of the Roses]] its name.

## In Common Sense
The Lancastrian claim to the throne was no more or less legitimate than the Yorkist claim — and this, for [[thomas-paine|Paine]], is precisely the point. When legitimacy is determined by genealogy, there is no principled way to distinguish between competing claims. The result is civil war, not settled government. The entire dynastic framework is a category error that confuses family lineage with [[authority|political authority]].
