# Steelhead

Steelhead is a mysterious warrior and mentor figure in The [[sundering-flood]]. He appears to Osberne at pivotal moments, bestowing gifts of ancient [[dwarfs|dwarf]]-craft — including the sword Boardcleaver and a bow of exceptional power — and offering guidance that shapes Osberne's destiny.

## Nature and Identity

Steelhead's true nature is never fully revealed. He gave his name to Osberne at their first meeting, saying "thou mayst call me Steelhead" and promising Osberne would know him by that name when they met again (007-017). He appeared as a wayfarer, a guest called Waywearer when he came to [[wethermel]] (012-004).

## Gifts to Osberne

Steelhead gifted Osberne a bow of dwarf-make, declaring that whosoever received it as a gift from him would hit whatever they shot at (007-014, 007-015). Later, he bestowed upon Osberne the sword Boardcleaver, fashioned by the Dwarf-folk in ancient times (013-009). He warned Osberne never to draw the sword in false quarrel or drunken brawl (013-011).

## Mentorship

Steelhead's guidance extended beyond material gifts. He taught Osberne the wisdom of restraint — when to fight and when to hold back. Osberne followed his counsel, going often by the riverside as Steelhead had advised (008-006).
