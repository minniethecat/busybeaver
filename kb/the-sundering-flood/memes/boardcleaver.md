# Boardcleaver

A legendary sword of [[dwarfs|Dwarf]]-make, gifted by Steelhead to the young Osberne of Wethermel, serving as his primary weapon throughout his adventures along the [[sundering-flood]].

## Nature

Boardcleaver is a physical object — a sword of exceptional craftsmanship, forged by the Dwarf-folk in ancient times (013-009). It is a weapon of war, meant for true battle rather than idle quarrels.

## Description

The sword was fashioned by the Dwarf-folk, makers of legendary weapons in the world of the Sundering Flood (013-009). Steelhead, a mysterious warrior and friend to Osberne, presented the sword to the young man as a gift, telling him its name and history (013-009). Osberne was warned by Steelhead never to draw Boardcleaver in false quarrel or drunken brawl, but only in earnest cause (013-011). Though the blade was heavy, Steelhead assured Osberne that his strength would grow to wield it properly (013-014).

Alongside the sword, Steelhead gave Osberne other gifts that complemented the blade, saying they "shall go well with Boardcleaver" (014-002). Osberne practiced with the sword and grew more skillful in its use (014-003). He wore Boardcleaver at his side when he visited Elfhild at the Bight of the Cloven Knoll, proudly showing it to his friend (014-005).

In battle, Boardcleaver proved its worth many times over. When Hardcastle threatened Wethermel, Osberne was girded with the sword and clad in scarlet, ready to defend his home (016-002). In the [[slaying-of-hardcastle]], Osberne drew Boardcleaver and the blade drank blood for the first time in his hands (017-002, 017-003). Later, in [[war-of-the-dales|the great war]]s against the [[barons-league]], Osberne rode into battle with Boardcleaver at his side, the sword calling for the lives of his enemies (024-006, 024-009, 024-010).

## Significance

Boardcleaver is more than a weapon — it is a symbol of Osberne's coming of age and his transition from shepherd boy to warrior and leader. The sword represents the passing of ancient Dwarf-craft into the hands of a new generation, and serves as a tangible link between Osberne and his mysterious benefactor Steelhead. Its name, "Boardcleaver," evokes the image of a blade so mighty it can cleave through shield-walls, befitting the heroic destiny that awaits its wielder.
