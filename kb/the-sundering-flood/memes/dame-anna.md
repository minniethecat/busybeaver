# Dame Anna

Dame Anna is Elfhild's kinswoman who becomes her guardian on the west side of the Sundering Flood. After Elfhild's parents died in the great pestilence, Dame Anna took her in and raised her near the Cloven Mote (030-016).

## Role as Guardian

When Elfhild's kinswoman — likely her mother — died, "she was brought to earth by the church of Allhallows the West, hard by the place of the Cloven Mote" (030-016). In the wake of this loss, Dame Anna assumed responsibility for the young Elfhild, providing her a home in the western dale.

Dame Anna is a practical, protective presence. When Elfhild was distressed by a mysterious stranger, Dame Anna intervened directly, saying: "Heed him not, for..." (030-018). Her protective instinct and steady presence offered Elfhild stability in a world shadowed by loss and separation.

## Significance

Dame Anna represents the domestic anchor in Elfhild's life on the west side of the Sundering Flood. While Osberne had Steelhead as his supernatural benefactor, Elfhild's support came from the more ordinary but no less vital bonds of kinship, embodied by Dame Anna's guardianship.
