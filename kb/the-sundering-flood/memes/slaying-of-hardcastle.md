# Slaying of Hardcastle

The slaying of [[hardcastle]] marks Osberne's first act of mortal combat and his transformation from shepherd boy to warrior. Wielding the sword Boardcleaver, Osberne defended his home of Wethermel against the man who sought to seize it.

## Prelude

Hardcastle came to Wethermel as a guest, brought by [[surly-john]], but his intentions were hostile — he meant to take the stead for himself (015-006, 016-002). Osberne, girded with Boardcleaver and clad in scarlet, stood ready to defend his home.

## The Fight

When the confrontation came, Osberne drew Boardcleaver from its sheath and faced his enemy (017-002). The fight was fierce and close, ending with Hardcastle slain. Afterward, Osberne wiped the blood from Boardcleaver with a lap of the dead man's garment (017-003).

## Aftermath

News of the slaying reached [[elfhild]] across the [[sundering-flood]], marking a turning point in Osberne's life and his relationship with her (018-001).
