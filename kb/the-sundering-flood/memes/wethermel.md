# Wethermel

Wethermel is the re[[cloven-mote|mote]] homestead on the eastern side of the Sundering Flood where Osberne was born and spent his early years. A stead more lonely than most, its isolation was a defining force in Osberne's childhood.

## Location

Wethermel lay on the east side of the Sundering Flood, set in a countryside of scattered homesteads where neighbors were few and far between (002-001).

## Character

The stead had no great name for luck, and its fortunes had waned in recent years (002-002). Osberne's father, the master of Wethermel, had died young, leaving the household diminished (002-003).

## Significance

It was from the humble and isolated Wethermel that Osberne rose — the shepherd boy who would become a warrior known across the Dales. The loneliness of the place forged his independent spirit, and it was from Wethermel's riverbank that he first met the mysterious [[steelhead]] and began his journey toward greatness.
