# Organization Index

This page provides a complete index of all organization pages in the
wiki — churches, political bodies, newspapers, educational institutions,
and voluntary societies that figure in Matthew Arnold's argument
throughout *Culture and Anarchy*.

::: query
type: organization
display: table
fields: [label, quality, total_refs]
field_labels:
  label: Organization
  quality: Quality
  total_refs: Citations
sort: label
order: asc
:::

## About This Index

The index draws on the `type: organization` filter and renders quality
tier and citation count alongside each organization's label. All data
comes from individual page frontmatter, maintained through the standard
wiki workflow.
