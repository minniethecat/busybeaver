# British Figures

This page lists all person pages in the wiki whose nationality is
recorded as British. These are the politicians, clergy, writers,
reformers, and public intellectuals who populate Matthew Arnold's
critique of Victorian England in *Culture and Anarchy*. Arnold's
argument is deeply embedded in the particular personalities of his
country — from Gladstone and John Bright in Parliament to Bishop Wilson
and the Nonconformist divines in the churches, from Frederic Harrison
among the Positivists to the "young lions" of the *Daily Telegraph*.

## About This Index

Filtering is by `type: person` and `nationality: British`. The
nationality field is maintained on each person page's frontmatter;
coverage is 100% across all person pages. The table renders
affiliation and citation count alongside each name.
