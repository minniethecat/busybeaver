**The Levantine** is a merchant figure in Constantinople encountered during Orlando's ambassadorship. The Levant — the eastern Mediterranean region — was a zone of trade and cultural exchange between Europe and the Ottoman Empire, and the Levantine merchant embodies this cosmopolitan world that Orlando briefly inhabits.
