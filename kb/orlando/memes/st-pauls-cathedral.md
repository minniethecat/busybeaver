**St. Paul's Cathedral** is the Anglican cathedral of London, rebuilt by Sir Christopher Wren after the Great Fire of 1666. In *Orlando*, the cathedral appears as a landmark in the ever-changing London cityscape that Orlando moves through across the centuries.
