***The Faerie Queene*** (1590–1596) is Edmund Spenser's allegorical epic, the greatest poetic achievement of the Elizabethan period. It is alluded to in *Orlando* as part of the literary tradition that shapes Orlando's sensibility and the novel's engagement with the codes of chivalry and courtly love.
