***The Spectator*** (1711–1712) was a daily periodical founded by Joseph Addison and Richard Steele, whose essays on manners, literature, and philosophy shaped the taste of 18th-century England. In *Orlando*, the periodical represents the Augustan literary world through which Orlando moves.
