The **Embassy Garden at Constantinople** is a walled garden within the British Embassy compound. It provides a space of solitude and reflection for Orlando during the ambassadorship, and it is from this garden that Orlando witnesses the distant fires and tumult of the insurrection that coincides with the transformation.
