# About This Wiki

## Source Material

This wiki is based on **Orlando: A Biography**, a novel by Virginia Woolf, first published in 1928. The text follows the fantastical life of Orlando, a poet who changes sex and lives for over three centuries, meeting key figures of English literary history along the way.
