**The London Ball** refers to the society balls of 18th-century London that Orlando attends as a woman. These events provide the stage on which Orlando experiences the gendered rituals of courtship — dancing, conversation, the exchange of glances — from a perspective radically different from that of the male courtier Orlando once was.
