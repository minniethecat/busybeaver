**Mrs. Stewkley** is a domestic servant at Orlando's country estate. Like other minor characters who populate the background of Orlando's long life, she represents the continuity of domestic existence — the ordinary rhythms of service and household management that persist while Orlando undergoes extraordinary transformations.
