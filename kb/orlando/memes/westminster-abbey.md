**Westminster Abbey** is the Collegiate Church of Saint Peter at Westminster, the traditional site of English and British coronations. In *Orlando*, the Abbey is part of the London cityscape through which Orlando moves across centuries — a monument to the continuity of English institutions that the novel both celebrates and ironises.
