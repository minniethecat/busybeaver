The **Russian Embassy** (also referred to as the Muscovite Embassy) is the diplomatic mission sent by the Tsar of Russia to the court of King James I. The embassy's arrival in London brings Sasha — the Russian princess with whom Orlando falls in love during the Great Frost — into Orlando's life.
