The **Bosphorus** is the narrow strait that divides Europe from Asia at Constantinople (Istanbul), connecting the Black Sea to the Sea of Marmara. In *Orlando*, the Bosphorus serves as a geographic symbol of the boundary-crossing that defines Orlando's life: the crossing of gender, of centuries, of cultural identities.
