The **Admiralty** is the British government department responsible for the command of the Royal Navy. In *Orlando*, the Admiralty represents the institutional framework of British maritime power — the world of ships and sailors that Shelmerdine inhabits and that serves as a counterpoint to Orlando's land-bound literary life.
