**Brusa** (modern Bursa) is a city in northwestern Anatolia, in the foothills of Mount Uludağ. In *Orlando*, the hills near Brusa are where the gipsy tribe makes its camp, and where Orlando lives after leaving Constantinople following the transformation.

## In the Novel

Brusa and its surrounding countryside provide the setting for the gipsy episode — a pastoral interlude between the splendour of Constantinople and the return to English society. The landscape near Brusa is described in terms that contrast sharply with the English countryside Orlando has known, emphasising the geographical and cultural distance travelled.
