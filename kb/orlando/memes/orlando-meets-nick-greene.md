**Orlando Meets Nick Greene** refers to the first encounter between Orlando and the poet-critic who will reappear in different guises throughout the novel. Greene visits Orlando's country estate, where he is entertained as a literary guest — an encounter that inaugurates Orlando's direct engagement with the literary world.
