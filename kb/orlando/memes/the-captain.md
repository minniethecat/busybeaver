**The Captain** is the commander of the vessel *Enamoured Lady*, the ship on which Marmaduke Bonthrop Shelmerdine serves as a sailor. He appears in the novel's Victorian sections, where his ship provides the setting for Shelmerdine's maritime adventures — voyages around Cape Horn that become a recurring refrain in the latter part of the novel.

## Role in the Novel

The Captain is a peripheral figure, but his ship serves an important symbolic function: the *Enamoured Lady* and its voyages represent the masculine world of action and adventure that Shelmerdine inhabits, in counterpoint to Orlando's more contemplative, land-bound existence. The Captain's authority over the vessel mirrors the novel's broader interest in command, obedience, and the gendered division of experience.
