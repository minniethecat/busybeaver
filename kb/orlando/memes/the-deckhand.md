**The Deckhand** is an unnamed sailor serving aboard the *Enamoured Lady* under Captain Shelmerdine. Like other minor maritime figures in the novel, the deckhand represents the world of masculine labour and adventure that serves as a counterpoint to Orlando's land-bound literary life.
