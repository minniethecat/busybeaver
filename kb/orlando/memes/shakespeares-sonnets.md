**Shakespeare's Sonnets** (published 1609) are a collection of 154 sonnets by William Shakespeare, exploring themes of love, beauty, time, and immortality. The sonnets are alluded to in *Orlando* as part of the rich literary texture of the Elizabethan and Jacobean periods through which Orlando lives.
