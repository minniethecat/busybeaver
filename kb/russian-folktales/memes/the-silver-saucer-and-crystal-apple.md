# The Silver Saucer and Crystal Apple

Among the magical artifacts of Russian folklore, the silver saucer and crystal apple stand out for their function as an instrument of total vision. When rolled across the silver surface, the crystal apple reveals "all the cities and villages, all the seas and the ships, all the mountains and the forests" — the entire world contained in the motion of a fruit across a dish.

The Introduction classifies magical objects among the "common Aryan stock" of narrative elements found across Indo-European folk traditions (P02-024). The crystal apple's scrying function has parallels in the Celtic tradition of magical vessels that reveal truth.
