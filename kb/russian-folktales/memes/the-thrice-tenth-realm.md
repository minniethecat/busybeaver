# The Thrice-Tenth Realm

The thrice-tenth realm (*tridesya-toe tsarstvo*) is the destination of [[the-heros-quest|the folk hero's journey]] — a kingdom lying one step beyond the [[thrice-nine-lands]], the thirtieth realm in the cosmic enumeration. It is where quest objects reside: the [[firebird]], the [[magical-horse|golden-maned horse]], the captive princess such as [[vasilisa-the-fair]], the waters of Life and Death.

## Cosmic Geography

The thrice-tenth realm is reached only by crossing the thrice-nine lands — a journey that "only magical creatures can traverse" (020-053). The [[grey-wolf|grey Wolf]] is one such creature, carrying [[ivan-tsarevich|Iván Tsarévich]] across the cosmic distance. The realm's defining characteristic is that it cannot be reached by ordinary means: it requires supernatural transport, magical guidance, or the hero's own death and rebirth.
