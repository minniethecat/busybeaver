# Prince Vladímir

Brief person page for Phase 9 pilot.

## Folkloric Context

The Introduction identifies the bogatyrí as central figures: "He is a person of magical power and gigantic stature and prowess. Some of the bogatyrí are decidedly demigods; others more decisively human" (075-004). The word bogatýr "is only found in Russian, and in no other Slavonic language" (075-004).

The Slav peoples "conserved primitive Aryan customs almost up to the middle of the nineteenth century" (P02-026), and the tales preserve these courtly and martial figures.
