# LETTERS

[P01-002]Lady M——y W——y M——e;

###### TO

[P01-003]Persons of Distinction, Men of Letters, &c. in different PARTS of EUROPE.

[P01-004]Which contain, among other curious Relations, ACCOUNTS of the POLICY and MANNERS of the TURKS.

[P01-005]Drawn from Sources that have been inaccessible to other Travellers.
