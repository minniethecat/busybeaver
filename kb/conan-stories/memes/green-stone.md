## Overview

The Green Stone is a unique material found only on the islands of the [[vilayet-sea|Vilayet Sea]]. Remarkably hard and ironlike in quality, it was used by an unknown ancient race to construct cyclopean structures — buildings and walls of immense scale that seemed beyond human capability to shape or place.

> "Built of gigantic blocks of the peculiar ironlike green stone found only on the islands of Vilayet." [[1]](#ref-1)

## Physical Properties

The Green Stone is described as having an ironlike quality — dense, hard, and durable beyond ordinary stone. Its green coloration is distinctive and immediately recognizable. The stone's resilience is such that even a direct thunderbolt strike, which could splinter ton-heavy blocks "like so much glass" and reduce other blocks to green dust, could not entirely destroy the structures built from it.

> "The thunderbolt had splintered the ton-heavy blocks like so much glass, reduced others to green dust, and ripped away the whole arch of the dome." [[1]](#ref-1)

## Ancient Structures

The Green Stone is associated with the ruins of a forgotten civilization on the Vilayet islands. Conan encountered a broken dome-like structure built from the Green Stone — an edifice so massive that it seemed impossible for human hands to have shaped the blocks. The architecture of these ruins followed a distinctive plan: walls and towers that later builders recognized as the blueprint for structures they themselves had seen elsewhere.

> "The walls and towers followed the plan of the ruins. It was as if the crumbling lines had grown back into the structures they originally were." [[2]](#ref-2)

The [[xuchotlans|inhabitants of Xuchotl]] also possessed lapis lazuli ceilings adorned with clusters of great green stones that gleamed with a "poisonous radiance," suggesting that the Green Stone — or materials resembling it — appeared across the ancient world.

> "The vaulted ceiling was of lapis lazuli, adorned with clusters of great green stones that gleamed with a poisonous radiance." [[3]](#ref-3)

## Origins and Mystery

The origin of the Green Stone remains unknown. That it is found "only on the islands of Vilayet" suggests a geological uniqueness, but the ancient builders who quarried and shaped it are lost to history. Whether they were the predecessors of the [[hyrkanians|Hyrkanians]], a separate pre-human race, or the builders of the [[pre-cataclysmic-age|pre-Cataclysmic Age]], the Green Stone stands as a mute testament to their power and skill.
