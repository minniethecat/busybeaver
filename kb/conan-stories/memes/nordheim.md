# Nordheim

## Overview

Nordheim is the northernmost inhabited region of the Hyborian Age, a land of snow and ice lying north of the Hyborian kingdoms and Cimmeria. It is home to two related but distinct peoples: the golden-haired, blue-eyed Æsir of Asgard, and the red-haired Vanir of Vanaheim (017-025). The Nordheimir are descendants of the blond arctic savages who harried the northern Hyborian tribes in earlier ages, growing in power and numbers until they drove the remaining Hyborians from the snow countries (017-022).

## Peoples

The people of Nordheim are of "similar build, but with white skins, blue eyes and golden or red hair" (017-027). The Æsir and Vanir, though closely related, are often at war with each other — a division that the Hyborian kingdoms have historically exploited. Despite their internecine conflicts, the Nordheimir represent a growing power. Between wars with each other, the Æsir "assailed Hyperborea and pushed back the frontier, destroying city after city" (017-031).

## Military Prowess

The Nordheimir excel at ferocious hand-to-hand fighting. When Aquilonia invaded the snow countries, their heavy cavalry — which had proven irresistible against the Hyrkanians and most southern foes — was "overwhelmed by the ferocious hand-to-hand fighting of the Nordics" (017-045). The glacier ages and the "great Nordic drift" would eventually drive the Nordheimir southward, where they swept away the Pictish empire and laid the ethnic foundations of modern European peoples (017-055)(017-056).

## Related Entities

- **Hyperborea** — The ancient Hyborian kingdom that resists Nordic onslaught from the north (017-025)
- **Cimmeria** — Neighboring barbarian land, engaged in "incessant bickering along the Cimmerian borders" with Nordheim (017-031)
- **Asgard and Vanaheim** — The two kingdoms of the Æsir and Vanir respectively
