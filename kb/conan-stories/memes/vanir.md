## Overview

The Vanir are one of the two barbarian peoples of [[nordheim|Nordheim]], the snowy lands of the far north. They are distinguished by their red hair and inhabit the western region of Nordheim known as Vanaheim. Together with the yellow-haired [[Aesir]] of Asgard, the Vanir are descendants of the blond arctic savages who migrated south during the cataclysms of the [[hyborian-age|Hyborian Age]].

> "Their country is called Nordheim, and they are divided into the red-haired Vanir of Vanaheim, and the yellow-haired Æsir of Asgard." [[1]](#ref-1)

## Origins

The Vanir trace their ancestry to the same blond arctic stock that produced the northern barbarians. After the great cataclysms reshaped the world, the barbarian tribes pushed southward into the snow countries, eventually settling into two distinct branches: the red-haired Vanir and the yellow-haired Aesir.

## Distinction from Other Northerners

To the peoples of the southern climes — [[Shem]], [[Stygia]], and the Hyborian kingdoms — the distinctions between northern barbarians were largely lost. A Shemitish woman like [[Bêlit]] made no distinction between Aesir, Vanir, or [[cimmerians|Cimmerian]], seeing all northerners as ferocious blue-eyed giants who descended from their icy fastnesses with torch and sword.

> "With the unerring instinct of the elemental feminine, she knew she had found her lover, and his race meant naught, save as it invested him with the glamor of far lands." [[2]](#ref-2)

## Relations with the Aesir

The Vanir and Aesir share a common ancestry but are traditionally rivals. The two peoples war against each other, against the [[cimmerians|Cimmerians]], and against the [[hyborians|Hyborian]] kingdoms to the south — particularly [[hyperborea]], the only Hyborian kingdom that successfully resisted the barbarian onslaught from the north.

> "Now the Lemurians enter history again, as Hyrkanians. Their meaning is obscure. They push westward around the northern end of the inland sea, clash with the Nordheimr, the Hyborians, and the Stygians." [[1]](#ref-1)

## Physical Characteristics

The Vanir, like all Nordheimr, are described as blue-eyed giants and ferocious warriors. Their red hair is their most distinctive physical characteristic — setting them apart from the yellow-haired Aesir, the black-haired [[cimmerians|Cimmerians]], and the darker races of the south.
