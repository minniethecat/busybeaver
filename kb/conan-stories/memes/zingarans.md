## Overview

The Zingarans are a dark-complexioned people of the southwest, inhabiting the kingdom of Zingara which lies between Argos and the Western Ocean. Though Zingara is often grouped with the [[hyborians|Hyborian]] kingdoms, the Zingarans themselves are ethnically distinct — a people of darker skin and exotic habits, unrelated to the tawny-haired Hyborian stock.

> "Zamora lies to the east, and Zingara to the southwest of these kingdoms—people alike in darkness of complexion and exotic habits, but otherwise unrelated." [[1]](#ref-1)

## Maritime Culture

Zingara is a coastal kingdom, and the Zingarans are renowned as mariners and traders. Zingaran ships ply the waters between the ports of the southern coast, carrying trade goods and occasionally engaging in the less lawful pursuits common to the age.

> "Sheets from Zingara occasionally come and trade weapons and ornaments and wine to the coastal tribes for skins and copper ore and gold dust. Sometimes they trade ostrich plumes they got from the Stygians." [[2]](#ref-2)

Their maritime prowess extends to both peaceful trade and, on occasion, less friendly encounters. Conan himself had experience with Zingaran sailors: during his time with the Red Brotherhood, a Zingaran vessel sank his ship off the Shemitish coast.

## Physical Characteristics and Intermixing

The Zingarans are described as having a dark complexion that sets them apart from the fair-skinned Hyborian peoples of the northern kingdoms. Over centuries of contact, considerable intermixture has occurred between Zingarans and the peoples of Argos, where a Zingaran strain is noted in the population.

> "There is a strong Shemitish, even a Stygian strain among the peoples of Koth, and to a lesser extent, of Argos, while in the case of the latter, admixture with the Zingarans has been more extensive than with the Shemites." [[1]](#ref-1)

## Economy and Plantations

Southern Zingara is noted for its plantations, worked by black serfs, producing sugar and other agricultural goods. The imagery of slave-driven plantations in southern Zingara evokes a society built on conquest and exploitation — a darker side of Hyborian Age civilization.

> "the slave-drivers of the black serfs who toil in the plantations of southern Zingara" [[3]](#ref-3)

The Zingaran mountains also yield tin, which the [[picts|Picts]] historically raided, trading hides and other goods for the metal needed to forge bronze tools and weapons.

## Fate

During the fall of the Hyborian Age, Zingara was first annexed by the expanding [[aquilonians|Aquilonian]] empire, then overrun by the [[picts|Pictish]] horde that swept through Aquilonia and into the southwestern kingdoms. The Pictish empire eventually stretched from Vanaheim to the southernmost shores of Zingara.

> "a vast Pictish empire, wild, rude and barbaric, stretches from the coasts of Vanaheim in the north to the southernmost shores of Zingara." [[1]](#ref-1)

In the later post-Hyborian period, Vanir adventurers ravaged ancient Zingara and continued south into Stygia, establishing a new southern empire.
