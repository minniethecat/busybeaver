# Octavia

Octavia is a Nemedian noblewoman who appears in the Conan story "The Devil in Iron" (published in *Weird Tales*, August 1934). She is held captive on the island of Xapur by Jehungir Agha as bait to lure Conan into a trap. Over the course of the story she becomes an unwilling witness to the reawakening of the ancient demon [[Khosatral Khel]] and must navigate the dangers of the island alongside [[Conan]].

## Captivity on Xapur

Octavia is first introduced as a captive of Jehungir Agha, a lord of [[Turan|Turanian]]-held territory beyond the Vilayet Sea. She is brought to the island fortress of Dagon on [[Xapur]], where her presence is intended to draw Conan—now a pirate chief on the Vilayet—into Jehungir's ambush. Her captivity is confirmed early in the narrative:

> "Have your captive Octavia fetched here." [004-025]

Her beauty is noted by the Turanians, and she is subjected to the unwanted advances of Jehungir, who describes her as his "beautiful captive" [004-032]. Octavia reacts with visible anger and defiance:

> Octavia sprang up, her white fists clenched... [004-033]

When Jehungir proposes using her as bait to draw Conan into a trap, she protests vehemently, exclaiming that she would "never descend to such shame" [004-043]. Despite her protests, she is powerless to resist.

## Escape and Encounter with Conan

Octavia manages to escape from the Turanian camp into the jungle of Xapur. She evades the Shemite soldier sent to recapture her [004-047], fleeing through the wilderness in her torn garments. Her escape is desperate but effective—she reaches the ruined city of Dagon on her own:

> Octavia had escaped in good earnest... [004-050]

Conan finds her in the ancient fortress city. When she first sees the Cimmerian, she mechanically gasps his name, recognizing him instantly despite never having met him [004-145]. Conan—who has come to Xapur to confront Jehungir—takes Octavia under his protection, though not without mistrust.

## Witness to Khosatral Khel

Octavia becomes an unwilling witness to the reawakening of [[Khosatral Khel]], the iron-bodied demon who ruled Dagon in the pre-Cataclysmic Age. She is present in the fortress when the demon stirs, and her reactions convey the visceral horror of the supernatural:

> Octavia's resilient flesh crawled... [004-052]

As events escalate, Conan drags Octavia through the fortress, shaking her when necessary to keep her focused [004-160]. She witnesses the full terror of Khosatral Khel's pursuit:

> There were ghastly sounds, screams and moaning wails that made Octavia tremble. [004-171]

Her fear is rooted in survival instinct; Octavia senses "that grim peril lurked" [004-172] in the darkness of the fortress, and she clings to Conan as their only hope of escape.

## Escape from Xapur

Octavia witnesses the climactic battle between Conan and Khosatral Khel but does not see the entity's final fall [004-177]. Her scream at the end of the battle is not caused by the demon but by the sight of a giant serpent that blocks their path [004-178].

Conan manages to bring Octavia safely off the island. She survives the ordeal, shaken but alive—one of the few captives in the Conan stories to emerge from such supernatural peril largely unscathed. Her fate after the destruction of Xapur is not detailed in Howard's writings.
