## Overview

The Bossonians are a people of mixed Hyborian and aboriginal descent who inhabit the Bossonian marches, a buffer region between the civilized kingdom of [[aquilonia|Aquilonia]] and the savage [[picts|Pictish]] wilderness. They are renowned throughout the Hyborian world as the finest archers, and their longbows formed the backbone of Aquilonia's frontier defense for centuries.

> "Between Aquilonia and the Pictish wilderness lie the Bossonian marches, peopled by descendants of an aboriginal race, conquered by a tribe of Hyborians, early in the first ages of the Hyborian drift." [[1]](#ref-1)

## Origins and Physical Characteristics

The Bossonians originated from the intermingling of an early Hyborian tribe with an aboriginal race that predated the Hyborian migrations. This mixed heritage distinguished them from the purer [[hyborians|Hyborian]] stock of the central kingdoms.

> "This mixed people never attained the civilization of the purer Hyborians, and was pushed by them to the very fringe of the civilized world. The Bossonians are of medium height and complexion, their eyes brown or grey, and they are mesocephalic." [[1]](#ref-1)

## Way of Life

Unlike the urban Aquilonians, the Bossonians live mainly by agriculture, dwelling in large walled villages that serve as both home and fortress. Their society is more egalitarian and less stratified than that of the imperial Aquilonians, and they maintain a fierce independence despite their nominal allegiance to the Aquilonian crown.

They are skilled woodsmen as well as farmers, their proximity to the Pictish wilderness having honed their forest-craft across generations. The Bossonian longbow is legendary — capable of sending an arrow through armor at great range, it was the primary weapon that kept the Pictish hordes at bay.

> "I've seen good woodsmen from the Tauran. But the Bossonians have sheltered you Aquilonians from the outer wildernesses for too many centuries. You need hardening." [[2]](#ref-2)

## Role as Buffer State

The Bossonian marches served as Aquilonia's western shield. For centuries, Bossonian archers manned the frontier walls and repelled Pictish raids. When imperial Aquilonian troops were needed, they fought alongside Bossonian levies, but the Bossonians bore the brunt of the constant low-intensity warfare.

> "For years the superior armaments and sturdy courage of the Bossonians held the invaders at bay, aided, when necessary, by imperial Aquilonian troops." [[1]](#ref-1)

## The Bossonian Betrayal

The relationship between Aquilonia and the Bossonians soured as the empire grew more arrogant. The Aquilonians began to treat the Bossonians as conquered subjects rather than allies, imposing heavy taxes and conscripting them for distant wars of conquest from which the Bossonians gained nothing.

> "The Aquilonians had become haughty and intolerant. They derided the ruder, unsophisticated Bossonians, and hard feeling grew between them—the Aquilonians despising the Bossonians and the latter resenting the attitude of their masters." [[1]](#ref-1)

This resentment culminated in the Bossonians' desertion on the eve of a critical battle against Nemedia — a betrayal that cost Aquilonia the war. The imperial response was swift and brutal: Aquilonian regiments secretly massed on the Bossonian borders, Bossonian chiefs were massacred at a false conclave, and savage Shemitish mercenaries were quartered among the villages.

## Destruction

With the Bossonian buffer shattered, there was nothing to stop the Pictish invasion. The Picts, now led by chiefs who had served in Aquilonian armies and understood civilized warfare, swept through the marches and into Aquilonia proper.

> "This time there were no strong walled villages in their path, manned by sturdy archers, to hold back the rush until the imperial troops could be brought up. The remnants of the Bossonians were swept out of existence." [[1]](#ref-1)

The destruction of the Bossonians was a pivotal event in the fall of the Hyborian Age. Without them, the western frontier collapsed, and the [[picts|Pictish]] horde poured into the heartlands.
