# Cimmeria

Cimmeria is the homeland of [[Conan]] and his people, a land of barren, gloomy hills lying north of [[Aquilonia]]. It is a harsh country that breeds a harsh people—the Cimmerians are shaped by their environment of grey skies, drifting mists, and rugged terrain. Conan identifies himself by his homeland throughout the stories:

> "I am Conan, a Cimmerian." [012-028]

## Geography

Cimmeria occupies the hill country north of Aquilonia, bounded by the [[Bossonian Marches]] to the west and the snow lands of [[Nordheim]] farther north. The terrain is mountainous, rocky, and difficult—a landscape that has resisted Hyborian conquest and preserved Cimmerian independence for centuries:

> North of Aquilonia, the westernmost Hyborian kingdom, are the Cimmerians, ferocious savages, untamed by the invaders, but advancing rapidly because of contact with them. [017-023]

The land is poor for agriculture, offering little to attract Hyborian colonization but breeding warriors of exceptional toughness. Conan's hill-country upbringing gives him physical advantages that civilized men lack. The Venarium outpost, Aquilonia's one attempt to colonize Cimmerian territory, was overrun and destroyed by a Cimmerian horde—including a young Conan:

> The barbarians swept out of the hills in a ravening horde, without warning, and stormed Venarium with such fury none could stand before them. Men, women and children were butchered. Venarium was reduced to a mass of charred ruins, as it is to this day. [008-026]

> The Aquilonians were driven back across the marches, and have never since tried to colonize the Cimmerian country. [008-026]

## Inhabitants

The Cimmerians are descendants of the Atlanteans who survived the Cataclysm, "now progressing more steadily than their old enemies the Picts" [017-023]. Physically they are:

> tall and powerful, with dark hair and blue or grey eyes. [017-027]

Cimmerian society is organized by clans and tribes rather than kingdoms. The people live by hunting, herding, and raiding, engaging in:

> incessant bickering along the Cimmerian borders [017-031]

with the Nordheimir, Picts, and Bossonians. Their wars are described as "less invasions than mere plundering forays" [017-031], though they have occasionally raided deep into Aquilonia itself.

Conan's famous boast encapsulates the Cimmerian character—proud, fierce, and contemptuous of civilization:

> "That's what the Aquilonian renegades used to tell us Cimmerians when they came into the hills to raise an army to invade their own country. But we always fight by clans and tribes." [010-110]

## History

Cimmeria's history is one of successful resistance to outside domination. Aquilonia's attempt to subjugate the Cimmerians ended in the disastrous defeat of an imperial army sent into the hill country, where Hyborian heavy cavalry proved helpless against Cimmerian ferocity and guerrilla tactics [017-045].

The Cimmerians' Atlantean ancestry places them among the oldest surviving peoples of the Hyborian Age—contemporaries of the Picts and older than the Hyborian kingdoms themselves. Their isolation in the northern hills has preserved their racial purity:

> The barbarians have kept their bloodstream pure. [017-027]
