# Dagozai

The Dagozai are a hill tribe dwelling in the Himelian Mountains, featured in Robert E. Howard's [[conan|Conan]] story "The People of the Black Circle." They serve as scouts and spies in the complex tribal politics of the mountains, allied with [[Kerim Shah]] and the [[Irakzai]] mercenaries in the search for [[Conan]] and the captive Devi [[Yasmina]].

## Scouts of the Mountains

The Dagozai occupy a specialized niche among the Himelian hill tribes — they are scouts and intelligence-gatherers rather than warriors. Their intimate knowledge of the mountain terrain makes them invaluable to war parties passing through the region. The Wazuli chief [[Yar Afzal]] receives a report from a Dagozai scout who witnessed the aftermath of [[conan|Conan]]'s initial capture of [[yasmina|Yasmina]]:

> "I talked with a Dagozai who saw the riders last night," said the scout. "He was lurking near when they came to the spot where we ambushed the lord Conan. He overheard their speech. Chunder Shan was with them. They found the dead horse, and one of the men recognized it as Conan's. Then they found the man Conan slew, and knew him for a Wazuli. It seemed to them that Conan had been slain and the girl taken by the Wazuli; so they turned aside from their purpose of following to Afghulistan." [005-214]

This passage reveals the Dagozai's mode of operation — lurking near events, overhearing conversations, and carrying intelligence back to their allies. The scout's account shapes the tactical decisions of the [[wazuli|Wazulis]] and the Kshatriya soldiers alike.

## Alliance with Kerim Shah

The Dagozai work alongside the [[irakzai|Irakzai]] mercenaries under [[kerim-shah|Kerim Shah]]'s command:

> "The Dagozai did not know; with him were thirty Irakzai of the lower villages. They rode into the hills and disappeared." [005-214]

This alliance places the Dagozai in the orbit of the [[turan|Turan]]ian agent's network, serving as the eyes and ears of his operations in the Himelian passes. Their role is subordinate to the [[irakzai|Irakzai]] — while the [[irakzai|Irakzai]] are fighting men, the Dagozai provide the intelligence that guides the fighters.

## The Ambush at Shalizah Pass

The Dagozai are involved in the ambush at Shalizah Pass, a critical turning point for [[kerim-shah|Kerim Shah]]'s band. The "singing arrows of the ambushed Dagozai" drive the [[irakzai|Irakzai]] back from the pass [005-291], contributing to the fatalistic decision that compels them to follow [[conan|Conan]] to Yimsha rather than attempt to fight their way home.

## Tribal Position

The Dagozai occupy a precarious position in Himelian tribal politics. They are neither strong enough to stand alone against the major powers — the [[vendhya|Vendhyan kingdom]], the [[afghuli|Afghulis]], the [[wazuli|Wazulis]] — nor entirely subservient to any single faction. Their scouting skills give them leverage, as all parties need intelligence about mountain movements, but their dependence on more powerful allies for military protection makes them vulnerable.
