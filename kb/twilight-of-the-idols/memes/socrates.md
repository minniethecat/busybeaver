# Socrates (Concept)

## Definition

A significant concept in Nietzsche's philosophical system, discussed in *Twilight of the [[idol|Idol]]s*.

## In *Twilight of the Idols*

Nietzsche treats this concept in connection with his broader critique of Western philosophy and morality. The concept serves as an important element in his diagnosis of cultural decadence and his project of the revaluation of all values.
