# Idol

## Definition

A concept articulated in Nietzsche's *Twilight of the Idols*, forming part of his critique of Western philosophy and morality.

## In *Twilight of the Idols*

Nietzsche deploys this concept in his analysis of philosophical errors, cultural decadence, and the revaluation of values. It connects to his broader project of diagnosing and overcoming nihilism.
