# The Pirate Chase

## Summary

During the return [[voyage-and-sailing|voyage]] from [[africa-expedition|Africa]], [[john-dolittle|Doctor Dolittle]]'s [[voyage-and-sailing|ship]] is pursued by the [[barbary-coast|Barbary]] pirates in their swift red-sailed vessel. When the sluggish [[voyage-and-sailing|ship]] — a parting gift from [[prince-bumpo|Prince Bumpo]] — cannot outrun the pursuers, [[polynesia]] summons thousands of swallows who pull [[john-dolittle|the Doctor]]'s [[voyage-and-sailing|ship]] to safety with a thousand ropes. The party later sneaks aboard the abandoned pirate vessel and claims it for their own, turning the tables on their would-be captors.

## Context

The pirate chase occurs as [[john-dolittle|the Doctor]] and his animals sail home after their successful mission to [[africa-expedition|Africa]]. They must pass [[barbary-coast|the coast of Barbary]] — the seashore of the Great Desert, a wild and lonely place of sand and stones. The [[barbary-coast|Barbary]] pirates were notorious for preying on passing [[voyage-and-sailing|ships]], robbing them, and throwing prisoners into the sea if no ransom was paid (013-001, 013-002).

## Narrative

### Detection

One sunshiny day, as a fresh wind blew the boat along with everyone in good spirits, [[dab-dab|Dab-Dab]] spotted a red sail on the horizon. "I don't like the look of that sail," she said. "I have a feeling it isn't a friendly [[voyage-and-sailing|ship]]" (013-004).

Meanwhile, [[jip-the-dog|Jip]] was napping in the sun — and talking in his sleep. "I smell roast beef cooking — underdone roast beef — with brown gravy over it," he mumbled. The Doctor was astonished: the roast beef was on a [[voyage-and-sailing|ship]] ten miles away, yet [[jip-the-dog|Jip]] was smelling it in his sleep (013-006, 013-011). Then [[jip-the-dog|the dog]]'s dream turned darker:

> "I smell bad men — the worst men I ever smelt. I smell trouble. I smell a fight — six bad scoundrels fighting against one brave man. I want to help him. Woof-oo-woof!" (013-014)

[[jip-the-dog|Jip]] woke himself with his own barking. The [[voyage-and-sailing|ship]] drew nearer, its three red sails unmistakable. "They are surely the pirates of [[barbary-coast|Barbary]]," [[jip-the-dog|Jip]] declared (013-016).

### The Chase

The Doctor ordered all available sails hoisted, but the [[voyage-and-sailing|ship]] [[prince-bumpo|Prince Bumpo]] had given them was hopelessly slow. [[gub-gub|Gub-Gub]] lamented: "Might as well try to win a race in a soup-tureen as hope to get away from them in this old barge. Look how near they are now! — You can see the mustaches on the faces of the men — six of them" (013-020).

Desperate, [[john-dolittle|the Doctor]] sent [[dab-dab|Dab-Dab]] to consult [[the-swallow-messenger|the swallows]]. Their solution was ingenious: unravel lengths of rope into thin strings, tie them to the front of the [[voyage-and-sailing|ship]], and let [[the-swallow-messenger|the swallows]] pull. Two thousand swallows — each pair gripping a string — took flight, and in an instant [[john-dolittle|the Doctor]] found himself [[travel-and-exploration|traveling]] so fast he had to hold his hat on with both hands (013-022, 013-024).

> The ship itself seemed to fly through waves that frothed and boiled with speed. (013-024)

The animals laughed and danced in the rushing air as the red sails shrank to specks on the horizon. Between the pirates' greed and [[the-swallow-messenger|the swallows]]' generosity, speed had decided the day.

### Turning the Tables

After the escape, [[john-dolittle|the Doctor]]'s party reached land. Under cover of darkness, guided by the rats' warning that the pirates had abandoned [[voyage-and-sailing|ship]], they crept aboard the swift red-sailed vessel (014-028). The pirate [[voyage-and-sailing|ship]] — far superior to [[prince-bumpo|Prince Bumpo]]'s gift — became their new transport home.

### The Barbary Dragon

The final confrontation came when the pirates attempted to return. The pushmi-pullyu, startled, sneezed so loudly that the pirates mistook the sound for the roar of a dragon and fled in terror (015-002). The chase had come full circle: the hunted had become the hunters, and the pirates' own [[voyage-and-sailing|ship]] now carried [[john-dolittle|the Doctor]] homeward.
