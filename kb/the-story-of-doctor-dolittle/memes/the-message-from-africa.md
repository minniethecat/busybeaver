# The Message from Africa

## Summary

One cold winter night, while [[john-dolittle|the Doctor]] and his animals sat around the kitchen fire, a swallow arrived at the window bearing dire news from [[africa-expedition|Africa]]. The monkey population was being devastated by a terrible sickness, and they desperately needed [[john-dolittle|the Doctor]]'s help. Despite the enormous distance and the harshness of the winter, [[john-dolittle|the Doctor]] resolved to go — a decision that would launch his most famous [[travel-and-exploration|adventure]].

## The Swallow's Arrival

That winter was bitterly cold. The Doctor and his animal family — [[jip-the-dog|Jip]] [[jip-the-dog|the dog]], [[gub-gub|Gub-Gub]] [[gub-gub|the baby pig]], [[polynesia|Polynesia the parrot]], and [[too-too|Too-Too the owl]] — sat huddled around the warm kitchen fire. Suddenly [[chee-chee|Chee-Chee]], [[chee-chee|the monkey]], cried out that a swallow was outside the window, nearly perished from the cold.

> "A swallow," said Chee-Chee. "She is outside the window — she has nearly perished with the cold. The swallows flew South six weeks ago!" (004-006, 004-007)

They brought the shivering bird inside and warmed her. When she recovered, she delivered her message — a cousin of [[chee-chee|Chee-Chee]]'s in [[africa-expedition|Africa]] had sent word that a terrible sickness was sweeping through [[chee-chee|the monkey]] population.

> "I've just had a message from a cousin of mine in Africa. There is a terrible sickness among the monkeys out there." (004-004)

## The Decision

"Is there a terrible sickness among [[chee-chee|the monkeys]]?" [[john-dolittle|the Doctor]] asked [[chee-chee|Chee-Chee]]. The monkey confirmed the grim news. The monkeys in [[africa-expedition|Africa]] were growing worried as more and more of their kind fell ill (005-005). They had heard of the wonderful Doctor of [[puddleby-on-the-marsh|Puddleby-on-the-Marsh]] who could speak the language of animals and cure their ailments, and they begged him to come to [[africa-expedition|Africa]] and save them (005-036).

Without hesitation, [[john-dolittle|the Doctor]] resolved to make the long and dangerous [[travel-and-exploration|journey]]. He borrowed a boat and supplies, and with his animal companions, set sail following [[the-swallow-messenger|the swallow]] who guided them across the rolling sea (005-002).

## Outcome

The message from [[africa-expedition|Africa]] transformed [[john-dolittle|Doctor Dolittle]] from a local animal doctor into a figure of international significance. The [[travel-and-exploration|journey]] that followed would test his courage, expand his reputation, and cement his legacy as the greatest animal doctor the world has ever known.
