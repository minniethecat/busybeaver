## Summary

While [[voyage-and-sailing|sailing]] home from [[africa-expedition|Africa]], [[john-dolittle|the Doctor]]'s [[voyage-and-sailing|ship]] is spotted and chased by [[barbary-coast|Barbary]] pirates in a swift red-sailed vessel. When the wind alone cannot outrun the pirates, [[john-dolittle|the Doctor]] enlists the help of migrating swallows—two thousand of them—who pull the [[voyage-and-sailing|ship]] by strings to safety, far outpacing the pirates and reaching the [[canary-islands|Canary Islands]].

## Context

After their long stay in [[africa-expedition|Africa]] healing the sick monkeys and finding the [[pushmi-pullyu|Pushmi-Pullyu]] ((012-000)), [[john-dolittle|Doctor Dolittle]] and his animals set sail for home across the sea ((013-000)). They were [[voyage-and-sailing|sailing]] along the [[barbary-coast|Barbary Coast]]—a region notorious for pirates who would capture [[voyage-and-sailing|shipwrecked]] sailors and hold them for ransom, or throw them into the sea if no [[money-and-wealth|money]] came ((013-001)–(013-002)).

## Narrative

### The Pirate Chase

One day, [[jip-the-dog|Jip]] spotted a boat coming after them with a red sail—the mark of the [[barbary-coast|Barbary]] pirates ((013-003), (013-016)). The Doctor and his animals knew the pirates would be swift and dangerous. [[jip-the-dog|Jip]] hurried below and dragged up every sail he could find to catch the wind. But even with all sails raised, "the boat did not go nearly as fast as the pirates'—which kept coming on behind, closer and closer" ((013-018)–(013-019)).

### The Swallows' Plan

In desperation, [[john-dolittle|the Doctor]] asked [[dab-dab|Dab-Dab]] to fly up and tell [[the-swallow-messenger|the swallows]] flying overhead that pirates were coming after them, and to ask what he should do ((013-021)).

> "When the swallows heard this, they all came down on to the Doctor's ship; and they told him to unravel some pieces of long rope and make them into a lot of thin strings as quickly as he could." ((013-022))

The animals set to work unraveling ropes into thin strings.

### Two Thousand Swallows

The ends of the strings were tied to the front of the [[voyage-and-sailing|ship]]. The swallows took hold of the strings with their feet and flew off, pulling the boat along ((013-022)). Though a single swallow is not particularly strong, two thousand swallows pulling together made a mighty force:

> "And there, tied to the Doctor's ship, were a thousand strings; and two thousand swallows were pulling on each string—all terribly swift fliers." ((013-023))

The animals on the [[voyage-and-sailing|ship]] laughed and danced in the rushing air as they looked back and saw the pirates' red sails growing smaller and smaller, "being left far, far behind" ((013-025)).

### Rest at the Canary Islands

After two or three hours of hard work dragging the [[voyage-and-sailing|ship]], [[the-swallow-messenger|the swallows]] grew "tired in the wings and short of breath" ((014-001)). They told [[john-dolittle|the Doctor]] they needed a rest and would pull the boat to a nearby island to hide it in a deep bay. The [[voyage-and-sailing|ship]] was safely tucked into a hidden bay on what turned out to be the [[canary-islands|Canary Islands]], where the canaries led [[john-dolittle|the Doctor]] to a beautiful spring of cool water ((014-018)–(014-021)).

### A Second Alarm

While the company rested and ate, two swallows came hurrying up, "very flustered and excited" ((014-023)). They reported that the pirates had found the bay and boarded [[john-dolittle|the Doctor]]'s [[voyage-and-sailing|ship]]. Rushing back to the shore, the company saw the pirates' red-sailed [[voyage-and-sailing|ship]] standing empty in the water—all the pirates were below deck on [[john-dolittle|the Doctor]]'s [[voyage-and-sailing|ship]], searching for things to steal ((014-027)).

This discovery set the stage for [[john-dolittle|the Doctor]]'s counter-attack: he and his animals crept silently onto the pirates' own [[voyage-and-sailing|ship]] ((014-028)).

## Participants

- **[[john-dolittle]]**: Devises the plan to enlist [[the-swallow-messenger|the swallows]]
- **[[dab-dab]]**: Flies up as messenger to [[the-swallow-messenger|the swallows]]
- **The swallows**: Two thousand migrating swallows who pull the [[voyage-and-sailing|ship]] by strings
- **The [[barbary-coast|Barbary]] pirates**: In pursuit with red sails
- **The canaries**: Welcome the company to the [[canary-islands|Canary Islands]] and lead them to water ((014-021))
- **[[jip-the-dog|Jip]]**: Spots the pirates' red sail and gathers extra sails ((013-016)–(013-018))

## Outcome & Consequences

- The Doctor's [[voyage-and-sailing|ship]] escaped the pirates through the collective strength of two thousand swallows
- The company made landfall at the [[canary-islands|Canary Islands]] for rest and water
- The pirates later found the hidden [[voyage-and-sailing|ship]], setting up a reversal in which [[john-dolittle|the Doctor]] and his animals snuck aboard the pirates' own vessel ((014-028))
- The successful coordination with [[the-swallow-messenger|the swallows]] established a pattern—[[john-dolittle|the Doctor]] would later call on them again to pull the [[voyage-and-sailing|ship]] when [[voyage-and-sailing|sailing]] home ((018-000))
