# Too-Too

Too-Too the owl is the intellectual of [[doctor-dolittle-m-d|Doctor Dolittle]]'s animal household. Where [[gub-gub|Gub-Gub]] feels and [[dab-dab|Dab-Dab]] does, Too-Too thinks — he keeps the accounts, notices what others miss, and whispers strategic counsel in moments of crisis.

## The Household Accountant

Too-Too is introduced as one of [[john-dolittle|the Doctor]]'s core animals:

> …Polynesia the parrot, and the owl Too-Too. (001-004)

After [[sarah-dolittle|Sarah Dolittle]] leaves, the animals organize a self-sufficient household, and Too-Too finds his calling:

> …the owl, Too-Too, was to keep the accounts… (003-019)

His mathematical aptitude is noted as exceptional:

> And the owl, Too-Too, who was good at arithmetic, worked out… (003-017)

| Duty | Skill |
|------|-------|
| Accounts | Arithmetic and bookkeeping |
| Strategy | Perceptive observation |
| Night watch | Owl's nocturnal vision |

## The Observer

Too-Too's nature as an owl — perceptive, silent, nocturnal — shapes his role. He speaks when he has something important to say:

> …the owl, Too-Too, suddenly said… (004-001)

His observations carry weight precisely because he does not waste words.

## Strategic Advisor

During the confrontation with [[ben-ali|the Barbary Dragon]], Too-Too provides crucial tactical advice:

> But the owl, Too-Too, whispered to the Doctor… (015-006)

Whispering is Too-Too's signature mode — he advises quietly, from the shadows, embodying the owl's traditional association with wisdom rather than action.

## Limitations

Like several of his companions, Too-Too has physical limitations that constrain him during the [[africa-expedition|African voyage]]:

> …the pig and the dog and the owl, Too-Too, could do nothing in such heat. (005-003)

He is a creature of temperate [[puddleby-on-the-marsh|Puddleby]], not tropical [[africa-expedition|Africa]]. His value lies not in physical endurance but in strategic intelligence.

## The Voyage and Return

Too-Too is one of the select animals on the [[travel-and-exploration|expedition]] and returns with [[john-dolittle|the Doctor]]:

> …Jip and the owl, Too-Too, went on to the ship with the Doctor. (012-033)

His presence on the return [[voyage-and-sailing|voyage]] reinforces his status as a core companion — not a specialist like [[dab-dab|Dab-Dab the diver]] but a generalist thinker whose value is constant.

## Significance

Too-Too represents the intellectual dimension of [[john-dolittle|the Doctor]]'s household. He balances the emotionalism of [[gub-gub|Gub-Gub]], the physicality of [[dab-dab|Dab-Dab]], and the scheming of [[polynesia]] — the household's resident brain.
