# Bumpo's Transformation

## Summary

While imprisoned by [[king-of-the-jolliginki|the King]] of [[jolliginki|Jolliginki]], [[john-dolittle|Doctor Dolittle]] received a secret nighttime visit from [[prince-bumpo|Prince Bumpo]]. The Prince, who had long dreamed of becoming a white prince like those in his fairy-tale books, begged [[john-dolittle|the Doctor]] to use his [[medicine-and-magic|magic]] to change his appearance. In exchange for [[john-dolittle|the Doctor]]'s help, [[prince-bumpo|Bumpo]] unlocked the prison and set the entire animal party free. The Doctor mixed a basin of [[medicine-and-magic|medicines]], [[prince-bumpo|Bumpo]] dipped his face in, and his skin turned "as white as snow" — though [[john-dolittle|the Doctor]] later worried the effect would not last (012-003)(012-017)(012-021)(012-026).

## Context

The Doctor and his animals had been thrown into prison by [[king-of-the-jolliginki|the King]] of [[jolliginki|Jolliginki]], [[prince-bumpo|Bumpo]]'s father, who was furious that the white strangers had not immediately complied with his demands. [[polynesia|Polynesia the parrot]], however, had spotted their escape route: she overheard [[prince-bumpo|Prince Bumpo]] snoring and realized that [[prince-bumpo|the Prince]] — an ardent believer in fairy tales — could be persuaded to help (006-019).

[[prince-bumpo|Prince Bumpo]] had a lifelong obsession with Europe's fairy tales. Years earlier, he had gone in search of The Sleeping Beauty, "whom I had read of in a book," [[travel-and-exploration|traveling]] across the world only to find that she was already married and had children. The memory still pained him, and he remained convinced that the white men possessed [[medicine-and-magic|magical]] powers (012-008).

## Narrative

### The Secret Visit

Late at night, [[prince-bumpo|Prince Bumpo]] came secretly to [[john-dolittle|the Doctor]] in prison. [[polynesia|Polynesia]], who had been watching, whispered to [[john-dolittle|the Doctor]]: "[[prince-bumpo|Prince Bumpo]] is coming here tonight to see you. And you've got to find some way to turn him white. It's the only way you'll get out of prison" (012-003).

The Doctor, ever the rationalist, protested. "It isn't so easy to turn a black man white. You speak as though he were a dress to be re-dyed" (012-004). But [[polynesia|Polynesia]] was insistent: "You *must* turn this coon white. Think of a way — think hard!" (012-005).

The Prince arrived and made his plea directly:

> "White Man, I am an unhappy prince. Years ago I went in search of The Sleeping Beauty, whom I had read of in a book. And after traveling many days, I found her and kissed her and she awoke. But when she saw my face she cried out and ran away. I want to be a white prince, so that I may find her and win her love." (012-008)

### The Bargain

The Doctor, looking thoughtfully at the bottles in his [[medicine-and-magic|medicine]] bag, negotiated carefully. "Supposing I made your hair a beautiful blonde?" he suggested. [[prince-bumpo|Bumpo]] refused — nothing less than full transformation would satisfy him. He wanted to "wear shining armor and gauntlets of steel, like the other white princes" (012-010)(012-012).

When asked how much of his body needed to be white, [[prince-bumpo|Bumpo]] replied: "Yes, all over — and I would like my eyes blue too, but I suppose that would be very hard to do" (012-014).

The Doctor accepted the terms. He mixed many [[medicine-and-magic|medicines]] in a basin — "a lot of [[medicine-and-magic|medicines]]" — and instructed [[prince-bumpo|Bumpo]] to dip his face in it (012-017). The Prince leaned down and submerged his face "right up to the ears" (012-018).

### The Result

When [[prince-bumpo|the Prince]] finally lifted his face from the basin, breathing hard, all the animals cried out in surprise. "The Prince's face had turned as white as snow, and his eyes, which had been mud-colored, were a manly gray!" (012-021)

[[prince-bumpo|Bumpo]] was overjoyed. He begged to keep [[john-dolittle|the Doctor]]'s looking-glass — the only one in [[king-of-the-jolliginki|the Kingdom]] of [[jolliginki|Jolliginki]] — so he could admire his transformation at every opportunity (012-023). True to his word, [[prince-bumpo|the Prince]] took a bunch of copper keys from his pocket, undid the great double locks, and released [[john-dolittle|the Doctor]] and all his animals into the night (012-024).

## Participants

- **[[prince-bumpo|Prince Bumpo]]** — the fairy-tale-obsessed heir to the throne of [[jolliginki|Jolliginki]], desperate to become a white prince
- **[[john-dolittle|John Dolittle]]** — the imprisoned Doctor, who used his medical knowledge to create a temporary chemical transformation
- **[[polynesia|Polynesia]]** — [[polynesia|the parrot]] who devised the escape plan and coached [[john-dolittle|the Doctor]] through the negotiation
- **[[dab-dab|Dab-Dab]]** — [[dab-dab|the duck]] who brought the basin for mixing the [[medicine-and-magic|medicines]]

## Outcome & Consequences

The transformation succeeded spectacularly — but [[john-dolittle|the Doctor]] had his doubts. Watching [[prince-bumpo|Bumpo]] lean against the garden wall as the party crept down to the seashore, he confided:

> "I feel sorry about Bumpo. I am afraid that medicine I used will never last. Most likely he will be as black as ever when he wakes up in the morning." (012-026)

The Doctor's animals reminded him that [[prince-bumpo|Bumpo]] deserved no sympathy — they had been imprisoned on [[king-of-the-jolliginki|the King]]'s orders, not his — but [[john-dolittle|the Doctor]] still wondered "if I ought to go back and tell him" the temporary nature of the spell (012-026)(012-029).

[[prince-bumpo|Bumpo]]'s transformation enabled the escape from [[jolliginki|Jolliginki]] and the party's return to the seashore, where they would board a [[voyage-and-sailing|ship]] and begin the long [[voyage-and-sailing|voyage]] home. The episode also revealed an uncomfortable truth about the colonial world [[john-dolittle|the Doctor]] inhabited: [[prince-bumpo|Bumpo]]'s deepest wish — to be white — reflected the fairy-tale logic of a European imagination imposed upon an [[africa-expedition|African]] prince.
