# Canary Islands

The Canary Islands appear briefly in [[doctor-dolittle-m-d|Doctor Dolittle]]'s return [[voyage-and-sailing|voyage]] from [[africa-expedition|Africa]] — a real-world archipelago that serves as a geographic waypoint and a marker of [[john-dolittle|the Doctor]]'s progress toward home. Unlike the fictional [[jolliginki|Jolliginki]] or the dangerous [[barbary-coast|Barbary Coast]], the Canaries represent a recognizable point on the map, grounding the fantasy in real geography.

## Sighting

The islands are first spotted and identified by [[dab-dab|Dab-Dab]]:

> "Why, these are the Canary Islands," said Dab-Dab. "Don't you know that?" (014-018)

[[dab-dab|Dab-Dab]]'s recognition of the islands reflects [[dab-dab|the duck]]'s practical intelligence — she knows geography, not just water.

## A Contemplative Pause

The Doctor's response to the islands is contemplative rather than practical:

> …the Doctor sat in the stern of the ship with his pipe in his mouth, watching the Canary Islands fade away in the blue distance. (016-002)

This is one of the book's quietest and most atmospheric moments — [[john-dolittle|the Doctor]], pipe in mouth, watching islands recede. It is not a scene of action but of reflection, a pause between the dangers of [[barbary-coast|Barbary]] and the comforts of home.

## Real and Imagined Geography

The Canary Islands are one of the few explicitly real locations in the book's geography. Their inclusion alongside fictional places like [[jolliginki|Jolliginki]] creates a blended map — [[john-dolittle|the Doctor]]'s world is both our own and an imagined one. The Canaries [[voyage-and-sailing|anchor]] the fantasy in navigational reality.

## Significance

The Canary Islands serve a quiet but important narrative function:

1. **Geographic realism**: A real place on a real map, grounding the [[travel-and-exploration|adventure]]
2. **Pacing**: A moment of calm between the perils of [[barbary-coast|Barbary]] and the homecoming
3. **Character**: The Doctor's silent contemplation reveals his inner life
