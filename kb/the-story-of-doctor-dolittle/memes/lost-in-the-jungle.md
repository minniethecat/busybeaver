# Lost in the Jungle

After escaping from the [[king-of-the-jolliginki|King of the Jolliginki]]'s dungeon with the help of [[prince-bumpo|Prince Bumpo]], [[john-dolittle|Doctor Dolittle]] and his animals attempted to make their way to the seashore to find passage home. Their [[travel-and-exploration|journey]] through [[africa-expedition|the African jungle]], however, took a perilous turn.

## The Separation

While passing through a very thick part of the forest, [[chee-chee|Chee-Chee]] went ahead of the party to look for coconuts. In his absence, "[[john-dolittle|the Doctor]] and the rest of the animals, who did not know the jungle-paths so well, got lost in the deep woods" (011-006). Unlike [[chee-chee|Chee-Chee]], who knew "all the paths through the jungle — better even than [[king-of-the-jolliginki|the King]]'s men did" (007-002), [[john-dolittle|the Doctor]] and the other animals had no sense of direction in the dense [[africa-expedition|Africa]]n forest.

## Days of Wandering

The party "wandered around and around but could not find their way down to the seashore" (011-006). [[chee-chee|Chee-Chee]], when he realized they were missing, was "terribly upset." He "climbed high trees and looked out from the top branches to try and see [[john-dolittle|the Doctor]]'s high hat; he waved and shouted; he called to all the animals by name. But it was no use. They seemed to have disappeared altogether" (011-005).

The situation became increasingly desperate:

> At last, after blundering about like this for many days, getting their clothes torn and their faces covered with mud, they walked right into the King's back-garden by mistake.
>
> (011-007)

## Recapture

The King's men came running up at once and caught them. [[polynesia|Polynesia]] later explained to [[chee-chee|Chee-Chee]] what had happened: "The Doctor and all the animals have been caught by [[king-of-the-jolliginki|the King]]'s men and locked up again. We lost our way in the jungle and blundered into the palace-garden by mistake" (011-010). [[chee-chee|Chee-Chee]] was furious with [[polynesia|the parrot]] for letting them get lost while he was away searching for coconuts (011-011).

## Significance

This episode demonstrates [[john-dolittle|the Doctor]]'s vulnerability outside his element — for all his gifts with animals and [[medicine-and-magic|medicine]], he is helpless in the [[africa-expedition|Africa]]n wilderness without his animal companions' specialized knowledge. It also sets up the second imprisonment and the events that lead to [[prince-bumpo|Prince Bumpo]]'s transformation and the eventual escape.
