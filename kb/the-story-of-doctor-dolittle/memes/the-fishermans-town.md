# The Fisherman's Town

> "And when they had come to the land which the man had spoken of, they saw a little fishing-town at the foot of a rocky mountain." (020-013)

## Description

The Fisherman's Town is a small fishing village situated at the foot of a rocky mountain on the coast. It is a modest settlement — a fishing-village — where [[the-fisherman|the red-haired fisherman]] and his sister (the mother of the little boy) make their home (020-013).

The village has a beach where the great [[voyage-and-sailing|ship]] with red sails could [[voyage-and-sailing|anchor]], and a hill overlooking the sea where the [[the-fisherman|fisherman]]'s sister sat watching for twenty days, waiting for her brother's return (020-014).

## Role in the Story

After [[john-dolittle|Doctor Dolittle]] rescued [[the-fisherman|the red-haired fisherman]] from the Rock, he asked [[the-swallow-messenger|the swallow]]s to guide the [[voyage-and-sailing|ship]] to the [[the-fisherman|fisherman]]'s home. Upon arrival, the [[the-fisherman|fisherman]]'s sister came running down to the shore, laughing and crying at the same time, having waited and watched for twenty days (020-014).

> "She had been sitting on a hill for twenty days, watching the sea and waiting for them to return." (020-014)

The townspeople were so grateful for [[john-dolittle|the Doctor]]'s defeat of [[ben-ali|the Barbary Dragon]] that they insisted he stay for two and a half days. During this time:

- The Doctor was invited to teas, luncheons, dinners, and parties (020-020)
- Ladies sent boxes of flowers and candies (020-020)
- The village-band played tunes under his window every night (020-020)
- The little boys of the village gathered on the beach to admire the captured pirate [[voyage-and-sailing|ship]] (020-018)

> "Look! That was a pirate-ship﻿—Ben Ali's﻿—the most terrible pirate that ever sailed the Seven Seas!" (020-019)

## The Mayor's Ceremony

Before [[john-dolittle|the Doctor]]'s departure, the Mayor of the town, accompanied by pageboys with shining trumpets, presented gifts in a formal ceremony (020-024, 020-025):

- **To [[john-dolittle|Doctor Dolittle]]**: a perfectly beautiful watch with real diamonds in the back (020-026)
- **To [[jip-the-dog|Jip]]**: a dog-collar made of solid [[money-and-wealth|gold]], inscribed with "[[jip-the-dog|Jip]]﻿—The Cleverest Dog in the World" (020-030, 020-031)

> "It is a great pleasure for me to present to the man who rid the seas of the Dragon of Barbary this little token from the grateful people of our worthy Town." (020-025)

## Significance

The Fisherman's Town represents the gratitude of ordinary people freed from the terror of piracy. It is the setting for one of [[john-dolittle|the Doctor]]'s most formal recognitions — a civic ceremony honoring his quiet heroism. The town also marks the final stop before [[john-dolittle|the Doctor]]'s return [[voyage-and-sailing|voyage]] to [[puddleby-on-the-marsh|Puddleby]].
