# Story Overview

Hugh Lofting's *The Story of [[john-dolittle|Doctor Dolittle]]* unfolds across three natural arcs: life in [[puddleby-on-the-marsh|Puddleby]] before the [[travel-and-exploration|journey]], the [[travel-and-exploration|expedition]] to [[africa-expedition|Africa]] to cure [[chee-chee|the monkey]]s' sickness, and the return [[voyage-and-sailing|voyage]] with its [[travel-and-exploration|adventure]]s and discoveries. This page maps the key wiki entries onto that narrative structure.

## Part I — Puddleby

Before the great [[travel-and-exploration|journey]], [[john-dolittle|Doctor Dolittle]] lives quietly in the little town of [[puddleby-on-the-marsh|Puddleby-on-the-Marsh]], learning the language of animals from [[polynesia|Polynesia the parrot]] and building his unusual household.

## Part II — The African Expedition

News arrives that [[chee-chee|the monkey]]s of [[africa-expedition|Africa]] are dying of a terrible sickness. [[john-dolittle|Doctor Dolittle]], his animal companions, and a crew set sail on a [[voyage-and-sailing|voyage]] that takes them through storms, [[voyage-and-sailing|ship]]wreck, and [[king-of-the-jolliginki|the King]]dom of [[jolliginki|Jolliginki]] to the [[land-of-the-monkeys|Land of the Monkeys]].
