# The World of Doctor Dolittle

Hugh Lofting's story spans two continents and the sea between them. [[john-dolittle|Doctor Dolittle]]'s world extends from his quiet house in [[puddleby-on-the-marsh|Puddleby-on-the-Marsh]], with its famous garden and animal hospital, to the exotic shores of [[africa-expedition|Africa]] — [[king-of-the-jolliginki|the King]]dom of [[jolliginki|Jolliginki]], the [[land-of-the-monkeys|Land of the Monkeys]], and the [[barbary-coast|Barbary Coast]] where pirates roam.
