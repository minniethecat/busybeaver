# Chief Chimpanzee

The Chief Chimpanzee was a leader among the monkeys of the African jungle. He presided over [[the-monkeys-council|the Monkeys' Council]] called to discuss [[john-dolittle|Doctor Dolittle]]'s unexpected decision to return to [[puddleby-on-the-marsh|Puddleby]] rather than remain with the monkeys who wished to honor him.

## Role in the Council

When the monkeys gathered to discuss why the Doctor was leaving, the Chief Chimpanzee rose first to address the assembly. "Why is it the good man is going away?" he asked. "Is he not happy here with us?" (009-003, 009-004)

Later, upon learning from [[chee-chee|Chee-Chee]] that money was required for food and shelter in the [[land-of-the-white-men|Land of the White Men]], the Chief Chimpanzee turned to the Oldest Orangutan in astonishment:

> "Cousin, surely these Men be strange creatures! Who would wish to live in such a land? My gracious, how paltry!"
>
> (009-015)
