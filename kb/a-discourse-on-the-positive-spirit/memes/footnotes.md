This work was published 1830--1842, with the title of *Course of Positive Philosophy*, because it was based upon a course of lectures delivered 1826--1829. But since that time I have always given it the more appropriate name of System. Should the work reach a second edition, the correction will be made formally: meanwhile, this will, I hope, remove all misconception on the subject.
