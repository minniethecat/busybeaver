# Frederic Harrison

**Frederic Harrison** (1831–1923) was an English jurist, historian, and leading English adherent of [[positivism]]. He wrote the Introduction to the 1908 edition of [[Auguste Comte]]'s *A General View of [[positivism|Positivism]]*, in which he presented Comte's system to English readers.

Harrison was a prominent public intellectual in Victorian and Edwardian England, serving as president of the English Positivist Committee and advocating for Comte's philosophy in numerous essays and books.
