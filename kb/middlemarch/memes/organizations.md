# Organizations

Middlemarch is populated not only by individuals but by the institutions they form, join, and contest. From parish vestries and medical boards to election committees and newspapers, these organizations constitute the "web" of collective life that Eliot traces with sociological precision. This page indexes every documented organization in the wiki.
