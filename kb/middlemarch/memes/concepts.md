# Concepts

Beneath its narrative surface, Middlemarch is a novel of ideas. Eliot weaves together questions of morality, epistemology, politics, and science — the "web" of connections that binds individual lives to the larger movement of history. This page indexes every documented concept in the wiki, from "sympathy" and "vocation" to "Reform" and "provincial life."
