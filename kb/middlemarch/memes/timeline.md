# Timeline

The action of Middlemarch unfolds across a few pivotal years in the early 1830s, against the backdrop of the Reform Bill debates, medical innovation, and quiet provincial dramas. This page lists every documented event in approximate chronological order, each linking to a full narrative account with causes, participants, and consequences.

## Narrative Arc

The events cluster around four major plotlines: Dorothea Brooke's moral and romantic education, Tertius Lydgate's medical ambitions and marital disillusionment, Fred Vincy's coming-of-age, and the exposure of Nicholas Bulstrode's hidden past. Together they form a panoramic portrait of provincial life in an age of quiet transformation.
