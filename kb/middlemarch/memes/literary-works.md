# Literary Works

Middlemarch is a novel saturated with other books. Characters quote Shakespeare, argue theology with Milton, study medicine with Bichat, and measure their lives against the legends of Dante and the paradigms of classical antiquity. This page indexes every literary and artistic work referenced in the novel, from Homer to the latest scientific treatise.
