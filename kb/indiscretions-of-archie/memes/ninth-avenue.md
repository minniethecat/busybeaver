# Ninth Avenue

A Manhattan thoroughfare where [[archie-moffam|Archie Moffam]] walks in despair following the disastrous injury to [[looney-biddle|Looney Biddle]]'s left hand.

## In the Novel

After the Looney Biddle catastrophe, Archie goes out into the street and walks pensively "down a now peaceful Ninth Avenue" (020-009), contemplating the ruin of his fortunes and the loss of [[lucille-brewster|Lucille]]'s birthday present. Ninth Avenue serves as the backdrop to Archie's darkest moment, its peacefulness contrasting with the turmoil of his thoughts.
