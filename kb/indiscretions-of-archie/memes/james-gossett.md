# James Gossett

James Gossett is a motion-picture industry figure who appears in the novel's cultural episodes. His mention connects the world of the Hotel Cosmopolis to the broader landscape of 1920s American entertainment. Archie describes him succinctly: "He's a motion-picture man" (026-185).

## Role in the Novel

Gossett represents the expanding cultural horizon of Jazz Age America, where the motion-picture industry was emerging as a dominant force. His presence in the novel places the Cosmopolis within this larger cultural moment, where hotel lobbies and picture-palaces were competing venues for the attention of the American public (026-185).
