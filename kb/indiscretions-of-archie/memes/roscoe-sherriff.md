# Roscoe Sherriff

Roscoe Sherriff appears in the novel's business episodes, a figure whose life Archie imagines in characteristically colorful terms. He is mentioned as the subject of a "picture of Roscoe Sherriff's life as it was imagined by Archie" (006-108) — one of several minor characters whose primary function is to populate the social and commercial world around which the plot revolves.

## Role in the Novel

Sherriff serves as background texture in the novel's business-world episodes. His name and implied circumstances suggest the network of commercial relationships that form the backdrop to Archie's adventures (006-062).
