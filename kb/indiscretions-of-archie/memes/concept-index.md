# Concept Index

This page provides a complete, automatically generated index of every [[concept]] page in the wiki. Concepts range from thematic motifs (honor, class) to social practices (elopement, tipping) to recurring comic devices (the Englishman abroad). The table is rendered dynamically — new concept pages are included automatically.

::: query
type: concept
display: table
fields: [label, domain, quality, total_refs]
field_labels:
  label: Concept
  domain: Domain
  quality: Quality
  total_refs: References
sort: label
order: asc
:::

## Notes

- The **Domain** field categorizes the concept: social, cultural, historical, literary, or sports.
- The **References** column counts inbound links from other wiki pages.
- Concepts are the wiki's connective tissue — they link people, places, and events through shared themes.
