# The Art Purchase Negotiation

The art purchase negotiation is a characteristic Wodehouse comic set-piece. Archie, acting as an intermediary in a painting transaction, manages to misunderstand every detail of the deal while somehow keeping all parties hopeful. "The business negotiations in connection with the picture were beginning to take shape" (010-118) — beginning, but never quite arriving.

## Summary

The episode follows the classic Wodehouse pattern: Archie's good intentions create chaos, his transparency disarms suspicion, and the negotiation collapses and revives in cycles of comic misunderstanding. The painting itself becomes almost irrelevant; what matters is the dance of personalities around it.
