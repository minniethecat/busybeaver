# Parker

Parker is part of the staff infrastructure of the Hotel Cosmopolis, one of the unseen hands that maintain the establishment of which Daniel Brewster is so proud. His presence, like that of other service characters, contributes to the textured social world of the hotel.

## Role in the Novel

As a hotel staff member, Parker represents the operational reality behind Brewster's boast that "at the Cosmopolis things never went wrong" (001-093). The staff are the mechanism by which Brewster's exacting standards are maintained.
