## 位置与环境

The Wrychester Golf Club occupied the golf-links on Wrychester Common, a stretch of open land on the outskirts of the city. (022-001) The clubhouse stood on the Common, accessible by footpaths that made it a regular destination for walkers from the Close. (010-041)

[[mary-bewery|Mary Bewery]] was a regular golfer who walked to the links nearly every morning to play. (010-042) The course was also frequented by other Wrychester residents, including [[sackville-bonham|Sackville Bonham]]. (022-002)

## Bryce's Confrontation with Mary Bewery

The golf club was the setting for a pivotal encounter between [[pemberton-bryce|Pemberton Bryce]] and Mary Bewery. Following his dismissal from Dr. Ransford's service, Bryce waylaid Mary as she walked towards the golf clubhouse across the Common, ambushing her at a wicket gate on the footpath. (010-041)

> She set out, alone, for the Wrychester Golf Club. It was her habit to go there almost every day, and Bryce was well acquainted with her movements and knew precisely where to waylay her.
>
> (010-041)

> "Do you call this manly conduct, Dr. Bryce?" she demanded, turning an indignant and flushed face on him. "To waylay me here, when you know that I don't want to have anything more to do with you. Let me through, please — and go away!"
>
> (010-042)

During this encounter, Bryce insinuated that Dr. Ransford might have been involved in the death of [[john-braden|John Braden]] — planting the seeds of suspicion that would complicate the investigation and strain the relationship between Mary and her guardian. Mary, deeply shaken by the suggestion, played no golf that morning. (010-042)

## The Glassdale Sighting

The golf-links later figured in the pursuit of Glassdale, the ex-convict whose testimony was crucial to unraveling the Paradise mystery. Bryce, having identified Glassdale at [[saxonsteade-arms|the Saxonsteade Arms]], rode out to the golf-links on the city outskirts to locate him. (022-001) The pursuers who tracked Glassdale subsequently lost his trail at the golf-links, where Bryce had last left him. (026-049)
