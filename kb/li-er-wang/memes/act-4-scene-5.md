[025-001]*Enter Regan and Oswald.*

[025-002]REGAN. But are my brother’s powers set forth?

[025-003]OSWALD. Ay, madam.

[025-004]REGAN. Himself in person there?

[025-005]OSWALD. Madam, with much ado. Your sister is the better soldier.

[025-006]REGAN. Lord Edmund spake not with your lord at home?

[025-007]OSWALD. No, madam.

[025-008]REGAN. What might import my sister’s letter to him?

[025-009]OSWALD. I know not, lady.

[025-010]REGAN. Faith, he is posted hence on serious matter. It was great ignorance, Gloucester’s eyes being out, To let him live. Where he arrives he moves All hearts against us. Edmund, I think, is gone In pity of his misery, to dispatch His nighted life; moreover to descry The strength o’ th’enemy.

[025-011]OSWALD. I must needs after him, madam, with my letter.

[025-012]REGAN. Our troops set forth tomorrow; stay with us; The ways are dangerous.

[025-013]OSWALD. I may not, madam: My lady charg’d my duty in this business.

[025-014]REGAN. Why should she write to Edmund? Might not you Transport her purposes by word? Belike, Somethings, I know not what, I’ll love thee much. Let me unseal the letter.

[025-015]OSWALD. Madam, I had rather—

[025-016]REGAN. I know your lady does not love her husband; I am sure of that; and at her late being here She gave strange oeillades and most speaking looks To noble Edmund. I know you are of her bosom.

[025-017]OSWALD. I, madam?

[025-018]REGAN. I speak in understanding; y’are, I know’t: Therefore I do advise you take this note: My lord is dead; Edmund and I have talk’d, And more convenient is he for my hand Than for your lady’s. You may gather more. If you do find him, pray you give him this; And when your mistress hears thus much from you, I pray desire her call her wisdom to her. So, fare you well. If you do chance to hear of that blind traitor, Preferment falls on him that cuts him off.

[025-019]OSWALD. Would I could meet him, madam! I should show What party I do follow.

[025-020]REGAN. Fare thee well.

[025-021]<div style="text-align:right">[Exeunt.]</div>
