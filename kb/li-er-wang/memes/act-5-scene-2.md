[030-001]*Alarum within. Enter with drum and colours, Lear, Cordelia and their Forces, and exeunt.*

[030-002]*Enter Edgar and Gloucester.*

[030-003]EDGAR. Here, father, take the shadow of this tree For your good host; pray that the right may thrive: If ever I return to you again, I’ll bring you comfort.

[030-004]GLOUCESTER. Grace go with you, sir!

[030-005]<div style="text-align:right">[Exit Edgar.]</div>

[030-006]*Alarum and retreat within. Enter Edgar.*

[030-007]EDGAR. Away, old man, give me thy hand, away! King Lear hath lost, he and his daughter ta’en: Give me thy hand; come on!

[030-008]GLOUCESTER. No further, sir; a man may rot even here.

[030-009]EDGAR. What, in ill thoughts again? Men must endure Their going hence, even as their coming hither; Ripeness is all. Come on.

[030-010]GLOUCESTER. And that’s true too.

[030-011]<div style="text-align:right">[Exeunt.]</div>
