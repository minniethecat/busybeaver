## Profile

Chief Inspector Mitchell is a senior officer at [[scotland-yard|Scotland Yard]] and the immediate superior of [[joseph-french|Inspector Joseph French]]. Described as "an exceedingly shrewd man" with "a perfectly uncanny way of delving to the bottom of problems and of seeing [[evidence|clue]]s that other people missed" [2861-2861], Mitchell plays the role of the wise and supportive superior who assigns [[joseph-french|French]] to the [[starvel|Starvel Hollow]] case and provides strategic guidance throughout the investigation.

## Role in the Narrative

Chief Inspector Mitchell first appears when he summons [[joseph-french|Inspector French]] to his office and assigns him to investigate the [[starvel|Starvel Hollow]] tragedy. His opening remark — "The people down there don't appear to know much about it, and the whole thing may turn out to be a mare's nest" [574-574] — proves prescient, as [[joseph-french|French]] himself later wonders whether this was "another of his chief's amazing intuitions" [574-574].

Mitchell provides steady oversight as [[joseph-french|French]]'s investigation unfolds. When [[joseph-french|French]] presents the evolving case, Mitchell listens carefully and authorizes key investigative steps, including the trip to France to trace [[pierce-whymper|Pierce Whymper]]'s background. He greets [[joseph-french|French]]'s progress with good-humoured support, at one point teasing "Pulling your leg, [[joseph-french|French]]" before endorsing the overseas investigation [1068-1068].

In the novel's closing chapters, Mitchell plays a crucial role by wiring an urgent recall to [[joseph-french|French]] [2861-2861] — a summons that initially causes [[joseph-french|French]] anxiety but ultimately leads to a critical breakthrough in the case. The Chief Inspector receives [[joseph-french|French]]'s final reports with satisfaction, and [[joseph-french|French]] himself dreams of one day succeeding Mitchell as Chief Inspector of the C.I.D. [2195-2195].

## Key Appearances

- **Chapter V**: Assigns [[joseph-french|French]] to the [[starvel|Starvel Hollow]] case [530-530].
- **Mid-investigation**: Reviews [[joseph-french|French]]'s progress and authorizes the [[joseph-french|French]] inquiry [1062-1068].
- **Chapter XXIX**: Wires an urgent recall that leads to the case's resolution [2861-2861].
- **Final chapters**: Receives [[joseph-french|French]]'s concluding reports [2927-2943].
