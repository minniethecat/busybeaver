## Profile

Major Valentine serves as the Chief Constable of the County and is a key figure in the escalation of the [[starvel|Starvel Hollow]] investigation. Though his direct appearances are limited, his decisions shape the entire course of the case. Described as a man of discernment, he quickly recognizes that [[starvel-hollow-fire|the Starvel tragedy]] involves complexities beyond the capacity of the local constabulary, and his application to [[scotland-yard|Scotland Yard]] for assistance brings [[joseph-french|Inspector French]] into the investigation.

## Role in the Narrative

Major Valentine enters the story when [[tarkington|Mr. Tarkington]] and [[oxley|Mr. Oxley]] bring their suspicions to his attention. After hearing their statement, the Chief Constable "was impressed by the statement" and "listened with the keenest interest, interjecting only an occasional 'By Jove!'" [202-202]. He thanks the two men for their information and promises to institute inquiries without delay.

Two days later, Valentine writes to Tarkington with significant developments. He confirms that "his belief that the safe was fire[[evidence|proof]] was well founded" and reveals that he "strongly suspected that more had taken place at Starvel on that tragic night than had come out in [[the-inquest|the inquest]]" [202-202]. Crucially, he concludes that "the matter was rather outside the local men's capacity" and has applied to Scotland Yard for help [202-202].

This decision — to recognize the limitations of local resources and call in outside expertise — is the turning point that transforms the [[starvel|Starvel Hollow]] case from a local tragedy into a Scotland Yard investigation led by [[joseph-french|Inspector French]]. Valentine's professional humility and sound judgment set the entire second half of the novel in motion.

## Relationships

- **[[sergeant-kent|Sergeant Kent]]**: Kent is Valentine's subordinate in the county constabulary, handling the initial field response.
- **[[joseph-french|Inspector French]]**: Valentine requests [[joseph-french|French]]'s assistance from Scotland Yard, effectively handing over the investigation to a more experienced detective.
- **[[tarkington|Mr. Tarkington]]**: Valentine corresponds confidentially with Tarkington about the progress of the investigation, acknowledging the bank manager's crucial role in uncovering the financial angle.
- **[[oxley|Mr. Oxley]]**: Acquaintance through the investigation.

## Key Appearances

- **Chapter III onward**: Receives Tarkington and Oxley's statement, launches inquiries, and corresponds with Tarkington about developments [202-202].
- **Meeting at the Junior Services Club in [[leeds|Leeds]]**: Valentine joins Tarkington and Oxley for a private conference over "whiskies and sodas" and "excellent cigars" [202-202].
