## Overview

The General Post Office (GPO) was the British government department responsible for postal and telecommunications services. In *The Starvel Hollow Tragedy*, the General Post Office in Edinburgh is mentioned as the point of origin of a crucial telegram in the final phase of the investigation [019-038].

## Role in the Investigation

The General Post Office in Edinburgh enters the narrative near the climax of French's pursuit of [[john-roper|John Roper]]. French, working with the solicitors [[dashwood-and-munce|Dashwood and Munce]], has set a trap for the fugitive, believing that Roper will make contact to claim his inheritance.

After sending a wire to the Edinburgh solicitors, French waits anxiously at his hotel for a reply. At about four in the afternoon, a telegram arrives. It was handed in at the General Post Office in Edinburgh [019-038]. The message — "Will meet Munce as suggested" — reveals that Roper has taken the bait, and the trap is about to spring. This telegram, routed through the Edinburgh GPO, marks the turning point that leads directly to Roper's capture.
