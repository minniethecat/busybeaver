# Communism

## Definition

In [[jean-grave|Grave]]'s anarchist analysis, communism is the economic system in which the means of production are held in common and the products of labor are distributed according to need. It is the necessary corollary of the abolition of private property — once the institutional basis of individual appropriation is removed, "the products of the earth" are sufficient for all, and the scarcity that drives competition is revealed as an artifact of property relations rather than a natural condition [019-031]. The anarchist conception of communism differs from authoritarian versions in one fundamental respect: it is inseparable from liberty and [[association|free association]], rejecting [[government|the state]] as an instrument of economic organization.

## Private Property as the Root of Inequality

[[jean-grave|Grave]] grounds his argument for communism in a critique of private property. He contends that private property "is accessible only to those who exploit their fellows" — a historical fact demonstrated by the transformation of common holdings into exclusive domains through [[force-and-violence|force]] and legal artifice [005-021]. [[private-property|Property]] is not a natural right but a social institution that has "rested upon other bases than those upon which it is today supported" and "owes its present destiny only to [[force-and-violence|force]], conquest, [[force-and-violence|violence]], and ruse" [006-005]. The economists, [[jean-grave|Grave]] argues, have taken social facts resulting from centuries of class domination and "set them up as 'natural [[the-law|law]]s,' making them the cause of what is actually only their effect" [005-010].

> Private property is accessible only to those who exploit their fellows. The history of humanity shows that this form of property was not that of the first human aggregations. [005-021]

## Free Association and Collective Organization

For [[jean-grave|Grave]], the transition from private property to communism does not proceed through the seizure of [[the-state|state]] power but through the voluntary [[association|federation]] of [[association|free association]]s. The anarchist vision replaces the coercive apparatus of [[government|the state]] and capital with "[[free-association|cooperative association]]s for production and consumption" — organizations that grow organically from the solidarity inherent in social life [001-007]. Under such conditions, production ceases to be driven by the profit motive and is instead directed toward human need.

> The reign of solidarity must replace that of competition. But can anyone seriously hope to establish solidarity while maintaining the cause which engenders competition? [016-009]

The abolition of private property creates the material conditions for a new social order. When no one may exclude others from the means of subsistence, "nobody need fear for the morrow," and the structural antagonism that pits worker against worker dissolves [010-014]. The collective strength of associated producers replaces the atomized competition of the market.

## Communism and Authority

[[jean-grave|Grave]] is careful to distinguish anarchist communism from [[the-state|state]] socialism. He recognizes that institutions claiming to represent the collective — [[government|the state]], the [[the-law|law]], the magistracy — "flow directly from private property" and serve to defend it [001-010]. A communism imposed by authority would reproduce the very relations of domination it claims to abolish. [[anarchy|Anarchy]], as "the negation of authority," requires that communism be realized through free agreement, not [[the-state|government]]al decree [001-002]. The "common need of solidarity" that leads people to associate is fundamentally incompatible with the hierarchical organization of society [008-008].

Anarchist ideas developed with "energy and rapidity" precisely because they address "those who suffer" — offering not the distant promise of electoral reform but the im[[press|media]]te possibility of organizing production and consumption on cooperative lines without waiting for permission from above [001-010].
