# Proletariat

The **proletariat** is the class of wage laborers who, lacking ownership of the means of production, must sell their labor power to the [[bourgeoisie]] in order to survive. In Jean Grave's anarchist analysis in *Moribund Society and Anarchy*, the emancipation of the proletariat requires not the seizure of [[the-state]] — which would merely replace one set of rulers with another — but the abolition of the entire system of [[capitalism]], [[private-property]], and [[authority]] that produces class division.

## The Condition of the Proletariat

Grave describes the systematic immiseration of the working class under capitalism. Every technological [[progress]], every increase in productivity, benefits only the possessing class:

> "Every fresh step in progress profits him, and him only, who has the wherewithal to put it into operation. Does there arise a new invention which enables production to go on more rapidly and with less expense? He who possesses will profit by it to diminish his working force, the eliminated part of which, thrown upon the street, will go to swell the somber army of the unemployed, the starving." [020-738]

Those who remain employed see their wages driven down by [[competition]] with the unemployed:

> "The rest will continue to toil as hard, as long, as before, even seeing their wages reduced in consequence of the competition waged against them by those who have been thrown out of the workshop." [020-738]

## Beyond State Socialism

Grave's anarchism distinguishes itself from state socialism precisely in its analysis of the proletariat's path to emancipation. The Marxist strategy of seizing state power merely perpetuates the cycle of domination:

> "Hence it is not a question of saying or writing that the laborer is exploited; it is necessary to explain to him above all how in changing masters he does not cease to be exploited, and how, were he to put himself in his master's place, he would in turn become an exploiter, leaving behind him the exploited." [001-108]

The [[revolution]] Grave envisions is not a transfer of property but the abolition of the very conditions that make property a source of power over others. The proletariat's liberation is inseparable from the liberation of all humanity from [[authority]].
