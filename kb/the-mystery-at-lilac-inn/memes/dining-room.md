# Dining Room

The dining room at Lilac Inn is the primary setting for the opening events of the mystery. It is where Nancy Drew and Emily Crandall dined, and crucially, where the theft of Mrs. Willoughby's handbag occurred.

## Description

The dining room was a popular establishment. When Nancy and Emily entered, "they saw that the dining room was crowded" (001-022). Despite the crowd, the head waiter recognized Nancy and escorted them to a desirable table. The room offered "an excellent view of the lake and the grounds" (001-023), making it an attractive venue.

## The Luncheon

Nancy and Emily enjoyed their meal in the dining room, discussing old times and the circumstances that brought them together. After finishing, they "left the dining room, pausing outside the door" (001-057) before Nancy went to make her telephone call.

## The Theft

The dining room later became the scene of the crime when Mrs. Willoughby's handbag, containing the Crandall jewels, was stolen from her table. The crowded nature of the room complicated the investigation, as many potential suspects had been present.
