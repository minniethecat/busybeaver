# Dick

## Overview

Dick Farnham is Emily Crandall's fiancé, whose engagement to Emily is revealed early in the novel. Emily tells Nancy: "I'm engaged to Dick Farnham. You've met him, Nancy" (004-041). His connection to the Crandall family places him within the circle affected by the jewel theft.

## Relationship with Emily

Dick and Emily wish to marry, as Emily explains: "Dick and I would like to get married" (004-043). Nancy recalls that "Dick has excellent chances" (004-044) of success, suggesting he is a promising young man.

## Connection to the Jewels

Emily hopes that "a few of those jewels" might help "set Dick up in business" (004-051), revealing that the Crandall jewels are tied to the couple's future plans. When the jewels are stolen, Emily fears that "she won't be able to help Dick" (007-013).
