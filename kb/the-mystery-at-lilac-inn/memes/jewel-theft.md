# The Jewel Theft at Lilac Inn

## Overview

The theft of the Crandall jewels at Lilac Inn is the central crime in *The Mystery at Lilac Inn*. The jewels, being transported by Mrs. Willoughby and Mrs. Potter, are stolen during a luncheon, setting in motion the investigation that drives the novel's plot.

## The Events

Mrs. Willoughby and Mrs. Potter arrive at Lilac Inn for luncheon, carrying the valuable Crandall jewels (005-005). Mrs. Willoughby carries the jewels in a handbag with an ornate clasp that attracts attention (005-011). Despite her precautions and her nervous command to Mrs. Potter that "no one must know I have it" (005-006), the theft occurs.

## Discovery and Aftermath

The theft is discovered after the meal, and the news shocks the community. Nancy Drew learns that "the Crandall jewels" have been "stolen" (007-011), and the revelation devastates Emily Crandall. The police investigation immediately begins, with suspicion falling on Mrs. Willoughby.
