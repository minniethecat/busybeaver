# Employment Agency

## Overview

The employment agency in River Heights serves as a background institution in *The Mystery at Lilac Inn*. It is connected to the "manager of the best employment agency" (002-027). The agency provides a crucial service for the community, matching workers with employers. and provides domestic staff referrals for households in the area.

## Role in the Story

The agency represents the institutional infrastructure of River Heights. Hannah Gruen interacts with the agency, and characters seeking domestic work—including those with suspicious motives—may pass through its doors. Nancy speculates that someone "might find work at Lilac Inn" (010-076) through the agency, noting that "they're nearly always looking" for staff. This connection becomes significant as Nancy traces the movements of suspects. through the agency (010-076).
