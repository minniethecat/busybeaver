# Hannah Gruen

## Overview

Hannah Gruen is the devoted housekeeper of the Drew household, having cared for Nancy Drew since she was a child. She provides domestic stability and maternal care, allowing Nancy and her father to focus on their respective investigations and legal work.

## Role in the Household

Hannah manages the Drew household with competence and dedication. Carson Drew mentions her in his daily interactions, reminding her to "catch your train, Hannah" (002-017). The household revolves around her domestic management, and preparations intensify "before Judge Graham's arrival" (001-070) and coordinating household matters with her. Her presence frees Nancy to pursue her detective work without domestic concerns.

## Mother Figure

Having served the Drew family since Nancy's childhood, Hannah functions as a mother figure in the household. The household aims to be ready for guests, coordinating schedules around significant visits (002-025). Her loyalty and care create the emotional foundation that supports both Carson Drew's legal career and Nancy's investigative adventures.
