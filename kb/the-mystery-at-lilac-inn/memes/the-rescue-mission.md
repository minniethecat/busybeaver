# The Rescue Mission

## Overview

A dramatic rescue mission forms part of the climactic action in *The Mystery at Lilac Inn*. When a character is trapped in a sinking vessel with "little hope of a rescue" (022-032), Nancy Drew is prepared "if need be, to attempt a rescue herself" (022-055).

## The Crisis

The rescue unfolds on the water, where dangerous conditions test the limits of those involved. Some witnesses are paralyzed by fear, lacking "the courage to attempt a rescue" (022-007), but Nancy's determination prevails.
