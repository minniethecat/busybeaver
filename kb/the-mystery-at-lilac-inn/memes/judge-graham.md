# Judge Graham

## Overview

Judge Graham is a prominent figure in the legal community and a family friend of the Drews. His impending weekend visit to River Heights creates a sense of urgency in the Drew household, as Hannah Gruen and Nancy prepare for his arrival.

## The Expected Visit

Judge Graham is described as "one of the big" legal figures (001-062), and his planned visit requires the Drew household to prepare. Nancy is told that she "must get busy" preparing for the visit (001-066). The household aims to be ready "by the time Judge Graham arrived for his weekend visit" (002-025).

## Significance

While Judge Graham does not directly participate in the mystery's resolution, his visit serves as a narrative backdrop, adding domestic urgency to the Drew household. His prominence in legal circles underscores the Drew family's connections to the justice system.
