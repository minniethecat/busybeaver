# Tom

## Overview

Tom is a character whose involvement in the Crandall jewels theft is uncovered through Nancy Drew's persistent investigation. His role in the criminal conspiracy becomes clear as the mystery unfolds.

## Connection to the Crime

Tom is linked to Mary Mason and the theft of the Crandall jewels. Evidence gathered by Nancy reveals that Mary Mason had stolen the Crandall jewels and that "her brother and Tom" participated in the scheme (019-048). The conspirators had "plenty to get your hands on the Crandall jewels" (021-026) as their motive. His involvement places him among the conspirators whose actions drive the central mystery.

## The Investigation

Nancy's investigation gradually reveals Tom's role in the plot. She works to prove that "Mary Mason had stolen the Crandall jewels" (019-012) and that Tom aided in the crime. As she pieces together the evidence, his connection to the criminal network behind the theft becomes apparent. The resolution of the case depends on exposing his involvement and recovering the stolen items.
