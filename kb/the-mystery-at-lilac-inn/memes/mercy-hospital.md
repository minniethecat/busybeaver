**Mercy Hospital** is the medical institution serving [[River Heights]] and its surroundings. While never depicted directly — no scene takes place inside its walls — it serves as an indispensable background node in the [[automobile-accident]] investigation chain, anchoring the witness-verification phase.

## Role in the Narrative

### Accident Response

When the [[automobile-accident]] occurs outside [[lilac-inn-business|Lilac Inn]], the victims are rushed to the hospital by two unidentified diners:

> "After the victims had been rushed to the hospital, all came back except these two." (008-024)

The two Good Samaritans who transported the injured to the hospital become persons of interest — their departure raises the question of whether they were connected to the [[jewel-theft|jewel theft]].

### The Witness Verification

A researcher working for [[carson-drew|Carson Drew]] contacts Mercy Hospital to identify the two men who brought in the victims:

> "I called the Mercy Hospital this morning and learned that the victims of the automobile accident were brought there by two persons who had been dining here." (009-052)

[[nancy-drew|Nancy Drew]] follows up herself the next morning:

> "The next morning Nancy telephoned the hospital and through the officials learned the names of the two men who had brought in the victims of the automobile accident." (010-003)

The two men tell "a straightforward story" about driving to the accident scene and doing what needed to be done (010-003). The hospital becomes an administrative witness — its records provide the factual bedrock for clearing the two men of suspicion.

### Elimination

> "The two who took the auto victims to the hospital are out of the picture. They had a perfect alibi." (011-046)

With the hospital-referred witnesses cleared, the investigation must narrow its focus elsewhere. The hospital's function — providing an authoritative roster of who was present at a critical moment — is exhausted, and the narrative moves on.

## Narrative Functions

1. **Administrative witness** — the hospital's records provide authoritative identification that no character could independently supply
2. **Investigation gate** — verifying the ambulance Good Samaritans is a necessary step before pursuing other leads
3. **Institutional background** — Mercy Hospital fills in the civic infrastructure of River Heights without demanding scene-level depiction
