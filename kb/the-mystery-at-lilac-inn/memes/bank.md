# The Bank

## Overview

The bank in River Heights plays a crucial role in the events leading up to the jewel theft. Mrs. Willoughby states that she "intends to place them in a bank vault immediately" (002-044), indicating the bank's role as the intended secure repository. The Crandall jewels were kept in the bank's vault for safekeeping before being removed—a decision that set in motion the chain of events leading to their theft at Lilac Inn.

## The Vault

Nancy Drew visits "the bank vault where the Crandall jewels were kept" (017-028) as part of her investigation. Mrs. Willoughby had been advised "to leave them in the bank vault" (004-027) rather than carrying them, a warning that tragically went unheeded. The bank's security measures and the circumstances under which the jewels were removed become important elements of the case.
