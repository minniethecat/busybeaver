# Bud Mason

## Role in the Story

Bud Mason is Mary Mason's brother and an active participant in the criminal conspiracy operating out of Lilac Inn. Unlike his sister Mary, who appears to have been drawn into the scheme under pressure, Bud is depicted as a willing confederate — physically menacing and directly involved in the capture and confinement of Nancy Drew. He works alongside Tom Tozzle and the other criminals, pushing back chairs, staggering through cabins, and stepping out into storms as part of the operation to transport and guard their captive (019-051, 021-046, 021-066).

Bud Mason's role in the story is straightforward: he is muscle for the criminal operation. When he and Mary are eventually apprehended (022-039), the investigation confirms their active participation in the conspiracy.

## Appearances

- **Chapter XIX (019-051–053)**: Bud Mason pushes back his chair abruptly as the conspirators' meeting ends. He turns his face toward the storeroom where Nancy is being held captive.
- **Chapter XX (020-051)**: When an escape opportunity is proposed, Mary and Bud Mason do not share the speaker's enthusiasm — suggesting their deeper investment in keeping Nancy confined.
- **Chapter XXI (021-046, 021-066)**: Bud Mason comes "staggering into the cabin" during a storm, affected by unpleasant meditation. Later, wrapping his slicker more tightly about him, he steps back out into the storm.
- **Chapter XXII (022-030, 022-039)**: After transporting their captive, Mary and Bud Mason assume Nancy has been left securely confined. By the chapter's end, they are apprehended.

## Key Actions

- **Participates in the criminal conspiracy** (019-051): Present at the confederates' meeting, actively involved in planning.
- **Guards the captive Nancy Drew** (019-053, 020-051): Takes a direct role in surveilling and confining Nancy.
- **Transports the prisoner during the storm** (021-046–066, 022-030): Moves Nancy from the inn to the boat during dangerous weather conditions.
- **Is apprehended** (022-039): Captured along with his sister Mary when the authorities close in.
