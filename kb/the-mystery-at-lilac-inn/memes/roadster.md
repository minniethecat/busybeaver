# Roadster

Nancy Drew's roadster is a bright blue, low-swung automobile that appears throughout the mystery at Lilac Inn. It serves as her primary means of transportation as she travels between locations investigating the jewel theft.

## Description

The roadster is described as "a bright blue roadster, low-swung and smart" that "rolled swiftly along the winding lake road" (001-001). Its distinctive blue color matched Nancy's attire — she wore "a frock which either by accident or design exactly matched the blue of the automobile" (001-001).

## Role in the Investigation

Nancy used her roadster extensively during the investigation. She drove to Lilac Inn, where she "guided the car into the side road" and "swung the roadster into line with the row of automobiles parked in the yard" (001-003). The roadster carried her to Dockville, to the Mason residence, and to other key locations as she pursued leads.

The automobile represented Nancy's independence and mobility, allowing her to conduct her detective work across the region without relying on others for transportation.
