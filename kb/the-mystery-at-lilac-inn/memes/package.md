# Package

Packages delivered to Mary Mason in Dockville serve as an important clue in Nancy Drew's investigation of the Crandall jewels theft. The deliveries helped establish Mary's connections and activities.

## The Delivery Discovery

While investigating in Dockville, Nancy observed a delivery driver who "opened the rear doors and took out a number of packages. They were all sizes and shapes" (013-013). She then watched as "Mary Mason took the packages" (013-015) and brought them inside, confirming Mary's presence at the address and her receipt of goods.

## Investigative Value

The packages and their delivery provided Nancy with concrete evidence of Mary Mason's whereabouts and activities. By tracking the delivery, Nancy was able to establish patterns that contributed to solving the case.
