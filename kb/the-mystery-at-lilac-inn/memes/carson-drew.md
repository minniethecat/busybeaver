# Carson Drew

## Overview

Carson Drew is Nancy Drew's father and a prominent criminal and mystery-case lawyer. He serves as both a mentor and source of support for Nancy throughout her investigation of the Crandall jewels theft. He is described as greeting Nancy warmly, beginning phone calls with "Hello, Nancy" (001-060).

## Professional Background

As a "famous criminal and mystery-case lawyer" (002-021), Carson Drew maintains a busy practice in River Heights. His work frequently brings him into contact with criminal cases, and Nancy has often been present when he "discussed important cases with his colleagues" (002-022), absorbing the knowledge that would serve her in her own investigations.

## Role in the Mystery

Carson Drew provides crucial guidance to Nancy during her investigation. He advises her to investigate thoroughly before making accusations (014-046) and helps her analyze evidence. His legal expertise gives Nancy access to resources and perspectives that aid her detective work.

## Key Relationships

- **Nancy Drew** — His daughter, whom he mentors in investigative techniques
- **Hannah Gruen** — The Drew family housekeeper
