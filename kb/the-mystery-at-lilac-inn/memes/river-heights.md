# River Heights

## Overview

River Heights is the hometown of Nancy Drew and the primary setting for much of *The Mystery at Lilac Inn*. Nancy drives to and from River Heights throughout the novel as she investigates events at nearby Lilac Inn (001-007). It serves as Nancy's base of operations as she investigates the jewel theft at nearby Lilac Inn.

## Location

River Heights is situated near Lilac Inn, within driving distance. The main road connecting River Heights to Melville passes by Lilac Inn (002-007), making it a convenient stop for travelers between the two towns.

## The Drew Household

The Drew family home in River Heights is where Nancy lives with her father, Carson Drew. He greets her with characteristic warmth: "Hello, Nancy" (001-060), as she returns from her adventures.
