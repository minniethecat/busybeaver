# Mrs. Sadie Carter

## Role in the Story

Mrs. Sadie Carter is the Drew family's housekeeper, described as "an elderly woman who suited her in every way" (004-003). She manages the Drew household with quiet efficiency — preparing meals, answering the door, and relaying messages. Though she plays no direct role in the mystery, she provides the stable domestic backdrop against which Nancy's investigations unfold. She is "neat in appearance" and quickly proves her worth to the household (004-004).

## Appearances

- **Chapter IV (004-003–004)**: Mrs. Carter is hired into the Drew household. Nancy's father and she find her to be a good fit — neat, capable, and well-suited to the work.
- **Chapter VII (007-067)**: Mrs. Carter appears in the outside hall with a calling card, discreetly announcing a visitor.
- **Chapter VIII (008-002)**: She politely offers a visitor a chair before discreetly withdrawing, demonstrating her proper household manners.
- **Chapter XI (011-008, 011-010, 011-024)**: Mrs. Carter can be heard "bustling about in the kitchen" at five o'clock. Nancy tells her she may be late for dinner. Later, Mrs. Carter has prepared "an excellent meal."
- **Chapter XIII (013-036)**: Nancy notes it is nearly luncheon time and that Mrs. Carter will be expecting her.
- **Chapter XV (015-011, 015-013)**: Nancy gives Mrs. Carter a few orders after finishing the dishes, then informs her she is going out.
- **Chapter XVI (016-003)**: As Nancy is about to drive away, Mrs. Carter thrusts her head out of the front door to relay a last-minute message.
- **Chapter XVII (017-004)**: Mrs. Carter reports that someone has been trying to reach Nancy all afternoon.

## Key Actions

- **Joins the Drew household** (004-003–004): Hired as housekeeper and quickly establishes herself as capable and reliable.
- **Provides domestic continuity** (007–017): Throughout the novel, Mrs. Carter maintains the Drew home — cooking meals, receiving callers, and keeping the household running smoothly while Nancy pursues her investigations.
- **Relays critical messages** (016-003, 017-004): On multiple occasions, Mrs. Carter ensures that Nancy receives important communications, including a persistent caller who had been trying to reach her all afternoon.
