# River Heights Police Department

## Overview

The River Heights Police Department is the law enforcement agency tasked with investigating the theft of the Crandall jewels. Their official inquiry runs parallel to Nancy's independent investigation, and they are "inclined to suspect Mrs. Willoughby" (007-054). of the Crandall jewels. Their investigation runs parallel to Nancy Drew's, and their early focus on Mrs. Willoughby as a suspect shapes the course of the inquiry.

## The Investigation

The police investigation into the jewel theft begins immediately after the crime is discovered. They question witnesses at Lilac Inn and begin gathering statements from those present during the theft. The police are "inclined to suspect Mrs. Willoughby" (007-054) as their primary person of interest. Nancy asks whether the police have "any real clue" (007-053) beyond their suspicions, reflecting her concern that the investigation may be too narrowly focused. Their official inquiry complements Nancy's independent detective work, though Nancy sometimes disagrees with their conclusions.
