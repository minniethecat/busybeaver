# Nancy Drew

## Overview

Nancy Drew is the protagonist of *The Mystery at Lilac Inn* and an accomplished amateur detective. She is recognized in her community for her investigative skills, as evidenced when a stranger exclaims "Nancy Drew of all people!" upon recognizing her (001-009). Her specialty is solving mysteries, and she approaches each case with determination and intelligence (001-006).

## Background

Nancy is the daughter of Carson Drew, a famous criminal and mystery-case lawyer (002-021). She has learned much from observing her father's work and has solved several previous mysteries. Despite her accomplishments, she is described as modest, with others noting she is "too modest to admit" her abilities (001-025).

## Role in the Mystery

When her friend Emily Crandall reveals she is about to receive the Crandall family jewels as an inheritance, Nancy becomes drawn into the case. After the jewels are stolen at Lilac Inn, Nancy dedicates herself to the investigation, following clues and confronting danger with characteristic determination.

## Key Relationships

- **Carson Drew** — Nancy's father, a lawyer who provides guidance and support
- **Emily Crandall** — Nancy's friend and the heir to the stolen jewels
- **Hannah Gruen** — The Drew family housekeeper
