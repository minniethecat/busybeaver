## Definition

A stronghold in [[raoul-the-terrible|the]] novel's military geography is any fortified position — typically a castle or walled town — that dominates [[raoul-the-terrible|the]] surrounding territory and serves as a base for military operations. Strongholds are [[raoul-the-terrible|the]] nodes around which campaigns are organised: [[raoul-the-terrible|the]]y must be besieged to be taken, [[garrison|garrisoned]] to be held, and governed to be productive.
