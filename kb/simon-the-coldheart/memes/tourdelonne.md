Tourdelonne is a French location mentioned during [[the-lady-margaret|the Lady Margaret]]'s journey through France. It lies along the route she travels while seeking aid against the English occupation of [[belremy|Belrémy]].

## In the Novel

Tourdelonne is one of the waypoints in the geography of Margaret's flight. It appears in the narrative of her desperate journey through the French countryside, accompanied by [[jeanne|Jeanne]] and [[ranaud|Ranaud]].
