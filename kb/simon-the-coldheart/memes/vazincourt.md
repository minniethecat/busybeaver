## Overview

Vazincourt is an estate in France, home to the Sieur de Larousie. It appears in the narrative as a destination sought by the [[the-lady-margaret|Lady Margaret]] during her flight, and later as a waypoint considered by [[simon-of-beauvallet]] and his companions during their pursuit of [[raoul-the-terrible]].

## The Lady Margaret's Destination

The Lady [[the-lady-margaret|Margaret]], travelling under duress, inquired about the distance to Vazincourt, intending to reach the Sieur de Larousie:

> "To Vazincourt. To the Sieur de Larousie." [021-117]

Her companion warned her that she would never reach Vazincourt under the circumstances [021-126].

## The Pursuit

When [[simon-of-beauvallet|Simon]] and [[geoffrey-of-malvallet]] learned that their quarry had planned to journey to Vazincourt, they considered following the same route. However, they instead turned north, deserting the road to Vazincourt to pursue [[raoul-the-terrible|Raoul]] in a different direction [023-145][023-146]. They later confirmed the route by questioning a peasant on the road to Vazincourt [023-151].
