Balderin is a small French village located approximately two and a half leagues from [[belremy|Belrémy]]. It is the first stop on [[the-lady-margaret|the Lady Margaret]] and [[jeanne|Jeanne]]'s escape route from Belrémy.

## In the Novel

After slipping past the English guards at Belrémy, Margaret and Jeanne walk to Balderin, arriving around eight in the evening (020-006). They breakfast at a tavern there, where Margaret — disguised as a pageboy — swaggers and kisses the serving wench to maintain her cover (020-007). From Balderin they continue onward to Razincourt, and eventually encounter [[ranaud|Ranaud]].
