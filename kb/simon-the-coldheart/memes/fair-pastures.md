Fair Pastures is an English estate mentioned in the novel. Its lord is among the noblemen who gather at formal occasions, representing the landed gentry of early 15th-century England.

## In the Novel

Fair Pastures is named among the estates whose lords participate in the political and social life of the English nobility. It represents the network of landed properties that formed the economic and military foundation of feudal England.
