## Overview

Joulinceaulx is a location in France mentioned as the site of a festival. It appears in the narrative when the Lady [[the-lady-margaret|Margaret]] and her sister planned to attend the festival there.

## The Festival

During the Lady [[the-lady-margaret|Margaret]]'s captivity at [[belremy]], she used the festival at Joulinceaulx as a pretext within an escape plan:

> "...my sister and I journey to Joulinceaulx for the festival. Have ye room for us in your hall to-night?" [020-057]

She later requested leave to journey to Joulinceaulx for the festival [020-081], and one of her companions offered to accompany her part of the way, saying "All ways are one to me" [020-132].
