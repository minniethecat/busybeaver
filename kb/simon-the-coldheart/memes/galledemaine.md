Galledemaine is a French estate whose lady, the Comtesse de Galledemaine, appears in the novel. It is situated in the region affected by the English campaign in [[normandy|Normandy]].

## In the Novel

The Comtesse de Galledemaine is mentioned among the French nobility encountered during the political and military events of the narrative. Galledemaine represents one of the many French estates whose loyalties and fates were shaped by the English invasion.
