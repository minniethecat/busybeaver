Barminster is an English estate mentioned in the novel. It appears in the context of a banquet attended by members of the English nobility.

## In the Novel

Barminster is referenced during a gathering of lords where news and political matters are discussed. It serves as one of the many English estates that form the backdrop of the novel's aristocratic world.
