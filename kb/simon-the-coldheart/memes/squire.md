## Definition

A squire in [[raoul-the-terrible|the]] medieval world [[geoffrey-of-malvallet|of]] [[raoul-the-terrible|the]] novel is a young man in attendance upon a [[knighthood|knight]], serving as shield-bearer, lance-carrier, and personal attendant while training for [[knighthood|knight]]hood himself. [[the-chevalier|The]] relationship between [[knighthood|knight]] and squire is one [[geoffrey-of-malvallet|of]] [[raoul-the-terrible|the]] novel's central social bonds, shaping [[simon-of-beauvallet|Simon]]'s character in both roles.
