## Overview

[[the-chevalier|The]] House of Malvallet is an English noble family seated at [[malvallet|Malvallet estate]]. [[the-chevalier|The]] house achieves narrative significance through its two sons: [[geoffrey-of-malvallet|Geoffrey of Malvallet]], [[raoul-the-terrible|the]] legitimate heir and a [[knighthood|knight]] [[geoffrey-of-malvallet|of]] [[raoul-the-terrible|the]] realm, and [[simon-of-beauvallet|Simon of Beauvallet]], [[raoul-the-terrible|the]] illegitimate son born to Jehanne [[geoffrey-of-malvallet|of]] [[raoul-the-terrible|the]] [[malvallet|Malvallet]] household.

> "She was one Jehanne, of Malvallet's household."
>
> — [001-097]

[[the-chevalier|The]] house exemplifies [[raoul-the-terrible|the]] medieval dynastic structure — legitimacy, inheritance, and bloodline — against which [[simon-of-beauvallet|Simon]]'s merit-based rise is measured. [[the-chevalier|The]] [[malvallet|Malvallet]] name carries [[raoul-the-terrible|the]] weight [[geoffrey-of-malvallet|of]] established nobility; [[simon-of-beauvallet|Simon]] carries [[raoul-the-terrible|the]] blood but not [[raoul-the-terrible|the]] title.

## Key Members

- **[[geoffrey-of-malvallet|Geoffrey of Malvallet]]** — [[raoul-the-terrible|the]] legitimate heir, a [[knighthood|knight]] serving [[king-henry-v|the King]]. Initially hostile to [[simon-of-beauvallet|Simon]], he eventually becomes an ally.
- **[[simon-of-beauvallet|Simon of Beauvallet]]** — [[raoul-the-terrible|the]] bastard son, who rises independently to found his own house, [[house-of-beauvallet|House of Beauvallet]].

## Political Alignment

[[the-chevalier|The]] House of Malvallet owes allegiance to [[raoul-the-terrible|the]] [[house-of-lancaster|House of Lancaster]] and serves [[king-henry-v|the King]] in [[raoul-the-terrible|the]] [[normandy|Normandy]] campaign. [[geoffrey-of-malvallet|Geoffrey of Malvallet]]'s presence at [[raoul-the-terrible|the]] [[kings-council-at-westminster|King's Council at Westminster]] confirms [[raoul-the-terrible|the]] house's standing in Lancastrian governance.

## Relationship to Other Houses

[[the-chevalier|The]] [[malvallet|Malvallet]]s exist in tension with [[house-of-montlice|House of Montlice]] — [[fulk-of-montlice|Fulk of Montlice]] has no love for [[raoul-the-terrible|the]] [[malvallet|Malvallet]] name, yet recognises its stamp on [[simon-of-beauvallet|Simon]]. [[the-chevalier|The]] house also connects to [[house-of-beauvallet|House of Beauvallet]] through [[simon-of-beauvallet|Simon]], whose elevation creates a cadet branch [[geoffrey-of-malvallet|of]] [[raoul-the-terrible|the]] [[malvallet|Malvallet]] bloodline under a new name.
