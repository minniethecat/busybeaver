# Mr. Standish

Mr. Standish is a minor character in Georgette Heyer's *[[john-carstares|The Black Moth]]*. He is described as a "bowing acquaintance" who arrives at a social gathering in Chapter XXI.

## In The Black Moth

Mr. Standish appears briefly at a social event, where his manner is noted by the narrator:

> …later, a bowing acquaintance, Mr. Standish, also arrived. Seeing that he… (021-001)

The phrase "bowing acquaintance" suggests a relationship of polite formality rather than close friendship, illustrating the stratified nature of Georgian social interactions.
