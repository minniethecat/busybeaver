# Mr. Stapely

Mr. Stapely is a minor character in Georgette Heyer's *The Black Moth*. He appears in Chapter XXI as a dance partner at a social gathering, where he is asked to identify [[lavinia-carstares|Lady Lavinia Carstares]].

## In The Black Moth

Mr. Stapely serves a brief narrative function when another guest asks him about [[lavinia-carstares|Lavinia]]'s identity during a dance:

> …ance, and I asked my partner, Mr. Stapely, who you were. He told me, an… (021-029)

His role as a dance partner who can furnish information about fellow guests reflects the interconnected nature of [[london|London]]'s [[fashion|fashion]]able society.
