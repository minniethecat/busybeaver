# Lord March

Lord March is a minor character in Georgette Heyer's *The Black Moth*. He appears briefly in Chapter XXIII, where he encounters [[lavinia-carstares|Lady Lavinia Carstares]] in the foyer of a social venue.

## In The Black Moth

Lord March's single mention occurs as he walks towards the foyer of a gathering and notices [[lavinia-carstares|Lavinia]]:

> …walked towards the foyer. There Lord March espied Lavinia, who was alway… (023-032)

He is part of the wider circle of society figures who populate the [[fashion|fashion]]able world of the novel.
