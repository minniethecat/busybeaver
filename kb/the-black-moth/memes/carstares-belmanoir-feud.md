# Carstares-Belmanoir Feud

The rivalry between the [[john-carstares|Carstares]] and Belmanoir families is the engine of *[[john-carstares|The Black Moth*'s]] plot. It begins with a card game,[[honour| escal]]ates through exile and predation, and resolves — as honour codes demand — in a duel. This page maps the feud's key participants, its pivotal events, and the web of relationships that make it more than a simple contest between hero and villain.

## Thematic Dimensions

The feud is not merely personal — it embodies a clash of values:

- **[[honour|Honour]] vs. Power**: Jack defends honour; Tracy wields power. The feud tests which is stronger.
- **Family Loyalty vs. Moral Right**: [[Lavinia Carstares|Lavinia]] must choose between her birth family (Belmanoir) and her married family (Carstares).
- **Redemption vs. Damnation**: Jack rises from [[scandal|disgrace]]; Tracy descends from power. The feud's resolution is a moral judgment on both houses.
