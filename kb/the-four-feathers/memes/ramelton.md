# Ramelton
The small town in [[donegal|County Donegal]] where [[lennon-house|Lennon House]] is situated — a place of 'drowsy quiet' (004-005) on the [[the-lennon-river|Lennon River]], with its old grey bridge, steep cobbled streets, and the dark thicket of trees climbing the far side of the river. Ramelton is the Irish world into which [[harry-feversham|Harry]] briefly enters as [[ethne-eustace|Ethne]]'s fiancé, and from which he is expelled by the arrival of the [[the-white-feather|feathers]].
