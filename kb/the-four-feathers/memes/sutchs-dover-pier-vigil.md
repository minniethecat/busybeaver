# Sutch's Dover Pier Vigil
[[lieutenant-sutch|Lieutenant Sutch]]'s silent vigil on the pier at [[dover|Dover]], watching the mail packet 'steam away into darkness and rain' (025-027), carrying [[harry-feversham|Harry Feversham]] toward his unknown fate in the Sudan. [[lieutenant-sutch|Sutch]] prays 'that he might live until this great moment should come' — the moment of [[harry-feversham|Harry]]'s vindication.
