# The Telegram Dinner
The dinner at [[harry-feversham|Harry Feversham]]'s [[hill-street|Hill Street]] lodgings where [[castleton|Castleton]]'s telegram arrives, announcing the regiment's deployment to [[cairo|Egypt]]. Present are [[captain-trench|Trench]], [[willoughby|Willoughby]], and [[jack-durrance|Durrance]] — the key figures in the drama to come. [[harry-feversham|Harry]]'s suppression of the news from [[captain-trench|Trench]] is the seed of the accusation.
