# The Nile River
The great river along which much of the novel's Sudan action takes place — the route of approach to [[omdurman|Omdurman]], the artery of the British military campaign, and the symbolic boundary between the known world of [[england|England]] and the transformative wilderness where [[harry-feversham|Harry]] earns his [[redemption]]. The Nile sequences mark [[harry-feversham|Harry]]'s passage from [[disgrace]] to vindication.
