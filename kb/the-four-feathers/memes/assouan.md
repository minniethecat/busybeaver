# Assouan
The [[cairo|Egypt]]ian town at the First Cataract of the Nile, Assouan serves as a transit point for characters moving between [[england|England]] and the Sudan. It is here that [[calder|Calder]] boards a train — "disappointed at his failure to obtain news of [[harry-feversham|Feversham]]" (025-001) — while elsewhere the truth of [[harry-feversham|Harry]]'s [[redemption]] is breaking into the open. Assouan represents the threshold between the known world and [[the desert]], a place of transition and incomplete knowledge.
