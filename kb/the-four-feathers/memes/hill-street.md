# Hill Street
[[harry-feversham|Harry Feversham]]'s [[london|London]] lodgings, where the fateful dinner takes place at which the telegram announcing the [[regiment]]'s deployment arrives. It is from Hill Street that [[harry-feversham|Harry]] sends his resignation, and it is to Hill Street that the jeweller's box containing the three [[four-feathers|white feathers]] is forwarded before following him to Ireland.
