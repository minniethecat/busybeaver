# County Donegal
The rugged county in northwest Ireland where [[the Eustace family]] has its seat. Donegal represents a world apart from the military society of southern [[england|England]] — a place of rivers, hills, and an ancient hospitality embodied in [[dermod-eustace|Dermod Eustace]]. It is the landscape of [[ethne-eustace|Ethne]]'s childhood, the setting of the novel's crisis, and — in the end — the place where [[forgiveness|reconciliation]] becomes possible.
