# The British Army

The institution within which the novel's male characters define their identities — a professional force transitioning from purchased commissions to meritocracy while retaining an aristocratic ethos. The Army's values of [[courage]], [[honour]], and regimental [[loyalty]] are the standards by which every character in [[four-feathers|The Four Feathers]] is measured.
