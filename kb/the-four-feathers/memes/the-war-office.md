# The War Office
The seat of British military administration, referenced as the place where [[harry-feversham|Harry]]'s resignation is processed and where, in the novel's background, the machinery of imperial military power operates. [[lieutenant-sutch|Sutch]] stands outside [[british-military-administration|the War Office]] in Pall Mall on the night of [[harry-feversham|Harry]]'s resignation, a symbolic positioning at the threshold of the institution that has judged his protégé.
