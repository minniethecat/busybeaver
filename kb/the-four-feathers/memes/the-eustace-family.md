# The Eustace Family

The Irish gentry family of [[donegal|County Donegal]], headed by [[dermod-eustace|Dermod Eustace]] of [[lennon-house|Lennon House]] — a household characterized by warmth, eccentricity, and an ancient hospitality that 'forced bed and board even upon strangers' (004-031). The Eustaces represent a world distinct from both English military society and the rising Irish nationalism of the period.
