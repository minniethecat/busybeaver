# The Fall of Khartoum
The historical event that forms the novel's military backdrop: the capture of [[khartoum|Khartoum]] by Mahdist forces and the death of [[general-feversham|General]] Charles Gordon. Though not directly depicted, the fall of [[khartoum|Khartoum]] creates the strategic situation that sends British [[the-british-officer-corps|officers]] — including [[harry-feversham|Harry]]'s accusers — into the Sudan.
