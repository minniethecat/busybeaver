# The Criterion Grillroom
The [[london|London]] restaurant where [[harry-feversham|Harry Feversham]] confesses his resignation to [[lieutenant-sutch|Lieutenant Sutch]], and where [[lieutenant-sutch|Sutch]] gives his promise of silence — 'I promised faithfully in the Criterion grillroom' (025-003). The Criterion is the novel's threshold space: the last place [[harry-feversham|Harry]] is seen in English society before disappearing into the Sudan, and the place where the secret that drives the entire plot is en[[faith|trust]]ed to its sole keeper.
