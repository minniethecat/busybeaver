# British Military Administration

The administrative apparatus of British imperial military power in the Victorian era. [[the-war-office|The War Office]] processes [[harry-feversham|Harry Feversham]]'s resignation; the chain of command orders regiments to [[cairo|Egypt]]; and the entire machinery of empire creates the geopolitical stage on which the novel's personal drama of [[honour]] and [[disgrace]] is enacted. The administration is impersonal — it neither knows nor cares about [[harry-feversham|Harry]]'s inner torment — yet its decisions are the efficient causes of the novel's plot.
