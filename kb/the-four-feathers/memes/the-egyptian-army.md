# The Egyptian Army

The military force of Khedivate [[cairo|Egypt]], engaged alongside British forces in [[mahdist-war|the Sudan campaign]]s. The Egyptian Army represents the broader imperial context in which British [[the-british-officer-corps|officers]] operate, and its presence underscores the novel's colonial setting in North Africa.
