# The Last Ride Together
The final ride of [[harry-feversham|Harry Feversham]] and [[jack-durrance|Jack Durrance]] through the English countryside before [[harry-feversham|Harry]]'s resignation — an elegy for their [[friendship]] conducted in near-silence. '[[harry-feversham|Feversham]] rode by his friend's side in silence' (003-007), each man aware that something is ending but unable to name it.
