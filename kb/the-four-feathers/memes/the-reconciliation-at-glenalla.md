# The Reconciliation at Glenalla
The novel's final event: [[harry-feversham|Harry Feversham]] and [[ethne-eustace|Ethne Eustace]], after years of separation, find their way back to each other at [[glenalla|Glenalla]] in [[devonshire|Devonshire]]. The novel does not dramatize the words of [[forgiveness|reconciliation]] — it shows us the quiet resumption of a bond severed in Chapter 4, a structural symmetry that is forgiveness's architecture.
