# Durrance's Blinding
The injury in the Sudan that costs [[jack-durrance|Jack Durrance]] his sight — a wound that ends his active military career but deepens his moral role in the novel. Blinded, [[jack-durrance|Durrance]] becomes dependent on others in ways that complicate his engagement to [[ethne-eustace|Ethne]] and sharpen his perception of the truth about [[harry-feversham|Harry]].
