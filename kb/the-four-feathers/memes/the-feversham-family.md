# The Feversham Family

The family whose [[honour]] [[harry-feversham|Harry Feversham]] must restore — a lineage of military men whose portraits line the hall at [[broad-place|Broad Place]], each one a silent judge. As [[harry-feversham|Harry]] confesses, he feared '[[disgrace|dishonour]]ing my father and all the dead men whose portraits hung ranged in the hall' (004-068). The family name is the inheritance [[harry-feversham|Harry]] nearly destroys and spends the novel reclaiming.
