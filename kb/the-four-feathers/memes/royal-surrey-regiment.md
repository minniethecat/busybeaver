# Royal Surrey Regiment

The fictional British Army regiment in which [[harry-feversham|Harry Feversham]], [[jack-durrance|Jack Durrance]], [[captain-trench|Captain Trench]], [[willoughby|Mr. Willoughby]], and [[castleton|Mr. Castleton]] serve — the institutional embodiment of the [[honour|military honour]] code that judges [[harry-feversham|Harry]] and, ultimately, vindicates him. The regiment is the source of the three [[four-feathers|white feathers]] sent as accusations of [[cowardice|coward]]ice, and the community to which [[harry-feversham|Harry]] must prove his worth through years of secret heroism in the Sudan.
