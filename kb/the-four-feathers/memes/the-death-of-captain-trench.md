# The Death of Captain Trench
[[captain-trench|Captain Trench]]'s death in the House of Stone in [[omdurman|Omdurman]], during the escape engineered by [[harry-feversham|Harry Feversham]]. His dying act — asking [[willoughby|Willoughby]] to return his feather — is the first formal withdrawal of the accusation: 'he withdrew his charge of [[cowardice|coward]]ice against [[harry-feversham|Harry Feversham]]' (025-026).
