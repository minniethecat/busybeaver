# Ethne's Runaway Horse
The incident in [[ramelton|Ramelton]] where [[ethne-eustace|Ethne Eustace]] climbs over the shafts of a runaway dogcart to seize the reins — the definitive demonstration of her physical [[courage]]. 'She climbed over the front of the dogcart as it pitched and raced down the hill, and balancing herself along the shafts, reached the reins at the horse's neck' (004-006).
