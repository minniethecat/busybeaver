# The Gordon Relief Expedition

The British military expedition sent to relieve [[general-feversham|General]] Charles Gordon at [[khartoum|Khartoum]] — the historical campaign that provides the backdrop for [[harry-feversham|Harry Feversham]]'s secret service in the Sudan. The expedition's failure to reach [[khartoum|Khartoum]] in time shapes the novel's military landscape and the conditions of [[harry-feversham|Harry]]'s [[redemption]].
