# The Donegal Gentry

The social class of Irish landowners in [[donegal|County Donegal]] to which [[the Eustace family]] belongs — a world of big houses, hunting, and open-handed hospitality. This social milieu provides the setting for the novel's crisis ([[the-white-feather-ball|the ball at Lennon House]]) and its resolution (the [[forgiveness|reconciliation]] at [[glenalla|Glenalla]]).
