# Berber
A Nile town in Sudan that features in the military geography of the novel's second half. [[harry-feversham|Harry Feversham]] passes through or near Berber during his years of secret service in the Sudan, part of the network of desert settlements and garrisons that constitute the theatre of his [[redemption]].
