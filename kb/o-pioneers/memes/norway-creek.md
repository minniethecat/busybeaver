## Overview

Norway Creek — The creek near the Divide where Ivar lives in isolation — a place of natural beauty and spiritual retreat in O Pioneers!.

## In the Novel

Norway Creek appears prominently in the narrative of *O Pioneers!*, shaping the course of events and revealing character. The novel's treatment of this place illuminates Cather's larger themes of pioneering, identity, and the American frontier.

## Significance

Norway Creek contributes to the rich tapestry of *O Pioneers!*, demonstrating Cather's ability to weave individual stories into a larger vision of life on the Nebraska prairie.
