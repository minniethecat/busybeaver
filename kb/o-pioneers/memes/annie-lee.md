## Overview

Annie Lee — A neighbor girl on the Divide who provides a glimpse of the younger generation's prospects..

## In the Novel

Annie Lee features in *O Pioneers!* as part of the narrative fabric of life on the Nebraska Divide. The novel captures this person with Cather's characteristic attention to the interplay between individual experience and the broader forces of frontier existence.

## Significance

The role of Annie Lee contributes to the novel's rich portrait of pioneer life — its challenges, its transformations, and its enduring human significance.
