## Overview

The River Farms — The prosperous farms along the river valley that Alexandra studies as models for improving her own land on the Divide.

## In the Novel

The River Farms appears prominently in the narrative of *O Pioneers!*, shaping the course of events and revealing character. The novel's treatment of this place illuminates Cather's larger themes of pioneering, identity, and the American frontier.

## Significance

The River Farms contributes to the rich tapestry of *O Pioneers!*, demonstrating Cather's ability to weave individual stories into a larger vision of life on the Nebraska prairie.
