## Overview

Lincoln — The state capital where Alexandra negotiates with lawyers and experiences city life..

## In the Novel

Lincoln features in *O Pioneers!* as part of the narrative fabric of life on the Nebraska Divide. The novel captures this place with Cather's characteristic attention to the interplay between individual experience and the broader forces of frontier existence.

## Significance

The role of Lincoln contributes to the novel's rich portrait of pioneer life — its challenges, its transformations, and its enduring human significance.
