## Overview

Ivar — Crazy Ivar — the elderly Norwegian mystic who lives in harmony with nature and serves as Alexandra's spiritual advisor.

## In the Novel

Ivar appears prominently in the narrative of *O Pioneers!*, shaping the course of events and revealing character. The novel's treatment of this person illuminates Cather's larger themes of pioneering, identity, and the American frontier.

## Significance

Ivar contributes to the rich tapestry of *O Pioneers!*, demonstrating Cather's ability to weave individual stories into a larger vision of life on the Nebraska prairie.
