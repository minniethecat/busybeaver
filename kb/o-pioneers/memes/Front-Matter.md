[P01-024]IN WHOSE BEAUTIFUL AND DELICATE WORK

[P01-025]THERE IS THE PERFECTION

[P01-026]THAT ENDURES

[P01-027]PRAIRIE SPRING

[P01-028]Evening and the flat land,

[P01-029]Rich and sombre and always silent;

[P01-030]The miles of fresh-plowed soil,

[P01-031]Heavy and black, full of strength and harshness;

[P01-032]The growing wheat, the growing weeds,

[P01-033]The toiling horses, the tired men;

[P01-034]The long empty roads,

[P01-035]Sullen fires of sunset, fading,

[P01-036]The eternal, unresponsive sky.

[P01-037]Against all this, Youth,

[P01-038]Flaming like the wild roses,

[P01-039]Singing like the larks over the plowed fields,

[P01-040]Flashing like a star out of the twilight;

[P01-041]Youth with its insupportable sweetness,

[P01-042]Its fierce necessity,

[P01-043]Its sharp desire,

[P01-044]Singing and singing,

[P01-045]Out of the lips of silence,

[P01-046]Out of the earthy dusk.
