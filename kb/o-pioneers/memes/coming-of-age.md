## Overview

Coming of Age — The journey from youth to adulthood on the Nebraska frontier, exemplified by Emil Bergson in O Pioneers!.

## In the Novel

Coming of Age appears prominently in the narrative of *O Pioneers!*, shaping the course of events and revealing character. The novel's treatment of this concept illuminates Cather's larger themes of pioneering, identity, and the American frontier.

## Significance

Coming of Age contributes to the rich tapestry of *O Pioneers!*, demonstrating Cather's ability to weave individual stories into a larger vision of life on the Nebraska prairie.
