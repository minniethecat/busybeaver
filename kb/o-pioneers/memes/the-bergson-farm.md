## Overview

The Bergson Farm — The Bergson family homestead on the Nebraska Divide — the novel's central setting, transformed from struggling claim to prosperous enterprise.

## In the Novel

The Bergson Farm appears prominently in the narrative of *O Pioneers!*, shaping the course of events and revealing character. The novel's treatment of this place illuminates Cather's larger themes of pioneering, identity, and the American frontier.

## Significance

The Bergson Farm contributes to the rich tapestry of *O Pioneers!*, demonstrating Cather's ability to weave individual stories into a larger vision of life on the Nebraska prairie.
