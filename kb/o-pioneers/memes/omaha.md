## Overview

Omaha — The Nebraska city where Alexandra travels for business and where Carl Linstrum departs from..

## In the Novel

Omaha features in *O Pioneers!* as part of the narrative fabric of life on the Nebraska Divide. The novel captures this place with Cather's characteristic attention to the interplay between individual experience and the broader forces of frontier existence.

## Significance

The role of Omaha contributes to the novel's rich portrait of pioneer life — its challenges, its transformations, and its enduring human significance.
