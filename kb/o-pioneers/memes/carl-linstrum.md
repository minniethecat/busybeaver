Carl Linstrum appears prominently in the narrative of *O Pioneers!*, shaping the course of events and revealing character. The novel's treatment of this person illuminates Cather's larger themes of pioneering, identity, and the American frontier.

Carl Linstrum contributes to the rich tapestry of *O Pioneers!*, demonstrating Cather's ability to weave individual stories into a larger vision of life on the Nebraska prairie.
