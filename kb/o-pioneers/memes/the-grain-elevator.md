## Overview

The Grain Elevator — The landmark structure at the north end of Hanover, symbolizing the economic infrastructure of prairie farming..

## In the Novel

The Grain Elevator features in *O Pioneers!* as part of the narrative fabric of life on the Nebraska Divide. The novel captures this place with Cather's characteristic attention to the interplay between individual experience and the broader forces of frontier existence.

## Significance

The role of The Grain Elevator contributes to the novel's rich portrait of pioneer life — its challenges, its transformations, and its enduring human significance.
