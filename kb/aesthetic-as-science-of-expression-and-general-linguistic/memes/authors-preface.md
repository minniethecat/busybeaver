[P04-001] This second edition of the *Æsthetic* will be found to contain the

[P04-002] complete translation of the historical portion, which I was obliged to

[P04-003] summarize in the first edition. I have made a number of alterations and

[P04-004] some additions to the theoretical portion, following closely the fourth

[P04-005] (definitive) Italian edition, and in so doing have received much advice

[P04-006] and assistance of value from Mrs. Salusbury, to whom I beg to tender

[P04-007] my best thanks. I trust that this new edition will enable all those

[P04-008] desirous of studying the work to get into direct touch with the thought

[P04-009] of the author.

[P04-010] THE ATHENÆUM, PALL MALL, S.W.,

[P04-011] *November* 1920.
