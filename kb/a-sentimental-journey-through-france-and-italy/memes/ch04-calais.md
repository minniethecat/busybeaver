## CALAIS.

[004-001]My heart smote me the moment he

[004-002]shut the door—Psha! said I, with an air of carelessness,

[004-003]three several times—but it would not do: every ungracious

[004-004]syllable I had utter’d crowded back into my imagination: I

[004-005]reflected, I had no right over the poor Franciscan, but to deny

[004-006]him; and that the punishment of that was enough to the

[004-007]disappointed, without the addition of unkind language.—I

[004-008]consider’d his gray hairs—his courteous figure seem’d

[004-009]to re-enter and gently ask me what injury he had done

[004-010]me?—and why I could use him thus?—I would have given

[004-011]twenty livres for an advocate.—I have behaved very ill,

[004-012]said I within myself; but I have only just set out upon my

[004-013]travels; and shall learn better manners as I get along.
