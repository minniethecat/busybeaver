## MOULINES.

[065-001]Though I hate salutations and

[065-002]greetings in the market-place, yet, when we got into the middle

[065-003]of this, I stopp’d to take my last look and last farewell

[065-004]of Maria. 

[065-005]Maria, though not tall, was nevertheless of the first order of

[065-006]fine forms:—affliction had touched her looks with something

[065-007]that was scarce earthly;—still she was feminine;—and

[065-008]so much was there about her of all that the heart wishes, or the

[065-009]eye looks for in woman, that could the traces be ever worn out of

[065-010]her brain, and those of Eliza out of mine, she should not only

[065-011]eat of my bread and drink of my own cup , but Maria should lie

[065-012]in my bosom, and be unto me as a daughter. 

[065-013]Adieu, poor luckless maiden!—Imbibe the oil and wine

[065-014]which the compassion of a stranger, as he journeyeth on his way,

[065-015]now pours into thy wounds;—the Being, who has twice bruised

[065-016]thee, can only bind them up for ever.
