## CALAIS.

[008-001]It must needs be a hostile kind of

[008-002]a world, when the buyer (if it be but of a sorry post-chaise)

[008-003]cannot go forth with the seller thereof into the street to

[008-004]terminate the difference betwixt them, but he instantly falls into

[008-005]the same frame of mind, and views his conventionist with the same

[008-006]sort of eye, as if he was going along with him to Hyde-park

[008-007]corner to fight a duel. For my own part, being but a poor

[008-008]swordsman, and no way a match for Monsieur Dessein, I felt the

[008-009]rotation of all the movements within me, to which the situation

[008-010]is incident;—I looked at Monsieur Dessein through and

[008-011]through—eyed him as he walk’d along in

[008-012]profile,—then, en face ;—thought like a

[008-013]Jew,—then a Turk,—disliked his wig,—cursed him

[008-014]by my gods,—wished him at the devil.— 

[008-015]—And is all this to be lighted up in the heart for a

[008-016]beggarly account of three or four louis d’ors, which is the

[008-017]most I can be overreached in?—Base passion! said I, turning

[008-018]myself about, as a man naturally does upon a sudden reverse of

[008-019]sentiment,—base, ungentle passion! thy hand is against

[008-020]every man, and every man’s hand against thee.—Heaven

[008-021]forbid! said she, raising her hand up to her forehead, for I had

[008-022]turned full in front upon the lady whom I had seen in conference

[008-023]with the monk:—she had followed us

[008-024]unperceived.—Heaven forbid, indeed! said I, offering her my

[008-025]own;—she had a black pair of silk gloves, open only at the

[008-026]thumb and two fore-fingers, so accepted it without

[008-027]reserve,—and I led her up to the door of the Remise. 

[008-028]Monsieur Dessein had diabled the key above fifty times

[008-029]before he had found out he had come with a wrong one in his hand:

[008-030]we were as impatient as himself to have it opened; and so

[008-031]attentive to the obstacle that I continued holding her hand

[008-032]almost without knowing it: so that Monsieur Dessein left us

[008-033]together with her hand in mine, and with our faces turned towards

[008-034]the door of the Remise, and said he would be back in five

[008-035]minutes. 

[008-036]Now a colloquy of five minutes, in such a situation, is worth

[008-037]one of as many ages, with your faces turned towards the street:

[008-038]in the latter case, ’tis drawn from the objects and

[008-039]occurrences without;—when your eyes are fixed upon a dead

[008-040]blank,—you draw purely from yourselves. A silence of

[008-041]a single moment upon Mons. Dessein’s leaving us, had been

[008-042]fatal to the situation—she had infallibly turned

[008-043]about;—so I begun the conversation instantly.— 

[008-044]—But what were the temptations (as I write not to

[008-045]apologize for the weaknesses of my heart in this tour,—but

[008-046]to give an account of them)—shall be described with the

[008-047]same simplicity with which I felt them.
