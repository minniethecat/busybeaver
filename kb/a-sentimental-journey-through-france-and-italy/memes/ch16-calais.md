## CALAIS.

[016-001]Monsieur Dessein came back to let

[016-002]us out of the chaise, and acquaint the lady, the count de

[016-003]L—, her brother, was just arrived at the hotel.

[016-004]Though I had infinite good will for the lady, I cannot say that I

[016-005]rejoiced in my heart at the event—and could not help

[016-006]telling her so;—for it is fatal to a proposal, Madame, said

[016-007]I, that I was going to make to you— 

[016-008]—You need not tell me what the proposal was, said she,

[016-009]laying her hand upon both mine, as she interrupted me.—A

[016-010]man my good Sir, has seldom an offer of kindness to make to a

[016-011]woman, but she has a presentiment of it some moments

[016-012]before.— 

[016-013]Nature arms her with it, said I, for immediate

[016-014]preservation.—But I think, said she, looking in my face, I

[016-015]had no evil to apprehend,—and, to deal frankly with you,

[016-016]had determined to accept it.—If I had—(she stopped a

[016-017]moment)—I believe your good will would have drawn a story from

[016-018]me, which would have made pity the only dangerous thing in the

[016-019]journey. 

[016-020]In saying this, she suffered me to kiss her hand twice, and

[016-021]with a look of sensibility mixed with concern, she got out of the

[016-022]chaise,—and bid adieu.
