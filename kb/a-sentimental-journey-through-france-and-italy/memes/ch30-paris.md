## PARIS.

[030-001]When a man can contest the point by

[030-002]dint of equipage, and carry all on floundering before him with

[030-003]half a dozen of lackies and a couple of cooks—’tis

[030-004]very well in such a place as Paris,—he may drive in at

[030-005]which end of a street he will. 

[030-006]A poor prince who is weak in cavalry, and whose whole infantry

[030-007]does not exceed a single man, had best quit the field, and

[030-008]signalize himself in the cabinet, if he can get up into

[030-009]it;—I say up into it —for there is no

[030-010]descending perpendicular amongst ’em with a “ Me

[030-011]voici ! mes enfans ”—here I

[030-012]am—whatever many may think. 

[030-013]I own my first sensations, as soon as I was left solitary and

[030-014]alone in my own chamber in the hotel, were far from being so

[030-015]flattering as I had prefigured them. I walked up gravely to

[030-016]the window in my dusty black coat, and looking through the glass

[030-017]saw all the world in yellow, blue, and green, running at the ring

[030-018]of pleasure.—The old with broken lances, and in helmets

[030-019]which had lost their vizards;—the young in armour bright

[030-020]which shone like gold, beplumed with each gay feather of the

[030-021]east,—all,—all, tilting at it like fascinated knights

[030-022]in tournaments of yore for fame and love.— 

[030-023]Alas, poor Yorick! cried I, what art thou doing here? On the very

[030-024]first onset of all this glittering clatter thou art reduced to an

[030-025]atom;—seek,—seek some winding alley, with a

[030-026]tourniquet at the end of it, where chariot never rolled or

[030-027]flambeau shot its rays;—there thou mayest solace thy soul

[030-028]in converse sweet with some kind grisette of a barber’s

[030-029]wife, and get into such coteries!— 

[030-030]—May I perish! if I do, said I, pulling out the letter

[030-031]which I had to present to Madame de R—.—I’ll

[030-032]wait upon this lady, the very first thing I do. So I called

[030-033]La Fleur to go seek me a barber directly,—and come back and

[030-034]brush my coat.
