## CALAIS.

[002-001]I had scarce uttered the words,

[002-002]when a poor monk of the order of St. Francis came into the room

[002-003]to beg something for his convent. No man cares to have his

[002-004]virtues the sport of contingencies—or one man may be

[002-005]generous, as another is puissant;— sed non quoad

[002-006]hanc —or be it as it may,—for there is no regular

[002-007]reasoning upon the ebbs and flows of our humours; they may depend

[002-008]upon the same causes, for aught I know, which influence the tides

[002-009]themselves: ’twould oft be no discredit to us, to suppose

[002-010]it was so: I’m sure at least for myself, that in many a

[002-011]case I should be more highly satisfied, to have it said by the

[002-012]world, “I had had an affair with the moon, in which there

[002-013]was neither sin nor shame,” than have it pass altogether as

[002-014]my own act and deed, wherein there was so much of both. 

[002-015]—But, be this as it may,—the moment I cast my eyes

[002-016]upon him, I was predetermined not to give him a single sous; and,

[002-017]accordingly, I put my purse into my pocket—buttoned

[002-018]it—set myself a little more upon my centre, and advanced up

[002-019]gravely to him; there was something, I fear, forbidding in my

[002-020]look: I have his figure this moment before my eyes, and think

[002-021]there was that in it which deserved better. 

[002-022]The monk, as I judged by the break in his tonsure, a few

[002-023]scattered white hairs upon his temples, being all that remained

[002-024]of it, might be about seventy;—but from his eyes, and that

[002-025]sort of fire which was in them, which seemed more temper’d

[002-026]by courtesy than years, could be no more than sixty:—Truth

[002-027]might lie between—He was certainly sixty-five; and the

[002-028]general air of his countenance, notwithstanding something

[002-029]seem’d to have been planting wrinkles in it before their

[002-030]time, agreed to the account. 

[002-031]It was one of those heads which Guido has often

[002-032]painted,—mild, pale—penetrating, free from all

[002-033]commonplace ideas of fat contented ignorance looking downwards upon

[002-034]the earth;—it look’d forwards; but look’d as if

[002-035]it look’d at something beyond this world.—How one of

[002-036]his order came by it, heaven above, who let it fall upon a

[002-037]monk’s shoulders best knows: but it would have suited a

[002-038]Bramin, and had I met it upon the plains of Indostan, I had

[002-039]reverenced it. 

[002-040]The rest of his outline may be given in a few strokes; one

[002-041]might put it into the hands of any one to design, for ’twas

[002-042]neither elegant nor otherwise, but as character and expression

[002-043]made it so: it was a thin, spare form, something above the common

[002-044]size, if it lost not the distinction by a bend forward in the

[002-045]figure,—but it was the attitude of Intreaty; and, as it now

[002-046]stands presented to my imagination, it gained more than it lost

[002-047]by it. 

[002-048]When he had entered the room three paces, he stood still; and

[002-049]laying his left hand upon his breast (a slender white staff with

[002-050]which he journey’d being in his right)—when I had got

[002-051]close up to him, he introduced himself with the little story of

[002-052]the wants of his convent, and the poverty of his order;—and

[002-053]did it with so simple a grace,—and such an air of

[002-054]deprecation was there in the whole cast of his look and

[002-055]figure,—I was bewitch’d not to have been struck with

[002-056]it. 

[002-057]—A better reason was, I had predetermined not to give

[002-058]him a single sous.
