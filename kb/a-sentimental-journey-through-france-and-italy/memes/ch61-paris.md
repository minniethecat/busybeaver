## PARIS.

[061-001]I stepped hastily after him: it was

[061-002]the very man whose success in asking charity of the women before

[061-003]the door of the hotel had so puzzled me;—and I found at

[061-004]once his secret, or at least the basis of it:—’twas

[061-005]flattery. 

[061-006]Delicious essence! how refreshing art thou to Nature! how

[061-007]strongly are all its powers and all its weaknesses on thy side!

[061-008]how sweetly dost thou mix with the blood, and help it through the

[061-009]most difficult and tortuous passages to the heart! 

[061-010]The poor man, as he was not straiten’d for time, had

[061-011]given it here in a larger dose: ’tis certain he had a way

[061-012]of bringing it into a less form, for the many sudden cases he had

[061-013]to do with in the streets: but how he contrived to correct,

[061-014]sweeten, concentre, and qualify it,—I vex not my spirit

[061-015]with the enquiry;—it is enough the beggar gained two

[061-016]twelve-sous pieces—and they can best tell the rest, who

[061-017]have gained much greater matters by it.
