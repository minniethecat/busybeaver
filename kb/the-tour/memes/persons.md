## About the Characters

The novel follows [[lucius|Publius Lucius Sabinus]], a young Roman sculptor, on a grand tour through Egypt in the reign of [[tiberius|Tiberius]]. His travelling companions include his uncle [[catullus|Catullus]], the tutor [[thrasyllus|Thrasyllus]], and the merchant [[caleb|Caleb]]. The cast also encompasses historical figures such as the emperor [[tiberius|Tiberius]] and the geographer [[strabo|Strabo]], legendary Egyptian pharaohs like [[sesostris|Sesostris]], and mythological [[egyptian-deities|deities]] whose worship the travellers encounter.
