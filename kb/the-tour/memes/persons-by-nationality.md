## Overview

The characters of "The Tour" span the Mediterranean world of the early Roman Empire. This page groups all person entries by nationality, from the Roman protagonists to the Egyptian priests, Greek scholars, [[saba|Saba]]ean merchants, and [[jewish|Hebrew]] traders who populate the novel's landscape.
