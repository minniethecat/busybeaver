## Thematic Landscape

"The Tour" is a novel of ideas as much as a travelogue. [[lucius|Lucius]]'s journey through Egypt is simultaneously a search for [[philosophy|wisdom]], a recovery from heartbreak, and an encounter with the ancient religious world. The concept pages trace these themes: oracle-consultations, temple architecture, Egyptian funerary practices, Graeco-Roman philosophy, and the syncretic religious landscape of the early Roman Empire.
