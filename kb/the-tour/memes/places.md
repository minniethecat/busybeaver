## Overview

This page provides a complete index of all place entries in the wiki, tracing the geographical scope of the travellers' journey through Egypt and the Mediterranean in "The Tour." From the metropolitan [[alexandria|Alexandria]] to the thrice-sacred [[memphis|Memphis]], from the Nile cataracts to [[cape-dire|Cape Dire]] on the [[arabian-gulf|Red Sea]], the places form the backbone of the novel's travelogue structure.

## The Journey

The travellers' route follows the Nile southward from [[alexandria|Alexandria]], through the Delta's many [[oracle|oracles]] and shrines, to [[memphis|Memphis]] and the [[pyramid|Pyramid]]s, onward to [[thebes|Thebes]] and the first cataract, then across the eastern desert to the [[arabian-gulf|Red Sea]] coast at [[cape-dire|Cape Dire]]. The return journey loops back through the Mediterranean.
