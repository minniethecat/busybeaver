## Events as Narrative Structure

Hardy builds *The Woodlanders* around turning points rather than continuous dramatic action — the auction of Winterborne's property, the Midsummer Eve gathering, the man-trap incident, the final accident. These events function as hinges on which the novel's emotional and social architecture turns.
