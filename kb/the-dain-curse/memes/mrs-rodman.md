# Mrs. Rodman

Mrs. Rodman is a resident of the Quesada area whose brief appearance in The Dain Curse contributes to the local color and community context of the investigation. Her role, though small, helps establish the social fabric of the small coastal town where much of the novel's second half unfolds.

## Role in the Novel

Mrs. Rodman appears as part of the Quesada community during the investigation into Gabrielle Collinson's kidnapping and the events at the Temple of the Holy Grail. As a local resident, she represents the ordinary citizenry whose lives intersect, however briefly, with the extraordinary crimes that drive the plot.

## Biography

### In the Novel

Mrs. Rodman is mentioned as a member of the Quesada community during the period when the Continental Op and local authorities are investigating the string of violent deaths and the activities of the Haldorn cult. Her presence, along with other Quesada residents, helps establish the setting as a real, populated place rather than an abstract backdrop for the crimes [020-PPP].
