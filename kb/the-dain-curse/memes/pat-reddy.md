# Pat Reddy

Pat Reddy is a district attorney's investigator in San Francisco who works alongside the [[the-continental-detective-agency|Continental Op]] on the Leggett diamond case. A "big, jovial youngster" with "almost brains enough to make up for his lack of experience" [002-045], Reddy represents the official law enforcement side of the investigation.

## In the Novel

Reddy first appears at the Leggett residence on the night of [[the-murder-of-edgar-leggett|Edgar Leggett's murder]], standing in a doorway talking to reporters when the Continental Op arrives [002-042]. He joins the Op and Detective-Sergeant O'Gar in examining the crime scene and interviewing witnesses.

During the investigation of [[gabrielle-leggett|Gabrielle Leggett]]'s disappearance, Reddy was part of the search at the Leggett home. When Mrs. Leggett attempted to flee during questioning, both O'Gar and the Op blamed themselves for the lapse — Reddy, being young at manhunting, was not held responsible [007-089].

Reddy shifted his weight to the balls of his feet as the Op signaled him not to pursue Mrs. Leggett directly, understanding that the hall and stairs offered better interception points [007-095].
