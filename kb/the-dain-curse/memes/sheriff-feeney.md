# Sheriff Feeney

Sheriff Feeney is the county law enforcement officer whose jurisdiction covers Quesada and the coastal area where the climactic events of [[the-dain-curse|The Dain Curse]] take place.

## In the Novel

Feeney appears during the investigation of events at [[the-house-in-the-cove|the House in the Cove]], where the final confrontations of the novel unfold. As the local sheriff, he represents the rural county's law enforcement presence, distinct from the San Francisco police and the [[the-continental-detective-agency|Continental Detective Agency]] operatives who dominate the investigation.

The sheriff's role places him at the intersection of the urban and rural settings that define the novel's geography — the San Francisco streets where the crimes originate and the isolated coastal house where they reach their conclusion.
