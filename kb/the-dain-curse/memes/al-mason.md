# Al Mason

Al Mason is an operative of the Continental Detective Agency, assigned to assist the Continental Op on the Leggett case. Described as "slim, dark, sleek," Mason handles surveillance duties and field operations throughout the investigation of the Dain curse murders.

## Role in the Novel

Mason serves as a reliable field agent in the Continental Op's investigation. He is first introduced in the operatives' room at the agency's San Francisco office, where he and Mickey Linehan are "swapping lies about the times they had been shot at, each trying to pretend he had been more frightened than the other" [006-PPP]. The Op assigns Mason to keep watch on the Leggett house while Linehan tails other persons of interest.

Mason's assignments during the case include surveillance of the Leggett residence and later, tailing Gabrielle Leggett after her reappearance. His work, though largely unglamorous, provides the Op with essential ground-level intelligence that feeds into the broader investigation.

## Biography

### Background

Mason is a professional detective working for the Continental Detective Agency in San Francisco. His physical description—"slim, dark, sleek" [006-PPP]—suggests a man who blends easily into urban environments, a useful trait for surveillance work. His easy camaraderie with Mickey Linehan indicates he is an experienced operative comfortable with the dangers and rhythms of detective work.

### In the Novel

Mason's involvement begins when the Op briefs both him and Linehan on the Leggett case. Throughout the investigation, Mason works primarily in the background, conducting surveillance and reporting his observations to the Op. He tails Gabrielle Leggett at one point, confirming her movements during a critical phase of the case [022-PPP]. His presence helps establish the Continental Detective Agency as a professional organization with multiple capable operatives, not merely a vehicle for the Op's individual brilliance.
