# Hubert Collinson

Hubert Collinson is the father of [[eric-collinson|Eric Collinson]] and a San Francisco businessman who engages the [[the-continental-detective-agency|Continental Detective Agency]] to investigate his son's case.

## In the Novel

When Eric Collinson was arrested for the murder of [[gabrielle-leggett|Gabrielle Leggett]]'s estranged husband, Hubert Collinson came down to Quesada with his other son, Laurence. He is described as "a big calm man who looked capable of taking as many more blows as the world might aim at him" [015-042].

Hubert Collinson quietly told the Continental Op to "go ahead; get to the bottom of it," becoming the fourth client for whom the agency was concerned with the case [015-044]. He also offered a thousand-dollar reward for information leading to the discovery of Gabrielle's whereabouts, matching a similar offer from Madison Andrews [015-048].

The Op later visited Hubert Collinson's San Francisco office to report his findings, though Hubert was not in at the time — the Op instead told his story to Laurence [015-052].
