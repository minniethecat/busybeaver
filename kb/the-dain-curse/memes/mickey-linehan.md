# Mickey Linehan

Mickey Linehan is an operative of the [[continental-detective-agency|Continental
Detective Agency]] who assists the [[continental-op|Continental Op]] in guarding
[[gabrielle-leggett|Gabrielle Leggett]] at the isolated house in the cove. He
drives Mrs. Herman to Quesada and returns with [[macman|MacMan]] and supplies,
then shares sentry duty with him. Linehan represents the reliable, unglamorous
manpower that the Agency can deploy — competent, loyal, and interchangeable.

## Role in the Novel

Linehan appears only in the later chapters (XX–XXI) as part of the protective
detail around Gabrielle. His role is functional: he drives, stands watch, and
mixes drinks. Like MacMan, he embodies the institutional competence of the
Continental Detective Agency, providing the Op with reliable backup in an
increasingly dangerous situation.

## Biography

### Background

Little is revealed about Linehan's background. He is an employee of the
Continental Detective Agency, trusted by the Op enough to be assigned to the
sensitive task of guarding Gabrielle Leggett at a remote location.

### In the Novel

The Op instructs Linehan to share sentry duty with MacMan: "You and he will have
to do the watchdog. Divide it between you anyway you want, but one will have to
be on the job all the time" (020). The next morning, Linehan drives Mrs. Herman —
the housekeeper — to Quesada, returning with MacMan and groceries (021).

That night, when the Op returns from San Francisco, he finds Linehan "in the
kitchen, yawning and mixing himself a drink before relieving MacMan on sentry
duty" (021). The three men — Op, Linehan, and MacMan — share a drink before the
Op goes upstairs to check on Gabrielle.

The Op's instruction that Gabrielle must "take either Linehan or MacMan with
you" when going out (021) underscores Linehan's primary function: he is a
bodyguard, not a detective.

## Relationships

- **[[continental-op|The Continental Op]]** — Employer. Linehan follows the Op's
  orders as part of the Agency's protective operation.
- **[[macman|MacMan]]** — Fellow operative. The two share alternating sentry
  shifts at the cove house.
- **[[gabrielle-leggett|Gabrielle Leggett]]** — Protectee.
