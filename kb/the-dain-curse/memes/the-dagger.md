# The Dagger

A large, double-edged dagger with a bronze cross-shaped hilt, used as a murder weapon
in the killing of Doctor Riese at the Temple of the Holy Grail, and subsequently
handled as evidence by Gabrielle Leggett, the Continental Op, and Aaronia Haldorn.

## Overview

The dagger is first seen when Gabrielle Leggett appears barefooted, wearing only a
yellow silk nightgown, carrying it before her "in both hands, held out in front of her
as she walked" (010-027). It is described as "a large dagger, almost a sword... red
and wet." The Op takes it from her and examines the weapon: "It was a broad,
thick-bladed weapon, double-edged, with a bronze hilt like a cross" (010-033). The
weapon produces four identically sized wounds in Doctor Riese's chest — "all the size
and shape that the weapon the girl had given me would have made" (010-048).

## Narrative Role

### Plot Significance

The dagger is the murder weapon in the death of Doctor Riese, the Temple's physician.
Gabrielle carries it openly, apparently in a dissociated or drugged state, and the Op
takes it from her (010-033). He places it beside Riese's body (010-068) but later
returns to find it gone — along with the body and all traces of blood, except a faint
yellow stain (010-100). The dagger's disappearance and later reappearance
become key to reconstructing events at the Temple. Aaronia Haldorn retrieved it and
"wrapped the dagger in the dressing-gown, and stuck them in a corner where the police
could find them" (012-032), manufacturing evidence to support Gabrielle's guilt. The
Op later uses the dagger as a weapon in his own defense during the chaotic
confrontations in the Temple corridors (011-024, 011-025, 011-044).

### Associated Characters

- **[[Gabrielle Leggett]]** — found carrying the bloody dagger; either committed the murder with it or was framed for doing so (010-027)
- **[[The Continental Op]]** — takes the dagger from Gabrielle, examines it, loses it, and later uses it defensively (010-033, 011-025, 011-044)
- **[[Doctor Riese]]** — the victim, killed by four stab wounds matching the dagger's blade dimensions (010-048)
- **[[Aaronia Haldorn]]** — retrieved the dagger, concealed it, then manufactured evidence by wrapping it in Gabrielle's dressing-gown (012-032)
