# Rhino Tingley

Rhino Tingley is a San Francisco underworld figure connected to the diamond smuggling operations that form part of the backstory to the Leggett case in [[the-dain-curse|The Dain Curse]].

## In the Novel

Tingley is mentioned in connection with the diamond-smuggling network that [[edgar-leggett|Edgar Leggett]] was entangled with before his murder. The Continental Op's investigation into Leggett's past brought him into contact with various criminal associates, including Tingley, who operated in the San Francisco area.

Tingley's nickname and underworld connections place him among the minor criminal figures that populate the margins of Hammett's San Francisco — small-time operators whose paths cross with the larger forces of the Leggett diamond conspiracy.
