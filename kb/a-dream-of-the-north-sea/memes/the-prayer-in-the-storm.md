# The Prayer in the Storm

The **Prayer in the Storm** is a striking episode during the novel's great gale. Lewis Ferrier, moved by the extremity of the situation, suggests that the crew and passengers gather for prayer. Skipper Larmor organizes the meeting, and in the midst of the howling wind and crashing seas, the fishermen and their guests join in an act of collective faith. Afterward, Ferrier 'shook hands with Larmor before they once more faced the war of the night' (006-003) — a moment of solidarity that transcends social class.
