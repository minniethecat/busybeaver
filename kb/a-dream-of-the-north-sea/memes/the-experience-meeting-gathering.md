# The Experience Meeting Gathering

The **Experience Meeting Gathering** depicts the distinctive religious practice of the North Sea fishermen. In place of formal liturgy or clerical mediation, the smacksmen shared personal testimonies — a practice that Runciman describes as a maritime adaptation of evangelical worship. 'In some instances, the smacksmen had substituted the experience meeting for the confessional' (010-004), creating a form of spiritual expression uniquely suited to their rough, honest, and democratic culture.
