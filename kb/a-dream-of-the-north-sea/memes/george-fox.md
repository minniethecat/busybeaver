# George Fox

**George Fox** (1624–1691), founder of the Religious Society of Friends (Quakers), is referenced in James Runciman's *A Dream of the North Sea* in connection with the religious culture of the fishermen. The reference to Fox situates the fishermen's experiential, anti-institutional faith within a longer tradition of English religious dissent.
