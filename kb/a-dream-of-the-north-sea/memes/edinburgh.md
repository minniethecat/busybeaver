# Edinburgh

**Edinburgh** is the Scottish capital and university city where Lewis Ferrier completed the first stage of his medical education. Runciman notes that Ferrier "came south to Cambridge after he had done well at Edinburgh" (002-003), establishing the young doctor's credentials before his transformative journey to the North Sea.
