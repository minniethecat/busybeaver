# The Great Gale

The **Great Gale** is the central dramatic episode of James Runciman's *A Dream of the North Sea*. Over the course of thirty-six hours, the full fury of the North Sea is unleashed upon the Mission's yacht and the surrounding trawling fleet, creating a crisis that reveals the character of every person aboard.
