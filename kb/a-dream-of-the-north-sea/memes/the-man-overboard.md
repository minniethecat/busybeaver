# The Man Overboard

The **Man Overboard** incident occurs during the height of the great gale. A crew member is swept from the deck by a violent sea — 'a low, strangled moan made them both start' — and Marion Dearsley, 'pointing with convulsive fingers,' raises the alarm. (003-007) The ensuing rescue operation, carried out in near-impossible conditions, demonstrates both the seamanship of the fishermen and the desperate mutual obligation that bound the North Sea community.
