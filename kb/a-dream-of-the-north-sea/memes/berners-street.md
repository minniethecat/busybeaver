# Berners Street

**Berners Street** is a street in central London where James Nisbet and Co., the publishers of *A Dream of the North Sea*, were located at number 21. The address — "London: James Nisbet and Co., 21, Berners Street, W." — appears on the novel's title page and situates the work within the world of Victorian religious publishing, of which Nisbet was a leading house.
