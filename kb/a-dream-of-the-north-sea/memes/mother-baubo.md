# Mother Baubo

**Mother Baubo** is a figure from Greek mythology referenced in James Runciman's *A Dream of the North Sea*. In the Eleusinian Mysteries, Baubo was the servant who made the grieving Demeter laugh through obscene jesting — a figure of earthy humor and irreverence. Her brief mention in the novel reflects the breadth of Runciman's literary reference, reaching from the Bible and evangelical hymns to classical mythology.
