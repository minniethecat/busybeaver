# The Launch of the Hospital Cruiser

The **Launch of the Hospital Cruiser** represents the realization of the Mission's central ambition. Runciman makes an impassioned appeal for funding: 'Give the Mission to Deep Sea Fishermen its hospital ship, for every fleet that scours the trawling grounds, but especially a fast steam cruiser — a Robert Cassall — so that the wounded fisherman' may receive prompt care. (026-003) The launch of this vessel marks a turning point in the welfare of the North Sea fishing community.
