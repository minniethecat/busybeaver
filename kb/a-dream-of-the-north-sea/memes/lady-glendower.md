# Lady Glendower

**Lady Glendower** is a minor aristocratic figure mentioned in James Runciman's *A Dream of the North Sea*. Her brief appearance in the text reflects the social range of the Mission's supporters — from the Queen herself to the titled philanthropists whose patronage was essential to Victorian charitable enterprises.
