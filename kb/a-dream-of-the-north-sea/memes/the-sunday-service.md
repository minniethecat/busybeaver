# The Sunday Service

The **Sunday Service** aboard the Mission yacht is one of the most memorable episodes in Runciman's novel. 'The yacht was transformed into a mission hall' (009-006), and fishermen from across the fleet gathered for worship. The service featured the fishermen's distinctive hymn-singing — lively, joyful, and utterly unlike the somber church music of Victorian England — and represented the Mission's spiritual work at its most intimate and effective.
