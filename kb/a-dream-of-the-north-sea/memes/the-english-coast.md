# The English Coast

The **English coast** — specifically the eastern seaboard facing the North Sea — is the geographic boundary that defines the world of Runciman's novel. Every fishing voyage began by crossing from this coast into the open sea, and for the families of the smacksmen, the coast represented the threshold between safety and danger, home and the unknown.
