# The Collision at Sea

The **Collision at Sea** occurs during the confusion of the great gale. As vessels struggle to maintain position in heavy seas and reduced visibility, a smack and another vessel come perilously close to disaster. 'There was a shock, and Ferrier thought the mainsheet had parted; then the crashing and grinding sound' of hull against hull — a moment of pure terror that, miraculously, does not end in catastrophe. (003-007)
