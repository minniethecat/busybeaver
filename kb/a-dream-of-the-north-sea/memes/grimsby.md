# Grimsby

**Grimsby** (Great Grimsby) is a fishing port on the Lincolnshire coast, and alongside Yarmouth and Hull, one of the principal bases for the North Sea trawling fleets in Runciman's time. The port represented the northern reach of the east-coast fishing industry that sustained the distant-water trawling economy.
