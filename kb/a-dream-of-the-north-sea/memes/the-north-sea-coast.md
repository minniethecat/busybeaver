# The North Sea Coast

The **North Sea coast** is the shoreline that borders the North Sea — the margin between the settled world of land and the wild realm where the fishermen worked and too often died. For the families in Yarmouth, Hull, and Grimsby, this coastline was a vantage point from which they watched for returning sails, and from which news of disaster arrived.
