# The Wreck of the Smack

The **Wreck of the Smack** is one of the most harrowing episodes in Runciman's novel. During the great gale, a fishing smack — 'almost like a buoy in a tideway' — is overwhelmed by the sea. 'The sea came over her, screaming as it swept aft,' and despite the desperate efforts of her crew, the vessel founders, adding her crew to the 'score of men in the North Sea every year' who never return to port. (005-006, 008-002)
