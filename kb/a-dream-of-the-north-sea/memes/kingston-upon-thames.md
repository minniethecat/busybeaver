# Kingston upon Thames

**Kingston upon Thames** is the Surrey town where James Runciman resided and from which he dated his preface to *A Dream of the North Sea* on May 1, 1889. It represents the author's home base — far from the North Sea storms he described with such vivid authority, but close enough to London's publishing world for a working journalist.
