# Miss Ranken

**Miss Ranken** is a character in James Runciman's *A Dream of the North Sea* (1876). Lena Ranken is Mr. Blair's niece, accompanying her uncle on the North Sea expedition. Described as creasing 'her brow in pert little wrinkles,' she brings a note of intelligent skepticism to the proceedings (007-005). When asked for her opinion on a moral question, she deflects with characteristic modesty: 'I'm not sure that I know anything; Marion there studies things' — a remark that reveals her sharp awareness of social dynamics among the expedition's members.
