# The Rescue of the Crew

The **Rescue of the Crew** is a moment of exhilaration amid the gale's devastation. When a smack is seen to be in distress, the Mission yacht maneuvers to render assistance. In conditions that make every movement perilous, the crew manage to take the distressed fishermen aboard — an act of seamanship and courage that Runciman presents as the highest expression of the maritime code. The episode demonstrates why the fishermen's mutual obligation to rescue was 'the binding thread of community' in the North Sea.
