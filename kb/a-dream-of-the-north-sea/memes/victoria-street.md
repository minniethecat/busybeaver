# Victoria Street

**Victoria Street** is a major thoroughfare in Westminster, London, mentioned in connection with the social world of Runciman's novel. As a prominent London street in the Victorian era, it represented the metropolitan center of power and influence from which the Mission to Deep Sea Fishermen sought support for its work among the North Sea fleets.
