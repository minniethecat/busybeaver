# The Mission Expedition

The **Mission Expedition** is the framing event of Runciman's novel. Organized by Henry Fullerton, the expedition brings together Lewis Ferrier, Marion Dearsley, Mrs. Walton, Tom Lennard, and Mr. Blair aboard the Mission's yacht for a voyage to the distant North Sea trawling grounds. Fullerton's purpose was to show the young doctor 'something of the wild work that goes on' among the fishing fleets (002-005), but the expedition becomes far more than an educational tour — it is a transformative journey for everyone aboard.
