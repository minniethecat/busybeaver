[P02-001] To

[P02-002] **Samuel Rogers, Esquire.**

[P02-003] My Dear Sir,

[P02-004] Let me have *my* Pleasures of [[memory|Memory]] in connection with this book, by dedicating it to a Poet whose writings (as all the world knows) are replete with generous and earnest feeling; and to a Man whose daily life (as all the world does not know) is one of active sympathy with the poorest and humblest of his kind.

[P02-005] Your faithful friend,

[P02-006] **Charles Dickens**.
