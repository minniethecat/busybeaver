# About This Wiki

This wiki is a structured knowledge base for Charles Dickens's fourth novel,

*The Old Curiosity Shop*, first published serially in 1840–1841 as part of

the periodical *Master Humphrey's Clock*.

## Source Material

- **Title**: *The Old Curiosity Shop*

- **Author**: Charles Dickens

- **First Publication**: 1840–1841 (serialized in *Master Humphrey's Clock*)

- **Edition**: Public domain

## Usage Notes

This wiki is designed for literary scholars, students, and Dickens enthusiasts

who wish to explore the characters, places, events, themes, and structure of

the novel in a linked, searchable format.
