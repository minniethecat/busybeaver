# The Miners

The working men who dig into [[The Princess and the Goblin|the mountain]] for precious metals. Led by [[peter]] and including his son [[curdie]], the miners form the first line of defense against [[goblins|the goblins]]' underground threat. Their practical knowledge of tunnels and excavation proves decisive in the novel's climax (001-004, 032-002).

## Role in the Story

The miners function as both world-builders and plot-drivers. Their excavations first uncovered the [[goblins|goblin]] tunnels, setting the entire conflict in motion:

> In the course of digging, the miners came upon many of these natural conduits, and through them the goblins had access to the upper world (001-004).

When [[goblins|the goblins]] plot to flood the mine and kidnap [[irene|Princess Irene]], it is the miners — organized by [[peter]] and warned by [[curdie]] — who mount the counter-offensive. They seal the goblin tunnels and flood them instead, ending the threat (031-046, 032-005).

## Composition

The miners are a community of hard-working, practical men living on the mountain. They include [[peter]] (the foreman), [[curdie]] (his son and the novel's hero), and their families. The miners' wives and children appear in the background, with [[curdie-mother|Curdie's mother]] notably providing care to the wounded (012-002).

## Key Relationships

- [[peter]] — foreman and leader of the miners
- [[curdie]] — Peter's son, a miner by trade, whose underground knowledge saves [[irene|the princess]]
- [[goblins]] — the miners' ancient enemies, competing for the mountain's underground spaces
- [[king-papa]] — the king who summons the miners for the final battle

## Significance and Themes

The miners embody MacDonald's vision of virtuous labor. Unlike the scheming [[goblins|goblins]] who dig for conquest, the miners work honorably for their living. Their practical courage — facing real physical danger without the [[courage-and-faith|faith]] that guides [[irene]] — provides a complementary form of heroism to [[irene|Irene]]'s spiritual journey. The miners' victory over [[goblins|the goblins]] is achieved through skill, organization, and loyalty to [[king-papa|their king]].
