# The Lawn

The lawn is the grassy open space within the [[the-garden|garden]] of the [[the-palace|king's house]] on [[the-mountain|the mountains]]ide. It serves as a recur[[the-ring|ring]] setting for scenes of play, supernatural intrusion, and homecoming in "The Princess and the Goblin."

## Location

The lawn is situated within the garden, visible from [[the-palace|the palace]]. It is where [[irene|Irene]] plays in the daytime:

> Irene, who was playing on a lawn in the garden, heard the distant blast of a horn (010-003)

The lawn is accessible to [[the-goblins-creatures|goblin creatures]] who come up from the [[the-mountain|mountain]] at night:

> gambolling on the lawn in the moonlight (013-003)

The [[the-goblins-creatures|goblin creatures]] treat the lawn as a novel surface:

> a smooth lawn such as they had never seen in their own underground country (013-006)

The [[men-at-arms]] monitor the lawn for [[goblins|goblin]] activity:

> the moment they should leave the lawn, to report the place clear (013-007)

## Key Events

1. **[[irene|Irene]] and the King** (Ch. 10): [[irene|Irene]] is playing on the lawn when she hears [[king-papa|the king]]'s horn (010-003).

2. **Creature Sightings** (Ch. 13): The [[the-goblins-creatures|goblin creatures]] are seen gambolling on the lawn in [[light|moonlight]] (013-003, 013-006). This leads to the [[men-at-arms]]' increased vigilance.

3. **The Thread's Destination** (Ch. 23): [[irene|Irene]] and [[curdie|Curdie]] emerge from their underground journey onto the lawn:

   > they came out upon the lawn in the garden of the king's house (023-004)

   The lawn thus serves as the endpoint of [[the-magical-thread|the thread]]'s guidance — the safe return to home.

4. **[[curdie|Curdie]]'s Watch** (Ch. 25): [[curdie|Curdie]] is shot at while on the lawn du[[the-ring|ring]] his night watch (025-007).

## Narrative Role

The lawn functions as:
- **A threshold space** between [[the-palace|palace]] interior and wild [[the-mountain|mountain]] exterior
- **A marker of safety**: emerging onto the lawn means returning to the human world
- **A contested ground**: [[the-goblins-creatures|goblin creatures]] trespass on the lawn, asserting their reach into human territory
