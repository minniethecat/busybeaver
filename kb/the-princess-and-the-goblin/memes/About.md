# About This Wiki

## Source Material

This wiki is based on **The Princess and the Goblin**, a children's fantasy novel by Scottish author **George MacDonald**, first published in **1872**. The novel follows the adventures of Princess Irene and her friend Curdie as they confront the goblins who dwell beneath the mountains.

## Usage Notes

This knowledge wiki catalogs the characters, places, concepts, events, and chapters of the novel. Use the search bar to find specific entries, or browse by category from the home page.
