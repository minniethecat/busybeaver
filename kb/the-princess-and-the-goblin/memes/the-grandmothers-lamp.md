# The Grandmother's Lamp

A supernatural lamp that burns perpetually in [[irenes-great-great-grandmother|the grandmother]]'s [[the-grandmothers-tower|tower chamber]], visible across the [[the-mountain|mountain]]. It guides [[irenes-great-great-grandmother|the grandmother]]'s pigeons home and serves as a beacon of safety and watchfulness, described by [[irene|Irene]] as like a "star" shining through the night (014-002, 014-009).

## Description and Properties

The lamp is first mentioned when [[irene|Irene]] visits her [[irenes-great-great-grandmother|grandmother]]'s [[the-grandmothers-tower|tower room]]. It is described as a constant presence:

> "The great lamp that sent its light out through the roof, and doubtless guided her pigeons home through the darkest night." (014-009)

The lamp glows with a soft, steady light and is visible from the outside, acting as a celestial beacon. [[irene|Irene]] later refers to her [[irenes-great-great-grandmother|grandmother]]'s tower light as being like a moon or star (011-004–011-010). Unlike ordinary lamps, it never needs refilling and never goes out.

## Relationship to Pigeons

The [[irenes-great-great-grandmother|grandmother]] keeps a flock of beautiful white pigeons that roost in her tower. The lamp serves as their homing beacon:

- The pigeons come and go freely through openings in [[the-grandmothers-tower|the tower]] (003-073)
- They feed themselves and return to [[irenes-great-great-grandmother|the grandmother]] (003-079)
- The lamp on the roof guides them home through [[darkness]] (014-009)
- The pigeons themselves may carry messages or serve as symbols of [[irenes-great-great-grandmother|the grandmother]]'s reach

The lamp represents [[irenes-great-great-grandmother|the grandmother]]'s benevolent oversight. Like the magical [[the-magical-thread|thread]], it is a silent, constant presence that guides and protects without demanding acknowledgment.

## Symbolic Meaning

The lamp functions at multiple symbolic levels:

- **Watchfulness**: The lamp shines continuously, suggesting [[irenes-great-great-grandmother|the grandmother]] never sleeps and is always watching over [[irene|Irene]]
- **Guidance**: Like a lighthouse or star, [[light|the lamp]] provides direction to those who look for it
- **Divine presence**: In MacDonald's Christian allegorical framework, [[light|the lamp]] evokes the biblical imagery of light as divine truth

Together with the fire roses and the spinning wheel, [[light|the lamp]] completes [[irenes-great-great-grandmother|the grandmother]]'s symbolic toolkit — fire for purification, [[the-magical-thread|thread]] for guidance, and light for watchfulness.
