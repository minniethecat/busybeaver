## Overview

This directory indexes every location in *Murder by the Clock*, dynamically drawn from the wiki's [[place]]-type entries. The [[endicott-house|Endicott mansion]] serves as the novel's primary setting — a sealed crime scene where [[lieutenant-valcour|Lieutenant Valcour]] conducts his investigation over the course of a single night. Several of its rooms (the [[endicott-house-balcony|balcony]], [[endicott-house-attic|attic]], [[herbert-endicotts-bedroom|bedroom]]) are individually documented as they become key locations in the murder investigation.
