## Summary

At 6:30 a.m., [[lieutenant-valcour|Lieutenant Valcour]] confronts [[mrs-endicott|Mrs. Endicott]] in [[endicott-house-attic|the attic]] of the Endicott house. He presents two competing theories of the crime: either [[mrs-endicott|Mrs. Endicott]] herself shot her husband to protect Hollander, or she is harboring [[marge-myles|Marge Myles]] — whom [[lieutenant-valcour|Valcour]] now believes to be the real killer. [[mrs-endicott|Mrs. Endicott]] remains defiant, and [[lieutenant-valcour|Valcour]] places her under formal arrest pending a full search of the house.

## Valcour's Accusation

[[lieutenant-valcour|Valcour]] lays out his case methodically. He reveals that he has found the [[the-postscript-forgery|forged postscript]] letter in Endicott's trunk [029-009] and explains that [[marge-myles|Marge Myles]] came to the house to retrieve it, believing Endicott would be away at her apartment [029-013]. When Endicott unexpectedly returned, the confrontation triggered his [[herbert-endicotts-heart-condition|heart attack]] [029-017]. [[lieutenant-valcour|Valcour]] then presents [[mrs-endicott|Mrs. Endicott]] with two stark alternatives: either she shot Endicott to prevent him from accusing Hollander, or she is hiding [[marge-myles|Marge Myles]] somewhere within the house [029-024].

## Mrs. Endicott's Response

[[mrs-endicott|Mrs. Endicott]] remains composed and dismissive. She challenges [[lieutenant-valcour|Valcour]]'s logic, pointing out that the slipper found on the balcony was hers, not [[marge-myles|Marge Myles]]'s [029-025]. When [[lieutenant-valcour|Valcour]] suggests that cooperation might benefit her, she responds with indifference: "Any course that I might take can have but a common, a desired ending. The method of achievement is utterly inconsequential to me" [029-028].

## Aftermath

[[lieutenant-valcour|Valcour]] concludes that [[mrs-endicott|Mrs. Endicott]]'s obsession with death renders further argument futile [029-029]. He escorts her downstairs, places her under formal arrest, and orders [[cassidy|Cassidy]] to detain her in the entrance hall under O'Brian's guard while the police prepare to search the entire house [030-003].
