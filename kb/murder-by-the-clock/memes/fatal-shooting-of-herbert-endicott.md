## Overview

Despite surviving [[hollander|Thomas Hollander]]'s knife attack, [[herbert-endicott|Herbert Endicott]] is killed hours later by a gunshot fired from the balcony outside his room. The fatal shooting occurs while Endicott lies unconscious, and the bullet's trajectory becomes a crucial piece of evidence. [[lieutenant-valcour|Valcour]] later determines that the shot was fired by someone inside the house who had access to the balcony [019-031].

## Chronology

- **The shot is heard**: A single shot rings out from the balcony, killing the unconscious Endicott [019-031].
- **[[lieutenant-valcour|Valcour]] questions the timeline**: He focuses on establishing who was where at the time of the shot — particularly whether the sound would have carried to the kitchen or other rooms [019-031].
- **[[nurse-vickers|Nurse Vickers]] is interviewed**: [[lieutenant-valcour|Valcour]] questions [[nurse-vickers|Nurse Vickers]], who was attending [[mrs-endicott|Mrs. Endicott]], about what she heard and the behavior of [[roberts|Roberts]] afterward [019-029][019-034].
- **Ballistic analysis**: [[lieutenant-valcour|Valcour]] considers the caliber of the bullet and the angle of its course to determine whether it could have been fired by one of his own officers' weapons [017-053].

## Participants

- **[[herbert-endicott|Herbert Endicott]]** — The victim, already unconscious from an earlier collapse.
- **[[lieutenant-valcour|Lieutenant Valcour]]** — The detective who must now solve a murder that occurred under police protection.
- **[[dr-sanford-worth|Dr. Sanford Worth]]** — The physician present in the house.
- **[[nurse-vickers|Nurse Vickers]]** — Attending [[mrs-endicott|Mrs. Endicott]] during the shooting; provides testimony about the night's events [019-022][019-029].

## Significance

Endicott's murder is the central crime of the novel and represents a catastrophic failure of the police cordon [[lieutenant-valcour|Valcour]] established. The fact that Endicott is killed while under police guard raises the stakes dramatically — the murderer is operating from within the sealed house. The shooting forces [[lieutenant-valcour|Valcour]] to consider every occupant of the Endicott household as a potential suspect.

## Evidence

- The bullet's trajectory from the balcony [019-031]
- Ballistic analysis comparing the fatal bullet to police weapons [017-053]
- [[nurse-vickers|Nurse Vickers]]'s testimony about the shot and [[roberts|Roberts]]'s reaction [019-029][019-034]
