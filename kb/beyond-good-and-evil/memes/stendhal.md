## Overview

Stendhal (Marie-Henri Beyle, 1783–1842) was a French novelist. Nietzsche references him in *Beyond Good and Evil* §256 as one of the great European "higher men" who transcended national boundaries.

## In *Beyond Good and Evil*

> I think of such men as Napoleon, Goethe, Beethoven, Stendhal, Heinrich Heine, Schopenhauer... [008-019]

Stendhal is praised by Nietzsche as an atheist and psychologist in §39 of *Beyond Good and Evil*:

> Stendhal furnishes a last feature of the atheist who is not yet free enough... [002-016]
