The **injection cock** is a valve that controls the admission of cold water into the [[condenser]] of a [[steam]] engine. A jet of cold water, introduced through the injection cock, condenses the [[eduction|exhaust]] steam and maintains the partial vacuum necessary for the engine's efficient operation. The injection cock is one of the principal manual controls available to the engine operator and is present in both land and [[marine-engine|marine]] installations.

## Function

In a [[condensation|condensing engine]], the [[eduction|exhaust]] steam after performing work in the [[cylinder]] is discharged into the [[condensation|condenser]]. The injection cock supplies the cold water spray that collapses this steam back into liquid:

> [010-045] To open the injection cock a little, to enable water to enter for the condensation of the steam.

The dramatic reduction in volume when steam condenses (approximately 1,600:1) creates the vacuum on the [[eduction|exhaust side]] of the piston, significantly increasing net power output.

## Construction

Marine injection cocks, which draw seawater directly, are robustly constructed of brass or gunmetal to resist corrosion:

> [040-108] The sea injection cocks are usually made in the same fashion as the sea blow-off cocks, and of about the same size, or rather larger. The injection cock is generally made with a hemispherical or conical plug.

## Placement in horizontal air pumps

[[john-penn|John Penn]] and other [[marine-engine|marine engine]]ers introduced injection cocks at each end of the [[air-pump]] in horizontal configurations, improving condensation efficiency:

> [040-019] I introduced an injection cock into each end of the air pump in steam vessels, from which I had obtained advantageous results; and in all horizontal air pumps I would recommend the piston and valve plates to be so constructed that the whole of the water will be expressed by the piston.

> [040-023] I would also recommend an injection cock to be introduced at each end of the pump.

## Operational significance

The injection cock, together with the [[throttle-valve]] and the [[blow-off]] cocks, forms part of the engine operator's primary control set. During maneuvering or when the engine is required to stop quickly, the operator may close the injection cock to break the vacuum and reduce braking effect. Conversely, opening the injection cock wider increases the condensing rate and deepens the vacuum.
