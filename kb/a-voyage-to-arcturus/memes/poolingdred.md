# Poolingdred

## Location

Poolingdred is a distinctive settlement on Tormance, situated in and around a cup-shaped mountain. Joiwind identifies it by pointing to "the cup-shaped mountain" and declaring, "That's Poolingdred" [007-050]. The settlement overlooks a dark green lake at its base [007-172].

## Description

Poolingdred is characterized by its unusual cup-like geological formation — an approximately circular mountain that rises from the landscape "varied in height" [008-088]. At its base lies a dark green lake. Looking back after departing, Maskull can see Poolingdred "miles to the south" as a "distant cup-shaped mountain" on the horizon [008-084].

The waters of Poolingdred are distinctive: from a distance they appear tinted by color, but at the settlement itself the water is "crystal clear" [007-130]. This clarity of water symbolizes the moral and perceptual clarity of Poolingdred's inhabitants, particularly Joiwind and Panawe.

## Narrative Events

Poolingdred is Maskull's first destination on Tormance. Joiwind directs him there after their initial meeting, guiding him across the landscape [007-062]. At Poolingdred, Maskull meets Panawe and experiences the gentle, harmonious way of life that characterizes the planet's more benign regions [018-025].

Later, Maskull's time in Poolingdred is recalled by characters he meets elsewhere. Oceaxe scornfully remarks, "You came through Poolingdred, didn't you?" [010-032], contrasting its gentle ways with the harshness of Ifdawn. Digrung, a traveler from Matterplay, also mentions Poolingdred with interest [027-063].

## Symbolic Significance

Poolingdred represents the contemplative starting-point of Maskull's spiritual journey. Its cup-like shape suggests receptivity and containment, and its crystal-clear waters symbolize the unclouded perception possible in a state of spiritual innocence. As Maskull ventures further into Tormance, Poolingdred recedes both physically and metaphorically — his memory of its clarity becomes a point of comparison for the increasingly strange and disturbing experiences to come.

Joiwind's origin in Poolingdred is significant: she embodies the gentle wisdom that the settlement cultivates, and her guidance of Maskull represents the transmission of that wisdom before he must face harsher tests.
