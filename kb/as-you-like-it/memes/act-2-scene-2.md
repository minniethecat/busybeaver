(005-001)Enter Duke, with Lords.

(005-002)Duk. Can it be possible that no man saw them?

(005-003)It cannot be, some villaines of my Court

(005-004)Are of consent and sufferance in this

(005-005)1. Lo. I cannot heare of any that did see her,

(005-006)The Ladies her attendants of her chamber

(005-007)Saw her a bed, and in the morning early,

(005-008)They found the bed vntreasur'd of their Mistris

(005-009)2. Lor. My Lord, the roynish [[touchstone|Clown]], at whom so oft,

(005-010)Your Grace was wont to laugh is also missing,

(005-011)Hisperia the Princesse Gentlewoman

(005-012)Confesses that she secretly ore-heard

(005-013)Your daughter and her Cosen much commend

(005-014)The parts and graces of the Wrastler

(005-015)That did but lately foile the synowie [[charles|Charles]],

(005-016)And she beleeues where euer they are gone

(005-017)That [[youth]] is surely in their companie

(005-018)Duk. Send to his brother, fetch that gallant hither,

(005-019)If he be absent, bring his Brother to me,

(005-020)Ile make him finde him: do this sodainly;

(005-021)And let not search and inquisition quaile,

(005-022)To bring againe these foolish runawaies.

(005-023)Exeunt.
