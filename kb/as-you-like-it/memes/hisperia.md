# Hisperia

## Character Overview

Hisperia is [[celia|Celia]]'s gentlewoman, briefly mentioned in Act 2 Scene 2. When [[duke-frederick|Duke Frederick]] discovers that [[celia|Celia]] and [[rosalind|Rosalind]] have fled the court, a courtier reports that "Hisperia the Princesse Gentlewoman" (005-011) had overheard [[celia|Celia]] and [[rosalind|Rosalind]] praising [[orlando|Orlando]] and could confirm their affection for him. Hisperia thus serves as the unwitting informant whose report confirms to [[duke-frederick|Duke Frederick]] where the fugitives have likely gone.
