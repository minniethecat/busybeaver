(019-001)Enter [[touchstone|Clowne]] and Awdrie.

(019-002)Clow. We shall finde a time Awdrie, patience gentle

(019-003)Awdrie

(019-004)Awd. Faith the Priest was good enough, for all the

(019-005)olde gentlemans saying

(019-006)Clow. A most wicked Sir [[oliver|Oliuer]], Awdrie, a most vile

(019-007)Mar-text. But Awdrie, there is a [[youth]] heere in the

(019-008)Forrest layes claime to you

(019-009)Awd. I, I know who 'tis: he hath no interest in mee in the world: here comes the man you meane. Enter [[william|William]].

(019-010)Clo. It is meat and drinke to me to see a Clowne, by my troth, we that haue good wits, haue much to answer for: we shall be flouting: we cannot hold

(019-011)Will. Good eu'n [[audrey|Audrey]]

(019-012)Aud. God ye good eu'n William

(019-013)Will. And good eu'n to you Sir

(019-014)Clo. Good eu'n gentle friend. Couer thy head, couer

(019-015)thy head: Nay prethee bee couer'd. How olde are you

(019-016)Friend?

(019-017)Will. Fiue and twentie Sir

(019-018)Clo. A ripe age: Is thy name William?

(019-019)Will. William, sir

(019-020)Clo. A faire name. Was't borne i'th Forrest heere?

(019-021)Will. I sir, I thanke God

(019-022)Clo. Thanke God: A good answer:

(019-023)Art rich?

(019-024)Will. 'Faith sir, so, so

(019-025)Cle. So, so, is good, very good, very excellent good:

(019-026)and yet it is not, it is but so, so:

(019-027)Art thou wise?

(019-028)Will. I sir, I haue a prettie [[wit]]

(019-029)Clo. Why, thou saist well. I do now remember a saying: The Foole doth thinke he is wise, but the wiseman knowes himselfe to be a Foole. The Heathen Philosopher, when he had a desire to eate a Grape, would open his lips when he put it into his mouth, meaning thereby, that Grapes were made to eate, and lippes to open. You do [[love|loue]] this maid? Will. I do sir

(019-030)Clo. Giue me your hand: Art thou Learned?

(019-031)Will. No sir

(019-032)Clo. Then learne this of me, To haue, is to haue. For it is a figure in Rhetoricke, that drink being powr'd out of a cup into a glasse, by filling the one, doth empty the other. For all your Writers do consent, that ipse is hee: now you are not ipse, for I am he

(019-033)Will. Which he sir? Clo. He sir, that must marrie this woman: Therefore you Clowne, abandon: which is in the vulgar, leaue the societie: which in the boorish, is companie, of this female: which in the common, is woman: which together, is, abandon the society of this Female, or Clowne thou perishest: or to thy better vnderstanding, dyest; or (to wit) I kill thee, make thee away, translate thy life into death, thy libertie into bondage: I will deale in poyson with thee, or in bastinado, or in steele: I will bandy with thee in faction, I will ore-run thee with policie: I will kill thee a hundred and fifty wayes, therefore tremble and depart

(019-034)Aud. Do good William

(019-035)Will. God rest you merry sir.

(019-036)Exit

(019-037)Enter [[corin|Corin]].

(019-038)Cor. Our Master and Mistresse seekes you: come away,

(019-039)away

(019-040)Clo. Trip Audry, trip Audry, I attend,

(019-041)I attend.

(019-042)Exeunt.
