(009-001)Enter [[orlando|Orlando]], & [[adam|Adam]]

(009-002)Adam. Deere Master, I can go no further:

(009-003)O I die for food. Heere lie I downe,

(009-004)And measure out my graue. Farwel kinde master

(009-005)Orl. Why how now Adam? No greater heart in thee:

(009-006)Liue a little, comfort a little, cheere thy selfe a little.

(009-007)If this vncouth Forrest yeeld any thing sauage,

(009-008)I wil either be food for it, or bring it for foode to thee:

(009-009)Thy conceite is neerer death, then thy powers.

(009-010)For my sake be comfortable, hold death a while

(009-011)At the armes end: I wil heere be with thee presently,

(009-012)And if I bring thee not something to eate,

(009-013)I wil giue thee leaue to die: but if thou diest

(009-014)Before I come, thou art a mocker of my labor.

(009-015)Wel said, thou look'st cheerely,

(009-016)And Ile be with thee quickly: yet thou liest

(009-017)In the bleake aire. Come, I wil beare thee

(009-018)To some shelter, and thou shalt not die

(009-019)For lacke of a dinner,

(009-020)If there liue any thing in this Desert.

(009-021)Cheerely good Adam.

(009-022)Exeunt.
