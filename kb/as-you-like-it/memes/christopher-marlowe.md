# Christopher Marlowe

Christopher Marlowe was a major English playwright and poet of the Elizabethan era, a contemporary and influence on Shakespeare. In [[as-you-like-it|As You Like It]], [[phebe|Phebe]] quotes his poem *Hero and Leander* when she falls in love with [[ganimed|Ganymede]]:

> Dead Shepheard, now I find thy saw of might, / Who euer lov'd, that lou'd not at first sight? (015-085)

The quotation is rich with irony: Phebe invokes Marlowe's authority on love at first sight while falling in love with a woman she believes to be a boy.
