## About this index

Featured pages are the wiki's flagship content, selected by search relevance (backlink count as proxy for reader interest) and verified through `compute_quality.py` scoring. Each has been enriched through multiple rounds of RCH1/RCH2 editing to reach the featured threshold. This index provides a one-page overview of the wiki's best work, useful both for readers seeking authoritative entries and for editors identifying quality models by type.
