## About this index

Dostoevsky uses the crime genre to pose deeper questions: What is justice? Is legal punishment the same as moral punishment? Can suffering atone for a crime? These entries cover the factual (the pawnbroker's murder, the investigation), the procedural (police, confession, Siberian imprisonment), and the philosophical (the nature of law, the extraordinary man theory).
