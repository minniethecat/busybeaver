A chronological index of all wiki pages documenting the major events of *Crime and Punishment*. The novel's narrative is structured around a series of pivotal scenes — the overheard conversation in the tavern, the murder and its immediate aftermath, the cat-and-mouse confrontations with Porfiry, the dramatic confession, and the epilogue's quiet redemption. Each event page in this index provides a detailed account of its scene, its participants, its narrative function, and its contribution to the novel's thematic architecture.

## About this index

The event pages in this wiki reconstruct the novel's key scenes with close attention to narrative detail and thematic significance. Each entry situates its event within the chapter structure, identifies the participants and their motivations, and traces the event's consequences through the remainder of the plot. Events range from the inciting incident (Raskolnikov's overhearing of the murder plan) through the central crime and investigation to the resolution in confession and epilogue. Quality ratings reflect the depth of analysis in each entry; `standard` pages include PN-cited textual evidence and narrative analysis.
