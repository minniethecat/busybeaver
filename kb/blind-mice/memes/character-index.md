# Character Index

This page provides a complete alphabetical listing of all named characters who appear in C. Kay Scott's *Blind Mice*. Each entry links to a dedicated page with biographical details, narrative role, and cited passages from the novel.

The Merwent family and their social circle in Russellville and New York form the core cast. Major characters such as [[lucy-winter]], [[nannie-merwent]], and [[john-winter]] drive the central plot, while minor figures like [[miss-brennan]] and [[professor-walsh]] illuminate the social world of the early twentieth-century Midwest.
