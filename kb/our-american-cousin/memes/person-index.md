## About This Index

The character system of *Our American Cousin* reflects the social hierarchies of Victorian England
juxtaposed with the democratic sensibilities of American visitors. Characters range from the aristocratic
Trenchard family to servants, sailors, and rural Vermonters, creating a cross-section of mid-19th-century society.
