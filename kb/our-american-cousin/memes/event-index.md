## Dramatic Structure

The events of the play unfold across three acts, each building tension through
a combination of romantic complications, class-based comedy, and the looming threat
of financial ruin. The arrival of the American cousin serves as the catalyst that
disrupts the established order and ultimately leads to resolution.
