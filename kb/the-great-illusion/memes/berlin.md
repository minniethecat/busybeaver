# Berlin

Capital of the militarist power — whose financial vulnerability Angell exposes.

## Geographic Context

A significant location in the global landscape Angell maps in *The Great Illusion*, whose economic and political characteristics bear directly on the book's central argument.

## Role in the Book

Angell references this place to demonstrate the mechanisms of economic interdependence, the impossibility of cultural conquest, or the self-defeating nature of military competition. It serves as a concrete instance of the abstract forces his analysis describes.
