## Overview

Captain Bingo Hale is a key figure in Chapter 6 of *[[partners-in-crime-novel|Partners in Crime]]*, the [[affair-of-the-pink-pearl]]. Known to his friends as Bingo Hale, he is suspected of stealing a valuable pink pearl belonging to Lady Laura Merivale during a fancy dress ball. [[tommy-beresford]], assisting [[inspector-marriot]] in the investigation, ultimately determines that Hale is innocent.

## Background

Captain Hale was a friend of Sir Arthur and Lady Laura Merivale, and was "known to all his friends as a thoroughly good fellow" (006-014). He had been paying considerable attention to Lady Laura, which aroused Sir Arthur's jealousy (006-020). Both Sir Arthur and Captain Hale attended the same fancy dress ball where the pink pearl was stolen.

> "I won't believe, that Bingo Hale ever hurt a hair on Vere's head." (006-011)

## The Pink Pearl Incident

At the ball, Sir Arthur noticed that Captain Hale was wearing a costume with a missing piece, suggesting that "it might have been torn from Captain Hale's costume" (006-080). Furthermore, a note had been "slipped into Captain Hale's hand, asking him not to speak to Lady Laura" (006-079), a detail that complicated the investigation.

The key alibi fact emerged when a witness confirmed she was "actually dancing with Captain Hale last night at twelve o'clock" (006-071) — the very time the theft was alleged to have occurred. This placed Hale away from the scene of the crime.

[[tommy-beresford|Tommy Beresford]]'s investigation established that "Sir Arthur was a bad lot, and that Captain Hale was all right" (006-104). The real culprit was [[sir-arthur-merivale]] himself, who had been embezzling his wife's fortune and feared exposure. Hale was ultimately vindicated.

## Key Relationships

- **[[sir-arthur-merivale]]** — Friend turned accuser. Sir Arthur tried to frame Hale for the pearl theft to cover his own financial crimes.
- **Lady Laura Merivale** — Sir Arthur's wife, to whom Captain Hale had been paying attention, provoking Sir Arthur's jealousy.
- **[[inspector-marriot]]** — The [[scotland-yard|Scotland Yard]] detective who investigated the pearl theft and brought Tommy into the case.

## Appearances

Captain Hale appears in Chapter 6, the [[affair-of-the-pink-pearl]], where he becomes the prime suspect in the jewel theft at the fancy dress ball before Tommy clears his name.
