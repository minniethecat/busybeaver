**Comiskey Park** was the home of the Chicago White Sox from 1910 to 1990. Located at 35th Street and Shields Avenue on Chicago's South Side, it was the stage for Jack Keefe's professional dreams — the mound from which he threw his fast balls, spitters, and occasional tantrums.
