The **San Francisco Seals** of the Pacific Coast League represent Jack Keefe's brush with professional failure. When the White Sox send him down, the Seals are where he lands — a demotion that tests his confidence and his relationship with Florrie.

## In the Stories

Jack's time in San Francisco is a nadir. He frames it as temporary, a mere detour on his inevitable path back to the majors. But the experience colors his subsequent letters with a new awareness of the precariousness of his career. The PCL was the highest-level minor league, and a stint there could be either a stepping stone or a dead end.
