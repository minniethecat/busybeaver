The **Boston Red Sox** of the American League appear in Jack Keefe's letters primarily through their stars — Babe Ruth, whom Jack claims to have struck out twice, and Tris Speaker, the Hall of Fame center fielder. Jack's encounters with the Red Sox place his career in the context of the American League's elite.
