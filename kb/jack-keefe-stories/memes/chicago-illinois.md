**Chicago** is the setting for much of Jack Keefe's adult life — home of the White Sox, Comiskey Park, and the apartment he shares with Florrie and Little Al. As America's second city and a booming industrial metropolis, Chicago in the 1910s was also a great baseball town, with both the White Sox (American League) and Cubs (National League) drawing passionate crowds.
