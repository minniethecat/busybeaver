# Julie

Julie (Julie Le Tellier) is a Frenchwoman living in Petersburg who becomes one of Viéra Pavlovna's early friends and allies. As a foreigner, she stands somewhat outside the Russian social structure and provides Viéra with connections that help her navigate — and eventually escape — her constrained circumstances.

## Role in Viéra's Development

Julie's friendship with Viéra represents an important early bridge between the "cellar" of the Rozálsky household and the wider world. Her French background gives her a perspective that is not fully determined by Russian social conventions, and her social connections provide Viéra with access to circles that would otherwise be closed to her. She serves as one of the novel's several examples of decent people who are not "new people" in the full philosophical sense but who nonetheless contribute to the conditions that make the "new people's" liberation possible.
