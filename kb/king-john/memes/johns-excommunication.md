# John's Excommunication

## Event Overview

[[pandulph|Pandulph]] excommunicates [[king-john|King John]] (005-137) for refusing to accept Stephen Langton as Archbishop, triggering the French invasion.


Pandulph pronounces the sentence of excommunication upon King John before the assembled courts of England and France:

> Then by the lawfull power that I haue,
> Thou shalt stand curst, and excommunicate,
> And blessed shall he be that doth reuolt
> From his Allegeance to an heretique
>
> (005-136--139)

The excommunication places immense pressure on King Philip of France, who must choose between his new alliance with John and the spiritual authority of Rome:

> If thou stand excommunicate, and curst?
>
> (005-191)
