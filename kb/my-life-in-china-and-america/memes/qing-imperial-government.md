# Qing Imperial Government

## Overview

The Qing dynasty (1644–1912) was the imperial government of China throughout Yung Wings life. Its conflicted approach to modernization — embracing Western military technology while resisting deeper reform — shaped every aspect of his career. The dynasty approved the Educational Mission in 1870 and terminated it in 1881 (019-001).
