# Yung Wings First Return to China

## Overview

After eight years in America, Yung Wing returned to China in 1855. His Occidental education had wrought a profound metamorphosis in his nature; he felt like a being from a different world (P03-002). The challenge of finding work and re-adapting to Chinese society marked the beginning of his public career.
