# Yung Wings Father

## Overview

Yung Wings father was a resident of Nam Ping village on Pedro Island near Macao. He made the fateful decision to send his youngest son to Mrs. Gutzlaffs English school rather than to a traditional Confucian school, where his elder son was already placed (001-005).

## A Fateful Decision

Yung Wing described his fathers choice as a mystery — one that defied Chinese public sentiment and the conventional path to success through the civil service examinations. He speculated that his father, sensing the growing importance of foreign trade, wanted one son to learn English as an investment in the future. The father died in 1840 during the Opium War, leaving the family destitute (001-014).
