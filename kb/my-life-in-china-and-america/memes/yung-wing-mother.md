# Yung Wings Mother

## Overview

After the death of her husband in 1840 during the Opium War, Yung Wings mother was left to support four children without means (001-014). Her resilience and willingness to allow her youngest son to pursue foreign education were remarkable for a woman of her time and station.

## Supporting Her Sons Education

Despite her poverty, Yung Wings mother gave consent for him to leave the printing office and join the Morrison School when Dr. Benjamin Hobson came asking (001-018). This decision, made with reluctance and faith, ultimately changed the course of Chinese educational history.
