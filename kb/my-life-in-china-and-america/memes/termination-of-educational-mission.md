# Termination of the Educational Mission

## Overview

Conservative opposition within the Qing government, fueled by fears that the students were becoming too Westernized, led to the abrupt termination of the Educational Mission in 1881. All 120 students were recalled to China (019-001). The termination was a devastating personal and professional blow to Yung Wing.
