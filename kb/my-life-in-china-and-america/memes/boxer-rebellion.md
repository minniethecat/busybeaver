# Boxer Rebellion

## Overview

The Boxer Rebellion was a violent anti-foreign and anti-Christian movement that swept northern China. It represented everything Yung Wing had worked to prevent — the destructive clash between Chinese xenophobia and Western imperialism that education might have mediated.
