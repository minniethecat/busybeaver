[025-001]![The [[standard-ebooks|Standard Ebooks]] logo.](../../docs/wiki/images/002.png)

[025-002]*My Life in China and America*

[025-003]was published in 1909 by

[025-004][Yung Wing](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Yung_Wing).

[025-005]This ebook was produced for

[025-006][Standard Ebooks](https://standardebooks.org/)

[025-007]by

[025-008][Weijia Cheng](https://www.weijiarhymeswith.asia/),

[025-009]and is based on a transcription produced in 2017 by

[025-010]**Mary Glenn Krause**, **Chuck Greif**, **MFR**, **University of Toronto - Robarts Library**, and [Distributed Proofreaders](https://www.pgdp.net/)

[025-011]for

[025-012][[[project-gutenberg|Project Gutenberg]]](https://www.gutenberg.org/ebooks/54635)

[025-013]and on digital scans from the

[025-014][Internet Archive](https://archive.org/details/mylifeinchinaame00yunguoft).

[025-015]The cover page is adapted from

[025-016]*The European Factories, [[canton|Canton]]*,

[025-017]a painting completed in 1806 by

[025-018][William Daniell](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/William_Daniell).

[025-019]The cover and title pages feature the

[025-020]**League Spartan** and **Sorts Mill Goudy**

[025-021]typefaces created in 2014 and 2009 by

[025-022][The League of Moveable Type](https://www.theleagueofmoveabletype.com/).

[025-023]This edition was released on

[025-024]**June 30, 2026, 2:48 a.m.**

[025-025]and is based on

[025-026]**revision 6c3cb36**.

[025-027]The first edition of this ebook was released on

[025-028]May 29, 2026, 8:37 p.m.

[025-029]You can check for updates to this ebook, view its revision history, or download it for different ereading systems at

[025-030][standardebooks.org/ebooks/yung-wing/my-life-in-china-and-america](https://standardebooks.org/ebooks/yung-wing/my-life-in-china-and-america).
