# Taiping Heavenly Kingdom

## Overview

The Taiping Heavenly Kingdom was the revolutionary theocratic state led by Hong Xiuquan. At its height, it controlled territory containing approximately 30 million people. Yung Wings 1860 visit convinced him of its fatal flaws despite some progressive elements (010-001).
