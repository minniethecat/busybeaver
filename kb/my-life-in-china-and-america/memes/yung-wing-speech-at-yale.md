# Yung Wings Yale Commencement

## Overview

Yung Wing graduated from Yale College in 1854 with a Bachelor of Arts degree, winning prizes in English composition. His graduation was a historic milestone — he was the first Chinese person to earn a degree from an American university. The achievement validated his academic ability and strengthened his conviction about the value of Western education for Chinese students (005-001).
