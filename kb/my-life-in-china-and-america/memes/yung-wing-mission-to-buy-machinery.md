# Yung Wings Mission to Buy Machinery

## Overview

Tsang Kwoh Fan dispatched Yung Wing to America to purchase modern machinery for Chinas first major industrial complex. This mission demonstrated Yung Wings unique value as a trusted intermediary between China and the West (014-001).
