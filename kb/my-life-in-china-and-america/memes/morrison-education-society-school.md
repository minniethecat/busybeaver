# Morrison Education Society School

## Overview

The Morrison Education Society School was founded by Protestant missionaries to provide Western education to Chinese boys. Yung Wing entered the school after his time with Mrs. Gutzlaff (001-018). Under S. R. Browns leadership, it prepared the first Chinese students for study in America.
