# Wong Shing

## Overview

Wong Shing (Huang Sheng) was one of Yung Wings two classmates from the Morrison Education Society School who accompanied S. R. Brown to the United States in 1847. Along with Wong Foon, these three boys formed the vanguard of Chinese students studying in America.

## Journey to America

The three students sailed with Brown from Hong Kong to New York, beginning a journey that would transform their lives. Wong Shing later became a prominent figure in Hong Kongs Chinese community, contributing to journalism and public affairs.
