# Wong Foon

## Overview

Wong Foon (Huang Kuan) accompanied Yung Wing and Wong Shing to America in 1847 under S. R. Browns care. After completing his American preparatory studies, he went on to study medicine at the University of Edinburgh, becoming the first Chinese graduate of a European medical school.

## Legacy

Wong Foons career demonstrated the potential of Western-educated Chinese to acquire professional expertise abroad and return to serve their country. His path paralleled and validated Yung Wings vision for systematic educational exchange.
