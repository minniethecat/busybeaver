# Theodore Dwight Woolsey

## Overview

Theodore Dwight Woolsey served as President of Yale College from 1846 to 1871, including the period when Yung Wing was a student there. A distinguished classicist and scholar of international law, Woolsey represented the highest ideals of American higher education in the mid-19th century.

## Yung Wings Yale Years

Under Woolseys administration, Yung Wing excelled academically, winning prizes in English composition despite English being his second language. The classical liberal arts education Yung Wing received at Yale profoundly shaped his vision for Chinas modernization (005-001).
