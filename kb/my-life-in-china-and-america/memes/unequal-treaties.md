# Unequal Treaties

## Overview

The unequal treaty system that began with the Treaty of Nanking (1842) fundamentally shaped 19th-century China. These treaties granted extraterritoriality, opened treaty ports, fixed low tariffs, and included most-favored-nation clauses.

## Impact on Reform

The humiliations of the unequal treaty system motivated the Self-Strengthening Movement and later reform efforts, including Yung Wings educational mission. Reformers argued that only by modernizing could China regain its sovereign rights (020-008).
