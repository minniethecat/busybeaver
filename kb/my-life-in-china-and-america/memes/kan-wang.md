# Kan Wang (Shield King)

## Overview

The Kan Wang (Shield King), also known as Hong Rengan, was a cousin of the Taiping leader Hong Xiuquan and one of the most Western-oriented Taiping leaders. He had spent time in Hong Kong where he was exposed to Western ideas and Christian teaching.

## Meeting with Yung Wing

During Yung Wings visit to the Taiping capital at Nanking in 1860, the Kan Wang received him and listened to his reform proposals. Among the Taiping leadership, the Kan Wang showed the most genuine interest in modernization. However, Yung Wing concluded that even the most progressive Taiping leaders were incapable of implementing the systematic reforms China needed (011-013).
