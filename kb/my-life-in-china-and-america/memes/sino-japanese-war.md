# First Sino-Japanese War

## Overview

The First Sino-Japanese War was a humiliating defeat for China that demonstrated Japans successful modernization under the Meiji Restoration. The wars outcome discredited the narrow technological focus of the Self-Strengthening Movement and fueled demands for more fundamental reform.
