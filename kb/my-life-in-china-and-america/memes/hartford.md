# Hartford, Connecticut

## Overview

Hartford, Connecticut served as the headquarters of the Chinese Educational Mission from its establishment in the 1870s until its termination in 1881. The Mission built a dedicated building there to house and educate the 120 Chinese students (019-014).

## Yung Wings American Home

Yung Wing made Hartford his American home, marrying Marie Kellogg there and raising his two sons. The citys educational and cultural institutions provided an ideal environment for the Chinese students who studied at local schools before entering universities.
