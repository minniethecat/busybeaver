# Chin Lan Pin

## Overview

Chin Lan Pin (Chen Lanbin) was appointed by the Qing government as co-commissioner of the Chinese Educational Mission, serving alongside Yung Wing in Hartford, Connecticut. His role was to supervise the Chinese studies of the mission students and ensure they maintained their Confucian education.

## Tensions with Yung Wing

The relationship between Chin and Yung Wing was sometimes strained, reflecting the broader tension between the Missions educational goals and conservative fears about students becoming too Westernized. These tensions ultimately contributed to the Missions termination.
