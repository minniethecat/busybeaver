# Marie Yung

## Overview

Marie Louise Yung (née Kellogg) was the American wife of Yung Wing. Their marriage represented the personal dimension of Yung Wings bicultural life — a Chinese scholar-official married to an American woman, raising a family between two worlds.

## Family

Yung Wing and Marie had two sons, Morrison Brown Yung and Bartlett Golden Yung, to whom he dedicated his autobiography. The dedication to his "devoted sons" reflects the deep personal bonds that grounded Yung Wings public career.
