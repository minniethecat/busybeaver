# Modern Chinese Nationalism

## Overview

The late 19th century witnessed the birth of modern Chinese nationalism as intellectuals and reformers grappled with Chinas decline. This nationalism sought to reconcile pride in Chinese civilization with the need for radical modernization.

## Yung Wings Contribution

Yung Wings autobiography, written in English for an American audience, was itself an act of cultural diplomacy. By presenting China sympathetically while advocating reform, he helped shape Western understanding of Chinas modernization struggle.
