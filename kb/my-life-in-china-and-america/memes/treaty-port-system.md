# Treaty Port System

## Overview

The treaty port system established after the Opium Wars created zones of foreign residence and commerce along Chinas coast and major rivers. Cities like Shanghai, Canton, and Tientsin were transformed by the influx of foreign merchants, missionaries, and institutions.

## Macao as Proto-Treaty Port

Macao, where Yung Wing was born nearby, functioned as a de facto treaty port even before the Opium Wars. The Portuguese colony provided a foothold for foreign missionaries and traders, including the Gutzlaff school where Yung Wing began his education (001-002).
