# Death of Tsang Kwoh Fan

## Overview

Tsang Kwoh Fan died in 1872, shortly after the launch of the Chinese Educational Mission. His death was a severe blow to Yung Wings work, as it removed the Missions most influential champion at court. Without Tsangs protection, the Mission became increasingly vulnerable to conservative attack, culminating in its termination in 1881.
