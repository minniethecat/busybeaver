# Hundred Days Reform

## Overview

The Hundred Days Reform of 1898 was an ambitious but short-lived attempt by the Guangxu Emperor to modernize Chinas government. Yung Wing was in Peking during this period and associated with reformers. When the Empress Dowager Cixi staged a coup, Yung Wing narrowly escaped with his life (022-001).
