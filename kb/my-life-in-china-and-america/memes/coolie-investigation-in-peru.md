# Coolie Investigation in Peru

## Overview

The Qing government sent Yung Wing to Peru to investigate reports of abuse of Chinese coolies. His investigation documented brutal conditions and led to diplomatic negotiations. This mission reflected Yung Wings expanding role as a Qing diplomat (018-001).
