# Macao

## Overview

Macao was a Portuguese trading colony established in the 16th century on a peninsula and islands on the southern coast of China. By the early 19th century, it was one of the few locations where foreigners could reside and operate in China (001-002).

## Yung Wings Birthplace

Yung Wing was born in the village of Nam Ping on Pedro Island, about four miles southwest of Macao. The proximity to this foreign enclave shaped his early life, enabling his father to learn of Mrs. Gutzlaffs school through a neighbor who worked as a comprador in Macao (001-002).

## Educational Hub

Macao was home to the first Western schools available to Chinese children, including Mrs. Gutzlaffs school and later the Morrison Education Society School. It was also where Dr. Benjamin Hobson operated his missionary hospital.
