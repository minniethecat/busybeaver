# Pedro Island

## Overview

Pedro Island lies west of the Macao peninsula, separated by a channel about half a mile wide. The village of Nam Ping (South Screen) on this island was Yung Wings birthplace (001-002).

## Yung Wings Early Home

It was from Pedro Island that young Yung Wing attempted his famous escape from Mrs. Gutzlaffs school, hiring a covered boat to take himself and six older girls across the channel back to his home village. The escape was foiled mid-channel when a faster boat caught up with them (001-009).
