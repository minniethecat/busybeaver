# Coolie Trade

## Overview

The coolie trade was a system of indentured labor that transported Chinese workers to plantations and mines in the Americas and Southeast Asia. Often likened to a new form of slavery, it involved deception, kidnapping, and brutal conditions.

## Yung Wings Investigation

In 1873, the Qing government dispatched Yung Wing to Peru to investigate the conditions of Chinese coolies working there. His investigation and subsequent report led to diplomatic negotiations and efforts to protect Chinese laborers abroad.
