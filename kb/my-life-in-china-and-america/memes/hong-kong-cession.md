# Hong Kong Cession

## Overview

Under the Treaty of Nanking (1842), China ceded Hong Kong Island to Britain in perpetuity. This was later expanded by the Convention of Peking (1860) to include Kowloon, and by the 1898 lease of the New Territories.

## Significance for Yung Wing

The British presence in Hong Kong, adjacent to Yung Wings native Macao region, created the cosmopolitan environment that made his early English education possible. The colony became a crucial transit point for Chinese students heading to America.
