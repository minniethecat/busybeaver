# Second Opium War

## Overview

The Second Opium War (Arrow War) resulted in the Treaty of Tientsin, opening more ports and legalizing the opium trade. Harry Parkes, brother of Mrs. Gutzlaffs nieces, played a conspicuous role in instigating the conflict (001-011).
