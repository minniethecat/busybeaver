# First Opium War

## Overview

The First Opium War was a watershed in modern Chinese history. British military superiority forced the Qing to sign the Treaty of Nanking (1842), opening five ports, ceding Hong Kong, and establishing extraterritoriality. Yung Wing was a child during the war; his father died in 1840 while it was ongoing (001-014).
