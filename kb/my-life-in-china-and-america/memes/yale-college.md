# Yale College

## Overview

Yale College was Yung Wings alma mater, where he earned his Bachelor of Arts degree in 1854. The classical liberal arts curriculum profoundly shaped his intellectual development (005-001). His success at Yale demonstrated that Chinese students could excel in Western academic settings, validating his later educational mission.
