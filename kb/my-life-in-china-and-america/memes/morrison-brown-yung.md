# Morrison Brown Yung

## Overview

Morrison Brown Yung was the elder son of Yung Wing and Marie Kellogg Yung. His name reflects his fathers lifelong gratitude to the Morrison Education Society and to Samuel Robins Brown — the two institutions most responsible for Yung Wings education and career.

## Legacy

Along with his brother Bartlett Golden Yung, Morrison represented the next generation of the Chinese-American experience that his father pioneered. Yung Wings autobiography, dedicated to his two sons, was intended in part as an explanation of their fathers life and mission.
