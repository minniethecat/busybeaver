# Yung Wings Visit to the Taipings

## Overview

In 1860, Yung Wing undertook an expedition to the Taiping tea district and visited the rebel capital at Nanking. He met with Taiping leaders including the Kan Wang and presented seven reform proposals. Despite some sympathy for their cause, he concluded the Taipings were incapable of systematic reform (010-001).
