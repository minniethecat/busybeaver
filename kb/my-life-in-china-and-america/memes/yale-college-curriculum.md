# Yale College Curriculum

## Overview

When Yung Wing entered Yale College in 1850, the curriculum emphasized Greek, Latin, mathematics, rhetoric, and moral philosophy. This classical education, combined with his Chinese upbringing, gave Yung Wing a unique bicultural perspective that would define his lifes work.

## Yung Wings Experience

Yung Wing excelled in his studies despite financial hardships, winning prizes in English composition. His college days were formative in shaping his vision for Chinas modernization through Western education (005-001).
