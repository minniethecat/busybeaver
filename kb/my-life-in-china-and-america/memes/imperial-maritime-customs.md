# Imperial Maritime Customs Service

## Overview

The Imperial Maritime Customs Service was one of the most successful examples of Sino-Western cooperation in the late Qing period. Staffed largely by foreign nationals, it collected tariff revenue efficiently and funded many modernization projects.
