# Yung Wings First Journey to America

## Overview

In 1847, S. R. Brown returned to America due to health reasons and offered to bring Chinese students with him. Yung Wing, Wong Shing, and Wong Foon accepted. The 98-day voyage via the Cape of Good Hope marked the beginning of Yung Wings transformative American experience.
