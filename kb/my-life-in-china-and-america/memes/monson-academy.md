# Monson Academy

## Overview

Monson Academy in Monson, Massachusetts was the preparatory school where Yung Wing completed his American secondary education after arriving in the United States with S. R. Brown. The academy prepared him in the classical curriculum required for admission to Yale College.

## Yung Wings Experience

At Monson, Yung Wing applied himself to Greek, Latin, and mathematics — subjects that were entirely foreign to him but essential for college admission. His success at Monson demonstrated his academic ability and paved the way for his historic enrollment at Yale.
