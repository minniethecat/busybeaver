# Benjamin Hobson

## Overview

Dr. Benjamin Hobson was a British medical missionary who operated a hospital in Macao. He played a brief but crucial role in Yung Wings life by acting on Mrs. Gutzlaffs final request to locate the boy and enroll him in the Morrison School (001-017).

## Finding Yung Wing

Hobson tracked down Yung Wing, who was then working as a folder in a Catholic printing office earning $4.50 a month. He told Yung Wing of Mrs. Gutzlaffs last message before she sailed for America: to find the boy and place him in the Morrison Education Society School as soon as it opened.

## The Hospital Stay

Before taking Yung Wing to the Morrison School, Hobson had the boy stay with him at the hospital for a couple of months. During this time, Yung Wing worked with mortar and pestle preparing medicines and accompanied Hobson on his rounds visiting patients (001-018).
