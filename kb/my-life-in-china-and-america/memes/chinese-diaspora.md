# Chinese Diaspora

## Overview

The 19th century saw significant Chinese emigration to Southeast Asia, the Americas, and elsewhere. Yung Wing himself straddled this diaspora, becoming a naturalized American citizen while maintaining deep ties to China.

## Educational Dimension

The Chinese Educational Mission sent 120 students to America, creating a unique diaspora community in Hartford, Connecticut. Many of these students later became prominent figures in Chinese diplomacy, engineering, and government.
