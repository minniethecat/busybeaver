# Charles Gutzlaff

## Overview

The Reverend Charles (Karl) Gutzlaff was a Prussian Protestant missionary who arrived in East Asia in the 1820s. A gifted linguist, he translated the Bible into several Asian languages and served as an interpreter during the First Opium War.

## Connection to Yung Wing

While it was Gutzlaffs wife who directly taught Yung Wing, Charles Gutzlaffs missionary network and reputation in Macao created the institutional foundation for the school that gave Yung Wing his start. His role in Sino-Western relations exemplified the complex intertwining of missionary work, commerce, and imperialism.
