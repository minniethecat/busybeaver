# Domesticity and Social World in *Interim*

Much of *Interim* unfolds within the enclosed world of a London boarding house, where domestic routine and social ritual provide the scaffolding for Miriam Henderson's inward life. This page groups entries that examine two facets of that world.

The domestic sphere in *Interim* — meals, furnishings, household rhythms — is rendered with a quiet attention that elevates the ordinary. These entries trace how Richardson finds meaning in the textures of daily life.
