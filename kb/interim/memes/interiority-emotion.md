# Interiority and Emotion in *Interim*

Richardson's *Interim* turns inward, tracing the contours of Miriam Henderson's inner life with unprecedented fidelity. This page groups entries that examine two facets of that inward turn: interiority (the texture of self-awareness) and emotion (the felt register of daily life).

## Interiority

Interiority in *Interim* is not introspection in the conventional sense — it is the continuous, pre-reflective awareness that colours every moment. These entries explore how Richardson renders the feel of being inside a consciousness.
