## Overview

Grace Broom is one of the Broom sisters and Miriam Henderson's closest friend in *Interim*. She is characterized by her gentle, anxious affection for Miriam, her soft voice, and her deep emotional sensitivity. Grace lives with her widowed mother Mrs. Philps and her sister Florrie.

## Role in the Narrative

Grace appears throughout Miriam's Christmas visit, consistently expressing tender concern for Miriam's comfort and well-being. She greets Miriam at the door [001-001], escorts her to her room with "timid gentle kisses" [001-001], and repeatedly checks on her throughout the stay. Grace's anxious, pleading solicitude forms a constant emotional backdrop to the visit.

Grace confides in Miriam about personal difficulties, seeking her advice on matters involving a romantic relationship [001-014]. Their friendship is marked by physical affection — kisses, hand-holding — and by Grace's unwavering admiration, which Miriam sometimes finds both comforting and overwhelming.
