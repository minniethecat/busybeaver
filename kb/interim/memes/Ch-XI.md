## XI

[011-001] [[miriam-henderson|Miriam]] got up early the next morning and went to her [[window-in-interim|window]] in her nightgown. There was a thick August haze in the square. The air smelt moist. She leaned out into the chill of it. Her body was full of sleep and strength; all one strength from head to feet. She heard life in the [[silence-in-interim|silence]], and went through her getting up as quickly as possible, listening all the [[time-in-interim|time]] to the fresh silence.

[011-002] She went downstairs feeling like a balloon on a string; her feet touching the stairs lightly as if there were no weight in her body. At the end of the long journey came the smiling familiar surprise of the hall. The hall table was clear, a stretch of grey marble in the morning [[light-in-interim|light]]. The [[letter-in-interim|letters]] had been taken into the dining-[[room-in-interim|room]]. There was something, a package, on the far corner, a book package, with a note, Silurian blue, *[[eleanor|Eleanor]]*. Small straggly round handwriting, yes, Eleanor’s, *R. Rodkin, Esq*: *Ah.* Mr. *Rodkin*. How had she done it? When? Carrying off a book. Pretending she had forgotten, and writing. Sly cleverness. What a blessing she had gone. … Booming through her uneasiness came a great voice from the dining-room. Through the misty corridors of the *Dawn* it bellowed. She went gladly in towards poetry. Mrs. [[bailey|Bailey]] was presiding over an early breakfast. The Irishman, sitting back mirthfully in his chair on the far side of the table and at his side a big stout man with a bushy black beard, brilliant [[laughter-in-interim|laughing]] eyes staring at nothing from a flushed [[face-in-interim|face]]. Mrs. Bailey was watching him with a polite smile; he looked as though he were at [[dinner-in-interim|supper]]; making the room seem hot, obliterating the time of day. I expect you had a rough crossing, she said politely. I *saw* her, he bellowed flinging back his head and roaring out words and laughter together. She [[walking-in-interim|walks]] in *Beauty*. I saw her sandalled feet; upon the *Hills*.
