## Overview

Egypt (Égypte) appears in the Guide primarily as the historical context of the [[exodus|Exodus]] and as a symbol in prophetic allegory. [[maimonides|Maimonides]] references Egypt in discussing the historical circumstances of [[israel|Israel]]'s formation and the nature of divine deliverance.

## In the Guide

[[maimonides|Maimonides]] occasionally references Egypt when discussing historical examples of [[providence|divine providence]] and the formation of the [[israel|Israel]]ite nation. The [[exodus|Exodus]] from Egypt serves as the archetypal example of divine intervention in history.
