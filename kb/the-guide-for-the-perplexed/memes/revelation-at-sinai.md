## Overview

The Revelation at Sinai is the event in which [[god|God]] gave the [[law|Torah]] to [[moses|Moses]] and the people of [[israel|Israel]]. For [[maimonides|Maimonides]], this event establishes the unique status of Mosaic [[prophets|prophecy]] — a public revelation witnessed by an entire nation, not a private visionary experience.

## In the Guide

[[maimonides|Maimonides]] uses the Sinai revelation as a p[[accident|arad]]igm for [[wisdom|understanding]] the hierarchy of [[prophets|prophecy]]. Unlike other prophets who received private visions, [[moses|Moses]] received the [[law|Torah]] in a fully wakeful state, directly from [[god|God]], before the entire assembly of [[israel|Israel]].
