# [[the-secret-trust|The Secret Trust]]

The secret trust is the novel's central legal mechanism: a private arrangement, unknown to Magdalen and Norah, that redirects Noel Vanstone's fortune through Admiral Bartram to an undisclosed beneficiary. Wilkie Collins, who trained at Lincoln's Inn, constructs this device with lawyerly precision — and uses it to expose the injustice of a legal system that can strip daughters of their inheritance in one stroke and restore it in another, all without their knowledge or consent. This page aggregates every page connected to the secret trust's creation, operation, and eventual destruction.
