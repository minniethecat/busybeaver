# XIII.

*Extract from the East [[aldborough|Suffolk]] Argus.*

“[[aldborough|ALDBOROUGH]].—We notice with pleasure the arrival of visitors to this healthful and far-famed watering-place earlier in the season than usual during the present year. *Esto Perpetua* is all we have to say.

“VISITORS’ LIST.—Arrivals since our last. [[aldborough|North Shingles Villa]]—Mrs. Bygrave; Miss Bygrave.”
