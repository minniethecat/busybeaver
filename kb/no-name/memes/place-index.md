# Place Index

This page indexes the locations that form the geographical and social landscape of *No Name* (1862). Collins moves his characters across a carefully delineated map of mid-Victorian England — from the genteel countryside of [[combe-raven|Combe-Raven]] in Somerset, to the seaside resort of Aldborough, to the gloomy labyrinth of [[admiral-bartram|Admiral Bartram]]'s [[st-crux-in-the-marsh|St. Crux-in-the-Marsh]]. Each location serves as more than backdrop; it shapes the possibilities and constraints within which the novel's plot unfolds.
