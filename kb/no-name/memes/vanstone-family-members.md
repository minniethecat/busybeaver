# Vanstone Family Members

The Vanstone family sits at the heart of Wilkie Collins' *No Name* (1862). The novel's central conflict — the [[disinheritance|disinheritance]] of Magdalen and Norah Vanstone after their parents' sudden death — hinges on the legal distinction between legitimate and illegitimate children in Victorian England. This page aggregates all Vanstone family members across the wiki, from the tragic Andrew and Mrs. Vanstone to the scheming [[michael-vanstone|Michael Vanstone]] and his sickly son Noel.
