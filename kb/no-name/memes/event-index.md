# Event Index

This page indexes the significant plot events that drive the narrative of *No Name* (1862). Wilkie Collins structures the novel around a cascade of legal and personal catastrophes — the sudden [[vanstone-parents-death|death of the Vanstone parents]], the discovery of their daughters' illegitimacy, and Magdalen Vanstone's elaborate campaign to reclaim her inheritance through a series of daring deceptions.
