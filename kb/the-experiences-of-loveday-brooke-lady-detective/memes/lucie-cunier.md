## Overview

Lucie Cunier was a young Frenchwoman who acted as amanuensis to [[mrs-druce|Mrs. Druce]]. Her sudden vanishing after a visit from [[princess-dullah-veih|the Princess]] Dullah-Veih became the central mystery of Chapter 4, "A Princess's Vengeance."[PN=004-002]

## Role in the Stories

Lucie Cunier's disappearance occurred shortly after [[princess-dullah-veih|the Princess]] Dullah-Veih's visit to [[mrs-druce|Mrs. Druce]]'s household. [[loveday-brooke|Loveday Brooke]] was called in to investigate, uncovering a plot that involved [[princess-dullah-veih|the Princess]], [[major-druce|Major Druce]], and a carefully orchestrated scheme of abduction and concealment.[PN=004-002][PN=004-131]

Loveday eventually confronted those responsible, revealing the truth of what had become of "Lucie Cunier, lately your mother's amanuensis."[PN=004-131]
