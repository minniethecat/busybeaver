## Overview

Alguida is a small hamlet in South Italy, the ancestral home of [[count-mascagni|Count Mascagni]] and the birthplace of his daughter Lena. When [[loveday-brooke|Loveday Brooke]] investigates the disappearance of [[disappearance-of-irene-golding|Irene Golding]] in "[[missing|Missing!]]," her inquiries lead her to Alguida, where the family vault holds secrets crucial to solving the case.

## Location

Alguida is situated in South Italy, within a short distance — approximately fifteen miles — of a larger town or city. It is small enough to be described as a "hamlet," suggesting a rural settlement.

> "A small hamlet within fifteen miles of the place." [007-181]

The hamlet is also the location of the Mascagni family vault, where Lena is supposedly buried:

> "Buried in the family vault at Alguida." [007-173]

## Description

Alguida is depicted as a quiet, rural Italian hamlet, far removed from the cosmopolitan world of [[london|London]] where the Golding case originates. The presence of the Mascagni family vault ties the hamlet to an aristocratic lineage, suggesting a settlement with at least one grand family residence or church.

The hamlet's remoteness is emphasized by [[loveday-brooke|Loveday Brooke]]'s having to track Lena home from England to Alguida — a cross-continental investigation that demonstrates the international reach of [[ebenezer-dyer|Dyer]]'s agency.

> "He tracked Lena home to Alguida." [007-181]

Travelling actors are noted as passing through Alguida, suggesting it lies on a route used by itinerant performers — a detail that becomes relevant to the plot.

> "A company of actors, then passing through Alguida." [007-183]

## Significance

Alguida represents the farthest geographic reach of [[loveday-brooke|Loveday Brooke]]'s investigations — a South Italian hamlet that extends the series' scope beyond England. The hamlet's family vault serves as a symbol of the secrets buried within aristocratic lineages, a recurring theme in the stories. Alguida's remoteness from [[london|London]] highlights [[loveday-brooke|Loveday Brooke]]'s willingness to pursue the truth across national boundaries, marking the international dimension of [[ebenezer-dyer|Dyer]]'s agency.
