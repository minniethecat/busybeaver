## Overview

Langford Cross was the railway station that served [[langford-hall|Langford Hall]], the Leicestershire estate of [[richard-golding|Richard Golding]]. It featured a police station and was the arrival point for [[loveday-brooke|Loveday Brooke]] and other visitors to the Golding estate during the investigation of [[irene-golding|Irené Golding]]'s disappearance.[PN=007-002][PN=007-026]

## In the Stories

Langford Cross appeared in Chapter 7, "Missing!" [[loveday-brooke|Loveday Brooke]] arrived at Langford Cross Station where she was met by [[inspector-ramsay|Inspector Ramsay]], described as "a lanky, bony Scotchman."[PN=007-026] Later, Loveday was seen "pacing the platform of Langford Cross Station, waiting for the incoming of the [[london|London]] train," during a critical moment in her investigation.[PN=007-171]

[[lord-guilleroy|Lord Guilleroy]] drove Loveday and [[miss-golding|René Golding]] from Langford Cross to [[langford-hall|Langford Hall]] during the case's resolution.[PN=007-167]
