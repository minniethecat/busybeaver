## Overview

Langford Hall was the [[langford-cross|Leicestershire]] country estate of [[richard-golding|Richard Golding]], a wealthy Australian merchant who settled in England. It served as the primary setting for the disappearance of [[irene-golding|Irené Golding]], the central case of Chapter 7, "Missing!"[PN=007-002]

## Location

Langford Hall was situated in a hunting district of [[langford-cross|Leicestershire]]. The surrounding countryside was described as "as flat as if a gigantic stretching machine had been passed over it," characteristic of the hunting country of the English Midlands.[PN=007-035] The nearest railway station was [[langford-cross|Langford Cross]].[PN=007-002]

## In the Stories

[[irene-golding|Irené Golding]], the only daughter of [[richard-golding|Richard Golding]], vanished from Langford Hall on Monday, September 20th. A reward of five hundred pounds was offered for information about her disappearance.[PN=007-002] The case brought [[loveday-brooke|Loveday Brooke]] to Langford Hall, where she worked alongside [[inspector-ramsay|Inspector Ramsay]] and [[lord-guilleroy|Lord Guilleroy]] to unravel the mystery surrounding [[irene-golding|Irené]]'s disappearance.[PN=007-029]
