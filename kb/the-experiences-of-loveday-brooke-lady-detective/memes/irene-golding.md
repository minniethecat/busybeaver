## Overview

Irené Golding was the only daughter of [[richard-golding|Richard Golding]], a wealthy Australian merchant who had settled at [[langford-hall|Langford Hall]], [[langford-cross|Leicestershire]]. Her mysterious disappearance on Monday, September 20th, prompted a reward of five hundred pounds and brought [[loveday-brooke|Loveday Brooke]] to [[langford-hall|Langford Hall]] to investigate.[PN=007-002]

## Role in the Stories

Irené is the central figure of Chapter 7, "Missing!" Her disappearance from [[langford-hall|Langford Hall]] triggers an extensive investigation. She was described as "a lovable and most attractive young woman, with her half-Australian, half-French nature" though possessing a "quick temper."[PN=007-021]

Her father [[richard-golding|Richard Golding]] was an Australian merchant who had "come over to England about a dozen years ago, and settled at [[langford-hall|Langford Hall]]" after marrying a Frenchwoman, by whom he had this one child.[PN=007-013]

## Key Appearances

- Chapter 7, "Missing!" — her disappearance is the central mystery[PN=007-002][PN=007-013][PN=007-021]

## Biography

Irené inherited a combined Australian-French heritage from her parents. Her mother was French, and her father had made his fortune in Australia before settling in England. Irené was the sole heiress to the Golding estate at [[langford-hall|Langford Hall]].[PN=007-013]
