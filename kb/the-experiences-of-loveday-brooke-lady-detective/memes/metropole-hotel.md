## Overview

The Métropole is a prominent hotel on the seafront at [[brighton|Brighton]], where [[loveday-brooke|Loveday Brooke]] takes a room on the fifth floor on doctor's orders after being "overtaxed" by work. Her stay at the Métropole — intended as a rest cure — is quickly interrupted when the [[the-ghost-of-fountain-lane|Fountain Lane ghost case]] finds her, transforming the hotel from a place of convalescence into an investigative headquarters.

## Location

The Métropole is situated on the [[brighton|Brighton]] seafront, one of the premier addresses in this fashionable seaside resort. [[loveday-brooke|Loveday Brooke]]'s room is on the fifth floor, with a view that overlooks the sea and the [[brighton|Brighton]] promenade.

> "A room on the fifth floor of the Métropole, at Brighton, whither Loveday, overtaxed in body and mind, had gone for a brief rest." [006-005]

## Description

The Métropole is a grand Victorian hotel, typical of the era's opulent seaside architecture. [[loveday-brooke|Loveday Brooke]] occupies a room on the fifth floor, suggesting a building of considerable height for the period. The hotel serves as both a refuge from [[newspaper|the press]]ures of [[investigation|detective work]] and, paradoxically, a convenient base from which Loveday can be drawn back into investigation.

Visitors call upon her at the hotel, and it becomes a temporary operational centre:

> "Someone called upon Miss Brooke at the Métropole." [006-128]

## Significance

The Métropole represents the permeable boundary between [[loveday-brooke|Loveday Brooke]]'s professional and personal life. Even when ordered to rest, she cannot escape the gravitational pull of detection — crime finds her even at the seaside. The hotel's grandeur also reflects Loveday's professional success; a [[loveday-brooke|lady detective]] who can afford a room at the Métropole is clearly prospering in her trade. The contrast between the hotel's ordered luxury and the squalid mystery of [[fountain-lane|Fountain Lane]] underscores the social range across which Loveday operates.
