[008-002]*[[the-experiences-collection|The Experiences]] of [[loveday-brooke|Loveday Brooke]], Lady Detective*

[008-003] was published between 1893 and 1894 by

[008-004] [Catherine Louisa Pirkis](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Catherine_Louisa_Pirkis).

[008-005]This ebook was produced for

[008-006] [Standard Ebooks](https://standardebooks.org/)

[008-007] by

[008-008] [Robin Whittleton](https://www.robinwhittleton.com/),

[008-009] and is based on a transcription produced in 2003 by

[008-010] **Faith Sederquist** and **Mary Mark Ockerbloom**

[008-011] for

[008-012] [A Celebration of Women Writers](https://digital.library.upenn.edu/women/pirkis/brooke/brooke.html)

[008-013] and on digital scans from the

[008-014] [Internet Archive](https://standardebooks.org/ebooks/catherine-louisa-pirkis/the-experiences-of-loveday-brooke-lady-detective#page-scans).

[008-015]The cover page is adapted from

[008-016] *Woman Reading*,

[008-017] a painting completed in 1882 by

[008-018] [Édouard Manet](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/%C3%89douard_Manet).

[008-019] The cover and title pages feature the

[008-020] **League Spartan** and **Sorts Mill Goudy**

[008-021] typefaces created in 2014 and 2009 by

[008-022] [The League of Moveable Type](https://www.theleagueofmoveabletype.com/).

[008-023]This edition was released on

[008-024] **July 6, 2026, 9:32 p.m.**

[008-025] and is based on

[008-026] **revision 7728b04**.

[008-027] The first edition of this ebook was released on

[008-028] March 23, 2026, 2:45 a.m.

[008-029] You can check for updates to this ebook, view its revision history, or download it for different ereading systems at

[008-030] [standardebooks.org/ebooks/catherine-louisa-pirkis/the-experiences-of-loveday-brooke-lady-detective](https://standardebooks.org/ebooks/catherine-louisa-pirkis/the-experiences-of-loveday-brooke-lady-detective).
