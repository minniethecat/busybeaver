[007-001]Let all my songs gather together their diverse strains into a single

[007-002]current and flow to a sea of silence in one salutation to thee.

[007-003]Like a flock of homesick cranes flying night and day back to their

[007-004]mountain nests let all my life take its voyage to its eternal home in

[007-005]one salutation to thee.
