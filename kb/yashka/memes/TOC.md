This page lists all chapters of [[maria-bochkareva|Maria Bochkareva]]'s memoir _[[yashka-memoir|Yashka: My Life as Peasant, Officer and Exile]]_, organized by the book's four major parts. Each chapter corresponds to a key phase in Bochkareva's journey — from her peasant childhood in [[siberia|Siberia]], through her military service in [[world-war-i|World War I]], to the tumult of the [[february-revolution|Russian Revolution]] and the [[bolshevism|Bolshevik]] terror that followed.

## Introduction

The memoir's front matter introduces the historical context and Bochkareva's unique position as the first woman to command a Russian combat unit — the [[womens-battalion-of-death|Women's Battalion of Death]].
