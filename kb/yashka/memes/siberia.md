## Overview

Siberia is a location in [[maria-bochkareva|Maria Bochkareva]]'s memoir *[[maria-bochkareva|Yashka]]*, significant to the narrative of her life as a soldier and witness to the Russian Revolution.

## In the Memoir

[[maria-bochkareva|Yashka]] passed through, lived in, or fought at Siberia during the period covered by her memoir (1914-1918). Her account provides a ground-level view of this location during a time of war and revolution.

## Significance

This place figures in [[maria-bochkareva|Yashka]]'s story as part of the geography of revolutionary Russia — the vast canvas across which her extraordinary journey unfolded.
