## Overview

Yashka's American Mission is an event documented in [[maria-bochkareva|Maria Bochkareva]]'s memoir, providing firsthand testimony of the revolutionary period in Russia.

## Course of Events

This event occurred during the tumultuous years of 1914-1918, as [[maria-bochkareva|Yashka Bochkareva]] transitioned from peasant woman to decorated soldier to political actor caught in the vortex of revolution and civil war.

## Yashka's Perspective

[[maria-bochkareva|Yashka]]'s memoir provides a unique perspective — that of a peasant soldier confronting the great figures and forces of her time. Her account highlights the human dimension that official histories often miss.
