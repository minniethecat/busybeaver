## Overview

Imperial Russian Army was an organization active during the Russian Revolution, documented in [[maria-bochkareva|Maria Bochkareva]]'s memoir *[[maria-bochkareva|Yashka]]* (1919).

## Role in the Memoir

Imperial Russian Army appears in [[maria-bochkareva|Yashka]]'s firsthand account of revolutionary Russia, sometimes as an institution she encountered and other times as a force shaping the events around her.
