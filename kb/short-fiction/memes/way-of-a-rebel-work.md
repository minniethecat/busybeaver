# Way of a Rebel

## Publication

"Way of a Rebel" was first published in 1954. It appears as the fifth story in this Standard Ebooks collection.

## Setting

The story is set within the [[federation|Western Federation of Autonomous States]], Miller's recurring bureaucratic dystopia. The protagonist is a military officer caught between institutional loyalty and personal conscience.

## Themes

- **Duty versus conscience**: The central conflict — a soldier must decide whether orders justify actions
- **Institutional power**: The Federation's military apparatus as an extension of its broader control systems
- **Individual resistance**: The possibility and cost of saying no
