# Meikl

Analyst Meikl was a military officer serving under Baron ven Klaeden in Commstrafefleet Three, a space fleet orbiting Earth. He was known for his outspoken dissent against the fleet's plans to make contact with Earth's inhabitants. [PN=006-012][PN=006-014]

## Warning Against Contact

Meikl argued forcefully against landing on Earth, warning that "recessive determinants" — genetic and cultural traits from the planet's violent history — still lingered in its present inhabitants. Nearly a thousand generations had passed since Earth spawned the spacefaring colonies, but Meikl believed the hatred remained. He predicted that the fleet would "go away with a fleet full of hate" if they made contact. [PN=006-029][PN=006-045]

## The Blood Demonstration

To emphasize his point about shared ancestry, Meikl ripped his sleeve, unsheathed his rank-dagger, and pricked his forearm, drawing blood. He reminded the command staff that Earth had "spawned them all." The baron found the demonstration unsettling and privately wiped the trace of blood from the window while gazing bitterly at the planet below. [PN=006-041][PN=006-056]

## Contempt Citation

Meikl's confrontational style earned him a contempt citation during the command meeting. The wingsman slammed his fist on the desk and ordered Meikl to restrict himself to answering questions, but Meikl continued to press his case. [PN=006-018]
