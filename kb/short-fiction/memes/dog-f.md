# Dog-F

## The Canine Series

The dog-F is a genetically engineered canine companion, part of the same graded system of substitute pets as the [[cat-q5|cat-Q-5]]. Like the feline series, dog-Fs are "emotionally safer" than quasi-human [[neutroid|neutroids]] — they offer companionship without the risk of grief that attends the death of a more human-seeming substitute.

## The Graded Companionship System

The existence of multiple engineered companion series (cat-Q, dog-F, chimp-K neutroids) demonstrates the sophistication of the [[federation|Federation]]'s substitute economy. The system offers a spectrum of emotional investment, calibrated to what [[class-c-citizen|Class-C]] couples can "endure." The language of endurance — "most couples could endure the death of a cat-Q or a dog-F" (001-075) — treats grief as a manageable quantity, a resource to be allocated.
