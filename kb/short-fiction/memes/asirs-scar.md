# Asir's Scarring

## The Mark

After the [[bleeding-blade|bleeding-blade]] falls — into the post above his head, not his neck — [[asir|Asir]] is marked. [[slubil|Slubil]] cuts his forehead and rubs the wound with rust: "he found a fresh wound, raw and rubbed with rust to make a scar" (002-173). The scar is permanent, visible, a physical sign that Asir is an outcast.

## The Body as Record

The scar, like [[old-donegal|Donegal]]'s radiation sickness and the nail-holes through Asir's forearms, transforms the body into a record of institutional action. The Council's judgment is not merely spoken; it is written on flesh. Any who see Asir will know what he is: a thief, an outcast, a man marked by the state.
