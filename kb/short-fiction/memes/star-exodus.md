# Star Exodus

The **Star Exodus** was the historical event in which humanity departed from Earth and spread across the galaxy. It occurred millennia before the events chronicled in "[[the-ties-that-bind-work|The Ties That Bind]]" and fundamentally transformed human civilization [008-045].

## Historical Significance

The Star Exodus marked the end of humanity's association with Earth as its primary homeworld. After the Exodus, human history "had ceased in any way to be associated with Earth," and humanity became "a space creature, a denizen of the interstellum" [008-045]. This transformation was so complete that returning to Earth became a distant memory rather than an active goal.

## Cultural Legacy

The clans that returned to Earth in "The Ties That Bind" preserved traditions dating from before the Star Exodus. The sword grip technique used by the clansmen — wrapping fingers around the hilt — was passed down from "the fingers of his fathers done in the ages before the Star Exodus" [008-045]. These ancestral customs served as a tangible link to humanity's terrestrial origins.

## In Clan Lore

The council wisdom of the clans acknowledged the Star Exodus in their oral traditions: "The sons of men — they've come home from the Star Exodus. Our brothers" [008-045]. This framing positioned the Exodus not as an abandonment of Earth but as a journey from which humanity might one day return.
