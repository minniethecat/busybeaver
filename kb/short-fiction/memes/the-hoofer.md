# The Hoofer

**"The Hoofer"** is a 1955 science fiction short story by [[walter-m-miller-jr|Walter M. Miller Jr.]], included in the Standard Ebooks *Short Fiction* collection. It follows Big Hogey Parker, a spaceman returning home to Earth after years working in the deep space region known as Big Bottomless [006-030].

## Plot

Big Hogey Parker returns from space to reunite with his wife Marie and their young son. Having spent years in the vast emptiness of Big Bottomless, Parker struggles to readjust to domestic life and terrestrial society. His alienated perspective and erratic behavior create tension with his family, culminating in a confrontation that reveals the irreparable distance between his spacefaring identity and the expectations of home [006-030].

## Themes

The story examines the psychological cost of space travel — the alienation of the returning traveler who no longer belongs to either world. Parker's "hoofing" (dancing/unsteady movement) symbolizes his inability to find footing in domestic reality after the weightlessness of deep space [006-030].
