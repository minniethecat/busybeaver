# Evon

Evon was a young man of the Pedaga people on a colony world in "The Ties That Bind." He was restless, outgoing, and emotionally expressive — a stark contrast to the grim military forces occupying his world. [PN=006-070]

## Character and Temperament

Evon was known for his boisterous personality and staccato laugh. He was a restless, outgoing fellow who was nearly always doing something or looking for something to do. His face was described as bony and brown, with a long narrow jaw that jutted forward "like a plowshare under an elastic smirk" — a rubbery face that could twist into horrid masks for the amusement of the young. He rarely sat still, which made his prolonged meditation on the arrival of the sky-fleet all the more striking to his companions. [PN=006-070][PN=006-073][PN=006-075]

## Reaction to the Sky-Fleet

When the star-ships of the Third Fleet appeared in the sky, Evon was deeply unsettled. He sat alone in the glade, tearing twigs and staring at the sky through the foliage. When Letha asked him what troubled him, he admitted it felt "as if I know them — the star-birds" — a premonition that the arrival would change everything. His companions, accustomed to his usual high spirits, found his pensive mood disturbing. [PN=006-077][PN=006-084]

## Letha's Death

The crisis came to a head when ven Klaeden's forces confronted the Pedaga in the glade. When Letha was shot trying to run, Evon cried out her name and then stood frozen in shock. He knelt beside her body, staring, not touching her, until his knee found the blade of a fallen sword. Rising slowly, gripping the sword and touching the red-stained steel, Evon looked to Meikl. When Meikl acknowledged that Evon had loved Letha too, the shared grief forged a bond between them. [PN=006-330][PN=006-333][PN=006-345][PN=006-350]
