# District Pound

## The Facility

The district pound is the facility where [[norris|Norris]] keeps [[neutroid|neutroids]] — both those seized for inspection and the "unclaimed" inventory awaiting destruction. It is a place of cages, numbered compartments, and the "mindless chatter" of doll-like beings who do not understand that they are inventory.

## Architecture of Disposal

The pound's physical arrangement mirrors its moral function. Cages separate neutroids by series; an "unclaimed inventory" holds those destined for destruction; the inspector's office oversees the operation. The architecture makes killing administratively convenient — the condemned are already caged, already numbered, already processed.
