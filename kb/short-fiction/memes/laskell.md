# Lieutenant Laskell

Lieutenant Mitch Laskell was a Navy submariner and the protagonist of "Way of a Rebel." He surfaced his one-man submarine at the start of the story, setting in motion a chain of events that would test his loyalty and conscience. [PN=005-001]

## The Mission

Laskell operated a one-man submarine on covert naval missions. After surfacing, he switched off his shortwave set, severing communication with his command — an act that carried profound consequences. His technical skill as a submariner was matched by an independent streak that put him at odds with military discipline. [PN=005-001][PN=005-004]

## Defiance

As the situation escalated, Laskell received a final ultimatum over the radio: "Last warning, Laskell! Get on that microphone or surrender your command." The threat marked the culmination of his growing insubordination, as Barkley's clipped tones — now no longer Barkley's but the voice of the fleet command itself — demanded his compliance. Laskell's choice in that moment defined his rebellion. [PN=005-055][PN=005-059]
