# The Hoofer's Return

## The Final Trip

"The Hoofer" tells the story of a space hobo's final journey home. [[big-hogey-parker|Big Hogey Parker]], a working-class spacer who has spent his life hopping freighters across the solar system, is making his last run — not as an adventure but as a necessity, the body giving out after years in the void.

## Working-Class Space

Miller's "The Hoofer" is notable for its focus on the economic reality of space travel. Parker is not an explorer or a hero but a worker — one of the countless laborers who keep the space lanes running. His journey is not glamorous but grim: the accumulated damage of a working life, the loneliness of the lanes, the return to a home that may no longer recognize him.
