# Cat-Q-5

## A Safer Substitute

The cat-Q-5 is a genetically engineered feline companion manufactured for [[class-c-citizen|Class-C]] couples in the [[federation|Federation]]. [[norris|Norris]] observes that "a class-C couple, allowed no children of their own, could get quite attached to a cat-Q-5. The felines were emotionally safer than the quasi-human chimp-K series called 'neutroids'" (001-075).

## Emotional Calculus

The distinction between cat-Q-5s and [[neutroid|neutroids]] reveals the Federation's emotional calculus. Neutroids, with their quasi-human appearance, create deeper attachments — and deeper grief when they die. Cat-Q-5s provide a lesser version of the same comfort with lesser emotional risk. The system calibrates the humanity of its products to the emotional resilience of its consumers.
