# Check and Checkmate

**"Check and Checkmate"** is a 1953 science fiction short story by [[walter-m-miller-jr|Walter M. Miller Jr.]], included in the Standard Ebooks *Short Fiction* collection. Set in a future world divided between the Western Federation of Autonomous States and the Asian Proletarian League, it follows President John Smith XVI as he navigates a high-stakes diplomatic confrontation [003-020].

## Plot

President John Smith XVI, the sixteenth President of the Western Federation, faces a crisis when the Asian Proletarian League, led by Ivan Ivanovitch IX ("The Peoplesfriend"), proposes a diplomatic meeting. Smith employs stand-ins — decoy doubles — to manage the political risks. The story unfolds across diplomatic negotiations, intelligence maneuvering, and the countdown to a deadline set for Fried Pie Week [003-020].

## Key Elements

The story introduces several distinctive world-building elements: the Hell Wall separating East and West, the Big Silence (forty years of communication blackout), the stand-in system for protecting high officials, and the unique calendar system featuring weeks like Fried Pie Week and Veto Week [003-020].
