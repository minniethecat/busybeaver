# Tokra

## The Chief Commoner

Tokra is the Chief Commoner of the Mars colony village in "It Takes a Thief." While [[welkir|Welkir]] serves on the Council of Senior Kinsmen, Tokra appears to hold the highest civil authority. It is to Tokra that [[mara|Mara]] goes "to plead with him for the pilferer's life" (002-023).

## Power at a Distance

Tokra never appears directly in the story. He is referenced through [[asir|Asir]]'s anxious imaginings: "he had visions of them chuckling together in Tokra's villa over a glass of amber wine, while life drained slowly from a young thief" (002-023). The villa, the amber wine, the casual laughter — these details paint Tokra as a figure of comfortable power, insulated from the suffering his judgments produce.
