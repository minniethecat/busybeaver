# Anthropos, Inc.

## The Corporation

Anthropos, Inc. is the corporation that manufactures [[neutroid|neutroids]] and other genetically engineered beings in the [[federation|Federation]]. The name — from the Greek *anthropos*, "human being" — is bitterly ironic: the company produces substitutes for human children, beings engineered to be almost-but-not-quite human.

## Operations

The company operates facilities including the [[bermuda-factory|Bermuda Factory]], where ova are processed through [[evolvotron|egg-multipliers]] to produce neutroids of various series (K-99, cat-Q-5, dog-F). Its products are distributed through wholesale offices in cities like [[wylo-city|Wylo City]], where [[norris|Norris]] goes to obtain serial number lists for the recall.

Anthropos operates at industrial scale. Norris's district alone handles "around three hundred animals a month" in replacement quota (001-087). The scale is crucial to the moral logic: when production reaches industrial volumes, individual beings become statistical units, and moral concern becomes administratively impossible.

## Moral Status

As a commercial entity, Anthropos embodies the economic logic that drives Miller's dystopia. The corporation does not make moral judgments; it meets market demand. That the market demand was created by the state's denial of [[reproduction-rights|reproduction rights]] is not the corporation's concern. Anthropos manufactures products; the [[federation|Federation]] classifies citizens; [[norris|Norris]] disposes of defective units. Each actor does their job; the moral shape of the whole is nobody's job.
