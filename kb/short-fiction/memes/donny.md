# Donny

## The Son

Donny is the son of [[old-donegal|Old Donegal]] and [[martha|Martha]] in "Death of a Spaceman." His presence in the story represents the generational transition that his father's death will complete: the old spaceman's world is ending, and Donny's generation must decide what to carry forward.

## Narrative Role

Donny serves primarily as witness and foil — the younger generation confronting the legacy of the old spacers. Where Donegal's body carries the physical record of a lifetime in space, Donny represents a future in which space travel is safer, more automated, and less heroic — a future the old spaceman can imagine but not inhabit.
