# Firebirds of the Sun

## The Divine Chariots

"*The ancient gods, our Fathers, rode down from the heavens in the Firebirds of the Sun*" (002-002). The Firebirds are the spacecraft of the original Earth colonists, mythologized over centuries into divine chariots. The name captures the process by which technology becomes miracle: a landing craft, trailing fire through the Martian atmosphere, becomes a Firebird; the colonists who piloted it become gods.

## Memory and Myth

The Firebirds represent the transformation of memory into myth. The original colonists knew they were flying spacecraft; their descendants know only that the gods arrived in fire. The fact is preserved but its meaning is lost — the same pattern that characterizes the entire [[ritual-economy|ritual economy]] of the Mars colony.
