# Marswolf

## The Native Predator

The marswolf is a predatory creature native to Mars, mentioned in "It Takes a Thief" as the model for [[big-joe|Big Joe]]'s terrifying head: "a monstrous head shaped like a Marswolf, with long silver fangs" (002-385). The ancient colonists who built Big Joe deliberately modeled their guardian on a creature the Martian colonists would fear.

## Design and Terror

The choice to model a killing machine on a marswolf — rather than on an abstract or purely functional design — reveals the builders' intent. Big Joe was not merely designed to kill but to terrify. The fangs, Miller pointedly notes, serve no mechanical purpose: "Why should a metal-creature have fangs, unless he had been built to kill?" (002-385).
