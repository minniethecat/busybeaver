# Secretary Garson

## The Ultimatum

Secretary Garson is a [[federation|Federation]] cabinet member whose wartime ultimatum is broadcast to the nation in "Way of a Rebel": "Heretofore we have refrained from employing [certain measures]" — the specific measures are redacted in the broadcast, but the tone signals escalation toward total war.

## Function

Garson functions as the voice of institutional authority — the government speaking in its official capacity, delivering threats that implicate everyone while being accountable to no one. For [[laskell|Lieutenant Laskell]], listening alone in his submarine, Garson's broadcast is one more piece of evidence that the government he serves has abandoned moral restraint.
