# Murder of Doctor Georges

## The Crime

At seven o'clock in the evening, [[sarah-glubbes|Sarah Glubbes]] — a [[class-c-citizen|Class-C]] woman whose [[neutroid|neutroid]] child had died — burst into the dining room of [[doctor-georges|Doctor Hiram Georges]] "brandishing a pistol and screaming, 'You stole my baby! You gave me the wrong baby! Where's my baby?'" (001-293). She shot the doctor dead in front of his family.

## What the News Reported

[[james-duncan|James Duncan]], reporting from a mobile unit outside the Georges residence, described "Doctor Georges' death [which] has shocked the community deeply" (001-287). Police Chief [[erskine-miler|Erskine Miler]] provided the official account. The news report told what happened but not why: a Class-C mother had killed her family physician over a neutroid.

## What the News Could Not Report

The news could not report that the neutroid was a substitute for the child Mrs. Glubbes was forbidden to have. That the disease killing her neutroid was treatable — in humans. That Doctor Georges, a family physician who had known the Glubbes family for years, "forgot she was class-C" (001-093) — a forgetting that was itself a function of the [[genetic-classification|classification system]], which trains citizens not to see Class-C as fully human. That the murder was not a random act of violence but the logical endpoint of a system that manufactures love-objects and then disposes of them.

## Norris's Response

[[norris|Norris]] learns of the murder via radio broadcast and sits "breathing quickly" (001-289). He had spoken to Georges that very morning, asking him to sign off on a dying neutroid. Georges had agreed, laughing faintly: "I won't, Norris. Thanks a million" (001-125). The doctor's casual collusion with the system — his willingness to "forget" that his patient was Class-C — now reads as complicity punished.

## Systemic Murder

Miller frames the murder not as individual crime but as systemic outcome. The [[federation|Federation]]'s classification system, the [[neutroid|neutroid]] industry, the medical establishment that treats humans and substitutes differently — these structures produce the conditions for murder. Mrs. Glubbes pulled the trigger, but the system loaded the gun.
