# Wylo City

**Wylo City** is an urban center in the world of "[[conditionally-human-work|Conditionally Human]]." It serves as a commercial hub housing the district wholesale offices of Anthropos, Inc. and several retail pet shops that sold mutant animals [001-020].

## Commercial Role

Wylo City was a key node in the mutant animal trade. The Anthropos wholesale house in the city maintained shipping lists tracking the distribution of mutant animals, including the Bermuda-K-99 series. Of the thirty-five Bermuda-K-99 animals that entered the district in July, distribution records showed they were equally divided among five pet shops, three of which were located in Wylo City [001-020].

## Norris's Investigation

Inspector Norris visited Wylo City during his investigation of the Delmont Negligency Case. He checked shipping lists at the wholesale house and called each retail dealer to trace the distribution of mutant animals. His journey to Wylo City was interrupted by a radiophone call, highlighting the city's role as a nexus of the investigation [001-020].
