# Welkir

## Senior Kinsman

Welkir is a Senior Kinsman of the Mars colony village and the father of [[mara|Mara]]. After his banishment, [[asir|Asir]] comes to Welkir's house to plead — not for himself but for the urgency of the knowledge he carries about Mars's failing atmosphere.

## The Elder's Door

"A light flickered feebly through Welkir's door. Asir rattled it and waited" (002-177). The imagery of a "feeble" light suggests the dimming of the old order — the Council of Senior Kinsmen, the [[ritual-economy]], the entire social structure that has presided over Mars's decline.

Welkir represents the established authority that Asir must persuade. As the father of Mara and a member of the Council, Welkir embodies the power structure that condemned Asir as a thief. The thief's appeal to the elder who sentenced him — asking him to recognize the value of the knowledge he stole — is the story's central dramatic reversal.
