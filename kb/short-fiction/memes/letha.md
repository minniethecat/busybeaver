# Letha

Letha was a young woman living on Earth during the visit of Commstrafefleet Three. She belonged to the Pedaga, a group living a simple, communal life in a forest glade. [PN=006-069]

## Appearance and Personality

Letha was described as graceful but kittenish rather than feline — full-bodied and soft, well-shaped despite a trace of plumpness, with thick masses of black hair. She was playful, once chasing a companion named Marrita around the glade with a switch while shrieking threats of mayhem. [PN=006-071][PN=006-101]

## First Contact

When the space fleet's presence became known, Letha suggested to the other Pedaga that they should tell the visitors to go away. She was skeptical of the outsiders. [PN=006-069]

## Encounter with the Visitors

One of the fleet's officers became drawn to Letha, telling her "I love you" and explaining the differences between their cultures. He described how his society had passed through three phases of development, while hers had perhaps entered a fourth and final phase. He noted that in her culture, "life" was the highest value — a concept that differed from the spacefaring civilization's priorities. [PN=006-236][PN=006-258][PN=006-266]
