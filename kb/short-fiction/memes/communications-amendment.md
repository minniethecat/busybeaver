# Communications Amendment

## Legal Incrementalism

The Communications Amendment is part of the series of constitutional amendments in "Check and Checkmate" that progressively restructured the [[federation|Federation]]'s governance. Alongside the [[qualifications-amendment|Qualifications Amendment]], Education Amendment, Genetic Amendment, and Selection Amendment, it represents another layer in the legal architecture of control.

## Function

The amendment restricts or redefines communications — the specific provisions are not detailed in the story, but its mention among the other restrictive amendments places it within the Federation's broader program of legal incrementalism. Each amendment, [[stand-in|Smith]] explains, was reasonable on its own terms; together they constructed a system of total control.
