# Baron ven Klaeden

Ernstli Baron ven Klaeden was the military commander of the Third Fleet who led an expedition to a colony planet in "The Ties That Bind." His harsh command and authoritarian style drove the conflict with the native Pedaga people. [PN=006-029]

## Military Command

Ven Klaeden commanded the Third Fleet with an iron hand. He issued the order to land on the planet and reported to the elders of the Geoark about the thirty-one mile radius of their operations, demonstrating both tactical precision and bureaucratic thoroughness. His command style was characterized by barked orders and irritable demands. [PN=006-114][PN=006-136][PN=006-164]

## Exploitation and Cruelty

Under ven Klaeden, the ships of the Third Fleet engaged in arrogant exploitation. His treatment of the Pedaga was brutal — he viewed their resistance as insubordination to be crushed. When natives fled his forces, he ordered them hunted down with lethal force. [PN=006-203][PN=006-235][PN=006-251]

## The Killing of Letha

Ven Klaeden's cruelty reached its peak during the confrontation in the glade. When Letha attempted to escape, ven Klaeden's officer shot and killed her. Ven Klaeden himself sat like "a gaunt Buddha on the moss" as the girl lay dead nearby. In the chaos that followed, ven Klaeden slipped to his knees on the moss, reduced from commanding officer to a stunned witness of the violence he had unleashed. [PN=006-333][PN=006-344][PN=006-345]
