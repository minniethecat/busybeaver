# The Hoofer

## Publication

"The Hoofer" was first published in 1955. It appears as the seventh story in this Standard Ebooks collection.

## Plot

[[big-hogey-parker|Big Hogey Parker]] is a "hoofer" — a space hobo who hops freighters across the solar system. His final journey home is not an adventure but a reckoning: the body worn out, the life spent in the lanes, the return to a world that may no longer have a place for him.

## Themes

- **Working-class space**: Space travel as labor, not exploration
- **The body's limits**: The physical cost of a life in the void
- **Homecoming**: What you return to when there's nothing left to leave for
