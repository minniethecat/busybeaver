# Sherman II

## The City

Sherman II is a city in the [[federation|Western Federation of Autonomous States]], one of the communities in [[norris|Norris]]'s district. It is the home of [[doctor-georges|Doctor Hiram Georges]] and the site of his murder by [[sarah-glubbes|Sarah Glubbes]].

## In "Conditionally Human"

Sherman II appears in the story primarily through the radio broadcast that reports Georges' death: "speaking to you from our mobile unit in front of the late Doctor Hiram Georges' residence just west of Sherman II" (001-287). The city's name suggests a planned community — the "II" implying a second iteration — characteristic of the Federation's bureaucratic approach to settlement.
