# Justice Section

The **Justice Section** is a law enforcement and investigative organization operating in the occupied zone on Earth in "[[the-ties-that-bind-work|The Ties That Bind]]." It is responsible for tracking escaped personnel, maintaining order, and enforcing legal authority among the returning clans [008-045].

## Functions

Justice Section's primary duties included hunting down escaped men. Over three hundred men had gone missing, and Justice Section successfully located sixty-three of them, with the remainder considered "probably hopeless" [008-045]. The Section operated with authority over the ausland territories, where escaped men would flee to avoid military service or other obligations.

## Legal Authority

When a man escaped from the occupied zone into the ausland, Justice Section could send a detail to demand his return. However, if the escapee refused extradition, the Section's authority was limited by legal constraints — a reflection of the complex jurisdictional arrangements between the military administration and the civilian clans [008-045].

## Internal Tensions

Justice Section was not immune to the broader conflicts of the occupation. Staff officers and officers within the Section experienced direct action from resistance elements, demonstrating that even the enforcers of order were vulnerable in the contested environment of occupied Earth [008-045].
