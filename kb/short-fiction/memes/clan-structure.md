# Clan Structure

## Kinship as Government

The Mars colony's social organization is built around clans — kinship groups that function simultaneously as family, economic unit, and political faction. The Council of Senior Kinsmen, which governs the village in "It Takes a Thief," derives its authority from clan representation: each senior kinsman speaks for a clan.

## Political Function

Clan politics drive the conflict in "The Ties That Bind," where competing clan interests intersect with the ancient knowledge preserved in [[ritual-economy|ritual formulas]]. The clan structure creates competing claims to authority — each clan possesses different fragments of the ancient knowledge, and no clan possesses enough to govern alone.
