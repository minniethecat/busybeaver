# Nora

Nora was the daughter of Old Donegal and Martha, and the mother of Ken. She appears in "Death of a Spaceman" during a visit to her parents' home. [PN=004-046]

## Appearance and Personality

Nora dyed her hair black, concealing the gray streaks. She wore scarlet lipstick and smoked cigarettes nervously while looking out the window. [PN=004-103][PN=004-113] She was opinionated and strong-willed, unafraid to speak her mind about the neighbors. [PN=004-122]

## Relationship with Ken

Nora's son Ken had been enrolled in the space academy but changed his mind, wanting to attend medical school instead. Nora supported this decision. [PN=004-065] Old Donegal believed that Nora had "made Ken a yellow-belly" by coddling him, and that "the boots won't fit" — meaning Ken lacked the toughness for the space service. [PN=004-156]

## Visit to the Donegal Home

Nora visited her parents without Ken, who was attending a party at the Keith residence next door. She expressed contempt for the Keith family and their celebration for Ron Keith the Third's academy admission, calling the attendees "female butterflies and puppy-dogs in sport jackets." [PN=004-116] She later tried unsuccessfully to get the Keiths to quiet their loud party. [PN=004-176]
