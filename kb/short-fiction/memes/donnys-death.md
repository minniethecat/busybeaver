# Donny's Death

## The Pre-Story Loss

Before the events of "Death of a Spaceman," [[old-donegal|Old Donegal]] and [[martha|Martha]] lost their son [[donny|Donny]]. The circumstances are referenced obliquely in the story, but the loss forms the emotional substratum of Donegal's confrontation with his own mortality.

## Grief and Legacy

Donny's death compounds the story's meditation on endings. Donegal is dying of radiation cancer — a professional death, earned in space. Donny's death is different: premature, untimely, the death of the generation that was supposed to carry forward. Together, the two deaths frame the story's central question: what survives when both the old and the young are gone?
