# Bermuda Factory

## The Facility

The Bermuda Factory is a [[neutroid|neutroid]] production facility operated by [[organization-anthropos|Anthropos, Inc.]] It produces K-series neutroids for distribution throughout the [[federation|Federation]]. The factory's name — "Bermuda" — carries unsettling connotations: a place where things disappear, a triangle of mystery.

## Industrial Production of Life

The factory represents the industrialization of Miller's central concern: the manufacturing of quasi-human beings. Ova are processed through "egg-multipliers," bombarded by the [[evolvotron]], and incubated to produce the doll-like, neuter creatures that serve as substitute children for [[class-c-citizen|Class-C]] couples.

The factory is the site of [[delmont|Delmont]]'s fraud — his passing of a defective ovum to meet a production quota. The incident reveals the factory's fundamental logic: it treats living beings as products, and products are measured by output, not by the moral significance of what is being produced.
