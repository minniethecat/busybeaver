# Father Paulson

Father Paulson was a priest who visited Norris's home to investigate the welfare of James O'Reilley in "Conditionally Human." [PN=001-304]

## Visit to Norris

Paulson appeared at Norris's door calling on behalf of O'Reilley, whom he described as "a sick man." He explained that O'Reilley had been asking for his family — his animals — and that Norris had taken them all away. Paulson pressed Norris about what had happened to O'Reilley's creatures. [PN=001-304][PN=001-308]

## Observation of Peony

During his visit, Paulson observed Peony, the deviant neutroid that Norris was hiding as his daughter. He kept his eyes on her every movement, watching as she babbled gibberish. When he asked Peony directly about O'Reilley, she seemed to recognize the name despite her apparent mental condition. Paulson declared that he had come only to assure himself that O'Reilley was sane — and reminded Norris, "You invited me to see for myself." [PN=001-320][PN=001-324][PN=001-327][PN=001-329]

## Departure

After confronting Norris, Paulson grew increasingly uncomfortable with what he had witnessed. He fumbled with his coat button and stared at the floor while pondering. Finally, he told Norris he would not want to be in his position, and that if he were, he would "withhold her from my superiors" and "quit my job and go away." Paulson then excused himself and departed. [PN=001-334][PN=001-344]
