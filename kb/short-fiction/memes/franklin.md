# Chief Franklin

Chief Franklin was Norris's superior at the Federal Bio-Administration (F.B.A.), serving as Chief Agent overseeing multiple district inspectors including Norris. [PN=001-032]

## Physical Description

Franklin was a middle-aged man with a thin hawk face, seamed with wrinkles, and abnormally black hair. His lanky, emaciated body was loosely draped in gray tweeds. He carried himself with a slow, almost sarcastic demeanor. [PN=001-391]

## Role in the Bureau

As Chief Agent, Franklin was responsible for enforcing Bio-Administration regulations across multiple territories. He issued a memo to all district inspectors ordering a systematic survey of Bermuda-K-99 neutroids following the Delmont Negligency Case, requiring all animals in that series to be seized, impounded, and tested for mental and glandular deviation. [PN=001-032] Franklin personally visited Norris's district to ensure compliance with the survey order. [PN=001-382]

## Death

During an inspection of Norris's kennel, Franklin was killed when a light bulb detonated escaping anesthetic vapor in the gas chamber. The official account recorded his death as accidental — that he reached up to tighten a flickering light and triggered the explosion. [PN=001-474][PN=001-486] In reality, Norris deliberately shattered the bulb and rigged the detonation to prevent Franklin from discovering and destroying Peony, a deviant neutroid. [PN=001-463][PN=001-470] Norris justified the killing to his wife Anne by saying it was "Franklin or a little girl." [PN=001-503]
