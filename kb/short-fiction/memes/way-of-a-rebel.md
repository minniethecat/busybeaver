# Way of a Rebel

**"Way of a Rebel"** is a 1954 science fiction short story by [[walter-m-miller-jr|Walter M. Miller Jr.]], included in the Standard Ebooks *Short Fiction* collection. Set in a future naval warfare context, it follows Lieutenant Laskell, a one-man submarine operator who refuses orders to launch nuclear missiles [007-010].

## Plot

Lieutenant Laskell surfaces his submarine and is contacted by Captain Barkley aboard Commsubfleet Jaybird, ordering him to proceed to Savannah and launch missiles. Laskell questions the legitimacy of the order and ultimately refuses, choosing to surface and face the consequences rather than participate in what he suspects is an unjustified attack [007-010].

## Themes

The story explores themes of individual conscience versus military obedience, moral responsibility in warfare, and the courage required to defy authority when orders conflict with ethical judgment. Laskell's rebellion is framed not as cowardice but as a principled stand against institutional violence [007-010].
