# Egg-Multiplier

## Industrial Reproduction

The egg-multiplier is the technology that mass-produces the biological starting material for [[neutroid|neutroid]] manufacturing. "Unfertilized chimpanzee ova" are taken "out of the egg-multiplier" and mounted in the [[evolvotron]] for bombardment — the process that creates the engineered beings (001-107).

## Scale and Horror

The egg-multiplier's name reveals its logic: it multiplies — takes a single biological unit and produces many, at industrial scale. The language is technical and clinical, but the product is living beings. The gap between the technical description and the moral reality is precisely Miller's point: technologies do not announce their ethical implications.
