# Leaves of Grass

**Leaves of Grass** (1855) by Walt Whitman. Whitman's landmark poetry collection, representing the American literary tradition that Dreiser's naturalism both inherits and challenges.

## Significance

This work occupies a place in the literary and intellectual landscape that surrounds *The Titan*. Whether as direct influence, contemporary parallel, or cultural context, it illuminates the world Dreiser depicts.
