# Arneel and Company

**Arneel and Company** is one of the institutions that constitute the economic landscape of *The Titan* — a node in the network of banks, railways, trusts, and political bodies through which power flows in Gilded Age Chicago.

## Role in the Novel

Arneel and Company participates in the novel's central drama of consolidation and competition. Whether as ally or adversary to Cowperwood, it represents a concentration of capital and influence that shapes the possibilities available to every character.
