## About This Index

This page aggregates all entries tagged with spiritualism, supernatural, séance,
mediumship, or spiritualist — the thematic core of *The Necromancers*. Benson's novel
is structured around the collision between Victorian spiritualism and Catholic orthodoxy,
and this index gathers every page that touches that central conflict.

The spiritualist movement of the late nineteenth century — with its mediums, table-turnings,
and claimed communications with the dead — provides both the novel's plot machinery and
its theological foil. These pages trace the phenomenon from [[spiritualism|its definition]]
through [[mr-vincent|Mr. Vincent]]'s séances to the novel's ultimate affirmation of
Catholic sacramental order.
