# Sunway

A settlement lying not far from the Castle of Abundance, visited by the Lady during her journey through the western lands. It is associated with the enigmatic Knight of the Sun.

## Overview

Sunway is a modest settlement situated within a short distance of the Castle of Abundance. The Lady passed through it in the company of the Knight of the Sun, and it later features in the tale of Ralph's return through the western lands [033-007].

## Geography & Description

Sunway lies "no great way from the Castle of Abundance" [033-007]. The surrounding country includes woodland where travellers may pass the night. A hermitage stands nearby, in a thicket at the end of a water [024-012].

## Inhabitants

Little is told of Sunway's permanent inhabitants. It is visited by the Lady in the company of the Knight of the Sun, and later by Ralph during his return journey.

## Role in the Narrative

### The Lady's Passage

The Lady tells Ralph that she went to Sunway with a companion — one who "would have me wed him, as it were against my will" — before she eventually came to the Castle of Abundance [033-007]. This sojourn at Sunway marks a period of constraint before her liberation.

### The Knight of the Sun

Sunway is connected with the Knight of the Sun, a figure who appears in the woodland chapters. He treats with the Lady and Ralph near the hermitage, striking bargains and offering guidance [024-014]. His association with Sunway suggests it as a meeting point between the settled lands and the wild.

### Later Return

When Ralph returns through the western lands after his quest, the Prior of the Thorn tells him that none has more right to the castle there than he, implying that the lordship of Sunway and its surrounds has passed into his hands by right of lineage or conquest [103-031].

### Significance

Sunway is a minor but significant waypoint — a place of passage, constraint, and eventual liberation. It connects the Castle of Abundance with the wider road network of the western lands, and its lordship passes to Ralph as part of the larger settlement of the realm.
