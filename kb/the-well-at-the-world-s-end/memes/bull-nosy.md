# Bull Nosy

**Bull Nosy** (or **the Long-nosed Man of the Kindred of the Bull**) is a kinsman
of [[bull-shockhead|Bull Shockhead]] and a man of the Bull kindred.

## Capture with the Lady of Abundance

Bull Nosy was in the company of the Lady of Abundance when they were ambushed
by armed men in the mountain passes. As they came round a corner of the cliffs,
they happened upon a band of weaponed men encamped under a rock. Bull Nosy
attempted to flee with the Lady, but four horsemen overtook them and brought
them before the [[the-lord-of-utterbol|Lord of Utterbol]].

The Lord dismissed Bull Nosy as of no account, focusing his attention entirely
on the Lady. The Lady later recalled that she "was sorry for Bull Nosy, for he
was no ill man and had become my friend."

## Death and Memorial

Bull Nosy was slain, and his brother [[bull-shockhead|Bull Shockhead]] raised a
great howe over the place where his body was found. The howe was "heaped up
exceeding high, covered in with earth," and bore a great standing stone on its
top, carved with the image of a bull and a sword beneath it — a memorial raised
by his brother after Bull Shockhead became the new Lord of
[[utterbol|Utterbol]].

## Name

The name "Bull Nosy" follows the custom of the Bull kindred, in which men are
named after the bull. The epithet "Nosy" or "the Long-nosed" distinguished him
among his kinsmen.
