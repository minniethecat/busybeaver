# Joyce

**Joyce** (also called **Dame Joyce**) is a handmaiden at the court of
[[utterbol|Utterbol]] and a rival to [[the-queen-of-utterbol|the Queen of
Utterbol]] for the Lord's favor.

## At the Court of Utterbol

Joyce held a privileged position at the court of Utterbol. She was seen seated
beside the Lord at a great feast, where "he thought it naught but good to hold
her hand and play with the fingers thereof, though all those great men were by."
Observers noted that Joyce, "being well nigh as tall as the Queen, and as white
of skin, was otherwise a far fairer woman."

## Rivalry with the Queen

The Queen of Utterbol regarded Joyce with intense jealousy. The Queen's anger
flared when she learned the Lord had been looking at Joyce, and she declared
that once the Lord's attention turned to a new captive, she would "have my will
of her." The Queen was tormented by the thought that Joyce had effectively
taken her place, and [[agatha|Agatha]] the Carline warned the Queen that Joyce
would be "set in thy seat by my Lord's leave."

## Family

Joyce is referred to as "Dame Joyce," and mention is made of her "youngest born,
the fair little maiden that we left sick of a fever." This suggests Joyce was a
mother with at least one child.
