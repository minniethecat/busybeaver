# Michael-a-dale

**Michael-a-dale** (also called **Michael-a-Hurst**, **Michael of the Hatch of Swevenham**,
and **Michael-a-green**) is a man from [[swevenham|Swevenham]] who joins
[[ralph-of-upmeads|Ralph of Upmeads]]'s company as a guide and scout.

## Origin

Michael comes from the Hatch, a district of Swevenham. His full style is "Michael
of the Hatch of Swevenham," and the [[the-sage-of-swevenham|Sage of Swevenham]]
vouches for his honesty, noting that his kindred have not "turned thieves in the
third generation." He is described as a stalwart man of some forty winters.

## Service in Ralph's Company

Michael joins Ralph's company as a guide, helping them navigate the roads and
avoid dangers. He proves his worth when he recognizes the riders of
[[cheaping-knowe|Cheaping Knowe]] lying in wait at a mountain pass, enabling
the company to take precautions. On another occasion, he directs the company to
turn suddenly into a deep and narrow lane to evade waylayers on the highway.

## Names

Michael is referred to by several names:

- **Michael-a-dale** — his primary name, from his home district
- **Michael-a-Hurst** — an alternate form
- **Michael-a-green** — a nickname
- **Michael of the Hatch of Swevenham** — his full formal designation

## Parting

When the company nears Swevenham, Michael parts ways with Ralph to seek his
kindred. The Sage of Swevenham reads Michael's palm and prophesies that he will
live long and one day seek out Ralph at [[upmeads|Upmeads]]. Michael departs
hastily, "as one who should hurry away from a grief."
