# Hurliguerly

**Hurliguerly** is the boatswain of the [[the-halbrane|Halbrane]], a man of about forty-four years who came from the Isle of Wight [002-009].

## Role in the Novel

Hurliguerly serves as a key intermediary between [[jeorling|Jeorling]] and [[captain-len-guy|Captain Len Guy]]. It is through Hurliguerly — introduced via [[atkins|Atkins]] — that Jeorling secures his passage aboard the Halbrane for the Antarctic expedition [002-009].

He describes himself as "the faithful companion of Captain Len Guy, who will listen to me" and demonstrates a bluff, honest demeanor characteristic of a seasoned sailor [002-011].

## Character

Short and sturdy in build, Hurliguerly embodies the reliable career seaman. His loyalty to Captain Len Guy is unwavering, and his practical knowledge of the ship and crew makes him an essential figure in the day-to-day operations of the Halbrane.
