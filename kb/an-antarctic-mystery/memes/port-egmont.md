# Port Egmont

**Port Egmont** is a harbour on the northern coast of West [[falkland-islands|Falkland]], serving as the [[the-halbrane|Halbrane]]'s anchorage during the expedition's stop at the Falkland Islands. It is here that the schooner takes on additional crew — including the mysterious [[hunt|Hunt]] — and makes her final preparations before entering the Antarctic unknown.

## Location

Port Egmont lies "on the north of West Falkland," one of the two principal islands of the Falkland Group. The Falklands are "composed of two large islands, East Falkland and West Falkland, and about a hundred islets" lying approximately three hundred miles east of the Strait of Magellan (011-084).

## Role in the Novel

Port Egmont is the setting for one of the novel's most consequential decisions: the recruitment of [[hunt|Hunt]] (later revealed as [[dirk-peters|Dirk Peters]]) as a member of the crew. It is also here that the final complement of sailors is assembled, and where the tension between the expedition's humanitarian purpose and the commercial expectations of the newly-recruited sealers begins to surface.

The harbour represents the last secure anchorage before the [[the-halbrane|Halbrane]] enters the Antarctic seas. From Port Egmont, there would be "no delay until our arrival at the Falklands" on the return — a return that, for many aboard, would never come (011-028).
