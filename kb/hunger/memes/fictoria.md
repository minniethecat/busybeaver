This page documents a bibliographic curiosity in Edwin Björkman's introduction to *Hunger* (1921): the reference to 'Fictoria,' which is a typographical error for *Victoria*, Knut Hamsun's 1898 novel.

## The Error

Björkman writes: 'To the English-speaking world he has so far been made known only through the casual publication at long intervals of a few of his books: "Hunger," "Fictoria" and "Shallow Soil."' (P04-044). The name should read 'Victoria.'

## Significance

This error, preserved through multiple printings of the Knopf edition, is a small testament to the haphazard nature of Hamsun's early English-language reception — the very 'negligence' that Björkman hoped would 'be remedied.'
