Trondheim, historically known as Nidaros, is Norway's third-largest city and was the medieval capital. In the 19th century, it was an important educational and ecclesiastical center, home to the Nidaros Cathedral and the Norwegian University of Science and Technology's predecessor institutions.

## Relation to Hamsun

While Trondheim does not appear directly in *Hunger*, it forms part of the national geography of Norway that contextualizes the novel's Kristiania setting. Hamsun, born in central Norway (Lom, in the Gudbrandsdal valley), had personal connections to the broader region.
