# Psychological Themes

*Hunger* is a landmark of psychological literature, rendered almost entirely through the protagonist's interior monologue. This index brings together all pages that engage with the novel's mental landscapes — states of delirium, creative mania, shame spirals, and the existential texture of consciousness under duress.
