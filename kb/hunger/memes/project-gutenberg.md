Project Gutenberg (PG) is the oldest digital library, founded by Michael Hart in 1971 with the goal of making public-domain works freely available in electronic form.

## Hunger in the Digital Age

The English translation of *Hunger* by George Egerton, with Edwin Björkman's introduction, is available as Project Gutenberg eBook #8387. First posted in 2005, this digital edition has made Hamsun's novel accessible to a global readership beyond the constraints of print availability.

## Significance

The Project Gutenberg edition is the source text for many subsequent reprints and digital editions of *Hunger*. Its availability in the public domain has facilitated scholarly work, new print editions, and the novel's continued presence in world literature curricula.
