Drammen is a port city in Norway, located approximately 40 kilometers southwest of Oslo (Kristiania). In the late 19th century, it was an important industrial and shipping center.

## In Hunger

Drammen appears in the geographical consciousness of *Hunger* as part of the wider Norwegian world beyond Kristiania. The narrator occasionally references other Norwegian locations, creating a spatial framework that extends beyond the city limits and situating Kristiania within a national geography.
