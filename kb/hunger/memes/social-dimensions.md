# Social Dimensions

The social world of *Hunger* is a city stratified by class yet traversed by a protagonist who belongs to none. From the commercial establishments that exclude him to the fleeting encounters with figures of authority and charity, social dynamics shape the narrative's texture as much as its psychology. This index gathers pages that illuminate the class, commercial, and institutional dimensions of Hamsun's Christiania.
