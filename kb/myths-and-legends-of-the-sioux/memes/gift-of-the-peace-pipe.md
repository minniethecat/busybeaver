## Overview

**The Gift of the Peace Pipe** is one of the foundational sacred events in Sioux tradition. It tells of the moment when a mysterious woman — believed to be a divine being who could transform between human and buffalo form — appeared to two young Sioux men and bestowed upon the people the sacred Peace Pipe (_Chanunpa_). This event established the ceremonial protocols that would govern Sioux diplomacy, spirituality, and the treatment of the dead for all time.

## Narrative

The event occurred when two young men were walking at night, talking of love affairs. Passing around a hill, they came to a ravine and saw a beautiful woman approaching, dressed in the finest materials. One young man desired to steal her as his wife; the other cautioned that she might be holy.

The woman held out a pipe, offering it first to the sky, then to the earth, before extending it to the young men. She then laid the pipe down and transformed into a buffalo cow — pawing the ground, lifting the pipe in her hoofs, and becoming a woman again.

> "I am come to give you this gift," she said. "It is the peace pipe. Hereafter all treaties and ceremonies shall be performed after smoking it. It shall bring peaceful thoughts into your minds. You shall offer it to the Great Mystery and to mother earth." (013-017–013-020)

She added that when setting free the spirits of the dead, a white buffalo cow skin must be used (013-024). The young men ran to tell the village, and when all came to witness, she repeated her instructions, gave the pipe to the medicine men, transformed once more into a buffalo cow, and fled to the land of buffaloes.

## Significance

This event is foundational to Sioux ceremonial life. Every treaty, every sacred ceremony, every release of the dead — all trace their protocols back to this single divine encounter. The white buffalo cow skin requirement links this event to the most sacred animal in Sioux cosmology: the white buffalo. The offering sequence (sky, then earth) encodes the Sioux understanding of their place between the Great Mystery above and Mother Earth below.
