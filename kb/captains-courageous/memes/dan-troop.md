# Dan Troop

Disko Troop's son, raised on the Grand Banks and fishing since he could walk. Aboard the *We're Here*, Dan occupies the middle ground between the adult crew and the green second boy. He is Harvey Cheyne's teacher, tormentor, and eventual friend — the elder brother Harvey never had (001-103, 003-036).

## 身份与背景

Dan is the son of a Gloucester fishing captain and has never known any life but the sea. He baited trawl hooks "ashore 'fore I could well walk" and can handle a dory, read the water, and split cod with casual competence (003-155). At approximately eighteen, he is already a full share fisherman (001-103).

## 外貌与性格

Dan is practical, sardonic, and impatient with incompetence — but fundamentally decent. He mocks Harvey's soft hands yet is the one who teaches Harvey to bait a hook, heave a lead, and swab a dory. His humor is dry; he pushes Harvey harder than any adult would, because he knows that softness on the Banks is dangerous (003-036).

## 主要经历

Dan is the first person aboard the *We're Here* to treat Harvey as a peer rather than a curiosity. He takes Harvey into the red dory *Hattie S.* for a day's fishing, which becomes the occasion for Harvey's first real test. When Harvey lands his halibut, Dan's assessment — "you've took 'baout as logy a halibut's we're apt to find this trip" — is at once dismissive and acknowledging (003-043).
