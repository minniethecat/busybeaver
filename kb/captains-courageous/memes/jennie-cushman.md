# Jennie Cushman

The *Jennie Cushman* is a Gloucester fishing schooner operating on the North Atlantic fishing grounds. She gains notoriety in the fleet through an incident that becomes a recurring source of humor among the fishermen.

## The Jennie Cushman Incident

During one season on the Banks, news of a mishap involving the *Jennie Cushman* spreads through the fleet. Dan Troop, upon hearing the report, reacts with hysterical laughter, calling out the vessel's name between fits [007-010]. The nature of the incident is significant enough that it serves as a cautionary tale aboard other vessels.

## Impact on the Fleet

The "Jennie Cushman business" becomes a standing reference point among fishermen. Disko Troop later brings it up to underscore a point about seamanship, asking rhetorically whether anyone has "heard about the Jennie Cushman" as a prelude to his lesson [007-035]. The incident's timing — "coming on an empty stomach" — makes it particularly memorable for those aboard the *We're Here* [007-055].
