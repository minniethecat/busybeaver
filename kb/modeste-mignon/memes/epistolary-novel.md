# Epistolary Novel

*Modeste Mignon* is constructed around letters — they drive the plot, reveal character, and sustain the central romantic illusion until reality intervenes.

## Definition

The epistolary form in *Modeste Mignon* is not the novel's exclusive mode (unlike *Les Liaisons dangereuses*) but its catalytic device. Modeste's letter to Canalis — written under the pseudonym O. d'Este M. — initiates the central plot mechanism. The narrator notes the intensity of her anticipation: "Full of admiration for the post-office, she followed her little sheet of paper on its way; she was happy, as we all are happy at twenty years of age, in the first exercise of our will" (010-016). Letters in the novel function as instruments of self-fashioning: Modeste constructs an identity through correspondence that her provincial life denies her.

## In the Novel

### Manifestations

The letter-writing plot unfolds through multiple correspondents. Modeste writes to Canalis as "O. d'Este M." (010-053); Canalis delegates the response to his secretary La Brière, creating a chain of misdirected identity. The letters themselves become objects of intense scrutiny — reread, analyzed, worn "between her flesh and her corset" (012-025). The epistolary form allows Balzac to dramatize the gap between written persona and lived self: Canalis's letters are beautiful but hollow; La Brière's are sincere but unsigned.

The novel's comedy derives from epistolary misidentification. Modeste falls in love with letters written by La Brière while believing them to be Canalis's. This structural irony — the reader knows more than the heroine — is a classic epistolary device adapted to Balzac's realist framework.

## In Balzac's *La Comédie Humaine*

Balzac rarely used the epistolary form as centrally as in *Modeste Mignon*. *Mémoires de deux jeunes mariées* is his other major epistolary work. The choice of letters as plot device in *Modeste Mignon* reflects the novel's treatment of romantic idealism: letters are the medium of illusion, and the resolution comes when characters finally meet face to face.
