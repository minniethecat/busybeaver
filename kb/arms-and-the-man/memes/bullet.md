# Bullet

The bullet — or rather, its absence — is one of the play's quietest and most devastating symbols. When [[raina-petkoff|Raina]] demands to see [[captain-bluntschli|Bluntschli]]'s revolver and discovers it contains chocolate creams instead of cartridges（001-396）, Shaw collapses the entire weight of military romanticism into a single absent object. The soldier without a bullet is, by conventional reckoning, useless. But the soldier who knows his bullets wouldn't fit his gun is a professional. The absent bullet becomes the play's most eloquent argument for realism.

## In the Play

The bullet's absence reverberates throughout the drama. Bluntschli's professional assessment of the cavalry charge at Slivnitza — "the Serbs had machine guns but they hadn't the right cartridges"（001-493）— turns on the same material detail. Sergius's glorious victory was enabled by a logistical failure: the enemy had guns but the wrong bullets. The romance of the charge is built on a foundation of mismatched ammunition. In Shaw's universe, the bullet — humble, material, unglamorous — determines the outcome that poetry attributes to [[courage]].

## Key Scene

> MAN. I've no ammunition. What use are cartridges in battle? I always carry chocolate instead.（001-395–396）

The exchange makes two arguments simultaneously. The surface argument is comic: a soldier who carries chocolate instead of bullets is absurd. The deeper argument is philosophical: in a world where bullets don't fit guns, chocolate is the more useful thing to carry. The chocolate feeds a hungry man; the mismatched bullet feeds nothing.

## Shaw's Position

Shaw's materialism — his insistence that the physical world determines the moral world — is nowhere more concentrated than in his treatment of the bullet. Wars are not won by courage or honor or patriotic fervor; they are won by the right bullets in the right guns. The romantic imagination, by ignoring the bullet in favor of the charge, makes wars possible; the realist mind, by attending to the bullet, might make wars less likely.
