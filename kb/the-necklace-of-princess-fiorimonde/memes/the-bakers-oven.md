# The Baker's Oven

The baker's oven is the central location in the tale of [[the-bread-and-discontents-story|"The Bread of Discontent"]], where a supernatural bargain transforms an ordinary village bakery into the source of a spreading affliction.

## The Elf in the Oven

The oven becomes inhabited after a batch of bread is burnt and the baker, in a rage, throws the loaves about. From one exceptionally burnt loaf emerges a dark supernatural creature:

> He threw the loaves all about the floor, when one, burnt blacker than the rest, broke in half, and out of it crept a tiny thin black man, no thicker than an eel, with long arms and legs. [1065-P1]

The creature offers the baker a bargain: let him dwell in the oven, and in return he will ensure the bread is always perfectly baked. The baker, concerned only with his trade, accepts:

> "Agreed!" said the baker. So the little imp crept into the oven and curled himself into the darkness behind, and the baker saw no more of him. [1077-P1]

## The Enchanted Bread

From within the oven, the elf bakes bread that is perfectly made in every outward respect — but carries a hidden curse. The elf warns the baker plainly:

> "It will taste very good," replied the imp, "but it will make all who eat it discontented, and they will think themselves very unfortunate whether they are so or no; but this will not do you any harm, and I promise you that you shall sell as much as you wish." [1075-P1]

The baker, indifferent to the suffering of others, agrees to the arrangement without hesitation.

## The Spreading Discontent

The bread baked in this oven passes through the entire village, affecting people of every station. The [[the-village-of-discontent|village]] descends into quarrelling and misery as each person who eats it finds reasons to be dissatisfied — the chief justice with his inheritance, the doctor with his patients, and the farmer with his house.

Only the baker remains cheerful, his trade flourishing as never before. The oven thus becomes the hidden source of a community-wide affliction, with the elf working unseen behind the darkness of the oven door.
