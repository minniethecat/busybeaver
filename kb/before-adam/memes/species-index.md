## Reading Guide

Species are the ecological context of the novel. [[Saber-Tooth]] is the apex predator that haunts the Folk's lives; [[wild-dogs|Wild Dogs]] hunt in packs and pose a different kind of threat. The **quality** column reflects development depth — featured pages have undergone enrichment with detailed behavioral and evolutionary analysis. The **References** count identifies the species most woven into the narrative and cross-referenced from other pages.
