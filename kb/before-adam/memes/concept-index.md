## Reading Guide

Concepts in *Before Adam* often emerge from the tension between instinct and learned behavior. The narrator's modern consciousness interprets pre-Adamic life through categories like **atavism** (reversion to ancestral traits) and **territory** (spatial competition between groups). Pages marked **featured** have been enriched with multi-paragraph explanations and citation-backed examples.
