## Reading Guide

The **Period** column places each event in the narrative arc: **early** events cover the Folk's life in the caves; **mid** events mark their displacement and journey; **late** events trace their arrival at new lands. Events with higher **References** counts are woven into more pages across the wiki — they are the turning points that define the story.

Together, these events form the arc of the novel: discovery of fire, conflict with the Fire People, the great migration, the massacre at the caves, and the final journey to the sea. Each is a step in the long trajectory from the Younger World toward the dawn of humanity.
