## Reading Guide

Places in *Before Adam* are defined by their affordances: a **cave** is safety, a **swamp** is food and danger, a **river** is both boundary and resource. Pages with higher reference counts typically mark locations central to multiple story beats — these are the best entry points for understanding the geography.
