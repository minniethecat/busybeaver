## Reading Guide

Characters are the emotional backbone of the narrative. Big-Tooth, the protagonist and first-person narrator, recounts his life among the Folk; Swift-One, his companion; and the Fire People represent a newer, more advanced hominid group. The **quality** column reflects how thoroughly each page has been developed — featured pages have undergone depth enrich and citation verification.

The **References** count helps identify the most central figures: characters appearing across multiple chapters and in cross-references tend to be narrative anchors.
