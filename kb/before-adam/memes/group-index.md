## Reading Guide

The **Type** column distinguishes tribes (the Horde, the Fire People) from clans (the Tree People). Groups with higher **Quality** scores have undergone enrichment with detailed analysis of social structure, customs, and evolutionary significance. Pages with high **References** counts are the most interconnected — start there to understand the social landscape.

Groups in *Before Adam* are not just collections of individuals — they are competing evolutionary strategies. The Horde represents cooperative but primitive collective living; the Fire People represent technological and military superiority; the Tree People represent an older, more arboreal adaptation. The fate of each group — extermination, dispersal, or absorption — traces the arc of hominid evolution itself.
