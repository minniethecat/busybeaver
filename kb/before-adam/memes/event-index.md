## Reading Guide

Events in *Before Adam* often pivot on inter-group contact. The Fire People's incursions, the Folk's seasonal movements, and chance encounters with other hominid varieties drive the narrative forward. Featured events have detailed descriptions with PN citations tracing them to specific chapters.
