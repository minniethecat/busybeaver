# The Russian Academy of Sciences

The Imperial Academy that elected Bunin an Honorary Academician in 1909 — representing the institutional recognition of literature in late imperial Russia.

## Role in the Novel

This organization shapes the world of *The Village* — whether as an institution that characters interact with directly or as a structural force that conditions rural life.
