# Sery (Gray)

Sery — "Gray" — is a Durnovka peasant whose nickname is his identity. The name itself is Bunin's commentary: in the world of rural poverty, individuality is a luxury. Sery is gray like the Russian sky, gray like the unpainted wood of peasant huts, gray like a life lived at the threshold of visibility.
