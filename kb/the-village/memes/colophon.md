[050-002] *The Village* was published in 1910 by [[ivanushka|Ivan]] Bunin. It was translated from Russian in 1923 by Isabel F. Hapgood.

[050-003] This ebook was produced for Standard Ebooks by Robin Whittleton, and is based on a transcription produced in 2019 by **Tim Lindell**, **David E. Brown**, and Distributed Proofreaders for Project Gutenberg and on digital scans from the Internet Archive.

[050-004] The cover page is adapted from *Thunderstorm*, a painting completed in 1896 by Józef Chełmoński. The cover and title pages feature the **League Spartan** and **Sorts Mill Goudy** typefaces created in 2014 and 2009 by The League of Moveable Type.
