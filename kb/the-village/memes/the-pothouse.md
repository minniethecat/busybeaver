# The Pothouse

The drinking establishment the Krasoff father ran in Durnovka — a site where alcohol lubricates the dissolution of family and community.

## Role in the Narrative

This location appears in *The Village* as part of Bunin's detailed mapping of rural Russian space — a geography where every place carries moral and social weight.
