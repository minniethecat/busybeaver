# The Court System

The judicial institutions of rural Russia — where justice was administered by gentry magistrates and peasant elders under a legal code that favored the propertied.

## Role in the Novel

This organization shapes the world of *The Village* — whether as an institution that characters interact with directly or as a structural force that conditions rural life.
