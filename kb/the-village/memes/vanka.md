# Vanka

Vanka represents the younger generation of Durnovka peasants — rootless, restless, disconnected from both the land that sustained his ancestors and the traditions that gave their lives meaning. He is Deniska's counterpart: where Deniska is cruel, Vanka is merely adrift.
