# The River

The shallow river dividing the Old Town from the Black Suburb — a geographical and social threshold marked by the stench of the tanyards.

## Role in the Narrative

This location appears in *The Village* as part of Bunin's detailed mapping of rural Russian space — a geography where every place carries moral and social weight.
