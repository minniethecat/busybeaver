# The Tanyards

The leather-tanning district along the river in the Old Town — a zone of industrial stench that marks the boundary between town and suburb, commerce and poverty.

## Role in the Narrative

This location appears in or is referenced by *The Village*, contributing to Bunin's detailed geographical imagination of rural Russia.
