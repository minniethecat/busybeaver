# Standard Ebooks

The volunteer-driven digital publisher that produced the modern ebook edition of The Village — representing the contemporary institutional framework for preserving and distributing public domain literature.

## Role in the Novel

This organization shapes the world of *The Village* — whether as an institution that characters interact with directly or as a structural force that conditions rural life.
