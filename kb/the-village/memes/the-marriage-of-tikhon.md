# The Marriage of Tikhon Ilitch

The marriage that Tikhon contemplates and arranges — an economic transaction as much as a personal union, reflecting the commodification of all relationships in the rural economy.

## Role in the Narrative

This event occurs in or shapes the world of *The Village*, contributing to Bunin's depiction of rural Russian life as a cycle of repetition, catastrophe, and endurance.
