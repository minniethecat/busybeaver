# The Tax Collection

The recurring autumnal event when rural police extract taxes from the peasantry — the engine of rural dispossession and the rhythm of Tikhon Krasoff's commercial calendar.

## Role in the Narrative

This event occurs in or shapes the world of *The Village*, contributing to Bunin's depiction of rural Russian life as a cycle of repetition, catastrophe, and endurance.
