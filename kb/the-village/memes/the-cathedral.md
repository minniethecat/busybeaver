# The Cathedral of St. Sophia

The great cathedral in the Old Town where Kuzma stands during the Liturgy — a monument of institutional Orthodoxy observed by a peasant who cannot find answers within its walls.

## Role in the Narrative

This location appears in or is referenced by *The Village*, contributing to Bunin's detailed geographical imagination of rural Russia.
