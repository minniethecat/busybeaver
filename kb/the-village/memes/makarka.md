# Makarka

Makarka is a wandering pilgrim (*strannik*) who crosses paths with Kuzma, representing the tradition of Russian holy foolishness (*yurodstvo*) — a form of religious devotion that blurs the boundary between sanctity and madness. In a novel largely devoid of positive religious figures, Makarka's wandering piety offers an alternative to the institutional Orthodoxy that has lost its hold on the peasants.
