# Matorin

Matorin is the shopkeeper who employed the young Tikhon and Kuzma Krasoff — a representative of the established merchant class that Tikhon would eventually surpass. His shop in the bazaar is where the brothers learned the commercial arts that would shape their divergent destinies.
