# The State Vodka Monopoly

The government monopoly on vodka production and sale (1894–1902) — making the state the largest purveyor of spirits in the empire and the indirect cause of rural alcoholism.

## Role in the Novel

This organization shapes the world of *The Village* — whether as an institution that characters interact with directly or as a structural force that conditions rural life.
