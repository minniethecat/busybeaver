# The Fire

A destructive village fire — one of the recurring disasters that punctuate rural life, alongside famine, epidemic, and tax enforcement.

## Role in the Narrative

This event occurs in or shapes the world of *The Village*, contributing to Bunin's depiction of rural Russian life as a cycle of repetition, catastrophe, and endurance.
