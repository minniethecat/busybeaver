# The Pilgrim Community

The informal network of religious wanderers (stranniki) who moved through rural Russia — a decentralized spiritual institution operating outside the Orthodox hierarchy.

## Role in the Novel

This organization shapes the world of *The Village* — whether as an institution that characters interact with directly or as a structural force that conditions rural life.
