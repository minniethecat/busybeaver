# Suvritta

**Suvritta** is the daughter of a merchant in the island of [[Kâlayavana]], and the wife of [[Ratnodbhava]], son of a minister of the King of [[magadha|Magadha]].

## Biography

Suvritta lived on the island of [[kalayavana|Kâlayavana]], where a servant of a rich man named [[Kâlagupta]] waited upon her. (002-099) A young merchant named [[ratnodbhava|Ratnodbhava]], son of a minister of the King of [[magadha|Magadha]], arrived on the island and became acquainted with her. They were subsequently married.

While crossing the sea with her husband, a great storm arose, the ship sank, and Suvritta and her nurse were the only persons saved. (004-154–157) She was cast adrift and later encountered by other travellers.

> "This lady, whose name is Suvritta, is the daughter of a merchant in the island of Kâlayavana, and the wife of Ratnodbhava. While crossing the sea with her husband, there was a great storm, the ship sank, and this lady and I, her nurse, were the only persons saved." (004-154–157)
