# Matanga

Matanga was a brahman sage who became a key companion to Prince [[Râjavâhana]]
during his adventures. He was the son of a minister, an apprentice of the sage
[[vamadeva|Vâmadeva]], and was granted a divine vision by [[siva|Siva]] that led the prince on a
journey to the underworld realm of [[patala|Pâtâla]].
(003-053)

## Biography

Matanga introduced himself as the son of one of the king's ministers, brought
up in the court but drawn to a life of religious devotion.
(003-053)

> "I, Matanga by name, am the son of one of these, and was brought up..."
> (003-053)

While engaged in deep meditation and austerities, Matanga received a divine
visitation from the god [[siva|Siva]], who addressed him directly:

> "'Matanga, I am pleased with your devotions; they...'"
> (003-095)

The vision promised Matanga a great fulfilment, and he became entirely convinced
of its truth. His story greatly excited the curiosity of Prince [[rajavahana|Râjavâhana]].
(003-107) (003-120)

Matanga led the prince to a sacred place where they encountered a mysterious
woman who made obeisance and gave them a token. Matanga guided [[rajavahana|Râjavâhana]]
through the descent into [[patala|Pâtâla]], the underworld realm.
(003-128) (003-136)

## Role in the Narrative

Matanga serves as the spiritual guide who opens the path to [[patala|Pâtâla]]. His vision
from [[siva|Siva]] provides the divine mandate for the prince's descent into the underworld.
Without Matanga's devotion and the resulting vision, [[rajavahana|Râjavâhana]] would not have
made the journey that forms a central episode of his adventures.

> "Matanga, delighted at such a speedy fulfilment of the promise given in..."
> (003-150)

[[rajavahana|Râjavâhana]] was treated with great respect and kindness by Matanga and his
companions in [[patala|Pâtâla]], and Matanga accompanied the prince for part of his
journey back.
(003-154) (003-160)

According to the index of characters, Matanga later married [[kalindi|Kalindí]], who
became the Queen of [[patala|Pâtâla]].
(026-085)

## Relationships

Matanga was the apprentice of [[vamadeva|Vâmadeva]], the celebrated rishi consulted by
[[rajahansa|King Râjahansa]]. He became a trusted guide and companion to Prince [[rajavahana|Râjavâhana]].
He married [[kalindi|Kalindí]], who became Queen of [[patala|Pâtâla]].
(003-053) (026-085)
