# Krittika

## Definition

Krittika (the Pleiades) is a constellation used in the Hindoo Tales as an astrological reference point for establishing the timing of important events. Its position relative to the moon marks the month in which occurrences take place.

## In the Narrative

The constellation appears twice as a chronological marker. A story is set "when the moon is in the constellation Krittika" (017-144), giving temporal context to a narrative event. Similarly, another passage dates an event to "the very month while the moon is in Krittika" (025-093). These references reflect the text's use of astrological conventions to frame time.

> "…the moon is in the constellation Krittika (or the…" (017-144)

> "…very month while the moon is in Krittika (the…" (025-093)
