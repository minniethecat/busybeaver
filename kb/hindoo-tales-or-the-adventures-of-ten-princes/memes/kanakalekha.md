# Kanakalekha

**Kanakalekha** is the princess of [[Kalinga]], daughter of the King of [[kalinga|Kalinga]], and the wife of Prince [[Mantragupta]].

## Biography

Kanakalekha was the daughter of the King of [[kalinga|Kalinga]]. She was abducted by agents acting under orders, who brought her before their master at sword-point, disregarding her tears and entreaties. (021-077)

The King of [[andhra|Andhra]], a long-time enemy of the King of [[kalinga|Kalinga]], had taken Kanakalekha's father prisoner and was about to kill him. However, the King of [[andhra|Andhra]] fell in love with Kanakalekha and, wishing to marry her, spared her father's life and treated him with kindness for her sake. (021-158)

She later became the wife of Prince [[Mantragupta]], one of the companions of [[rajavahana|Râjavâhana]].
