# Caste

## Definition

Caste denotes the four-fold social division of traditional Hindu society. In the Hindoo Tales, the proper maintenance of caste boundaries is presented as a duty of kingship and a marker of just governance.

## In the Narrative

Caste appears as a social framework. A man encounters "a maiden of his own caste whose appearance he liked" (019-059), indicating caste as a criterion for matrimonial suitability. More prominently, a king declares "I kept each of the four castes in their proper sphere" (025-070) as a summary of his righteous administration, placing the preservation of caste order among the highest duties of a ruler.

> "…he saw a maiden of his own caste whose appearance he liked,…" (019-059)

> "…I kept each of the four castes in their proper sphere, and…" (025-070)
