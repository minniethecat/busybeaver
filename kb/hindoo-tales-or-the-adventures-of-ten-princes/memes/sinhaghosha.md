# Sinhaghosha

## Biography

Sinhaghosha was formerly a chief officer of police before rising to become the King of [[benares|Benâres]]. His fortunes underwent dramatic reversals — from imprisonment to kingship and finally to deposition.
> "There was at that time among the prisoners a man named Sinhaghosha, formerly a chief officer of police, but now imprisoned."
> (011-120)

When the narrator [[apaharavarma|Apahâravarma]] escaped from prison, Sinhaghosha informed the king of what had taken place the following morning.
> "In the morning after my escape, Sinhaghosha informed the king of what had taken place."
> (011-154)

## Role in the Narrative

Sinhaghosha was placed on the throne of [[benares|Benâres]] as a boy of about five years old by a group of ministers who then had him carefully educated. This made him a figurehead ruler, controlled by the ministers who had installed him.
> "other ministers, placed Sinhaghosha, a boy about five years old, on the throne, and had him carefully educated."
> (014-145)

As he grew older, Sinhaghosha longed to be freed from the restraint of his ministers. In an act of defiance, he caused his minister to be arrested when he came to the palace the next day as usual.
> "Sinhaghosha, longing to be freed from restraint, caused his minister to be arrested, when he came the next day to the palace, as usual."
> (015-001)

His attempt to assert independence failed. The narrator was liberated from the dungeon and Sinhaghosha was deposed. Having a double claim to the throne, the narrator was acknowledged as king in his stead.
> "deliverer from the dungeon; Sinhaghosha was deposed; and I, having double claim to the throne, was acknowledged king in his stead."
> (016-005)

He is recorded in the genealogical summary as the deposed King of [[benares|Benâres]].
> "_Sinhaghosha_, the deposed King of Benâres."
> (026-143)

## Relationships

- **Ministers of [[benares|Benâres]]** — The officials who installed Sinhaghosha as a boy-king and controlled the kingdom through him (014-145).
- **[[apaharavarma|Apahâravarma]]** — The prince who was imprisoned, escaped, and ultimately deposed Sinhaghosha to claim the throne of [[benares|Benâres]] (011-154, 016-005).
