# Sumantra

**Sumantra** is the son of Minister [[Dharmapâla]] and the elder brother of [[Kâmapâla]] and [[sumittra|Sumittra]].

## Biography

Sumantra was one of three sons of [[dharmapala|Dharmapâla]], a minister who had long served King [[Râjahansa]] and enjoyed his entire confidence. His brothers were [[sumittra|Sumittra]] and [[kamapala|Kâmapâla]]. (001-106) The family of [[dharmapala|Dharmapâla]] was one of three ministerial families serving the court of [[rajahansa|Râjahansa]], alongside the families of [[padmodbhava|Padmodbhava]] and [[sitavarma|Sitavarma]]. (001-105–107)

Sumantra is listed in the character index of Chapter 26 as the son of [[dharmapala|Dharmapâla]], confirming his place in the genealogy. (026-150) Unlike his brother [[kamapala|Kâmapâla]], Sumantra does not play an independent role in the narrative.

> "The first of these had three sons, Sumantra, Sumittra, and Kâmapâla; the second, two, Susruta and Ratnodbhava; and the last had also two, Sumati and Satyavarma." (001-106–107)
