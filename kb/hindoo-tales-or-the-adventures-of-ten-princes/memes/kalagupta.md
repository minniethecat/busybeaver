# Kâlagupta

**Kâlagupta** is a rich man living in the island of [[Kâlayavana]], father of [[Suvritta]], and the former master of the old woman who serves as nurse to [[suvritta|Suvritta]] and tells the story of [[Ratnodbhava]]'s voyage.

## Biography

Kâlagupta was a wealthy householder residing on the island of [[kalayavana|Kâlayavana]]. He had a beautiful daughter named [[suvritta|Suvritta]] and retained servants, including an old woman who attended upon his daughter. (002-098–099) When the young merchant [[ratnodbhava|Ratnodbhava]] arrived on the island, he became acquainted with Kâlagupta and subsequently married his daughter [[suvritta|Suvritta]]. (002-100–102)

> "I was the servant of a rich man, named Kâlagupta, living in the island of Kâlayavana, and I waited on his daughter Suvritta. One day a young merchant, named Ratnodbhava, son of a minister of the King of Magadha, arrived in the island, and having become acquainted with my master, he married his beautiful daughter."
> (002-098–102)
