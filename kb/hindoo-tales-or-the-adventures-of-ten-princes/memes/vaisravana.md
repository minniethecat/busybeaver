## Biography

Vaisravana was a merchant of [[pushpapuri|Pâtaliputra]]. (024-053) He was the father of [[sagaradatta|Sagaradatta]], whose daughter was the queen consort of the King of Oude. (024-053) Through this lineage, Vaisravana became connected to the royal houses of the narrative.

## Role

Vaisravana appears in the narrative solely through his familial connections, as the father of [[sagaradatta|Sagaradatta]] and the maternal grandfather of a queen. (024-053)

## Relationships

- **[[sagaradatta|Sagaradatta]]**: His daughter, mother of a queen. (024-053)
- **King of Oude**: His grandson-in-law, through his daughter [[sagaradatta|Sagaradatta]]. (024-053)

## Quotations

> Mother was Sagaradatta, daughter of Vaisravana, a merchant of Pâtaliputra. (024-053)
