# Râgamanjari

Râgamanjari was an actress and the sister of [[Kâmamanjari]]. She made her
first appearance as a dancer during the events of the ninth chapter.
(009-177) (026-130)

## Biography

Râgamanjari was the daughter of an old woman who managed a troupe of
performers. Her sister was [[kamamanjari|Kâmamanjari]], likewise an actress in the
same troupe.
(009-177)

> "Kâmamanjari — Râgamanjari by name — would make her first appearance
> as a dancer."
> (009-177)

She was cared for by a nurse named [[Sringâlika]], who attended to her
during her time with the acting company.
(026-146)

## Role in the Narrative

Râgamanjari appears in the adventures of the ninth and tenth chapters,
connected to the tale of the magic purse. Her sister [[kamamanjari|Kâmamanjari]] played
a central role in seducing the muni [[Mârîchi]], while Râgamanjari herself
was involved in the affairs of the troupe.
(009-177) (010-171)

## Relationships

- **[[Kâmamanjari]]** — her sister, also an actress (009-177)
- **[[Sringâlika]]** — her nurse (026-146)
- **[[Mârîchi]]** — the muni seduced by her sister (009-177)
