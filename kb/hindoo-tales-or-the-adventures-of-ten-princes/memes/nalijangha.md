# Nâlijangha

Nâlijangha was an old man rescued by [[visruta|Visruta]] from a well in the forest, who later became a loyal servant and trusted agent of [[mantragupta|Mantragupta]] in the kingdom of [[mahishmati|Mahishmati]].

## Biography

Nâlijangha first appears in the narrative as an old man whom [[visruta|Visruta]], one of the ten princes, rescued from a well. He later entered the service of [[mantragupta|Mantragupta]], another of the princes, and became his trusted agent and messenger.

> Nâlijangha, the old servant whom I had rescued in the forest, met me on my arrival, and was waiting at the place which I had appointed. (024-139)

When [[mantragupta|Mantragupta]] arrived at [[mahishmati|Mahishmati]], Nâlijangha kept him informed of the political situation, including the schemes of King [[amittravarma|Amittravarma]].

> After the forester was gone, I said to the old man (whose name was Nâlijangha): "That wretch Amittravarma is trying to make it up with his sister-in-law by promoting a good marriage for her daughter; no doubt he thinks to persuade her to recall her son, that he may have him in his power." (024-078–081)

## Role

Nâlijangha served as [[mantragupta|Mantragupta]]'s secret agent. Before [[mantragupta|Mantragupta]] made any overtures to the minister [[aryaketu|Aryaketu]], he sent Nâlijangha to test the minister's loyalty:

> Before, however, making any overtures to him, I desired Nâlijangha to try him secretly and ascertain his feelings towards me. (025-050)

Nâlijangha conducted multiple interviews with [[aryaketu|Aryaketu]], attempting to persuade him that a foreigner should not hold an important position and that it would be desirable to get rid of [[mantragupta|Mantragupta]]. When [[aryaketu|Aryaketu]] refused to speak against [[mantragupta|Mantragupta]], praising his courage, generosity, and kindness, Nâlijangha reported back, confirming the minister's fidelity (025-052–059).

> After hearing this from Nâlijangha, I tried the old minister in various ways, and seeing no reason to doubt his fidelity... (025-064–065)

## Relationships

- **[[visruta|Visruta]]** — The prince who rescued Nâlijangha from a well, saving his life (024-139, 026-118)
- **[[mantragupta|Mantragupta]]** — His master; Nâlijangha served as [[mantragupta|Mantragupta]]'s trusted agent and messenger in the affairs of [[mahishmati|Mahishmati]] (024-079, 025-050, 025-064)
- **[[amittravarma|Amittravarma]]** — The antagonist king of [[mahishmati|Mahishmati]] whose schemes Nâlijangha reported to [[mantragupta|Mantragupta]] (024-079)
