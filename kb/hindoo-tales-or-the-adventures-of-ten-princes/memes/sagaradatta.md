## Biography

Sagaradatta is mentioned in Section 24 as the mother of the Queen of Oude and the daughter of [[vaisravana|Vaisravana]], a merchant of [[pâtaliputra|Pâtaliputra]]. Her daughter married the King of Oude, establishing a family connection that plays a role in the narrative of [[mantragupta|Mantragupta]].

## Role

Sagaradatta appears only genealogically—her sole mention establishes a kinship link between characters in the story. Through her, the Queen of Oude is revealed to be a cousin by the motherʼs side to the protagonistʼs father, creating a familial bond that justifies the protection of a young relation.

## Quotations

> "She is the daughter of the King of Oude... and her mother was Sagaradatta, daughter of Vaisravana, a merchant of Pâtaliputra." (024-053)
