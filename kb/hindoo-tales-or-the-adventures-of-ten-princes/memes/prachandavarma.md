# Prachandavarma

## Biography

Prachandavarma was a prince of the royal house of [[vidarba|Vidarba]], the younger brother of the reigning king at [[mahishmati|Mahishmati]]. He rose to prominence in the turbulent period following the fall of [[anantavarma|Anantavarma]] and the death of King [[amittravarma|Amittravarma]].
> "the Prince Prachandavarma, the king's younger brother."
> (024-075)

He was about to marry the Princess [[manjuvadini|Manjuvâdini]], daughter of the fallen King [[anantavarma|Anantavarma]], at a time of great rejoicing in the kingdom.
> "the Prince Prachandavarma, the king's younger brother, is about to marry the Princess Manjuvâdini, and the rejoicings are on this account."
> (024-075)

## Role in the Narrative

After the king's death, the queen mother approached Prachandavarma and urged him to seize the vacant throne, declaring that he should occupy it at once and make her daughter his queen.
> "she said to Prachandavarma: 'The throne is now vacant; you should occupy it at once, and make my daughter your queen.'"
> (024-109)

Prachandavarma's rise was met with opposition from factions at court who were ill-affected towards him, spreading rumours of ill omens.
> "be ill-affected towards Prachandavarma, and said to them: 'Last night I had a dream which forebodes misfortune.'"
> (024-116)

Despite the warnings, he pressed forward. His reign was cut short by assassination — a murder universally attributed to his enemy, the King of [[asmaka|Asmaka]], who had previously orchestrated the downfall of [[anantavarma|Anantavarma]].
> "The assassination of Prachandavarma was universally attributed to his enemy, the King of Asmaka."
> (024-174)

He is recorded in the genealogical summary as King or Governor of [[mahishmati|Mahishmati]], killed by [[visruta|Visruta]] — the son of [[anantavarma|Anantavarma]] who avenged his father's death and reclaimed the throne of [[vidarba|Vidarba]].
> "_Prachandavarma_, King or Governor of Mahishmati, killed by Visruta."
> (026-124)

## Relationships

- **[[manjuvadini|Manjuvâdini]]** — Princess, daughter of [[anantavarma|Anantavarma]], betrothed to Prachandavarma (024-075).
- **Queen Mother** — Widow of the previous king who urged Prachandavarma to seize the throne and marry her daughter (024-109).
- **[[visruta|Visruta]]** — Son of [[anantavarma|Anantavarma]], who killed Prachandavarma and reclaimed the throne of [[vidarba|Vidarba]] (026-124).
- **King of [[asmaka|Asmaka]]** — The rival king widely believed to have orchestrated his assassination (024-174).
