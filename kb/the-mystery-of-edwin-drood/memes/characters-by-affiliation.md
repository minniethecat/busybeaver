## About This Index

This index reveals the social and institutional fabric of Cloisterham by grouping characters according to their affiliations — the Cathedral community, the Nuns' House, the philanthropic circle, the legal establishment, and the various outsiders whose paths intersect in Dickens' mystery. The affiliation field is populated for the majority of named characters; entries without a listed affiliation are still included and appear at the top of the table.
