# France

The country that produced two of [[george-bernard-shaw|Shaw]]'s most important intellectual influences: [[jean-baptiste-lamarck|Jean-Baptiste Lamarck]] and Henri Bergson. France represents, in [[george-bernard-shaw|Shaw]]'s intellectual geography, the tradition of vitalist and [[jean-baptiste-lamarck|Lamarck]]ian thought that provided an alternative to the mechanistic [[charles-darwin|Darwin]]ism dominant in [[england|England]].
