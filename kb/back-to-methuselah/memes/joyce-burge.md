# Joyce Burge

A politician in Parts II and III who represents the conventional political establishment confronting a transformation it cannot comprehend. Burge's long-winded rhetoric and political calculation are rendered irrelevant by the Barnabas brothers' achievement of extended life.

## Dramatis Personae

Joyce Burge is [[george-bernard-shaw|Shaw]]'s satirical portrait of the professional politician — ambitious, verbose, and fundamentally incapable of grasping that [[creative-evolution|Creative Evolution]] changes everything, including the foundations of political power.

## Role in the Play

### Parts II–III

Burge treats the Barnabas brothers' project as just another political issue to be managed. His failure to understand its significance is both comic and pointed — [[george-bernard-shaw|Shaw]] is arguing that [[political-science|politics]] as usual cannot address the biological revolution [[creative-evolution|Creative Evolution]] demands.
