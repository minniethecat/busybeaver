# Eden Garden

The dramatic setting of Part I of *[[back-to-methuselah|Back to Methuselah]]* — the physical garden where [[adam|Adam]], [[eve|Eve]], and [[the-serpent|the Serpent]] enact the first act of [[creative-evolution|Creative Evolution]]. Distinguished from the theological concept of the [[garden-of-eden|Garden of Eden]], this is the specific stage on which [[george-bernard-shaw|Shaw]]'s revision of [[garden-of-eden|Genesis]] takes place.
