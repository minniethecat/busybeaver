# Dublin

The city of [[george-bernard-shaw|Bernard Shaw]]'s birth in 1856 and the setting of the Preface's opening anecdote. Dublin represents [[george-bernard-shaw|Shaw]]'s intellectual starting point — the place where he first encountered the religious and intellectual questions that would eventually lead him to [[creative-evolution|Creative Evolution]].
