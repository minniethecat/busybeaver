# The He-Ancient

One of the [[longevity|long-lived]] beings of Part V, representing the endpoint of [[creative-evolution|Creative Evolution]] — a being whose [[immortality|extended lifespan]] has allowed capacities to develop that ordinary humanity cannot access. His body is merely "an automaton," freeing consciousness for pure contemplation.

## Dramatis Personae

The He-Ancient is what humanity becomes when the [[life-force|Life Force]] has worked for millennia. He is not a god but a natural product of evolution — calm, detached, serenely amused by the urgent concerns of short-lived beings.

## Role in the Play

### Part V: As Far as Thought Can Reach

The He-Ancient guides us through the world of the far future, explaining how the [[life-force|Life Force]] has transformed human nature. Love, ambition, art, identity itself — these are the concerns of beings who know they [[free-will|will]] die. For the Ancients, who have outlived mortality, they are of merely historical interest.
