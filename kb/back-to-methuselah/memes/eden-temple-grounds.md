# Eden Temple Grounds

The grounds surrounding the [[temple-of-the-oracle|Temple of the Oracle]] in Part IV. This transitional space — between the outside world and the Temple interior, between the short-lived and the [[longevity|long-lived]], between the familiar and the transformed — represents the threshold of [[creative-evolution|Creative Evolution]].

## Location in the Text

The Temple grounds are where visitors first encounter the world of the [[longevity|long-lived]]. Before entering the Temple proper, they must cross this threshold — a spatial representation of the conceptual threshold between the old human condition and the new.

## Significance

The Temple grounds represent the liminal state — the space of transition — that [[creative-evolution|Creative Evolution]] requires. One does not jump instantly from short-lived to [[longevity|long-lived]], from limited to expanded consciousness. There is a threshold, and crossing it takes time.
