# Mr. Bagley

## Character Overview

Mr. Bagley is a neighboring farmer mentioned occasionally in the narrative. Unlike the central neighbor figure John Jones, Bagley appears only in passing, representing the broader — but less intimate — rural community that surrounds the Durham farm.

## Role in the Story

Bagley is one of several neighbors who contribute to the texture of rural life without playing a major role in the plot. He is mentioned in connection with local news and farming activities, serving as part of the social landscape that the Durhams enter.
