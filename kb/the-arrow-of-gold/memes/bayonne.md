The French border town near the Spanish frontier, a key transit point for Carlist agents and arms smuggling operations

Described in the novel as a key setting of the Carlist intrigue.

## Role in the Narrative

Appears as a significant location in the novel's geography, connected to the Carlist gun-running operation and the characters' movements across the French-Spanish border region.
