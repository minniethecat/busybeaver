# The Alphonsist Forces

The government forces loyal to Alfonso XII, opposing the Carlist insurrection in the Third Carlist War. They appear in the novel primarily as a threat — the source of the artillery fire that Doña Rita and her escort must navigate during her crossing of the front lines.

## Role in the Narrative

The Alphonsist forces provide the novel's most visceral wartime imagery. During Rita's journey, the escort party is "absolutely exposed to artillery fire" and "the Alphonsist shells" fall "about two to the minute" (003-031). The laconic observation that "the Alphonsist shells are not much better than our own" captures the grim equality of artillery warfare, where both sides suffer equally from unreliable munitions.
