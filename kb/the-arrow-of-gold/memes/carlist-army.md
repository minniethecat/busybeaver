# The Carlist Army

The military forces of the Carlist cause, fighting in the hills and gorges of northern Spain for the claim of Don Carlos de Bourbon to the Spanish throne.

## Role in the Narrative

The Carlist army appears in the novel primarily as a destination — the arms being smuggled from France are bound for "the Carlist detachments in the South" (P04-007). Characters move between the army's operational zones and the civilian world of Marseilles. Doña Rita's visit to the Carlist headquarters at Tolosa provides the novel's most direct glimpse of military life, with its "Carlist uniforms" (003-031) and its atmosphere of beleaguered purpose.
