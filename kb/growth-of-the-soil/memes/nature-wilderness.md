# Nature & Wilderness

The natural world is both the setting and a central character in *Growth of the Soil*. From the untamed wilderness that Isak first clears at Sellanraa to the seasonal rhythms that govern pioneer life, Hamsun portrays nature as a force that shapes, rewards, and resists human effort. The following pages explore nature, wilderness, animals, and the land itself.
