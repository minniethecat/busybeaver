# Labor & Industry

The tension between traditional agricultural labor and encroaching industrialization runs through *Growth of the Soil*. The discovery of copper on Isak's land, the arrival of the telegraph, and the rise of trade in the wilderness mark a transformation from subsistence to commerce.
