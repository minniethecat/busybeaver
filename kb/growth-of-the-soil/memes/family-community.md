# Family & Community

From the isolated homestead of Sellanraa to the growing settlement that springs up around it, *Growth of the Soil* traces the emergence of community in the Norwegian wilderness. Family ties, neighborly relations, and the unwritten codes of pioneer life shape the novel's social world. The following pages explore family life, community, and the pioneer experience.
