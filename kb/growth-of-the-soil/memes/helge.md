# Helge

**Helge** 是 Brede Olsen 的幼子，Barbro 和 Katrine 的弟弟。
他是 Breidablik 家庭的一员，以出海捕鲱鱼（herring fishery）为生。

## 家庭背景

Helge 是 Brede Olsen 三个孩子中最年轻的一个。第 XII 章首次提及他时，
叙述者将三个孩子一并列出——Barbro、Helge、Katrine（012-057）：

> The Breidablik woman felt a sting at the sight; the cloak she could have done without—thank heaven, she set no store by such foolishness!—but … she had children of her own—Barbro, a great girl already, Helge, the next, and Kathrine, all of an age for school. (012-057)

三兄妹曾在山下村子里上过学，但全家搬到荒野中的 Breidablik 之后，
孩子们便失去了上学机会，如同叙述者所言，"let the children run heathen again"（012-057）。

随着小说推进，三个孩子陆续离开家庭：
- Barbro 嫁给了 Axel Ström，搬到 Maaneland
- Helge 出海去了鲱鱼渔场
- Katrine 到医生家帮佣（019-011）

> Helge was gone to the herring fisheries already, and Katrine was going to help at the doctor's. That left only the two youngest—well, well, there was a third on the way, true, but, anyhow … (019-011)

尽管离家，Helge 仍定期寄钱回家补贴家用，
这让 Brede 颇感欣慰（030-035）：

> There's Barbro married at Maaneland, and Helge out at the herring fishery; they send home something in money or money's worth as often as they can; ay, even Katrine, doing waiting at home, managed, strangely enough, to slip a five-Krone note into her father's hand last winter, when things were looking extra bad. (030-035)

## 性格与父子关系

Brede 对 Helge 的期望颇为现实——他希望儿子能承担对父母的经济责任，
但不反对年轻人抽烟或小酌。
第 XXX 章里，Brede 在店铺中对旁人发表他对子女义务的见解（030-036）：

> "Look you, now, my boy, Helge; if he smokes tobacco a bit, or takes a dram now and then, I've nothing against that, we've all been young in our time. But 'tis not right of him to go sending one letter home after another and nothing but words and wishes in. 'Tis not right to set his mother crying. 'Tis the wrong road for a lad." (030-036)

这种务实而不苛求的态度体现了 Brede 性格中松弛的一面。
他并不禁止 Helge 抽烟喝酒，但他期望儿子用实际行动。而非空洞的文字——来回报家庭。

## 五十克朗的来信

仿佛听到了父亲的那番话，Helge 随后寄来了一封附有五十克朗的信（030-037）：

> It almost seemed as if Helge had heard that speech of his father's, for there came a letter from him after with money in—fifty Kroner, no less. And then Bredes had a great time; ay, in their endless extravagance they bought both meat and fish for dinner, and a lamp all hung about with lustres to hang from the ceiling in the best room. (030-037)

这五十克朗让 Brede 一家着实阔绰了一番——他们买了鱼和肉做晚餐，
还买了一盏带玻璃垂饰的华丽吊灯挂在客厅天花板上。
叙述者以"endless extravagance"（无尽的奢侈）来调侃 Brede 一家拿到钱后的大手大脚。

## 在小说中的叙事功能

Helge 作为次要角色，体现了 Hamsun 笔下 Breidablik 一家的分化轨迹。
Brede Olsen 作为一个失败的垦荒者，其子女陆续离开家庭、各自谋生——
Helge 的出海代表了边远地区年轻人最典型的出路之一：渔业。
他与 Barbro 和 Katrine 一起构成了 Breidablik 年轻一代的群像，
三人的去向折射出荒野定居点的人口外流趋势。
