# Index of Characters

A comprehensive listing of all characters who appear in Lord Dunsany's *Fifty-One Tales*. The cast ranges from mythological figures (Pan, Charon, Odysseus) to allegorical personifications (Death, Time, Nature) and Dunsany's own invented beings.

## Character Types

The characters fall into several categories:
- **Mythological** — Figures from Greek, Egyptian, and other traditions (Pan, Athene, the Sphinx)
- **Allegorical** — Personified concepts (Death, Time, Nature, Fame)
- **Invented** — Dunsany's own creations (King Karna-Vootra, the Tradesman)
- **Historical/Contemporary** — Real-world figures and archetypes from Edwardian London
