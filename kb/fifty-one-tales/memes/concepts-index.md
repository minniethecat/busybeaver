A thematic index of the abstract concepts and ideas that Lord Dunsany weaves through *Fifty-One Tales*. These include grand philosophical themes — Death, Time, Nature, Fate — alongside more delicate notions like Beauty, Inspiration, and the quiet sorrow of passing things.

## Thematic Clusters

Dunsany's concepts cluster around several preoccupations:
- **Mortality and Time** — Death, Time, Decay, Eternity
- **Nature and the Divine** — Nature, Pan, the Gods, Creation
- **Art and Beauty** — Poetry, Song, Inspiration, Beauty
- **Human Condition** — Fate, Joy, Sorrow, Fame
