## Types of Places

- **Mythical** — Arcady, the Underworld, the Fields of Paradise
- **Real** — London, Piccadilly, the British Museum
- **Invented** — Sendara, Sooma, the Edge of the World
- **Symbolic** — Allegorical landscapes that represent states of being
