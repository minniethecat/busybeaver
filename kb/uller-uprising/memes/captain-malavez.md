# Captain Inez Malavez

Captain Inez Malavez was the [[terra|Terra]]n woman officer in charge of the [[skilk|Skilk]] [[telecast]] station, whose communications center became [[chartered-uller-company|the Company]]'s ad hoc command post during the first night of the [[uller-uprising|Uller Uprising]].

## Overview

Captain Inez Malavez commanded the [[skilk|Skilk]] [[telecast]] station, the critical communications facility that [[carlos-von-schlichten|von Schlichten]] seized as his command post when the uprising erupted. Her role as station chief placed her at the nerve center of [[chartered-uller-company|the Company]]'s tactical response during the first crucial hours of the siege. She maintained communications with scattered [[chartered-uller-company|Company]] forces, relayed [[carlos-von-schlichten|von Schlichten]]'s orders to field commanders, and her noncoms managed the flow of combat reports that fed the situation map.

## Background

Malavez was one of several women officers in [[chartered-uller-company|the Company]]'s military establishment on [[uller|Uller]], holding the rank of captain and commanding the [[skilk|Skilk]] area's primary communications facility. Her station housed the [[telecast]] equipment, switchboards, and radio gear that connected [[skilk|Skilk]] to [[konkrook|Konkrook]], the other [[chartered-uller-company|Company]] posts, and mobile forces in the field. She reported through the [[skilk|Skilk]] chain of command under Brigadier-General Barney Mordkovitz.

## Role in the Uprising

### Command Post Establishment

When the uprising erupted during the banquet, [[carlos-von-schlichten|von Schlichten]] immediately recognized the [[telecast]] station's strategic value. Arriving at the station, he gave Malavez her first combat assignment: "Captain Malavez, you know what to do about defending this station. Get busy doing it" （008-020）. Simultaneously, he ordered her to inform [[konkrook|Konkrook]] of the situation: "have that girl in booth three tell [[konkrook|Konkrook]] what's happened here, and say that I won't be coming down, as planned, just yet" （008-020）. Malavez's immediate priority was securing the physical facility — [[chartered-uller-company|the Company]]'s ability to coordinate a defense depended entirely on keeping this communications hub operational.

### Communications Coordination

Throughout the night of the uprising, Malavez managed the flood of communications that poured into the command post. Her facility connected [[carlos-von-schlichten|von Schlichten]] to forces across [[chartered-uller-company|the Company]] Reservation and beyond. She relayed orders to Colonel Jarman at the military airport — "tell him to get cracking on it" — directing him to prevent rebel forces from seizing contragravity equipment （008-066）. Her noncoms operated "with a headset, getting combat reports directly from the switchboard" （010-051）, feeding information to the headquarters staff.

### Governor-General Announcement

When [[carlos-von-schlichten|von Schlichten]] assumed the office of [[governor-general|Governor-General]] following [[sid-harrington|Harrington]]'s death, Malavez executed the official proclamation. Von Schlichten ordered: "Captain Malavez! You will send out an all-station [[telecast]], immediately: Military Commander-in-Chief [[carlos-von-schlichten|Carlos von Schlichten]], being informed of the deaths of both [[sid-harrington|Governor-General Harrington]] and Lieutenant-Governor [[eric-blount|Blount]], assumes the duties of [[governor-general|Governor-General]], as of 00:01 today" （009-058）. This broadcast formally established [[carlos-von-schlichten|von Schlichten]]'s political authority over all [[chartered-uller-company|Company]] personnel on [[uller|Uller]].

### Siege Communications Hub

By the following day, Malavez's station had evolved into [[chartered-uller-company|the Company]]'s de facto military headquarters. The facility hosted "a big terrain-board," multiple TV screens including one connected to a contragravity-mounted camera "at two thousand feet," and a constant flow of staff — "Half a dozen [[terra|Terra]]ns, of both sexes, were working furiously" to maintain the situation map while "one of Captain Inez Malavez's noncoms" managed combat communications （010-051）. The operation impressed even [[carlos-von-schlichten|von Schlichten]]: "Everything was clicking like well-oiled machinery" （010-051）.

## Characteristics & Traits

Malavez was a competent and unflappable communications officer whose technical expertise translated directly into tactical value. Her ability to maintain communications discipline under combat conditions — keeping the switchboard operational while the station was under threat of attack — was critical to [[chartered-uller-company|the Company]]'s survival on the first night. She functioned effectively in a command post dominated by senior male officers, demonstrating [[chartered-uller-company|the Company]]'s pragmatic approach to talent under fire.
