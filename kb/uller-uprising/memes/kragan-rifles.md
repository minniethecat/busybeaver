The **Kragan Rifles** are a native mercenary regiment serving the [[chartered-uller-company|Chartered Uller Company]]. Composed primarily of [[kragan|Kragan]] [[ulleran|Ullerans]], the regiment represents the Company's most effective instrument for projecting military force using indigenous manpower.

## Equipment and Adaptation

The regiment's weaponry consists of standard Federation infantry rifles that have been modified — "stocked and sighted to conform to their physical peculiarities" (001-039). This adaptation acknowledges the distinct Ulleran body plan: a four-foot stature and four-armed anatomy that requires different ergonomics than weapons designed for Terran soldiers. The fact that the Company invested in custom-modified weaponry for Kragan troops indicates the regiment's importance to colonial military strategy.

## Operational History

The Kragan Rifles have been deployed in key security operations, most notably the suppression of the Konkrook riot. This deployment of native troops against native civilians follows a well-established pattern of colonial counterinsurgency — using indigenous forces to suppress indigenous resistance, thereby reducing Terran casualties while deepening ethnic and tribal divisions within the subject population.

## Strategic Role

The Kragan Rifles serve multiple strategic functions for the Company. Militarily, they provide a combat-effective force that does not drain Federation manpower. Politically, they create a class of Kragan with a vested interest in Company rule, aligning the most culturally advanced Ulleran group with the colonial administration (005-045). Symbolically, their existence demonstrates that the Company can govern Uller through Ullerans, legitimizing the colonial order as something other than pure Terran domination.

## Leadership

The regiment operates under Company command, with Kragan soldiers serving under both Terran and Kragan officers. King [[king-kankad|Kankad]] of the Kragan supports the regiment as part of his broader policy of accommodation with the Company. The Kragan Rifles thus represent an institutional manifestation of the Kragan-Company alliance.

## Parallels

The Kragan Rifles have a counterpart in the Fifth Zirk Cavalry (006-007), another native auxiliary unit drawn from a different Ulleran tribe. The Company's practice of raising separate tribal regiments — Kragan infantry here, [[zirk|Zirk]] cavalry there — reinforces the divide-and-rule strategy that underpins colonial military organization.
