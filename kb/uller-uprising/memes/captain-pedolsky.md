# Captain Pedolsky

Captain Pedolsky was a [[terra|Terra]]n officer commanding [[kragan-rifles|Kragan Rifles]] airjeep units during the [[konkrook|Konkrook]] riot, whose rapid response to the assassination attempt on [[sid-harrington|Governor-General Harrington]] earned [[carlos-von-schlichten|von Schlichten]]'s commendation.

## Overview

Captain Pedolsky commanded [[kragan-rifles|Kragan Rifles]] in airjeep-mounted units that responded to the anti-[[terra|Terra]]n riot in [[konkrook|Konkrook]]. He was distinguished by the speed of his force's deployment — [[carlos-von-schlichten|von Schlichten]] praised his "very smart work; you must have those vehicles of yours on [[interstellar-travel|hyperspace]]-drive" when Pedolsky's airjeeps arrived at the riot scene with minimal delay （003-003）. After the assassination attempt on [[sid-harrington|Governor-General Harrington]], Pedolsky secured the crime scene and led the search of Keeluk's house for evidence.

## Background

Pedolsky held a captain's commission and commanded [[kragan-rifles|Kragan Rifles]] — [[chartered-uller-company|the Company]]'s [[uller|Uller]]-native mercenary troops — mounted in airjeeps. His unit was part of the [[konkrook|Konkrook]] garrison's rapid-response capability, designed to suppress urban disturbances before they could escalate. Like all officers commanding [[terran-military-doctrine|native troops]], he carried a "[[cross-species-communication|geek-speaker]]" — the false palate and tongue-clicker necessary for speaking [[ulleran|Ulleran]] — and could issue orders directly to his [[kragan|Kragan]] soldiers in their own language.

## Role in the Uprising

### Riot Response

When the [[konkrook|Konkrook]] riot erupted and [[sid-harrington|Governor-General Harrington]] was targeted for assassination, Pedolsky's unit responded with exceptional speed. Von Schlichten was impressed: "We seem to be, Captain Pedolsky. Very smart work" （003-003）. The rapid arrival of armed [[kragan-rifles|Kragan Rifles]] in airjeeps helped contain the violence and prevent the mob from overwhelming the [[terra|Terra]]ns before additional forces could arrive.

### Crime Scene Securement

Von Schlichten issued Pedolsky explicit orders regarding the investigation into [[sid-harrington|Harrington]]'s poisoning: "My compliments to Captain Pedolsky, and tell him he's to make immediate and thorough search of the house in front of which the incident occurred, and adjoining houses. For his information, that's Keeluk's house" （003-010）. The search parameters were specific — Pedolsky was to "look for traces of [[sid-harrington|Governor-General Harrington]]'s collie, or any of the other terrestrial animals that have been disappearing — that goat, for instance, or those rabbits" （003-010）. The disappearances of [[terra|Terra]]n animals were linked to the [[ulleran|Ulleran]] drug trade, and Keeluk was suspected of using them in drug preparation.

### Arrest Orders

Pedolsky was additionally ordered to "bring Keeluk in, alive and in condition to be interrogated" （003-010）. This required careful coordination — the suspect had to be seized before he could flee or be silenced by co-conspirators, and he needed to be in condition to provide actionable intelligence about the assassination plot.

### Command in Ulleran

Pedolsky demonstrated the linguistic competence necessary for commanding [[terran-military-doctrine|native troops]]. He "opened the pouch on his belt and took out the false palate and tongue-clicker without which no [[terra|Terra]]n could do more than mouth a crude and barely comprehensible pidgin-[[ulleran|Ulleran]]," then "began jabbering orders" to his [[kragan|Kragan]] soldiers （003-005）. This direct communication was operationally critical — relayed orders through interpreters would have cost precious seconds during the riot.

## Characteristics & Traits

Pedolsky was an efficient field officer whose primary assets were speed and reliability. His rapid deployment of airjeep-mounted [[kragan-rifles|Kragan Rifles]] demonstrated good unit readiness, and his ability to communicate directly with [[terran-military-doctrine|native troops]] in their own language was an essential skill for [[chartered-uller-company|Company]] officers. His role in the [[sid-harrington|Harrington]] investigation, while limited, showed that he could follow complex, multi-part orders under chaotic conditions.
