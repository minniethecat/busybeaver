The **Chartered Uller Company**, commonly referred to simply as "the Company," is the [[terran-federation|Terran Federation]]-chartered corporation that exercises sovereign authority over the planets [[uller|Uller]] and [[niflheim|Niflheim]]. It represents the primary institutional mechanism of Terran colonial expansion in the Uller system.

## Charter and Authority

The Company was originally chartered to exploit Uller alone, but was compelled by the Federation to also assume responsibility for Niflheim (004-025). This dual-world mandate stretched Company resources and created tensions between its commercial objectives and its administrative obligations. The charter grants the Company sweeping sovereign powers including the right to maintain military forces, conduct diplomacy with native populations, and administer civil governance.

## Organizational Structure

The Company is backed financially by the [[banking-cartel|Banking Cartel]], a consortium of Terran financial institutions whose investment in the Uller venture creates powerful political incentives to maintain profitability. General [[von-schlichten|Von Schlichten]], the Company's military commander, relies on the Cartel's political weight to confirm his authority in disputes with civilian administrators (012-006).

The Company maintains its own military apparatus, of which the [[kragan-rifles|Kragan Rifles]] are a critical component. These native mercenary troops, equipped with modified Federation rifles "stocked and sighted to conform to their physical peculiarities" (001-039), provide the Company with combat power that does not depend on Federation troop deployments.

## Labor Practices

Company labor practices have drawn scrutiny from humanitarian organizations on Terra. The [[extraterrestrials-rights-association|Extraterrestrials' Rights Association]], employing investigators such as [[paula-quinton|Paula Quinton]], has documented abuses in the Company's treatment of native workers — both [[ulleran|Ulleran]] laborers in the equatorial mines and [[nifl|Nifl]] workers forcibly relocated to the polar mining operations (004-026, 003-017).

## Colonial Strategy

The Company pursues a divide-and-rule strategy among the native population, selectively allying with accommodating Ulleran groups such as the [[kragan|Kragan]] and the Fifth Zirk Cavalry (006-007), while suppressing resistance from anti-colonial movements. This approach has created lasting fractures in Ulleran society that persist alongside the Company's extractive economic operations.
