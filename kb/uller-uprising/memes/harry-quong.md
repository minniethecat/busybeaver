# Harry Quong

Sergeant Harry Quong was a [[terra|Terra]]n aircar driver in the service of the [[chartered-uller-company|Chartered Uller Company]], an experienced veteran of operations on [[uller|Uller]] who served as personal pilot to General [[carlos-von-schlichten|Carlos von Schlichten]] and participated in key actions throughout the uprising.

## Overview

Harry Quong was one of the "old [[uller|Uller]] hands" — experienced [[terra|Terra]]n personnel who had spent years on the silicate world and knew its dangers intimately. As [[carlos-von-schlichten|von Schlichten]]'s primary aircar driver, he was present at critical moments throughout the novel, from the initial rescue at [[konkrook|Konkrook]] to the final aerial reconnaissance of the burning city. His competence under fire and familiarity with [[ulleran|Ulleran]] conditions made him an indispensable member of [[carlos-von-schlichten|von Schlichten]]'s inner circle.

## Background

Quong served as a sergeant in [[chartered-uller-company|the Company]] forces on [[uller|Uller]]. His long experience on the planet was a frequent point of reference — he and Corporal Hassan Bogdanoff, his frequent partner on the aircar's front seat, had "done this sort of work before" （001-010）, indicating extensive field experience in [[ulleran|Ulleran]] operations.

His service predated the arrival of many [[chartered-uller-company|Company]] officials, and his practical knowledge of [[ulleran|Ulleran]] conditions often contrasted with the theoretical understanding of newer Residents and administrators.

## Role in the Uprising

### The Konkrook Rescue

Quong was the driver of [[carlos-von-schlichten|von Schlichten]]'s command-car during the pivotal rescue at [[konkrook|Konkrook]]. When [[carlos-von-schlichten|von Schlichten]] spotted [[paula-quinton|Paula Quinton]] and Mohammed Ferriera being attacked by a mob, Quong executed a rapid combat descent. Together with Bogdanoff on the ball-turret guns, they "left a swath of mangled, killed, wounded, and stunned natives" before setting the car down hard on a clump of rioters as close as possible to the besieged [[terra|Terra]]ns （001-010）.

During the rescue, Quong caught the radiophone thrown forward by [[carlos-von-schlichten|von Schlichten]] and "began speaking rapidly and urgently into it while he steered with the other hand" — a display of the multi-tasking competence that characterized veteran [[chartered-uller-company|Company]] personnel （001-014）.

Quong's reaction to the revelation that an [[ulleran|Ulleran]] had called a [[terra|Terra]]n woman a "dog" — a mortal insult in [[ulleran|Ulleran]] terms — was a characteristic "Coo-bli'me!" （001-040）, reflecting the shared astonishment of the veteran [[terra|Terra]]ns at this cultural breach.

### Skilk Banquet & Night of the Uprising

Quong was present at the banquet in [[skilk|Skilk]] on the night [[sid-harrington|Governor-General Harrington]] died. On a world where "the social difference between a human general and a human aircar-driver was almost infinitesimal," Quong and Bogdanoff attended the same gathering as the senior officers （007-012）.

When the uprising struck, Quong survived the initial mutiny and was later seen emerging from the besieged [[chartered-uller-company|Company]] buildings alongside Jules Keaveney, Commander Prinsloo of the *Aldebaran*, and other [[terra|Terra]]ns during the [[kragan|Kragan]] counterattack （007-033）.

### Southern Reconnaissance

In the aftermath of the [[skilk|Skilk]] campaign, Quong piloted the aircar carrying [[carlos-von-schlichten|von Schlichten]] and [[paula-quinton|Paula Quinton]] south toward [[konkrook|Konkrook]], navigating by the dry bed of the Hoork River. He and Bogdanoff alternated driving duties while eating lunch — a routine flight operation conducted against the backdrop of the ongoing crisis （012-003）.

As they approached [[konkrook|Konkrook]], Quong circled high above the city while Bogdanoff handled radio communication with Gongonk Island, keeping the car "high enough to be above the trajectories of incoming shells" as they surveyed the burning city below （012-010）.

## Characteristics & Traits

Quong demonstrated the calm competence under fire that distinguished veteran [[chartered-uller-company|Company]] personnel. His driving skills were exceptional — he could maneuver an aircar at shoulder-height over a hostile mob, execute combat landings, and maintain altitude above artillery trajectories while navigating over hostile territory.

His partnership with Hassan Bogdanoff was a well-established working relationship; the two alternated between driving and gunner duties seamlessly, forming an effective combat crew.
