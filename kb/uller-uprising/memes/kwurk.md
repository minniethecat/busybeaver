# Kwurk

Kwurk was the northernmost of the Free Cities on the [[eastern-shore|Eastern Shore]] of [[uller|Uller]], situated in the coastal region east of [[konkrook|Konkrook]]. It hosted a small Company Residency — one of the Chartered Uller Company's outlying administrative posts — which was evacuated during the [[uller-uprising|Uller Uprising]] when the garrison could no longer hold the position against the scale of native unrest.

## Geography & Location

Kwurk occupied the northern extreme of the Eastern Shore's chain of independent Free Cities, a string of settlements that lay outside the direct control of the major Ulleran kingdoms (010-022). Its coastal location made it accessible by contragravity vessel, and it served as the Company's northernmost fixed administrative presence on the eastern seaboard.

## The Kwurk Residency

The Company maintained a small Residency at Kwurk, staffed by a minimal garrison — typical of Company outposts on Uller, which relied more on political arrangements with local rulers than on large standing garrisons for security. The Residency's function was primarily diplomatic and administrative: maintaining Company relations with the Free Cities of the Eastern Shore and monitoring developments in territory that fell under the influence of King Orgzild, the dominant native power on the eastern side of the planet.

## In the Uprising

The Company Residency at Kwurk was rendered untenable early in the Uprising. As native unrest spread from Konkrook outward, the isolated Kwurk garrison found itself cut off and exposed. "The entire garrison of the small Residency at Kwurk, the most northern of the eastern shore Free Cities, had arrived at Kankad's Town in two hundred-foot contragravity scows," successfully evacuating their post when defense was no longer feasible (010-022).

The evacuation of Kwurk was significant as an early indicator of the geographic scope of the uprising. It demonstrated that the unrest was not confined to Konkrook but had spread along the Eastern Shore, forcing the abandonment of Company positions hundreds of miles from the capital. The garrison's successful withdrawal to [[kankads-town|Kankad's Town]], a friendly Kragan stronghold, highlighted the importance of Company-allied native powers in maintaining operational flexibility during the crisis.

## Strategic Significance

Though a minor outpost, Kwurk's loss illustrated the vulnerability of isolated Company positions on Uller. With a total Terran population on the planet numbering only in the thousands, every outpost abandonment represented a meaningful reduction in the Company's territorial presence. The Kwurk garrison's integration into the forces at Kankad's Town helped bolster the Kragan capital's defenses, but the Free City itself fell under the sway of hostile forces for the remainder of the conflict.
