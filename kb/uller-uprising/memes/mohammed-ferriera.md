# Mohammed Ferriera

Mohammed Ferriera was a [[terra|Terra]]n field agent for the [[extraterrestrials-rights-association|Extraterrestrials' Rights Association]] ([[extraterrestrials-rights-association|ERA]]), a longtime colonist on [[uller|Uller]] who was critically injured during the [[konkrook|Konkrook]] riot that opened the [[uller-uprising|Uller Uprising]].

## Overview

Mohammed Ferriera was introduced as [[paula-quinton|Paula Quinton]]'s [[extraterrestrials-rights-association|ERA]] colleague on [[uller|Uller]], accompanying her on what should have been a routine field visit to a [[konkrook|Konkrook]] neighborhood. When an [[ulleran|Ulleran]] mob attacked them in the street, Ferriera was gravely wounded — one of the first [[terra|Terra]]n casualties of the rising anti-[[chartered-uller-company|Company]] violence. His injury catalyzed the initial confrontation and revealed the deteriorating security situation on [[uller|Uller]] that would shortly explode into full-scale rebellion.

## Background

Ferriera was an established figure on [[uller|Uller]], having been on the planet "for the last three months; ever since the *City of Canberra* got in from [[niflheim|Niflheim]]" （003-034）. As a field agent for the [[extraterrestrials-rights-association|Extraterrestrials' Rights Association]], he was part of the reformist movement that had previously "put an end to the peonage system on Yggdrasill" （005-011）. Von Schlichten, despite his general skepticism toward outside reformers, acknowledged Ferriera as "a decent bloke" whose organization had "really done a lot of good" （005-011）.

## Role in the Uprising

### The Konkrook Attack

Ferriera and [[paula-quinton|Paula Quinton]] ventured into a hostile district of [[konkrook|Konkrook]], a decision [[carlos-von-schlichten|von Schlichten]] later criticized: "I'd have thought Mohammed Ferriera would have had more sense than to take you there, or go there, himself, for that matter" （003-038）. When the mob attacked, Ferriera was among the first struck down. During the aircar rescue by [[carlos-von-schlichten|von Schlichten]]'s team, a native was seen raising a dagger at "the wounded [[terra|Terra]]n" — Ferriera — before [[paula-quinton|Paula Quinton]] intervened, slashing the attacker's knife-arm and then chopping down on the creature's neck （003-014）.

### Evacuation and Aftermath

After the rescue, Ferriera was unconscious and required immediate medical attention. The rescue party loaded him into [[command-car|von Schlichten's command car]], and upon arrival at the hospital, "a stretcher was waiting, with a [[terra|Terra]]n interne and two [[ulleran|Ulleran]] orderlies" to carry the still-unconscious man into treatment （004-007）. His critical condition underscored the severity of the [[konkrook|Konkrook]] mob violence and provided a visceral warning of the broader uprising to come.

### Symbolic Value

Ferriera's injury had political resonance beyond the immediate medical crisis. [[gorkrink|Prince Gorkrink]] of [[skilk|Skilk]] later "spoke briefly, in sympathy for the great and good friend of all [[ulleran|Ulleran]] peoples, Mohammed Ferriera, who had been injured, and hoping that he would soon enjoy full health again" （005-051）. This public acknowledgment from an [[ulleran|Ulleran]] prince demonstrated that Ferriera's reputation as a genuine advocate for native rights had earned him respect even among the local ruling class — distinguishing him from the more exploitative [[chartered-uller-company|Company]] personnel.

## Characteristics & Traits

Ferriera was a committed reformer who, unlike many [[terra|Terra]]n colonists, saw his mission as protecting native rights rather than extracting profit. His willingness to enter dangerous native districts — even if [[carlos-von-schlichten|von Schlichten]] considered it foolish — reflected a hands-on approach to field work that distinguished the [[extraterrestrials-rights-association|ERA]]'s methods from [[chartered-uller-company|the Company]]'s administrative distance. That [[carlos-von-schlichten|von Schlichten]], a hardened military pragmatist, held him in personal regard despite their institutional differences spoke to Ferriera's character.
