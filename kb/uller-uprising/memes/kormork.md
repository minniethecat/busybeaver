# Native-Major Kormork

Native-Major Kormork was a [[kragan|Kragan]] officer holding the rank of native-major in the [[chartered-uller-company|Chartered Uller Company]]'s military forces. As [[king-kankad|King Kankad]]'s co-parent and a trusted combat commander, Kormork led the [[kragan|Kragan]] relief column that broke through to besieged [[chartered-uller-company|Company]] House during the [[konkrook|Konkrook]] uprising and subsequently commanded critical counter-attack operations.

## Overview

Kormork was introduced by [[king-kankad|King Kankad]] as a trusted associate: "This is Kormork. He and I have borne young to each other" （012-056） — the [[kragan|Kragan]] practice of shared parenting representing the deepest bond of trust in their society. Assigned by [[king-kankad|Kankad]] to protect [[paula-quinton|Paula Quinton]] with the explicit instruction "Bring her back safe. Or else find yourself a good place to hide" （012-056）, Kormork proved himself one of the most capable [[kragan|Kragan]] combat commanders in [[chartered-uller-company|the Company]]'s service, leading the defense of [[chartered-uller-company|Company]] House, the counter-attack to retake strategic positions, and the clearance of the native-troops barracks.

## Background

### Kragan Physiology & Society

As a [[kragan|Kragan]], Kormork shared the physical and cultural characteristics of his species: silicon-based biology, four-armed anatomy adapted for astonishing rates of rifle fire, and the [[kragan|Kragan]] social institution of co-parenting — bearing young with another [[kragan|Kragan]] — which constituted the deepest form of personal alliance. His "freshly painted" [[chartered-uller-company|Company]] emblem （012-056） marked his formal allegiance to the [[terra|Terra]]n military organization, a visible commitment that carried particular weight in the context of the uprising.

### Relationship with King Kankad

Kormork's bond with [[king-kankad|Kankad]] — they "have borne young to each other" （012-056） — established him within the highest circles of [[kragan|Kragan]] leadership. This co-parenting relationship meant that Kormork was not merely a [[chartered-uller-company|Company]] soldier but a figure of significant standing within [[kragan|Kragan]] society, trusted with the protection of [[king-kankad|Kankad]]'s most valued [[terra|Terra]]n ally, [[paula-quinton|Paula Quinton]].

## Role in the Uprising

### Protection of Paula Quinton

[[king-kankad|Kankad]]'s assignment of Kormork to [[paula-quinton|Paula Quinton]]'s protection was a gesture of enormous significance in [[kragan|Kragan]] terms. By entrusting his co-parent with the safety of the [[terra|Terra]]n woman who had become his closest [[chartered-uller-company|Company]] ally, [[king-kankad|Kankad]] signaled both his personal commitment to the [[terra|Terra]]n cause and his confidence in Kormork's military competence. The threat implicit in "Or else find yourself a good place to hide" （012-056） underscored the gravity of the responsibility.

### Relief of Company House

When the [[konkrook|Konkrook]] uprising erupted, Kormork commanded the [[kragan|Kragan]] relief force that fought its way to the besieged [[chartered-uller-company|Company]] House — perhaps the single most important tactical action of the uprising's opening phase. His after-action report was crisp and professional: "We were in quarters, aboard the *Aldebaran* and in the guesthouse at the airport. We were attacked, fifteen minutes ago, by a mob. We took ten minutes beating them off, and five more getting here" （014-100）. The timeline he reported — fifteen minutes from initial attack to arrival at [[chartered-uller-company|Company]] House — represented an extraordinary feat of combat mobility.

Kormork had also exercised independent tactical initiative during the relief: "I sent Native-Captain Zeerjeek and the rest of the force to retake the supply-depot and the shops and lorry hangars, which had been taken, and relieve the military airport, which is under attack" （014-100）. This demonstrated both strategic awareness — recognizing which positions were critical to retake — and the command authority to dispatch forces independently.

Kormork confirmed the safety of the two [[chartered-uller-company|Company]] spaceships: "The two ships, the *Aldebaran* and the freighter *Northern Star*, are both safe. I saw them go on contragravity and rise to about a hundred feet" （014-102）. This intelligence was vital to [[carlos-von-schlichten|von Schlichten]]'s assessment of the strategic situation.

### Counter-Attack Operations

Von Schlichten immediately deployed Kormork's force for the next phase of operations: "Kormork, you take all your gang, and half these soldiers from the Eighteenth, here, and help clear the native-troops barracks. And don't bother taking any prisoners; we can't spare personnel to guard them" （014-108）. The order to take no prisoners was a significant departure from normal [[terra|Terra]]n military practice, and Kormork's reaction was telling: "Kormork grinned. The taking of prisoners had always been one of those irrational [[terra|Terra]]n customs which no [[ulleran|Ulleran]] regarded with favor, or even comprehension" （014-109）.

Later, during the ongoing clearing operations, reports filtered back: "Kormork's outfit are trying to take back the lorry-hangars. Jarman's got a couple of airjeeps and a combat-car helping them" （014-115）, and "Kormork's still working on that. Nothing definite, yet" （014-119） — evidence of sustained combat operations extending well beyond the initial relief action.

## Characteristics & Traits

Kormork exemplified the [[kragan|Kragan]] warrior ethos: disciplined in [[chartered-uller-company|the Company]]'s service, ferocious in combat, and unburdened by [[terra|Terra]]n niceties about prisoners. His professional competence was matched by personal loyalty — the co-parent bond with [[king-kankad|Kankad]] provided the social foundation for his military reliability. Unlike some [[ulleran|Ulleran]] characters whose allegiance shifted with political convenience, Kormork's commitment appears to have been grounded in the personal bond with [[king-kankad|Kankad]] and, through [[king-kankad|Kankad]], to [[chartered-uller-company|the Company]]. His crisp after-action reporting style and his willingness to exercise independent command initiative marked him as one of the most capable native officers in [[chartered-uller-company|the Company]]'s forces.
