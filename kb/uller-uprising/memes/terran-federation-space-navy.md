The **Terran Federation Space Navy** is the space fleet of the [[terran-federation|Terran Federation]]. It was responsible for the initial discovery and exploration of both [[uller|Uller]] and [[niflheim|Niflheim]], actions that placed both planets in the public domain and paved the way for the [[chartered-uller-company|Chartered Uller Company]]'s charter.

## Exploration and Discovery

The Space Navy's exploration missions identified both Uller and Niflheim as habitable worlds with exploitable resources and sentient native populations. By declaring these discoveries public domain, the Navy established the legal framework under which the Federation could charter private corporations to administer colonial territories. This pattern — discovery by the Navy followed by exploitation by chartered companies — represents the standard template for Terran colonial expansion.

## Military Infrastructure

Beyond its exploratory role, the Space Navy operates proving grounds on Mars, where new weapons systems and spacecraft are tested. Colonel [[grinell|Grinell]], who serves on Uller, is described as "old Terran Federation Regular Army" rather than Navy (012-057), reflecting the distinction between planetary ground forces and the space fleet.

## Relationship with the Company

The Space Navy maintains an ambiguous relationship with the Chartered Uller Company. While the Company exercises sovereign powers on Uller and Niflheim under its charter, the Navy retains ultimate military authority as the armed force of the Federation that issued that charter. The presence of [[von-schlichten|Von Schlichten]] as the senior military commander on Uller — a Company officer rather than a Navy officer — creates potential jurisdictional tensions.

The Navy's role in the Company's sphere is largely one of oversight and ultimate authority, rather than direct administration. The [[kragan-rifles|Kragan Rifles]] and other Company forces handle day-to-day security operations, while the Navy represents the Federation's strategic interest in the system.

## Institutional Culture

As the senior branch of the Federation's military, the Space Navy embodies Terran institutional culture and carries the prestige of discovery and exploration. Its officers represent the Federation's formal authority, standing apart from the commercial imperatives that drive the [[banking-cartel|Banking Cartel]] and the Company's day-to-day operations.
