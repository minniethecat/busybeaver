# King Kankad

King Kankad is the ruler of a [[kingdom-of-kankad|Kragan kingdom]] centered on [[kingdom-of-kankad|Kankad's Town]], and [[carlos-von-schlichten|von Schlichten]]'s most important native ally during the [[uller-uprising|Uller Uprising]]. A [[kragan|Kragan]] who has learned [[lingua-terra|Lingua Terra]] and educated his people in [[terra|Terra]]n languages, Kankad provides the decisive military intervention — a fleet of contragravity vehicles — that breaks the siege of [[konkrook|Konkrook]] and enables the [[terra|Terra]]n counteroffensive.

## Overview

Kankad is introduced when [[carlos-von-schlichten|von Schlichten]] visits him to secure [[kragan|Kragan]] support for [[chartered-uller-company|the Company]]'s war against the [[ulleran|Ulleran]] kingdoms (005-036). Unlike the [[ulleran|Ulleran]] kings — silicon-based hermaphrodites with whom [[terra|Terra]]ns share no common biology or cultural framework — the [[kragan|Kragans]] are warm-blooded oxygen-breathers whose physiology and psychology are more compatible with human norms. This biological commonality, combined with historical [[kragan|Kragan]] antagonism toward the [[ulleran|Ullerans]], makes the [[kragan|Kragans]] natural allies for [[chartered-uller-company|the Company]].

Kankad governs his people from [[kingdom-of-kankad|Kankad's Town]], a settlement that reflects his deliberate program of cultural modernization. His schools teach [[kragan|Kragan]] children to read and write [[lingua-terra|Lingua Terra]] using textbooks printed in Johannesburg and shipped across interstellar distances for the purpose (005-045). This educational investment represents a long-term bet on integration with [[terra|Terra]]n civilization — a bet that pays off when the uprising makes [[kragan|Kragan]] military support essential.

## Background

### Kragan Society

The [[kragan|Kragans]] are one of three warm-blooded native species on [[uller|Uller]], alongside the [[nifl|Nifl]] and the [[jeel|Jeel]]. They evolved on the same world as the silicon-based [[ulleran|Ullerans]] but occupy a different ecological niche, breathing oxygen and maintaining a constant body temperature. The historical relationship between [[kragan|Kragans]] and [[ulleran|Ullerans]] has been one of intermittent conflict, with the more numerous [[ulleran|Ullerans]] generally dominating the warm-blooded minorities.

[[kingdom-of-kankad|Kankad's kingdom]] represents the most advanced example of [[terra|Terra]]n-native cultural synthesis on [[uller|Uller]]. His people have adopted [[terra|Terra]]n languages, imported [[terra|Terra]]n educational materials, and integrated [[terra|Terra]]n technology — particularly contragravity — into their way of life. This synthesis is not a surrender of [[kragan|Kragan]] identity but a strategic adaptation: Kankad recognizes that [[chartered-uller-company|the Company]]'s presence on [[uller|Uller]] is permanent, and that [[kragan|Kragan]] survival depends on partnership rather than resistance.

### Military Preparation

Kankad's military forces are unusually well-equipped for a native power. His kingdom possesses "the only artillery of greater than 75mm caliber in non-[[terra|Terra]]n hands on the planet" (005-045) — a fact [[carlos-von-schlichten|von Schlichten]] notes with professional approval when inspecting [[kragan|Kragan]] armaments. This artillery, combined with a fleet of contragravity vehicles, makes Kankad's forces the most formidable native military on [[uller|Uller]], capable of operating alongside [[terra|Terra]]n units as near-equals rather than auxiliaries.

## Role in the Uprising

### The Alliance

Von Schlichten secures Kankad's active support early in the crisis, recognizing that the [[kragan|Kragan]] king's forces are essential to holding the widely dispersed [[chartered-uller-company|Company]] Residencies against simultaneous native attacks. The alliance is built on mutual interest: Kankad has no desire to see the [[ulleran|Ulleran]] kingdoms — traditional [[kragan|Kragan]] enemies — gain control of [[uller|Uller]], and [[carlos-von-schlichten|von Schlichten]] needs every combat-capable force he can deploy.

Their conversations are conducted bilingually in [[lingua-terra|Lingua Terra]], which Kankad speaks fluently (005-038). This direct communication — without translators or mechanical aids — symbolizes the depth of [[kragan|Kragan]] integration into the [[terra|Terra]]n sphere. Kankad addresses [[carlos-von-schlichten|von Schlichten]] as a peer rather than a supplicant, and [[carlos-von-schlichten|von Schlichten]] reciprocates with a respect notably absent from his dealings with most [[ulleran|Ullerans]].

### The Konkrook Airlift

Kankad's decisive contribution comes when the siege of the [[konkrook|Konkrook]] [[chartered-uller-company|Company]] Reservation reaches its most critical phase. With the Residency surrounded by overwhelming [[ulleran|Ulleran]] forces and ammunition running low, Kankad dispatches his contragravity fleet to break through the siege perimeter and deliver reinforcements, supplies, and fire support.

The airlift is a military and diplomatic masterstroke. "Kankad's contragravity airlift landed the [[kragan|Kragan]] infantry that turned the battle," [[carlos-von-schlichten|von Schlichten]] observes (010-007). The intervention transforms the strategic picture: from a desperate defensive stand, the [[terra|Terra]]n-[[kragan|Kragan]] forces shift to offensive operations, lifting the siege and pursuing the retreating [[ulleran|Ullerans]]. Similar operations at other Residencies consolidate the victory, with [[kragan|Kragan]] forces providing the mobility and local knowledge that [[terra|Terra]]n regulars lack (010-015).

### Post-War Vision

In [[carlos-von-schlichten|von Schlichten]]'s post-war political settlement, the [[kragan|Kragans]] are elevated from mercenaries to proconsular administrators — the ruling class of a militarized colonial order. Kankad's investment in [[terra|Terra]]n education and military modernization positions his people perfectly for this role. The [[kingdom-of-kankad|Kragan kingdom]] becomes the model for what [[carlos-von-schlichten|von Schlichten]] envisions: native peoples who accept [[terra|Terra]]n supremacy and earn their place within the colonial hierarchy through loyal service.

## Relationships

- **[[carlos-von-schlichten|Carlos von Schlichten]]**: Kankad's primary [[terra|Terra]]n counterpart. Their relationship is built on mutual respect and shared strategic interests, conducted in fluent [[lingua-terra|Lingua Terra]] (005-038)
- **The [[kragan|Kragans]]**: Kankad's subjects, whom he has systematically prepared for integration into [[terra|Terra]]n civilization through language education and military modernization
- **The [[ulleran|Ulleran]] Kings ([[prince-gurgurk|Gurgurk]], [[orgzild|Orgzild]])**: Traditional enemies of the [[kragan|Kragans]]. Kankad's alliance with [[chartered-uller-company|the Company]] is in part a continuation of pre-existing native conflicts
- **[[chartered-uller-company|Company]] Officials**: Kankad deals with [[chartered-uller-company|the Company]] as a sovereign negotiating with equals, not as a native supplicant — a status he has earned through military capability and cultural adaptation
