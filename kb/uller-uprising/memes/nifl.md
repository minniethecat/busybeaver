The **Nifl** are a fluorine-based sentient species native to the planet [[niflheim|Niflheim]]. Alongside the [[ulleran|Ullerans]], they are one of the two major non-human species within the [[chartered-uller-company|Chartered Uller Company]]'s sphere of influence. Terrans colloquially refer to them as "geeks."

## Physiology

Nifl possess four hands, a configuration well-suited to the operation of complex contragravity armor systems (004-026). Their fluorine-based biochemistry places them in a different branch of the cosmic tree of life from both carbon-based Terrans and silicon-based Ullerans. Like the Ullerans, their physical form presents challenges for interaction with Terran-designed equipment, though their manual dexterity makes them adept at handling machinery.

## Population and Distribution

The Nifl population is starkly divided between two locations. Approximately five hundred Nifl remain on Niflheim itself, while roughly ten thousand have been relocated to work at the Uller polar mines (004-026). This twenty-to-one ratio of displaced to homeworld Nifl reflects the [[terran-federation-space-navy|Terran Federation Space Navy]]'s practice of forcibly relocating native labor to meet the Company's industrial requirements.

## Labor and Society

The Nifl's four-handed anatomy makes them exceptionally proficient operators of contragravity armor, a technology that is central to mining operations on both Niflheim and Uller. Their mass relocation to the polar mines of Uller represents one of the more severe instances of colonial labor extraction documented in Company records.

Their treatment by the [[banking-cartel|Banking Cartel]]-backed Company has drawn attention from Terran humanitarian organizations. The [[extraterrestrials-rights-association|Extraterrestrials' Rights Association]] has investigated conditions in the polar mines, citing the Nifl as a case study in the Federation's failure to uphold its own standards for native welfare (003-017).

## Relations with Ullerans

While both the Nifl and Ullerans are subject to Company rule, they occupy distinct ecological and social niches within the colonial order. The Nifl's role as industrial labor in the polar regions keeps them largely separate from the Ulleran kingdoms concentrated in the equatorial and temperate zones, creating a geographic division that has shaped inter-species dynamics under Company administration.
