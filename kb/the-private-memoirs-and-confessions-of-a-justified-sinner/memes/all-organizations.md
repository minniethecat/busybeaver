# All Organizations

An index of the religious and civic institutions that structure the world of the novel. The Church of Scotland, its dissenting offshoots, and the civil courts all play roles in the events that lead to and follow George Colwan's death.

## About This List

The novel's institutional landscape reflects the fractured religious politics of early 18th-century Scotland. The established Kirk, the Antiburgher Seceders, and the civil magistracy each claim authority, and characters move uneasily among these competing jurisdictions.
