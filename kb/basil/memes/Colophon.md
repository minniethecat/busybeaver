[033-002]*[[basil|Basil]]*

[033-003]was published in 1852 by

[033-004][Wilkie Collins](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Wilkie_Collins).

[033-005]This ebook was produced for

[033-006][Standard Ebooks](https://standardebooks.org/)

[033-007]by

[033-008][David Grigg](https://thegriggs.org/david),

[033-009]and is based on a transcription produced in 2003 by

[033-010]**James Rusk**

[033-011]for

[033-012][Project Gutenberg](https://www.gutenberg.org/ebooks/4605)

[033-013]and on digital scans from the

[033-014][Internet Archive](https://archive.org/details/bub_gb_PfW4qWihA1IC).

[033-016]*The Bayswater Omnibus*,

[033-017]a painting completed in 1895 by

[033-018][George W. Joy](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/George_W._Joy).

[033-019]The cover and title pages feature the

[033-020]**League Spartan** and **Sorts Mill Goudy**

[033-021]typefaces created in 2014 and 2009 by

[033-022][The League of Moveable Type](https://www.theleagueofmoveabletype.com/).
