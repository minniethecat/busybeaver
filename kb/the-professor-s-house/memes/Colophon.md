[P05-004][Willa Cather](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Willa_Cather).

[P05-005]This ebook was produced for

[P05-006][Standard Ebooks](https://standardebooks.org/)

[P05-007]by

[P05-008]**Dylan Cook**,

[P05-010]**Laura Natal Rodrigues**

[P05-012][Project Gutenberg](https://www.gutenberg.org/ebooks/65465)

[P05-013]and on digital scans from the

[P05-014][Internet Archive](https://archive.org/details/professorshouse0000cath_d6i7).

[P05-016]*Desert Village, Arizona*,

[P05-018][Francis John McComas](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Francis_McComas_(painter)).
