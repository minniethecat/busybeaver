# Major Events

The narrative of Notre-Dame de Paris is driven by a sequence of public
spectacles, private betrayals, and climactic reversals. This page indexes
every documented story event.

## Notes

Event tags group episodes by character involvement and theme. The chronological
order of events in the novel does not always match the historical timeline of
1482; Hugo compresses and rearranges events for dramatic effect.
