# Notre-Dame Cathedral in Focus

The cathedral is the true protagonist of Hugo's novel — "la grande figure de
pierre." Hugo devotes an entire book (Book III) to describing it, and its
towers, bells, cloisters, and sanctuary status drive the plot. This page
gathers every wiki entry connected to the cathedral.

## Notes

Hugo's architectural philosophy, most fully expressed in Book III, Chapter 1
("Notre-Dame"), argues that architecture was the great book of humanity before
the printing press. The cathedral embodies this thesis — a stone encyclopedia
of medieval thought.
