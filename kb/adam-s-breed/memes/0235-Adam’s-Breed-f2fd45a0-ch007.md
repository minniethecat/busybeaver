# III

[005-001]That night, in spite of the Holy Oil, [[olga-boselli|Olga]] went on her journey. After the great love that lay hidden in her heart, after the great anguish that lay whimpering in the basket, the spirit that was Olga slipped silently away to the Maker who would have no need to question, knowing all things and the reason thereof.
