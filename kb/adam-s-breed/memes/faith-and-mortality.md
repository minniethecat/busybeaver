## About This Page

Religion in *Adam's Breed* is Catholic, ritualistic, and disappearing. Death is sudden, sacrificial, and often tied to food or service. These themes converge in [[gian-luca-boselli|Gian-Luca]]'s final act — a starvation that is at once suicide, sacrifice, and sacrament.
