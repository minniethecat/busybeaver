## About This Index

The novel traces the life of [[gian-luca-boselli|Gian-Luca Boselli]], an Italian immigrant waiter in London, and the community surrounding him. This index brings together all 39 person pages — from the Boselli family to the staff of the Doric restaurant, from the villagers of the Campagna to the clients of Soho. Each entry links to a fuller page with biography, relationships, and narrative significance.
