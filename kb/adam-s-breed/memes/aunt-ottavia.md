## Overview

[[ottavia|Aunt Ottavia]] is [[maddalena-boselli|Maddalena Boselli]]'s aunt, a small, talkative Italian woman living in Coldbath Square, London. She is a significant presence in the Boselli family's life, providing both emotional support and, ultimately, financial inheritance. Known for her merry disposition and practical wisdom, [[ottavia|Aunt Ottavia]] serves as a bridge between the family's Italian past and their London present.

## Biography

[[ottavia|Aunt Ottavia]] lives in Coldbath Square, London, where her house becomes a gathering place for the extended Boselli family [089-001]. When [[gian-luca-boselli|Gian-Luca]] first expresses interest in her, [[maddalena-boselli|Maddalena]] tells him "I would like to know [[ottavia|Aunt Ottavia]]" [088-032], suggesting the aunt was more a presence in family lore than daily life until the family's relocation to London.

She is described as small and talkative [129-005], with a merry disposition that animates family gatherings [094-001]. [[father-antonio|Father Antonio]] serves as her confessor, and through him [[gian-luca-boselli|Gian-Luca]] is able to have his wedding to [[olga-boselli|Olga]] performed at St. Peter's [091-001]——[[ottavia|Aunt Ottavia]] becomes "very voluble and busy" arranging the ceremony [091-003].

[[ottavia|Aunt Ottavia]] "knew life, very thoroughly" [092-002] and pretended to be poor while secretly possessing considerable means [092-004]. This penurious facade [096-014] masks her substantial savings, which become crucial to the family after her death. She catches cold in May and dies [129-005], leaving her money to the Bosellis——an inheritance that provides security for [[olga-boselli|Olga]] and [[gian-luca-boselli|Gian-Luca]]'s future [180-007].

## Relationships

- **[[maddalena-boselli|Maddalena Boselli]]**: [[ottavia|Aunt Ottavia]] is [[maddalena-boselli|Maddalena]]'s aunt by blood. After [[ottavia|Ottavia]]'s death, [[maddalena-boselli|Maddalena]] grieves deeply, "missing the tactless [[ottavia|Aunt Ottavia]]" and longing intensely for her [129-007].
- **[[father-antonio|Father Antonio]]**: Her confessor, through whom [[gian-luca-boselli|Gian-Luca]] arranges his church wedding [091-001].
- **[[gian-luca-boselli|Gian-Luca Boselli]]**: Though not directly related by blood, she treats [[gian-luca-boselli|Gian-Luca]] as family. She chides [[maddalena-boselli|Maddalena]] for spoiling him: "you are making a perfect fool of that boy!" [101-004].
- **[[olga-boselli|Olga Boselli]]**: [[ottavia|Aunt Ottavia]] helps arrange [[gian-luca-boselli|Gian-Luca]] and [[olga-boselli|Olga]]'s wedding at St. Peter's [091-001].

## Significance

[[ottavia|Aunt Ottavia]] embodies the immigrant generation that established itself in London before the Bosellis arrived——the network of extended family that made the [[italian-diaspora|Italian community]] in Coldbath Square possible. Her merry, pragmatic character contrasts with [[maddalena-boselli|Maddalena]]'s intensity, and her hidden wealth reveals the quiet accumulation of resources that underpinned immigrant family life. Her death marks a generational transition, passing financial security to the younger Bosellis even as the older generation fades away.
