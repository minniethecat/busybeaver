## Summary

During World War I, Italian immigrant men in London are conscripted into the Italian Army. [[geppe|Geppe]] receives orders to serve in an infantry regiment [098-001] and must learn "how to dig trenches" [098-001] for "two years" [098-001]. [[gian-luca-boselli|Gian-Luca]] does "not feel called upon to enlist" [107-003] — a decision that sets him apart from his community. The mobilisation culminates when [[geppe|Geppe]] receives the order to "rejoin his regiment at once" [111-001], leading to his death in combat and marking a definitive rupture in the immigrant community's sense of security.

## Detailed Account

### Conscription and Community

The novel portrays WWI as an external force that reaches into London's Italian quarter, demanding the lives of its young men. [[geppe|Geppe]]'s conscription into "an infantry regiment" [098-001] is narrated with foreboding — "Poor [[geppe|Geppe]], the life would be" hard [098-001] — establishing the war as a threat to the community's most vital members. The training includes trench-digging [098-001], a grimly specific skill that underscores the industrialised nature of the coming violence.

### Gian-Luca's Non-Enlistment

[[gian-luca-boselli|Gian-Luca]]'s position is ambiguous: he "does not feel called upon to enlist" [107-003]. This non-participation distinguishes him from [[geppe|Geppe]] and raises questions about duty, belonging, and the immigrant's relationship to the mother country's wars. Italy calls its sons to fight, but [[gian-luca-boselli|Gian-Luca]] — already navigating questions of identity — does not answer.

### Geppe's Departure and Death

The mobilisation's narrative climax is [[geppe|Geppe]]'s receipt of orders to "rejoin his regiment at once" [111-001]. He sits "staring helplessly" at the order [111-001], a reaction that captures the powerlessness of the immigrant soldier — conscripted by a homeland he may never have seen, fighting a war decided by powers far from Soho. [[geppe|Geppe]]'s subsequent death in combat is one of the novel's most consequential events, removing a central figure from the community and deepening [[gian-luca-boselli|Gian-Luca]]'s crisis.
