## About This Index

Place is identity in *Adam's Breed*. The novel moves between urban London ([[old-compton-street|Old Compton Street]], the Doric restaurant) and rural Italy (the Campagna, the Boselli ancestral village), with the [[new-forest|New Forest]] serving as [[gian-luca-boselli|Gian-Luca]]'s final refuge. This index gathers all named locations, each linked to its full page.
