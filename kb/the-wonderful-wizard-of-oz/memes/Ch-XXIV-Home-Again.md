## XXIV

[024-001] [[home|Home]] Again

[024-002] [[aunt-em|Aunt Em]] had just come out of the house to water the cabbages when she looked up and saw [[dorothy-gale|Dorothy]] running toward her.

[024-003] “My darling child!” she cried, folding the little girl in her arms and covering her face with kisses. “Where in the world did you come from?”

[024-004] “From [[land-of-oz|the Land of Oz]],” said Dorothy gravely. “And here is [[toto|Toto]], too. And oh, Aunt Em! I’m so glad to be at home again!”
