# Trial of the Guardian of the Gates

## Overview

The travelers must persuade the Guardian of the Gates to allow them entry into the Emerald City; they succeed and are fitted with green-lensed spectacles locked onto their heads. Taking place in **[[emerald-city|Emerald City]]** (Chapter 010), this event marks a significant moment in Dorothy's journey through the Land of Oz.

## Notes

This event reflects key themes in Baum's narrative: the triumph of resourcefulness over brute force, the power of belief and perception, and the importance of companionship. In the context of American children's literature at the turn of the century, such episodes emphasize moral growth and self-discovery through adventure.
