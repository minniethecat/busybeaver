# Winkie Country

> **This page has been merged.** See [[winkie-country]] for the complete entry.

Winkie Country is the western quadrant of the Land of Oz, home to the Winkie people. It is characterized by its yellow landscape and was formerly ruled by the Wicked Witch of the West until her destruction (012-019–012-030). For full details, see [[winkie-country]].
