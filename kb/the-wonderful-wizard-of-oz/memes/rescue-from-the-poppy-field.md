# Rescue from the Deadly Poppy Field

## Overview

Dorothy, Toto, and the Cowardly Lion fall into a deathlike sleep amid the narcotic poppies; the Scarecrow and Tin Woodman carry Dorothy out while the Queen of the Field Mice and her subjects pull the Lion to safety. Taking place in **[[deadly-poppy-field|Deadly Poppy Field]]** (Chapter 008-009), this event marks a significant moment in Dorothy's journey through the Land of Oz.

The event occurs within the context of the journey through Oz.

## Notes

This event reflects key themes in Baum's narrative: the triumph of resourcefulness over brute force, the power of belief and perception, and the importance of companionship. In the context of American children's literature at the turn of the century, such episodes emphasize moral growth and self-discovery through adventure.
