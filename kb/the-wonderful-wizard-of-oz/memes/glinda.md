# Glinda the Good Witch

> **This page has been merged.** See [[glinda-the-good-witch]] for the complete entry.

Glinda is the Good Witch of the South, the ruler of the Quadling Country, and the wisest figure in the Land of Oz (018-007–018-010). She reveals the secret of the Silver Shoes and enables Dorothy's return to Kansas (023-025–023-030). For full details, see [[glinda-the-good-witch]].
