# The Silver Shoes Carry Dorothy Home

## Overview

Glinda reveals the power of the Silver Shoes — three clicks of the heels will carry the wearer anywhere — and Dorothy bids farewell to her companions and flies back to Kansas. Taking place in **[[quadling-country|Quadling Country]]** (Chapter 023), this event marks the resolution of Dorothy's journey through the Land of Oz.

## Notes

This event reflects key themes in Baum's narrative: the power that was within Dorothy all along, the completion of the hero's journey, and the bittersweet nature of farewell. Dorothy's three clicks of the heels mirror the three wishes of fairy tales, while her return to a grey Kansas suggests that adventure has transformed her perception of home.
