# About This Wiki

## Source Material

This wiki is built from **The Wonderful Wizard of Oz** (1900), the first book in the Oz series by American author **L. Frank Baum**. The text is in the public domain.

- **Book**: The Wonderful Wizard of Oz

- **Author**: L. Frank Baum

- **First Published**: 1900

- **Illustrator**: W. W. Denslow

- **Source**: Public domain text

## Usage Notes

This wiki serves as a structured reference for the characters, places, concepts, and events in The Wonderful Wizard of Oz. Browse by category or use the search to find specific entries.
