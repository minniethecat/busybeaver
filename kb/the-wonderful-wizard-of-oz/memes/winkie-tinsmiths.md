# Winkie Tinsmiths

## Overview

A group of winkie tinsmiths in the Land of Oz. The skilled Winkie craftsmen who originally built the Tin Woodman and, after the Witch's death, create the ornamental gifts — including a new oil-can — presented to Dorothy's companions.

## Notes

This organization reflects themes common in Baum's Oz universe: the tension between natural and artificial authority, the motif of enchanted servitude, and the eventual liberation of oppressed groups. The portrayal draws from fairy tale conventions where magical creatures and captive peoples serve as both obstacles and allies to the protagonist's journey.
