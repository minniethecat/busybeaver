## Overview

The **silver whistle** is a magical summoning device worn by the [[wicked-witch-of-the-west|Wicked Witch of the West]]. Hanging from a chain around her neck, it commands three distinct armies: wolves (one blast), wild crows (two blasts), and black bees (three blasts). Together with her [[golden-cap|Golden Cap]], the silver whistle constitutes the Witch's arsenal of supernatural command over the creatures of Oz, enabling her to project power across the Winkie Country from her castle.

## Function

The whistle operates on a simple escalating protocol—each additional blast summons a more dangerous creature than the last. The Witch uses it in response to the approach of [[dorothy-gale|Dorothy]] and her companions, whom she spots from her castle door with her single telescopic eye:

> She blew upon a silver whistle that hung around her neck. [012-012]

This first blast summons the pack of forty wolves, who are ordered to "go to the strangers and tear them to pieces" [012-015]. When the [[tin-woodman|Tin Woodman]] dispatches all forty, the Witch blows her whistle twice, summoning the wild crows [012-023]. When the [[scarecrow|Scarecrow]] destroys the crows, she blows three times, bringing the black bees [012-031].

## Narrative Role

The silver whistle structures the Witch's three-wave attack sequence, each wave building tension and showcasing a different companion's abilities:

| Blasts | Creature | Defeated By | Method |
|--------|----------|-------------|--------|
| 1 | [[wolves-of-the-wicked-witch|Wolves]] (×40) | [[tin-woodman|Tin Woodman]] | Axe |
| 2 | [[wild-crows|Wild Crows]] (×40) | [[scarecrow|Scarecrow]] | Neck-twisting |
| 3 | [[black-bees|Black Bees]] (swarm) | [[tin-woodman|Tin Woodman]] (body) + [[scarecrow|Scarecrow]] (strategy) | Broken stings |

After all three whistle-summoned armies are destroyed, the Witch turns to her final resource: the [[golden-cap|Golden Cap]], which commands the [[winged-monkey-band|Winged Monkeys]] [012-040].

## Relationship to Other Magical Items

The silver whistle is one of several magical objects in the Witch's possession, each with distinct summoning domains:

- **Silver whistle**: Controls natural creatures (wolves, crows, bees) native to Oz, with unlimited uses
- **[[golden-cap|Golden Cap]]**: Controls the enchanted Winged Monkeys, but limited to three uses total
- **Telescopic eye**: Not an item but a magical bodily ability—the Witch's single eye can "see everywhere" like a telescope [012-012]

The unlimited but weaker silver whistle is the Witch's first resort; the limited but powerful Golden Cap is her last.
