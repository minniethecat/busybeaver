# La Baudraye

**La Baudraye** is the estate and country house near [[sancerre|Sancerre]] that serves as the primary residence of [[dinah-de-la-baudraye]] and her husband [[monsieur-de-la-baudraye]] in *The [[provincial-muse|Muse of the Department]]*.

> **See full article: [[la-baudraye-estate]]**

The estate consists of "a town-house and country-house all in one" (005-013), situated in [[saint-satur|Saint-Satur]] near [[sancerre|Sancerre]]. It is the setting for much of the novel's provincial drama — [[dinah-de-la-baudraye|Dinah]]'s [[literary-circle|literary salon]], her husband's miserly calculations, and the social surveillance of the [[sancerre|Sancerre]] community.
