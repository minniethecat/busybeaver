# Nobility

The Restoration aristocracy as object of [[ambition|aspiration]] and instrument of social climbing. The [[la-baudraye|La Baudraye]] family's ascent from sheriff to Baron to Count to peer. The dedication to [[ferdinand-de-gramont|Ferdinand de Gramont]] as a treatise on heraldry. Standard

## Context

This concept appears throughout Balzac's two stories set in and around [[sancerre|Sancerre]] and [[paris|Paris]]. The corpus at `corpus/raw/0255-[[paris|Parisian]]s in the Country-5df198de/doc_final.md` contains numerous references that inform this analysis.

## In the Novel

The theme is woven through both narratives, manifesting differently in the commercial satire of *[[felix-gaudissart|Gaudissart the Great]]* and the social drama of *The [[provincial-muse|Muse of the Department]]*.
