# Mortgage Society

A financial institution (Societe hypothicaire) that lent money on real estate security, referenced by [[felix-gaudissart|Gaudissart]] as an established and large-scale operation.

## Context in Balzac's Fiction

[[felix-gaudissart|Gaudissart]] mentions it in his pitch to [[margaritis|Margaritis]]: "We now have the Mortgage Society which lends on real estate, and works that system on a large scale."

## In the Novels

He contrasts the Mortgage Society's tangible-asset lending with his own firm's more speculative "intellectual capital" insurance, using it as a concrete example of an established financial innovation against which to measure his novel product.
