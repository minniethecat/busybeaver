## Book III

### 255.

### 255.


*Imitators.*—A: "What? You don't want to have

imitators?" B: "I don't want people to do anything

*after* me; I want every one to do something

*before* himself (as a pattern to himself)—just as *I*

do." A: "Consequently—?"
