# Oliver Wendell Holmes

## Biography

Oliver Wendell Holmes (1809-1894) was an American poetry, active during the 19th Century period. Physician, poet, and essayist, author of "The Autocrat of the Breakfast-Table" and "Old Ironsides."

## Literary Career

Associated with the Fireside Poets movement, Oliver Wendell Holmes worked primarily in poetry, essay, fiction. His/her writings contributed to the development of a distinctly American literary voice during a formative period in the nation's cultural history.

## Critical Reception

Oliver Wendell Holmes has been recognized as one of the significant voices in American literary history. Contemporary reception reflected the literary standards of the 19th Century era, and modern scholarship continues to study Oliver Wendell Holmes's contributions to Fireside Poets and American letters more broadly.
