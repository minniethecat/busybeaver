# Greenfield Hill

## Overview

Greenfield Hill (1794) is a descriptive poem by [[timothy-dwight]]. Dwight's long poem celebrating the Connecticut landscape and society, blending pastoral description with moral and patriotic themes.

First published in 1794, Greenfield Hill appeared during a significant period in American literary development. The work reflects the literary standards and cultural concerns of its era.
