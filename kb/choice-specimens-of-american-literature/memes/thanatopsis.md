# Thanatopsis

## Overview

Thanatopsis (1817) is a meditative poem by [[william-cullen-bryant]]. Bryant's masterful meditation on death and nature, written when he was only 17, establishing him as America's first significant Romantic poet.

## Publication History

First published in 1817, Thanatopsis appeared during a significant period in American literary development. The work reflects the literary standards and cultural concerns of its era.
