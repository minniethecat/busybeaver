# The Autocrat of the Breakfast-Table

## Overview

The Autocrat of the Breakfast-Table (1858) is a essay collection by [[oliver-wendell-holmes]]. Holmes's celebrated collection of conversational essays blending wit, wisdom, and observation, originally published serially in The Atlantic Monthly.

## Publication History

First published in 1858, The Autocrat of the Breakfast-Table appeared during a significant period in American literary development. The work reflects the literary standards and cultural concerns of its era.
