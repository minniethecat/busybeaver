# The Raven

## Overview

The Raven (1845) is a narrative poem by [[edgar-allan-poe]]. Poe's most famous poem, a narrative of mysterious visitation and psychological descent, celebrated for its musicality, stylized language, and supernatural atmosphere.

## Publication History

First published in 1845, The Raven appeared during a significant period in American literary development. The work reflects the literary standards and cultural concerns of its era.
