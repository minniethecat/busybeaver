# Freedom of the Will

## Overview

Freedom of the Will (1754) is a philosophical treatise by [[jonathan-edwards]]. Edwards's rigorous philosophical defense of theological determinism, arguing that human freedom is compatible with divine sovereignty.

## Publication History

First published in 1754, Freedom of the Will appeared during a significant period in American literary development. The work reflects the literary standards and cultural concerns of its era.
