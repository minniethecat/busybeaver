# The Autobiography of Benjamin Franklin

## Overview

The Autobiography of Benjamin Franklin (1791) is a memoir by [[benjamin-franklin]]. Franklin's unfinished memoir, covering his rise from humble origins to international prominence, a classic of self-making and Enlightenment values in American literature.

## Publication History

First published in 1791, The Autobiography of Benjamin Franklin appeared during a significant period in American literary development. The work reflects the literary standards and cultural concerns of its era.
