# Evangeline: A Tale of Acadie

## Overview

Evangeline: A Tale of Acadie (1847) is a epic poem by [[henry-wadsworth-longfellow]]. Longfellow's epic poem of the Acadian deportation, following the heroine Evangeline's lifelong search for her lost love Gabriel.

## Publication History

First published in 1847, Evangeline: A Tale of Acadie appeared during a significant period in American literary development. The work reflects the literary standards and cultural concerns of its era.
