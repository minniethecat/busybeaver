# A Model of Christian Charity

## Overview

A Model of Christian Charity (1630) is a lay sermon by [[john-winthrop]]. Winthrop's sermon delivered aboard the Arbella, articulating the Puritan vision of a "city upon a hill" and the covenant theology that shaped early New England.

## Publication History

First published in 1630, A Model of Christian Charity appeared during a significant period in American literary development. The work reflects the literary standards and cultural concerns of its era.
