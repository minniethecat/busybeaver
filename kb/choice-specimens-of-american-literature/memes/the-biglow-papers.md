# The Biglow Papers

## Overview

The Biglow Papers (1848) is a satirical poetry by [[james-russell-lowell]]. Lowell's satirical poems employing Yankee dialect to critique the Mexican-American War and the expansion of slavery.

## Publication History

First published in 1848, The Biglow Papers appeared during a significant period in American literary development. The work reflects the literary standards and cultural concerns of its era.
