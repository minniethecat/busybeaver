# Timothy Dwight

## Biography

Timothy Dwight (1752-1817) was an American poetry, active during the Early Republic period. Congregationalist minister, theologian, and poet, president of Yale College, a leading figure among the Connecticut Wits.

## Literary Career

Associated with the Connecticut Wits movement, Timothy Dwight worked primarily in poetry, sermon, theology. His/her writings contributed to the development of a distinctly American literary voice during a formative period in the nation's cultural history.
