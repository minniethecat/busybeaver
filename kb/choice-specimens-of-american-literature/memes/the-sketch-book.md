# The Sketch Book of Geoffrey Crayon, Gent.

## Overview

The Sketch Book of Geoffrey Crayon, Gent. (1819-1820) is a short story collection by [[washington-irving]]. A collection of 34 essays and short stories, including the immortal tales "Rip Van Winkle" and "The Legend of Sleepy Hollow," which established Irving as the first American writer to achieve international fame.

## Publication History

First published in 1819-1820, The Sketch Book of Geoffrey Crayon, Gent. appeared during a significant period in American literary development. The work reflects the literary standards and cultural concerns of its era.
