# William Cullen Bryant

## Biography

William Cullen Bryant (1794-1878) was an American poetry, active during the Early Republic / 19th Century period. Poet and journalist, author of "Thanatopsis" and long-time editor of the New York Evening Post.

## Literary Career

Associated with the Romanticism movement, William Cullen Bryant worked primarily in poetry, journalism, essay. His/her writings contributed to the development of a distinctly American literary voice during a formative period in the nation's cultural history.
