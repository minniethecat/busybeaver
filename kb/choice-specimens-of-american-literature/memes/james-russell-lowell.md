# James Russell Lowell

## Biography

James Russell Lowell (1819-1891) was an American poetry, active during the 19th Century period. Poet, critic, and diplomat, a central figure among the Fireside Poets and first editor of The Atlantic Monthly.

## Literary Career

Associated with the Fireside Poets movement, James Russell Lowell worked primarily in poetry, essay, literary criticism. His/her writings contributed to the development of a distinctly American literary voice during a formative period in the nation's cultural history.

James Russell Lowell has been recognized as one of the significant voices in American literary history. Contemporary reception reflected the literary standards of the 19th Century era, and modern scholarship continues to study James Russell Lowell's contributions to Fireside Poets and American letters more broadly.
