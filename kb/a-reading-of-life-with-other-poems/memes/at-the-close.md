## AT THE CLOSE

[013-001] TO Thee, dear God of Mercy, both appeal,

[013-002] Who straightway sound the call to arms. Thou know'st;

[013-003] And that black spot in each embattled host,

[013-004] Spring of the blood-stream, later wilt reveal.

[013-005] Now is it red artillery and white steel;

[013-006] Till on a day will ring the victor's boast,

[013-007] That 'tis Thy chosen towers uppermost,

[013-008] Where Thy rejected grovels under heel.

[013-009] So in all times of man's descent insane

[013-010] To brute, did strength and craft combining strike,

[013-011] Even as a God of Armies, his fell blow.

[013-012] But at the close he entered Thy domain,

[013-013] Dear God of Mercy, and if lion-like

[013-014] He tore the fall'n, the Eternal was his Foe.
