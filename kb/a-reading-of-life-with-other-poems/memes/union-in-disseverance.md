## UNION IN DISSEVERANCE

[009-001] SUNSET worn to its last vermilion he;

[009-002] She that star overhead in slow descent:

[009-003] That white star with the front of angel she;

[009-004] He undone in his rays of glory spent

[009-005] Halo, fair as the bow-shot at his rise,

[009-006] He casts round her, and knows his hour of rest

[009-007] Incomplete, were the light for which he dies,

[009-008] Less like joy of the dove that wings to nest.

[009-009] Lustrous momently, near on earth she sinks;

[009-010] Life's full throb over breathless and abased:

[009-011] Yet stand they, though impalpable the links,

[009-012] One, more one than the bridally embraced.
