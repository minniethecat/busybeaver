## SONG IN THE SONGLESS

[008-001] THEY have no song, the sedges dry,

[008-002] And still they sing.

[008-003] It is within my breast they sing,

[008-004] As I pass by.

[008-005] Within my breast they touch a string,

[008-006] They wake a sigh.

[008-007] There is but sound of sedges dry;

[008-008] In me they sing.
