## THE HUELESS LOVE

[007-001] UNTO that love must we through fire attain,

[007-002] Which those two held as breath of common air;

[007-003] The hands of whom were given in bond elsewhere;

[007-004] Whom Honour was untroubled to restrain.

[007-005] Midway the road of our life's term they met,

[007-006] And one another knew without surprise;

[007-007] Nor cared that beauty stood in mutual eyes;

[007-008] Nor at their tardy meeting nursed regret.

[007-009] To them it was revealed how they had found

[007-010] The kindred nature and the needed mind;

[007-011] The mate by long conspiracy designed;

[007-012] The flower to plant in sanctuary ground.

[007-013] Avowed in vigilant solicitude

[007-014] For either, what most lived within each breast

[007-015] They let be seen: yet every human test

[007-016] Demanding righteousness approved them good.

[007-017] She leaned on a strong arm, and little feared

[007-018] Abandonment to help if heaved or sank

[007-019] Her heart at intervals while Love looked blank,

[007-020] Life rosier were she but less revered.

[007-021] An arm that never shook did not obscure

[007-022] Her woman's intuition of the bliss--

[007-023] Their tempter's moment o'er the black abyss,

[007-024] Across the narrow plank--he could abjure.

[007-025] Then came a day that clipped for him the thread,

[007-026] And their first touch of lips, as he lay cold,

[007-027] Was all of earthly in their love untold,

[007-028] Beyond all earthly known to them who wed.

[007-029] So has there come the gust at South-west flung

[007-030] By sudden volt on eves of freezing mist,

[007-031] When sister snowflake sister snowdrop kissed,

[007-032] And one passed out, and one the bell-head hung.
