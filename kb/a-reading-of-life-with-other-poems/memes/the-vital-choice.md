## THE VITAL CHOICE

[001-001] I

[001-002] OR shall we run with Artemis

[001-003] Or yield the breast to Aphrodite?

[001-004] Both are mighty;

[001-005] Both give bliss;

[001-006] Each can torture if divided;

[001-007] Each claims worship undivided,

[001-008] In her wake would have us wallow.

[001-009] II

[001-010] Youth must offer on bent knees

[001-011] Homage unto one or other;

[001-012] Earth, the mother,

[001-013] This decrees;

[001-014] And unto the pallid Scyther

[001-015] Either points us shun we either

[001-016] Shun or too devoutly follow.
