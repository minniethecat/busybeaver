## WITH THE HUNTRESS

[002-001] THROUGH the water-eye of night,

[002-002] Midway between eve and dawn,

[002-003] See the chase, the rout, the flight

[002-004] In deep forest; oread, faun,

[002-005] Goat-foot, antlers laid on neck;

[002-006] Ravenous all the line for speed.

[002-007] See yon wavy sparkle beck

[002-008] Sign of the Virgin Lady's lead.

[002-009] Down her course a serpent star

[002-010] Coils and shatters at her heels;

[002-011] Peals the horn exulting, peals

[002-012] Plaintive, is it near or far.

[002-013] Huntress, arrowy to pursue,

[002-014] In and out of woody glen,

[002-015] Under cliffs that tear the blue,

[002-016] Over torrent, over fen,

[002-017] She and forest, where she skims

[002-018] Feathery, darken and relume:

[002-019] Those are her white-lightning limbs

[002-020] Cleaving loads of leafy gloom.

[002-021] Mountains hear her and call back,

[002-022] Shrewd with night: a frosty wail

[002-023] Distant: her the emerald vale

[002-024] Folds, and wonders in her track.

[002-025] Now her retinue is lean,

[002-026] Many rearward; streams the chase

[002-027] Eager forth of covert; seen

[002-028] One hot tide the rapturous race.

[002-029] Quiver-charged and crescent-crowned,

[002-030] Up on a flash the lighted mound

[002-031] Leaps she, bow to shoulder, shaft

[002-032] Strung to barb with archer's craft,

[002-033] Legs like plaited lyre-chords, feet

[002-034] Songs to see, past pitch of sweet.

[002-035] Fearful swiftness they outrun,

[002-036] Shaggy wildness, grey or dun,

[002-037] Challenge, charge of tusks elude:

[002-038] Theirs the dance to tame the rude;

[002-039] Beast, and beast in manhood tame,

[002-040] Follow we their silver flame.

[002-041] Pride of flesh from bondage free,

[002-042] Reaping vigour of its waste,

[002-043] Marks her servitors, and she

[002-044] Sanctifies the unembraced.

[002-045] Nought of perilous she reeks;

[002-046] Valour clothes her open breast;

[002-047] Sweet beyond the thrill of sex;

[002-048] Hallowed by the sex confessed.

[002-049] Huntress arrowy to pursue,

[002-050] Colder she than sunless dew,

[002-051] She, that breath of upper air;

[002-052] Ay, but never lyrist sang,

[002-053] Draught of Bacchus never sprang

[002-054] Blood the bliss of Gods to share,

[002-055] High o'er sweep of eagle wings,

[002-056] Like the run with her, when rings

[002-057] Clear her rally, and her dart,

[002-058] In the forest's cavern heart,

[002-059] Tells of her victorious aim.

[002-060] Then is pause and chatter, cheer,

[002-061] Laughter at some satyr lame,

[002-062] Looks upon the fallen deer,

[002-063] Measuring his noble crest;

[002-064] Here a favourite in her train,

[002-065] Foremost mid her nymphs, caressed;

[002-066] All applauded. Shall she reign

[002-067] Worshipped? O to be with her there!

[002-068] She, that breath of nimble air,

[002-069] Lifts the breast to giant power.

[002-070] Maid and man, and man and maid,

[002-071] Who each other would devour

[002-072] Elsewhere, by the chase betrayed,

[002-073] There are comrades, led by her,

[002-074] Maid-preserver, man-maker.
