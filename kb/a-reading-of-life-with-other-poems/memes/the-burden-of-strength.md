## THE BURDEN OF STRENGTH

[010-001] IF that thou hast the gift of strength, then know

[010-002] Thy part is to uplift the trodden low;

[010-003] Else in a giant's grasp until the end

[010-004] A hopeless wrestler shall thy soul contend.
