[112-001] That we ought to lay open our necessities to [[jesus-christ|Christ]] and to require His

[112-002] [[grace|Grace]]

[112-003] The Voice of the Disciple

[112-004] O most sweet and loving Lord, whom now I devoutly desire to receive,

[112-005] Thou knowest my infirmity and the necessity which I suffer, in what

[112-006] evils and vices I lie; how often I am weighed down, tempted, disturbed,

[112-007] and defiled. I come unto Thee for remedy, I beseech of Thee [[comfort|consolation]]

[112-008] and support. I speak unto Thee who knowest all things, to whom all my

[112-009] secrets are open, and who alone art able perfectly to comfort and help

[112-010] me. Thou knowest what good thing I most stand in need of, and how poor

[112-011] I am in virtues.

[112-012] 2. Behold, I stand poor and naked before Thee, requiring grace, and

[112-013] imploring [[forgiveness|mercy]]. Refresh the hungry suppliant, kindle my coldness with

[112-014] the fire of Thy love, illuminate my blindness with the brightness of

[112-015] Thy presence. Turn thou all earthly things into bitterness for me, all

[112-016] grievous and contrary things into patience, all things worthless and

[112-017] created into contempt and oblivion. Lift up my heart unto Thee in

[112-018] [[heaven|Heaven]], and suffer me not to wander over the earth. Be Thou alone sweet

[112-019] unto me from this day forward for ever, because Thou alone art my meat

[112-020] and drink, my love and joy, my sweetness and my whole good.

[112-021] 3. Oh that Thou wouldest altogether by Thy presence, kindle, consume,

[112-022] and transform me into Thyself; that I may be made one spirit with Thee,

[112-023] by the grace of inward union, and the melting of earnest love! Suffer

[112-024] me not to go away from Thee hungry and dry; but deal mercifully with

[112-025] me, as oftentimes Thou hast dealt wondrously with Thy saints. What

[112-026] marvel if I should be wholly kindled from Thee, and in myself should

[112-027] utterly fail, since Thou art fire always burning and never failing,

[112-028] love purifying the heart and enlightening the understanding.
