[099-001] That it is profitable to Communicate often

[099-002] The Voice of the Disciple

[099-003] Behold I come unto Thee, O [[jesus-christ|Lord]], that I may be blessed through Thy

[099-004] gift, and be made joyful in Thy holy feast which Thou, O God, of Thy

[099-005] goodness hast prepared for the poor.(1) Behold in Thee is all that I

[099-006] can and ought to desire, Thou art my salvation and redemption, my hope

[099-007] and strength, my honour and glory. Therefore rejoice the soul of Thy

[099-008] servant this day, for unto Thee, O Lord Jesus, do I lift up my soul.(2)

[099-009] I long now to receive Thee devoutly and reverently, I desire to bring

[099-010] Thee into my house, so that with Zacchaeus I may be counted worthy to

[099-011] be blessed by Thee and numbered among the children of Abraham. My soul

[099-012] hath an earnest desire for Thy Body, my heart longeth to be united with

[099-013] Thee.

[099-014] 2. Give me Thyself and it sufficeth, for besides Thee no [[comfort|consolation]]

[099-015] availeth. Without Thee I cannot be, and without Thy visitation I have

[099-016] no power to live. And therefore I must needs draw nigh unto Thee often,

[099-017] and receive Thee for the healing of my soul, lest haply I faint by the

[099-018] way if I be deprived of heavenly food. For so Thou, most merciful

[099-019] Jesus, preaching to the people and healing many sick, didst once say, I

[099-020] will not send them away fasting to their own homes, lest they faint by

[099-021] the way.(3) Deal therefore now to me in like manner, for Thou left

[099-022] Thyself for the consolation of the faithful in the Sacrament. For Thou

[099-023] art the sweet refreshment of the soul, and he who shall eat Thee

[099-024] worthily shall be partaker and inheritor of the eternal glory.

[099-025] Necessary indeed it is for me, who so often slide backwards and sin, so

[099-026] quickly wax cold and faint, to renew, cleanse, enkindle myself by

[099-027] frequent prayers and penitences and receiving of Thy sacred Body and

[099-028] Blood lest haply by too long abstinence, I fall short of my holy

[099-029] resolutions.

[099-030] 3. For the imaginations of man’s heart are evil from his youth,(4) and

[099-031] except divine medicine succour him, man slideth away continually unto

[099-032] the worse. The [[communion|Holy Communion]] therefore draweth us back from evil, and

[099-033] strengtheneth us for good. For if I now be so negligent and lukewarm

[099-034] when I communicate [or celebrate], how should it be with me, if I

[099-035] receive not this medicine, and sought not so great a help? [And though

[099-036] I am not every day fit nor well prepared to celebrate, I will

[099-037] nevertheless give diligent heed at due season, to receive the divine

[099-038] mysteries, and to become partaker of so great grace]. For this is the

[099-039] one principal consolation of a faithful soul, so long as it is absent

[099-040] from Thee in mortal body, that being continually mindful of its God, it

[099-041] receiveth its Beloved with devout spirit.

[099-042] 4. Oh wonderful condescension of Thy pity surrounding us, that Thou, O

[099-043] Lord God, Creator and Quickener of all spirits, deignest to come unto a

[099-044] soul so poor and weak, and to appease its hunger with Thy whole Deity

[099-045] and Humanity. Oh happy mind and blessed soul, to which is granted

[099-046] devoutly to receive Thee its Lord God, and in so receiving Thee to be

[099-047] filled with all spiritual joy! Oh how great a Lord doth it entertain,

[099-048] how beloved a Guest doth it bring in, how delightful a Companion doth

[099-049] it receive, how faithful a Friend doth it welcome, how beautiful and

[099-050] exalted a Spouse, above every other Beloved, doth it embrace, One to be

[099-051] loved above all things that can be desired! Oh my most sweet Beloved,

[099-052] let heaven and earth and all the glory of them, be silent in Thy

[099-053] presence; seeing whatsoever praise and beauty they have it is of Thy

[099-054] gracious bounty; and they shall never reach unto the loveliness of Thy

[099-055] Name, Whose [[wisdom|Wisdom]] is infinite.(5)

[099-056] (1) Psalm lxviii. 10. (2) Psalm lxxxvi. 4. (3) Matthew xv. 32. (4)

[099-057] Genesis viii. 21. (5) Psalm cxlvii. 5.
