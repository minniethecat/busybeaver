[039-001] What the truth saith inwardly without noise of words

[039-002] Speak [[jesus-christ|Lord]], for thy servant heareth.(1) I am Thy servant; O give me

[039-003] understanding that I may know Thy testimonies. Incline my heart unto

[039-004] the words of Thy mouth.(2) Let thy speech distil as the dew. The

[039-005] [[israel|children of Israel]] spake in old time to [[moses|Moses]], Speak thou unto us and

[039-006] we will hear, but let not the Lord speak unto us lest we die.(3) Not

[039-007] thus, O Lord, not thus do I pray, but rather with [[samuel|Samuel]] the prophet, I

[039-008] beseech Thee humbly and earnestly, Speak, Lord, for Thy servant

[039-009] heareth. Let not Moses speak to me, nor any prophet, but rather speak

[039-010] Thou, O Lord, who didst inspire and illuminate all the prophets; for

[039-011] Thou alone without them canst perfectly fill me with knowledge, whilst

[039-012] they without Thee shall profit nothing.

[039-013] 2. They can indeed utter words, but they give not the spirit. They

[039-014] speak with exceeding beauty, but when Thou art silent they kindle not

[039-015] the heart. They give us scriptures, but Thou makest known the sense

[039-016] thereof. They bring us mysteries, but Thou revealest the things which

[039-017] are signified. They utter [[law|commandments]], but Thou helpest to the

[039-018] fulfilling of them. They show the way, but Thou givest strength for the

[039-019] journey. They act only outwardly, but Thou dost instruct and enlighten

[039-020] the heart. They water, but Thou givest the increase. They cry with

[039-021] words, but Thou givest understanding to the hearer.

[039-022] 3. Therefore let not Moses speak to me, but Thou, O Lord my God,

[039-023] [[truth|Eternal Truth]]; lest I die and bring forth no fruit, being outwardly

[039-024] admonished, but not enkindled within; lest the word heard but not

[039-025] followed, known but not loved, believed but not obeyed, rise up against

[039-026] me in the judgment. Speak, Lord, for Thy servant heareth; Thou hast the

[039-027] words of [[eternity|eternal life]].(4) Speak unto me for some [[comfort|consolation]] unto my

[039-028] soul, for the amendment of my whole life, and for the praise and glory

[039-029] and eternal honour of Thy Name.
