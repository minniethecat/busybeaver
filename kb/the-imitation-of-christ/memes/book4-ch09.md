[105-001] That we ought to offer ourselves and all that is ours to God, and to

[105-002] pray for all

[105-003] The Voice of the Disciple

[105-004] [[jesus-christ|Lord]], all that is in the heaven and in the earth is Thine.(1) I desire

[105-005] to offer myself up unto thee as a freewill offering, and to continue

[105-006] Thine for ever. Lord, in the uprightness of mine heart I willingly

[105-007] offer(2) myself to Thee to-day to be Thy servant for ever, in humble

[105-008] [[obedience|submission]] and for a sacrifice of perpetual praise. Receive me with

[105-009] this holy [[communion|Communion]] of Thy precious Body, which I celebrate before Thee

[105-010] this day in the presence of the Angels invisibly surrounding, that it

[105-011] may be for the salvation of me and of all Thy people.

[105-012] 2. Lord, I lay before Thee at this celebration all my [[sin|sins]] and offences

[105-013] which I have committed before Thee and Thy holy Angels, from the day

[105-014] whereon I was first able to sin even unto this hour; that Thou mayest

[105-015] consume and burn them every one with the fire of Thy [[love|charity]], and

[105-016] mayest do away all the stains of my sins, and cleanse my conscience

[105-017] from all offence, and restore me to Thy favour which by sinning I have

[105-018] lost, fully forgiving me all, and mercifully admitting me to the kiss

[105-019] of peace.

[105-020] 3. What can I do concerning my sins, save humbly to confess and lament

[105-021] them and unceasingly to beseech Thy propitiation? I beseech Thee, be

[105-022] propitious unto me and hear me, when I stand before Thee, O my God. All

[105-023] my sins displease me grievously: I will never more commit them; but I

[105-024] grieve for them and will grieve so long as I live, steadfastly

[105-025] purposing to repent me truly, and to make restitution as far as I can.

[105-026] Forgive, O God, forgive me my sins for Thy holy Name’s sake; save my

[105-027] soul, which Thou hast redeemed with Thy precious blood. Behold I commit

[105-028] myself to Thy [[forgiveness|mercy]], I resign myself to Thy hands. Deal with me

[105-029] according to Thy [[mercy|loving-kindness]], not according to my wickedness and

[105-030] iniquity.

[105-031] 4. I offer also unto Thee all my goodness, though it is exceedingly

[105-032] little and imperfect, that Thou mayest mend and sanctify it, that Thou

[105-033] mayest make it well pleasing and acceptable in Thy sight, and ever draw

[105-034] it on towards perfection; and furthermore bring me safely, slothful and

[105-035] useless poor creature that I am, to a happy and blessed end.

[105-036] 5. Moreover I offer unto Thee all pious desires of the devout,

[105-037] necessities of parents, friends, brothers, sisters, and all who are

[105-038] dear to me, and of those who have done good to me, or to others for Thy

[105-039] love; and those who have desired and besought my prayers for themselves

[105-040] and all belonging to them; that all may feel themselves assisted by Thy

[105-041] grace, enriched by [[comfort|consolation]], protected from dangers, freed from

[105-042] pains; and that being delivered from all evils they may joyfully give

[105-043] Thee exceeding thanks.

[105-044] 6. I offer also to Thee prayers and Sacramental intercessions for those

[105-045] specially who have injured me in aught, made me sad, or spoken evil

[105-046] concerning me, or have caused me any loss or displeasure; for all those

[105-047] also whom I have at any time made sad, disturbed, burdened, and

[105-048] scandalized, by words or deeds, knowingly or ignorantly; that to all of

[105-049] us alike, Thou mayest equally pardon our sins and mutual offences. Take

[105-050] away, O Lord, from our hearts all suspicion, indignation, anger, and

[105-051] contention, and whatsoever is able to injure charity and diminish

[105-052] brotherly love. Have mercy, have mercy, Lord, on those who entreat Thy

[105-053] mercy; give grace to the needy; and make us such that we may be worthy

[105-054] to enjoy Thy grace, and go forward to the life eternal. Amen.
