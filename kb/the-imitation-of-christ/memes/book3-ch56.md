[093-001] That we ought to deny ourselves, and to imitate [[jesus-christ|Christ]] by means of the

[093-002] Cross

[093-003] My Son, so far as thou art able to go out of thyself so far shalt thou

[093-004] be able to enter into Me. As to desire no outward thing worketh

[093-005] internal peace, so the forsaking of self inwardly joineth unto God. I

[093-006] will that thou learn perfect self-denial, living in My will without

[093-007] contradiction or complaint. Follow Me: I am the way, the truth, and the

[093-008] life.(1) Without the way thou canst not go, without the truth thou

[093-009] canst not know, without the life thou canst not live. I am the Way

[093-010] which thou oughtest to follow; the [[truth|Truth]] which thou oughtest to

[093-011] believe; the Life which thou oughtest to hope for. I am the Way

[093-012] unchangeable; the Truth infallible; the Life everlasting. I am the Way

[093-013] altogether straight, the Truth supreme, the true Life, the blessed

[093-014] Life, the uncreated Life. If thou remain in My way thou shalt know the

[093-015] Truth, and the truth shall make thee free,(2) and thou shalt lay hold

[093-016] on [[eternity|eternal life]].

[093-017] 2. “If thou wilt enter into life, keep the [[law|commandments]].(3) If thou

[093-018] wilt know the truth, believe in Me. If thou wilt be perfect, sell all

[093-019] that thou hast. If thou wilt be My [[discipleship|disciple]], deny thyself. If thou

[093-020] wouldst possess the blessed life, despise the life which now is. If

[093-021] thou wilt be exalted in heaven, humble thyself in [[world|the world]]. If thou

[093-022] wilt reign with Me, bear the cross with Me; for only the servants of

[093-023] the cross find the way of blessedness and of true light.”

[093-024] 3. O Lord Jesu, forasmuch as Thy life was straitened and despised by

[093-025] the world, grant unto me to imitate Thee in [[contempt-of-the-world|despising the world]], for

[093-026] the servant is not greater than his lord, nor the disciple above his

[093-027] master.(4) Let Thy servant be exercised in Thy life, because there is

[093-028] my salvation and true holiness. Whatsoever I read or hear besides it,

[093-029] it refresheth me not, nor giveth me delight.

[093-030] 4. “My son, because thou knowest these things and hast read them all,

[093-031] blessed shalt thou be if thou doest them. He who hath My commandments

[093-032] and keepeth them, he it is that loveth Me, and I will love him, and

[093-033] will manifest Myself to him,(5) and I will make him to sit down with Me

[093-034] in My Father’s Kingdom.”

[093-035] 5. O Lord Jesu, as Thou hast said and promised, even so let it be unto

[093-036] me, and grant me to prove worthy. I have received the cross at Thy

[093-037] hand; I have carried it, and will carry it even unto death, as Thou

[093-038] hast laid it upon me. Truly the life of a truly devoted servant is a

[093-039] cross, but it leadeth to paradise. I have begun; I may not return back

[093-040] nor leave it.

[093-041] 6. Come, my brothers, let us together go forward. Jesus shall be with

[093-042] us. For Jesus’ sake have we taken up this cross, for Jesus’ sake let us

[093-043] persevere in the cross. He will be our helper, who was our Captain and

[093-044] Forerunner. Behold our King entereth in before us, and He will fight

[093-045] for us. Let us follow bravely, let no man fear terrors; let us be

[093-046] prepared to die bravely in battle, and let us not so stain our

[093-047] honour,(6) as to fly from the cross.

[093-048] (1) John xiv. 6. (2) John viii. 32. (3) Matthew xix. 17, 21. (4)

[093-049] Matthew x. 24. (5) John xiv. 21. (6) 1 Mac. ix. 10.
