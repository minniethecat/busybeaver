# Index of Virtues

The cultivation of virtue lies at the heart of *The Imitation of Christ*.
Thomas a Kempis presents the Christian life as a progressive growth in virtues
such as humility, patience, charity, and obedience. This page gathers all
wiki entries tagged as virtues, offering a comprehensive map of the moral
and spiritual excellences the text commends.

## Scope

The virtue pages in this wiki span the theological virtues (faith, hope, charity),
the cardinal virtues (prudence, justice, fortitude, temperance), and the distinctly
Christian virtues emphasised by Kempis — above all humility, which he calls the
foundation of the spiritual life. Each page explores the virtue's definition,
scriptural basis, and its role in the imitation of Christ.
