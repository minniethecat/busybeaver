[110-001] Of the fervent desire of certain devout persons to receive the Body and

[110-002] Blood of [[jesus-christ|Christ]]

[110-003] The Voice of the Disciple

[110-004] O how great is the abundance of Thy sweetness, O Lord, which Thou hast

[110-005] laid up for them that fear Thee. When I call to mind some devout

[110-006] persons who draw nigh to Thy Sacrament, O Lord, with the deepest

[110-007] devotion and affection, then very often I am confounded in myself and

[110-008] blush for shame, that I approach Thine altar and table of Holy

[110-009] [[communion|Communion]] so carelessly and coldly, that I remain so dry and without

[110-010] affection, that I am not wholly kindled with love before Thee, my God,

[110-011] nor so vehemently drawn and affected as many devout persons have been,

[110-012] who out of the very earnest desire of the Communion, and tender

[110-013] affection of heart, could not refrain from weeping, but as it were with

[110-014] mouth of heart and body alike panted inwardly after Thee, O God, O

[110-015] Fountain of Life, having no power to appease or satiate their hunger,

[110-016] save by receiving Thy Body with all joyfulness and spiritual eagerness.

[110-017] 2. O truly ardent faith of those, becoming a very proof of Thy Sacred

[110-018] Presence! For they verily know their Lord in the breaking of bread,

[110-019] whose heart so ardently burneth within them(1) when Jesus walketh with

[110-020] them by the way. Ah me! far from me for the most part is such love and

[110-021] devotion as this, such vehement love and ardour. Be merciful unto me, O

[110-022] Jesus, good, sweet, and kind, and grant unto Thy poor suppliant to feel

[110-023] sometimes, in [[eucharist|Holy Communion]], though it be but a little, the cordial

[110-024] affection of Thy love, that my faith may grow stronger, my hope in Thy

[110-025] goodness increase, and my [[love|charity]], once kindled within me by the

[110-026] tasting of the heavenly manna, may never fail.

[110-027] 3. But Thy [[forgiveness|mercy]] is able even to grant me the grace which I long for,

[110-028] and to visit me most tenderly with the spirit of [[devotion|fervour]] when the day

[110-029] of Thy good pleasure shall come. For, although I burn not with desire

[110-030] so vehement as theirs who are specially devout towards Thee, yet,

[110-031] through Thy grace, I have a desire after that greatly inflamed desire,

[110-032] praying and desiring to be made partaker with all those who so

[110-033] fervently love Thee, and to be numbered among their holy company.

[110-034] (1) Luke xxiv. 32.
