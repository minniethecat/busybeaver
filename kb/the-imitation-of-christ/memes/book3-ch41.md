[078-001] Of contempt of all temporal honour

[078-002] “My Son, make it no matter of thine, if thou see others honoured and

[078-003] exalted, and thyself despised and humbled. Lift up thine heart to Me in

[078-004] heaven, and then the contempt of men upon earth will not make thee

[078-005] sad.”

[078-006] 2. O [[jesus-christ|Lord]], we are in blindness, and are quickly seduced by vanity. If I

[078-007] look rightly within myself, never was injury done unto me by any

[078-008] creature, and therefore I have nought whereof to complain before Thee.

[078-009] But because I have many times and grievously sinned against Thee, all

[078-010] [[creation|creatures]] do justly take arms against me. Therefore to me confusion and

[078-011] contempt are justly due, but to Thee praise and honour and glory. And

[078-012] except I dispose myself for this, namely, to be willing that every

[078-013] creature should despise and desert me, and that I should be esteemed

[078-014] altogether as nothing, I cannot be inwardly filled with peace and

[078-015] strength, nor spiritually enlightened, nor fully united to Thee.
