# Index of Spiritual Practices

*The Imitation of Christ* is not merely a work of theology but a practical
manual of the interior life. Kempis commends a range of spiritual practices —
prayer, meditation, self-examination, sacramental participation, and the
mortification of the passions — as means by which the soul draws nearer to
God. This page indexes all wiki entries tagged as spiritual practices.

## Scope

The practice pages cover both the interior disciplines of the soul (mental
prayer, recollection, detachment) and the exterior observances of the
Christian life (fasting, almsgiving, participation in the Eucharist). Each
page situates the practice within Kempis's overall vision of the soul's
ascent to union with God.
