[102-001] An inquiry concerning preparation for [[communion|Communion]]

[102-002] The Voice of the Disciple

[102-003] When I consider Thy dignity, O [[jesus-christ|Lord]], and mine own vileness, I tremble

[102-004] very exceedingly, and am confounded within myself. For if I approach

[102-005] not, I fly from life; and if I intrude myself unworthily, I run into

[102-006] Thy displeasure. What then shall I do, O my God, Thou helper and

[102-007] Counsellor in necessities.

[102-008] 2. Teach Thou me the right way; propound unto me some short exercise

[102-009] befitting [[eucharist|Holy Communion]]. For it is profitable to know how I ought to

[102-010] prepare my heart devoutly and reverently for Thee, to the intent that I

[102-011] may receive Thy Sacrament to my soul’s health [or it may be also for

[102-012] the celebrating this so great and divine mystery].
