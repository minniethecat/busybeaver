[046-001] That all things are to be referred to God, as the final end

[046-002] “My Son, I must be thy Supreme and final end, if thou desirest to be

[046-003] truly happy. Out of such purpose thy affection shall be purified, which

[046-004] too often is sinfully bent upon itself and upon [[creation|created things]]. For if

[046-005] thou seekest thyself in any matter, straightway thou wilt fail within

[046-006] thyself and grow barren. Therefore refer everything to Me first of all,

[046-007] for it is I who gave thee all. So look upon each blessing as flowing

[046-008] from the Supreme Good, and thus all things are to be attributed to Me

[046-009] as their source.

[046-010] 2. “From Me the humble and great, the poor and the rich, draw water as

[046-011] from a living fountain, and those who serve Me with a free and faithful

[046-012] spirit shall receive grace for grace. But he who will glory apart from

[046-013] Me, or will be delighted with any good which lieth in himself, shall

[046-014] not be established in true joy, nor shall be enlarged in heart, but

[046-015] shall be greatly hindered and thrown into [[suffering|tribulation]]. Therefore thou

[046-016] must not ascribe any good to thyself, nor look upon virtue as belonging

[046-017] to any man, but ascribe it all unto God, without whom man hath nothing.

[046-018] I gave all, I will receive all again, and with great strictness require

[046-019] I the giving of thanks.

[046-020] 3. “This is the [[truth|Truth]], and by it the vanity of boasting is put to

[046-021] flight. And if heavenly grace and true [[love|charity]] shall enter into thee,

[046-022] there shall be no envy, nor straitening of the heart, nor shall any

[046-023] self-love take possession of thee. For [[charity|divine charity]] conquereth all

[046-024] things, and enlargeth all the powers of the soul. If thou art truly

[046-025] wise, thou wilt rejoice in Me alone, thou wilt hope in Me alone; for

[046-026] there is none good but one, that is God,(1) Who is to be praised above

[046-027] all things, and in all things to receive blessing.”

[046-028] (1) Luke xviii. 19.
