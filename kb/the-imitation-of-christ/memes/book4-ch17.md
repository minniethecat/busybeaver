[113-001] Of fervent love and vehement desire of receiving [[jesus-christ|Christ]]

[113-002] The Voice of the Disciple

[113-003] With the deepest devotion and fervent love, with all affection and

[113-004] [[devotion|fervour]] of heart, I long to receive Thee, O Lord, even as many Saints

[113-005] and devout persons have desired Thee in communicating, who were

[113-006] altogether well pleasing to Thee by their sanctity of life, and dwelt

[113-007] in all ardent devotion. O my God, Eternal Love, my whole Good,

[113-008] Happiness without measure, I long to receive Thee with the most

[113-009] vehement desire and becoming reverence which any Saint ever had or

[113-010] could have.

[113-011] 2. And although I be unworthy to have all those feelings of devotion,

[113-012] yet do I offer Thee the whole affection of my heart, even as though I

[113-013] alone had all those most grateful inflamed desires. Yea, also,

[113-014] whatsoever things a pious mind is able to conceive and long for, all

[113-015] these with the deepest veneration and inward fervour do I offer and

[113-016] present unto Thee. I desire to reserve nothing unto myself, but freely

[113-017] and entirely to offer myself and all that I have unto Thee for a

[113-018] sacrifice. O Lord my God, my Creator and Redeemer! with such affection,

[113-019] reverence, praise, and honour, with such gratitude, worthiness, and

[113-020] love, with such faith, hope, and purity do I desire to receive Thee

[113-021] this day, as Thy most blessed Mother, the glorious [[mary|Virgin Mary]],

[113-022] received and desired Thee, when she humbly and devoutly answered the

[113-023] Angel who brought unto her the glad tidings of the mystery of the

[113-024] [[incarnation|Incarnation]]. Behold the handmaid of the Lord; be it unto me according

[113-025] to thy word.(1)

[113-026] 3. And as Thy blessed forerunner, the most excellent of Saints, John

[113-027] Baptist, being full of joy in Thy presence, leapt while yet in the womb

[113-028] of his mother, for joy in the [[holy-spirit|Holy Ghost]]; and afterwards discerning

[113-029] Jesus walking amongst men, humbled himself exceedingly, and said, with

[113-030] devout affection, The friend of the bridegroom, who standeth and

[113-031] heareth him, rejoiceth greatly because of the bridegroom’s voice;(2)

[113-032] even so I wish to be inflamed with great and holy desires, and to

[113-033] present myself unto Thee with my whole heart. Whence also, on behalf of

[113-034] myself and of all commended to me in prayer, I offer and present unto

[113-035] Thee the jubilation of all devout hearts, their ardent affections,

[113-036] their mental ecstasies, and supernatural illuminations and heavenly

[113-037] visions, with all the virtues and praises celebrated and to be

[113-038] celebrated by every creature in heaven and earth; to the end that by

[113-039] all Thou mayest worthily be praised and glorified for ever.

[113-040] 4. Receive my prayers, O Lord my God, and my desires of giving Thee

[113-041] infinite praise and unbounded benediction, which, according to the

[113-042] multitude of Thine unspeakable greatness, are most justly due unto

[113-043] Thee. These do I give Thee, and desire to give every day and every

[113-044] moment; and with beseechings and affectionate desires I call upon all

[113-045] celestial spirits and all Thy faithful people to join with me in

[113-046] rendering Thee thanks and praises.

[113-047] 5. Let all peoples, nations, and tongues praise Thee, and magnify Thy

[113-048] holy and sweet-sounding Name, with highest jubilations and ardent

[113-049] devotion. And let all who reverently and devoutly celebrate Thy most

[113-050] high Sacrament, and receive it with full assurance of faith, be

[113-051] accounted worthy to find grace and [[forgiveness|mercy]] with Thee, and intercede with

[113-052] all [[prayer|supplication]] for me a sinner; and when they shall have attained

[113-053] unto their wished-for devotion and joyous union with Thee, and shall

[113-054] depart full of comfort and wondrously refreshed from Thy holy, heavenly

[113-055] table, let them vouchsafe to be mindful of me, for I am poor and needy.

[113-056] (1) Luke i. 38. (2) John iii. 29.
