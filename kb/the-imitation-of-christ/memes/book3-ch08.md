[045-001] Of a low estimation of self in the sight of God

[045-002] I will speak unto my [[jesus-christ|Lord]] who am but dust and ashes. If I count myself

[045-003] more, behold Thou standest against me, and my iniquities bear true

[045-004] testimony, and I cannot gainsay it. But if I abase myself, and bring

[045-005] myself to nought, and shrink from all self-esteem, and grind myself to

[045-006] dust, which I am, Thy grace will be favourable unto me, and Thy light

[045-007] will be near unto my heart; and all self-esteem, how little soever it

[045-008] be, shall be swallowed up in the depths of my nothingness, and shall

[045-009] perish for ever. There Thou showest to me myself, what I am, what I

[045-010] was, and whither I have come: so foolish was I and ignorant.(1) If I am

[045-011] left to myself, behold I am nothing, I am all weakness; but if suddenly

[045-012] Thou look upon me, immediately I am made strong, and filled with new

[045-013] joy. And it is great marvel that I am so suddenly lifted up, and so

[045-014] graciously embraced by Thee, since I am always being carried to the

[045-015] deep by my own weight.

[045-016] 2. This is the doing of Thy love which freely goeth before me and

[045-017] succoureth me in so many necessities, which guardeth me also in great

[045-018] dangers and snatcheth me, as I may truly say, from innumerable evils.

[045-019] For verily, by loving myself amiss, I lost myself, and by seeking and

[045-020] sincerely loving Thee alone, I found both myself and Thee, and through

[045-021] love I have brought myself to yet deeper nothingness: because Thou, O

[045-022] most sweet Lord, dealest with me beyond all merit, and above all which

[045-023] I dare ask or think.

[045-024] 3. Blessed be Thou, O my God, because though I be unworthy of all Thy

[045-025] benefits, Thy bountiful and [[goodness|infinite goodness]] never ceaseth to do good

[045-026] even to ingrates and to those who are turned far from Thee. Turn Thou

[045-027] us unto Thyself, that we may be grateful, humble, and godly, for Thou

[045-028] art our salvation, our courage, and our strength.

[045-029] (1) Psalm lxxiii. 22.
