[104-001] Of the oblation of [[jesus-christ|Christ]] upon the cross, and of [[resignation|resignation of self]]

[104-002] The Voice of the Beloved

[104-003] As I of my own will offered myself unto God the Father on the Cross for

[104-004] thy [[sin|sins]] with outstretched hands and naked body, so that nothing

[104-005] remained in Me that did not become altogether a sacrifice for the

[104-006] Divine propitiation; so also oughtest thou every day to offer thyself

[104-007] willingly unto Me for a pure and holy oblation with all thy strength

[104-008] and affections, even to the utmost powers of thine heart. What more do

[104-009] I require of thee than thou study to resign thyself altogether unto Me?

[104-010] Whatsoever thou givest besides thyself, I nothing care for, for I ask

[104-011] not thy gift, but thee.

[104-012] 2. As it would not be sufficient for thee if thou hadst all things

[104-013] except Me, even so whatsoever thou shalt give Me, if thou give Me not

[104-014] thyself, it cannot please Me. Offer thyself to Me, and give thyself

[104-015] altogether for God, so shall thy offering be accepted. Behold I offered

[104-016] Myself altogether to the Father for thee, I give also My whole body and

[104-017] blood for food, that thou mightest remain altogether Mine and I thine.

[104-018] But if thou stand in thyself, and offer not thyself freely to My will,

[104-019] thy offering is not perfect, neither shall the union betwixt us be

[104-020] complete. Therefore ought the freewill offering of thyself into the

[104-021] hands of God to go before all thy works, if thou wilt attain liberty

[104-022] and grace. For this is the cause that so few are inwardly enlightened

[104-023] and made free, that they know not how to deny themselves entirely. My

[104-024] word standeth sure, Except a man forsake all, he cannot be My

[104-025] [[discipleship|disciple]].(1) Thou therefore, if thou wilt be My disciple, offer thyself

[104-026] to Me with all thy affections.

[104-027] (1) Luke xiv. 33.
