[032-001] Of loving [[jesus-christ|Jesus]] above all things

[032-002] Blessed is he who understandeth what it is to love Jesus, and to

[032-003] despise himself for Jesus’ sake. He must give up all that he loveth for

[032-004] his Beloved, for Jesus will be loved alone above all things. The love

[032-005] of [[creation|created things]] is deceiving and unstable, but the love of Jesus is

[032-006] faithful and lasting. He who cleaveth to created things will fall with

[032-007] their slipperiness; but he who embraceth Jesus will stand upright for

[032-008] ever. Love Him and hold Him for thy friend, for He will not forsake

[032-009] thee when all depart from thee, nor will he suffer thee to perish at

[032-010] the last. Thou must one day be separated from all, whether thou wilt or

[032-011] wilt not.

[032-012] 2. Cleave thou to Jesus in life and death, and commit thyself unto His

[032-013] [[faith|faithfulness]], who, when all men fail thee, is alone able to help thee.

[032-014] Thy Beloved is such, by nature, that He will suffer no rival, but alone

[032-015] will possess thy heart, and as a king will sit upon His own throne. If

[032-016] thou wouldst learn to put away from thee every created thing, Jesus

[032-017] would freely take up His abode with thee. Thou wilt find all [[trust-in-god|trust]]

[032-018] little better than lost which thou hast placed in men, and not in

[032-019] Jesus. [[trust|Trust]] not nor lean upon a reed shaken with the wind, because all

[032-020] flesh is grass, and the goodliness thereof falleth as the flower of the

[032-021] field.(1)

[032-022] 3. Thou wilt be quickly deceived if thou lookest only upon the outward

[032-023] appearance of men, for if thou seekest thy comfort and profit in

[032-024] others, thou shalt too often experience loss. If thou seekest Jesus in

[032-025] all things thou shalt verily find Jesus, but if thou seekest thyself

[032-026] thou shalt also find thyself, but to thine own hurt. For if a man

[032-027] seeketh not Jesus he is more hurtful to himself than all [[world|the world]] and

[032-028] all his adversaries.

[032-029] (1) [[isaiah|Isaiah]] xl. 6.
