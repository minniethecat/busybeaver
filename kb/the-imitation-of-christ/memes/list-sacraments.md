# Index of Sacraments

The sacraments occupy a central place in the spirituality of *The Imitation
of Christ*. Book IV is devoted almost entirely to the Eucharist, which Kempis
treats as the supreme means of union with Christ. This page indexes all wiki
entries tagged under the sacrament category, encompassing both the seven
sacraments of the Church and broader sacramental themes.

## Scope

The sacrament pages in this wiki give particular attention to the Eucharist,
which Kempis approaches with profound reverence and practical counsel on
worthy reception. Other sacraments — Baptism, Confession/Penance, Holy Orders —
are treated in their relation to the soul's journey toward God.
