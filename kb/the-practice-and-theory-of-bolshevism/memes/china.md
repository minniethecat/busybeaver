# China

**China** appeared in Russell's discussion of [[bolshevism|Bolshevism]] as part of the broader Asian context of Bolshevik anti-colonial strategy. The 1920 [[baku-congress|Baku Congress]] was designed to extend Bolshevik influence across Asia, and China — then in a state of political fragmentation following the 1911 Revolution — was among the intended targets.
