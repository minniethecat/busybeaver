# London

**London** served as an implicit point of contrast in Bertrand Russell's analysis of [[moscow|Moscow]] and Bolshevik Russia. Writing as a British observer for a Western audience, Russell was acutely aware of the cultural and political distances separating the revolutionary atmosphere of Moscow from the parliamentary normality of London.
