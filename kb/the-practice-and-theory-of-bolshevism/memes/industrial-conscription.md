## Overview

Industrial conscription, also termed the "militarization of labour," was a key Bolshevik economic policy during the Russian Civil War (1918–1921). It involved the compulsory mobilization of workers, the prohibition of strikes, and the application of military discipline to industrial production.

Russell observed this policy firsthand during his 1920 visit to Russia, describing it as central to the Bolsheviks' efforts to reorganize industry. The policy aimed to combat "labour desertion" — workers fleeing towns for the countryside in search of food — through punitive measures including fines, labour detention, and internment in concentration camps.

## Policy Framework

The Ninth Congress of the Russian Communist Party (1920) formally endorsed the militarization of labour in a resolution that Russell quotes directly:

> The ninth Congress approves of the decision of the Central Committee of the Russian Communist Party on the mobilization of the industrial proletariat, compulsory labour service, militarization of production and the application of military detachments to economic needs. [001-100]

The Congress further decreed that Party organizations should assist Trade Unions in registering all skilled workers for deployment "with the same consistency and strictness as was done ... in relation to the commanding staff for army needs."

## Russell's Assessment

Russell was sharply critical of the system, arguing that it had produced conditions worse than those of capitalism. He listed its features:

> A sweated wage, long hours, industrial conscription, prohibition of strikes, prison for slackers, diminution of the already insufficient rations in factories where the production falls below what the authorities expect, an army of spies ready to report any tendency to political disaffection and to procure imprisonment for its promoters — this is the reality of a system which still professes to govern in the name of the proletariat. [002-082]

He concluded that "Bolshevism may be defended, possibly, as a dire discipline through which a backward nation is to be rapidly industrialized; but as an experiment in Communism it has failed." [002-083]
