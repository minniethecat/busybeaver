# Red Terror

The **Red Terror** was the Bolshevik campaign of political repression, mass arrests, and executions carried out during the [[russian-civil-war|Russian Civil War]]. Russell regarded the Terror not as an aberration but as a logical consequence of Bolshevik doctrine — the necessary instrument, in Leninist theory, for suppressing counter-revolution.

## Russell's Analysis

Russell argued that the Terror was not a temporary defensive measure but an expression of the Bolshevik belief that class enemies could never be persuaded — only destroyed. The Cheka, the Bolshevik secret police, became the institutional embodiment of this logic.
