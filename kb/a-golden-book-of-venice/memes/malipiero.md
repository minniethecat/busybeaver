## Overview

Signor Mali[[piero|piero]] was a Venetian nobleman who, together with his secretary, accompanied [[fra-paolo-sarpi|Fra Paolo Sarpi]] on the evening of October 5, 1607. He was present with the celebrated Servite consultore only an hour before the assassination attempt that nearly claimed Fra Paolo's life.

## In the Narrative

Malipiero appears in a single brief but dramatic passage. After leaving Fra Paolo's company, news reached the Council Chamber that the beloved Servite father had been attacked. The nobles recalled that Malipiero and his secretary had been with Fra Paolo only an hour earlier—a detail that underscored the suddenness and violence of the assault and the shock it delivered to the Republic.
