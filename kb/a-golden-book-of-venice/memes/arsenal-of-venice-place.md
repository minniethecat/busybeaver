## Overview

**Arsenal of Venice (Place)** is a notable location in Renaissance Venice. The vast state-owned shipyard complex, the largest industrial facility in Renaissance Europe. It formed part of the intricate geography of the [[la-serenissima|Most Serene Republic]] — a city of islands, canals, and basins whose physical layout shaped every aspect of its life.
