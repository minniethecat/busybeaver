# Arrival of Gabriel in Saragossa

## Overview

The opening event of the novel: [[gabriel-araceli|Gabriel]] and three companions arrive in Saragossa after escaping French captivity (001-001).

## Context

This event is part of the Peninsular War (1807–1814) and specifically [[siege-warfare|the siege]] of Saragossa as depicted in Galdós's *Saragossa: A Story of Spanish Valor*.
