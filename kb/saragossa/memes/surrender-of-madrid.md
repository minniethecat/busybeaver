# Surrender of Madrid

## Overview

The surrender of Madrid to the French, recounted by [[gabriel-araceli|Gabriel]] to [[don-jose-montoria|Don José de Montoria]], establishing the contrast with Saragossa's refusal to surrender (003-004).

## Context

This event is part of the Peninsular War (1807–1814) and specifically [[siege-warfare|the siege]] of Saragossa as depicted in Galdós's *Saragossa: A Story of Spanish Valor*.
