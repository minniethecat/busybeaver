# Calle de Pabostre

## Overview

A street in [[Saragossa]] that became a critical front line during the house-to-house fighting, with cannons raking its length from both sides.

## Location

Calle de Pabostre is situated in Eastern [[Saragossa]].

## Role in the Siege

During [[siege-warfare|the siege]], Calle de Pabostre served as a defensive_position. First appearing at PN (018-003).
