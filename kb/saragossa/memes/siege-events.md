# Siege Events of Saragossa

The two sieges of Saragossa (1808–1809) produced a dense sequence of battles, assaults, sorties, and turning points.
This page lists every event entry tagged with the siege, spanning from the first investment in June 1808 to the capitulation in February 1809.
