# Seminary

## Overview

The Seminary of Saragossa, whose students took up arms during the first [[siege-warfare|siege]], providing some of the most fervent defenders of the city.

## Location

Seminary is situated in Central Saragossa. It is a key site in the geography of besiege-warfared Saragossa in Galdós's novel.

## Role in the Siege

As a landmark during [[siege-warfare|the siege]], Seminary appears in the narrative beginning at PN (005-007).
