[033-001] *Saragossa*

[033-002] was published in 1874 by

[033-003] [[[benito-perez-galdos|Benito Pérez Galdós]]](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Benito_P%C3%A9rez_Gald%C3%B3s).

[033-004] It was translated from Spanish in 1899 by

[033-005] **Minna Caroline Smith**.

[033-006] This ebook was produced for

[033-007] [Standard Ebooks](https://standardebooks.org/)

[033-008] by

[033-009] [Jon Azose](https://scholar.google.com/citations?user=91K_qfAAAAAJ),

[033-010] and is based on a transcription produced in 2014 by

[033-011] **Josep Cols Canals**, **Roberto Marabini**, and [Distributed Proofreaders](https://www.pgdp.net/)

[033-012] for

[033-013] [Project Gutenberg](https://www.gutenberg.org/ebooks/47769)

[033-014] and on digital scans from the

[033-015] [Internet Archive](https://archive.org/details/saragossa0000unse).

[033-017] *The Defense of Saragossa*,

[033-019] [David Wilkie](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/David_Wilkie_(artist)).
