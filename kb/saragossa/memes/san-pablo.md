# San Pablo

## Overview

The church and district of San Pablo in Saragossa, near the house of [[don-jose-montoria|Don José de Montoria]], mentioned as a navigational reference point.

## Location

San Pablo is situated in Central Saragossa. It is a key site in the geography of besiege-warfared Saragossa in Galdós's novel.

## Role in the Siege

As a landmark during [[siege-warfare|the siege]], San Pablo appears in the narrative beginning at PN (002-025).
