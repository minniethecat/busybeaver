# French Forces at Saragossa

The French forces besieging Saragossa were among Napoleon's most experienced troops — the armies of Moncey, Junot, Mortier, and finally Lannes and Suchet.
This page indexes every entry related to the French military presence in the two sieges.

## Notes

French forces under multiple marshals conducted two sieges: the first (June–August 1808) failed; the second (December 1808 – February 1809) succeeded.
