# Father Boggiero

## Overview

Father Boggiero is a priest who appears in a single but significant scene, delivering [[palafox|Palafox]]'s proclamation to the troops after the December 31 sortie. His role as intermediary between the commanding general and the common soldiers reflects the integration of clergy into Saragossa's [[resistance|defense]].

## Role in the Narrative

Father Boggiero delivers the proclamation that formally recognizes the defenders' [[courage|valor]]:

> Father Boggiero told us, among other things uttered by the mouth of our general, "Yesterday you marked the last day of the year with an action worthy of yourselves." (008-023)
