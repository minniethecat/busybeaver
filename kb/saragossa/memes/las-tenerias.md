# Las Tenerías

## Overview

A district of Saragossa where some of the most intense house-to-house combat occurred, with walls and buildings converted into improvised fortifications.

## Location

Las Tenerías is situated in Eastern Saragossa. It is a key site in the geography of besiege-warfared Saragossa in Galdós's novel.

## Role in the Siege

As a defensive_position during [[siege-warfare|the siege]], Las Tenerías appears in the narrative beginning at PN (018-003).
