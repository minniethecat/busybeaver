# Concepts of War and Siege

The siege of Saragossa gave rise to a dense thematic vocabulary — concepts of military conduct, the psychology of endurance, and the moral framework within which defenders and attackers alike understood their actions.
This page aggregates concept entries that bear on the nature of siege warfare and the values it foregrounds.
