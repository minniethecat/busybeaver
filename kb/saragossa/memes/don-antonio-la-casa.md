**[[Don Antonio]] la Casa** is a minor character mentioned in the narrative of [[the siege]] of [[Saragossa]]. Don Antonio La Casa was the incumbent of San Pablo parish in Saragossa, serving as a parish priest during the siege. (012-008)

## In the Narrative

Details about [[Don Antonio]] la Casa are sparse in the corpus. His title "Don" indicates he was a man of some social standing, and his connection to the events of [[the siege]] places him among the civilian or military participants.
