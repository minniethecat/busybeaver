# Huerva River

## Overview

The Huerva River, a tributary of the Ebro, which marked the boundary of the French encampment and the line of [[siege-warfare|the siege]].

## Location

Huerva River is situated in Saragossa.

## Role in the Siege

During [[siege-warfare|the siege]], Huerva River served as a landmark. First appearing at PN (003-003).

## In the Narrative

> Montoria said to us, “In the first siege I cut down my trees on my property on the other side of the Huerva; but this second siege that is being prepared for us is going to be much more terrible, to judge by the great number of troops that the French are sending.”
>
