The **Volunteers of Bourbon** were a regional volunteer battalion from the Bourbon region of Spain. They were among several regional volunteer units that converged on Saragossa to defend the city during the second siege.

## Muster at Saragossa

The volunteers of Bourbon are listed among the many units mustering for the defense: "The volunteers of Bourbon, Castile, Campo Segorbino, of Alicante, and of Soria" (006-001). This enumeration shows the wide geographic reach of Saragossa's defending forces, drawing volunteers from across Spain.

## Role

Like other regional volunteer battalions, the Bourbon volunteers formed part of the mixed force of regulars and militia assembled by General Palafox. These units from distant regions demonstrated the national character of the resistance.
