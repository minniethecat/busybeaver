The **Monastery of Altabás** was a small religious house near the Huerva bridge in Saragossa. It is briefly mentioned in the narrative as a landmark during the fighting around the bridge.

## Location

The monastery's position was notable for its proximity to the Huerva bridge: "over there and close to the bridge the little monastery of Altabás" (006-016). This placed it in the contested eastern perimeter of the city's defenses, near a critical crossing point.

## Siege Context

The monastery appears in a passage describing the obstacles French troops faced advancing toward the bridge: "these people, obstructed the bridge and obliged my battalion to look for an easier way to pass" (006-015), with the monastery mentioned as a nearby landmark (006-016). The area around the bridge and monastery was active with troop movements during the siege.
