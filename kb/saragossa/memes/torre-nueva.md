# Torre Nueva

## Overview

The leaning tower of Saragossa, whose clock struck ten as [[gabriel-araceli|Gabriel]] and his companions entered the city, serving as a prominent navigational landmark throughout the novel.

## Location

Torre Nueva is situated in Central Saragossa. It is one of the key locations in Galdós's *Saragossa*, appearing repeatedly as the narrative traces the geography of the [[siege-warfare|besieged city]].

## Role in the Siege

> As we entered by the Puerta de Sancho we heard the clock in the Torre Nueva strike ten. (001-001)

As a landmark, Torre Nueva played a significant role in the [[resistance|defense]] of Saragossa. Its first appearance at PN (001-001) establishes its presence in the novel's spatial imagination.
