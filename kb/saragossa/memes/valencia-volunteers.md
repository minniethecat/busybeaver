The **Valencia Volunteers** were a volunteer unit from Valencia that fought in the defense of Saragossa during the second siege. They appear in the narrative fighting alongside other regional volunteer formations.

## Combat Service

The Valencian volunteers are mentioned in action alongside other units: "the Huesca and the Valencia volunteers, the Walloon guards, and various members of the reserve" (010-014). This combat listing places them in the thick of the fighting alongside some of the most prominent defending units.

## Role in the Defense

As part of the multi-regional force assembled at Saragossa, the Valencia volunteers represented another region of Spain contributing to the city's defense. Their presence alongside the Huesca volunteers and professional troops like the Walloon Guards reflects the broad national character of the resistance.
