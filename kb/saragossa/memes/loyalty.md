**Loyalty** is a core value in the Saragossa narrative, expressed as fidelity to flag, comrades, city, and cause. It is one of the virtues that sustained the defense against overwhelming odds.

## Military Loyalty

The professional soldiers demonstrated loyalty to their oath despite desperate circumstances. Failure would mean "his loyalty sworn to his flag be trampled" (029-070), a fate worse than death for honorable soldiers.

## Civilian Loyalty

Loyalty extended beyond the military sphere. Civilians demonstrated loyalty to their city and community by continuing to resist, care for the wounded, and maintain social bonds under bombardment.
