# Castle Aljafería

## Overview

The medieval Islamic castle-palace of Saragossa, which served as a strategic fortification during [[siege-warfare|the siege]] and was identified by [[napoleon|Napoleon]] as a target for assault.

## Location

Castle Aljafería is situated in Western Saragossa.

## Role in the Siege

During [[siege-warfare|the siege]], Castle Aljafería served as a defensive_position. First appearing at PN (010-004).

## In the Narrative

> "If Don Mariano Cereso had not defended the Castle Aljafería as he did defend it, nothing would have been done in the Portillo quarter."
>
> (002-023)
