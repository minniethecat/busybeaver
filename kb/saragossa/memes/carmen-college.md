# Carmen College

## Overview

**Carmen College** was an educational institution in Saragossa located near the Puerta del Carmen — one of the city's principal gates. It stood next to the [[puerta-del-carmen|Puerta del Carmen]] with a neighboring orchard, in proximity to the Trinitarios district:

> Next to the Carmen college, with its neighboring orchard. (004-017)

## Location

The college was situated near the Puerta del Carmen, a gate that formed part of the southern defensive perimeter of Saragossa. The [[puerta-del-carmen|Puerta del Carmen]] saw heavy fighting during the siege, and the area around it — including the college and its orchard — was part of the defensive zone:

> Some by the Puerta del Carmen, some by the Portillo, all the people rushed to the defense. (008-020)

## Role in the Siege

The Puerta del Carmen gate was one of the key points in the defensive line that ran from the mouth of the [[huerva|Huerva River]]:

> From the mouth of Huerva to the Carmen gate they stationed fifty cannons. (012-001)

The college, with its orchard, lay within this fortified perimeter. Its proximity to the gate meant it was directly in the path of the French advance and likely served as a strongpoint or billet during the defense.

The gate itself was one of several critical defensive points — alongside the [[puerta-quemada|Puerta Quemada]], the [[portillo|Portillo]], and the [[santa-engracia|Santa Engracia]] gate — where the defenders concentrated their artillery and held against repeated assaults.
