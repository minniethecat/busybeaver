# Assault on Monte Torrero

## Overview

The opening battle of the second [[siege-warfare|siege]], in which the French attacked Monte Torrero and the Arrabal simultaneously.

## Context

This event is part of the Peninsular War (1807–1814) and specifically [[siege-warfare|the siege]] of Saragossa as depicted in Galdós's *Saragossa: A Story of Spanish Valor*.

## Narrative Account

> "The French, impatient to defeat us, began operations early on the twenty-first, attacking simultaneously and with great vigor Monte Torrero, and the Arrabal, the suburb on the left of the Ebro, points without whose possession it was impossible to dream of conquering the valiant city."
>
> (004-014)
