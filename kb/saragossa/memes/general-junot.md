**General Jean-Andoche Junot** (1771–1813) was a French general who served in Napoleon's Spanish campaign. Junot took command during the siege operations after Marshal Moncey's departure. (009-001)

## Peninsular War Service

Junot was one of Napoleon's senior commanders in the Peninsular War, initially leading the invasion of Portugal in 1807. His presence in the narrative contextualizes the wider French military operation of which the siege of Saragossa was a part.
