**Señora Maria Consolación Azlor** was a Spanish noblewoman mentioned as a peer of the [[countess-de-bureta|Countess of Bureta]] during the siege of Saragossa (002-027). She was part of the city's aristocratic circle that contributed to the defense effort.

## Association

Maria Consolación Azlor was a contemporary of the Countess of Bureta, both women of social standing who lived through the siege. Her presence in the narrative situates the broader social context of the defense.
