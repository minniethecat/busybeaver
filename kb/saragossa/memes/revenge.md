**Revenge** functions as a motivating force in the Saragossa narrative. The desire for vengeance against the French invader sustained resistance even when military logic dictated surrender.

## In Combat

Individual characters express determination to exact revenge. One figure "swore that he would be revenged" (027-008), reflecting the personal dimension of what was also a national conflict. Revenge gave emotional fuel to tactical decisions.

## Collective Dimension

The desire for revenge operated at both personal and collective levels. The city's resistance was in part an act of collective vengeance for the destruction wrought by the first siege and the ongoing bombardment.
