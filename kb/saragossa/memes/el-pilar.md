# Basilica of El Pilar

## Overview

The principal church of Saragossa, dedicated to the Virgin del Pilar, patroness of Spain, which served as a focal point for collective prayer and morale during [[siege-warfare|the siege]].

## Location

Basilica of El Pilar is situated in Plaza del Pilar, Saragossa. It is one of the key locations in Galdós's *Saragossa*, appearing repeatedly as the narrative traces the geography of the [[siege-warfare|besieged city]].

## Role in the Siege

> A great crowd was rushing into Del Pilar. The prayers, the entreaties, and the demonstrations of rejoicing did not seem like the prayers of any class of the faithful. (007-003)

As a landmark, Basilica of El Pilar played a significant role in the [[resistance|defense]] of Saragossa. Its first appearance at PN (007-003) establishes its presence in the novel's spatial imagination.
