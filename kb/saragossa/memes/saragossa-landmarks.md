# Landmarks of Saragossa

Saragossa in 1808 was a city of churches and convents, winding medieval streets, and ancient walls — all of which became the stage for one of the most desperate urban defenses of the Napoleonic Wars.
This page indexes the churches, convents, and landmarks that hosted both worship and warfare.
