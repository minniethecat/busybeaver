**El Marcelo** was a location in Saragossa that served as a defensive position during the siege. It is described as an adjacent crossfire position to [[san-lazarus|San Lazarus]].

The place is mentioned in the siege narrative as one of the defensive positions held by the Spanish defenders (006-017).
