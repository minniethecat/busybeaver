# Churches and Convents of Saragossa

Saragossa's dense network of religious houses played a critical role during the sieges: convents became strongpoints, monasteries served as hospitals, and churches sheltered civilians.
This page collects place entries for religious buildings — many of which appear in the siege narrative as key defensive positions.
