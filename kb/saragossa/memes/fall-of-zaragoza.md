# Fall of Zaragoza

## Overview

The capitulation of Saragossa after two months of [[siege-warfare|siege]], epidemic, and [[hunger|starvation]], marking the end of organized resistance (030-018).

## Context

This event is part of the Peninsular War (1807–1814) and specifically [[siege-warfare|the siege]] of Saragossa as depicted in Galdós's *Saragossa: A Story of Spanish Valor*. When the French demanded surrender, the Spanish famously responded that they did not know how to surrender (004-010).
