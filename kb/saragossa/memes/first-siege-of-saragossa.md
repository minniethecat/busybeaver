# First Siege of Saragossa

## Overview

The first French [[siege-warfare|siege]] of Saragossa, during which the city's defenders repelled the initial assault, establishing the city's legendary reputation for resistance. The events of the first siege created an immense moral force that inspired the defenders throughout the second siege. (003-013) The constancy of the Spanish defenders astonished even the French attackers. (004-009)

## Context

This event is part of the Peninsular War (1807–1814) and specifically [[siege-warfare|the siege]] of Saragossa as depicted in Galdós's *Saragossa: A Story of Spanish Valor*.
