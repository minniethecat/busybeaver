# Villacampa

## Overview

General Villacampa was one of the Spanish commanders who participated in the [[resistance|defense]] of Saragossa. The volunteers of Huesca, of whom Don Pedro Villacampa was leader, were among the units that fought at key positions throughout the siege. (006-002)

## Role in the Narrative

Villacampa is referenced among the military leadership organizing the city's defense. During the fighting, seeing the French advancing, Villacampa, commander of the Huesca men, took decisive action. (020-004) Don Pedro Villacampa, commander of the Huesca volunteers, led his men through an underground door to engage the enemy. (020-007) In a moment of solitary heroism, Villacampa alone pressed forward against the attackers. (020-009)
