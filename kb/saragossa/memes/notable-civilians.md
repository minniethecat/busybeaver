# Notable Civilians of Saragossa

Beyond the soldiers and commanders, the siege narrative of Saragossa is populated by civilians — merchants, clergy, noble residents, and ordinary citizens whose lives were transformed by the two French investments.
This page indexes the non-combatant figures whose stories appear in the narrative.
