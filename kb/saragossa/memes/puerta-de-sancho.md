# Puerta de Sancho

## Overview

The gate through which [[gabriel-araceli|Gabriel]] and his companions first entered Saragossa, situated on the northern side of the city near the Ebro.

## Location

Puerta de Sancho is situated in Northern Saragossa.

## Role in the Siege

During [[siege-warfare|the siege]], Puerta de Sancho served as a defensive_position. First appearing at PN (001-001).
