# The Treasure Hunters

## Overview

The treasure hunters are an informal coalition of [[shadyside-school|Shadyside]] and [[salsette-academy|Salsette]] students brought together by the mystery of the [[the-treasure-of-indian-chasm|lost treasure]]. United by curiosity, courage, and [[friendship|friends]]hip, they pursue the secret that [[norma-guerin|Norma Guerin]] carries — that the treasure lies "at the bottom of [[indian-chasm|Indian Chasm]]." (013-003)

## Members

- [[Norma Guerin]] — knows the secret
- [[Betty Gordon]] — courageous adventurer
- [[Bob Henderson]] — leads the final expedition
- [[Bobby Littell]] — enthusiastic supporter

## Role

The treasure hunters represent the novel's core adventure dynamic — a group of [[friendship|friends]] working together to solve a mystery. Their collaboration embodies the novel's values of loyalty, courage, and the power of collective effort to overcome obstacles.
