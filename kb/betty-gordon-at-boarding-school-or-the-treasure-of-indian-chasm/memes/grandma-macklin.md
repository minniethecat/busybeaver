# Grandma Macklin

## Overview

Grandma Macklin is a resident of [[Edentown]] known for her lavish hospitality and generosity. Her story includes both prosperity and financial reversal.

## Role in the Story

Grandma Macklin is first mentioned when [[bob-henderson|Bob]] asks [[betty-gordon|Betty]], "You never met Grandma Macklin, did you, [[betty-gordon|Betty]]?" (012-030). She is described as famous for the way she ran things with lavish hospitality (012-046).

However, her fortunes changed. After one of her daughters' mother had finished school, Grandma Macklin "came to the end of her bank account" (012-047), indicating a financial reversal.

At the conclusion of the story, it is mentioned that one of the girls could now graduate from [[shadyside-school|Shadyside]], and Grandma Macklin could spend the rest of the winter in comfort (025-047), suggesting her circumstances had improved.
