# Shadyside Student Body

## Overview

The student body of [[shadyside-school|Shadyside School]] is the novel's primary community — a diverse group of girls from [[vermont-countryside|Vermont]], [[washington-dc|Washington]], and beyond, brought together by the shared experience of boarding [[boarding-school-life|school life]]. From [[betty-gordon|Betty Gordon]]'s arrival through the treasure discovery, the student community provides [[friendship|friends]]hip, rivalry, and the social context for every adventure.

## Members

- [[Betty Gordon]] — new student, central figure
- [[Bobby Littell]] — spirited leader among the girls
- [[Libbie Littell]] — sensitive younger student
- [[Norma Guerin]] — holds the treasure secret
- [[Alice Guerin]] — [[norma-guerin|Norma]]'s sister
- [[Frances Martin]] — quiet [[vermont-countryside|Vermont]] girl

## Role

The [[shadyside-school|Shadyside]] student body is more than a backdrop — it is the crucible in which [[betty-gordon|Betty]]'s character is tested and her [[friendship|friends]]hips forged. The interconnected dormitory suites, the shared meals, and the collective adventures create bonds that define the novel's emotional landscape. (011-001)
