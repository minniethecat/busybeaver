# The Gordon Household

## Overview

The Gordon household, headed by Richard "[[uncle-dick|Uncle Dick]]" Gordon, is the familial anchor for both [[betty-gordon|Betty Gordon]] and [[bob-henderson|Bob Henderson]]. [[uncle-dick|Uncle Dick]], an oil company representative, provides a stable, loving home for his two wards. From this foundation, [[betty-gordon|Betty]] and [[bob-henderson|Bob]] venture out to boarding school, secure in the knowledge that they have a home to return to. (002-013)

## Members

- [[Uncle Dick]] — guardian and patriarch
- [[Betty Gordon]] — ward
- [[Bob Henderson]] — ward, taken in at [[bob-henderson|Bob]]'s and his aunts' request (002-018)

## Role

The Gordon household represents the ideal of chosen family. Neither [[betty-gordon|Betty]] nor [[bob-henderson|Bob]] was born into this home, but both have found belonging there. [[uncle-dick|Uncle Dick]]'s willingness to send [[betty-gordon|Betty]] to [[shadyside-school|Shadyside]] — "[[uncle-dick|Uncle Dick]] says so" (001-019) — shows a guardian who balances protection with trust. The household is the emotional center from which the characters' adventures radiate.
