# Salsette Cadet Corps

## Overview

The Salsette Cadet Corps is the student body of [[salsette-academy|Salsette Academy]], the military school across the lake from [[shadyside-school|Shadyside]]. The cadets, recognizable by their "blue and silver uniforms" (010-004), are a constant presence in [[shadyside-student-body|the Shadyside girls]]' social world. [[tommy-tucker|Tommy Tucker]]'s proud declaration — "I'm [[salsette-academy|Salsette]] born!" (009-001) — captures the esprit de corps.

## Members

- [[Bob Henderson]] — cadet, [[betty-gordon|Betty]]'s closest friend
- [[Tommy Tucker]] — the musical, humorous cadet
- [[Teddy Tucker]] — [[tommy-tucker|Tommy]]'s more earnest twin

## Role

The Cadet Corps provides the novel's male energy and the romantic-social tension that enriches the boarding school setting. The proximity of [[salsette-academy|Salsette]] to [[shadyside-school|Shadyside]] — "across the lake" (010-017) — creates natural opportunities for [[friendship|friends]]hips and shared adventures.
