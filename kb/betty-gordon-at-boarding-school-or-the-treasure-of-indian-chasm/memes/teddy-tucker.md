# Teddy Tucker

## Overview

Teddy Tucker is [[tommy-tucker|Tommy Tucker]]'s twin brother, a cadet at [[salsette-academy|Salsette Academy]]. While [[tommy-tucker|Tommy]] is known for his humor and musical outbursts, Teddy has a more direct and earnest personality. Together, the Tucker twins add liveliness to the [[shadyside-school|Shadyside]]-[[salsette-academy|Salsette]] social scene. (007-015)

## Role in the Story

### The Other Twin

Teddy is introduced alongside his brother [[tommy-tucker|Tommy]] among the guests at [[fairfields|Fairfields]]. (007-015) He shares his brother's good nature but has a distinct personality — more straightforward and less prone to [[tommy-tucker|Tommy]]'s theatrical flourishes. When the boys are roughhousing, Teddy begins to protest about "boys pawing them" before [[betty-gordon|Betty]] intervenes. (009-015)

### A Blunt Admirer

Teddy's honest nature is on display when he blurts out to [[betty-gordon|Betty]]'s chaperone: "Do you know? You're my idea of a chaperone!" (016-012) His unfiltered compliments, while sometimes awkward, reflect a genuine and warm-hearted character who says exactly what he thinks.
