# Mrs. Guerin

## Overview

Mrs. Guerin is the mother of [[norma-guerin|Norma]] and [[alice-guerin|Alice Guerin]], and the wife of [[doctor-guerin|Doctor Guerin]]. She lives with her family and is enthusiastic about plans for the future.

## Role in the Story

Mrs. Guerin is first mentioned in connection with her family background. Her people had lived around the area when it was only a day school (002-048). She had expected to have quite a fortune (002-052).

At the end of the story, Mrs. Guerin appears as part of the happy resolution. It was noted that dear Doctor and Mrs. Guerin could doctor and nurse in Florida (025-047). Both Doctor and Mrs. Guerin were enthusiastic in their praise of the plans being made for the future (025-049).
