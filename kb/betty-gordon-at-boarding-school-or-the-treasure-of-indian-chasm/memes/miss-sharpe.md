# Miss Sharpe

## Overview

Miss Sharpe is a teacher at [[Shadyside School]] mentioned in connection with student discipline and faculty communications.

## Role in the Story

Miss Sharpe is first mentioned when [[betty-gordon|Betty]] tells someone that if Miss Sharpe asks for her, they should claim not to know where she is (019-035), suggesting she is a teacher whose class or supervision [[betty-gordon|Betty]] was avoiding.

[[bobby-littell|Bobby Littell]] overheard [[miss-anderson|Miss Anderson]] telling Miss Sharpe something about [[bobby-littell|Bobby]] when [[miss-anderson|Miss Anderson]] did not think [[bobby-littell|Bobby]] could hear (021-059).

Later, [[betty-gordon|Betty]] reported hearing [[miss-anderson|Miss Anderson]] telling Miss Sharpe about [[ada-nansen|Ada Nansen]]'s scratched face after an incident (023-047), indicating Miss Sharpe is part of the faculty communication network.

## Relationships

- [[betty-gordon|Betty Gordon]] — Student who seeks to avoid her notice (019-035)
