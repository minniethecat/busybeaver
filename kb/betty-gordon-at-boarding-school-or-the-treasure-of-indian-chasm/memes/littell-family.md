# The Littell Family

## Overview

The Littell family — [[Mrs. Littell]] and her daughters [[Bobby Littell]] and [[Libbie Littell]] — are the warm, beating heart of the novel's social world.

Living in [[washington-dc|Washington]] and maintaining a country estate at [[Fairfields]], the Littells open their doors to [[Betty Gordon]] and set the tone of generosity, hospitality, and good humor that characterizes the story. Mr. and [[mrs-littell|Mrs. Littell]] together "knew to perfection the secret of making young people have a good time." (007-045)

### Mrs. Littell: The Matriarch

[[Mrs. Littell]] is the family's anchor — a woman of warmth, competence, and genuine kindness. She invites [[betty-gordon|Betty]] to [[washington-dc|Washington]] (001-061), hosts the [[fairfields|Fairfields]] house party with flair, and nurses [[Libbie Littell|Libbie]] through her fainting spell with steady hands and a calm voice. Her letters and gift packages reach the girls at [[shadyside-school|Shadyside]] throughout the school year. (022-019)

### Bobby Littell: The Spirited Leader

[[Bobby Littell]] is the elder daughter, a natural leader among [[shadyside-student-body|the Shadyside girls]]. She organizes the secret society (013-017), shares a room with [[betty-gordon|Betty]], and provides the spark of mischief and initiative that drives many of the story's adventures. Her practical, outgoing nature balances [[libbie-littell|Libbie]]'s dreaminess.

### Libbie Littell: The Sensitive Soul

[[Libbie Littell]] is the younger daughter, more delicate and emotional than her sister. Her fainting spell during the [[fairfields|Fairfields]] party (008-032–044) is one of the novel's dramatic high points, and her fear of [[Indian Chasm]] (017-047) contrasts with the bolder characters. Her romantic sensibility adds emotional depth to the story.

## Role in the Story

The Littell family serves three essential roles:

1. **Social Hub** — [[washington-dc|Washington]] and [[fairfields|Fairfields]] host [[shadyside-school|Shadyside]] and [[Salsette Academy]] gatherings.
2. **Moral Center** — Their warmth models kindness, inclusion, and generosity.
3. **Plot Catalyst** — [[mrs-littell|Mrs. Littell]]'s invitation launches the story; [[bobby-littell|Bobby]]'s society advances the mystery.
