# Bobby Littell

## Overview

Bobby Littell is one of [[betty-gordon|Betty Gordon]]'s closest [[friendship|friends]] at [[shadyside-school|Shadyside School]]. Impulsive, warm-hearted, and full of energy, Bobby is a natural leader among the girls. She is the daughter of [[mrs-littell|Mrs. Littell]] and the sister of [[libbie-littell|Libbie Littell]]. (001-019)

## Role in the Story

### A Spirited Friend

Bobby is introduced through a letter she writes to [[betty-gordon|Betty]], announcing that she too is going to boarding school. (001-019) [[bob-henderson|Bob Henderson]] notes wryly that "with you and Bobby Littell about, I doubt if the school will ever settle down to quiet studies." (001-041) Bobby's high spirits make her a central figure in the social life of [[shadyside-school|Shadyside]].

### At Fairfields

Bobby organizes [[fairfields-house-party|the house party]] at [[fairfields|Fairfields]], writing to invite the other girls. (006-038) She greets [[betty-gordon|Betty]] at [[shadyside-station|the station]] with a "gay voice" and characteristic warm embrace. (007-004) Her greeting to [[bob-henderson|Bob Henderson]] — "[[bob-henderson|Bob]], you've grown a foot!" — shows her familiar, teasing manner. (007-006)

### Loyal Companion

Bobby is fiercely loyal to her [[friendship|friends]]. When [[betty-gordon|Betty]] faces challenges at [[shadyside-school|Shadyside]], Bobby stands by her. She dislikes formal attire and "wouldn't be caught traveling in a party dress." (005-032) Throughout the story, Bobby's energy and enthusiasm drive many of the girls' adventures.

## Relationships

- [[Betty Gordon]] — close friend and schoolmate at [[shadyside-school|Shadyside]]
- [[Libbie Littell]] — younger sister, also at [[shadyside-school|Shadyside]]
- [[Bob Henderson]] — friendly with [[betty-gordon|Betty]]'s closest friend
- [[mrs-littell|Mrs. Littell]] — mother, who hosts [[betty-gordon|Betty]] in [[washington-dc|Washington]] before school begins (001-061)
