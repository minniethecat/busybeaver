# Miss Jessup

## Overview

Miss Jessup is a teacher at [[Shadyside School]] who teaches current events. Her class discussions about associations and organizations play a role in the students' thinking.

## Role in the Story

Miss Jessup appears primarily in connection with her current events class. Students made their way to recitation with Miss Jessup, whose current events class was an important part of the curriculum (018-053).

During one class session, Miss Jessup was speaking to her students about associations and organizations. One student struggled with an answer (018-054). Miss Jessup called on [[ada-nansen|Ada Nansen]] when she raised her hand, asking "Key, [[ada-nansen|Ada]]?" (018-057). When [[ada-nansen|Ada]] could not answer, Miss Jessup then prompted another student with "Bee?" (018-062).

The discussion in Miss Jessup's class had a significant impact. [[bob-henderson|Bob Henderson]] reported excitedly that Miss Jessup had been talking about associations that morning, which had sparked an important idea (019-019).
