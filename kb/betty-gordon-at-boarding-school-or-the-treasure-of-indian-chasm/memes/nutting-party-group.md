# The Nutting Party Group

## Overview

The nutting party group is the collection of students who venture into the woods for the autumn tradition of gathering nuts. This informal expedition includes students from both [[shadyside-school|Shadyside]] and [[salsette-academy|Salsette]], and it is during this outing that [[bobby-littell|Bobby Littell]] spots [[coasting-hill|the hill]] and exclaims, "What a dandy hill for coasting!" (016-036)

## Members

- [[Betty Gordon]] — participant
- [[Bob Henderson]] — participant
- [[Bobby Littell]] — spotted the coasting hill
- [[Tommy Tucker]] — comic presence

## Role

The nutting party group exemplifies the wholesome outdoor culture of the schools. Their expedition connects the characters to the natural landscape and plants seeds for later adventures, including [[betty-gordon|Betty]]'s coasting in Chapter 24.
