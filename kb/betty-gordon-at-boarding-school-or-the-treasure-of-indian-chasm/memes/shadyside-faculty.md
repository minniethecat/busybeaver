# Shadyside Faculty

## Overview

The faculty of [[shadyside-school|Shadyside School]] are the teachers and administrators who provide the educational framework for the students' lives. While individual faculty members are not prominently featured, their presence is felt in the orderly routines of [[boarding-school-life|school life]] — classes, rules, and the structure that contains the students' adventures.

## Role

The faculty represent the adult world of authority and education. They maintain the boundaries within which the students' [[friendship|friends]]hips and adventures unfold. Their behind-the-scenes presence ensures that [[shadyside-school|Shadyside]] remains a place of learning even as it serves as the setting for mystery and adventure.
