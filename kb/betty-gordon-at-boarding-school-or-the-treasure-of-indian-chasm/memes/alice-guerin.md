# Alice Guerin

## Overview

Alice Guerin is [[norma-guerin|Norma Guerin]]'s sister and a fellow new student at [[shadyside-school|Shadyside School]]. [[betty-gordon|Betty]] learns with delight that "[[norma-guerin|Norma]] and Alice Guerin are going to [[shadyside-school|Shadyside]]!" (002-040) Alice and her sister become part of [[betty-gordon|Betty]]'s close circle of [[friendship|friends]] at the school.

## Role in the Story

### Arrival at Shadyside

Alice is first mentioned when [[betty-gordon|Betty]] reads [[norma-guerin|Norma]]'s letter and discovers both [[guerin-family|Guerin sisters]] are enrolled. (002-040) [[bobby-littell|Bobby Littell]] writes to [[betty-gordon|Betty]] that "[[norma-guerin|Norma]] and Alice Guerin" will be among the guests, adding that [[betty-gordon|Betty]] will like them. (007-027)

### Rooming Together

At [[shadyside-school|Shadyside]], Alice and [[norma-guerin|Norma]] share a room, a detail that emphasizes their closeness. (010-050) Their room adjoins the one assigned to [[betty-gordon|Betty]] and five other girls, making them part of the interconnected dormitory community. (011-001)

### A Quiet Presence

While [[norma-guerin|Norma]] takes a more prominent role in the treasure mystery, Alice is a steady, quieter presence in the social fabric of [[shadyside-school|Shadyside]]. As [[norma-guerin|Norma]]'s sister, she shares in the family history that connects [[guerin-family|the Guerins]] to the [[the-treasure-of-indian-chasm|lost treasure]] of [[indian-chasm|Indian Chasm]].
