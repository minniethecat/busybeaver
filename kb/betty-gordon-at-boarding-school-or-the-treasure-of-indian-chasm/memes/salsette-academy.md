# Salsette Academy

## Overview

Salsette Academy is the boys' military school located across the lake from [[shadyside-school|Shadyside School]]. [[bob-henderson|Bob Henderson]], [[tommy-tucker|Tommy Tucker]], and [[teddy-tucker|Teddy Tucker]] are among its cadets, who wear distinctive blue and silver uniforms. (010-004) The academy provides the male counterpart to [[shadyside-school|Shadyside]]'s female student body, and social events regularly bring [[salsette-shadyside-alliance|the two schools]] together.

## Setting Description

Salsette is visible across the lake from [[shadyside-school|Shadyside]]. When [[betty-gordon|Betty]] first arrives at the [[shadyside-station|train station]], she sees "Salsette cadets in their blue and silver uniforms" everywhere, and her first thought is "of how nice [[bob-henderson|Bob Henderson]] would look in uniform." (010-004) The academy has its own traditions and culture, and its students are a constant presence in [[shadyside-student-body|the Shadyside girls]]' social lives.

## Significance

Salsette Academy serves as the source of the boys' characters and enables the co-ed social dynamics that enrich the story. The proximity of [[salsette-shadyside-alliance|the two schools]] creates natural opportunities for [[friendship|friends]]hips, rivalries, and shared adventures. [[tommy-tucker|Tommy Tucker]]'s proud declaration — "I'm Salsette born!" (009-001) — captures the school spirit that defines the academy's culture.
