# Tucker Twins

## Overview

The **Tucker twins** — [[tommy-tucker|Tommy]] and [[teddy-tucker|Teddy]] — are an inseparable pair of brothers attending [[salsette-academy|Salsette Academy]]. They are among "the five boys [[richard-littell|Mr. Littell]] wrote you about" who became [[friendship|friends]] of [[bob-henderson|Bob Henderson]] and [[shadyside-student-body|the Shadyside girls]]. (006-039) Known for their school songs, irrepressible pranks, and what their classmates called "the Tucker twins' nonsense," they brought a contagious energy to every gathering. (008-061) [[tommy-tucker|Tommy]] was the "[[tommy-tucker|dark Tucker twin]]" — the more solemn and pessimistic of the pair — while [[teddy-tucker|Teddy]] was the "light Tucker twin," quick to nod in agreement with his brother's pronouncements. (008-051) (008-052)

## Role in the Story

### Friends of the Five Boys

The Tucker twins were part of a group of boys [[richard-littell|Mr. Littell]] identified as [[friendship|friends]] of [[bob-henderson|Bob Henderson]]'s prospective military academy. Along with [[timothy-derby|Timothy Derby]], [[sydney-cooke|Sydney Cooke]], and [[winifred-marion-brown|Winifred Marion Brown]], the twins formed the core of the [[salsette-academy|Salsette]]-[[shadyside-school|Shadyside]] social circle. (006-039) (007-015)

### High Spirits and Mischief

The twins were famous at [[salsette-academy|Salsette]] for their antics. They were once "caught sliding down the banisters" during a nighttime escapade at [[fairfields|Fairfields]], thoroughly abashed when discovered. (007-034) Their reputation for pranks preceded them: [[betty-gordon|Betty Gordon]] felt "slightly apprehensive" when she learned the Tucker twins were involved in an event, knowing they were "famous for their 'adjusting.'" (009-027)

### School Spirit and Music

The piano at [[salsette-academy|Salsette Academy]] belonged to the Tucker twins, and it became a gathering point where [[norma-guerin|Norma]] and [[alice-guerin|Alice Guerin]] were "rushed" by the boys during social events. (016-007) The twins were also known for their school songs: on the back platform of a departing train, "the Tucker twins raised their voices in a school song" that echoed across [[shadyside-station|the station]]. (008-061)

### The Dark Twin and the Light Twin

Despite their inseparability, the twins had distinct personalities. [[tommy-tucker|Tommy]], the "[[tommy-tucker|dark Tucker twin]]," was given to solemn pronouncements: "From four to ten — just six hours," he lamented of a party's brevity. (008-051) [[teddy-tucker|Teddy]], the "light Tucker twin," "nodded in confirmation" — always backing his brother's declarations without adding a word of his own. (008-052) This dynamic — [[tommy-tucker|Tommy]] the spokesman, [[teddy-tucker|Teddy]] the silent partner — defined their interactions throughout the story.

### The Coasting Adventure

The twins joined [[bob-henderson|Bob Henderson]] and [[timothy-derby|Timothy Derby]] on the coasting expedition in Chapter 24, where "[[bob-henderson|Bob Henderson]], the Tucker twins and [[timothy-derby|Timothy Derby]] appeared" for the winter sport. (024-012)
