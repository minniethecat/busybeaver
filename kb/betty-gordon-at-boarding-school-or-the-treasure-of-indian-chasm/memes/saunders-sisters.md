# The Saunders Sisters

## Overview

Miss Faith and [[miss-charity-saunders|Miss Charity Saunders]] are [[bob-henderson|Bob Henderson]]'s maternal aunts — his mother's sisters who, together with [[uncle-dick|Uncle Dick]] Gordon, provide [[bob-henderson|Bob]] with a home after his harsh years at the county poor farm and [[bramble-farm|Bramble Farm]]. [[bob-s-family|Bob's family]] farm was sold, and with [[uncle-dick|Uncle Dick]]'s help, the aunts became part of [[bob-henderson|Bob]]'s new life. (002-018)

## Role

The Saunders sisters represent [[bob-henderson|Bob]]'s connection to his mother's family and his escape from the poverty of his early years. Along with [[uncle-dick|Uncle Dick]], they form the adult support system that allows [[bob-henderson|Bob]] to attend [[salsette-academy|Salsette Academy]] and build a better future.
