# Timothy Derby

## Overview

**Timothy Derby** is a student at [[salsette-academy|Salsette Academy]] and one of the five boys recommended to [[bob-henderson|Bob Henderson]] by [[richard-littell|Mr. Littell]]. Known for his love of poetry and his quiet, earnest nature, [[tommy-tucker|Timothy]] was described by [[bobby-littell|Bobby Littell]] as "Timothy Derby who reads poetry." (007-015) He is a gentle, thoughtful presence among the more boisterous [[salsette-academy|Salsette]] boys, often appearing alongside the [[tucker-twins|Tucker twins]] and [[sydney-cooke|Sydney Cooke]] in the [[shadyside-school|Shadyside]]-[[salsette-academy|Salsette]] social circle.

## Role in the Story

### One of the Five Boys

[[tommy-tucker|Timothy]] was among the group of boys that [[richard-littell|Mr. Littell]] identified as [[friendship|friends]] of the military academy to which [[bob-henderson|Bob Henderson]] was applying. [[betty-gordon|Betty Gordon]] mentioned him by name: "Timothy Derby is one of the boys. He's a sort of cousin of [[littell-family|the Littells]]." (003-021) Along with the [[tucker-twins|Tucker twins]], [[sydney-cooke|Sydney Cooke]], and [[winifred-marion-brown|Winifred Marion Brown]], [[tommy-tucker|Timothy]] formed the core of the boys' contingent. (006-039)

### A Lover of Poetry

[[tommy-tucker|Timothy]]'s defining trait was his fondness for verse. At the [[nutting-party|Nutting Party]], [[libbie-littell|Libbie Littell]] "brought a book of poems for Timothy Derby, who solemnly loaned her one of his own treasured volumes in return." (016-008) This exchange of poetry books, solemnly conducted, captured [[tommy-tucker|Timothy]]'s earnest and slightly old-fashioned character.

### The Adjuster's Target

On the journey to school, [[tommy-tucker|Timothy]] became the subject of good-natured teasing when he found himself paired with [[betty-gordon|Betty Gordon]] for the train ride. "She and Timothy Derby, ignoring the jeers of their companions, settled themselves with their books," demonstrating [[tommy-tucker|Timothy]]'s ability to rise above the raillery of his peers. (009-010) He endured the [[tucker-twins|Tucker twins]]' adjustment schemes with quiet dignity.

### The Rescue Party

[[tommy-tucker|Timothy]] was among the boys who joined the search during critical moments. In Chapter 22, "after them came [[gilbert-lane|Gilbert Lane]] and Timothy Derby and [[winifred-marion-brown|Winifred Marion Brown]]," forming part of the rescue party that burst through the doors. (022-041) He also participated in the coasting expedition, appearing alongside [[bob-henderson|Bob Henderson]] and the Tucker twins. (024-012)
