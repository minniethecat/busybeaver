# Libbie's Secret

## Overview

Chapter 18, "LIBBIE'S SECRET," centers on a mystery closer to home than the [[treasure-discovery|treasure of Indian Chasm]]. [[libbie-littell|Libbie Littell]], the sensitive younger sister of [[bobby-littell|Bobby]], harbors a secret that creates a subplot of intrigue within [[shadyside-dormitory|the dormitory]]. The event explores [[libbie-littell|Libbie]]'s character beyond her tendency toward anxiety.

## Narrative

[[libbie-littell|Libbie]]'s secret consumes her attention and worries her [[friendship|friends]]. [[betty-gordon|Betty]], true to her protective nature, tries to help. The revelation of the secret — whatever it may be — deepens the reader's understanding of [[libbie-littell|Libbie]] and strengthens the bonds among the girls.

## Significance

This subplot provides a counterpoint to the treasure mystery — a personal, emotional secret rather than a physical one. It shows that even within the warm community of [[shadyside-school|Shadyside]], each girl has her own inner world and concerns.
