# Gilbert Lane

## Overview

**Gilbert Lane** is a student at [[salsette-academy|Salsette Academy]], described as "the freckled-faced coxswain" — the boy who steered the rowing shell for the [[salsette-academy|Salsette]] crew. (017-022) A loyal friend with a quick wit and an even quicker recovery from physical mishaps, Gilbert appears prominently in the later chapters of the story, particularly during [[caught-in-the-storm|the storm]] episode and its aftermath.

## Role in the Story

### The Coxswain

Gilbert served as coxswain for the [[salsette-academy|Salsette]] rowing crew, a position requiring both authority and tactical judgment. During the boating expedition in Chapter 17, he was in charge of one of the shells that carried [[shadyside-student-body|the Shadyside girls]] across the lake. (017-022) His freckled face and steady demeanor made him a recognizable figure among the [[salsette-academy|Salsette]] boys.

### Steadfast in the Storm

When [[caught-in-the-storm|the storm]] broke and the party faced danger, Gilbert demonstrated his loyalty. "Sydney and I intend to stay," Gilbert assured the others quickly, refusing to abandon his post despite the worsening weather. (017-061) This steadfastness marked him as a dependable presence in moments of crisis.

### The First to Recover

After [[caught-in-the-storm|the storm]] had passed, Gilbert was "the first to recover his spirits." (018-001) His resilience set the tone for the group's recovery from the frightening experience, and his ability to bounce back — both physically and emotionally — became a defining characteristic.

### A Witty Remark

Late in the story, Gilbert showed his sense of humor. When a scheme was uncovered, he quipped, "I'd put one over on you," demonstrating the easy, teasing rapport he shared with his [[friendship|friends]]. (023-013)

### The Rescue Party

Gilbert was part of the group that rushed to help during the climax of the mystery. "After them came Gilbert Lane and [[timothy-derby|Timothy Derby]] and [[winifred-marion-brown|Winifred Marion Brown]]," joining [[bob-henderson|Bob Henderson]] and the [[tucker-twins|Tucker twins]] who had burst through first. (022-041)
