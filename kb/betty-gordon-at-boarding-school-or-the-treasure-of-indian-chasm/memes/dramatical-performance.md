# The Dramatics

## Overview

Chapter 21, "DRAMATICS," features a student theatrical performance that brings the school community together. The event showcases the artistic side of boarding [[boarding-school-life|school life]], with students revealing talents that surprise their classmates.

## Narrative

The students prepare and perform a play or dramatic piece for the school. [[tommy-tucker|Tommy Tucker]]'s theatrical inclinations — previously seen in his impromptu musical outbursts — find a proper outlet. The event is both entertaining and bonding, strengthening the school community.

## Significance

The dramatics represent the cultural dimension of boarding school education. They show that [[shadyside-school|Shadyside]] and [[salsette-academy|Salsette]] nurture not just academic achievement but artistic expression, preparing students for a well-rounded life.
