# Madame

## Overview

Madame is the French teacher at [[Shadyside School]]. She is a minor adult character who appears in several scenes throughout the story, overseeing French lessons and school theatrical events.

## Role in the Story

Madame is mentioned as the French teacher whose classes students must attend. [[ada-nansen|Ada Nansen]] was reported to have "flunked twice in French and was impudent to Madame" (014-029), indicating Madame's authority in the classroom.

During rehearsals for the school play, Madame observed [[bobby-littell|Bobby Littell]]'s acting attempts and sighed that [[bobby-littell|Bobby]] was "impossible" (021-051), though [[bobby-littell|Bobby]] took the criticism in stride and volunteered to be a stage hand instead.

On the night of the performance, Madame was in the wings and cried out hysterically when [[bobby-littell|Bobby]] accidentally walked across the stage carrying a prop tree while following [[betty-gordon|Betty]] (023-041).

Madame also appears during a regular school day, politely redirecting [[betty-gordon|Betty]] and [[libbie-littell|Libbie]]'s attention back to their French translation work when they were distracted by plans for coasting (024-004).

## Relationships
