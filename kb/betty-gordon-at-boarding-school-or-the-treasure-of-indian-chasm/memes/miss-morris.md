# Miss Morris

## Overview

Miss Morris is the school nurse at [[Shadyside School]]. She appears in Chapter 18, tending to a sick student and interacting with [[betty-gordon|Betty Gordon]].

## Role in the Story

Miss Morris is introduced when she enters a room with [[miss-lacey|Miss Lacey]], moving quietly so as not to wake [[bobby-littell|Bobby Littell]] (018-018). When [[betty-gordon|Betty]] looked concerned about Elizabeth's condition, Miss Morris hastily reassured her that Elizabeth was not worse (018-019).

Later, Miss Morris came in to send [[betty-gordon|Betty]] back to her own room after a period of watching over the patient (018-046).

## Relationships

- [[betty-gordon|Betty Gordon]] — Student she reassures and supervises (018-019, 018-046)
- [[bobby-littell|Bobby Littell]] — Student she is careful not to wake (018-018)
- [[miss-lacey|Miss Lacey]] — Colleague who accompanies her (018-018)
