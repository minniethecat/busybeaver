# Coasting Hill

## Overview

The coasting hill near [[shadyside-school|Shadyside School]] is a favorite winter recreation spot for the students. [[bobby-littell|Bobby Littell]] exclaims "What a dandy hill for coasting!" when she first sees it. (016-036) The hill becomes the setting for a key adventure in Chapter 24, titled "BETTY GOES COASTING," where [[betty-gordon|Betty]]'s courage and spirit are on full display.

## Setting Description

The hill is located in the wooded area near the school, with a slope suitable for sledding and coasting. In winter, when snow covers the ground, the hill transforms into a playground for the students. Its location in the woods adds an element of wildness to the recreation — the thrill of speed combined with the beauty of the winter landscape.

## Significance

The coasting hill represents the joy and freedom of boarding [[boarding-school-life|school life]] — the moments between studies when students can embrace the outdoors and their own adventurous spirits. [[betty-gordon|Betty]]'s [[betty-goes-coasting|coasting adventure]] in Chapter 24 is a celebration of her character: brave, spirited, and undaunted by challenges.
