# The Vermont Contingent

## Overview

The [[vermont-countryside|Vermont]] contingent at [[shadyside-school|Shadyside School]] consists of students who hail from the Green Mountain State, including [[libbie-littell|Libbie Littell]] and [[frances-martin|Frances Martin]]. Their shared regional background — rural, wholesome, hardworking — creates a natural bond among them.
