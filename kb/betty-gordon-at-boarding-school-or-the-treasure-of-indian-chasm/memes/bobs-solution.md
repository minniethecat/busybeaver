# Bob's Solution

## Overview

Chapter 19, "BOB'S SOLUTION," features [[bob-henderson|Bob Henderson]] at his best — clever, resourceful, and determined. When faced with a difficult problem, [[bob-henderson|Bob]] devises an ingenious solution that demonstrates the intelligence and resilience he has developed through his challenging background.

## Narrative

[[bob-henderson|Bob]] confronts the problem methodically, drawing on practical experience rather than book learning. His solution impresses those around him and reinforces his role as a dependable, capable figure in [[betty-gordon|Betty]]'s life. The event echoes his earlier declaration that he and [[betty-gordon|Betty]] share "a common bond of [[friendship|friends]]hip." (002-015)

## Significance

[[bob-henderson|Bob]]'s solution is a character-defining moment that shows what he brings to the story beyond his relationship with [[betty-gordon|Betty]]. It demonstrates that his difficult past at [[bramble-farm|Bramble Farm]] and the county poor farm has equipped him with practical skills that serve him well.
