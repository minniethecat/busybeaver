# Just Deserts

## Overview

Chapter 23, "JUST DESERTS," brings moral resolution to several threads of the novel. Characters who have acted virtuously are rewarded, while those who have caused trouble face consequences. The event encapsulates the novel's moral framework — kindness and honesty are ultimately rewarded.

## Narrative

The chapter weaves together multiple character arcs, bringing closure to subplots and setting the stage for the final treasure discovery. [[betty-gordon|Betty]] and her [[friendship|friends]] see their loyalty and courage vindicated.

## Significance

This event represents the moral architecture of the novel — the belief that character determines destiny. It provides emotional satisfaction before the climactic treasure discovery of Chapter 25.
