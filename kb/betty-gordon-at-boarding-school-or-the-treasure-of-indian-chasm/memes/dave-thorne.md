# Dave Thorne

## Overview

Dave Thorne is a section foreman who works with [[uncle-dick|Uncle Dick]] ([[uncle-dick|Richard Gordon]]) at the [[oil-company|oil company]]. He is entrusted with keys and responsibilities, including receiving [[uncle-dick|Uncle Dick]]'s horse.

## Role in the Story

Dave Thorne first appears when [[betty-gordon|Betty Gordon]] mentions that [[uncle-dick|Uncle Dick]] has gone somewhere with him (001-030).

Later, when arrangements were being made for property and assets, it was decided that the key would be delivered to Dave Thorne, the section foreman, who would take charge (003-039).

When [[uncle-dick|Uncle Dick]] prepared to leave, he decided to give his mount (horse) to Dave Thorne, the section foreman, who had been a reliable worker (004-026). The superintendent also gave items into the charge of Dave Thorne (004-028).

## Relationships

- [[uncle-dick|Uncle Dick]] — Employer and associate; [[uncle-dick|Uncle Dick]] trusts Dave with keys and his horse (001-030, 003-039, 004-026)
