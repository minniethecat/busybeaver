# Miss Faith Saunders

## Overview

**Miss Faith Saunders** was one of the two elderly Saunders sisters who owned the Saunders farm, located near [[bramble-farm|Bramble Farm]] in the countryside. She and her sister, [[miss-charity-saunders|Miss Charity Saunders]], were [[saunders-sisters|the aunts]] of [[bob-henderson|Bob Henderson]] — their nephew. (002-019) The sisters lived quiet lives on their farm until the discovery of oil on their property transformed their circumstances, culminating in the sale of the Saunders farm for a remarkable sum. (002-018)

## Role in the Story

### The Oil Discovery

The story of the Saunders farm was one of sudden fortune. "The story of the 'Saunders' place' and of the unscrupulous land sharks who had tried to cheat the two old ladies out of their land was a familiar one to [[betty-gordon|Betty]]. It had culminated in the sale of the Saunders farm for ninety thousand dollars." (002-018) The sisters had held firm against those who sought to take advantage of them, and their patience was rewarded.

### Relationship with Bob

[[bob-henderson|Bob Henderson]] spent considerable time at the Saunders farm during his difficult months in the countryside. [[betty-gordon|Betty]] spent her time "divided...between the Saunders' place and the Watterby farm, where she and [[bob-henderson|Bob]] had shared many a simple meal." (002-020) When [[bob-henderson|Bob]] needed a clean shirt for a visit to town, he went to the Saunders farm: "[[bob-henderson|Bob]] had driven over to the Saunders' farm to borrow a clean shirt from his aunts." (002-025)

### The Farmhouse

After the sale of [[bramble-farm|the farm]], [[betty-gordon|Betty Gordon]] visited the old Saunders farmhouse. "With her parents into the old Saunders farmhouse. [[betty-gordon|Betty]] had never seen her friend look so happy." (004-027) The farmhouse, though no longer owned by [[miss-charity-saunders|the Saunders sisters]], remained a place of warm memories.
