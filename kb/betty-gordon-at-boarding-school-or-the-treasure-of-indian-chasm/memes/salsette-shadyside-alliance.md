# Salsette-Shadyside Alliance

## Overview

The [[salsette-academy|Salsette]]-[[shadyside-school|Shadyside]] alliance is the informal social network that connects the two schools across the lake. [[salsette-academy|Salsette Academy]]'s cadets and [[shadyside-school|Shadyside School]]'s girls interact through house parties, shared outings, and station encounters. The lake that separates the schools also defines their relationship — close enough for [[friendship|friends]]hip, far enough for intrigue. (010-017)

## Members

- Students of [[Shadyside School]] — the girls
- Cadets of [[Salsette Academy]] — the boys

## Role

This cross-school network provides the novel's social dynamic. Without it, the story would lack the co-ed dimension that enriches the characters' interactions — the [[friendship|friends]]hips, teasing, and mutual support between the boys' and girls' schools.
