# Miss Prettyman

## Overview

Miss Prettyman is a teacher at [[Shadyside School]] known for her strict and austere demeanor. She appears in connection with the [[Salsette Academy]] visit to [[shadyside-school|Shadyside]].

## Role in the Story

Miss Prettyman is introduced when [[bobby-littell|Bobby Littell]] presents [[teddy-tucker|Teddy]] and [[tommy-tucker|Tommy Tucker]] to her, asking "Miss Prettyman, may I present my [[friendship|friends]]?" (015-011). She responds austerely, questioning whether [[mrs-eustice|Mrs. Eustice]] had given permission for the visit (015-012).

When [[bobby-littell|Bobby]] made the mistake of openly telling Miss Prettyman about the proposed visit to [[salsette-academy|Salsette Academy]], she reacted with disapproval (015-014). Behind her back, [[betty-gordon|Betty]] signaled to [[bobby-littell|Bobby]] to be more discreet (015-016).

Miss Prettyman announced the [[salsette-academy|Salsette Academy]] visit with "grim satisfaction" (015-020) and maintained an icy glare toward the Tucker twins throughout their visit (015-023). She followed the boys into the reception room and remarked coldly about the situation (015-024, 015-027).

Later, [[bobby-littell|Bobby]] had to ask Miss Prettyman for permission to telephone [[salsette-academy|Salsette Academy]] (020-022).

## Relationships

- [[mrs-eustice|Mrs. Eustice]] — Colleague at [[shadyside-school|Shadyside]]; Miss Prettyman defers to her authority (015-012)
- [[bobby-littell|Bobby Littell]] — Student whose informal manner clashes with her strictness (015-011)
- [[teddy-tucker|Teddy Tucker]] and [[tommy-tucker|Tommy Tucker]] — Visitors she regarded with suspicion (015-012)
- [[betty-gordon|Betty Gordon]] — Student who navigated her strict supervision (015-016)
- [[ada-nansen|Ada Nansen]] — Student present during the [[salsette-academy|Salsette]] visit (015-030)
