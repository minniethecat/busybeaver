# Shadyside Kitchen Staff

## Overview

The kitchen staff at [[shadyside-school|Shadyside School]] work behind the scenes to keep the students fed and the school running. Most prominently, a Chinese cook offers [[betty-gordon|Betty]] "velly nice apple tart" in the novel's opening chapter, a small moment of warmth that establishes the welcoming atmosphere [[betty-gordon|Betty]] will find at [[shadyside-school|Shadyside]]. (001-001)

## Role

The kitchen staff represent the unseen labor of boarding [[boarding-school-life|school life]]. While the students' adventures dominate the narrative, the staff's daily work — cooking, cleaning, maintaining — provides the foundation on which [[boarding-school-life|school life]] rests. The Chinese cook's brief appearance adds cultural texture to the school community.
