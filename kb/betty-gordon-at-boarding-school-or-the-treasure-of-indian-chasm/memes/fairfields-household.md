# The Fairfields Household

## Overview

The [[fairfields|Fairfields]] household comprises the domestic staff who maintain the Littell family's country estate. Most visible among them is [[carter-butler|Carter]], the butler who presides with "the personification of propriety" over the formal running of the house. (007-016) The staff's quiet competence provides the infrastructure for the novel's social events.

## Members

- [[Carter]] — butler, the personification of propriety

## Role

The [[fairfields|Fairfields]] household represents the unseen labor that makes the gracious world of the novel possible. When "telescope grips and tennis rackets" pile up in [[carter-butler|Carter]]'s domain, it is the staff who absorb the chaos and keep the household running. (007-017)
