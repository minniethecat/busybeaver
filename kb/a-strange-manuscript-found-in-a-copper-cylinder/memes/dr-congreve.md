# Dr. Congreve

## Role in the Frame Narrative

Dr. Congreve is one of the four English gentlemen aboard the yacht Falcon who discover and debate [[adam-more|Adam More]]'s manuscript. As the medical attendant and friend of Featherstone, Congreve approaches the manuscript with the empirical mindset of a man of science. His presence in the frame narrative provides a counterweight to [[oxenden|Oxenden]]'s speculative linguistics, though his scientific rationalism proves no more adequate than [[oxenden|Oxenden]]'s theories for explaining the Kosekin phenomenon.
