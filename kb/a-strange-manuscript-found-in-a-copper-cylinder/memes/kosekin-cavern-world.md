# Kosekin Cavern World

## Geography

The Kosekin Cavern World is a vast subterranean realm located beneath the Antarctic ice cap, accessed from the Southern Ocean through a channel that sweeps beneath the mountains. Illuminated and warmed by active volcanoes, the caverns contain an inland sea, settled shores, and the volcanic Island of Fire known as Magones. The narrator describes volcanoes "vomiting forth flames and smoke" (003-058) and "the lurid glow of the volcanoes, which grew brighter as the darkness deepened" (004-023).

## Ecosystem

The cavern environment preserves prehistoric life forms extinct on the surface, including the athaleb (flying reptile) and jantannin (sea monsters). Giant ferns and cycads suggest a Carboniferous-era environment. The perpetual twilight created by volcanic illumination shapes both the ecosystem and Kosekin culture.
