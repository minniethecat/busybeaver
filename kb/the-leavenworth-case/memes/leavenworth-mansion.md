# Leavenworth Mansion

The luxurious Fifth Avenue mansion where Horatio Leavenworth lived and died. The library, where the body was found, becomes the focal point of the investigation. The house's layout, the servants' quarters, and the relationship between public and private spaces all figure in the evidence.

## Significance in the Investigation

This location plays a role in the unfolding of the Leavenworth murder investigation, contributing to the spatial and social geography of the novel's 1870s New York setting.
