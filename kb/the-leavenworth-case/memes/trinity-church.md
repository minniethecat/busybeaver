# Trinity Church

The church where Eleanore and Henry Clavering's secret marriage was performed. The marriage register provides legal proof of the union that is central to understanding Eleanore's silence and the motive for the crime.

## Significance in the Investigation

This location plays a role in the unfolding of the Leavenworth murder investigation, contributing to the spatial and social geography of the novel's 1870s New York setting.
