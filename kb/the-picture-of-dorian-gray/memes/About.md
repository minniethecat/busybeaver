# About This Wiki

This wiki is a structured encyclopedia covering **Oscar Wilde's _The Picture of [[dorian-gray|Dorian Gray]]_** (1890/1891), the only novel by one of the most celebrated writers of the Victorian era. It catalogues characters, concepts, events, locations, notable quotations, and chapter summaries, offering a navigable knowledge graph of Wilde's decadent masterpiece.

## Source Material

- **Title**: _The Picture of [[dorian-gray|Dorian Gray]]_

- **Author**: Oscar Wilde (1854–1900)

- **First publication**: _Lippincott's Monthly Magazine_, July 1890 (13 chapters)

- **Revised edition**: Ward, Lock & Co., April 1891 (20 chapters, with new preface)

- **Language**: English

- **Source format**: EPUB

## Usage Notes

- **Navigation**: Use the sidebar to browse by page type (person, concept, event, location, chapter). The search bar supports both title search and full-text search.

- **Chapter pages**: Each chapter page includes the full text with paragraph numbers (PN) for precise citation.

- **Cross-references**: Entity pages link to related characters, events, and concepts via wikilinks, building a rich network of associations.

- **Quality grades**: Pages are graded 1–5 (stub → comprehensive), visible as badges on each page.
