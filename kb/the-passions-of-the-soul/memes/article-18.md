# Article 18: The will

[018-001] Our volitions in their turn divide into two sorts: •actions of the soul that aim only at something in the soul itself, as when we will [see Glossary] to love God or in any way to apply our mind to some object that isn’t material; and •actions of the soul that aim at some event in our body, as when we will to walk. . . .
