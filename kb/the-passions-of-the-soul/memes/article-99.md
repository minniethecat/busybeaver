# Article 99: ...in joy

[099-001] In joy ·I observe· that the pulse is regular and faster than normal, but not as strong or full as in love; we feel a pleasant heat not only in the chest but also spreading into all the external parts of the body along with the blood that we see entering them in abundance; yet sometimes there’s a loss of appetite because the digestion is less active than usual.
