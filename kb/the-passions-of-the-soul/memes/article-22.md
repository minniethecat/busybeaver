# Article 22: How these other perceptions differ from one another

[022-001] All the perceptions that I haven’t yet explained—·i.e. all but article 21’s imaginings·—come to the soul by means of the nerves. They differ amongst themselves in that we take some to be perceptions of external objects that strike our senses, others to be perceptions of our body or some of its parts, and still others to be perceptions of our soul.
