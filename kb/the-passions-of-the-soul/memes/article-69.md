# Article 69: There are only six basic passions

[069-001] But there aren’t many simple and basic passions. Look over my list and you’ll easily see that there are only six:

[069-002] wonder | (articles 70–73, 75–78)

[069-003] love | (79–85)

[069-004] hatred | (79–80, 84–85)

[069-005] desire | (86–90)

[069-006] joy | (91, 93–95)

[069-007] sadness | (92–95)

[069-008] All the others are either composed from some of these six or they are species of them. So I’ll help you to find your way through the great multitude of passions by •treating the six basic ones separately, and then •showing how all the others stem from them.
