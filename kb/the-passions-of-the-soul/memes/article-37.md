# Article 37: How it becomes apparent that the passions are all caused by some movement of the spirits

[037-001] Something similar happens with all the other passions: they are caused chiefly when spirits contained in the brain’s cavities make their way to nerves that •expand or constrict the orifices of the heart, or •drive blood towards the heart in a distinctive way from other parts of the body, or •maintain the passion in some other way. So you can see why I included in my definition of the passions [article 27] that they are caused by some particular movement of the spirits.
