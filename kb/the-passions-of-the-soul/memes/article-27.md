# Article 27: Definition of the passions of the soul

[027-001] Having considered how the passions of the soul differ from all its other thoughts, it seems to me that we can define them generally as

[027-002] •perceptions, sensations or commotions of the soul which

[027-003] •we relate particularly to the soul, and are caused, maintained and strengthened by some movement of the spirits.
