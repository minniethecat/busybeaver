# Article 152: What can cause us to esteem ourselves

[152-001] One of the principal parts of wisdom is to know how and why anyone ought to esteem or contemn himself, so I shall try to give here my view about this. I see only one thing in us that could entitle us to esteem ourselves, namely the exercise of our free will and our command of our volitions. For we can be rightly praised or blamed only for actions that depend on this free will; it makes us like God in a way, by making us masters of ourselves, provided we don’t lose the rights it gives us through shrinking reluctance [see Glossary].
