# Article 114: Changes in colour

[114-001] We can’t so easily prevent ourselves from blushing or growing pale when some passion disposes us to do so, because these changes don’t depend on the nerves and muscles as the preceding ones do. They come more directly from the heart, which can be called ‘the source of the passions’ because it prepares the blood and the spirits to produce them. Blood flows continually from the heart through the arteries into the veins and then back into the heart, and the changes in the face’s colour depend solely on how much this blood fills the small veins just under the skin.
