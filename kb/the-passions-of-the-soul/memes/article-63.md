# Article 63: Self-satisfaction and repentance

[063-001] We can also think about the cause of a present good or evil as well as of a past one. A good that we have done gives us an internal satisfaction that is the sweetest of all the passions, whereas an evil ·that we have done· arouses repentance, which is the most bitter [see Glossary].
