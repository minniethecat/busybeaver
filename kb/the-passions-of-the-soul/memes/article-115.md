# Article 115: How joy makes one flush

[115-001] Thus joy makes the colour brighter and rosier because by opening the heart’s sluices it makes the blood flow faster in all the veins. As the blood becomes warmer and more fluid it makes all the parts of the face swell a little, thus making it look more smiling and cheerful.
