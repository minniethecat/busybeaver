# Article 80: What it is to be joined or separated de volonté

[080-001] I am not using the phrase de volonté to talk about desire, which is a completely different passion relating to the •future. I mean rather our consent to considering ourselves from •now on as joined with the thing we love in such a way that we imagine a whole of which we are one part and the beloved thing is the other. In the case of hatred, on the other hand, we think of ourselves alone as a ·self-contained· whole that is separate from the thing for which we have an aversion.
