# Article 3: The rule to be followed in doing this

[003-001] We won’t find this very hard to do if we bear this in mind: anything we experience as being in us, and which we see can also exist in wholly inanimate bodies, can be attributed only to our body; and anything in us that we can’t conceive of as being somehow had by a body must be attributed to our soul.
