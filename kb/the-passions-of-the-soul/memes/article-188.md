# Article 188: Those who aren’t affected by pity

[188-001] What sorts of people don’t have pity for anyone? Only •malicious and envious minds that naturally hate everyone, and •ones who are so brutish—and made so blind by good fortune or desperate by bad—that they don’t think any more misfortune could befall them.
