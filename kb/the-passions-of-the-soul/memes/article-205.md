# Article 205: Shame

[205-001] Shame, on the other hand, is a kind of sadness based also on self-love, which comes from expecting to be blamed or being anxiously aware that one may be blamed. And it is also a kind of modesty, or humility and diffidence about oneself. When our self-esteem is so great that we can’t imagine anyone contemning us, we can’t easily be ashamed!
