# Article 88: The various kinds of desire

[088-001] We could divide up desire into as many different kinds as there are different kinds of objects that we pursue. Curiosity, for example, is nothing but a desire for knowledge, and it differs greatly from a desire for glory, which in turn differs from a desire for vengeance, and so on. But in the present context ·we don’t need all those details·; all we need to know is that there are as many kinds of desire as there are of love or hatred, and that the strongest and most significant desires are the ones arising from attraction and revulsion.
