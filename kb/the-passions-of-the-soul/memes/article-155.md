# Article 155: What humility as a virtue consists in

[155-001] Thus the most generous people are usually also the humblest. We have humility as a virtue when, by reflecting on the infirmity of our nature and on the wrongs we may have done or could yet do (wrongs that are no less serious than other people’s), we •don’t rate ourselves higher than anyone else and •think that since others have free will just as we do, they may use it just as well as we use ours.
