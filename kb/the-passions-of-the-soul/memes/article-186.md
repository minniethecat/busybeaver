# Article 186: Those who are most given to pity

[186-001] Those who feel very weak and very open to the adversities of fortune seem to be more inclined to this passion than others, because they think of others’ misfortunes as possibly happening to themselves. Thus they are moved to pity more by their love for themselves than by love for others.
