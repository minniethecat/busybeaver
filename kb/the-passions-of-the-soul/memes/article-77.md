# Article 77: It is not the stupidest or the cleverest people who are most given to wonder

[077-001] Although it’s only dull and stupid folk who are not naturally disposed to wonder, this doesn’t mean that those with the best minds are always the most inclined to it. In fact, most wondering is done by people who are equipped with pretty good common sense but have no high opinion of their competence.
