# Article 14: Differences among the spirits may also cause them to take various different courses

[014-001] (ii) The other cause of variety in the flow of the animal spirits to the muscles is the variety in how fast the spirits are moving and how small their parts are. When some of their parts are coarser and more agitated than others, they penetrate more deeply in a straight line into the brain’s cavities and pores, and in this way they are directed to muscles that they wouldn’t have gone to if they’d had less force.
