# Article 163: Scorn

[163-001] What I call ‘scorn’ is our soul’s inclination to contemn a free cause, thinking that although it is by nature capable of doing good or evil, it so far beneath us that it can’t help or harm us. The movement of the spirits that arouses scorn is composed of the movements that produce wonder and confidence or boldness.
