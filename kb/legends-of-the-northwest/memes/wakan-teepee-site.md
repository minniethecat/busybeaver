Gordon's *[[legend|Legend]]s of the Northwest* is grounded in the geography of the upper Mississippi region, which he studied alongside [[dakota|Dakota]] [[tradition|traditions]] (P01-007).

[[wakan-teepee|Wakan Teepee]] appears in Gordon's poetic narratives. His [[legend|legends]] are set against the backdrop of this landscape. The place names reflect both [[dakota|Dakota]]/[[ojibway|Ojibway]] originals and European adaptations.
