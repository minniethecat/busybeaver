# Fur Trade Company

The fur trading companies that employed [[voyageurs|voyageur]]s and established commerce with the [[dakota|Dakota]] people.

## Connection to the Legends

Gordon's *[[legend|Legend]]s of the Northwest* is grounded in the social organization of the [[dakota|Dakota]] and neighboring peoples. His years of study (P01-007) informed his portrayal of these groups.

## In the Book

Fur Trade Company appears in the context of Gordon's poetic narratives. His preface acknowledges his debt to scholars like Riggs (P01-063) who documented [[dakota|Dakota]] social organization.

Gordon acknowledges his debt to the Reverend S. R. Riggs, whose ethnographic work provided the foundation for his [[legend|legends]] (P01-063).

His preface claims fidelity to [[dakota|Dakota]] [[tradition|customs]], life, [[tradition|traditions]], and [[superstition|superstitions]] (P01-174).
