# Virgins' Dance

The ceremonial dance of [[dakota|Dakota]] maidens at the [[feast-of-the-virgins|Feast of the Virgins]].

Fifty merry [[dakota|Dakota]] maidens participated in the ceremonial [[dance|dances]] at the lakeside (001-087). The dance was a highlight of the [[feast-of-the-virgins|Feast of the Virgins]] celebration, combining courtship ritual with communal festivity (003-108).

## In Gordon's Legends

Gordon preserves these [[dakota|Dakota]] events through English verse, informed by his years of study and the work of missionaries like Riggs (P01-063). His representations aim to be faithful to the original [[tradition|traditions]] (P01-174).
