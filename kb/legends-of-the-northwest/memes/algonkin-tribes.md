# Algonkin Tribes

The [[algonkin|Algonkin]]-speaking tribes of the [[lake-superior|Great Lake]]s, including the [[ojibway|Ojibway]], Ottawa, and Potawatomi.

## Connection to the Legends

Gordon's *[[legend|Legend]]s of the Northwest* is grounded in the social organization of the [[dakota|Dakota]] and neighboring peoples. His years of study (P01-007) informed his portrayal of these groups.

## In the Book

Algonkin Tribes appears in the context of Gordon's poetic narratives. His preface acknowledges his debt to scholars like Riggs (P01-063) who documented [[dakota|Dakota]] social organization.


Gordon's *[[legend|Legend]]s of the Northwest* preserves this in the context of [[dakota|Dakota]] life (009-153).
