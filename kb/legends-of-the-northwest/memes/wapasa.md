# Wapasa

[[dakota|Dakota]] chief and grandfather figure mentioned in Gordon's [[legend|legends]].

## Connection to the Legends

Gordon's *[[legend|Legend]]s of the Northwest* references Wapasa in the context of [[dakota|Dakota]] history and culture. His preface details his years of study (P01-007) and his debt to previous scholars and missionaries.

## In the Book

Gordon's footnotes and preface acknowledge multiple sources, including Riggs (P01-063), whose work among the [[dakota|Dakotas]] provided the ethnographic foundation for the [[legend|legends]].


Wapasa appears in Gordon's text as a [[dakota|Dakota]] chief and the grandfather of a key character (016-020). The name also appears as a geographical designation — the town of Wabasha, [[minnesota|Minnesota]], bears a version of this [[dakota|Dakota]] name.
