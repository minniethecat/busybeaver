# Mississippi River

The great river at the heart of Gordon's Prelude — Michesee Seebee in [[ojibway|Ojibway]] (010-116) — the central waterway of [[dakota|Dakota]] country.

## Connection to the Legends

Gordon's *[[legend|Legend]]s of the Northwest* is grounded in the geography of the upper Mississippi region, which he studied alongside [[dakota|Dakota]] [[tradition|traditions]] (P01-007).

## In the Book

Mississippi River appears in Gordon's poetic narratives. His [[legend|legends]] are set against the backdrop of this landscape. The place names reflect both [[dakota|Dakota]]/[[ojibway|Ojibway]] originals and European adaptations.


Gordon's *[[legend|Legend]]s of the Northwest* preserves this in the context of [[dakota|Dakota]] life (010-116).
