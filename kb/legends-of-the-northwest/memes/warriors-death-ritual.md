# Warrior's Death Ritual

The ritual preparations and [[song|songs]] of [[dakota|Dakota]] warriors before death in battle.

[[dakota|Dakota]] warriors facing death performed specific rituals, including the death song. These rites affirmed the warrior's courage and readiness to join the [[spirit|spirits]] (001-047). The tradition reflected the [[dakota|Dakota]] belief in an afterlife in the land of [[spirit|spirits]] (001-192).

## In Gordon's Legends

Gordon preserves these [[dakota|Dakota]] events through English verse, informed by his years of study and the work of missionaries like Riggs (P01-063). His representations aim to be faithful to the original [[tradition|traditions]] (P01-174).
