# French Trading Posts

The network of French fur trading posts established by [[voyageurs|voyageur]]s along the Mississippi and its tributaries.

## Connection to the Legends

Gordon's *[[legend|Legend]]s of the Northwest* is grounded in the social organization of the [[dakota|Dakota]] and neighboring peoples. His years of study (P01-007) informed his portrayal of these groups.

## In the Book

French Trading Posts appears in the context of Gordon's poetic narratives. His preface acknowledges his debt to scholars like Riggs (P01-063) who documented [[dakota|Dakota]] social organization.
