# Spirit Ceremony

Ceremonies invoking the [[soul|spirit]] world, conducted by [[dakota|Dakota]] medicine men.

The Wicasta [[wakan|Wakan]] (medicine man) conducted ceremonies to invoke or appease [[spirit|spirits]] (017-044). These rituals connected the [[dakota|Dakota]] to the sacred power — [[wakan|Wakan]] — that pervaded their world (018-002).

## In Gordon's Legends

Gordon preserves these [[dakota|Dakota]] events through English verse, informed by his years of study and the work of missionaries like Riggs (P01-063). His representations aim to be faithful to the original [[tradition|traditions]] (P01-174).
