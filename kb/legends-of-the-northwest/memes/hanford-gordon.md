# Hanford Lennox Gordon

Author of [[legend|Legend]]s of the Northwest (1878), poet and student of [[dakota|Dakota]] culture.

## Connection to the Legends

Gordon's *[[legend|Legend]]s of the Northwest* references Hanford Lennox Gordon in the context of [[dakota|Dakota]] history and culture. His preface details his years of study (P01-007) and his debt to previous scholars and missionaries.

## In the Book

Gordon's footnotes and preface acknowledge multiple sources, including Riggs (P01-063), whose work among the [[dakota|Dakotas]] provided the ethnographic foundation for the [[legend|legends]].


Gordon devoted years to studying [[dakota|Dakota]] language, history, [[tradition|traditions]], [[tradition|customs]], and [[superstition|superstitions]] (P01-007). His *[[legend|Legend]]s of the Northwest* renders [[dakota|Dakota]] and [[ojibway|Ojibway]] oral [[tradition|traditions]] into English verse. Gordon acknowledges his debt to Riggs (P01-063) and other scholars in the preface (P01-174).
