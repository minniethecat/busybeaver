# Treaty Signing

The formal agreements between [[dakota|Dakota]] bands and European powers, events marking the end of [[dakota|Dakota]] sovereignty.

Gordon's preface laments that the day of the [[dakota|Dakotas]] is done (P01-046). The treaties that followed European contact marked the end of the old [[dakota|Dakota]] way of life that the [[legend|legends]] preserve.

## In Gordon's Legends

Gordon's [[legend|legends]] capture these events within the broader narrative of [[dakota|Dakota]] life. His preface notes his study of [[dakota|Dakota]] [[tradition|customs]] (P01-007) and his debt to Riggs' ethnographic work (P01-063).
