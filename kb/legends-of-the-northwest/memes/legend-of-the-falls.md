# Legend of the Falls

The Legend of the Falls is [[hanford-lennox-gordon|H. L. Gordon]]'s narrative poem recounting the [[dakota|Dakota]] tradition of how the [[falls-of-st-anthony|Falls of St. Anthony]] were formed — a story of love, betrayal, and the wrath of [[unktéhee|Unktéhee]] that split the river (008-278, 016-109).

## Definition / Identity

The [[legend|Legend]] occupies the third major narrative block of *[[legend|Legend]]s of the Northwest*, spanning the chapters that bear its name. It tells the [[dakota|Dakota]] origin story of the great cataract through the intertwined fates of [[anpetu-sapa|Anpétu-Sâpa]] ([[anpetu-sapa|Dark Sun]]) and her warrior lover.

- **Origin**: [[dakota|Dakota]] oral tradition, versified by H. L. Gordon
- **Language**: English (Gordon's poem); [[dakota|Dakota]] source terms preserved in footnotes
- **Domain**: legend / narrative / origin-story

Gordon explicitly references the [[legend|Legend]] in his footnotes: "See Legend of the Falls or Note 28 — Appendix" (008-278), and refers to his own work as "my 'Legend of the Falls'" (016-109).

## In the Book

The Legend of the Falls occupies chapters 013 through 016 of Gordon's work. It follows the [[winona|Winona]] narrative and precedes The [[sea-gull|Sea-Gull]], forming the central pillar of Gordon's three-legend structure.

### Role in the Legends

The [[legend|Legend]] weaves together the cosmic conflict between [[unktéhee|Unktéhee]] (the [[unktéhee|Water Spirit]]) and [[thunder-bird|Wakínyan]] (the [[thunder-bird|Thunder-Bird]]) with the human drama of [[anpetu-sapa|Anpétu-Sâpa]]. When the subterranean [[unktéhee|Unktéhee]] rages, the earth ruptures and the river plunges into the newly formed abyss — creating [[falls-of-st-anthony|the Falls]] as both a geological and [[soul|spirit]]ual landmark.

Key figures in the narrative include [[anpetu-sapa|Anpétu-Sâpa]] ([[anpetu-sapa|Dark Sun]]), the [[dakota|Dakota]] maiden whose name links her to the [[anpetu-wee|sun deity]]; her warrior lover; and the [[soul|spirit]]ual powers whose cosmic struggle reshapes the landscape.
