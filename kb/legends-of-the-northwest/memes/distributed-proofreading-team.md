# Distributed Proofreading Team

The [[project-gutenberg|Project Gutenberg]] volunteer team that digitized and preserved Gordon's [[legend|Legend]]s of the Northwest (P01-001).

## Connection to the Legends

Gordon's *[[legend|Legend]]s of the Northwest* is grounded in the social organization of the [[dakota|Dakota]] and neighboring peoples. His years of study (P01-007) informed his portrayal of these groups.

## In the Book

Distributed Proofreading Team appears in the context of Gordon's poetic narratives. His preface acknowledges his debt to scholars like Riggs (P01-063) who documented [[dakota|Dakota]] social organization.
