# Riggs Mission

The missionary enterprise of Reverend S. R. Riggs among the [[dakota|Dakotas]], which produced key ethnographic records.

## Connection to the Legends

Gordon's *[[legend|Legend]]s of the Northwest* is grounded in the social organization of the [[dakota|Dakota]] and neighboring peoples. His years of study (P01-007) informed his portrayal of these groups.

## In the Book

Riggs Mission appears in the context of Gordon's poetic narratives. His preface acknowledges his debt to scholars like Riggs (P01-063) who documented [[dakota|Dakota]] social organization.


Gordon's *[[legend|Legend]]s of the Northwest* preserves this in the context of [[dakota|Dakota]] life (P01-064).
