# Henry H. Sibley

First governor of [[minnesota|Minnesota]] and figure in the [[dakota|Sioux]] War referenced in Gordon's footnotes.

## Connection to the Legends

Gordon's *[[legend|Legend]]s of the Northwest* references Henry H. Sibley in the context of [[dakota|Dakota]] history and culture. His preface details his years of study (P01-007) and his debt to previous scholars and missionaries.

## In the Book

Gordon's footnotes and preface acknowledge multiple sources, including Riggs (P01-063), whose work among the [[dakota|Dakotas]] provided the ethnographic foundation for the [[legend|legends]].


Sibley was the first governor of [[minnesota|Minnesota]] and a key figure in the US-[[dakota|Dakota]] War. Gordon's footnote references Sibley in connection with the history of the [[dakota|Sioux]] War (015-247). [[edward-neill|Neill]]'s history of the [[dakota|Sioux]] War also documents Sibley's role.
