# Edward D. Neill

Edward D. Neill was a historian of [[minnesota|Minnesota]] whose works on the [[dakota|Dakota]] people and the [[dakota|Sioux]] War are frequently cited in Gordon's footnotes.

## Scholarly Contribution

Neill authored multiple historical works on the early history of [[minnesota|Minnesota]] and the [[dakota|Dakota]] people. Gordon cites Neill's *History of [[minnesota|Minnesota]]* and histories of the [[dakota|Sioux]] War as sources for his [[legend|legends]] (015-247).

## In Gordon's Footnotes

Gordon references Neill alongside Riggs as a key scholarly source. While Riggs provided the linguistic and missionary perspective (P01-063), Neill contributed the historical narrative of [[dakota|Dakota]]-European relations (015-247).

Gordon's preface acknowledges his reliance on these scholars (P01-007), whose combined work provided the foundation for the [[legend|legends]].
