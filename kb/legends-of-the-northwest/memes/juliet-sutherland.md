# Juliet Sutherland

A producer of the [[project-gutenberg|Project Gutenberg]] digital edition of [[legend|Legend]]s of the Northwest.

## Connection to the Legends

These figures appear in or contributed to the preservation of *[[legend|Legend]]s of the Northwest*. Gordon's years of study among the [[dakota|Dakotas]] (P01-007) informed his portrayal of [[dakota|Dakota]] and [[ojibway|Ojibway]] characters.

## In the Book

The [[legend|legends]] depict a world populated by warriors, maidens, chiefs, and editors whose combined efforts preserved these [[dakota|Dakota]] and [[ojibway|Ojibway]] [[tradition|traditions]] in English verse.


Juliet Sutherland was one of the producers of the [[project-gutenberg|Project Gutenberg]] digital edition that made *[[legend|Legend]]s of the Northwest* widely accessible (P01-001). Along with [[susan-skinner|Susan Skinner]] and the [[distributed-proofreading-team|Distributed Proofreading Team]], she contributed to the preservation of Gordon's work.

The [[project-gutenberg|Project Gutenberg]] edition, produced by Skinner, Sutherland, and the [[distributed-proofreading-team|Distributed Proofreading Team]] (P01-001), ensures Gordon's work remains available to modern readers.
