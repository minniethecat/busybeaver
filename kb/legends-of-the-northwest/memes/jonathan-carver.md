# Jonathan Carver

18th-century explorer whose accounts of the [[dakota|Dakota]] informed later writers.

## Connection to the Legends

Gordon's *[[legend|Legend]]s of the Northwest* references Jonathan Carver in the context of [[dakota|Dakota]] history and culture. His preface details his years of study (P01-007) and his debt to previous scholars and missionaries.

## In the Book

Gordon's footnotes and preface acknowledge multiple sources, including Riggs (P01-063), whose work among the [[dakota|Dakotas]] provided the ethnographic foundation for the [[legend|legends]].


Carver explored the upper Mississippi region in the mid-18th century and published accounts of the [[dakota|Dakota]] people. His observations of [[dakota|Dakota]] [[tradition|customs]] and the geography of the region informed later ethnographic work. Gordon references Carver in the context of early exploration (P01-007).
