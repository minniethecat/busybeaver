# Dakota Confederation

The confederation of [[dakota|Dakota]] bands united by a common language and origin — [[dakota|Dakota]] signifies alliance or confederation (P01-010).

## Connection to the Legends

Gordon's *[[legend|Legend]]s of the Northwest* is grounded in the social organization of the [[dakota|Dakota]] and neighboring peoples. His years of study (P01-007) informed his portrayal of these groups.

## In the Book

Dakota Confederation appears in the context of Gordon's poetic narratives. His preface acknowledges his debt to scholars like Riggs (P01-063) who documented [[dakota|Dakota]] social organization.
