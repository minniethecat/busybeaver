## Chronological Summary

### December 1928

- **Christmas at Whittington Terrace**: [[christmas-1928-whittington-terrace|Christmas 1928]] — The Harrison household gathers at 15 Whittington Terrace, Bayswater. [[harwood-lathom|Harwood Lathom]] and [[john-munting|John Munting]] are part of the social circle.

### Early 1929

- **The Mushroom Dinner Party**: [[the-mushroom-dinner-party|The Mushroom Dinner Party]] — A dinner at which George Harrison serves an experimental mushroom dish to his guests.
- **Affair Begins**: [[the-affair|The Affair]] — Margaret Harrison and Harwood Lathom's romantic relationship develops.

### Summer 1929

- **The Shack Weekend**: [[the-shack-weekend|The Shack Weekend]] — George Harrison, Harwood Lathom, and John Munting spend time at The Shack in Manaton, Devon.
- **Paris Trip**: [[paris-trip-december-1928|Paris Trip]] — Margaret and George Harrison travel to Paris.
- **Letters Exchanged**: [[margaret-harrison-letters-to-lathom|Margaret Harrison's letters to Lathom]] document the affair through the summer.

### October 1929

- **Paul Harrison Returns from Africa**: [[paul-harrison-returns-from-africa|Paul Harrison Returns from Africa]] — George Harrison's son returns from abroad.
- **Discovery of Poison Evidence**: [[munting-discovers-poison-evidence|Munting Discovers Poison Evidence]] — John Munting finds evidence of muscarine poisoning.
- **Death of George Harrison**: [[death-of-george-harrison|Death of George Harrison]] — 19 October 1929. George Harrison is found dead at The Shack under suspicious circumstances.
- **Forensic Investigation**: [[forensic-investigation|Forensic Investigation]] — The police and Home Office Analyst Sir James Lubbock investigate.

### November–December 1929

- **The Inquest**: [[the-inquest|The Inquest]] — The coroner's inquest into George Harrison's death hears testimony from Sir James Lubbock, Dr. Hughes, and others.
- **The Telegram**: [[the-telegram-february-1929|The Telegram]] — Evidence emerges of communications between the principals.

### 1930

- **Lathom Arrested**: [[lathom-arrest|Lathom Arrest]] — Harwood Lathom is arrested on suspicion of murder.
- **The Trial**: [[the-trial|The Trial]] — The trial of Harwood Lathom for the murder of George Harrison.
- **The Conviction**: [[the-conviction|The Conviction]] — The verdict and its aftermath.
