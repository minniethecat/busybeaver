# Paris Trip (December 1928)

## Summary

George and [[margaret-harrison|Margaret Harrison]] traveled to Paris from December 15 to December 28, 1928 [PN=025-002]. This trip — their last holiday together before George's murder — occurred immediately after [[harwood-lathom|Lathom]] joined the household at [[whittington-terrace|15 Whittington Terrace]].

## Context

The Paris trip serves as a chronological marker in the dossier. [[paul-harrison|Paul Harrison]] notes it specifically: "my father and stepmother were in Paris from the 15th of December to the 28th" [PN=025-002]. The trip's timing is significant — it separates the Christmas period (when [[harwood-lathom|Lathom]] arrived) from the new year (when the affair with [[margaret-harrison|Margaret]] intensified).

The trip may have been intended as a respite from household tensions, or simply a holiday. In either case, it failed: [[harwood-lathom|Lathom]] remained at [[whittington-terrace|Whittington Terrace]], and [[margaret-harrison|Margaret]] returned to the situation she had briefly escaped.
