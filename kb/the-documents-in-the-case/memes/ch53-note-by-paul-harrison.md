As the letter No.Â [46](chapter-46.xhtml) shows, she has taken pains to protect herself against any suspicion of complicity. Although, morally, she is quite equally guilty with [[harwood-lathom|Lathom]], and though I have personally no doubt that the letter is an impudent hypocrisy, it will probably be difficult to bring home to her a guilty knowledge of the actual commission of the crime. I trust that you will use every possible endeavour to prevent this abominable woman from getting off scot-free.
