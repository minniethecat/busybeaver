## About the Novel

*The Perennial Bachelor* is a novel of manners that traces the life of Victor Campion and his three sisters across the late nineteenth and early twentieth centuries. Set primarily in Wilmington, Delaware, it chronicles the slow decline of a family, the economics of courtship and marriage, and the particular pathos of lives shaped by duty rather than desire.
