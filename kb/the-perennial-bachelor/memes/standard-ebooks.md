# Standard Ebooks

## Overview

Standard Ebooks is the volunteer-driven digital publisher that produced the modern ebook edition of Anne Parrish's *The Perennial Bachelor* (1925). The organization takes public domain texts from sources like Project Gutenberg and produces carefully formatted, professionally designed ebook editions. Their edition of *The Perennial Bachelor* serves as the source text for this wiki.

## The Edition

The Standard Ebooks edition of *The Perennial Bachelor* was produced from the Project Gutenberg transcription of the original 1925 Harper & Brothers publication. Standard Ebooks' process involves semantic markup, modern typography, careful proofreading, and the production of clean, accessible ebook files. Their edition represents the novel in its most widely accessible contemporary form.

## Significance

Standard Ebooks' role in the novel's contemporary life is infrastructural rather than interpretive. They do not annotate, critique, or contextualize the novel — they make it available. This wiki builds upon their labor, using the clean digital text they produced as the foundation for the kind of deep reading, cross-referencing, and contextual analysis that their edition enables but does not perform.
