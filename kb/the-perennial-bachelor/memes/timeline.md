## Victor Campion's Arc

Victor's life spans most of this timeline. He begins as a young man in a household of women — the "perennial bachelor" of the title — and ages through the novel as courtships swirl around him and his sisters marry, sicken, or remain. The [[Victor Campion]] page traces his full arc; [[The Perennial Bachelor]] concept page analyzes the titular theme.
