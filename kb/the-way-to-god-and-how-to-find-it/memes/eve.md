# Eve

## Overview

Eve was the first woman, created by God as a companion for [[adam|Adam]]. Together with [[adam|Adam]], she fell into sin through believing [[satan|Satan]]'s lie. [[dwight-l-moody|D. L. Moody]] mentions Eve in connection with [[adam|Adam]]: [[satan|Satan]] "succeeded in making our first parents believe this lie" — that God does not love them — "and he too often succeeds with their children" (001-007). Eve's deception illustrates how the devil attacks the fundamental truth of God's love.

## Role in Moody's Teaching

### The Original Deception

Moody's single reference to Eve places her at the very beginning of [[satan|Satan]]'s campaign against the love of God: "For more than six thousand years he has been trying to persuade men that God does not love them. He succeeded in making our first parents believe this lie" (001-007). The lie that God hates rather than loves is the devil's oldest and most effective strategy.

### A Warning for All

Eve's deception is not merely ancient history. The same lie continues to deceive — people still believe God hates them when they sin, when in fact He loves the sinner while hating the sin (001-019). Eve's story is repeated in every soul that runs from God under the delusion that God is angry rather than loving.
