# Resurrection

The resurrection of [[jesus-christ|Jesus Christ]] from the dead is the divine vindication of His person and work. [[dwight-l-moody|D. L. Moody]] references the resurrection as the ground of the believer's [[assurance]] — [[jesus-christ|Christ]] "was delivered for our offences, and was raised again for our justification" (Romans 4:25) (007-047). The resurrection is not merely a historical event but the present source of the believer's [[faith]] and hope.

## Definition

The resurrection is the bodily rising of [[jesus-christ|Jesus Christ]] from the dead on the third day after His [[the-crucifixion|crucifixion]]. It is the central miracle of [[jesus-christ|Christ]]ianity — the event that authenticates [[jesus-christ|Christ]]'s claims to deity and guarantees the future resurrection of all believers. Moody treats the resurrection as inseparable from the cross: [[jesus-christ|Christ]] died to pay for sin and rose to secure justification.

## Moody's Teaching

### The Ground of Assurance

Moody appeals to the resurrection as the ground of the believer's confidence. Quoting Romans 4:25 — [[jesus-christ|Christ]] "was delivered for our offences, and was raised again for our justification" — he argues that the resurrection is God's public declaration that [[jesus-christ|Christ]]'s payment was accepted (007-047). The empty tomb is the receipt for sin's debt.

### The Power for Present Living

Paul's desire to "know Him and the power of His resurrection" (Philippians 3:10) represents the highest ambition of the [[jesus-christ|Christ]]ian life (008-002). The resurrection is not merely a future hope but a present power — the same power that raised [[jesus-christ|Christ]] from the dead is at work in the believer.

### Christ's Own Declaration

[[jesus-christ|Christ]] declared: "I am the resurrection, and the life: he that believeth in Me, though he were dead, yet shall he live" (John 11:25) (007-049). For Moody, this is the ultimate promise — that union with [[jesus-christ|Christ]] guarantees participation in His resurrection life.

## Biblical Foundation

- **John 11:25** — "I am the resurrection and the life" (007-049)
- **Romans 4:25** — "Raised again for our justification" (007-047)
- **Philippians 3:10** — "The power of His resurrection" (008-002)
