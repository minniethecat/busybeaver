## Historical Context

In the context of 1890s [[england|England]] — the setting of *Tracks in the Snow* — deduction reflected the social, legal, and intellectual currents of the late Victorian period. The novel's treatment of this subject draws on contemporary understanding and practice.

## Analysis

The portrayal of deduction in *Tracks in the Snow* contributes to the novel's broader thematic concerns. Through the first-person narrative of **[[long-wilton|Long Wilton]]**, the reader encounters this concept not as abstract theory but as lived experience — shaped by the pressures of a murder investigation and the moral weight of seeking justice.
