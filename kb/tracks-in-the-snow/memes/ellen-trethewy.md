# Ellen Trethewy

Daughter or relative of [[reuben-trethewy|Reuben Trethewy]] — her death (by drowning) is the second tragedy in the novel and shifts the investigation. Ellen Trethewy appears approximately 24 times in the novel, making her a significant figure in the narrative.

## Overview

Ellen Trethewy is a character in *Tracks in the Snow*, the 1890s murder mystery narrated by **[[long-wilton|Long Wilton]]**. Their role in the investigation of **[[eustace-peters|Eustace Peters]]**' murder is revealed through [[long-wilton|Wilton]]'s first-person narration.

## Role in the Novel

Ellen Trethewy appears in the context of the murder investigation. Their actions, [[evidence|testimony]], or relationships contribute to the unfolding of the mystery and its eventual resolution.
