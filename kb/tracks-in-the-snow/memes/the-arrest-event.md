## Overview

The Arrest of Trethewy is one of the key events in *Tracks in the Snow*, occurring during the investigation of **[[eustace-peters|Eustace Peters]]**' murder. The event shapes the trajectory of the narrative and the understanding of the characters involved.

## In the Novel

The event unfolds within the 1890s English setting, observed and narrated by **[[long-wilton|Long Wilton]]** in the first person. Its significance to the murder investigation becomes apparent as the narrative develops.
