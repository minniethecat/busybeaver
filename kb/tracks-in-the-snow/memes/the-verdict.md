# The Verdict

The jury's decision in the trial — the legal resolution of the murder case that brings the novel's investigation to its formal conclusion.

> "So the verdict was given and the sentence was"
> — （022-019）

## Overview

The Verdict is one of the key events in *Tracks in the Snow*, occurring during the investigation of **[[eustace-peters|Eustace Peters]]**' murder. The event shapes the trajectory of the narrative and the understanding of the characters involved.

## In the Novel

The event unfolds within the 1890s English setting, observed and narrated by **[[long-wilton|Long Wilton]]** in the first person. Its significance to the murder investigation becomes apparent as the narrative develops.
