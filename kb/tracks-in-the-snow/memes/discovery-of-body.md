# Discovery of the Body

The housemaid discovers [[eustace-peters|Eustace Peters]] dead in his bedroom on the morning of 29 January, setting the investigation in motion.

> "On the morning of the 29th of January, 1896, Eustace Peters was found murdered in his bed at his house, Grenvile Combe, in the parish of Long Wilton, of which I was then"
> — （001-001）

## Overview

Discovery of the Body is one of the key events in *Tracks in the Snow*, occurring during the investigation of **[[eustace-peters|Eustace Peters]]**' murder. The event shapes the trajectory of the narrative and the understanding of the characters involved.

## In the Novel

The event unfolds within the 1890s English setting, observed and narrated by **[[long-wilton|Long Wilton]]** in the first person. Its significance to the murder investigation becomes apparent as the narrative develops.
