# Berlin

**Berlin** in *Greenmantle* serves as the capital of Imperial Germany and the headquarters of the German intelligence operation that Richard Hannay must infiltrate. It is the enemy counterpart to London — a city of grey efficiency and hidden danger where Hannay's cover identity as a Boer sympathiser is tested to its limit.

## Journey to Berlin

### Departure from London

Hannay's journey to Berlin begins almost immediately upon his recruitment by Sir Walter Bullivant. The haste of the departure underscores the urgency of the mission:

> The train for Berlin leaves in half an hour. Pray make your preparations accordingly. [003-011]

### The Journey

The journey itself is described as surprisingly ordinary, a deliberate narrative choice that heightens the tension by contrast:

> My chief recollection of our journey to Berlin was its commonplaceness. [003-059]

## In Enemy Territory

### A City of Danger

Berlin is the place where Hannay is most vulnerable. He cannot speak German fluently, and his disguise depends on the forbearance of those around him. The city represents the heart of enemy power:

> ...Berlin and all the rest of it seemed unreal and far away. [006-016]

### Intelligence Operations

Berlin houses the German intelligence machinery. It is here that Hannay gains insight into the German approach to the war:

> ...a house in Berlin, and I was about as likely to find out anything there... [008-022]

### The Contrast with Constantinople

As Hannay moves eastward, Berlin recedes in his mind, replaced by the more immediate dangers of Constantinople:

> Constantinople! I had thought myself a long way from it in Berlin, but I was a longer way now. [008-044]

## Symbolic Function

| Aspect | London | Berlin |
|--------|--------|--------|
| **Role** | Allied headquarters | Enemy headquarters |
| **Atmosphere** | Grey, bureaucratic | Grey, menacing |
| **Hannay's safety** | Relative security | Maximum danger |
| **Narrative function** | Departure/return point | Infiltration target |

## Key Encounters

- **Colonel von Stumm** — Hannay is taken to supper at a Berlin restaurant where he first encounters German military intelligence up close [005-092]
- **Hilda von Einem** — Though her main operations are in the East, her connection to Berlin's intelligence circles is implied
