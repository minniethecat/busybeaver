# The Mission

## Overview

The mission in *[[greenmantle-concept|Greenmantle]]* is a covert British operation to infiltrate the Ottoman Empire and uncover the nature of a German-orchestrated Islamic holy war. Commissioned by [[sir-walter-bullivant|Sir Walter]] [[sir-walter-bullivant|Bullivant]] of the [[british-intelligence|Foreign Office]] and led by [[richard-hannay|Major Richard Hannay]], the mission spans from [[london|London]] to the eastern [[anatolia|Anatolia]]n fortress of Erzerum [001-023].

> "And the mission you spoke of for me is to go and find out?" He nodded gravely. "That is the crazy and impossible mission." [001-062]

## Context

By late 1915, the [[trench-warfare|Western Front]] had settled into bloody stalemate. [[sir-walter-bullivant|Sir Walter]] [[sir-walter-bullivant|Bullivant]] identifies a new threat: Germany is attempting to open an Eastern front not of armies but of religious insurrection [001-020]. Intelligence suggests that a mysterious figure or symbol — "[[greenmantle-concept|Greenmantle]]" — is being prepared to unite [[islam|the Islamic world]] against the Allies [001-031]. If successful, this would threaten British India, the Suez Canal, and the entire Eastern Mediterranean.

[[sir-walter-bullivant|Sir Walter]] summons Hannay precisely because the mission requires someone who is neither a professional spy nor a diplomat — a soldier who can think on his feet and survive in hostile territory [001-046]. Hannay's pre-war experience with the Black Stone conspiracy makes him uniquely suited to understand the nature of the threat.

> "His name," he said at last, "was Harry Bullivant. He was my son. God rest his brave soul!" [001-151]

## Key Moments

The mission begins in [[london|London]] with [[sir-walter-bullivant|Sir Walter]]'s briefing at the [[british-intelligence|Foreign Office]] [001-053]. Hannay recruits [[john-blenkiron|Blenkiron]] in [[london|London]] [001-100] and arranges for [[sandy-arbuthnot|Sandy]] and [[peter-pienaar|Peter]] to join. The team travels separately through [[lisbon|Lisbon]] and into Germany, using [[john-blenkiron|Blenkiron]]'s American cover [001-110].

> "Somehow or other we have to reach Constantinople, and to beat the biggest area of country we must go by different roads." [002-006]

The journey continues through the Balkans toward Constantinople, with each leg presenting new dangers. In Constantinople, [[sandy-arbuthnot|Sandy]] discovers the conspiracy's true nature: "Greenmantle is dying — has been dying for months" [015-114], revealing that the prophet figure around whom the holy war is being organized is mortally ill.

> "Yes. It means just what it reads and no more. Greenmantle is dying — has been dying for months. This afternoon they brought a German doctor to see him, and the man gave him a few hours of life." [015-114]

The mission's climax occurs at Erzerum, where the conspiracy's true nature is revealed and the team must act to prevent catastrophe [001-146]. [[sandy-arbuthnot|Sandy]] recognizes the strategic significance: the Germans are sending Greenmantle to the Eastern front to rally the Turkish defence against the Russian advance.

> "The great hills! ... We're going to Erzerum ... Don't you see that the Germans are playing their big card? They're sending Greenmantle to the point of danger in the hope that his coming will rally the Turkish defence." [016-110]

At Erzerum, [[peter-pienaar|Peter]] crosses the Russian lines with intelligence about the fortress's weak point, leading to the final assault. The mission culminates in victory as the Russian forces break through.

> "Sandy," I yelled, "Peter has got through. The Russians are round the flank. The town is burning. Glory to God, we've won, we've won!" [022-030]

## Participants

Richard Hannay leads the mission, recruited directly by [[sir-walter-bullivant|Sir Walter]]. [[john-blenkiron|John S. Blenkiron]] provides financial resources, American cover, and continental connections. [[sandy-arbuthnot|Sandy]] Arbuthnot operates independently, using his Oriental expertise to infiltrate the conspiracy's inner circle. [[peter-pienaar|Peter]] [[peter-pienaar|Pienaar]] handles the practical logistics of covert movement through hostile terrain [001-119].

## Outcome

The mission succeeds in uncovering and disrupting the [[greenmantle-concept|Greenmantle]] conspiracy at Erzerum. Each team member survives to pursue different postwar paths: Hannay commands his battalion on the [[trench-warfare|Western Front]], [[sandy-arbuthnot|Sandy]] serves at Basra, [[john-blenkiron|Blenkiron]] returns to America, and [[peter-pienaar|Peter]] joins the Flying Corps [001-191].

## Connections

The mission is directly connected to Hannay's earlier adventure in *The Thirty-Nine Steps*, which established his relationship with [[sir-walter-bullivant|Sir Walter]] and his experience with German intelligence operations [001-046]. The [[greenmantle-concept|Greenmantle]] conspiracy is linked to the broader German strategy of leveraging Islamic unrest against the British Empire.
