# Talaat Pasha

Talaat Pasha was a leader of the Young Turk movement and a key figure in the Ottoman government during World War I. In *Greenmantle*, he appears as a background political figure mentioned alongside Enver Pasha. Blenkiron notes that Enver and Talaat's authority is contested in the provinces: "It is powerful in the provinces, and Enver and Talaat are not" [012-010].
