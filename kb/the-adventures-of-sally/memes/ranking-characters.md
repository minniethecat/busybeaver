# Characters by Importance

Not all characters are equally central to the story. This page ranks every character in *The Adventures of Sally* by their **total reference count** — a proxy for narrative importance derived from how often other wiki entries link to them.

::: query
type: person
sort: total_refs
order: desc
display: table
fields: [label, total_refs, total_chapters, quality]
field_labels:
  label: Character
  total_refs: Total Refs
  total_chapters: Chapters
  quality: Quality
:::

## Understanding the Rankings

Reference counts come from wikilinks: when another page mentions a character by linking to their entry, that counts as one reference. Characters linked from many different pages — and spanning many chapters — are typically the most significant to the novel's plot and themes.

For an alphabetical listing of all characters, see the [Character Index](character-index).
