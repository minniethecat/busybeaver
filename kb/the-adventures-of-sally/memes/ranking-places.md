# Places by Importance

From the boarding houses of New York to the casino at Roville-sur-Mer, locations shape the story of *The Adventures of Sally*. This page ranks every place by its **total reference count**, revealing which settings are most woven into the narrative.

::: query

## Understanding the Rankings

Reference counts are computed from wikilinks between pages. A place with many incoming links is frequently mentioned across the wiki — a strong signal that it matters to the story. Chapter spread distinguishes locations that appear briefly but memorably from those that recur throughout the novel.
