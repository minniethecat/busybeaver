# Loco

Loco was an Apache warrior whose name appears in [[geronimo|Geronimo]]'s autobiography in two distinct contexts: first, as a member of the close-knit band of warriors that included Geronimo himself, and second, as one of the Apache scouts who served in later campaigns.

## Warrior Band

In the chapter recounting the [[kas-ki-yeh-massacre|Kas-Ki-Yeh]] tragedy, Geronimo lists the warriors who accompanied him. Loco appears among this inner circle:

> Geronimo, Nanne, Ozone, Chihuahua, Loco. (006-010)

This enumeration places Loco within Geronimo's immediate warrior cohort — the men he trusted and fought alongside during the period of intense raiding and warfare that followed the massacre of his family. The inclusion of Loco's name in this short, declarative list suggests he was a recognized member of Geronimo's fighting band during one of the most consequential phases of the Apache resistance.

## Service as a Scout

Loco's name also appears in a later context as one of the Apache scouts. In the account of successful raids, Geronimo records:

> Apache scouts: Natchie, Goody, John Loco, Porico, Jasen. (008-006)

The designation "John Loco" — with the addition of the Anglo given name "John" — reflects the common practice among Apache men who served as U.S. Army scouts of adopting or being assigned English names alongside their Apache names. The role of Apache scouts was complex: they served the U.S. military while maintaining tribal loyalties, often caught between two worlds during the Apache Wars.

## Significance in the Narrative

Loco is not a major figure in Geronimo's autobiography, but his dual appearances — first as a warrior in Geronimo's band and later as a named scout — illustrate the trajectory of many Apache fighters during this period. Men who began as resistance fighters under leaders like Geronimo and [[mangus-colorado|Mangus-Colorado]] sometimes later served as scouts for the very forces they had fought against, a pattern driven by survival, pragmatism, and the shifting circumstances of the Apache Wars.

## Key Events

- **Post-Kas-Ki-Yeh period** — Fights as a member of Geronimo's warrior band (006-010)
- **Raiding campaigns** — Serves as an Apache scout alongside Natchie, Goody, Porico, and Jasen (008-006)
