# About This Wiki

## Source Material

This wiki is built from *Il Principe* (*The Prince*) by Niccolò Machiavelli, written in 1513 and published in 1532. It is one of the most influential works of political philosophy in Western history, examining how rulers acquire and maintain political power.

## Usage Notes

This knowledge base provides structured entries for concepts, people, events, and organizations found in or related to *The Prince*. Chapter pages present the original text with paragraph numbering (PN) for precise citation. Entity pages offer analysis and cross-references.
