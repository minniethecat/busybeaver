# The Gathering at Gault

The novel begins with dispersal and converges toward gathering. Characters arrive at Gault from London, Paris, New York, Tollerdown — each bringing their own history, their own desires, their own relationship to the place. "The transfer of Picus and Clarence for an indefinite stay" (003-038): the gathering is at once a social arrangement and a mythic assembly, the Grail company convening in the modern Grail castle.

## The Event

The gathering is less a single event than a process — arrivals spread across the novel's early chapters (I–V). Picus and Clarence come from Tollerdown. Carston comes from London via Paris and New York. The doctor comes from Starn. Each arrival adds a new element to the Gault House chemistry, and the novel's central drama is what happens when these elements combine.
