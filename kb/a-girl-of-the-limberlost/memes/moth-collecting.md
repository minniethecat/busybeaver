# Moth Collecting

## Definition

Moth collecting is the practice of finding, capturing, preserving, and cataloging moths (Lepidoptera). In *A Girl of [[limberlost-as-symbol|the Limberlost]]*, it serves as both [[elnora-comstock|Elnora Comstock]]'s primary source of income and a symbol of her connection to the natural world. (001-001)

## Role in the Story

[[elnora-comstock|Elnora Comstock]] begins collecting moths as a way to pay for her [[education-as-liberation|high school]] [[education-as-liberation|education]]. Her mother, [[katharine-comstock|Katharine Comstock]], initially opposes this activity but later comes to appreciate its value. (001-045)

The [[limberlost-swamp|Limberlost Swamp]] provides a rich habitat for rare and beautiful moths, particularly the Yellow Emperor moth (*Eacles imperialis*) and the Luna moth (*Actias luna*). [[elnora-comstock|Elnora]]'s expertise in finding and preserving these specimens earns her the respect of the [[bird-woman|Bird Woman]] and other collectors. (002-120)

Moth collecting represents [[womanhood-and-gender-roles|Elnora's independence]], intelligence, and determination. It connects her to the natural world and provides her with financial independence at a time when young women had few opportunities. (005-230)

## Related Characters

- **[[elnora-comstock|Elnora Comstock]]** — Expert moth collector, uses moth sales to fund her [[education-as-liberation|education]] (001-001)
- **[[bird-woman|The Bird Woman]]** — Mentor who teaches [[elnora-comstock|Elnora]] scientific methods of preservation (003-050)
- **[[philip-ammon|Philip Ammon]]** — Initially skeptical of moth collecting, later becomes [[elnora-comstock|Elnora]]'s partner in exploration (012-100)

## Themes

### Nature and Self-Reliance
The [[limberlost-swamp|Limberlost Swamp]] symbolizes both danger and opportunity. [[elnora-comstock|Elnora]]'s ability to navigate and harvest from [[limberlost-swamp|the swamp]] demonstrates her [[work-and-self-reliance|self-reliance]] and deep understanding of the natural world. (004-300)

### Education and Social Mobility
Moth collecting provides [[elnora-comstock|Elnora]] with the means to attend [[education-as-liberation|high school]], bridging the gap between her rural poverty and the world of [[education-as-liberation|education]] and culture. (001-055)
