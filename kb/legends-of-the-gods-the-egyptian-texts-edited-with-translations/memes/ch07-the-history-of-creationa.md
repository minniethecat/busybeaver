## THE BOOK OF KNOWING THE EVOLUTIONS[FN#49] OF RA, AND OF OVERTHROWING APEP.

[FN#49] Kheperu. The verb Kheper means "to make, to form, to produce,
[007-001] to become, and to roll;" kheperu here means "the things which come into
[007-002] being through the rollings of the ball of the god Kheper (the roller),"
[007-003] i.e., the Sun.

[007-004] [These are] the words which the god Neb-er-tcher spake after he had
[007-005] come into being:—"I am he who came into being in the form of the god
[007-006] Khepera, and I am the creator of that which came into being, that is to
[007-007] say, I am the creator of everything which came into being: now the
[007-008] things which I created, and which came forth out of my month after that
[007-009] I had come into being myself were exceedingly many. The sky (or
[007-010] heaven) had not come into being, the earth did not exist, and the
[007-011] children of the earth[FN#50], and the creeping, things, had not been
[007-012] made at that time. I myself raised them up from out of Nu[FN#51], from
[007-013] a state of helpless inertness. I found no place whereon I could stand.
[007-014] I worked a charm[FN#52] upon my own heart (or, will), I laid the
[007-015] foundation [of things] by Maat,[FN#53] and I made everything which had
[007-016] form. I was [then] one by myself, for I had not emitted from myself
[007-017] the god Shu, and I had not spit out from myself the goddess Tefnut; and
[007-018] there existed no other who could work with me. I laid the foundations
[007-019] [of things] in my own heart, and there came into being multitudes of
[007-020] created things, which came into being from the created things which
[007-021] were born from the created things which arose from what they brought
[007-022] forth. I had union with my closed hand, and I embraced my shadow as a
[007-023] wife, and I poured seed into my own mouth, and I sent forth from myself
[007-024] issue in the form of the gods Shu and Tefnut. Saith my father Nu:—My
[007-025] Eye was covered up behind them (i.e., Shu. and Tefnut), but after two
[007-026] hen periods had passed from the time when they departed from me, from
[007-027] being one god I became three gods, and I came into being in the earth.
[007-028] Then Shu and Tefnut rejoiced from out of the inert watery mass wherein
[007-029] they I were, and they brought to me my Eye (i.e., the Sun). Now after
[007-030] these things I gathered together my members, and I wept over them, and
[007-031] men and women sprang into being from the tears which came forth from my
[007-032] Eye. And when my Eye came to me, and found that I had made another
[007-033] [Eye] in place where it was (i.e., the Moon), it was wroth with (or,
[007-034] raged at) me, whereupon I endowed it (i.e., the second Eye) with [some
[007-035] of] the splendour which I had made for the first [Eye], and I made it
[007-036] to occupy its place in my Face, and henceforth it ruled throughout all
[007-037] this earth."

[FN#50] i.e., serpents and snakes, or perhaps plants.

[FN#51] The primeval watery mass which was the source and origin of
[007-038] all beings and things.

[FN#52] i.e., he uttered a magical formula.

[FN#53] i.e., by exact and definite rules.

[007-039] "When there fell on them their moment[FN#54] through plant-like clouds,
[007-040] I restored what had been taken away from them, and I appeared from out
[007-041] of the plant-like clouds. I created creeping things of every kind, and
[007-042] everything which came into being from them. Shu and Tefnut brought
[007-043] forth [Seb and] Nut; and Seb and Nut brought forth Osiris, and Heru-
[007-044] khent-an-maati,[FN#55] and Set, and Isis, and Nephthys[FN#56] at one
[007-045] birth, one after the other, and they produced their multitudinous
[007-046] offspring in this earth."

[FN#54] i.e., the period of calamity wherein their light was veiled
[007-047] through plant-like clouds.

[FN#55] i.e., the Blind Horus.

[FN#56] i.e., these five gods were all born at one time.
