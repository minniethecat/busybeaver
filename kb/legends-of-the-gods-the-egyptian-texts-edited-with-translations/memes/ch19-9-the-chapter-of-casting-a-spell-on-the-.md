[019-001] Recite [the following formula]:—

[019-002] "Hail, Ra, come to thy daughter! A scorpion hath stung her on a
[019-003] lonely road. Her cry hath penetrated the heights of heaven, and is
[019-004] heard along the paths. The poison hath entered into her body, and
[019-005] circulateth through her flesh. She hath set her mouth against
[019-006] it;[FN#200] verily the poison is in her members.

[FN#200] i.e., she hath directed her words against it.

[019-007] "Come then with thy strength, with thy fierce attack, and with thy red
[019-008] powers, and force it to be hidden before thee. Behold, the poison hath
[019-009] entered into all the members of this Cat which is under my fingers. Be
[019-010] not afraid, be not afraid, my daughter, my splendour, [for] I have set
[019-011] myself near (or, behind) thee. I have overthrown the poison which is
[019-012] in all the limbs of this Cat. O thou Cat, thy head is the head of Ra,
[019-013] the Lord of the Two Lands, the smiter of the rebellious peoples.
[019-014] Thy[FN#201] fear is in all lands, O Lord of the living, Lord of
[019-015] eternity. O thou Cat, thy two eyes are the Eye of the Lord of the Khut
[019-016] uraeus, who illumineth the Two Lands with his Eye, and illumineth the
[019-017] face on the path of darkness. O thou Cat, thy nose is the nose of
[019-018] Thoth, the Twice Great, Lord of Khemenu (Hermopolis), the Chief of the
[019-019] Two Lands of Ra, who putteth breath into the nostrils of every person.
[019-020] O thou Cat, thine ears are the ears of Nebertcher, who hearkeneth unto
[019-021] the voice of all persons when they appeal to him, and weigheth words
[019-022] (i.e., judgeth) in all the earth. O thou Cat, thy mouth is the mouth
[019-023] of Tem, the Lord of life, the uniter (?) of creation, who hath caused
[019-024] the union (?) of creation; he shall deliver thee from every poison. O
[019-025] thou Cat, thy neck (nehebt) is the neck of Neheb-ka, President of the
[019-026] Great House, vivifier of men and women by means of the mouth of his two
[019-027] arms. O thou Cat, thy breast is the breast of Thoth, the Lord of
[019-028] Truth, who hath given to thee breath to refresh (?) thy throat, and
[019-029] hath given breath to that which is therein. O thou Cat, thy heart is
[019-030] the heart of the god Ptah, who healeth thy heart of the evil poison
[019-031] which is in all thy limbs. O thou Cat, thy hands 25 are the hands of
[019-032] the Great Company of the gods and the Little Company of the gods, and
[019-033] they shall deliver thy hand from the poison from the mouth of every
[019-034] serpent. O thou Cat, thy belly is the belly of Osiris, Lord of
[019-035] Busiris, the poison shall not work any of its wishes in thy belly. O
[019-036] thou Cat, thy thighs are the thighs of the god Menthu, who shall make
[019-037] thy thighs to stand up, and shall bring the poison to the ground. O
[019-038] thou Cat, thy leg-bones are the leg-bones of Khensu,[FN#202] who
[019-039] travelleth over all the Two Lands by day and by night, and shall lead
[019-040] the poison to the ground. O thou Cat, thy legs (or, feet) are the legs
[019-041] of Amen the Great, Horus, Lord of Thebes, who shall stablish thy feet
[019-042] on the earth, and shall overthrow the poison. O thou Cat, thy haunches
[019-043] are the haunches of Horus, the avenger (or, advocate) of his father
[019-044] Osiris, and they shall place Set in the evil which he hath wrought. O
[019-045] thou Cat, thy soles are the soles of Ra, who shall make the poison to
[019-046] return to the earth. O thou Cat, thy bowels are the bowels of the Cow-
[019-047] goddess Meh-urt, who shall overthrow and cut in pieces the poison which
[019-048] is in thy belly and in all the members in thee, and in [all] the
[019-049] members of the gods in heaven, and in [all] the members of the gods on
[019-050] earth, and shall overthrow every poison in thee. There is no member in
[019-051] thee without the goddess who shall overthrow and cut in pieces the
[019-052] poison of every male serpent, and every female serpent, and every
[019-053] scorpion, and every reptile, which may be in any member of this Cat
[019-054] which is under the knife. Verily Isis weaveth and Nephthys spinneth
[019-055] against the poison. This woven garment strengtheneth this [being,
[019-056] i.e., Horus], who is perfect in words of power, through the speech of
[019-057] Ra Heru-khuti, the great god, President of the South and North: 'O evil
[019-058] poison which is in any member of this Cat which is under the knife,
[019-059] come, issue forth upon the earth.'"

[FN#201] Literally "his."

[FN#202] He was the messenger of the gods, and travelled across the
[019-060] sky under the form of the Moon; he sometimes appears as a form of
[019-061] Thoth.
