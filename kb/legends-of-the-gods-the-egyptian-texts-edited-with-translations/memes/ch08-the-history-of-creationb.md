### THE BOOK OF KNOWING THE EVOLUTIONS OF RA, AND OF OVERTHROWING APEP.

[008-001] [These are] the words of the god Neb-er-tcher, who said: "I am the
[008-002] creator of what hath come into being, and I myself came into being
[008-003] under the form of the god Khepera, and I came into being in primeval
[008-004] time. I came into being in the form of Khepera, and I am the creator
[008-005] of what did come into being, that is to say, I formed myself out of the
[008-006] primeval matter, and I made and formed myself out of the substance
[008-007] which existed in primeval time. My name is AUSARES (i.e., Osiris), who
[008-008] is the primeval matter of primeval matter. I have done my will in
[008-009] everything in this earth. I have spread myself abroad therein, and I
[008-010] have made strong my hand. I was ONE by myself, for they (i.e., the
[008-011] gods) had not been brought forth, and I had emitted from myself neither
[008-012] Shu nor Tefnut. I brought my own name[FN#57] into my mouth as a word
[008-013] of power, and I forthwith came into being under the form of things
[008-014] which are and under the form of Khepera. I came into being from out of
[008-015] primeval matter, and from the beginning I appeared under the form of
[008-016] the multitudinous things which exist; nothing whatsoever existed at
[008-017] that time in this earth, and it was I who made whatsoever was made. I
[008-018] was ONE: by myself, and there was no other being who worked with me in
[008-019] that place. I made all the things under the forms of which I appeared
[008-020] then by means of the Soul-God which I raised into firmness at that time
[008-021] from out of Nu, from a state of inactivity. I found no place
[008-022] whatsoever there whereon I could stand, I worked by the power of a
[008-023] spell by means of my heart, I laid a foundation [for things] before me,
[008-024] and whatsoever was made, I made. I was ONE by myself, and I laid the
[008-025] foundation of things [by means of] my heart, and I made the other
[008-026] things which came into being, and the things of Khepera which were made
[008-027] were manifold, and their offspring came into existence from the things
[008-028] to which they gave birth. I it was who emitted Shu, and I it was who
[008-029] emitted Tefnut, and from being the ONE, god (or, the only god) I became
[008-030] three gods; the two other gods who came into being on this earth sprang
[008-031] from me, and Shu and Tefnut rejoiced (or, were raised up) from out of
[008-032] Nu in which they were. Now behold, they brought my Eye to me after two
[008-033] hen periods since the time when they went forth from me. I gathered
[008-034] together my members which had appeared in my own body, and afterwards
[008-035] I had union with my hand, and my heart (or, will) came unto me from out
[008-036] of my hand, and the seed fell into my mouth, and I emitted from myself
[008-037] the gods Shu and Tefnut, and so from being the ONE god (or, the only,
[008-038] god) I became three gods; thus the two other gods who came into being
[008-039] on this earth sprang from me, and Shu and Tefnut rejoiced (or, were
[008-040] raised up) from out of Nu in which they were. My father Nu saith:—
[008-041] They covered up (or, concealed) my Eye with the plant-like clouds which
[008-042] were behind them (i.e., Shu and Tefnut) for very many hen periods.
[008-043] Plants and creeping things [sprang up] from the god REM, through the
[008-044] tears which I let fall. I cried out to my Eye, and men and women came
[008-045] into existence. Then I bestowed upon my Eye the uraeus of fire, and it
[008-046] was wroth with me when another Eye (i.e., the Moon) came and grew up in
[008-047] its place; its vigorous power fell on the plants, on the plants which I
[008-048] had placed there, and it set order among them, and it took up its place
[008-049] in my face, and it doth rule the whole earth. Then Shu and Tefnut
[008-050] brought forth Osiris, and Heru-khenti-an-maa, and Set, and Isis, and
[008-051] Nephthys and behold, they have produced offspring, and have created
[008-052] multitudinous children in this earth, by means of the beings which came
[008-053] into existence from the creatures which they produced. They invoke my
[008-054] name, and they overthrow their enemies, and they make words of power
[008-055] for the overthrowing of Apep, over whose hands and arms AKER keepeth
[008-056] ward. His hands and arms shall not exist, his feet and leas shall not
[008-057] exist, and he is chained in one place whilst Ra inflicts upon him the
[008-058] blows which are decreed for him. He is thrown upon his accursed back,
[008-059] his face is slit open by reason of the evil which he hath done, and he
[008-060] shall remain upon his accursed back."

[FN#57] i.e., I uttered my own name from my own mouth as a word of
[008-061] power.
