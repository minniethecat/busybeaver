### CHAPTER II.

[010-001] II. This Chapter shall be said over [a figure of] the cow.—The
[010-002] supporters [called] Heh-enti shall be by her shoulder. The supporters
[010-003] [called] Heh-enti shall be at her side, and one cubit and four spans of
[010-004] hers shall be in colours, and nine stars shall be on her belly, and Set
[010-005] shall be by her two thighs and shall keep watch before her two legs,
[010-006] and before her two legs shall be Shu, under her belly, and he shall be
[010-007] made (i.e., painted) in green qenat colour. His two arms shall be under
[010-008] the stars, and his name shall be made (i.e., written) in the middle of
[010-009] them, namely, Shu himself. "A boat with a rudder and a double shrine
[010-010] shall be therein, and Aten (i.e., the Disk) shall be above it, and Ra
[010-011] shall be in it, in front of Shu, near his hand, or, as another reading
[010-012] hath, behind him, near his hand. And the udders of the Cow shall be
[010-013] made to be between her legs, towards the left side. And on the two
[010-014] flanks, towards the middle of the legs, shall be done in writing [the
[010-015] words], "The exterior heaven," and "I am what is in me," and "I will
[010-016] not permit them to make her to turn." That which is [written] under
[010-017] the boat which is in front shall read, "Thou shalt not be motionless,
[010-018] my son;" and the words which are written in an opposite direction shall
[010-019] read, "Thy support is like life," and "The word is as the word there,"
[010-020] and "Thy son is with me," and "Life, strength, and health be to thy
[010-021] nostrils!" And that which is behind Shu, near his shoulder, shall
[010-022] read, "They keep ward," and that which is behind him, written close to
[010-023] his feet in an opposite direction, shall read, "Maat," and "They come
[010-024] in," and "I protect daily." And that which is under the shoulder of
[010-025] the divine figure which is under the left leg, and is behind it shall
[010-026] read, "He who sealeth all things." That which is over his head, under
[010-027] the thighs of the Cow, and that which is by her legs shall read,
[010-028] "Guardian of his exit." That which is behind the two figures which are
[010-029] by her two legs, that is to say, over their heads, shall read, "The
[010-030] Aged One who is adored as he goeth forth," and The Aged One to whom
[010-031] praise is given when he goeth in." That which is over the head of the
[010-032] two figures, and is between the two thighs of the Cow, shall read,
[010-033] "Listener," "Hearer," "Sceptre of the Upper Heaven," and "Star" (?).
