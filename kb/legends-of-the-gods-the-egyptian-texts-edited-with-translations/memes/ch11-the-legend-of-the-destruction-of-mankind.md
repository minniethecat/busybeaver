### CHAPTER III.

[011-001] III. Then the majesty of this god spake unto Thoth, [saying] "Let a
[011-002] call go forth for me to the Majesty of the god Seb, saying, 'Come, with
[011-003] the utmost speed, at once."' And when the Majesty of Seb had come, the
[011-004] Majesty of this god said unto him, "Let war be made against thy worms
[011-005] (or, serpents) which are in thee; verily, they shall have fear of me as
[011-006] long as I have being; but thou knowest their magical powers. Do thou
[011-007] go to the place where my father Nu is, and say thou unto him, 'Keep
[011-008] ward over the worms (or, serpents) which are in the earth and water.'
[011-009] And moreover, thou shalt make a writing for each of the nests of thy
[011-010] serpents which are there, saying, 'Keep ye guard [lest ye] cause injury
[011-011] to anything.' They shall know that I am removing myself [from them],
[011-012] but indeed I shall shine upon them. Since, however, they indeed wish
[011-013] for a father, thou shalt be a father unto them in this land for ever.
[011-014] Moreover, let good heed be taken to the men who have my words of power,
[011-015] and to those whose mouths have knowledge of such things; verily my own
[011-016] words of power are there, verily it shall not happen that any shall
[011-017] participate with me in my protection, by reason of the majesty which
[011-018] hath come into being before me. I will decree them to thy son Osiris,
[011-019] and their children shall be watched over, the hearts of their princes
[011-020] shall be obedient (or, ready) by reason of the magical powers of those
[011-021] who act according to their desire in all the earth through their words
[011-022] of power which are in their bodies."
