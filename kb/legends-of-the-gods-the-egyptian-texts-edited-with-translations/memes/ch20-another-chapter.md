[020-001] Say the [following] words:—

[020-002] "O Ra-[Khuti], come to thy daughter. O Shu, come to thy wife. O Isis,
[020-003] come to thy sister, and deliver her from the evil poison which is in
[020-004] all her members. Hail, O ye gods, come ye and overthrow ye the evil
[020-005] poison which is in all the members of the Cat which is under the knife.

[020-006] "Hail, O aged one, who renewest thy youth in thy season, thou old man
[020-007] who makest thyself to be a boy, grant thou that Thoth may come to me at
[020-008] [the sound of] my voice, and behold, let him turn back from me Netater.
[020-009] Osiris is on the water, the Eye of Horus is with him. A great
[020-010] Beetle spreadeth himself over him, great by reason of his grasp,
[020-011] produced by the gods from a child. He who is over the water appeareth
[020-012] in a healthy form. If he who is over the water shall be approached
[020-013] (or, attacked), the Eye of Horus, which weepeth, shall be approached.

[020-014] "Get ye back, O ye who dwell in the water, crocodiles, fish, that
[020-015] Enemy, male dead person and female dead person, male fiend and female
[020-016] fiend, of every kind whatsoever, lift not up your faces, O ye who dwell
[020-017] in the waters, ye crocodiles and fish. When Osiris journeyeth over
[020-018] you, permit ye him to go to Busiris. Let your nostrils [be closed],
[020-019] your throats stopped up.

[020-020] "Get ye back, Seba fiends! Lift ye not up your faces against him that
[020-021] is on the water . . . . . Osiris-Ra, riseth up in his Boat to look at
[020-022] the gods of Kher-ahat, and the Lords of the Tuat stand up to slay thee
[020-023] when [thou] comest, O Neha-her, against Osiris. [When] he is on the
[020-024] water the Eye of Horus is over him to turn your faces upside down and
[020-025] to set you on your backs.

[020-026] "Hail, ye who dwell in the water, crocodiles and fish, Ra shutteth up
[020-027] your mouths, Sekhet stoppeth up your throats, Thoth cutteth out your
[020-028] tongues, and {cont} Heka blindeth your eyes. These are the four great
[020-029] gods who protect Osiris by their magical power, and they effect the
[020-030] protection of him that is on the water, of men and women of every kind,
[020-031] and of beasts and animals of every kind which are on the water by day.
[020-032] Protected are those who dwell in the waters, protected is the sky
[020-033] wherein is Ra, protected is the great god who is in the sarcophagus,
[020-034] protected is he who is on the water.

[020-035] "A voice [which] crieth loudly is in the House of Net (Neith), a loud
[020-036] voice is in the Great House, a great outcry from the mouth of the Cat.
[020-037] The gods and the goddesses say, 'What is it? What is it?' [It]
[020-038] concerneth the Abtu Fish which is born. Make to retreat from me thy
[020-039] footsteps, O Sebau fiend. I am Khnemu, the Lord of Her-urt. Guard
[020-040] thyself again from the attack which is repeated, besides this which
[020-041] thou hast done in the presence of the Great Company of the gods. Get
[020-042] thee back, retreat thou from me. I am the god. Oh, Oh, O [Ra], hast
[020-043] thou not heard the voice which cried out loudly until the evening on
[020-044] the bank of Netit, the voice of all the gods and goddesses which cried
[020-045] out loudly, the outcry concerning the wickedness which thou hast done,
[020-046] O wicked Sebau fiend? Verily the lord Ra thundered and growled
[020-047] thereat, and he ordered thy slaughter to be carried out. Get thee
[020-048] back, Seba fiend! Hail! Hail!"
