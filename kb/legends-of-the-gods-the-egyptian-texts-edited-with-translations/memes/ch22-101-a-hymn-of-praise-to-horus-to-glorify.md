[022-006] speak with thy magical utterance. I pronounce a spell in thine own
[022-007] words, which thy heart hath created, and all the spells and
[022-008] incantations which have come forth from thy mouth, which thy father Keb

[022-012] shut the mouth of every reptile which is in heaven, and on the earth,
[022-013] and in the waters, to make men and women to live, to make the gods to
[022-014] be at peace [with thee], and to make Ra to employ his magical spells
[022-015] through thy chants of praise. Come to me this day, quickly, quickly,
[022-016] as thou workest the paddle of the Boat of the god. Drive thou away
[022-017] from me every lion on the plain, and every crocodile in the waters, and
[022-018] all mouths which bite (or, sting) in their holes. Make thou them
[022-019] before me like the stone of the mountain, like a broken pot lying about
[022-020] in a quarter of the town. Dig thou out from me the poison which riseth
[022-021] and is in every member of him that is under the knife. Keep thou watch

[022-023] invoked this day. Let thy power (qefau) come into being in him. Exalt

[022-025] closed up. Then shall mankind give thee praise, and the righteous (?)
[022-026] shall give thanks unto thy forms. And all the gods likewise shall
[022-027] invoke thee, and in truth thy name shall be invoked this day. I am
[022-028] Horus [of] Shet[enu] (?).
