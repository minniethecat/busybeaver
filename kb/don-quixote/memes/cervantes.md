# Miguel de Cervantes

*Stub page — full article pending.*

Miguel de Cervantes Saavedra (1547–1616) was a Spanish novelist, poet, and playwright, widely regarded as the greatest writer in the Spanish language. His experiences as a soldier (wounded at Lepanto, 1571) and as a captive in Algiers (1575–1580) are reflected throughout *Don Quixote*, most notably in the Captive's Tale.
