[030-003]then, señor licentiate, he who did that deed was my master; and it was

[030-004]not for want of my telling him beforehand and warning him to mind what

[030-005]he was about, and that it was a sin to set them at liberty, as they

[030-006]were all on the march there because they were special scoundrels.”

[030-008]knights-errant to inquire whether any persons in affliction, in chains,

[030-010]suffer as they do because of their faults or because of their

[030-011]misfortunes. It only concerns them to aid them as persons in need of

[030-012]help, having regard to their sufferings and not to their rascalities. I

[030-013]encountered a chaplet or string of miserable and unfortunate people,

[030-016]sacred dignity of the señor licentiate and his honoured person, I say

[030-034]plucked off a moustache.”

[030-125]“That is what I meant to say,” said Dorothea.
