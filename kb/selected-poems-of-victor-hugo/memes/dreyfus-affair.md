## Overview

The **Dreyfus Affair** (1894–1906) was the historical scandal that Anatole France satirizes through **[[the-affair|The Affair of the Eighty Thousand Bundles of Hay]]** in Livre VI of *Penguin Island*. France, a committed Dreyfusard, transposes the case into the Penguin setting: [[pyrot|Pyrot]] stands for Alfred Dreyfus, [[greatauk|Greatauk]] for the French general staff, and the eighty thousand bundles of hay for the *bordereau*.

For the full analysis of the satirical retelling, see the main article.
