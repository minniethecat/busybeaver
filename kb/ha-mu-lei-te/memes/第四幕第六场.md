# 第四幕 第六场

[004-001] the Castle.

[004-002] Enter Horatio and a Servant.

[004-003] HORATIO.

[004-004] What are they that would speak with me?

[004-005] SERVANT.

[004-006] Sailors, sir. They say they have letters for you.

[004-007] HORATIO.

[004-008] Let them come in.

[004-009] [Exit Servant.]

[004-010] I do not know from what part of the world

[004-011] I should be greeted, if not from Lord Hamlet.

[004-012] Enter Sailors.

[004-013] FIRST SAILOR.

[004-014] God bless you, sir.

[004-015] HORATIO.

[004-016] Let him bless thee too.

[004-017] FIRST SAILOR.

[004-018] He shall, sir, and’t please him. There’s a letter for you, sir. It comes from

[004-019] th’ambassador that was bound for England; if your name be Horatio, as I am let

[004-020] to know it is.

[004-021] HORATIO.

[004-022] [Reads.] ‘Horatio, when thou shalt have overlooked this, give these

[004-023] fellows some means to the King. They have letters for him. Ere we were two days

[004-024] old at sea, a pirate of very warlike appointment gave us chase. Finding

[004-025] ourselves too slow of sail, we put on a compelled valour, and in the grapple I

[004-026] boarded them. On the instant they got clear of our ship, so I alone became

[004-027] their prisoner. They have dealt with me like thieves of mercy. But they knew

[004-028] what they did; I am to do a good turn for them. Let the King have the letters I

[004-029] have sent, and repair thou to me with as much haste as thou wouldst fly death.

[004-030] I have words to speak in thine ear will make thee dumb; yet are they much too

[004-031] light for the bore of the matter. These good fellows will bring thee where I

[004-032] am. Rosencrantz and Guildenstern hold their course for England: of them I have

[004-033] much to tell thee. Farewell.

[004-034] He that thou knowest thine,

[004-035] HAMLET.’

[004-036] Come, I will give you way for these your letters,

[004-037] And do’t the speedier, that you may direct me

[004-038] To him from whom you brought them.

[004-039] [Exeunt.]
