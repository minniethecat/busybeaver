# 第一幕 第三场

[001-001] Polonius’s house.

[001-002] Enter Laertes and Ophelia.

[001-003] LAERTES.

[001-004] My necessaries are embark’d. Farewell.

[001-005] And, sister, as the winds give benefit

[001-006] And convoy is assistant, do not sleep,

[001-007] But let me hear from you.

[001-008] OPHELIA.

[001-009] Do you doubt that?

[001-010] LAERTES.

[001-011] For Hamlet, and the trifling of his favour,

[001-012] Hold it a fashion and a toy in blood;

[001-013] A violet in the youth of primy nature,

[001-014] Forward, not permanent, sweet, not lasting;

[001-015] The perfume and suppliance of a minute;

[001-016] No more.

[001-017] OPHELIA.

[001-018] No more but so?

[001-019] LAERTES.

[001-020] Think it no more.

[001-021] For nature crescent does not grow alone

[001-022] In thews and bulk; but as this temple waxes,

[001-023] The inward service of the mind and soul

[001-024] Grows wide withal. Perhaps he loves you now,

[001-025] And now no soil nor cautel doth besmirch

[001-026] The virtue of his will; but you must fear,

[001-027] His greatness weigh’d, his will is not his own;

[001-028] For he himself is subject to his birth:

[001-029] He may not, as unvalu’d persons do,

[001-030] Carve for himself; for on his choice depends

[001-031] The sanctity and health of this whole state;

[001-032] And therefore must his choice be circumscrib’d

[001-033] Unto the voice and yielding of that body

[001-034] Whereof he is the head. Then if he says he loves you,

[001-035] It fits your wisdom so far to believe it

[001-036] As he in his particular act and place

[001-037] May give his saying deed; which is no further

[001-038] Than the main voice of Denmark goes withal.

[001-039] Then weigh what loss your honour may sustain

[001-040] If with too credent ear you list his songs,

[001-041] Or lose your heart, or your chaste treasure open

[001-042] To his unmaster’d importunity.

[001-043] Fear it, Ophelia, fear it, my dear sister;

[001-044] And keep you in the rear of your affection,

[001-045] Out of the shot and danger of desire.

[001-046] The chariest maid is prodigal enough

[001-047] If she unmask her beauty to the moon.

[001-048] Virtue itself ’scapes not calumnious strokes:

[001-049] The canker galls the infants of the spring

[001-050] Too oft before their buttons be disclos’d,

[001-051] And in the morn and liquid dew of youth

[001-052] Contagious blastments are most imminent.

[001-053] Be wary then, best safety lies in fear.

[001-054] Youth to itself rebels, though none else near.

[001-055] OPHELIA.

[001-056] I shall th’effect of this good lesson keep

[001-057] As watchman to my heart. But good my brother,

[001-058] Do not as some ungracious pastors do,

[001-059] Show me the steep and thorny way to heaven;

[001-060] Whilst like a puff’d and reckless libertine

[001-061] Himself the primrose path of dalliance treads,

[001-062] And recks not his own rede.

[001-063] LAERTES.

[001-064] O, fear me not.

[001-065] I stay too long. But here my father comes.

[001-066] Enter Polonius.

[001-067] A double blessing is a double grace;

[001-068] Occasion smiles upon a second leave.

[001-069] POLONIUS.

[001-070] Yet here, Laertes? Aboard, aboard, for shame.

[001-071] The wind sits in the shoulder of your sail,

[001-072] And you are stay’d for. There, my blessing with you.

[001-073] [Laying his hand on Laertes’s head.]

[001-074] And these few precepts in thy memory

[001-075] Look thou character. Give thy thoughts no tongue,

[001-076] Nor any unproportion’d thought his act.

[001-077] Be thou familiar, but by no means vulgar.

[001-078] Those friends thou hast, and their adoption tried,

[001-079] Grapple them unto thy soul with hoops of steel;

[001-080] But do not dull thy palm with entertainment

[001-081] Of each new-hatch’d, unfledg’d comrade. Beware

[001-082] Of entrance to a quarrel; but being in,

[001-083] Bear’t that th’opposed may beware of thee.

[001-084] Give every man thine ear, but few thy voice:

[001-085] Take each man’s censure, but reserve thy judgement.

[001-086] Costly thy habit as thy purse can buy,

[001-087] But not express’d in fancy; rich, not gaudy:

[001-088] For the apparel oft proclaims the man;

[001-089] And they in France of the best rank and station

[001-090] Are of a most select and generous chief in that.

[001-091] Neither a borrower nor a lender be:

[001-092] For loan oft loses both itself and friend;

[001-093] And borrowing dulls the edge of husbandry.

[001-094] This above all: to thine own self be true;

[001-095] And it must follow, as the night the day,

[001-096] Thou canst not then be false to any man.

[001-097] Farewell: my blessing season this in thee.

[001-098] LAERTES.

[001-099] Most humbly do I take my leave, my lord.

[001-100] POLONIUS.

[001-101] The time invites you; go, your servants tend.

[001-102] LAERTES.

[001-103] Farewell, Ophelia, and remember well

[001-104] What I have said to you.

[001-105] OPHELIA.

[001-106] ’Tis in my memory lock’d,

[001-107] And you yourself shall keep the key of it.

[001-108] LAERTES.

[001-109] Farewell.

[001-110] [Exit.]

[001-111] POLONIUS.

[001-112] What is’t, Ophelia, he hath said to you?

[001-113] OPHELIA.

[001-114] So please you, something touching the Lord Hamlet.

[001-115] POLONIUS.

[001-116] Marry, well bethought:

[001-117] ’Tis told me he hath very oft of late

[001-118] Given private time to you; and you yourself

[001-119] Have of your audience been most free and bounteous.

[001-120] If it be so,—as so ’tis put on me,

[001-121] And that in way of caution,—I must tell you

[001-122] You do not understand yourself so clearly

[001-123] As it behoves my daughter and your honour.

[001-124] What is between you? Give me up the truth.

[001-125] OPHELIA.

[001-126] He hath, my lord, of late made many tenders

[001-127] Of his affection to me.

[001-128] POLONIUS.

[001-129] Affection! Pooh! You speak like a green girl,

[001-130] Unsifted in such perilous circumstance.

[001-131] Do you believe his tenders, as you call them?

[001-132] OPHELIA.

[001-133] I do not know, my lord, what I should think.

[001-134] POLONIUS.

[001-135] Marry, I’ll teach you; think yourself a baby;

[001-136] That you have ta’en these tenders for true pay,

[001-137] Which are not sterling. Tender yourself more dearly;

[001-138] Or,—not to crack the wind of the poor phrase,

[001-139] Running it thus,—you’ll tender me a fool.

[001-140] OPHELIA.

[001-141] My lord, he hath importun’d me with love

[001-142] In honourable fashion.

[001-143] POLONIUS.

[001-144] Ay, fashion you may call it; go to, go to.

[001-145] OPHELIA.

[001-146] And hath given countenance to his speech, my lord,

[001-147] With almost all the holy vows of heaven.

[001-148] POLONIUS.

[001-149] Ay, springes to catch woodcocks. I do know,

[001-150] When the blood burns, how prodigal the soul

[001-151] Lends the tongue vows: these blazes, daughter,

[001-152] Giving more light than heat, extinct in both,

[001-153] Even in their promise, as it is a-making,

[001-154] You must not take for fire. From this time

[001-155] Be something scanter of your maiden presence;

[001-156] Set your entreatments at a higher rate

[001-157] Than a command to parley. For Lord Hamlet,

[001-158] Believe so much in him that he is young;

[001-159] And with a larger tether may he walk

[001-160] Than may be given you. In few, Ophelia,

[001-161] Do not believe his vows; for they are brokers,

[001-162] Not of that dye which their investments show,

[001-163] But mere implorators of unholy suits,

[001-164] Breathing like sanctified and pious bawds,

[001-165] The better to beguile. This is for all:

[001-166] I would not, in plain terms, from this time forth

[001-167] Have you so slander any moment leisure

[001-168] As to give words or talk with the Lord Hamlet.

[001-169] Look to’t, I charge you; come your ways.

[001-170] OPHELIA.

[001-171] I shall obey, my lord.

[001-172] [Exeunt.]
