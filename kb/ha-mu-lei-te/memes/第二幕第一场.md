# 第二幕 第一场

[002-001] Enter Polonius and Reynaldo.

[002-002] POLONIUS.

[002-003] Give him this money and these notes, Reynaldo.

[002-004] REYNALDO.

[002-005] I will, my lord.

[002-006] POLONIUS.

[002-007] You shall do marvellous wisely, good Reynaldo,

[002-008] Before you visit him, to make inquiry

[002-009] Of his behaviour.

[002-010] REYNALDO.

[002-011] My lord, I did intend it.

[002-012] POLONIUS.

[002-013] Marry, well said; very well said. Look you, sir,

[002-014] Enquire me first what Danskers are in Paris;

[002-015] And how, and who, what means, and where they keep,

[002-016] What company, at what expense; and finding

[002-017] By this encompassment and drift of question,

[002-018] That they do know my son, come you more nearer

[002-019] Than your particular demands will touch it.

[002-020] Take you as ’twere some distant knowledge of him,

[002-021] As thus, ‘I know his father and his friends,

[002-022] And in part him’—do you mark this, Reynaldo?

[002-023] REYNALDO.

[002-024] Ay, very well, my lord.

[002-025] POLONIUS.

[002-026] ‘And in part him, but,’ you may say, ‘not well;

[002-027] But if’t be he I mean, he’s very wild;

[002-028] Addicted so and so;’ and there put on him

[002-029] What forgeries you please; marry, none so rank

[002-030] As may dishonour him; take heed of that;

[002-031] But, sir, such wanton, wild, and usual slips

[002-032] As are companions noted and most known

[002-033] To youth and liberty.

[002-034] REYNALDO.

[002-035] As gaming, my lord?

[002-036] POLONIUS.

[002-037] Ay, or drinking, fencing, swearing,

[002-038] Quarrelling, drabbing. You may go so far.

[002-039] REYNALDO.

[002-040] My lord, that would dishonour him.

[002-041] POLONIUS.

[002-042] Faith no, as you may season it in the charge.

[002-043] You must not put another scandal on him,

[002-044] That he is open to incontinency;

[002-045] That’s not my meaning: but breathe his faults so quaintly

[002-046] That they may seem the taints of liberty;

[002-047] The flash and outbreak of a fiery mind,

[002-048] A savageness in unreclaimed blood,

[002-049] Of general assault.

[002-050] REYNALDO.

[002-051] But my good lord—

[002-052] POLONIUS.

[002-053] Wherefore should you do this?

[002-054] REYNALDO.

[002-055] Ay, my lord, I would know that.

[002-056] POLONIUS.

[002-057] Marry, sir, here’s my drift,

[002-058] And I believe it is a fetch of warrant.

[002-059] You laying these slight sullies on my son,

[002-060] As ’twere a thing a little soil’d i’ th’ working,

[002-061] Mark you,

[002-062] Your party in converse, him you would sound,

[002-063] Having ever seen in the prenominate crimes

[002-064] The youth you breathe of guilty, be assur’d

[002-065] He closes with you in this consequence;

[002-066] ‘Good sir,’ or so; or ‘friend,’ or ‘gentleman’—

[002-067] According to the phrase or the addition

[002-068] Of man and country.

[002-069] REYNALDO.

[002-070] Very good, my lord.

[002-071] POLONIUS.

[002-072] And then, sir, does he this,—

[002-073] He does—What was I about to say?

[002-074] By the mass, I was about to say something. Where did I leave?

[002-075] REYNALDO.

[002-076] At ‘closes in the consequence.’

[002-077] At ‘friend or so,’ and ‘gentleman.’

[002-078] POLONIUS.

[002-079] At ‘closes in the consequence’ ay, marry!

[002-080] He closes with you thus: ‘I know the gentleman,

[002-081] I saw him yesterday, or t’other day,

[002-082] Or then, or then, with such and such; and, as you say,

[002-083] There was he gaming, there o’ertook in’s rouse,

[002-084] There falling out at tennis’: or perchance,

[002-085] ‘I saw him enter such a house of sale’—

[002-086] Videlicet, a brothel, or so forth. See you now;

[002-087] Your bait of falsehood takes this carp of truth;

[002-088] And thus do we of wisdom and of reach,

[002-089] With windlasses, and with assays of bias,

[002-090] By indirections find directions out.

[002-091] So by my former lecture and advice

[002-092] Shall you my son. You have me, have you not?

[002-093] REYNALDO.

[002-094] My lord, I have.

[002-095] POLONIUS.

[002-096] God b’ wi’ you, fare you well.

[002-097] REYNALDO.

[002-098] Good my lord.

[002-099] POLONIUS.

[002-100] Observe his inclination in yourself.

[002-101] REYNALDO.

[002-102] I shall, my lord.

[002-103] POLONIUS.

[002-104] And let him ply his music.

[002-105] REYNALDO.

[002-106] Well, my lord.

[002-107] POLONIUS.

[002-108] Farewell.

[002-109] [Exit Reynaldo.]

[002-110] Enter Ophelia.

[002-111] How now, Ophelia, what’s the matter?

[002-112] OPHELIA.

[002-113] Alas, my lord, I have been so affrighted.

[002-114] POLONIUS.

[002-115] With what, in the name of God?

[002-116] OPHELIA.

[002-117] My lord, as I was sewing in my chamber,

[002-118] Lord Hamlet, with his doublet all unbrac’d,

[002-119] No hat upon his head, his stockings foul’d,

[002-120] Ungart’red, and down-gyved to his ankle,

[002-121] Pale as his shirt, his knees knocking each other,

[002-122] And with a look so piteous in purport

[002-123] As if he had been loosed out of hell

[002-124] To speak of horrors, he comes before me.

[002-125] POLONIUS.

[002-126] Mad for thy love?

[002-127] OPHELIA.

[002-128] My lord, I do not know, but truly I do fear it.

[002-129] POLONIUS.

[002-130] What said he?

[002-131] OPHELIA.

[002-132] He took me by the wrist and held me hard;

[002-133] Then goes he to the length of all his arm;

[002-134] And with his other hand thus o’er his brow,

[002-135] He falls to such perusal of my face

[002-136] As he would draw it. Long stay’d he so,

[002-137] At last,—a little shaking of mine arm,

[002-138] And thrice his head thus waving up and down,

[002-139] He rais’d a sigh so piteous and profound

[002-140] As it did seem to shatter all his bulk

[002-141] And end his being. That done, he lets me go,

[002-142] And with his head over his shoulder turn’d

[002-143] He seem’d to find his way without his eyes,

[002-144] For out o’ doors he went without their help,

[002-145] And to the last bended their light on me.

[002-146] POLONIUS.

[002-147] Come, go with me. I will go seek the King.

[002-148] This is the very ecstasy of love,

[002-149] Whose violent property fordoes itself,

[002-150] And leads the will to desperate undertakings,

[002-151] As oft as any passion under heaven

[002-152] That does afflict our natures. I am sorry,—

[002-153] What, have you given him any hard words of late?

[002-154] OPHELIA.

[002-155] No, my good lord; but as you did command,

[002-156] I did repel his letters and denied

[002-157] His access to me.

[002-158] POLONIUS.

[002-159] That hath made him mad.

[002-160] I am sorry that with better heed and judgement

[002-161] I had not quoted him. I fear’d he did but trifle,

[002-162] And meant to wreck thee. But beshrew my jealousy!

[002-163] It seems it is as proper to our age

[002-164] To cast beyond ourselves in our opinions

[002-165] As it is common for the younger sort

[002-166] To lack discretion. Come, go we to the King.

[002-167] This must be known, which, being kept close, might move

[002-168] More grief to hide than hate to utter love.

[002-169] [Exeunt.]
