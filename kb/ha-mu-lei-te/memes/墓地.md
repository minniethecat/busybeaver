# 墓地

## 地点概述

墓地场景是第五幕第一场。[[哈姆雷特]]与[[掘墓人]]的对话引出了"Alas, poor Yorick"的著名独白，[[奥菲莉娅]]亦在此下葬。

## 相关场景

- 第001幕（001-177）：*…ghost, my lord, come from the grave...*
- 第003幕（003-299）：*… still, most secret, and most grave,...*
- 第002幕（002-286）：*Into my grave?...*
- 第004幕（004-087）：*Go to their graves like beds, fight for a plot...*
- 第004幕（004-245）：*And on his grave rain’d many a tear.—...*
