# 第三幕 第四场

[003-001] the Castle.

[003-002] Enter Queen and Polonius.

[003-003] POLONIUS.

[003-004] He will come straight. Look you lay home to him,

[003-005] Tell him his pranks have been too broad to bear with,

[003-006] And that your Grace hath screen’d and stood between

[003-007] Much heat and him. I’ll silence me e’en here.

[003-008] Pray you be round with him.

[003-009] HAMLET.

[003-010] [Within.] Mother, mother, mother.

[003-011] QUEEN.

[003-012] I’ll warrant you, Fear me not.

[003-013] Withdraw, I hear him coming.

[003-014] [Polonius goes behind the arras.]

[003-015] Enter Hamlet.

[003-016] HAMLET.

[003-017] Now, mother, what’s the matter?

[003-018] QUEEN.

[003-019] Hamlet, thou hast thy father much offended.

[003-020] HAMLET.

[003-021] Mother, you have my father much offended.

[003-022] QUEEN.

[003-023] Come, come, you answer with an idle tongue.

[003-024] HAMLET.

[003-025] Go, go, you question with a wicked tongue.

[003-026] QUEEN.

[003-027] Why, how now, Hamlet?

[003-028] HAMLET.

[003-029] What’s the matter now?

[003-030] QUEEN.

[003-031] Have you forgot me?

[003-032] HAMLET.

[003-033] No, by the rood, not so.

[003-034] You are the Queen, your husband’s brother’s wife,

[003-035] And, would it were not so. You are my mother.

[003-036] QUEEN.

[003-037] Nay, then I’ll set those to you that can speak.

[003-038] HAMLET.

[003-039] Come, come, and sit you down, you shall not budge.

[003-040] You go not till I set you up a glass

[003-041] Where you may see the inmost part of you.

[003-042] QUEEN.

[003-043] What wilt thou do? Thou wilt not murder me?

[003-044] Help, help, ho!

[003-045] POLONIUS.

[003-046] [Behind.] What, ho! help, help, help!

[003-047] HAMLET.

[003-048] How now? A rat? [Draws.]

[003-049] Dead for a ducat, dead!

[003-050] [Makes a pass through the arras.]

[003-051] POLONIUS.

[003-052] [Behind.] O, I am slain!

[003-053] [Falls and dies.]

[003-054] QUEEN.

[003-055] O me, what hast thou done?

[003-056] HAMLET.

[003-057] Nay, I know not. Is it the King?

[003-058] [Draws forth Polonius.]

[003-059] QUEEN.

[003-060] O what a rash and bloody deed is this!

[003-061] HAMLET.

[003-062] A bloody deed. Almost as bad, good mother,

[003-063] As kill a king and marry with his brother.

[003-064] QUEEN.

[003-065] As kill a king?

[003-066] HAMLET.

[003-067] Ay, lady, ’twas my word.—

[003-068] [To Polonius.] Thou wretched, rash, intruding fool, farewell!

[003-069] I took thee for thy better. Take thy fortune,

[003-070] Thou find’st to be too busy is some danger.—

[003-071] Leave wringing of your hands. Peace, sit you down,

[003-072] And let me wring your heart, for so I shall,

[003-073] If it be made of penetrable stuff;

[003-074] If damned custom have not braz’d it so,

[003-075] That it is proof and bulwark against sense.

[003-076] QUEEN.

[003-077] What have I done, that thou dar’st wag thy tongue

[003-078] In noise so rude against me?

[003-079] HAMLET.

[003-080] Such an act

[003-081] That blurs the grace and blush of modesty,

[003-082] Calls virtue hypocrite, takes off the rose

[003-083] From the fair forehead of an innocent love,

[003-084] And sets a blister there. Makes marriage vows

[003-085] As false as dicers’ oaths. O such a deed

[003-086] As from the body of contraction plucks

[003-087] The very soul, and sweet religion makes

[003-088] A rhapsody of words. Heaven’s face doth glow,

[003-089] Yea this solidity and compound mass,

[003-090] With tristful visage, as against the doom,

[003-091] Is thought-sick at the act.

[003-092] QUEEN.

[003-093] Ay me, what act,

[003-094] That roars so loud, and thunders in the index?

[003-095] HAMLET.

[003-096] Look here upon this picture, and on this,

[003-097] The counterfeit presentment of two brothers.

[003-098] See what a grace was seated on this brow,

[003-099] Hyperion’s curls, the front of Jove himself,

[003-100] An eye like Mars, to threaten and command,

[003-101] A station like the herald Mercury

[003-102] New lighted on a heaven-kissing hill:

[003-103] A combination and a form indeed,

[003-104] Where every god did seem to set his seal,

[003-105] To give the world assurance of a man.

[003-106] This was your husband. Look you now what follows.

[003-107] Here is your husband, like a mildew’d ear

[003-108] Blasting his wholesome brother. Have you eyes?

[003-109] Could you on this fair mountain leave to feed,

[003-110] And batten on this moor? Ha! have you eyes?

[003-111] You cannot call it love; for at your age

[003-112] The hey-day in the blood is tame, it’s humble,

[003-113] And waits upon the judgement: and what judgement

[003-114] Would step from this to this? Sense sure you have,

[003-115] Else could you not have motion; but sure that sense

[003-116] Is apoplex’d, for madness would not err

[003-117] Nor sense to ecstacy was ne’er so thrall’d

[003-118] But it reserv’d some quantity of choice

[003-119] To serve in such a difference. What devil was’t

[003-120] That thus hath cozen’d you at hoodman-blind?

[003-121] Eyes without feeling, feeling without sight,

[003-122] Ears without hands or eyes, smelling sans all,

[003-123] Or but a sickly part of one true sense

[003-124] Could not so mope. O shame! where is thy blush?

[003-125] Rebellious hell,

[003-126] If thou canst mutine in a matron’s bones,

[003-127] To flaming youth let virtue be as wax,

[003-128] And melt in her own fire. Proclaim no shame

[003-129] When the compulsive ardour gives the charge,

[003-130] Since frost itself as actively doth burn,

[003-131] And reason panders will.

[003-132] QUEEN.

[003-133] O Hamlet, speak no more.

[003-134] Thou turn’st mine eyes into my very soul,

[003-135] And there I see such black and grained spots

[003-136] As will not leave their tinct.

[003-137] HAMLET.

[003-138] Nay, but to live

[003-139] In the rank sweat of an enseamed bed,

[003-140] Stew’d in corruption, honeying and making love

[003-141] Over the nasty sty.

[003-142] QUEEN.

[003-143] O speak to me no more;

[003-144] These words like daggers enter in mine ears;

[003-145] No more, sweet Hamlet.

[003-146] HAMLET.

[003-147] A murderer and a villain;

[003-148] A slave that is not twentieth part the tithe

[003-149] Of your precedent lord. A vice of kings,

[003-150] A cutpurse of the empire and the rule,

[003-151] That from a shelf the precious diadem stole

[003-152] And put it in his pocket!

[003-153] QUEEN.

[003-154] No more.

[003-155] HAMLET.

[003-156] A king of shreds and patches!—

[003-157] Enter Ghost.

[003-158] Save me and hover o’er me with your wings,

[003-159] You heavenly guards! What would your gracious figure?

[003-160] QUEEN.

[003-161] Alas, he’s mad.

[003-162] HAMLET.

[003-163] Do you not come your tardy son to chide,

[003-164] That, laps’d in time and passion, lets go by

[003-165] The important acting of your dread command?

[003-166] O say!

[003-167] GHOST.

[003-168] Do not forget. This visitation

[003-169] Is but to whet thy almost blunted purpose.

[003-170] But look, amazement on thy mother sits.

[003-171] O step between her and her fighting soul.

[003-172] Conceit in weakest bodies strongest works.

[003-173] Speak to her, Hamlet.

[003-174] HAMLET.

[003-175] How is it with you, lady?

[003-176] QUEEN.

[003-177] Alas, how is’t with you,

[003-178] That you do bend your eye on vacancy,

[003-179] And with the incorporal air do hold discourse?

[003-180] Forth at your eyes your spirits wildly peep,

[003-181] And, as the sleeping soldiers in the alarm,

[003-182] Your bedded hairs, like life in excrements,

[003-183] Start up and stand an end. O gentle son,

[003-184] Upon the heat and flame of thy distemper

[003-185] Sprinkle cool patience. Whereon do you look?

[003-186] HAMLET.

[003-187] On him, on him! Look you how pale he glares,

[003-188] His form and cause conjoin’d, preaching to stones,

[003-189] Would make them capable.—Do not look upon me,

[003-190] Lest with this piteous action you convert

[003-191] My stern effects. Then what I have to do

[003-192] Will want true colour; tears perchance for blood.

[003-193] QUEEN.

[003-194] To whom do you speak this?

[003-195] HAMLET.

[003-196] Do you see nothing there?

[003-197] QUEEN.

[003-198] Nothing at all; yet all that is I see.

[003-199] HAMLET.

[003-200] Nor did you nothing hear?

[003-201] QUEEN.

[003-202] No, nothing but ourselves.

[003-203] HAMLET.

[003-204] Why, look you there! look how it steals away!

[003-205] My father, in his habit as he liv’d!

[003-206] Look where he goes even now out at the portal.

[003-207] [Exit Ghost.]

[003-208] QUEEN.

[003-209] This is the very coinage of your brain.

[003-210] This bodiless creation ecstasy

[003-211] Is very cunning in.

[003-212] HAMLET.

[003-213] Ecstasy!

[003-214] My pulse as yours doth temperately keep time,

[003-215] And makes as healthful music. It is not madness

[003-216] That I have utter’d. Bring me to the test,

[003-217] And I the matter will re-word; which madness

[003-218] Would gambol from. Mother, for love of grace,

[003-219] Lay not that flattering unction to your soul

[003-220] That not your trespass, but my madness speaks.

[003-221] It will but skin and film the ulcerous place,

[003-222] Whilst rank corruption, mining all within,

[003-223] Infects unseen. Confess yourself to heaven,

[003-224] Repent what’s past, avoid what is to come;

[003-225] And do not spread the compost on the weeds,

[003-226] To make them ranker. Forgive me this my virtue;

[003-227] For in the fatness of these pursy times

[003-228] Virtue itself of vice must pardon beg,

[003-229] Yea, curb and woo for leave to do him good.

[003-230] QUEEN.

[003-231] O Hamlet, thou hast cleft my heart in twain.

[003-232] HAMLET.

[003-233] O throw away the worser part of it,

[003-234] And live the purer with the other half.

[003-235] Good night. But go not to mine uncle’s bed.

[003-236] Assume a virtue, if you have it not.

[003-237] That monster custom, who all sense doth eat,

[003-238] Of habits evil, is angel yet in this,

[003-239] That to the use of actions fair and good

[003-240] He likewise gives a frock or livery

[003-241] That aptly is put on. Refrain tonight,

[003-242] And that shall lend a kind of easiness

[003-243] To the next abstinence. The next more easy;

[003-244] For use almost can change the stamp of nature,

[003-245] And either curb the devil, or throw him out

[003-246] With wondrous potency. Once more, good night,

[003-247] And when you are desirous to be bles’d,

[003-248] I’ll blessing beg of you. For this same lord

[003-249] [Pointing to Polonius.]

[003-250] I do repent; but heaven hath pleas’d it so,

[003-251] To punish me with this, and this with me,

[003-252] That I must be their scourge and minister.

[003-253] I will bestow him, and will answer well

[003-254] The death I gave him. So again, good night.

[003-255] I must be cruel, only to be kind:

[003-256] Thus bad begins, and worse remains behind.

[003-257] One word more, good lady.

[003-258] QUEEN.

[003-259] What shall I do?

[003-260] HAMLET.

[003-261] Not this, by no means, that I bid you do:

[003-262] Let the bloat King tempt you again to bed,

[003-263] Pinch wanton on your cheek, call you his mouse,

[003-264] And let him, for a pair of reechy kisses,

[003-265] Or paddling in your neck with his damn’d fingers,

[003-266] Make you to ravel all this matter out,

[003-267] That I essentially am not in madness,

[003-268] But mad in craft. ’Twere good you let him know,

[003-269] For who that’s but a queen, fair, sober, wise,

[003-270] Would from a paddock, from a bat, a gib,

[003-271] Such dear concernings hide? Who would do so?

[003-272] No, in despite of sense and secrecy,

[003-273] Unpeg the basket on the house’s top,

[003-274] Let the birds fly, and like the famous ape,

[003-275] To try conclusions, in the basket creep

[003-276] And break your own neck down.

[003-277] QUEEN.

[003-278] Be thou assur’d, if words be made of breath,

[003-279] And breath of life, I have no life to breathe

[003-280] What thou hast said to me.

[003-281] HAMLET.

[003-282] I must to England, you know that?

[003-283] QUEEN.

[003-284] Alack,

[003-285] I had forgot. ’Tis so concluded on.

[003-286] HAMLET.

[003-287] There’s letters seal’d: and my two schoolfellows,

[003-288] Whom I will trust as I will adders fang’d,—

[003-289] They bear the mandate, they must sweep my way

[003-290] And marshal me to knavery. Let it work;

[003-291] For ’tis the sport to have the enginer

[003-292] Hoist with his own petard, and ’t shall go hard

[003-293] But I will delve one yard below their mines

[003-294] And blow them at the moon. O, ’tis most sweet,

[003-295] When in one line two crafts directly meet.

[003-296] This man shall set me packing.

[003-297] I’ll lug the guts into the neighbour room.

[003-298] Mother, good night. Indeed, this counsellor

[003-299] Is now most still, most secret, and most grave,

[003-300] Who was in life a foolish prating knave.

[003-301] Come, sir, to draw toward an end with you.

[003-302] Good night, mother.

[003-303] [Exit Hamlet dragging out Polonius.]
