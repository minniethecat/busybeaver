# 第三幕 第一场

[003-001] Enter King, Queen, Polonius, Ophelia, Rosencrantz

[003-002] and Guildenstern.

[003-003] KING.

[003-004] And can you by no drift of circumstance

[003-005] Get from him why he puts on this confusion,

[003-006] Grating so harshly all his days of quiet

[003-007] With turbulent and dangerous lunacy?

[003-008] ROSENCRANTZ.

[003-009] He does confess he feels himself distracted,

[003-010] But from what cause he will by no means speak.

[003-011] GUILDENSTERN.

[003-012] Nor do we find him forward to be sounded,

[003-013] But with a crafty madness keeps aloof

[003-014] When we would bring him on to some confession

[003-015] Of his true state.

[003-016] QUEEN.

[003-017] Did he receive you well?

[003-018] ROSENCRANTZ.

[003-019] Most like a gentleman.

[003-020] GUILDENSTERN.

[003-021] But with much forcing of his disposition.

[003-022] ROSENCRANTZ.

[003-023] Niggard of question, but of our demands,

[003-024] Most free in his reply.

[003-025] QUEEN.

[003-026] Did you assay him to any pastime?

[003-027] ROSENCRANTZ.

[003-028] Madam, it so fell out that certain players

[003-029] We o’er-raught on the way. Of these we told him,

[003-030] And there did seem in him a kind of joy

[003-031] To hear of it. They are about the court,

[003-032] And, as I think, they have already order

[003-033] This night to play before him.

[003-034] POLONIUS.

[003-035] ’Tis most true;

[003-036] And he beseech’d me to entreat your Majesties

[003-037] To hear and see the matter.

[003-038] KING.

[003-039] With all my heart; and it doth much content me

[003-040] To hear him so inclin’d.

[003-041] Good gentlemen, give him a further edge,

[003-042] And drive his purpose on to these delights.

[003-043] ROSENCRANTZ.

[003-044] We shall, my lord.

[003-045] [Exeunt Rosencrantz and Guildenstern.]

[003-046] KING.

[003-047] Sweet Gertrude, leave us too,

[003-048] For we have closely sent for Hamlet hither,

[003-049] That he, as ’twere by accident, may here

[003-050] Affront Ophelia.

[003-051] Her father and myself, lawful espials,

[003-052] Will so bestow ourselves that, seeing unseen,

[003-053] We may of their encounter frankly judge,

[003-054] And gather by him, as he is behav’d,

[003-055] If’t be th’affliction of his love or no

[003-056] That thus he suffers for.

[003-057] QUEEN.

[003-058] I shall obey you.

[003-059] And for your part, Ophelia, I do wish

[003-060] That your good beauties be the happy cause

[003-061] Of Hamlet’s wildness: so shall I hope your virtues

[003-062] Will bring him to his wonted way again,

[003-063] To both your honours.

[003-064] OPHELIA.

[003-065] Madam, I wish it may.

[003-066] [Exit Queen.]

[003-067] POLONIUS.

[003-068] Ophelia, walk you here.—Gracious, so please you,

[003-069] We will bestow ourselves.—[To Ophelia.] Read on this book,

[003-070] That show of such an exercise may colour

[003-071] Your loneliness.—We are oft to blame in this,

[003-072] ’Tis too much prov’d, that with devotion’s visage

[003-073] And pious action we do sugar o’er

[003-074] The devil himself.

[003-075] KING.

[003-076] [Aside.] O ’tis too true!

[003-077] How smart a lash that speech doth give my conscience!

[003-078] The harlot’s cheek, beautied with plastering art,

[003-079] Is not more ugly to the thing that helps it

[003-080] Than is my deed to my most painted word.

[003-081] O heavy burden!

[003-082] POLONIUS.

[003-083] I hear him coming. Let’s withdraw, my lord.

[003-084] [Exeunt King and Polonius.]

[003-085] Enter Hamlet.

[003-086] HAMLET.

[003-087] To be, or not to be, that is the question:

[003-088] Whether ’tis nobler in the mind to suffer

[003-089] The slings and arrows of outrageous fortune,

[003-090] Or to take arms against a sea of troubles,

[003-091] And by opposing end them? To die—to sleep,

[003-092] No more; and by a sleep to say we end

[003-093] The heart-ache, and the thousand natural shocks

[003-094] That flesh is heir to: ’tis a consummation

[003-095] Devoutly to be wish’d. To die, to sleep.

[003-096] To sleep, perchance to dream—ay, there’s the rub,

[003-097] For in that sleep of death what dreams may come,

[003-098] When we have shuffled off this mortal coil,

[003-099] Must give us pause. There’s the respect

[003-100] That makes calamity of so long life.

[003-101] For who would bear the whips and scorns of time,

[003-102] The oppressor’s wrong, the proud man’s contumely,

[003-103] The pangs of dispriz’d love, the law’s delay,

[003-104] The insolence of office, and the spurns

[003-105] That patient merit of the unworthy takes,

[003-106] When he himself might his quietus make

[003-107] With a bare bodkin? Who would these fardels bear,

[003-108] To grunt and sweat under a weary life,

[003-109] But that the dread of something after death,

[003-110] The undiscover’d country, from whose bourn

[003-111] No traveller returns, puzzles the will,

[003-112] And makes us rather bear those ills we have

[003-113] Than fly to others that we know not of?

[003-114] Thus conscience does make cowards of us all,

[003-115] And thus the native hue of resolution

[003-116] Is sicklied o’er with the pale cast of thought,

[003-117] And enterprises of great pith and moment,

[003-118] With this regard their currents turn awry

[003-119] And lose the name of action. Soft you now,

[003-120] The fair Ophelia! Nymph, in thy orisons

[003-121] Be all my sins remember’d.

[003-122] OPHELIA.

[003-123] Good my lord,

[003-124] How does your honour for this many a day?

[003-125] HAMLET.

[003-126] I humbly thank you; well, well, well.

[003-127] OPHELIA.

[003-128] My lord, I have remembrances of yours

[003-129] That I have longed long to re-deliver.

[003-130] I pray you, now receive them.

[003-131] HAMLET.

[003-132] No, not I.

[003-133] I never gave you aught.

[003-134] OPHELIA.

[003-135] My honour’d lord, you know right well you did,

[003-136] And with them words of so sweet breath compos’d

[003-137] As made the things more rich; their perfume lost,

[003-138] Take these again; for to the noble mind

[003-139] Rich gifts wax poor when givers prove unkind.

[003-140] There, my lord.

[003-141] HAMLET.

[003-142] Ha, ha! Are you honest?

[003-143] OPHELIA.

[003-144] My lord?

[003-145] HAMLET.

[003-146] Are you fair?

[003-147] OPHELIA.

[003-148] What means your lordship?

[003-149] HAMLET.

[003-150] That if you be honest and fair, your honesty should admit no discourse to your

[003-151] beauty.

[003-152] OPHELIA.

[003-153] Could beauty, my lord, have better commerce than with honesty?

[003-154] HAMLET.

[003-155] Ay, truly; for the power of beauty will sooner transform honesty from what it

[003-156] is to a bawd than the force of honesty can translate beauty into his likeness.

[003-157] This was sometime a paradox, but now the time gives it proof. I did love you

[003-158] once.

[003-159] OPHELIA.

[003-160] Indeed, my lord, you made me believe so.

[003-161] HAMLET.

[003-162] You should not have believed me; for virtue cannot so inoculate our old stock

[003-163] but we shall relish of it. I loved you not.

[003-164] OPHELIA.

[003-165] I was the more deceived.

[003-166] HAMLET.

[003-167] Get thee to a nunnery. Why wouldst thou be a breeder of sinners? I am myself

[003-168] indifferent honest; but yet I could accuse me of such things that it were

[003-169] better my mother had not borne me. I am very proud, revengeful, ambitious, with

[003-170] more offences at my beck than I have thoughts to put them in, imagination to

[003-171] give them shape, or time to act them in. What should such fellows as I do

[003-172] crawling between earth and heaven? We are arrant knaves all, believe none of

[003-173] us. Go thy ways to a nunnery. Where’s your father?

[003-174] OPHELIA.

[003-175] At home, my lord.

[003-176] HAMLET.

[003-177] Let the doors be shut upon him, that he may play the fool nowhere but in’s own

[003-178] house. Farewell.

[003-179] OPHELIA.

[003-180] O help him, you sweet heavens!

[003-181] HAMLET.

[003-182] If thou dost marry, I’ll give thee this plague for thy dowry. Be thou as chaste

[003-183] as ice, as pure as snow, thou shalt not escape calumny. Get thee to a nunnery,

[003-184] go: farewell. Or if thou wilt needs marry, marry a fool; for wise men know well

[003-185] enough what monsters you make of them. To a nunnery, go; and quickly too.

[003-186] Farewell.

[003-187] OPHELIA.

[003-188] O heavenly powers, restore him!

[003-189] HAMLET.

[003-190] I have heard of your paintings too, well enough. God hath given you one face,

[003-191] and you make yourselves another. You jig, you amble, and you lisp, and nickname

[003-192] God’s creatures, and make your wantonness your ignorance. Go to, I’ll no more

[003-193] on’t, it hath made me mad. I say, we will have no more marriages. Those that

[003-194] are married already, all but one, shall live; the rest shall keep as they are.

[003-195] To a nunnery, go.

[003-196] [Exit.]

[003-197] OPHELIA.

[003-198] O, what a noble mind is here o’erthrown!

[003-199] The courtier’s, soldier’s, scholar’s, eye, tongue, sword,

[003-200] Th’expectancy and rose of the fair state,

[003-201] The glass of fashion and the mould of form,

[003-202] Th’observ’d of all observers, quite, quite down!

[003-203] And I, of ladies most deject and wretched,

[003-204] That suck’d the honey of his music vows,

[003-205] Now see that noble and most sovereign reason,

[003-206] Like sweet bells jangled out of tune and harsh,

[003-207] That unmatch’d form and feature of blown youth

[003-208] Blasted with ecstasy. O woe is me,

[003-209] T’have seen what I have seen, see what I see.

[003-210] Enter King and Polonius.

[003-211] KING.

[003-212] Love? His affections do not that way tend,

[003-213] Nor what he spake, though it lack’d form a little,

[003-214] Was not like madness. There’s something in his soul

[003-215] O’er which his melancholy sits on brood,

[003-216] And I do doubt the hatch and the disclose

[003-217] Will be some danger, which for to prevent,

[003-218] I have in quick determination

[003-219] Thus set it down: he shall with speed to England

[003-220] For the demand of our neglected tribute:

[003-221] Haply the seas and countries different,

[003-222] With variable objects, shall expel

[003-223] This something settled matter in his heart,

[003-224] Whereon his brains still beating puts him thus

[003-225] From fashion of himself. What think you on’t?

[003-226] POLONIUS.

[003-227] It shall do well. But yet do I believe

[003-228] The origin and commencement of his grief

[003-229] Sprung from neglected love. How now, Ophelia?

[003-230] You need not tell us what Lord Hamlet said,

[003-231] We heard it all. My lord, do as you please,

[003-232] But if you hold it fit, after the play,

[003-233] Let his queen mother all alone entreat him

[003-234] To show his grief, let her be round with him,

[003-235] And I’ll be plac’d, so please you, in the ear

[003-236] Of all their conference. If she find him not,

[003-237] To England send him; or confine him where

[003-238] Your wisdom best shall think.

[003-239] KING.

[003-240] It shall be so.

[003-241] Madness in great ones must not unwatch’d go.

[003-242] [Exeunt.]
