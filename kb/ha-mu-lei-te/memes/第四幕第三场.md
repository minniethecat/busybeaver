# 第四幕 第三场

[004-001] the Castle.

[004-002] Enter King, attended.

[004-003] KING.

[004-004] I have sent to seek him and to find the body.

[004-005] How dangerous is it that this man goes loose!

[004-006] Yet must not we put the strong law on him:

[004-007] He’s lov’d of the distracted multitude,

[004-008] Who like not in their judgement, but their eyes;

[004-009] And where ’tis so, th’offender’s scourge is weigh’d,

[004-010] But never the offence. To bear all smooth and even,

[004-011] This sudden sending him away must seem

[004-012] Deliberate pause. Diseases desperate grown

[004-013] By desperate appliance are reliev’d,

[004-014] Or not at all.

[004-015] Enter Rosencrantz.

[004-016] How now? What hath befall’n?

[004-017] ROSENCRANTZ.

[004-018] Where the dead body is bestow’d, my lord,

[004-019] We cannot get from him.

[004-020] KING.

[004-021] But where is he?

[004-022] ROSENCRANTZ.

[004-023] Without, my lord, guarded, to know your pleasure.

[004-024] KING.

[004-025] Bring him before us.

[004-026] ROSENCRANTZ.

[004-027] Ho, Guildenstern! Bring in my lord.

[004-028] Enter Hamlet and Guildenstern.

[004-029] KING.

[004-030] Now, Hamlet, where’s Polonius?

[004-031] HAMLET.

[004-032] At supper.

[004-033] KING.

[004-034] At supper? Where?

[004-035] HAMLET.

[004-036] Not where he eats, but where he is eaten. A certain convocation of politic

[004-037] worms are e’en at him. Your worm is your only emperor for diet. We fat all

[004-038] creatures else to fat us, and we fat ourselves for maggots. Your fat king and

[004-039] your lean beggar is but variable service,—two dishes, but to one table. That’s

[004-040] the end.

[004-041] KING.

[004-042] Alas, alas!

[004-043] HAMLET.

[004-044] A man may fish with the worm that hath eat of a king, and eat of the fish that

[004-045] hath fed of that worm.

[004-046] KING.

[004-047] What dost thou mean by this?

[004-048] HAMLET.

[004-049] Nothing but to show you how a king may go a progress through the guts of a

[004-050] beggar.

[004-051] KING.

[004-052] Where is Polonius?

[004-053] HAMLET.

[004-054] In heaven. Send thither to see. If your messenger find him not there, seek him

[004-055] i’ th’other place yourself. But indeed, if you find him not within this month,

[004-056] you shall nose him as you go up the stairs into the lobby.

[004-057] KING.

[004-058] [To some Attendants.] Go seek him there.

[004-059] HAMLET.

[004-060] He will stay till you come.

[004-061] [Exeunt Attendants.]

[004-062] KING.

[004-063] Hamlet, this deed, for thine especial safety,—

[004-064] Which we do tender, as we dearly grieve

[004-065] For that which thou hast done,—must send thee hence

[004-066] With fiery quickness. Therefore prepare thyself;

[004-067] The bark is ready, and the wind at help,

[004-068] Th’associates tend, and everything is bent

[004-069] For England.

[004-070] HAMLET.

[004-071] For England?

[004-072] KING.

[004-073] Ay, Hamlet.

[004-074] HAMLET.

[004-075] Good.

[004-076] KING.

[004-077] So is it, if thou knew’st our purposes.

[004-078] HAMLET.

[004-079] I see a cherub that sees them. But, come; for England! Farewell, dear mother.

[004-080] KING.

[004-081] Thy loving father, Hamlet.

[004-082] HAMLET.

[004-083] My mother. Father and mother is man and wife; man and wife is one flesh; and

[004-084] so, my mother. Come, for England.

[004-085] [Exit.]

[004-086] KING.

[004-087] Follow him at foot. Tempt him with speed aboard;

[004-088] Delay it not; I’ll have him hence tonight.

[004-089] Away, for everything is seal’d and done

[004-090] That else leans on th’affair. Pray you make haste.

[004-091] [Exeunt Rosencrantz and Guildenstern.]

[004-092] And England, if my love thou hold’st at aught,—

[004-093] As my great power thereof may give thee sense,

[004-094] Since yet thy cicatrice looks raw and red

[004-095] After the Danish sword, and thy free awe

[004-096] Pays homage to us,—thou mayst not coldly set

[004-097] Our sovereign process, which imports at full,

[004-098] By letters conjuring to that effect,

[004-099] The present death of Hamlet. Do it, England;

[004-100] For like the hectic in my blood he rages,

[004-101] And thou must cure me. Till I know ’tis done,

[004-102] Howe’er my haps, my joys were ne’er begun.

[004-103] [Exit.]
