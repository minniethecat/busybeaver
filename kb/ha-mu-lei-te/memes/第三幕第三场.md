# 第三幕 第三场

[003-001] Castle.

[003-002] Enter King, Rosencrantz and Guildenstern.

[003-003] KING.

[003-004] I like him not, nor stands it safe with us

[003-005] To let his madness range. Therefore prepare you,

[003-006] I your commission will forthwith dispatch,

[003-007] And he to England shall along with you.

[003-008] The terms of our estate may not endure

[003-009] Hazard so near us as doth hourly grow

[003-010] Out of his lunacies.

[003-011] GUILDENSTERN.

[003-012] We will ourselves provide.

[003-013] Most holy and religious fear it is

[003-014] To keep those many many bodies safe

[003-015] That live and feed upon your Majesty.

[003-016] ROSENCRANTZ.

[003-017] The single and peculiar life is bound

[003-018] With all the strength and armour of the mind,

[003-019] To keep itself from ’noyance; but much more

[003-020] That spirit upon whose weal depend and rest

[003-021] The lives of many. The cease of majesty

[003-022] Dies not alone; but like a gulf doth draw

[003-023] What’s near it with it. It is a massy wheel

[003-024] Fix’d on the summit of the highest mount,

[003-025] To whose huge spokes ten thousand lesser things

[003-026] Are mortis’d and adjoin’d; which when it falls,

[003-027] Each small annexment, petty consequence,

[003-028] Attends the boist’rous ruin. Never alone

[003-029] Did the King sigh, but with a general groan.

[003-030] KING.

[003-031] Arm you, I pray you, to this speedy voyage;

[003-032] For we will fetters put upon this fear,

[003-033] Which now goes too free-footed.

[003-034] ROSENCRANTZ and GUILDENSTERN.

[003-035] We will haste us.

[003-036] [Exeunt Rosencrantz and Guildenstern.]

[003-037] Enter Polonius.

[003-038] POLONIUS.

[003-039] My lord, he’s going to his mother’s closet.

[003-040] Behind the arras I’ll convey myself

[003-041] To hear the process. I’ll warrant she’ll tax him home,

[003-042] And as you said, and wisely was it said,

[003-043] ’Tis meet that some more audience than a mother,

[003-044] Since nature makes them partial, should o’erhear

[003-045] The speech of vantage. Fare you well, my liege,

[003-046] I’ll call upon you ere you go to bed,

[003-047] And tell you what I know.

[003-048] KING.

[003-049] Thanks, dear my lord.

[003-050] [Exit Polonius.]

[003-051] O, my offence is rank, it smells to heaven;

[003-052] It hath the primal eldest curse upon’t,—

[003-053] A brother’s murder! Pray can I not,

[003-054] Though inclination be as sharp as will:

[003-055] My stronger guilt defeats my strong intent,

[003-056] And, like a man to double business bound,

[003-057] I stand in pause where I shall first begin,

[003-058] And both neglect. What if this cursed hand

[003-059] Were thicker than itself with brother’s blood,

[003-060] Is there not rain enough in the sweet heavens

[003-061] To wash it white as snow? Whereto serves mercy

[003-062] But to confront the visage of offence?

[003-063] And what’s in prayer but this twofold force,

[003-064] To be forestalled ere we come to fall,

[003-065] Or pardon’d being down? Then I’ll look up.

[003-066] My fault is past. But O, what form of prayer

[003-067] Can serve my turn? Forgive me my foul murder!

[003-068] That cannot be; since I am still possess’d

[003-069] Of those effects for which I did the murder,—

[003-070] My crown, mine own ambition, and my queen.

[003-071] May one be pardon’d and retain th’offence?

[003-072] In the corrupted currents of this world

[003-073] Offence’s gilded hand may shove by justice,

[003-074] And oft ’tis seen the wicked prize itself

[003-075] Buys out the law. But ’tis not so above;

[003-076] There is no shuffling, there the action lies

[003-077] In his true nature, and we ourselves compell’d

[003-078] Even to the teeth and forehead of our faults,

[003-079] To give in evidence. What then? What rests?

[003-080] Try what repentance can. What can it not?

[003-081] Yet what can it, when one cannot repent?

[003-082] O wretched state! O bosom black as death!

[003-083] O limed soul, that struggling to be free,

[003-084] Art more engag’d! Help, angels! Make assay:

[003-085] Bow, stubborn knees; and heart with strings of steel,

[003-086] Be soft as sinews of the new-born babe.

[003-087] All may be well.

[003-088] [Retires and kneels.]

[003-089] Enter Hamlet.

[003-090] HAMLET.

[003-091] Now might I do it pat, now he is praying.

[003-092] And now I’ll do’t. And so he goes to heaven;

[003-093] And so am I reveng’d. That would be scann’d:

[003-094] A villain kills my father, and for that

[003-095] I, his sole son, do this same villain send

[003-096] To heaven. O, this is hire and salary, not revenge.

[003-097] He took my father grossly, full of bread,

[003-098] With all his crimes broad blown, as flush as May;

[003-099] And how his audit stands, who knows save heaven?

[003-100] But in our circumstance and course of thought,

[003-101] ’Tis heavy with him. And am I then reveng’d,

[003-102] To take him in the purging of his soul,

[003-103] When he is fit and season’d for his passage? No.

[003-104] Up, sword, and know thou a more horrid hent:

[003-105] When he is drunk asleep; or in his rage,

[003-106] Or in th’incestuous pleasure of his bed,

[003-107] At gaming, swearing; or about some act

[003-108] That has no relish of salvation in’t,

[003-109] Then trip him, that his heels may kick at heaven,

[003-110] And that his soul may be as damn’d and black

[003-111] As hell, whereto it goes. My mother stays.

[003-112] This physic but prolongs thy sickly days.

[003-113] [Exit.]

[003-114] The King rises and advances.

[003-115] KING.

[003-116] My words fly up, my thoughts remain below.

[003-117] Words without thoughts never to heaven go.

[003-118] [Exit.]
