# 第四幕 第一场

[004-001] Enter King, Queen, Rosencrantz and Guildenstern.

[004-002] KING.

[004-003] There’s matter in these sighs. These profound heaves

[004-004] You must translate; ’tis fit we understand them.

[004-005] Where is your son?

[004-006] QUEEN.

[004-007] Bestow this place on us a little while.

[004-008] [To Rosencrantz and Guildenstern, who go out.]

[004-009] Ah, my good lord, what have I seen tonight!

[004-010] KING.

[004-011] What, Gertrude? How does Hamlet?

[004-012] QUEEN.

[004-013] Mad as the sea and wind, when both contend

[004-014] Which is the mightier. In his lawless fit

[004-015] Behind the arras hearing something stir,

[004-016] Whips out his rapier, cries ‘A rat, a rat!’

[004-017] And in this brainish apprehension kills

[004-018] The unseen good old man.

[004-019] KING.

[004-020] O heavy deed!

[004-021] It had been so with us, had we been there.

[004-022] His liberty is full of threats to all;

[004-023] To you yourself, to us, to everyone.

[004-024] Alas, how shall this bloody deed be answer’d?

[004-025] It will be laid to us, whose providence

[004-026] Should have kept short, restrain’d, and out of haunt

[004-027] This mad young man. But so much was our love

[004-028] We would not understand what was most fit,

[004-029] But like the owner of a foul disease,

[004-030] To keep it from divulging, let it feed

[004-031] Even on the pith of life. Where is he gone?

[004-032] QUEEN.

[004-033] To draw apart the body he hath kill’d,

[004-034] O’er whom his very madness, like some ore

[004-035] Among a mineral of metals base,

[004-036] Shows itself pure. He weeps for what is done.

[004-037] KING.

[004-038] O Gertrude, come away!

[004-039] The sun no sooner shall the mountains touch

[004-040] But we will ship him hence, and this vile deed

[004-041] We must with all our majesty and skill

[004-042] Both countenance and excuse.—Ho, Guildenstern!

[004-043] Re-enter Rosencrantz and Guildenstern.

[004-044] Friends both, go join you with some further aid:

[004-045] Hamlet in madness hath Polonius slain,

[004-046] And from his mother’s closet hath he dragg’d him.

[004-047] Go seek him out, speak fair, and bring the body

[004-048] Into the chapel. I pray you haste in this.

[004-049] [Exeunt Rosencrantz and Guildenstern.]

[004-050] Come, Gertrude, we’ll call up our wisest friends,

[004-051] And let them know both what we mean to do

[004-052] And what’s untimely done, so haply slander,

[004-053] Whose whisper o’er the world’s diameter,

[004-054] As level as the cannon to his blank,

[004-055] Transports his poison’d shot, may miss our name,

[004-056] And hit the woundless air. O, come away!

[004-057] My soul is full of discord and dismay.

[004-058] [Exeunt.]
