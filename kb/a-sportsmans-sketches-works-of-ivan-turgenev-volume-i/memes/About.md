# About This Wiki

**A Sportsman's Sketches — Works of Ivan Turgenev, Volume I** is a structured knowledge base built from the English translation of Turgenev's classic collection of short stories (also known as *Sketches from a Hunter's Album*), first published in 1852.

## Source Material

- **Work**: *A Sportsman's Sketches* (Записки охотника), Volume I

- **Author**: Ivan Turgenev (1818–1883)

- **Original Publication**: 1852

- **Language**: English translation

- **Format**: EPUB

This wiki is generated from the English translation of Turgenev's *A Sportsman's Sketches*, Volume I, which contains a collection of short stories depicting rural life in 19th-century Russia.

## Usage Notes

- Each short story is a **chapter** page with paragraph-level citations.

- **Person**, **place**, **concept**, and **event** pages link back to the source chapters.

- Use the **search** bar (top right) to find entries by name or keyword.

- The **[[TOC|Contents]]** button (top left) provides access to the full table of contents.
