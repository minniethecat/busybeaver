# Urban Planning & Garden Cities

Urban planning runs as a counter-current through *Sticks and Stones* — the effort to shape cities deliberately rather than letting industrial forces dictate urban form. From Ebenezer Howard's garden city vision to the Regional Planning Association of America, Mumford traces an alternative tradition of community design that challenges the speculative grid and the congested metropolis.
