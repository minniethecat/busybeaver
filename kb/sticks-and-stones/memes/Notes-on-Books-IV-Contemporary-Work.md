### IV

[013-001] Contemporary Work

[013-002] Portfolios of work by contemporary architects are so numerous that to single out any would be invidious. The files of the *Architectural Record*, the *American Architect*, *House and Garden*, and *Arts and Decorations*—to mention only the more available periodicals—should be consulted particularly for illustrations.
