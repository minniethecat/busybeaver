# Vernacular & Regional Traditions

Across *Sticks and Stones*, Mumford returns to a theme: architecture is healthiest when it grows from local conditions — climate, materials, craft traditions, and community life. This page gathers entries on vernacular building, regional adaptation, and the medieval tradition that Mumford holds up as a model of integrated architectural culture.
