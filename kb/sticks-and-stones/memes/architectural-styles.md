# Architectural Styles

*Sticks and Stones* traces the evolution of architectural expression across centuries — from the medieval tradition through the Renaissance, the Romantic era's Gothic Revival, the imperial Beaux-Arts, and into the machine age. This page groups wiki entries by the architectural styles and movements that shaped Mumford's narrative.

## Gothic Revival

A medievalist reaction against industrial society, the Gothic Revival sought moral and aesthetic renewal through a return to pre-Renaissance forms. Mumford traces its influence through Ruskin's writings and the Picturesque tradition.
