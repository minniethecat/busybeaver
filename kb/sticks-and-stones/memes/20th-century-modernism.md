# 20th Century & Modern Architecture

The twentieth century in *Sticks and Stones* marks architecture's confrontation with the machine. Mumford traces the emergence of modern architecture from the regionalism of the early 1900s through the full embrace — and critique — of machine-age building. This page aggregates twentieth-century and modern-architecture entries across the wiki.
