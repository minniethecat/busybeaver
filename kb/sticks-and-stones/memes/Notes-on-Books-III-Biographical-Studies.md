### III

[012-001] Biographical Studies

[012-002] Where formal description leaves off, the biographies of our principal architects enter. The following books traverse in order the entire period from the Revolution to the present generation.

[012-003] *Samuel McIntire: His Life and Work*. F. Cousins and P. M. Riley, Boston: 1916.

[012-004] *The Life and Letters of Charles Bulfinch*. Ellen Susan Bulfinch, [[new-york-city|New York]]: 1896.

[012-005] *The Journal of [[benjamin-latrobe|Latrobe]]*. Benjamin Henry Latrobe, New York: 1905.

[012-006] *[[henry-hobson-richardson|Henry Hobson Richardson]]*. Mrs. Schuyler Van Rensselaer, Boston: 1888.

[012-007] *[[charles-mckim|Charles Follen McKim]]*. A. H. Granger, Boston: 1913.

[012-008] *[[daniel-burnham|Daniel H. Burnham]]*. Charles Moore, New York: 1921.

[012-009] *The Autobiography of an Idea*. [[louis-sullivan|Louis H. Sullivan]], New York: 1924.
