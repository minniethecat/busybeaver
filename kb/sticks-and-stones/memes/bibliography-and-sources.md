## Notes on Books

Mumford's *Sticks and Stones* concludes with an extensive annotated bibliography organized into six sections:

- **I. Historical Background** — General histories of American civilization
- **II. Architectural History** — Survey works and specialized monographs
- **III. Biographical Studies** — Lives of architects and builders
- **IV. Contemporary Work** — Recent architectural writing and criticism
- **V. Esthetics** — Theoretical works on art and architectural philosophy
- **VI. Sociology** — Works on cities, housing, and community planning

These annotated sections are reproduced in full in the Notes on Books chapter pages of this wiki. See the [[all-pages-index|All Pages Index]] for chapter-level navigation.
