# About This Wiki

## Source Material

This wiki is built from **Walden** (1854), the philosophical memoir by **Henry David Thoreau**, chronicling his experiment in simple living at Walden Pond, Massachusetts. The text explores themes of self-reliance, solitude, nature, and the essentials of human existence.

- **Author**: Henry David Thoreau (1817–1862)

- **Published**: 1854, Ticknor and Fields, Boston

- **Chapters**: 18 (Economy through Conclusion)

- **Language**: English (original)

## Usage Notes

Each chapter page contains the full original text, segmented by paragraph and numbered for precise citation. 

Entity pages (concepts, people, places) link back to source paragraphs via PN (paragraph number) anchors.

- Browse by **chapter** via the Contents (TOC) panel

- Browse by **type** via the sidebar categories

- Use **full-text search** to find specific passages or topics

- PN citations like `(001-007)` link directly to Economy, paragraph 7
