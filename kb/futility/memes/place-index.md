## The Geography of Futility

The novel spans a vast geography: from pre-revolutionary Petrograd to the Far Eastern reaches of Vladivostok, from the drawing rooms of London to the battlefields of the Russian Civil War. These place pages map the physical and political terrain across which Gerhardie's characters drift, strive, and come to terms with futility.
