## Art as Consolation and Echo

Throughout *Futility*, works of art serve as both escape and mirror. Characters attend performances of Gounod's *Faust*, quote Chekhov's *Three Sisters*, and invoke the literary canon of Russia and Europe. These cultural references are not decorative — they articulate the novel's deeper meditation on the relationship between art and the futility of existence.
