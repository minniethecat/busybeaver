## Characters and Historical Figures

Gerhardie populates *Futility* with a wide cast: the narrator Andrei Andreiech, the elusive Nina, the bureaucratic Nikolai Vasilievich, and numerous expatriates, soldiers, and revolutionaries. Many pages also cover real historical figures — British diplomats, White Russian generals, Bolshevik leaders — whose actions shaped the world the novel inhabits.
