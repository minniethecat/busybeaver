## The Tumultuous Era

Set against the collapse of the Russian Empire, *Futility* unfolds through one of the most turbulent periods in modern history: the First World War, the Russian Revolution, the Civil War, and the Allied Intervention. These events are not merely background but active forces that buffet the characters and amplify the novel's central theme — the futility of human intention against the sweep of history.
