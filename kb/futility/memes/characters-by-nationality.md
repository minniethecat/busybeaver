# Characters by Nationality

_Futility_ is populated by an unusually international cast, reflecting Vladivostok's status as a multinational hub during the Allied intervention in the Russian Civil War. British diplomats, American naval officers, White Russian generals, Czech legionnaires, and displaced Russian aristocrats all move through Gerhardie's pages — each carrying a distinct national perspective on the unfolding catastrophe.

The novel's polyglot cast is one of its most distinctive features. Gerhardie, himself of Anglo-Russian background, is uniquely positioned to observe the comedy of miscommunication and mutual incomprehension among the Allied nations attempting to manage a situation none of them fully understands.
