## Overview

Ports punctuate the geography of Gallions Reach as nodes in the network of maritime trade. They are marked by the cryptic symbols of commerce — port-marks on cargo — and by the named waypoints that structure the shipping routes: Port Said, the London docks.

> They announced merely the names of ships, and seasons, and the cabalistic port-marks of consignors. (002-022)

## Port as Threshold

A port is a threshold between sea and land, between the open water and the city. For the sailors and traders of Gallions Reach, the port represents both a destination reached and a point of departure — a place where the voyage pauses but the commerce continues.

> Their ship touched earth again at Port Said. (013-007)
