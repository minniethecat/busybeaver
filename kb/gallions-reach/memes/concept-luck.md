## Overview

Luck in Gallions Reach hovers between fate and chance — sometimes indistinguishable from either. A character's luck, or fate, becomes a matter of uncertainty. Luck is what happens to you, but also what you make of what happens.

> His luck, or fate, I forget. (001-017)

## The Ambiguity of Fortune

The text blurs luck with other forces — is good fortune the chance luck of time and rain, or something more intentional? When luck arrives, in the form of a woman showing interest, it demands recognition and response.

> If the good fortune of that window was just the chance luck of time and rain, like that woman's pity, then it was good luck indeed. (003-021)
