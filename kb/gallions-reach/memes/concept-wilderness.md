## Overview

The wilderness in Gallions Reach is an ever-present backdrop — the jungle and forest that surround the clearings of human activity. It is a place of merged figures and unheard conferences, where the distinction between human and nature blurs.

> The four Malays were apart, conferring together, unheard, almost merged in the wilderness. (029-001)

## Inner Wilderness

The wilderness is not only external. Characters carry an inner wildness — a capacity for facing solitude and darkness that requires "hardened nerves." The wilderness becomes a mirror for the untamed aspects of the self.

> One would want hardened nerves to face only oneself, in the wilderness. (030-009)
