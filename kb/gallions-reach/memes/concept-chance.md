## Overview

Chance operates in Gallions Reach as the unpredictable counterforce to design and intention. A chance wayfarer might misinterpret signs; a chance hint might reveal truth. The world is shaped as much by accident as by will.

> A chance wayfarer might think he had found abundant evidence of a local craving for immortality. (002-002)

## Chance and Fate

Chance blurs into fate — what appears random may be pattern unrecognized. The text probes this boundary, asking whether events are meaningful or merely accidental.

> Was that a chance hint? (003-020)
