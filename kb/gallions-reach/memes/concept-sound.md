## Overview

Sound shapes the atmosphere of Gallions Reach — from the water's murmur to the grave word that announces the unknown. In a world of visual description, sound provides an alternative channel of perception and meaning.

> The sound of the waters, the quiet that was neither day nor night, and that grave word which announced the unknown, brought a sudden solemnity. (001-005)

## Voice and Water

Voices in Gallions Reach are frequently likened to water — a character's voice compared to the sound of water, a bubbling sound in a glass. Sound bridges the human and the elemental.

> His voice was like the sound of water. (001-021)
