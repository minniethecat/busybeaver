## Overview

Desperation runs through Gallions Reach as an undercurrent of human motivation. Characters act not from hope but from its absence — the desperation of empty existences driving hurried departures and rash gestures.

> Very likely such evenings were only the desperation of empty existences. (004-039)

## Manifestations

Desperation manifests in physical urgency — Mr. Perriam's haste to leave, Colet's desperate brand flung at the darkness. It is a state that transforms indecision into action, though not always wisely.

> Colet snatched up a flaming brand in desperation, and flung it at the uproar. (029-010)
