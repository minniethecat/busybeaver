# Colophon

[P02-001]![The Standard Ebooks logo.](../images/logo.png)

[P02-002]*[[the-book-of-khalid-work|The Book of Khalid]]*

[P02-003]was published in 1911 by

[P02-004][Ameen Rihani](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Ameen_Rihani).

[P02-005]This ebook was produced for

[P02-006][Standard Ebooks](https://standardebooks.org/)

[P02-007]by

[P02-008][Weijia Cheng](https://www.weijiarhymeswith.asia/),

[P02-009]and is based on a transcription produced in 2009 by

[P02-010]**Todd Fine** , **Dan Horwood** , and [Distributed Proofreaders](https://www.pgdp.net/)

[P02-011]for

[P02-012][Project Gutenberg](https://www.gutenberg.org/ebooks/29257)

[P02-013]and on digital scans from the

[P02-014][Internet Archive](https://archive.org/details/bookofkhalid00riha).

[P02-015]The cover page is adapted from

[P02-016]*Baalbec - Ruins of the Temple of Bacchus* ,

[P02-017]a painting completed in 1840 by

[P02-018][David Roberts](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/David_Roberts_\(painter\)).

[P02-019]The cover and title pages feature the

[P02-020]**League Spartan** and **Sorts Mill Goudy**

[P02-021]typefaces created in 2014 and 2009 by

[P02-022][The League of Moveable Type](https://www.theleagueofmoveabletype.com/).

[P02-023]This edition was released on

[P02-024]**June 30, 2026, 2:48 a.m.**

[P02-025]and is based on

[P02-026]**revision 9081903**.

[P02-027]The first edition of this ebook was released on

[P02-028]June 27, 2026, 4:48 p.m.
