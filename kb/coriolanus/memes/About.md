# About This Wiki

This wiki is a structured knowledge base for William Shakespeare's tragedy *Coriolanus* (c. 1605–1608).

## Source Material

- **Play**: *The Tragedy of Coriolanus*, by William Shakespeare

- **Edition**: First Folio (1623), with reference to modern critical editions

- **Period**: Jacobean era, set in the early Roman Republic (5th century BC)

## Intended Audience

- Students and scholars of Shakespeare

- Theatre practitioners and dramaturgs

- Readers interested in Roman history as dramatized by Shakespeare

## Usage Notes

Pages are organized by type: Characters, Events, Concepts, Factions, and Places.

Use the search bar to find specific entries, or browse the homepage sections.

Each page cites its sources with act/scene/line references in PN format.
