# Antium

## Location Overview

Antium is the Volscian capital, the seat of Tullus Aufidius's power. In Act 4, Coriolanus journeys to Antium after his banishment (014-002), offering his services to his former enemy. The city becomes the site of his final moments — killed in its streets by Volscian conspirators (020-001).

## Dramatic Function

This location serves as a significant setting in *Coriolanus*, its spatial and symbolic qualities contributing to the play's dramatic effect and thematic resonance.
