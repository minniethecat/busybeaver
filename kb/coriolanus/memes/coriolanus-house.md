# Coriolanus's House

## Location Overview

Coriolanus's House is the domestic space that reveals the private dimensions of the public warrior. Volumnia's dominance of the household (003-002), Virgilia's quiet resistance, and Young Marcius's nascent martial temperament (011-002) all unfold within this patrician domus.

## Dramatic Function

This location serves as a significant setting in *Coriolanus*, its spatial and symbolic qualities contributing to the play's dramatic effect and thematic resonance.
