## 事件背景

Plebeian Rebellion 是《Coriolanus》全剧的开篇事件。罗马平民因粮食短缺和贵族囤积谷物而走上街头，手持棍棒和武器——"staves, clubs, and other weapons"（001-002）。第一市民明确表达了平民阶层的愤怒："the leanness that afflicts us, the object of our misery, is as an inventory to particularize their abundance; our sufferance is a gain to them"（001-002）。他们将矛头直指 Caius Marcius，认定他是"chief enemy to the people"，并提出"Let us kill him, and we'll have corn at our own price"（001-002）。

## 肚子寓言（The Belly Fable）

Menenius Agrippa 介入调停，以著名的"肚子寓言"安抚群众。他将元老院比作肚子，将平民比作叛变的肢体：

> "There was a time when all the body's members / Rebell'd against the belly, thus accused it: / That only like a gulf it did remain / I' the midst o' the body, idle and unactive, / Still cupboarding the viand, never bearing / Like labour with the rest"（001-002）

肚子的回答是：虽然它最先接收食物，但它将养分"send it through the rivers of your blood, / Even to the court, the heart, to the seat o' the brain"，所有肢体最终"from me do back receive the flour of all, / And leave me but the bran"（001-002）。

Menenius 将此寓言应用于罗马社会："The senators of Rome are this good belly, / And you the mutinous members"（001-002）。他论证平民从元老院获得一切公共福祉——"No public benefit which you receive / But it proceeds or comes from them to you / And no way from yourselves"（001-002）。

## Marcius 的蔑视

然而 Marcius 到场后以极端的轻蔑口吻斥责暴民。他将叛乱者称为"dissentious rogues / That, rubbing the poor itch of your opinion, / Make yourselves scabs"（001-002），并全面否定了平民的政治判断力：

> "He that trusts to you, / Where he should find you lions, finds you hares; / Where foxes, geese: you are no surer, no, / Than is the coal of fire upon the ice, / Or hailstone in the sun."（001-002）

他进一步抨击平民的反复无常："With every minute you do change a mind, / And call him noble that was now your hate, / Him vile that was your garland"（001-002）。当 Menenius 告知叛乱的原因是粮食价格时（"For corn at their own rates"），Marcius 更是怒不可遏："Hang 'em! They say! / They'll sit by the fire, and presume / To know what's done i' the Capitol"（001-002）。

## 政治后果

叛乱最终迫使元老院做出让步——同意设立五名平民保民官（tribunes），包括 Junius Brutus 和 Sicinius Velutus，以代表平民利益。这一制度安排成为后续政治冲突的核心制度框架。

值得注意的是，元老院在做出这一让步的同时，立即收到了 Volscians 入侵的消息。Marcius 认为这是摆脱民众纠缠的好机会——战争将把平民的注意力从国内矛盾转向外部威胁。

## 戏剧功能

该事件一开场就确立了剧中的核心冲突——贵族与平民之间的阶级斗争。Menenius 的肚子寓言为社会有机体论提供了优雅的修辞外壳，但其本质是将不平等自然化。第一市民尖锐地反驳了这一逻辑：贵族"ne'er cared for us yet: suffer us to famish, and their storehouses crammed with grain; make edicts for usury, to support usurers"（001-002）。

事件同时奠定了 Coriolanus 的初始形象：一位蔑视民众的军事贵族，其傲慢直接和毫不掩饰——"Who deserves greatness / Deserves your hate"（001-002）。这一形象在第一场中就完整呈现，预示了他后续的政治命运。
