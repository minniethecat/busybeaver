# Coriolanus's Wounds

## Event Summary

Coriolanus's twenty-seven wounds are the physical ledger of his military service (009-002). The custom of displaying wounds to the people as proof of worthiness for office (011-005) is the ritual he cannot perform — not because he lacks wounds but because displaying them to those who did not earn them degrades the currency of honour.
