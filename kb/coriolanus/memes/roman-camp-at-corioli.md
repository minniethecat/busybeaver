# Roman Camp at Corioli

## Location Overview

The Roman Camp before Corioli is the military space where Coriolanus's *virtus* is most fully expressed (004-001). The camp is organized hierarchy — consul, generals, common soldiers — in which Coriolanus excels absolutely, and from which he emerges with a new name (005-002).

## Dramatic Function

This location serves as a significant setting in *Coriolanus*, its spatial and symbolic qualities contributing to the play's dramatic effect and thematic resonance.
