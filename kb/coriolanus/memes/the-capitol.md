# The Capitol

## Location Overview

The Capitol is the religious and political heart of Rome, site of the Senate's deliberations and the tribunes' tribunals (012-001). References to the Capitol throughout the play carry the weight of Roman sacred and civic authority (001-002).

## Dramatic Function

This location serves as a significant setting in *Coriolanus*, its spatial and symbolic qualities contributing to the play's dramatic effect and thematic resonance.
