# The Duke Visits Gulvarez

[[duke-of-shadow-valley|The Duke of Shadow Valley]]'s journey and his scheduled stay at [[gulvarez|Gulvarez]]'s castle is the social catalyst of *[[the-charwoman|The Charwoman]]'s [[shadow|Shadow]]*. It is through this visit that news travels, romantic ambitions are kindled, and the novel's various households are woven into a single social fabric.

## The Journey

(006-004) News of [[duke-of-shadow-valley|the Duke]]'s approach travels ahead of him: "they heard that [[duke-of-shadow-valley|the Duke]] of [[shadow-valley|Shadow Valley]], being upon a journey, would rest a night at his castle with [[gulvarez|Gulvarez]]. Nor did this rumour fade, as such often did, that came so far over the fields." The persistence of the rumour signals [[duke-of-shadow-valley|the Duke]]'s importance — his movements are matters of public interest.

(006-005) The Duke's prestige is enumerated: "This was that very potent Magnifico, the [[duke-of-shadow-valley|second Duke of Shadow Valley]], of whose illustrious father some tale was told in the Chronicles of Rodriguez... he had amongst many honours the perpetual right to stop any bullfight in Spain whilst he went to his seat."

## Social Consequences

(006-022) The visit creates romantic possibilities: "He is not ill to look on; two women not long since have called him handsome. And he is a friend of [[duke-of-shadow-valley|the Duke]] of [[shadow-valley|Shadow Valley]]." Association with [[duke-of-shadow-valley|the Duke]] raises social stock — [[mirandola|Mirandola]]'s interest is at least partly strategic.

(006-029) The visit prompts family consultations, marriage speculations, and the social manoeuvring that will eventually lead to the [[the-love-potion-commission|love potion commission]].
