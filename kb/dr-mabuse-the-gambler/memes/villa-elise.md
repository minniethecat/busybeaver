# Villa Elise

The **Villa Elise** is [[dr-mabuse|Dr. Mabuse]]'s secondary safe house in the
[[schachen-villa|Schachen]] area, connected to his primary villa by a concealed
passage. It serves as the gang's fallback position and logistics hub, and becomes
the focal point of the final police operation against Mabuse's network.

## Location and Design

The Villa Elise is situated near Mabuse's main Schachen residence, linked by a
secret underground passage. Its existence was initially concealed — even the sign
displayed the name in a coded form:

> These signs. They stood for "Villa Elise."
>
> (015-054)

The villa was equipped with a hidden wall telephone, installed by [[george|George]]:

> He had a telephone concealed in the wall, and this George had connected with
> a villa at the back of his garden.
>
> (017-029)

This connection was described as a "mouse-hole" — a discreet escape route and
communications link between the two properties:

> There was yet another mouse-hole, and it was in Schachen, at the Villa Elise!
>
> (017-029)

## The Counterfeit Storage

The Villa Elise functioned as the designated destination for Mabuse's counterfeit
operation materials. During the climactic pursuit, Mabuse instructed George to
transport the chest of forged notes there:

> Mabuse, with the Countess, would drive to the Villa Elise, where George, who
> had been instructed by pigeon-post, would be waiting with the chest containing
> the counterfeit notes.
>
> (018-002)

## The Police Siege

State Prosecutor [[von-wenk|von Wenk]] identified the Villa Elise as the key target
for the final operation. He gave explicit orders for its encirclement:

> You have had plenty of time to get to know the geography of the place and the
> opportunities it affords, but the very moment you receive my order to surround
> Villa Elise, you must go at it, hell for leather.
>
> (016-075)

| Phase | Action | Outcome |
|-------|--------|---------|
| Reconnaissance | Police study the villa's geography and approaches | Position identified |
| Encirclement | Officers surround the villa on Wenk's signal | Perimeter established |
| Entry | Police rush forward into the villa | Mabuse's staff found handcuffed |
| Search | Exhaustive search of the premises | Little evidence recovered |

When police entered, they found one of Mabuse's associates already restrained:

> The constable was sitting handcuffed inside the Villa Elise.
>
> (018-013)

## The Final Search

The post-raid investigation of the Villa Elise yielded surprisingly little:

> An exhaustive search in the Villa Elise brought little to light. It might
> have been the residence of an entirely respectable citizen.
>
> (019-045)

This emptiness demonstrates Mabuse's operational discipline — the villa was
maintained as a clean fallback, stripped of incriminating evidence, ready to
be abandoned or reoccupied as circumstances required.
