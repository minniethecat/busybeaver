[038-001]And so back to [[foreland|the Forelands]], and the run between the [[kent|Kentish]] corner and the [[goodwin|Goodwin Sands]], with a few kind words to Saint Augustine, and [[julius-caesar|Julius Caesar]], and the men that murdered Saint Thomas, and so grew tails, and such others as have landed at this turn of the coast; but none for Hengist and Horsa, who never lived at all, and yet have done all manner of harm. So through the Downs into the Channel again and back on my way to the [[the-sea|sea]] of [[sussex|Sussex]], and to the shores which may be drowned and swamped for a while by alien townsmen, but will keep their own counsel and in due time will rise again.
