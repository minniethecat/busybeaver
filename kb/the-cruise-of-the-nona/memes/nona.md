# Nona

## Overview

The *Nona* is the unnamed p[[corruption|rot]]agonist of [[hilaire-belloc|Belloc]]'s memoir — a small cruising yacht of indeterminate age and specification whose career of minor disasters provides the book with its title and its organising metaphor. [[hilaire-belloc|Belloc]] tells us almost nothing about her technical specifications, and the omission is deliberate: the *Nona* is not a piece of marine engineering but a character, a companion, and a mirror.

[[hilaire-belloc|Belloc]] introduces her with a long, affectionate catalogue of her failings: "threading out of harbours, taking the mud, trying to make further harbours, failing to do so, getting in the way of more important vessels, giving way to them, taking the mud again, waiting to be floated off by the tide, anchoring in the fairway, getting cursed out of it, dragging anchor on shingle and slime, [[fog|mist]]aking one [[lighthouse|light]] for another, rounding the wrong buoy, crashing into other people, and capsizing in dry harbours" (P04-021). This is not mockery — it is identification. [[hilaire-belloc|Belloc]] and the *Nona* are "nearly of an age, the darling, but she a little [[youth|young]]er, as is fitting" (P04-021).

## In the *Nona*

The *Nona* is present, literally or by implication, in every chapter. She carries [[hilaire-belloc|Belloc]] from [[holyhead|Holyhead]] to the Wash; she survives the gale in [[bardsey-sound|Bardsey Sound]] (001-012 through 001-025); she anchors in [[milford-haven|Milford Haven]], rounds [[portland-bill|Portland Bill]], threads the intricate channels of [[solent|the Solent]], and finally reaches the shallow waters of the Wash. At every stage, [[hilaire-belloc|Belloc]] records her behaviour with the affection of a man who has entrusted his life to a machine and found it worthy.

[[hilaire-belloc|Belloc]] gives her "perpetual [[courage|encouragement]]: for no boat will do her best unless you speak to her in that way" (015-009). The half-humorous remark captures something essential: the *Nona* is not a tool but a partner in the enterprise of living.
