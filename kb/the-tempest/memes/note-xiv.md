[030-001] III. 3. 17. The stage direction, which we have divided into two parts, is placed all at once in the folios after ‘as when they are fresh’ [Solemne and strange Musicke; and Prosper on the top (invisible:) Enter ... depart].

[030-002] Pope transferred it to follow Sebastian’s words, ‘I say, to night: no more.’
