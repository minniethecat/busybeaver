[021-001] I. 2. 377, 378:

[021-002] Courtsied when you have and kiss’d

[021-003] The wild waves whist.

[021-004] This punctuation seems to be supported by what Ferdinand says (391, 392):

[021-005] ‘The [[music|music]] crept by me upon the waters,

[021-006] Allaying both their fury and my passion, &c.’

[021-007] At the end of the stanza we have printed *Hark, hark! ... The watch-dogs bark* as that part of the burthen which ‘sweet sprites bear.’ The other part is borne by distant watch-dogs.
