[026-001] II. 1. 249-251. All editors except Mr Staunton have printed in italics (or between inverted commas) only as far as ‘*[[naples|Naples]]?*’, but as ‘*keep*’ is printed with a small k in the folios, they seem to sanction the arrangement given in our text.
