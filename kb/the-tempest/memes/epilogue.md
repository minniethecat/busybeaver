[015-001] *SPOKEN BY PROSPERO.*

[015-002] Now my charms are all o’erthrown,

[015-003] And what strength I have’s mine own,

[015-004] Which is most faint: now, ’tis true,

[015-005] I must be here confined by you,

[015-006] 5 Or sent to [[naples|Naples]]. Let me not,

[015-007] Since I have my dukedom got,

[015-008] And pardon’d the deceiver, dwell

[015-009] In this bare [[island|island]] by your spell;

[015-010] But [[freedom|release]] me from my bands

[015-011] 10 With the help of your good hands:

[015-012] Gentle breath of yours my sails

[015-013] Must fill, or else my project fails,

[015-014] Which was to please. Now I want

[015-015] Spirits to enforce, [[magic|art]] to enchant;

[015-016] 15 And my ending is despair,

[015-017] Unless I be relieved by prayer,

[015-018] Which pierces so, that it assaults

[015-019] Mercy itself, and frees all faults.

[015-020] As you from crimes would pardon’d be,

[015-021] 20 Let your indulgence set me free.
