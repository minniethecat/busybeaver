## About the Featured Tier

Featured pages represent the gold standard of this wiki's content. Each has been assessed through [[compute-quality]] scoring and manual review. Criteria include:

- **Prose depth**: Substantive original analysis beyond plot summary
- **Citation density**: Well-sourced claims with PN references to the Balzac text
- **Structural completeness**: All required sections per the page type's schema template
- **Multi-perspective coverage**: Where applicable, the page includes contrasting viewpoints ("in the eyes of others," critical reception, etc.)
