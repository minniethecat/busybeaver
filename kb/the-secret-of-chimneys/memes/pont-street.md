# Pont Street

[[virginia-revel|Virginia Revel]]'s [[london|London]] residence at 487 Pont Street, serving as the setting for multiple key scenes: the blackmail attempt by [[giuseppe-manelli|Giuseppe Manelli]], the discovery of Manelli's body, and critical conversations between [[virginia-revel|Virginia]], [[anthony-cade|Anthony Cade]], and [[lady-eileen-bundle-brent|Bundle]] Brent.

## Description

487 Pont Street is a fashionable [[london|London]] townhouse where [[virginia-revel|Virginia Revel]] lives following her husband's death. The house is staffed by the butler [[chilvers|Chilvers]] and features a drawing-room and a study. The telephone book lists [[virginia-revel|Virginia]] as "Hon. Mrs. Timothy Revel of 487, Pont Street" (1029-PN). [[anthony-cade|Anthony Cade]], searching for the recipient of [[count-stylptitch|Count Stylptitch]]'s memoirs, locates the address through the telephone directory after eliminating other candidates (1029-PN). [[bill-eversleigh|Bill Eversleigh]], on [[george-lomax|George Lomax]]'s instruction, rings "[[virginia-revel|Mrs. Revel]], 487, Pont Street" from the [[foreign-office|Foreign Office]] (713-PN).

## Role in the Novel

Pont Street functions as a narrative crossroads. It is where the blackmailer [[giuseppe-manelli|Giuseppe Manelli]] first approaches [[virginia-revel|Virginia]] (disguised as the leaflet collector from the "Why Did I Serve My Country?" racket), where Manelli's body is later discovered in the study, and where [[anthony-cade|Anthony Cade]] and [[virginia-revel|Virginia]] first meet in [[london|London]]. The house also becomes the site where [[virginia-revel|Virginia]]'s latchkey is stolen by [[giuseppe-manelli|Giuseppe]] during the blackmail attempt — a detail that later proves significant when [[superintendent-battle|Superintendent Battle]] reveals the keys were "pinched off [[giuseppe-manelli|Giuseppe]] that day in Pont Street" (4843-PN).

## Key Events

- **[[bill-eversleigh|Bill Eversleigh]] rings Pont Street** — On [[george-lomax|George Lomax]]'s instruction, Bill contacts [[virginia-revel|Virginia Revel]] at 487 Pont Street to invite her to [[chimneys|Chimneys]] (713-PN)
- **The blackmail attempt** — [[giuseppe-manelli|Giuseppe Manelli]], posing as a shabbily dressed leaflet collector, gains entry to the house at Pont Street and attempts to blackmail [[virginia-revel|Virginia]] with letters purportedly written by her (1157-PN)
- **[[giuseppe-manelli|Giuseppe]]'s body discovered** — [[virginia-revel|Virginia]] returns from tennis at [[ranelagh|Ranelagh]] to find that the "person" [[chilvers|Chilvers]] reluctantly admitted to the study is a blackmailer; his dead body is later found in the house (1683-PN)
- **The latchkey theft** — [[giuseppe-manelli|Giuseppe]] steals [[virginia-revel|Virginia]]'s latchkey during the visit; [[superintendent-battle|Superintendent Battle]] later confirms the keys were recovered from him "that day in Pont Street" (4843-PN)
- **[[lady-eileen-bundle-brent|Bundle]]'s recognition of [[james-mcgrath|Jimmy McGrath]]'s name** — [[lady-eileen-bundle-brent|Bundle]] reveals that she recognized the name [[james-mcgrath|Jimmy McGrath]] when Anthony mentioned it at Pont Street, because [[george-lomax|George Lomax]] had previously asked her to "make herself agreeable" to [[james-mcgrath|McGrath]] and extract [[count-stylptitch|Count Stylptitch]]'s memoirs (3383-PN)
