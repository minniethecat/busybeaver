# British Embassy in Herzoslovakia

The British Embassy in [[herzoslovakia|Herzoslovakia]] was the diplomatic mission where Tim Revel — the late husband of [[virginia-revel|Virginia Revel]] — served as an attaché before his death. This posting gave Virginia firsthand knowledge of Herzoslovakian politics and Balkan affairs, knowledge that [[george-lomax|George Lomax]] would later seek to exploit in recruiting her as a political hostess for the [[the-diplomatic-conference-at-chimneys|diplomatic conference at Chimneys]] (003-075).

## Narrative Function

The British Embassy connection serves two functions in the plot. First, it explains why [[virginia-revel|Virginia Revel]] possesses the Balkan expertise that makes her valuable to [[george-lomax|George Lomax]] — "Virginia's late husband, Tim Revel, had been attached to the British Embassy in [[herzoslovakia|Herzoslovakia]]" (003-075). Second, it establishes the network of diplomatic relationships that connects [[london|London]], [[herzoslovakia|Herzoslovakia]], and [[chimneys|Chimneys]] into a single field of political action.
