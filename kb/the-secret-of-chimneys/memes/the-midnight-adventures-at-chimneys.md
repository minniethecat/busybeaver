# The Midnight Adventures at Chimneys

Two successive nights of amateur detective work by [[bill-eversleigh|Bill Eversleigh]] and [[virginia-revel|Virginia Revel]], who investigate strange noises in [[council-chamber|the Council Chamber]] and discover intruders searching for something hidden within the historic suits of armour — an episode that reveals the secret of the Holbein portrait's sliding panel and the hidden passage beneath [[chimneys|Chimneys]].

## Description

The first night begins when [[virginia-revel|Virginia]], unable to sleep, hears mysterious sounds below. She creeps to Bill's room and, with difficulty, wakes him. Together they descend to [[council-chamber|the Council Chamber]], where Bill, peering through the keyhole, observes figures working on the suits of armour: "A subdued chink every now and then seemed to point to the fact that the invaders were still dealing with the figure in armour" (4081-PN). A figure flits across his line of vision, accompanied by a faint tapping on wood (4081-PN). Before they can act, the intruders flee (4081-PN).

The second night brings them back to [[council-chamber|the Council Chamber]], where they discover the Holbein portrait's sliding mechanism and the concealed passage that explains how the intruders moved undetected through the house (4175-PN). The servants report that "the thieves were dismantling the suits of armour in [[council-chamber|the Council Chamber]] when they were surprised and forced to flee" (4301-PN). The intrusion is ultimately linked to [[king-victor|King Victor]]'s search for [[koh-i-noor-diamond|the K]]oh-i-Noor diamond, which is hidden inside one of the hollow suit-of-armour figures.

## Role in the Novel

The midnight adventures are the novel's bridge between the social intrigue of the house party and the physical mystery of [[chimneys|Chimneys]] itself. They transform [[council-chamber|the Council Chamber]] from a decorative setting into an active puzzle-box, foreshadow the revelation of the secret passage, and deepen the bond between Bill and [[virginia-revel|Virginia]]. [[superintendent-battle|Superintendent Battle]] takes the intrusion seriously, observing that it proves "[[koh-i-noor-diamond|the diamond]] is still in the house" and someone is systematically searching for it.

- **Discovery of the secret passage** — The Holbein portrait is found to conceal a sliding panel connected to a hidden passage
- **[[superintendent-battle|Battle]]'s conclusion** — The superintendent confirms the intruders were searching for the jewel and were interrupted (4301-PN)
