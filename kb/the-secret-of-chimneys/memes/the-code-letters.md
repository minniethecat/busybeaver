# The Code Letters

The code letters are a packet of secret correspondence written by [[mademoiselle-brun|Angèle Mory]]—the French actress who became [[queen-varaga|Queen Varaga of Herzoslovakia]]—to [[king-victor|King Victor]] (then Captain O'Neill). Written in code and signed with the name of an English lady at the [[herzoslovakia|Herzoslovakia]]n Embassy, the letters are the object of a deadly pursuit spanning two countries (019-033).

## Origin

While performing at the Folies Bergères in [[paris|Paris]], [[mademoiselle-brun|Angèle Mory]] was approached by the revolutionary organization [[comrades-of-the-red-hand|Comrades of the Red Hand]] to infiltrate the [[herzoslovakia|Herzoslovakia]]n court. She accepted "a huge sum if she would aid them in their plans" (019-0[[TOC|31]]). As [[queen-varaga|Queen Varaga]], she continued to correspond with [[king-victor|King Victor]] "in a secret code, from the Court of [[herzoslovakia|Herzoslovakia]]" (019-033). For safety, the letters were written in English and signed with another woman's name.

## Contents and Purpose

The letters contained coded instructions for locating [[koh-i-noor-diamond|the K]]oh-i-Noor diamond, which [[queen-varaga|Queen Varaga]] had stolen from the [[herzoslovakia|Herzoslovakia]]n Crown Jewels. The last letter, bearing the heading "[[chimneys|Chimneys]]," held the final clue: "The instructions for finding [[koh-i-noor-diamond|the diamond]] are written in code in that letter" (020-048). [[superintendent-battle|Superintendent Battle]] explains that the letters, once decoded, "let us have the letters, and let us do the decoding, and let us find the hiding-place" (022-034).

## Journey Through the Novel

After the [[herzoslovakia|Herzoslovakia]]n revolution and the murder of the king and queen, [[king-victor|King Victor]] kept the packet of letters. They were eventually stolen by "some [[herzoslovakia|Herzoslovakia]]n go-between" (019-042). Years later, the packet came into the possession of [[giuseppe-manelli|Giuseppe]], an Italian blackmailer, who attempted to use them against [[virginia-revel|Virginia Revel]] (020-026). [[anthony-cade|Anthony Cade]] recovered the letters, only for them to mysteriously reappear on his dressing-table at [[chimneys|Chimneys]] (020-077)—placed there by [[boris-anchoukoff|Boris Anchoukoff]], [[king-victor|King Victor]]'s agent.

## Decoding

The letters were decoded at [[chimneys|Chimneys]] by a red-haired expert summoned by [[george-lomax|George Lomax]] (022-155). The decoded text revealed [[queen-varaga|Queen Varaga]]'s hiding place for [[koh-i-noor-diamond|the diamond]] within [[chimneys|Chimneys]] itself.
