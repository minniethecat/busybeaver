# Wyvern Abbey

[[george-lomax|George Lomax]]'s neighbouring country estate to [[chimneys|Chimneys]], connected by a historic secret passage, and the setting for [[superintendent-battle|Superintendent Battle]]'s critical report on the investigation's progress.

## Description

Wyvern Abbey is a country estate located adjacent to [[chimneys|Chimneys]]. It serves as [[george-lomax|George Lomax]]'s residence and operational base during the diplomatic conference. The library at Wyvern Abbey is described as having "a desk overflowing with papers" where [[george-lomax|George Lomax]] works (5219-PN). The estate is connected to [[chimneys|Chimneys]] by a historic secret passage — "Supposed to lead from [[chimneys|Chimneys]] to Wyvern Abbey. So it did in the old, old days, but it's all blocked up now. You can only get along it for about a hundred yards from this end" — as [[lady-eileen-bundle-brent|Bundle]] explains to [[virginia-revel|Virginia]] (5593-PN).

## Role in the Novel

Wyvern Abbey functions as the administrative nerve-centre for [[george-lomax|George Lomax]]'s diplomatic efforts. It is where [[superintendent-battle|Superintendent Battle]] delivers his formal reports on the investigation into the murder and the missing Koh-i-Noor diamond. The estate's proximity to [[chimneys|Chimneys]] and the existence of the secret passage linking the two properties adds to the atmosphere of intrigue and hidden connections that pervades the novel.

## Key Events

- **[[superintendent-battle|Superintendent Battle]] reports to [[george-lomax|George Lomax]]** — [[superintendent-battle|Battle]] stands in the library at Wyvern Abbey delivering "a brief and businesslike report" on the investigation's progress, including the recovery of the coded letters from [[anthony-cade|Anthony Cade]]'s dressing-table (5219-PN)
- **[[george-lomax|George Lomax]]'s visit to [[chimneys|Chimneys]]** — [[lord-caterham|Lord Caterham]] notes that "Mr. [[george-lomax|George Lomax]] is coming over from Wyvern Abbey shortly, and he'll be able to tell you far more than I could" regarding the investigation (2277-PN)
- **[[superintendent-battle|Battle]] departs for Wyvern Abbey** — At half-past ten in the evening, [[superintendent-battle|Battle]] announces he is "starting for Wyvern Abbey in ten minutes to report to [[george-lomax|Mr. Lomax]]" after questioning [[virginia-revel|Virginia]] about [[anthony-cade|Anthony Cade]] (5073-PN)
