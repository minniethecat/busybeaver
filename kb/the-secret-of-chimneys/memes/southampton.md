# Southampton

Southampton is the English port where the *Granarth Castle* docked, carrying [[anthony-cade|Anthony Cade]] from [[bulawayo|Bulawayo]] to [[england|England]] under the alias of [[james-mcgrath|Jimmy McGrath]] (875-PN). The ship's early arrival — two days ahead of schedule — was the result of a miscommunication at the [[union-castle-line|Union Castle Line]] office, where [[bill-eversleigh|Bill Eversleigh]]'s inaudible pronunciation caused a clerk to look up the wrong vessel.

## Narrative Function

Southampton serves as the transition point between Anthony's African life and his entry into English society and the [[herzoslovakia|Herzoslovakia]]n conspiracy. From Southampton, Anthony takes the boat train to [[waterloo-station|Waterloo Station]] in [[london|London]] and proceeds to the [[blitz-hotel|Blitz Hotel]] (875-PN).
