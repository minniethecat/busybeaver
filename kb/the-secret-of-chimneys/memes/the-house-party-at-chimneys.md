# The House Party at Chimneys

The weekend gathering at [[lord-caterham|Lord Caterham]]'s country estate that serves as the novel's dramatic crucible — assembling under one roof the diplomats, aristocrats, detectives, and criminals whose intersecting agendas drive the murder investigation and the pursuit of [[koh-i-noor-diamond|the K]]oh-i-Noor diamond.

## Description

The house party is the novel's central structural device, bringing together an eclectic cast: [[george-lomax|George Lomax]] ([[foreign-office|Foreign Office]]), [[bill-eversleigh|Bill Eversleigh]] (Lomax's aide), [[virginia-revel|Virginia Revel]] (aristocratic widow), [[lady-eileen-bundle-brent|Bundle]] Brent ([[lord-caterham|Lord Caterham]]'s daughter), [[superintendent-battle|Superintendent Battle]] ([[scotland-yard|Scotland Yard]]), [[monsieur-lemoine|M. Lemoine]] ([[surete|Sûreté]]), [[hiram-fish|Hiram P. Fish]] (ostensibly a first-edition collector, actually a [[pinkerton|Pinkerton]] agent), [[herman-isaacstein|Herman Isaacstein]] (financier), [[baron-lolopretjzyl|Baron Lolopretjzyl]] ([[herzoslovakia|Herzoslovakia]]n Royalist), and [[prince-michael-obolovitch|Prince Michael Obolovitch]] (the royal claimant). [[lord-caterham|Lord Caterham]], the reluctant host, complains about the intrusion: "It gives one such a restless feeling" (5090-PN). The party is the setting for the novel's key scenes: the body's discovery in [[council-chamber|the Council Chamber]], the coded letters appearing on Anthony's dressing-table, and the climactic confrontations.

## Role in the Novel

The house party structure is a classic Christie device — the closed circle of suspects in a country house. It compresses the novel's diverse characters into a single location where their secrets cannot remain hidden. The murder of Prince Michael transforms the social gathering into a crime scene, and the party's diplomatic purpose ([[george-lomax|George Lomax]]'s Balkan peace conference) provides the political stakes. Every character's behavior at the party — [[virginia-revel|Virginia]]'s composure, [[lady-eileen-bundle-brent|Bundle]]'s resourcefulness, Fish's nocturnal prowling — becomes evidence in [[superintendent-battle|Battle]]'s investigation.

- **Fish's true identity revealed** — The "American collector" is exposed as a [[pinkerton|Pinkerton]] agent
