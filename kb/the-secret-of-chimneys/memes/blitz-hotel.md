# Blitz Hotel

[[london|London]] hotel where [[anthony-cade|Anthony Cade]] checks in upon arriving in [[england|England]] from South Africa, and where he receives the critical visits — from [[baron-lolopretjzyl|Baron Lolopretjzyl]] of the [[herzoslovakia|Herzoslovakia]]n Legation and from the waiter [[giuseppe-manelli|Giuseppe]] — that draw him into the novel's central conspiracy.

## Description

The Blitz Hotel is a fictional [[london|London]] hotel (likely based on the Ritz). [[anthony-cade|Anthony Cade]] selects it as his base after arriving at Waterloo Station: "he stepped out of the boat train at Waterloo, hailed a taxi, and after a moment's hesitation ordered the driver to proceed to the Blitz Hotel" (875-PN). The hotel functions as Anthony's [[london|London]] headquarters where crucial encounters take place. It is described as having comfortable rooms where Anthony can "stretch himself out in the biggest chair" (after the style of [[bill-eversleigh|Bill Eversleigh]] at the [[foreign-office|Foreign Office]]).

## Role in the Novel

The Blitz Hotel serves as the initial staging ground for Anthony's involvement in the plot. It is here that he receives his first visitor — [[baron-lolopretjzyl|Baron Lolopretjzyl]], who attempts to buy [[count-stylptitch|Count Stylptitch]]'s memoirs for the [[herzoslovakia|Herzoslovakia]]n Legation. It is also where [[giuseppe-manelli|Giuseppe Manelli]], the waiter-turned-blackmailer, visits Anthony, inadvertently setting off the chain of events that leads to [[giuseppe-manelli|Giuseppe]]'s death. The hotel room becomes the scene of the coded-letter mystery: Anthony wakes to find [[the-code-letters|the packet]] of letters planted on his dressing-table, then immediately takes them to [[superintendent-battle|Superintendent Battle]].

## Key Events

- **Anthony's arrival** — After landing at Southampton on the Granarth Castle, Anthony takes a taxi from Waterloo to the Blitz Hotel to establish his [[london|London]] base (875-PN)
- **Visit from [[baron-lolopretjzyl|Baron Lolopretjzyl]]** — The Baron calls at the Blitz to negotiate for [[count-stylptitch-memoirs|the memoirs]] on behalf of the [[herzoslovakia|Herzoslovakia]]n Legation
- **Visit from [[giuseppe-manelli|Giuseppe]]** — The waiter [[giuseppe-manelli|Giuseppe]] visits Anthony at the hotel
- **The coded letters appear** — Anthony discovers [[the-code-letters|the packet]] of coded letters on his dressing-table at the hotel and takes them directly to [[superintendent-battle|Superintendent Battle]] (5219-PN)
