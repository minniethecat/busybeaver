# Pinkerton

The Pinkerton National Detective Agency — the American private investigation firm that employs [[hiram-fish|Hiram P. Fish]] to track [[king-victor|King Victor]] across the Atlantic and into the heart of the [[chimneys|Chimneys]] conspiracy.

## Description

Pinkerton's was (and remains) the most famous American private detective agency. In the novel, the agency is revealed as the employer of [[hiram-fish|Hiram P. Fish]], whose cover as a bumbling American collector of first editions conceals his true identity. The revelation comes from [[monsieur-lemoine|M. Lemoine]]: "You see, Madame, Mr. Fish is a Pinkerton's man" (6189-PN). [[anthony-cade|Anthony Cade]] later confirms: "Once I knew that he was a Pinkerton's man, trailing [[king-victor|King Victor]], my suspicions swung back again to their original object" (6769-PN).

## Role in the Novel

Pinkerton's involvement adds an American dimension to the international investigation. While [[scotland-yard|Scotland Yard]] and the [[surete|Sûreté]] represent official European police, Pinkerton represents the private-sector, transatlantic pursuit of [[king-victor|King Victor]]. The agency's independent operation — Fish trails [[king-victor|King Victor]] from America to [[england|England]] without coordinating with British authorities — creates narrative tension and contributes to the misidentification of Fish as a suspect. The Pinkerton connection makes Fish a red herring for much of the novel: his suspicious behavior (the light in his window on the night of the murder, his late-night prowling) is explained not by guilt but by his professional surveillance of [[king-victor|King Victor]].

## Key Events

- **Fish's identity revealed** — [[monsieur-lemoine|M. Lemoine]] reveals to the assembled company that [[hiram-fish|Hiram Fish]] "is a Pinkerton's man" (6189-PN), transforming Fish from suspect to ally
- **Trailing [[king-victor|King Victor]]** — Fish confirms he followed [[king-victor|King Victor]] from America to [[england|England]], paralleling Anthony's own investigation
- **[[dover|Dover]] confrontation** — Fish appears at the [[dover|Dover]] house, having "happened to catch the same train as you at [[king-victor|Victor]]ia," revealing to Anthony that they are on the same side (5909-PN)
- **Mutual explanation** — Anthony and Fish compare notes and realize they have both been pursuing [[king-victor|King Victor]], clearing Fish of suspicion (6769-PN)
