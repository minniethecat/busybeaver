## Overview

The Council Chamber is the most significant room at [[chimneys|Chimneys]]. Panelled in old oak, it houses suits of armour, a Holbein portrait of the Earl of Richmond, and — most crucially — a hidden passage accessible through a sliding panel. It is the room where Prince Michael's body is found, where the secret passage search for [[koh-i-noor-diamond|the K]]oh-i-Noor jewel takes place, and where the novel's final confrontation unfolds.

## Description

The Council Chamber is "panelled like the Council Chamber in old oak, and with a wide gallery above it" [013-047]. It takes its name from its historical function as the room where privy councils were held when [[chimneys|Chimneys]] was a more politically significant house. The room contains suits of armour — which intruders are caught "dismantling" during the night [018-060] — and a Holbein portrait of the Earl of Richmond, behind which the secret passage panel is located [022-175].

The room's connection to the [[white-gallery|White Gallery]] and its secret passage system makes it a nexus of the novel's hidden geography. As [[lady-eileen-bundle-brent|Bundle]] explains, the passage "supposed to lead from [[chimneys|Chimneys]] to [[wyvern-abbey|Wyvern Abbey]]," though "it's all blocked up now" [023-056].

## Role in the Novel

The Council Chamber is the novel's most important interior space. [[lord-caterham|Lord Caterham]] first takes [[superintendent-battle|Superintendent Battle]] there after the discovery of Prince Michael's body [010-072]. [[superintendent-battle|Battle]] uses it as his informal headquarters, interviewing witnesses and examining evidence.

The room becomes the focus of the jewel hunt. Anthony recognizes that "the Holbein portrait in the Council Chamber" is the key — the Earl of Richmond's rose emblem matches [[count-stylptitch|Count Stylptitch]]'s cryptic clue [022-175]. The midnight search party — [[lady-eileen-bundle-brent|Bundle]], [[virginia-revel|Virginia]], [[superintendent-battle|Battle]], [[monsieur-lemoine|Lemoine]], and Anthony — assembles in the Council Chamber at 2:30 a.m. to enter the secret passage [023-001].

The novel's climax also centres on the Council Chamber. Anthony summons [[baron-lolopretjzyl|the Baron]] and [[captain-andrassy|Captain Andrassy]] to "the Council Chamber at nine o'clock" for [[the-council-chamber-showdown|the final confrontation]] [026-023], and "the clock in the Council Chamber struck nine" as the dramatic denouement begins [027-001].
