# Prince Michael Obolovitch's Arrival in England

[[prince-michael-obolovitch|Prince Michael Obolovitch]] arrives in [[england|England]] at the end of the week with his valet [[boris-anchoukoff|Boris Anchoukoff]], while his equerry [[captain-andrassy|Captain Andrassy]] remains in [[london|London]] — his journey to reclaim the [[herzoslovakia|Herzoslovakia]]n throne sets in motion the events that culminate in murder at [[chimneys|Chimneys]] (003-003, 013-013).

## Summary

Prince Michael's arrival is eagerly anticipated by [[george-lomax|George Lomax]] and the [[foreign-office|British Foreign Office]], who have arranged for the entire [[herzoslovakia|Herzoslovakia]]n question to be settled at a weekend house party at [[chimneys|Chimneys]] under the guise of a shooting party (003-003). The Prince is backed by British financial interests, having pledged to grant oil concessions to [[herman-isaacstein|Isaacstein]]'s companies in return for support for his claim to the throne (013-013). He travels with his valet [[boris-anchoukoff|Boris Anchoukoff]], while his equerry [[captain-andrassy|Captain Andrassy]] remains in [[london|London]]. Upon arriving at [[chimneys|Chimneys]], the Prince declares himself "greatly fatigued" and retires to his apartments, where dinner is served to him privately — he does not meet the other guests that evening (013-013). [[scotland-yard|Scotland Yard]], aware of the threat from the [[comrades-of-the-red-hand|Comrades of the Red Hand]], has been monitoring the group "ever since Prince Michael landed in [[england|England]]" (013-013).
