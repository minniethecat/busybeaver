## Overview

Paris is a crucial off-stage setting in [[the-secret-of-chimneys|The Secret of Chimneys]]. Though none of the novel's present-day action takes place there, the city is the origin point for the novel's central criminal conspiracy. It is where [[king-victor|King Victor]] established his reputation as "the most notorious [[king-victor|jewel thief]] in the world" [002-052], where the young actress [[angele-mory|Angèle Mory]] first captivated [[nicholas-iv|King Nicholas IV]] of [[herzoslovakia|Herzoslovakia]] [001-118], and where [[count-stylptitch|Count Stylptitch]] lived in exile until his death [001-116].

## Role in the Novel

Paris serves as the novel's backstory capital. The key events that set the plot in motion — Stylptitch's death, [[king-victor|King Victor]]'s jewel thefts, Mory's recruitment by the [[comrades-of-the-red-hand|Comrades of the Red Hand]], and the [[surete|Sûreté]]'s investigation — all occurred in Paris. The city functions as a symbol of the sophisticated European criminal underworld that the English characters must contend with at [[chimneys|Chimneys]].

Captain O'Neill ([[king-victor|King Victor]]'s alias) maintained rooms at Rue de Quenelles No. 15, Paris, where [[queen-varaga|Queen Varaga]]'s coded letters were addressed [006-025], [019-036]. The [[surete|Sûreté]]'s investigation into these letters and [[koh-i-noor-diamond|the K]]oh-i-Noor theft is conducted from Paris, with [[monsieur-lemoine|M. Lemoine]] sent to [[england|England]] as their representative [018-150].

## Key Events (Backstory)

- [[anthony-cade|Anthony Cade]] rescues [[count-stylptitch|Count Stylptitch]] from attackers in a Paris street four years before the novel [001-112]
- [[count-stylptitch|Count Stylptitch]] lives in exile in Paris after the assassination of [[nicholas-iv|King Nicholas IV]] [001-116]
- [[angele-mory|Angèle Mory]] works as a music-hall performer in Paris before being recruited by the [[comrades-of-the-red-hand|Comrades of the Red Hand]] [001-118]
- [[king-victor|King Victor]] establishes his criminal network from Paris, earning his notorious reputation [002-052]
- The [[surete|Sûreté in Paris]] monitors [[king-victor|King Victor]]'s activities and eventually sends [[monsieur-lemoine|Lemoine]] to [[england|England]] [019-0[[TOC|31]]]
- Captain O'Neill receives coded letters from [[queen-varaga|Queen Varaga]] at his Paris address [019-036]
- [[count-stylptitch|Count Stylptitch]]'s memoirs are "conveyed away secretly" from Paris weeks before his death, triggering the plot [003-057]
