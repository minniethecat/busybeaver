# Anthony Cade

An adventurous Englishman who accepts a job delivering [[count-stylptitch-memoirs|the memoirs]] of [[count-stylptitch|Count Stylptitch]] to a [[london|London]] publisher, setting in motion the events of the novel. Formerly a guide in Africa, Cade is resourceful, quick-witted, and willing to take risks.

## Role in the Novel

Cade is the protagonist. His journey from a chance meeting in Bulawayo with [[james-mcgrath|Jimmy McGrath]] to the unraveling of the international conspiracy at [[chimneys|Chimneys]] drives the narrative. He is the reader's primary guide through the mystery.

## Character Profile

### Appearance and Manner

Young, athletic, with a direct manner and a ready sense of humor. Described as someone who can handle himself in difficult situations.

### Background

Worked as a guide and adventurer in Africa, including time in Bulawayo (Rhodesia). Has a previous acquaintance with [[james-mcgrath|Jimmy McGrath]], an old friend who offers him the fateful job of delivering [[count-stylptitch|Count Stylptitch]]'s memoirs.
