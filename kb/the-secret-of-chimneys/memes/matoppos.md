# Matoppos

The Matoppos (Matopo Hills) are a scenic region near [[bulawayo|Bulawayo]] in what was then Southern Rhodesia (present-day Zimbabwe). In the novel's opening pages, [[anthony-cade|Anthony Cade]]'s tour group with Castle's Select Tour is waiting to "motor to the Matoppos," providing the idle interval during which Anthony encounters his old friend [[james-mcgrath|Jimmy McGrath]] (001-003).

## Narrative Function

The Matoppos serve a purely structural role in the novel: they justify the moment of stillness that allows the inciting encounter between [[anthony-cade|Anthony Cade]] and [[james-mcgrath|Jimmy McGrath]] to occur. As [[anthony-cade|Anthony Cade]]'s tour group waits "until the moment should arrive to motor to the Matoppos," the tourists are reduced to buying picture postcards — and Anthony is free to accept Jimmy's proposition, setting the entire plot in motion (001-003).
