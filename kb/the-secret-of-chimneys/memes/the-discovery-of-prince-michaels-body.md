# The Discovery of Prince Michael's Body

On a June evening in [[london|London]], [[virginia-revel|Virginia Revel]] returns from [[ranelagh|Ranelagh]] to her [[pont-street|Pont Street]] house to find a dead man — [[prince-michael-obolovitch|Prince Michael Obolovitch]] — shot through the heart in her study, a small revolver lying on the floor beside him, and no one in the house except her French maid Élise.

## Description

[[virginia-revel|Virginia]] arrives home to find the servants gone — dismissed by a bogus telegram. Entering the study, she sees a man slumped in an armchair: "The man was not asleep. He was dead" (1800-PN). She recognizes immediately: "the little shining pistol lying on the floor, the little-singed hole just above the heart with the dark stain round it, and the horrible dropped jaw" (1800-PN). The dead man is [[giuseppe-manelli|Giuseppe Manelli]], an Italian waiter from [[blitz-hotel|the Blitz]] Hotel who had attempted to blackmail her the previous evening over a packet of incriminating letters.

Before [[virginia-revel|Virginia]] can decide what to do, [[anthony-cade|Anthony Cade]] arrives at the door. She blurts out: "There's a dead man in the next room. He's been murdered, and I don't know what to do about it" (1833-PN). Anthony responds with remarkable composure: "Excellent. I've always wanted to do a bit of amateur detective work" (1833-PN). Together they search the body, finding it stripped of identification except for a scrap of paper in a torn pocket lining — a fragment bearing the time "11:45" (2012-PN).

## Role in the Novel

This discovery is the novel's explosive inciting incident — it pivots the narrative from intrigue ([[count-stylptitch-memoirs|the memoirs]], the blackmail) into a murder investigation. It forges the alliance between [[virginia-revel|Virginia]] and Anthony, initiates [[superintendent-battle|Battle]]'s inquiry, and pulls the action from [[london|London]] to [[chimneys|Chimneys]]. The discovery also introduces the novel's central puzzle: why was Prince Michael killed, and by whom?

## Key Events

- **[[virginia-revel|Virginia]] returns to an empty house** — A bogus telegram has lured her servants away (1800-PN)
- **She finds the body** — [[giuseppe-manelli|Giuseppe]] is dead in the study armchair, shot through the heart (1800-PN)
- **[[anthony-cade|Anthony Cade]] arrives** — The adventurer accepts the situation with surprising nonchalance (1833-PN)
- **They search the body** — Anthony finds a torn scrap of paper reading "11:45" in the dead man's pocket lining (2012-PN)
- **The disposal** — Anthony resolves to move the body, sparing [[virginia-revel|Virginia]] from a police scandal
- **Body transported to [[staines|Staines]]** — Anthony drives the body to a roadside spot near [[staines|Staines]] (5095-PN)
