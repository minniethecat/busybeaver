# Herzoslovakian Revolutionaries

The republican political faction that overthrew the Obolovitch monarchy in [[herzoslovakia|Herzoslovakia]] and established the republic — allied with the [[comrades-of-the-red-hand|Comrades of the Red Hand]] and locked in a diplomatic struggle with the Royalist (Loyalist) party for international recognition and control of [[koh-i-noor-diamond|the K]]oh-i-Noor diamond.

## Description

The Revolutionary faction represents the republican government of [[herzoslovakia|Herzoslovakia]] following the assassination of [[nicholas-iv|King Nicholas IV]] and the abolition of the monarchy. They are the political rivals of the Loyalist party, competing for influence at the [[london|London]] diplomatic conference and for possession of [[koh-i-noor-diamond|the K]]oh-i-Noor as a symbol of legitimate authority. [[anthony-cade|Anthony Cade]] notes the crowded field of [[herzoslovakia|Herzoslovakia]]n exile politics: "I've already fallen foul of both the Loyalist and the Revolutionary parties. Soon, I suppose, the Nationalists and the Independent Liberals will be sending up a delegation" (1093-PN). The Revolutionaries maintain an alliance of convenience with the [[comrades-of-the-red-hand|Comrades of the Red Hand]], who provide the violent enforcement the political faction cannot publicly acknowledge.

## Role in the Novel

The Revolutionaries form the political backdrop against which the [[comrades-of-the-red-hand|Comrades of the Red Hand]] operate. While the [[comrades-of-the-red-hand|Comrades]] commit the actual violence, the Revolutionary party provides the political motivation — preventing the restoration of the monarchy, securing [[koh-i-noor-diamond|the K]]oh-i-Noor for the republic, and maintaining their grip on power. The diplomatic conference at [[chimneys|Chimneys]], organized by [[george-lomax|George Lomax]], is an attempt to broker peace between the Royalist and Revolutionary factions, but the murder of [[prince-michael-obolovitch|Prince Michael Obolovitch]] — the Royalist claimant — disrupts these efforts.

## Key Events

- **Overthrow of the monarchy** — Following [[assassination-of-nicholas-iv|King Nicholas IV's assassination]], the Revolutionary faction establishes the [[herzoslovakia|Herzoslovakia]]n republic (221-PN)
- **Alliance with the [[comrades-of-the-red-hand|Comrades]]** — The Revolutionaries maintain operational links with the [[comrades-of-the-red-hand|Comrades of the Red Hand]], who serve as their violent proxy (4551-PN)
- **Diplomatic conference** — The Revolutionaries are stakeholders in the [[london|London]] negotiations hosted at [[chimneys|Chimneys]], competing with the Royalists for recognition
- **Pursuit of [[koh-i-noor-diamond|the K]]oh-i-Noor** — Like the Royalists, the Revolutionaries seek [[koh-i-noor-diamond|the diamond]] as a symbol of legitimate governance
