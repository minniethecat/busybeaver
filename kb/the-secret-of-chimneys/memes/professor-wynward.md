## Overview

Professor Wynward is the cryptographer called upon by [[superintendent-battle|Superintendent Battle]] to decode the coded letters found among the [[chimneys|Chimneys]] papers. He arrives at [[chimneys|Chimneys]] and works on the correspondence between [[queen-varaga|Queen Varaga]] and [[king-victor|King Victor]], producing decryptions that advance the investigation at a critical moment.

## Character Profile

Wynward is associated with the British Museum, identified by [[superintendent-battle|Battle]] as "the man" for the decoding work [022-017]. He is one of several experts — along with [[monsieur-lemoine|Lemoine]] of the Sûreté and [[superintendent-battle|Battle]] of [[scotland-yard|Scotland Yard]] — brought together at [[chimneys|Chimneys]] to resolve the case. His role is purely technical: he applies his cryptographic expertise to the letters and produces results.

## Key Actions

When [[superintendent-battle|Superintendent Battle]] announces "There must be no delay. These letters must be decoded without any loss of time," he immediately identifies the right expert: "Professor Wynward" [022-018]. Wynward is summoned to [[chimneys|Chimneys]] and arrives to find the household in the midst of crisis.

[[superintendent-battle|Battle]] later reports to [[virginia-revel|Virginia]] and Anthony: "I've got Professor Wynward down here. Just this minute arrived. He's decoding the letters now. Would you like to see him at work?" [022-153]. The decoded letters prove revelatory — they reveal the relationship between [[queen-varaga|Queen Varaga]] and [[king-victor|King Victor]], and confirm the coded communication that [[virginia-revel|Virginia]] had been suspected of authoring [022-166].

When Anthony discovers a second cipher ("Cipher No. 2") in the secret passage, he wonders aloud whether "Professor Wynward would be any good at this one" [023-044] — a testament to the professor's established competence, though by this point the investigation is moving faster than academic cryptography can keep pace.

## Relationships

- **[[superintendent-battle|Superintendent Battle]]** — summons Wynward for his expertise and vouches for his competence [022-017]
- **[[anthony-cade|Anthony Cade]]** — regards Wynward as a useful resource for decoding work [023-044]
- **[[virginia-revel|Virginia Revel]]** — the decoded letters exonerate her from suspicions of being [[queen-varaga|Queen Varaga]]'s correspondent [022-166]

## Significance

Professor Wynward represents the novel's acknowledgement that detective work is not merely a matter of intuition and interrogation but also of technical expertise. His decryption work — though accomplished off-stage — provides the documentary evidence that confirms the investigators' theories about [[queen-varaga|Queen Varaga]]'s secret correspondence with [[king-victor|King Victor]]. He is a minor but essential piece in the puzzle, the technical specialist whose skills no detective could replicate.
