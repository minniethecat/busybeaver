# The Recovery of the Stolen Memoirs

[[count-stylptitch|Count Stylptitch]]'s coded memoirs — entrusted to [[james-mcgrath|James McGrath]] in Africa and delivered to [[anthony-cade|Anthony Cade]] — become the object of a frantic pursuit by multiple factions, each seeking to control the explosive political secrets they contain.

## Summary

[[james-mcgrath|James McGrath]] receives a parcel containing [[count-stylptitch|Count Stylptitch]]'s memoirs after the Count's death in [[paris|Paris]], with instructions to deliver [[count-stylptitch-memoirs|the manuscript]] to a [[london|London]] publisher by October 13th in exchange for a thousand pounds (001-001). He enlists [[anthony-cade|Anthony Cade]] to handle the delivery. Anthony quickly discovers that [[count-stylptitch-memoirs|the memoirs]] are a subject of intense interest: [[baron-lolopretjzyl|the Baron]] [[baron-lolopretjzyl|Lolopretjzyl]] of the Loyalist party attempts to buy them, warning that their publication could upset the [[herzoslovakia|Herzoslovakia]]n situation (005-005). The [[comrades-of-the-red-hand|Comrades of the Red Hand]] employ [[giuseppe-manelli|Giuseppe Manelli]] to steal [[the-code-letters|the packet]], leading to a hotel-room raid at [[blitz-hotel|the Blitz]] (005-005). Anthony, having begun reading [[count-stylptitch-memoirs|the manuscript]], finds it "insufferably dull" so far — though later chapters conceal a coded secret (005-005). The memoirs pass through multiple hands as the Loyalists, the [[comrades-of-the-red-hand|Comrades]], British intelligence, and Anthony himself each race to recover or control them.

## Key Participants

- [[anthony-cade]] — entrusted by [[james-mcgrath|McGrath]] to deliver [[count-stylptitch-memoirs|the memoirs]]; reads and guards them
- [[james-mcgrath]] — received [[count-stylptitch-memoirs|the memoirs]] from [[count-stylptitch|Count Stylptitch]] in Africa

- [[giuseppe-manelli]] — agent of the [[comrades-of-the-red-hand|Comrades of the Red Hand]] who attempts to steal [[the-code-letters|the packet]]
