# Sûreté

The French national detective police force, headquartered in [[paris|Paris]], that employs [[monsieur-lemoine|M. Lemoine]] and leads the European investigation into [[king-victor|King Victor]]'s jewel-theft network — working in parallel with [[scotland-yard|Scotland Yard]]'s inquiry at [[chimneys|Chimneys]].

## Description

The Sûreté (Sûreté Nationale) is the French detective police, described in the novel as an elite investigative agency. The organization "think no end of him at the Sûreté" regarding [[monsieur-lemoine|M. Lemoine]], their lead investigator on [[koh-i-noor-diamond|the K]]ing [[king-victor|Victor]] case (5349-PN). [[superintendent-battle|Superintendent Battle]] describes him as a "clever chap" (5349-PN). The Sûreté's reach extends beyond France: they issue warnings to [[scotland-yard|Scotland Yard]] about known criminals, such as the notification about [[giuseppe-manelli|Giuseppe Manelli]] as a "Notorious French crook, sir. We've had a warning from the Sûreté in Paris" ([[TOC|31]]67-PN).

## Role in the Novel

The Sûreté represents the international dimension of the investigation. While [[scotland-yard|Scotland Yard]] handles [[murder-at-chimneys|the murder at Chimneys]], the Sûreté pursues the transnational conspiracy — the theft of [[koh-i-noor-diamond|the K]]oh-i-Noor, [[king-victor|King Victor]]'s network, and the [[herzoslovakia|Herzoslovakia]]n revolutionary connections. The Sûreté's involvement adds a layer of Continental sophistication to the investigation, and their intelligence on [[king-victor|King Victor]]'s history proves crucial to unraveling the plot. The final revelation that the man claiming to be [[monsieur-lemoine|M. Lemoine]] is actually an impostor, and that "Allow me to present you to the real [[monsieur-lemoine|Monsieur Lemoine]] of the Sûreté" (6855-PN) is the real agent, delivers one of the novel's final twists.

## Key Events

- **Warning from [[paris|Paris]]** — The Sûreté alerts [[scotland-yard|Scotland Yard]] about [[giuseppe-manelli|Giuseppe Manelli]], a notorious French criminal operating in [[england|England]] ([[TOC|31]]67-PN)
- **[[monsieur-lemoine|M. Lemoine]]'s investigation** — The Sûreté dispatches their agent [[monsieur-lemoine|Lemoine]] to [[england|England]] to coordinate with [[superintendent-battle|Battle]] on [[koh-i-noor-diamond|the K]]ing [[king-victor|Victor]] case (4481-PN)
- **Surveillance of [[angele-mory|Angèle Mory]]** — "At the Sûreté we were given special instructions as to the course to be adopted to ensure the safety of His Majesty" during [[nicholas-iv|King Nicholas IV]]'s visit to [[paris|Paris]] (4551-PN)
- **The impostor revealed** — The false [[monsieur-lemoine|Lemoine]] is exposed, and "the real [[monsieur-lemoine|Monsieur Lemoine]] of the Sûreté" is presented (6855-PN)
