# Disposal of Prince Michael's Body

## Overview

The disposal of [[prince-michael-obolovitch|Prince Michael Obolovitch]]'s body was a clandestine operation carried out by [[anthony-cade|Anthony Cade]] and [[virginia-revel|Virginia Revel]] on the evening of 5 October, after discovering the prince shot dead in [[virginia-revel|Virginia]]'s study at her [[pont-street|Pont Street]] residence. Rather than reporting the murder to the police immediately, Anthony convinced [[virginia-revel|Virginia]] to allow him to conceal the body, fearing she would be implicated by a pistol engraved with her name left at the scene.

## Background

Earlier that afternoon, [[virginia-revel|Virginia Revel]] had returned to her [[pont-street|Pont Street]] flat to find an Italian man—later identified as [[giuseppe-manelli|Giuseppe Manelli]]—waiting in her study. He attempted to blackmail her with letters purportedly written by her to a lover. [[virginia-revel|Virginia]], recognizing the letters as forgeries, paid him forty pounds and sent him away [008-001].

When [[virginia-revel|Virginia]] returned from playing tennis at [[ranelagh|Ranelagh]], she discovered the same man shot dead in her study. A telegram sent from Barnes, timed to coincide with her afternoon outing, had been planted to suggest she could have sent it herself. The pistol left at the scene was a small, dainty weapon engraved with the name "[[virginia-revel|Virginia]]" [009-001].

## The Decision

> "The question is," said Anthony, "what are we going to do now? There are two courses open to us. A: Ring up the police, tell the whole story, and trust to your unassailable position in the world and your hitherto blameless life. B: An attempt on my part to dispose successfully of the body." [009-002]

Anthony presented [[virginia-revel|Virginia]] with two hypotheses: either the real "[[virginia-revel|Virginia]]" of the forged letters had tracked [[giuseppe-manelli|Giuseppe]] down and killed him, or someone had deliberately staged the murder at [[pont-street|Pont Street]] to incriminate her. A scrap of paper found in the dead man's pocket lining read "[[chimneys|Chimneys]] 11:45 Thursday"—the same evening [[virginia-revel|Virginia]] was expected at [[chimneys|Chimneys]] [009-003].

Anthony suspected that preventing [[virginia-revel|Virginia]] from going to [[chimneys|Chimneys]] might have been the murderer's objective. He urged her to proceed with her plans, arguing her arrival would "disconcert our unknown friends" [009-004].

## Execution

After sending [[virginia-revel|Virginia]]'s French maid Élise on a fabricated errand, Anthony and [[virginia-revel|Virginia]] proceeded to the basement where they selected a solid trunk of suitable size [009-005].

> "We can now proceed to the disposal of the body. It's a timeworn method, but I'm afraid I shall have to ask you if there's such a thing in the house as a trunk?" [009-006]

Anthony attended to placing the body in the trunk himself while [[virginia-revel|Virginia]] changed into travelling clothes. He strapped the trunk and waited in the hall. [[virginia-revel|Virginia]] was instructed to take a taxi to [[paddington-station|Paddington Station]], deposit the trunk in the Left Luggage Office, and drop the cloakroom ticket as she passed Anthony on the platform. He would retrieve the ticket and handle the trunk's disposal [009-007].

## The Pistol

Before leaving [[pont-street|Pont Street]], Anthony disposed of the incriminating pistol by climbing a tree and concealing it in the branches:

> "A very clever way of disposing of the pistol," said Anthony to himself with some approval. "Everybody hunts about on the ground, and drags ponds. But there are very few people in England who could climb that tree." [009-008]

## Aftermath

The following morning, Anthony arrived at [[chimneys|Chimneys]]. Shortly after, news came that the body of an unknown man had been discovered at [[pont-street|Pont Street]]. Superintendent [[superintendent-battle|Battle]] of [[scotland-yard|Scotland Yard]] was called in to investigate [011-001].

Anthony reflected on the precariousness of his position:

> "In the short space of twenty-four hours, he had become embroiled in two separate crimes. His actions in connection with the first would not bear looking into for a second. After deliberately disposing of one body, and so defeating the aims of justice, he had arrived upon the scene of the second crime at the exact moment when it was being committed." [012-001]

## Significance

The disposal of the body established the partnership between [[anthony-cade|Anthony Cade]] and [[virginia-revel|Virginia Revel]], binding them together in a shared secret. It demonstrated Anthony's quick thinking and willingness to take unconventional action, traits that would prove essential throughout the investigation at [[chimneys|Chimneys]]. The event also set in motion the chain of discoveries that would ultimately lead to the unmasking of [[king-victor|King Victor]].
