## Overview

[[superintendent-battle|Superintendent Battle]] of [[scotland-yard|Scotland Yard]] arrived at [[chimneys|Chimneys]] on the morning after the discovery of [[prince-michael-obolovitch|Prince Michael Obolovitch]]'s body. His arrival marked the transition from local police investigation to a high-level [[scotland-yard|Scotland Yard]] inquiry, reflecting the political sensitivity of the case due to the presence of [[george-lomax|George Lomax]] and the impending diplomatic conference.

## Arrival and Initial Assessment

[[superintendent-battle|Battle]] arrived approximately half an hour before his first meeting with the house party. He had already toured the scene of the crime with [[inspector-badgworthy|Inspector Badgworthy]] and interviewed [[dr-cartwright|Dr. Cartwright]], the police surgeon.

> "Superintendent Battle arrived half an hour ago. He has been round with Inspector Badgworthy, and seen Dr. Cartwright. He now wants a few facts from us" [011-016]

## Investigation of the House Party

[[superintendent-battle|Battle]] conducted interviews with the principal members of the house party, gathering facts about the events of the previous evening. His questioning was characteristically understated — he listened more than he spoke, his eyes occasionally widening at significant details while otherwise maintaining an impassive exterior. [[george-lomax|George Lomax]] impressed upon him the need for utmost discretion given the diplomatic stakes.

[[superintendent-battle|Battle]]'s methodical approach contrasted with [[george-lomax|George Lomax]]'s pompous manner. [[lord-caterham|Lord Caterham]] found the superintendent's offhand manner rather to his liking, while George's repeated emphasis on secrecy and discretion appeared to leave [[superintendent-battle|Battle]] unmoved.

> "I need hardly tell you, Battle, that this is a case in which we must use the utmost discretion" [011-018]

## Significance

[[superintendent-battle|Battle]]'s arrival transformed the investigation. Unlike the local police, [[superintendent-battle|Battle]] immediately grasped the political dimensions of the case — the connection to [[herzoslovakian-oil-concession|Herzoslovakian oil]] concessions, the presence of international financiers, and the imminent diplomatic conference. His quiet competence established him as the novel's central detective figure, contrasting with the amateur efforts of [[anthony-cade|Anthony Cade]] and the bureaucratic concerns of [[george-lomax|George Lomax]].
