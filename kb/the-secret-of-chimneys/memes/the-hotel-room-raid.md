# The Hotel Room Raid

On his first night in [[london|London]], [[anthony-cade|Anthony Cade]]'s room at [[blitz-hotel|the Blitz]] Hotel is targeted by agents of the [[comrades-of-the-red-hand|Comrades of the Red Hand]], who attempt to steal [[count-stylptitch-memoirs|the memoirs]] and letters in his possession.

## Summary

[[anthony-cade|Anthony Cade]] arrives in [[london|London]] under the name of [[james-mcgrath|Jimmy McGrath]] and checks into [[blitz-hotel|the Blitz]] Hotel. He locks [[the-code-letters|the packet]] of memoirs and the letters from [[giuseppe-manelli|Giuseppe]]'s wife in his suitcase before going down to dine (005-086).

While Anthony is dining in the Grill Room, his room is entered and searched. The intruder identifies himself with the sign of the [[comrades-of-the-red-hand|Comrades of the Red Hand]] (005-104). When Anthony returns to his room unexpectedly, he confronts the intruder — a man who gives his name as [[giuseppe-manelli|Giuseppe]] (005-121).

Anthony subdues the attacker, but the [[comrades-of-the-red-hand|Comrades]]' attempt reveals that [[count-stylptitch-memoirs|the memoirs]] are already the target of a determined revolutionary organization. From this point on, Anthony knows he is being watched and that the documents are worth killing for.

## The Comrades of the Red Hand

The [[comrades-of-the-red-hand|Comrades of the Red Hand]] are a revolutionary organization opposed to the monarchist faction in [[herzoslovakia|Herzoslovakia]]. They seek [[count-stylptitch-memoirs|the memoirs]] because the documents contain information that could destabilize their political ambitions. Their involvement connects the [[london|London]] crime to the broader European political conspiracy (005-104).

## Key Participants

- [[anthony-cade]] — target of the raid, in possession of [[count-stylptitch-memoirs|the memoirs]]
- [[giuseppe-manelli]] — the intruder, a Comrade of the [[red-hand|Red Hand]]

## Significance

The hotel room raid is the first act of physical violence in the novel, transforming the memoir delivery from a simple courier job into a dangerous mission. It establishes the [[comrades-of-the-red-hand|Comrades of the Red Hand]] as a credible threat and signals that multiple factions are actively pursuing the documents.
