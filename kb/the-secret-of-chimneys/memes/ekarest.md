## Description

Ekarest is the capital of the fictional Balkan nation of [[herzoslovakia|Herzoslovakia]]. It is described as the seat of the [[herzoslovakia|Herzoslovakia]]n monarchy, with a cathedral where royal ceremonies are held [0449-0449]. The country itself is characterized as having a "population, chiefly brigands" whose "hobby" is "assassinations of reigning monarchs and revolutions" [0449-0449].

## Role in the Novel

Ekarest appears primarily through backstory references. [[nicholas-iv|King Nicholas IV]] of [[herzoslovakia|Herzoslovakia]] married [[queen-varaga|Queen Varaga]], a music-hall performer, "in the cathedral at Ekarest with a couple of unwilling Archbishops to do the ceremony" [0449-0449]. This controversial marriage, conducted at the heart of the [[herzoslovakia|Herzoslovakia]]n capital, was one of the precipitating events that led to the revolution that overthrew the monarchy.
