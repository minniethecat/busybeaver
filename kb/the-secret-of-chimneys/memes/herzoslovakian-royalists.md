# Herzoslovakian Royalists

The Loyalist (monarchist) political faction of [[herzoslovakia|Herzoslovakia]], working in exile to restore the Obolovitch dynasty to the throne after the revolution that established the republic, and competing with the Revolutionary party for diplomatic recognition and the missing Koh-i-Noor diamond.

## Description

The Royalist party (also called the Loyalist party) represents the monarchist faction in [[herzoslovakia|Herzoslovakia]]n exile politics. Their [[london|London]] representative is [[baron-lolopretjzyl|Baron Lolopretjzyl]], who introduces himself: "I represent in [[london|London]] the Loyalist party of [[herzoslovakia|Herzoslovakia]]" (941-PN). The Royalists maintain an intelligence operation capable of tracking [[anthony-cade|Anthony Cade]]'s movements — Anthony "certainly congratulate[s] the Intelligence Department of the Loyalists" on their efficiency (995-PN). They are one of several [[herzoslovakia|Herzoslovakia]]n political factions Anthony encounters: "I've already fallen foul of both the Loyalist and the Revolutionary parties. Soon, I suppose, the Nationalists and the Independent Liberals will be sending up a delegation" (1093-PN).

## Role in the Novel

The Royalists are one of several competing factions seeking control of [[koh-i-noor-diamond|the K]]oh-i-Noor diamond, which they view as a symbol of legitimate monarchical authority. [[baron-lolopretjzyl|Baron Lolopretjzyl]] attempts to purchase [[count-stylptitch|Count Stylptitch]]'s memoirs from [[anthony-cade|Anthony Cade]] at [[blitz-hotel|the Blitz]] Hotel, believing they contain information vital to the Royalist cause. The Royalists' political rivals — the [[comrades-of-the-red-hand|Comrades of the Red Hand]] — are willing to use violence to prevent the restoration. [[superintendent-battle|Superintendent Battle]] notes that the Royalists, along with [[george-lomax|George Lomax]]'s diplomatic circle, are "in the soup" regarding the murder investigation ([[TOC|31]]05-PN).
