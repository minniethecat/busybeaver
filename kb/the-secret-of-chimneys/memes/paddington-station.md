# Paddington Station

[[london|London]] railway terminus serving the Great Western Railway, used as a key transit point in the novel where [[virginia-revel|Virginia Revel]] departs for [[chimneys|Chimneys]] and where [[anthony-cade|Anthony Cade]] orchestrates a cloakroom ticket hand-off to conceal [[koh-i-noor-diamond|the K]]oh-i-Noor diamond inside a diplomatic trunk.

## Description

Paddington Station is a major [[london|London]] railway terminus. In the novel it features the Left Luggage Office (cloak room) on the Departure side and a separate cloak room on the Arrival side — a detail that [[anthony-cade|Anthony Cade]] exploits for operational security. The station serves the line to [[chimneys|Chimneys]] and the surrounding country.

## Role in the Novel

Paddington Station serves as the operational hub for [[anthony-cade|Anthony Cade]]'s plan to transport [[koh-i-noor-diamond|the K]]oh-i-Noor diamond. Rather than carrying the jewel directly, he orchestrates a cloakroom ticket hand-off on the platform. [[boris-anchoukoff|Boris Anchoukoff]] deposits the trunk containing [[koh-i-noor-diamond|the diamond]] in the Left Luggage Office, drops the ticket as he passes Anthony on the platform, and Anthony retrieves it (2069-PN). He later returns to Paddington, collects the trunk, and moves it to "the other cloak room this time, the one on the Arrival side" — a tactical relocation to throw off pursuers (2097-PN). [[virginia-revel|Virginia Revel]] later confirms she will "pick up the trunk again when I go back to Paddington" (3293-PN).

## Key Events

- **[[virginia-revel|Virginia]] departs for [[chimneys|Chimneys]]** — [[virginia-revel|Virginia]] tells her chauffeur Walton she is "going by the 6:40 from Paddington" for the weekend at [[chimneys|Chimneys]] (1693-PN)
- **The cloakroom ticket hand-off** — Anthony instructs [[boris-anchoukoff|Boris]] to put the trunk in Left Luggage, then drop the cloak room ticket as he passes Anthony on the platform; Anthony picks it up in a staged "return" (2069-PN)
- **Trunk relocation** — Anthony drives back to Paddington, retrieves the trunk from the Departure-side cloak room, and re-deposits it in the Arrival-side cloak room for added security (2097-PN)
- **[[virginia-revel|Virginia]]'s recovery** — After the scheme succeeds, [[virginia-revel|Virginia]] confirms she can retrieve the trunk from Paddington (3293-PN)
