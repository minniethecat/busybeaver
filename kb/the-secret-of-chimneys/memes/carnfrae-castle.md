# Carnfrae Castle

Carnfrae Castle is a Scottish estate mentioned in the novel as one of the country houses that populate the aristocratic world surrounding [[chimneys|Chimneys]]. Like [[wyvern-abbey|Wyvern Abbey]] — [[george-lomax|George Lomax]]'s neighbouring estate — and [[granarth-castle|Granarth Castle]] — the Duke of Perth's seat — Carnfrae Castle forms part of the network of grand houses whose occupants are drawn into the [[herzoslovakia|Herzoslovakia]]n intrigue.

## Narrative Function

The mention of Carnfrae Castle, alongside other country houses, establishes the social milieu of the novel — a world of weekend parties, political houseguests, and informal diplomacy conducted across the great houses of Britain. This network of estates provides the social fabric against which the political conspiracy at [[chimneys|Chimneys]] unfolds.
