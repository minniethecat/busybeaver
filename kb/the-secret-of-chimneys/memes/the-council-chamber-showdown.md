# The Council Chamber Showdown

The climactic events of the novel unfold in [[council-chamber|the Council Chamber]] at [[chimneys|Chimneys]], where [[superintendent-battle|Superintendent Battle]] and [[monsieur-lemoine|M. Lemoine]] orchestrate the final confrontation that exposes [[king-victor|King Victor]]'s identity and resolves the [[herzoslovakia|Herzoslovakia]]n conspiracy.

## Summary

Throughout the house party, [[council-chamber|the Council Chamber]] at [[chimneys|Chimneys]] has been the focal point of intrigue — from the discovery of the body to the secret diplomatic conference. In the final chapters, [[superintendent-battle|Superintendent Battle]] and [[monsieur-lemoine|M. Lemoine]] use the Chamber as the stage for their carefully planned trap (025-073).

The investigation has revealed that [[king-victor|King Victor]], the master criminal, has been operating under an assumed identity among the guests at [[chimneys|Chimneys]]. [[superintendent-battle|Battle]] has known this for some time and has been tracking [[king-victor|King Victor]]'s movements (025-046). The climactic scene brings all the principal characters together in [[council-chamber|the Council Chamber]], where the web of deception is finally untangled.

Key revelations include: the true identity of the man calling himself [[king-victor|King Victor]], the location of the missing Koh-i-Noor diamond, the coded message hidden in [[count-stylptitch|Count Stylptitch]]'s memoirs, and the full scope of the plot to control [[herzoslovakia|Herzoslovakia]]'s oil-rich future (025-040).

## Location

[[the-council-chamber-at-chimneys|The Council Chamber at Chimneys]] is the historic great hall of the estate, where the original Koh-i-Noor diamond was once kept. Its symbolic significance as the site of the original theft and the final resolution ties the mystery together across decades.

## Key Participants

- [[superintendent-battle]] — orchestrates the final confrontation
- [[monsieur-lemoine]] — reveals his true investigative role
- [[anthony-cade]] — instrumental in piecing together the mystery
- [[lady-eileen-bundle-brent]] — provides key witness testimony

## Significance

The Council Chamber Showdown is the climax of the novel, resolving all three major plot threads: the murder of Prince Michael, the theft of [[koh-i-noor-diamond|the K]]oh-i-Noor, and the political conspiracy over [[herzoslovakia|Herzoslovakia]]. It completes the story arc that began with the memoir delivery in [[bulawayo|Bulawayo]].
