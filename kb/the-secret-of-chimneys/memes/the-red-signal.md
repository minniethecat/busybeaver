## Overview

The Red Signal was a coded warning sent by [[boris-anchoukoff|Boris Anchoukoff]] from the grounds of [[chimneys|Chimneys]] on the night of the diplomatic conference. Using a red light visible from a distance, [[boris-anchoukoff|Boris]] activated the emergency communication system of the [[comrades-of-the-red-hand|Comrades of the Red Hand]] — the revolutionary organisation to which both he and [[king-victor|King Victor]] belonged.

## The Coded Warning

[[superintendent-battle|Superintendent Battle]] and [[anthony-cade|Anthony Cade]] had been monitoring [[boris-anchoukoff|Boris]]'s movements. The red signal was the culmination of a tense surveillance operation conducted from the library at [[wyvern-abbey|Wyvern Abbey]], where [[superintendent-battle|Battle]] and [[george-lomax|George Lomax]] were coordinating the security arrangements for the conference.

> Superintendent Battle was standing in the library at Wyvern Abbey. [022-001]

[[superintendent-battle|Battle]] had already recovered the coded packet of letters that Anthony found on his dressing table — communications between the conspirators that [[superintendent-battle|Battle]] had been methodically decoding.

> "They're in code, you say?" "Just so, Mr. Lomax." [022-006]

## Strategic Significance

The red signal was part of the [[comrades-of-the-red-hand|Comrades of the Red Hand]]'s operational protocol. [[boris-anchoukoff|Boris]], a loyal lieutenant to [[king-victor|King Victor]], used it when he detected that [[superintendent-battle|Battle]]'s investigation was closing in on the master conspirator. The signal was intended to alert [[king-victor|King Victor]] to flee or take defensive action before the net tightened.

The incident demonstrated the sophistication of the revolutionary organisation's communication methods and confirmed [[superintendent-battle|Battle]]'s theory that the murder of Prince Michael was part of a larger conspiracy orchestrated by the [[comrades-of-the-red-hand|Comrades]].

## Aftermath

[[superintendent-battle|Battle]] intercepted the signal's meaning through his decoding work and used it to confirm [[king-victor|King Victor]]'s identity. Rather than acting prematurely, [[superintendent-battle|Battle]] allowed events to unfold toward the climactic confrontation in [[council-chamber|the Council Chamber]].
