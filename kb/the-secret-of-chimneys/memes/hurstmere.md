## Description

Hurstmere is a substantial private house located on [[dover|Langly Road]] in [[dover|Dover]]. It stands at the end of a long road, "well back, enclosed in its own grounds" [3026-3026]. The house has a terrace running along its frontage.

## Role in the Novel

[[anthony-cade|Anthony Cade]] traces [[koh-i-noor-diamond|the K]]ing [[king-victor|Victor]] gang to this address after finding the name and address written on a shirt-cuff: "[[dover|Hurstmere, Langly Road]], [[dover|Dover]]" [2991-2991]. He travels to [[dover|Dover]] and locates the house, which serves as the headquarters for [[king-victor|King Victor]]'s operations. Anthony approaches the house "with a careless smile on his lips" [3012-3012], maintaining his characteristic composure as he prepares to infiltrate the criminal hideout.
