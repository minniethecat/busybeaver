# Cape Town

Cape Town is a port city in [[south-africa|South Africa]], part of the colonial maritime network that provides the backdrop to [[anthony-cade|Anthony Cade]]'s African past. Along with [[bulawayo|Bulawayo]], Cape Town represents the southern African world from which Anthony emerges before his journey to [[england|England]].

## Connection to the Plot

The [[union-castle-line|Union Castle Line]] shipping route between South Africa and [[england|England]] — which brings [[anthony-cade|Anthony Cade]] to [[southampton|Southampton]] on the *Granarth Castle* — passes through Cape Town. The city forms part of the colonial infrastructure of shipping companies, tour operations (such as Castle's Select Tour), and adventuring Englishmen that constitutes Anthony's pre-[[chimneys|Chimneys]] existence.
