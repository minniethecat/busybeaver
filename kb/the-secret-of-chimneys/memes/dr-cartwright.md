## Role in the Novel

Dr. Cartwright is the local doctor who is summoned to Chimneys when Prince Michael
Obolovitch's body is discovered [010-001]. He accompanies Inspector Badgworthy and
Constable Johnson to the scene of the crime.

Upon arrival, Cartwright kneels by the body and conducts the preliminary medical
examination [010-006]. He establishes the cause of death — a bullet fired from a
revolver — and determines that death occurred several hours before the body was
found [011-010].

## Description

Described as "a comparatively young man" [010-002], Dr. Cartwright displays "a
keen interest" in the murder case [010-002]. His attitude is compared to that of
Constable Johnson — both are excited by the novelty of a real murder in their
quiet district [010-002].

Cartwright has a "faint twinkle in his eye" [010-005] and is not afraid to gently
tease Lord Caterham, questioning him when his account of events is inconsistent
[010-004]. His professional competence is confirmed by Superintendent Battle,
who interviews him upon arrival [011-009].

## Key Relationships

- **Inspector Badgworthy**: Accompanies the inspector to Chimneys [010-001]
- **Constable Johnson**: Fellow local official, shares his excitement at the case [010-002]
- **Lord Caterham**: Attends to the agitated Lord Caterham at Chimneys [010-004]
- **Superintendent Battle**: Interviewed by Battle as part of the investigation [011-009]

## Key Events

- Collected by Inspector Badgworthy en route to Chimneys [010-001]
- Examines Prince Michael's body in the Council Chamber and confirms death by
  gunshot [010-006]
- Establishes the approximate time of death — several hours before discovery,
  at approximately 7:45 a.m. [011-010]
- Interviewed by Superintendent Battle about his findings [011-009]

## Significance

Dr. Cartwright serves the narrative function of providing medical authority for the
murder investigation. His examination establishes the forensic facts — gunshot wound,
time of death — that frame the murder mystery. As a local figure, he also represents
the impact of the extraordinary events at Chimneys on the quiet village community.
