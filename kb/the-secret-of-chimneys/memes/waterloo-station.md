# Waterloo Station

Waterloo Station is the [[london|London]] railway terminus where [[anthony-cade|Anthony Cade]] arrives after disembarking at [[southampton|Southampton]]. Traveling under the name of [[james-mcgrath|Jimmy McGrath]], Anthony "stepped out of the boat train at Waterloo, hailed a taxi, and after a moment's hesitation ordered the driver to proceed to the [[blitz-hotel|Blitz Hotel]]" (875-PN).

## Narrative Context

Waterloo Station marks Anthony's entry point into [[england|England]] and the beginning of his entanglement in the [[herzoslovakia|Herzoslovakia]]n conspiracy. The arrival is complicated by a miscommunication at the Union Castle Line office: [[bill-eversleigh|Bill Eversleigh]]'s inaudible pronunciation caused the clerk to look up the wrong ship, resulting in Anthony arriving two days earlier than expected (875-PN).
