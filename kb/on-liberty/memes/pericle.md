# Pericle

Athenian statesman — Mill holds Pericles as the ideal balanced character, combining pagan self-development with self-mastery, superior to both Knox and Alcibiades

## Vita e contesto storico

Historical figure relevant to Mill's argument in *Sulla Libertà*.

## Ruolo in *Sulla Libertà*

Mill invokes Pericle as part of his broader argument about liberty, authority, and the development of human character. The historical significance of this figure serves as evidence for Mill's claims about the nature and limits of social and political power.
