# Martin Lutero

German theologian and initiator of the Protestant Reformation — Mill references Luther in the context of religious authority and the history of toleration

## Vita e contesto storico

Historical figure relevant to Mill's argument in *Sulla Libertà*.

## Ruolo in *Sulla Libertà*

Mill invokes Martin Lutero as part of his broader argument about liberty, authority, and the development of human character. The historical significance of this figure serves as evidence for Mill's claims about the nature and limits of social and political power.
