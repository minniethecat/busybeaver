# Impero Romano

The Roman Empire as the political context for early Christian persecution — Mill references the Empire to illustrate the relationship between political and religious authority

## Natura e contesto storico

A significant historical institution that Mill references in his analysis of liberty, authority, and the relationship between individual conscience and collective power.

## Ruolo in *Sulla Libertà*

Mill invokes this organization within his broader argument about the nature and limits of authority over the individual. As with all institutions discussed in *Sulla Libertà*, the primary significance lies in what this organization reveals about the dynamics of power, the conditions of liberty, and the historical struggle between freedom and control.
