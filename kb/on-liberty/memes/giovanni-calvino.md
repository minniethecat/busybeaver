# Giovanni Calvino

French theologian and founder of Calvinism — Mill critiques Calvinism as the theological doctrine most hostile to individual spontaneity and self-development

## Vita e contesto storico

Historical figure relevant to Mill's argument in *Sulla Libertà*.

## Ruolo in *Sulla Libertà*

Mill invokes Giovanni Calvino as part of his broader argument about liberty, authority, and the development of human character. The historical significance of this figure serves as evidence for Mill's claims about the nature and limits of social and political power.
