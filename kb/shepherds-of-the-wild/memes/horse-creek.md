# Horse Creek

Horse Creek is one of the named creeks in the Smoky Land region, part of the network of waterways that sustain the sheep flocks and define the geography of the mountain country.

## Overview

Horse Creek is mentioned alongside Silver Creek, Bear Canyon, and other landmarks of Smoky Land. Like the other named waters of the region, it serves both as a practical reference point for herders and as part of the novel's textured portrait of a specific American wilderness.

The name "Horse Creek" reflects the practical orientation of the mountaineers who named these features — creeks are named for the animals that frequent them or the uses to which they are put.
