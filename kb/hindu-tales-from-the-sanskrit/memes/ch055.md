[055-001]As [[person-sringa-bhuja|Sringa-Bhuja]] followed the servants, [[person-rupa-sikha|Rupa-Sikha]] managed to whisper to

[055-002]him, “Beware! await a message from me!” When he had bathed and was

[055-003]arraying himself in fresh [[object-robe|garments]] provided by his host, waited on,

[055-004]hand and foot, by servants who treated him with the greatest respect, a

[055-005]messenger arrived, bearing a sealed letter which he reverently handed

[055-006]to the prince. Sringa-Bhuja guessed at once from whom it came; and

[055-007]anxious to read it alone, he hastily finished his toilette and

[055-008]dismissed the attendants.

[055-009]“My beloved,” said the letter—which was, of course, from Rupa-Sikha—“My

[055-010]father is plotting against you; and very [[concept-foolishness|foolish]] were you to promise

[055-011]you would [[concept-obedience|obey]] him in all things. I have ten sisters all exactly like

[055-012]me, all wearing dresses and necklaces which are exact copies of each

[055-013]other, so that few can tell me from the others. Soon you will be sent

[055-014]for to the great Hall and we shall all be together there. My father

[055-015]will bid you choose your bride from amongst us; and if you make a

[055-016]mistake all will be over for us. But I will wear my necklace on my head

[055-017]instead of round my neck, and thus will you know your own true love.

[055-018]And remember, my dearest, to obey no future command without hearing

[055-019]from me, for I alone am able to outwit my terrible father.”

[055-020]Everything happened exactly as Rupa-Sikha described. The prince was

[055-021]sent for by [[person-agni-sikha|Agni-Sikha]], who, as soon as he appeared, gave him a garland

[055-022]of flowers and told him to place it round the neck of the maiden who

[055-023]was his promised bride. Without a moment’s hesitation Sringa-Bhuja

[055-024]picked out the right sister; and the [[concept-magic|magician]], though inwardly enraged,

[055-025]pretended to be so delighted at this proof of a lover’s

[055-026]clear-sightedness that he cried:

[055-027]“You are the son-in-law for me! The wedding shall take place

[055-028]to-morrow!”
