# Friendship

## In the Tales

The theme of friendship appears across multiple stories in the Hindu Tales. In "The Crow and Three Friends," a crow forms alliances with other creatures to overcome threats. The bonds between characters — whether genuine or strategic — serve as a narrative engine, propelling action and revealing the nature of those who keep faith and those who betray it.
