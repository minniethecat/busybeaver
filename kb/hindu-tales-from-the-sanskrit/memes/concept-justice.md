# Justice

## In the Tales

Justice in the Hindu Tales operates on multiple levels: the justice of kings who must judge wisely, the cosmic justice that ensures deeds have consequences, and the poetic justice that rewards cleverness and punishes greed. The tales frequently pose moral questions about what constitutes fair treatment, and characters are defined by their capacity to dispense — or subvert — justice.
