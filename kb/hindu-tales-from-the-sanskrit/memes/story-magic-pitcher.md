# The Magic Pitcher

## Overview

The Magic Pitcher is referenced indirectly in the Hindu Tales as part of the broader tradition of magical objects in Sanskrit storytelling. The magic pitcher (or vessel) motif often involves an object that can provide unlimited sustenance or grant wishes, testing the character of its owner.
