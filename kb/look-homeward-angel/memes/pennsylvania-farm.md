# Pennsylvania Farm

## Description

Pennsylvania Farm is a significant location in *Look Homeward, Angel*. The farm in Pennsylvania where Oliver Gant was born and from which he fled westward

> (001-026) This town of Altamont had been settled soon after the Revolutionary War.

## Significance

This place contributes to the novel's rich sense of place and the world that shapes Eugene's consciousness.

> (020-007) They came out at ten-thirty or eleven o'clock, on cold ringing pavements, into a world frozen bare.
