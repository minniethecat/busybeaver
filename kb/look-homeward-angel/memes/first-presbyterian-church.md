# First Presbyterian Church

## Overview

First Presbyterian Church is an organization in the world of *Look Homeward, Angel*. The Presbyterian church attended by Margaret Leonard and other Altamont worthies

## Role in the Novel

The organization serves as part of the institutional fabric of Altamont — the world that shapes and constrains the Gant family.

> (001-026) This town of Altamont had been settled soon after the Revolutionary War. It had been a convenient stopping-off place for cattledrovers and farmers.

## Significance

Wolfe portrays organizations and institutions as sites of belonging and constraint — places where the individual hunger of characters like Gant and Eugene either finds expression or meets resistance.

> (020-007) They came out at ten-thirty or eleven o'clock, on cold ringing pavements, into a world frozen bare — a dead city of closed shops.
