# Eugene's Departure

Eugene's Departure is a significant event in the narrative of *Look Homeward, Angel*. Eugene leaving Altamont for the wider world — the novel's final action, the fulfillment of the hunger for voyages

## Context

The event occurs within the larger arc of the Gant family's story in Altamont.
