[P02-001] To A. B.

> “Then, as all my soules bee,

> Emparadis’d in you, (in whom alone

> I understand, and grow and see,)

> The rafters of my body, bone

> Being still with you, the Muscle, Sinew, and Veine,

> Which tile this house, will come againe.”
