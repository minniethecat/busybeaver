# Shawmut College

Shawmut College appears in the novel's framing device: the Preface is dated from its Historical Section on December 26, 2000 (P01-001). The name 'Shawmut' references the original Native American name for the Boston peninsula, tying the future institution to the deep history of its location. The college's Historical Section preserves 19th-century documents, including West's narrative.
