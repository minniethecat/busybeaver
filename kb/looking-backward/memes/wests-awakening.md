# West's Awakening

Dr. Leete, excavating for a new laboratory in his garden, breaks into West's underground chamber and discovers a living man where only a corpse should be (003-050). The awakening is gradual (004-017) — West first hears 'feminine voices whispering' (004-018), then slowly regains full consciousness over days. The discovery astonishes the Leete family, but they adapt with the characteristic rationality of the utopia, and Leete undertakes the long process of educating West in the ways of the new world (004-005).
