# The Summoning of Love

The central magical event of "[[the-seeds-of-love|The Seeds of Love]]" — at midnight, under a great yew-tree, [[blanchelys|Blanchelys]] lights her [[magic-candles|magic candle]] and summons [[love|Love]], who gives her jewel-like seeds that will grow into the rose tree.

## Overview

[[blanchelys|Blanchelys]], having saved her [[magic-candles|magic candle]] as her grandmother advised, finally lights it on a dark, moonless night. She creeps from the cottage to the garden, where a great yew-tree stands. Under its drooping branches, she lights the candle — and [[love|Love]] appears: a boy with wings, a wreath of pink roses on his hair, carrying a rose branch. He gives her jewel-like seeds and instructions for planting them. (001-136, 001-137, 001-141)

## Context

[[blanchelys|Blanchelys]] and her [[zaire|cousin Zaire]] each received a [[magic-candles|magic candle]] from a [[fairy]]. [[zaire|Zaire]] used hers impulsively; [[blanchelys|Blanchelys]] waited. She has watched the Prince ride past daily, and love has stirred in her heart. The dark, starless night (001-134) provides concealment for an act she must perform alone.

## The Event

[[blanchelys|Blanchelys]] summons her courage, goes to the yew-tree, and lights the candle. [[love|Love]] appears with a soft whirring of wings. "I am [[love|Love]]. What do you want with me?" (001-137) [[blanchelys|Blanchelys]], trembling, makes her [[hope|wish]]. [[love|Love]] gives her seeds and tells her to plant them at sunrise, breathing the beloved's name over them. Then he vanishes, and the candle dies. (001-143)

> "I am Love. What do you want with me?" said the boy; and at the sound of his voice all the wood-doves in the neighbouring trees awoke and began to coo. But Blanchelys trembled and looked at him in silence.
>
> (001-137)

## Consequences

[[blanchelys|Blanchelys]] plants the seeds at sunrise. A tiny shoot appears that very night. The Prince begins stopping at the cottage daily. The rose tree grows in proportion to his love. [[blanchelys|Blanchelys]] marries the Prince, becomes Queen, and bears a son — her happiness directly tied to the tree's health (001-149, 001-151).
