# The Brook Grotto

The special underwater grotto beneath the brook where the [[water-people|water elves]] store [[lamorna|Lamorna]]'s [[reflection|stolen reflection]] — a magical treasure-room at the heart of the [[the-brook|water]] people's domain.

## Overview

After capturing [[lamorna|Lamorna]]'s reflection, the [[water-people|water elves]] need a place to keep it — "they found a special grotto in which to keep it, where they could all go and look at it" (001-049). The reflection is "thirty times as big as any of them," making it a monumental treasure in their miniature world. The grotto functions as both vault and museum — a place of safekeeping and of communal enjoyment.

## Geography and Description

The grotto is located "between some large rocks" beneath the brook (001-049). The reflection is "fastened down" inside to prevent it from rising to the surface and floating away. The space serves as a communal gallery — "all were allowed to look at it, though no one might touch it, for fear it should be injured" (001-049).

## Narrative Significance

The grotto represents the [[the-water-people|water elves]]' dual nature: they are mischievous thieves who stole [[lamorna|Lamorna]]'s reflection, but also careful curators who treasure and protect what they have taken. The reflection "looked very pretty, with the bright red kerchief over the head, and the lips smiling sweetly just as [[lamorna|Lamorna]]'s had smiled when she bent over the [[the-brook|water]]" (001-049).

> They found a special grotto for it—between some large rocks, and there they fastened it down—and all were allowed to look at it, though no one might touch it, for fear it should be injured. It looked very pretty, with the bright red kerchief over the head, and the lips smiling sweetly just as Lamorna's had smiled when she bent over the water.
>
> (001-049)
