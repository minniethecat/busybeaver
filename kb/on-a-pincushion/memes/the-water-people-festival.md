# The Water People's Festival

The celebratory event that concludes the [[the-brook|water]] people's intervention in "[[the-story-of-vain-lamorna|The Story of Vain Lamorna]]" — the [[water-people|water elves]] "made a great festival when they heard this, and danced till morning" (001-117).

## Overview

After [[lamorna|Lamorna]] repents and her reflection is restored, the [[water-people|water elves]] celebrate with a festival. The festival marks a job well done and closes the [[the-brook|water]] people's arc in the tale.

## Context

[[lamorna|Lamorna]] has admitted her fault, her reflection has been released, and she and [[erick|Erick]] have reconciled.

## The Event

The elves dance communally until dawn — a private celebration of moral work successfully completed.

## Consequences

The festival closes the tale's magical intervention arc. [[lamorna|Lamorna]] is cured, [[erick|Erick]] is happy, the moral order of the brook is restored.

## Participants

- [[water-people]] — the celebrating community
- [[the-wise-old-elf]] — the leader whose plan succeeded

> "Anyhow, that is one good thing we have done in the past year," they said. "We have cured Vain Lamorna."
>
> (001-118)
