# Taboret

The [[fairy|fairy godmother]] of [[princess-ursula|Princess Ursula]] in "[[a-toy-princess-tale|A Toy Princess]]," whose combination of magical power and maternal wisdom drives the tale's central substitution — replacing [[princess-ursula|the real princess]] with a magical double so she can be raised with love.

## Overview

Taboret is introduced as the fairy godmother whom [[princess-ursula|Princess Ursula]] "loved very much" (004-005). Unlike [[fairy-godmother|fairy godmothers]] who appear in showers of sparks to grant [[hope|wish]]es, Taboret operates through stealth: "the old fairy Taboret made herself invisible" (004-011) to observe [[princess-ursula|Ursula]]'s life before deciding on her intervention. This careful assessment before action marks Taboret as one of [[de-morgan-author-page|De Morgan]]'s most distinctive magical beings — powerful but restrained, interventionist but thoughtful.

> One day the old fairy Taboret made herself invisible, and flew over to the King's palace to see how things were going on there. She went straight up to the nursery, where she found poor little Ursula sitting by the window, with her head leaning on her hand.
>
> (004-011)

## In the Story

### Role and Significance

Taboret's genius is to solve the problem of [[princess-ursula|Ursula]]'s upbringing without confrontation. Rather than confronting the court, she creates a [[toy-princess|Toy Princess]] — a magical construct so perfect that no one notices the substitution. Her method embodies [[de-morgan-author-page|De Morgan]]'s recurring theme of gentle correction over punishment. Taboret is the collection's most purely benevolent magical figure: she asks nothing in return and her intervention has no hidden cost.

### Key Actions

- [[love|Love]]s [[princess-ursula|Princess Ursula]] and watches over her (004-005)
- Makes herself invisible to observe [[princess-ursula|Ursula]]'s daily life at court (004-011)
- Creates the [[toy-princess|Toy Princess]], a magical automaton indistinguishable from the real [[princess-ursula|Ursula]] (004-058)
- Reveals the substitution when [[princess-ursula|Ursula]] comes of age, reuniting the court with the genuine princess (004-072)
