## Description

Fireland is the underground kingdom of fire-[[fairy|fairies]] in "[[through-the-fire-tale|Through the Fire]]," located in the very centre of the earth "where there is always a nice comfortable fire" (005-021). It is reached by descending through a coal fire — the [[fairy|fire-fairy]] digs a hole in the coals, drops magic marbles into it, and the hole expands into "an immense black gulf" (005-054). The journey down is described as flying through pitch darkness until a faint red light appears, growing "larger and brighter every moment" until the travellers pass through an archway into the realm itself (005-058).

## Landscape

Fireland is a dramatic landscape of volcanic beauty: "There were great hills, and they were of every shade of red and orange, some pale, some bright, and on the hillsides were lakes of fire. The sky was one mass of [[fire-element|flame]], and many of the hills smoked" (005-059). The kingdom contains cities with "tall spires and bridges" (005-064).

## The King's Palace

Near the capital stands the royal palace, "made of red-hot iron, and glistening with precious stones" (005-064) — though [[jack|Jack]] soon discovers that what appeared to be jewels are actually "nothing but different-coloured fire, spouting out all over [[the-castle|the palace]]. There was blue fire, and red fire, and green fire, and yellow fire, shining against [[the-castle|the palace]] walls just like jewels" (005-068).

The palace garden is filled with [[life|living]] fireworks shaped like flowers: "every sort of catherine-wheel turning round as fast as possible, throwing off sparks; and every now and then a brilliant rocket went up into the air, and fell in shining stars" (005-069).

## Inhabitants

Fireland is ruled by the fire King and Queen and is home to their daughter, [[princess-pyra|Princess Pyra]], "much the loveliest woman in Fireland" (005-039). The fire-[[fairy|fairies]] who inhabit the realm are tiny beings about three inches high who breathe fire and live within it — without fire surrounding them, they "would go out at once" (005-015). Young fire-[[fairy|fairies]] under three hundred years old are delicate, and "the least wind is dangerous" to them (005-019).

> She is much the loveliest woman in Fireland, and she's wonderfully clever as well.

## Role in the Story

Fireland is the destination of [[little-jack|Little Jack]]'s first journey with the [[fairy|fire-fairy]]. Here he witnesses the romance between [[princess-pyra|Princess Pyra]] and [[prince-fluvius|Prince Fluvius]] and agrees to undertake the quest to the [[north-pole|North Pole]] on their behalf.
