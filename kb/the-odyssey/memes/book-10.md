[010-001] BOOK X.

[010-002] ARGUMENT.

[010-003] ADVENTURES WITH AEOLUS, THE LAESTRYGONS, AND CIRCE.

[010-004] [[odysseus|Ulysses]] arrives at the island of Æolus, who gives him prosperous

[010-005] winds, and incloses the adverse ones in a bag, which his companions

[010-006] untying, they are driven back again and rejected. Then they sail to the

[010-007] Laestrygons, where they lose eleven ships, and, with only one

[010-008] remaining, proceed to the island of [[circe|Circe]]. [[eurylochus|Eurylochus]] is sent first

[010-009] with some companions, all which, except Eurylochus, are transformed

[010-010] into swine. Ulysses then undertakes the adventure, and, by the help of

[010-011] [[hermes|Mercury]], who gives him the herb Moly, overcomes the enchantress, and

[010-012] procures the restoration of his men. After a year’s stay with her, he

[010-013] prepares, at her instigation, for his voyage to the infernal shades.

[010-014] “At length we reach’d Æolias’s sea-girt shore,

[010-015] Where great [[aeolus|Hippotades]] the sceptre bore,

[010-016] A floating isle! high-raised by toil divine,

[010-017] Strong walls of brass the rocky coast confine.

[010-018] Six blooming youths, in private grandeur bred,

[010-019] And six fair daughters, graced the royal bed;

[010-020] These sons their sisters wed, and all remain

[010-021] Their parents’ pride, and pleasure of their reign.

[010-022] All day they feast, all day the bowls flow round,

[010-023] And joy and music through the isle resound;

[010-024] At night each pair on splendid carpets lay,

[010-025] And crown’d with love the pleasures of the day.

[010-026] This happy port affords our wandering fleet

[010-027] A month’s reception, and a safe retreat.

[010-028] Full oft the monarch urged me to relate

[010-029] The fall of [[troy|Ilion]], and the Grecian fate;

[010-030] Full oft I told: at length for parting moved;

[010-031] The king with mighty gifts my suit approved.

[010-032] The adverse winds in leathern bags he braced,

[010-033] Compress’d their force, and lock’d each struggling blast.

[010-034] For him the mighty sire of gods assign’d

[010-035] The tempest’s lord, the tyrant of the wind;

[010-036] His word alone the listening storms obey,

[010-037] To smooth the deep, or swell the foamy sea.

[010-038] These in my hollow ship the monarch hung,

[010-039] Securely fetter’d by a silver thong:

[010-040] But Zephyrus exempt, with friendly gales

[010-041] He charged to fill, and guide the swelling sails:

[010-042] Rare gift! but O, what gift to fools avails!

[010-043] “Nine prosperous days we plied the labouring oar;

[010-044] The tenth presents our welcome native shore:

[010-045] The hills display the beacon’s friendly light,

[010-046] And rising mountains gain upon our sight.

[010-047] Then first my eyes, by watchful toils oppress’d,

[010-048] Complied to take the balmy gifts of rest:

[010-049] Then first my hands did from the rudder part

[010-050] (So much the love of home possess’d my heart):

[010-051] When lo! on board a fond debate arose;

[010-052] What rare device those vessels might inclose?

[010-053] What sum, what prize from Æolus I brought?

[010-054] Whilst to his neighbour each express’d his thought:

[010-055] “‘Say, whence ye gods, contending nations strive

[010-056] Who most shall please, who most our hero give?

[010-057] Long have his coffers groan’d with Trojan spoils:

[010-058] Whilst we, the wretched partners of his toils,

[010-059] Reproach’d by want, our fruitless labours mourn,

[010-060] And only rich in barren fame return.

[010-061] Now Æolus, ye see, augments his store:

[010-062] But come, my friends, these mystic gifts explore,’

[010-063] They said: and (oh cursed fate!) the thongs unbound!

[010-064] The gushing tempest sweeps the ocean round;

[010-065] Snatch’d in the whirl, the hurried navy flew,

[010-066] The ocean widen’d and the shores withdrew.

[010-067] Roused from my fatal sleep I long debate

[010-068] If still to live, or desperate plunge to fate;

[010-069] Thus doubting, prostrate on the deck I lay,

[010-070] Till all the coward thoughts of death gave way.
