## About This Index

Characters are listed alphabetically by their primary name as used in the novel. The quality tier reflects the depth of each dedicated page: **featured** pages include multi-perspective analysis and substantial quotation sections; **standard** pages cover biographical details, role in the narrative, and key textual references; **basic** pages provide essential identification and context.
