# Event Index

This page catalogues the pivotal events in *Daniel Deronda* (1876): social gatherings, marriage proposals, revelations of heritage, rescues, deaths, and departures that drive the intertwined plots of English society and Jewish destiny.

## About This Index

Events encompass dramatic turning points (Grandcourt's drowning, Deronda's heritage discovery), social rituals (archery contests, musical performances, wedding ceremonies), and moments of private revelation. Each event page situates the occurrence within the novel's narrative arc and traces its consequences.
