## Introduction

Hell Row was the original mining settlement in [[Bestwood]], a "block of thatched, bulging cottages" (001-002) that housed the first generation of colliers before it was demolished and succeeded by "[[the-bottoms|The Bottoms]]." Its name captures the harsh living conditions of the early mining community.

## In the Novel

Lawrence describes Hell Row in the novel's opening geographical survey (001-002–036). "Hell Row was a block of" cramped cottages that "through growing old" became notorious (001-021). It was eventually replaced — "on the site of Hell Row, they" built new dwellings (001-036) — marking the transition from the rough pioneer days of mining to the slightly more settled world of [[the-bottoms|The Bottoms]] that the [[walter-morel|Morel]] family inhabits.
