# Judicial Power Concepts

This page indexes concepts related to the judicial branch, the theory and practice of
judicial power, and the institutional design of the federal courts as defended by Publius
in The Federalist Papers. Alexander Hamilton's essays on the judiciary (Nos. 78–83)
provide the foundational argument for an independent judicial branch with the power of
constitutional review.
