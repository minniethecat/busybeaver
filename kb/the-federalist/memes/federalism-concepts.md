# Federalism Concepts

This page indexes concepts related to federalism, the innovative constitutional principle
at the heart of The Federalist Papers. Publius argued that the proposed Constitution
established a compound republic — neither a pure confederation nor a unitary national
government — in which power is divided between the general government and the states,
each sovereign in its proper sphere.
