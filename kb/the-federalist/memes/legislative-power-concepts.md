# Legislative Power Concepts

This page indexes concepts related to the legislative branch, the design of Congress,
and the theory of republican representation developed in The Federalist Papers. Publius
devoted extensive analysis to the House of Representatives and the Senate, arguing that
a bicameral legislature with distinct modes of election and tenure would best secure
both popular sovereignty and deliberative wisdom.
