# Constitutional Law Concepts

This page indexes concepts related to specific constitutional provisions, clauses,
and legal doctrines discussed in The Federalist Papers. Publius devoted considerable
attention to the text and meaning of particular constitutional clauses — the Supremacy
Clause, the Necessary and Proper Clause, the Commerce Clause, and others — arguing
that each provision was carefully designed to balance governmental energy with
individual liberty.
