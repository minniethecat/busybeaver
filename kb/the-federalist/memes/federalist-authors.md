# Federalist Authors

The 85 essays of The Federalist Papers were written by three men under the shared pseudonym
"Publius": Alexander Hamilton (51 essays), James Madison (29 essays), and John Jay (5 essays).
Together they produced one of the most influential works of political philosophy in American history.
