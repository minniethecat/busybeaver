## Overview

Portugal is the old colonial kingdom whose empire shadows *Eight Hundred Leagues on the Amazon*. Its crown ruled the great river's approaches — the king of Portugal had given the necessary permission for Madame des Odonais to descend the river to her husband. (EHLA-011-064)

## In the Narrative

Its royal house reaches into the New World. In 1824 Brazil proclaimed its independence by the voice of Don Juan, whom the French armies had chased from Portugal (EHLA-012-006), and King John VI of Portugal had a great diamond cut and wore it on his neck on great occasions. (EHLA-019-021)

The kingdom's African reach appears in *Dick Sand* — fugitives were made prisoners and brought back to Portugal (DSCF-019-005), and Paul de Loanda was the capital of the colony which set off from the kingdom of Portugal. (DSCF-019-032)

## Geography & Features

Its domains stretch across oceans. The *Ebba* might head for one of Portugal's insular domains among the Azores (FF-007-020), and vessels went up the coast of Portugal on the Atlantic passage. (TTLU-032-010)

The nation took its place among the powers — England, France, Spain, and Portugal could claim vast territories according to geographical situation (TT-015-024), and Portugal pushed her devotion to the extent of 3,000 cruzades for the lunar scheme. (FEM-012-024)
