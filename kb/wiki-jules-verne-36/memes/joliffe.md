# Corporal Joliffe

## Overview

He is the corporal and steward of the expedition, under whose auspices at Fort Reliance "the large room on the ground-floor was completely transformed" (FC-001-003).

He is a favourite of all the company, for the guests "asked Joliffe, who had a gracious word for every body" (FC-001-022).

## Role in the Story

He revives the half-frozen astronomer who arrives by sledge, since "Corporal Joliffe could think of no better means to restore the lost vital heat than to give him a bath in the bowl of hot punch" (FC-003-028), and "hurried to do the same for the inside" with glasses of punch (FC-003-036).

When the officers are away "the command of the fort fell to Corporal Joliffe, or rather to his little wife" (FC-026-010), and it is his voice that greets the returning party, for "the shouts of Corporal Joliffe welcomed their return to the factory" (FC-027-054).

He busies himself endlessly, undertaking "to amuse" the guests until "he had enough to do" (FC-030-002), and even plays "a kind of foster-father or nurse to the baby" (FC-034-030).

## Character & Traits

He is henpecked but content, for "Corporal Joliffe obeyed his wife without owning it" (FC-001-026).

He is an eager sportsman, announcing "with the greatest satisfaction that there were plenty of these ruminants on this coast" (FC-011-017), reindeer being game "for which Corporal Joliffe amongst others had a special predilection" (FC-011-015).

He frets over the promised wages, protesting that "the Company promised us double pay" (FC-024-014), yet confessing "it isn't that we think much about money, but that the money sticks to us" (FC-024-017).

## Relationships

- [[Jaspar Hobson]] — the lieutenant he serves, beside whom he braves the cold to meet the sledge (FC-003-007).
- [[Thomas Black]] — the half-frozen astronomer he restores with a bath of hot punch (FC-003-028).
- His little wife (Mrs Joliffe) — the shrewd partner he obeys "without owning it," who "nudged his elbow" before the Lieutenant (FC-024-010).

## Appearances

- [[The Fur Country]] — the polar expedition he provisions and enlivens, greeting the returning travellers at Fort Hope (FC-027-054).
