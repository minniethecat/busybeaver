# Neb

## Overview

Neb is one of the five castaways who found the colony on Lincoln Island in *[[The Mysterious Island]]*, and the devoted servant of the engineer [[Cyrus Harding]]. When Neb heard that his master had been made prisoner at Richmond, he left Massachusetts without hesitating an instant and, after risking his life twenty times over, penetrated the besieged town to reach him (MI-002-011).

His whole character is loyalty: he follows his master wherever Harding wishes to go (MI-002-036), and shares fully in the colony's labors, falling naturally, with the sailor [[Pencroft]], into the office of cook (MI-012-071).

## Role in the Story

When the balloon casts the party onto the island and Harding vanishes in the surf, Neb's grief is boundless, for all are strongly attached to the intrepid engineer (MI-003-020). His red eyes betray how he has wept, having lost all hope (MI-005-034), and the inconsolable Neb wanders all night long upon the shore (MI-005-066).

Yet he refuses to abandon the search until he has seen with his own eyes and touched with his own hands the body of his master (MI-006-006). His devotion is rewarded: he is found kneeling beside Harding, laid on a bed of grass and still alive (MI-007-079).

## Character & Traits

Neb's loyalty runs to the edge of death — had the dog Top not found the engineer, he says, he would have buried his master and then lain down on his grave to die (MI-008-017).

Steadfast and tireless, "the Negro Neb" is the emotional heart of the colony, his fortunes bound wholly to those of Cyrus Harding (MI-004-001).

## Relationships

- [[Cyrus Harding]] — the engineer and colony leader, Neb's master, whom he serves with absolute devotion (MI-002-011).
- [[Pencroft]] — the sailor and fellow colonist, with whom Neb shares the duties of cook (MI-012-071).
- [[Herbert]] and Gideon Spilett — fellow founding colonists of Lincoln Island.
