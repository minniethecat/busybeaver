# The Master of the World — CHAPTER 9

[MW-009-001]THE SECOND LETTER

[MW-009-002]On Board the Terror

[MW-009-003]July 15.

[MW-009-004]To the Old and New World,

[MW-009-005]The propositions emanating from the different governments of Europe, as also that which has finally been made by the United States of America, need expect no other answer than this:

[MW-009-006]I refuse absolutely and definitely the sums offered for my invention.

[MW-009-007]My machine will be neither French nor German, nor Austrian nor Russian, nor English nor American.

[MW-009-008]The invention will remain my own, and I shall use it as pleases me.

[MW-009-009]With it, I hold control of the entire world, and there lies no force within the reach of humanity which is able to resist me, under any circumstances whatsoever.

[MW-009-010]Let no one attempt to seize or stop me. It is, and will be, utterly impossible. Whatever injury anyone attempts against me, I will return a hundredfold.

[MW-009-011]As to the money which is offered me, I despise it! I have no need of it. Moreover, on the day when it pleases me to have millions, or billions, I have but to reach out my hand and take them.

[MW-009-012]Let both the Old and the New World realize this: They can accomplish nothing against me; I can accomplish anything against them.

[MW-009-013]I sign this letter:

[MW-009-014]The Master of the World.
