## Overview

Teheran is the Persian capital, a byword for the fabrics of the East and a terminus of the great Asian railways. There is a profusion of the fabrics of Teheran, Shiraz, and Kandahar, carpets marvelous in weaving and colour. (ASC-001-043) A branch line unites the Transcaspian with Teheran in Persia.
