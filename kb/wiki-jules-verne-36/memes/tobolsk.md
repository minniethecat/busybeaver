## Overview

Tobolsk is a government of Western Siberia and one of the provinces queried when the insurrection cuts the telegraph, named beside Yeniseisk, Omsk, and Semipolatinsk. (MS-002-021)

Siberia is divided into several such governments — Tobolsk, Yeniseisk, Irkutsk, Omsk, and Yakutsk — together with the districts of Okhotsk and Kamtschatka. (MS-003-002)

## In the Narrative

At the outbreak a telegram is sent to the Cossacks of the government of Tobolsk, ordering them to concentrate their forces. (MS-002-060) The loyal Cossacks of Tobolsk then advance by forced marches towards Tomsk, hoping to cut off the Tartar columns. (MS-013-031)

The province recurs as a landmark of exile and empire: political exiles return from Tobolsk, from Yakutsk, from Irkutsk. (MS-003-021)

## Geography & Features

The government stretches across the wild steppes of Western Siberia; the greater Tartar horde extends as far west as the governments of Omsk and Tobolsk. (MS-003-058)

It was amid these steppes of the provinces of Omsk and Tobolsk that the huntsman Strogoff raised his son Michael to endure hardship. (MS-004-035)
