## Overview

Iceland is the real northern island where the expedition of *[[journey-to-the-center-of-the-earth|Journey to the Center of the Earth]]* begins its true descent. [[professor-lidenbrock|Professor Lidenbrock]] travels there armed with letters of introduction to "the Governor of Iceland" (JCE-008-006), for the runic parchment that sets the whole adventure in motion is written in old Icelandic and points to an Icelandic volcano as the gateway underground.

The island is the threshold between the ordinary world and the subterranean one. For Lidenbrock its interest lies not in its surface but below: as he declares, "it is not what is upon this island, but what is underneath, that interests me" (JCE-009-039).

## In the Narrative

The travelers reach Iceland by sea aboard the schooner *Valkyria*, which after a long passage "dropped her anchor before Rejkiavik" (JCE-009-023), the island's small capital. Stepping ashore, the narrator finds himself at last "treading the soil of Iceland" (JCE-009-028), the launching point for the journey inland.

At Reykjavik they are received by the island's chief official, "the Governor of the island" (JCE-009-029), Baron Trampe, whose introductions smooth their way. From the capital the expedition sets out overland toward the volcano through which they will descend.

## Geography & Features

Iceland is presented as a remote, volcanic northern land, approached past its outlying islands and capes. The schooner makes out the southernmost of the offshore islands and rounds Cape Reykjanes before reaching the anchorage at the capital (JCE-009-023), a coastline of scattered islets and headlands.

The island also carries a deep cultural antiquity in the novel. Runic characters "were in use in Iceland in former ages" (JCE-002-024), and it is this Icelandic literary heritage — the runic manuscript — that furnishes the cipher leading Lidenbrock underground.

## Connections

Iceland is the homeland of the runic secret decoded by [[Professor Lidenbrock]] and the site of [[Snæfellsjökull]], the volcano through which the expedition descends toward the [[Liedenbrock Sea]] in *[[Journey to the Center of the Earth]]*.
