## Overview

Geneva is the lakeside city of *Master Zacharius* — the city of Geneva lies at the west end of the lake of the same name. (MZ-001-002)

## In the Narrative

It is the stage for the clockmaker's fall. There was no man in Geneva to compare in interest with this Zacharius. (MZ-001-005)

His craft made him the town's glory: the best workers in Geneva readily recognized his superiority, saying the glory of having invented the escapement belonged to him. (MZ-001-007) When his watches began to fail, the news of their strange derangements had a prodigious effect upon the master clockmakers of Geneva. (MZ-003-004)

## Geography & Features

It is a city of a particular spiritual moment — his daughter led a mystical existence in Geneva, which had not as yet been delivered over to the dryness of Calvinism. (MZ-001-017)

Its commercial temper was proverbial: the severity of the Geneva merchant in business matters has become a byword. (MZ-002-002)
