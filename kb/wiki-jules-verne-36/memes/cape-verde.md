# Cape Verde

## Overview

The Cape Verde Islands are the real volcanic archipelago off the West African coast — a waypoint on the *Duncan*'s Atlantic voyage in *In Search of the Castaways* and, for Captain Nemo, a surviving peak of the drowned Atlantis. Glenarvan's party debates calling there: "are there no ports in the Cape Verde Islands that we might touch at?" (SC-008-029)

## In the Narrative

Paganel expects scientific profit from the stop — "I have no doubt geographical science will be a gainer by your sojourn in the Cape Verde Islands." (SC-008-032)

He recalls an explorer who "touched at the Cape Verde Islands, and he explored the most interesting of the group, and went to the top of the volcano in Isle Fogo." (SC-008-058)

## Geography & Features

The group is a fair-weather anchorage: "The Cape Verde Islands are not much frequented by ships during the rainy season." (SC-008-043)

In Captain Nemo's Atlantis theory the islands are drowned mountaintops — among the peaks "(Madeira, the Azores, the Canaries, the Cape Verde Islands) still emerge above the waves." (TTLU-033-063)

Offshore the sea-floor rears up: "abreast of the Cape Verde Islands, there's another wall just as imposing," confining the submerged continent of Atlantis. (TTLU-041-042)
