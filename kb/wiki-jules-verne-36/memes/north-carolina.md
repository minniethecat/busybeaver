## Overview

North Carolina is one of the forty-four states of the Union at this epoch, its capital the rather important town of Raleigh, about one hundred and fifty miles in the interior of the province. (FF-001-006) The strange occurrences of Robur airship began in the western part of this great American State of North Carolina. (MW-001-004)

The state was traversed eastward by the thirty-fifth parallel of latitude, the same line which, extended over the ocean, must cut the African coast at Morocco. (FF-007-020)
