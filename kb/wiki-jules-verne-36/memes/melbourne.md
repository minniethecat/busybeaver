## Overview

Melbourne is the great city on Port Phillip that frames Lord Glenarvan's Australian expedition in search of Captain Grant. Paganel recounts how Batman and Falckner first began a settlement at Port Phillip, the bay on which the large city of Melbourne now stands. (SC-035-016)

## In the Narrative

The crossing begins when the Duncan's damaged screw forces a decision: "One of the branches of the screw is twisted, and we cannot get it repaired nearer than Melbourne." (SC-033-068) Paganel resolves it — "let the ship go to Melbourne then... and we will go without her to Twofold Bay." (SC-033-069)

The overland party means to traverse the continent from coast to coast, the alternative to reaching help "at Melbourne." (SC-034-011) The coast between is desolate, "a desert coast, with no communication between it and Melbourne or Adelaide." (SC-030-023) Later disaster strikes the express train which left Melbourne at 11:45 in the evening. (SC-037-032)

## Geography & Features

Set on Port Phillip Bay in the colony of Victoria, Melbourne is the southern anchor of the party's route and the nearest port of repair on the Australian coast.
