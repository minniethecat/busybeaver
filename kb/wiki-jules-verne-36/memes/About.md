# About This Wiki

The **Vernean Voyages Wiki** is a reader's companion to the *Voyages Extraordinaires* — the collected novels and short stories of **Jules Verne** (1828–1905). It catalogs the characters, places, vessels, and inventions that recur across his extraordinary journeys, with each entry cross-linked to the passages where it appears.

## Source Material

- **Corpus**: *The Collected Works of Jules Verne (36 Novels and Short Stories)*
- **Author**: Jules Verne (1828–1905)
- **Language**: English (public-domain translations)

## Usage Notes

- Browse by **All Pages**, or jump to **Random** for serendipity.
- Entries are grouped by type: characters, places, technology, works, events, and more.
- Passage citations use the form `[VVV-NNN-PPP]` (work · chapter · paragraph).
