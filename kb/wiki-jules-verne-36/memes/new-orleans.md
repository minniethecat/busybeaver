## Overview

New Orleans is the great Southern port at the mouth of the [[Mississippi]], a recurring hub of travel in Verne's American stories. It stands in the roll of great American cities, "the large towns of the Union, New York, Boston, Albany, Washington, Richmond, New Orleans" (FEM-003-009), all caught up in the delirium of the Gun Club's enterprise.

It is the natural terminus of the river journey. One traveller "had already visited the greater part of America, and was about to descend the Mississippi to New Orleans" (AMB-001-008) before the war recalled him to France, the city marking the foot of the continent's greatest waterway.

## In the Narrative

In *[[From the Earth to the Moon]]* New Orleans is a fixed point in the columbiad venture. After the journey south "the next day the four travelling companions reached New Orleans" (FEM-013-004), and later the engineer Murchison "re-embarked on board the _Tampico_ for New Orleans" (FEM-014-002), the port serving as the gateway to and from [[Tampa Town]].

It also figures as a wartime harbour. In *[[The Blockade Runners]]* the daring captain reckons that "ships have run the blockade of New Orleans, Wilmington, and Savannah" (BR-001-052), listing the city among the Confederate ports the runners dared, before setting his own course for Charleston.

## Geography & Features

New Orleans is one of the two great gateways for the Atlantic crossing. It is noted that travellers wishing to cross "must go either northwards to New York or southwards to New Orleans" (SC2-001-004), the Southern port standing as the alternative to the Northern one for the ocean passage.

It is likewise a seat of consuls and steamers. One old Breton recalls, "I was the French consul at New Orleans when my only daughter, Catherine, married" (WC-021-015) in *[[The Waif of the Cynthia]]*, and a steamer bound "for the port of New Orleans" (MW-018-004) crosses the Gulf of Mexico in *Master of the World*.

## Connections

New Orleans is the Mississippi-mouth port of the Gun Club's enterprise in *[[From the Earth to the Moon]]*, gateway to [[Tampa Town]], a blockade harbour in *[[The Blockade Runners]]*, and a consular seat in *[[The Waif of the Cynthia]]*, standing with [[New York]] as a door to the Atlantic.
