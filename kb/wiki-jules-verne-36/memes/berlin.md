## Overview

Berlin is the German capital, a centre of science whose observatory clashed with Vienna's over a disputed sighting until Russia showed both were right. (RC-001-025) The Royal Academy of Science in Berlin was among the learned bodies the popular press challenged. (TTLU-001-015)

## In the Narrative

The Conference at Berlin had formulated a special plan to guide the great powers claiming rights of colonization. (TT-001-026) When calamity struck the world's clocks, at London, Paris, Constantinople and Berlin there was not the least shock. (TT-019-017)

## Geography & Features

As a financial centre Berlin subscribed to the Gun Club's venture through the banking house of Mendelssohn. (FEM-012-009) It lies on the great overland route that runs from Paris by Berlin, Petersburg and Moscow. (ASC-022-007)

## Connections

Berlin ranks among the European capitals whose banks and academies help fund the [[Gun Club]] scheme. (FEM-012-009) It stands on the trans-continental road that also passes through [[Paris]]. (ASC-022-007)
