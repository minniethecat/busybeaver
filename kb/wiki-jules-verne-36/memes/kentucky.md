## Overview

Kentucky is a southern state of the United States. In the Vernean corpus it is above all the home of the Mammoth Cave, the vast cavern that Verne repeatedly invokes as the measure of subterranean grandeur across several novels.

It also appears in *The Master of the World* as one of the states over which the mysterious machine Terror is sighted.

## In the Narrative

In *Facing the Flag*, the caverns of Back Cup are ranked against the world's greatest grottoes, among them "the Mammoth Caves in Kentucky," which are judged more extensive still. (FF-009-019) The narrator adds that he knows these Kentucky grottoes personally, "having visited them, as many thousands of tourists have done." (FF-009-020)

Back Cup's finest chamber is later called comparable to "the most marvellous of the grottoes of Kentucky." (FF-009-047)

In *Journey to the Centre of the Earth*, the same cave supplies a yardstick for scale: "the immense mammoth cave in Kentucky" rises five hundred feet above an unfathomable lake, its ramifications explored to forty miles. (JCE-030-013) *The Mysterious Island* likewise numbers "the immense Mammoth caverns in Kentucky" among the great caves of the earth. (MI-057-108)

In *The Master of the World*, the police report the Terror's appearance "in Kentucky near Frankfort," one stop in the machine's tour of the American states. (MW-004-013)

## Geography & Features

Kentucky's defining feature in Verne is underground: the Mammoth Cave, a cavern of gigantic proportions whose vaulted roof rises five hundred feet above a hidden lake and whose galleries run for forty miles. (JCE-030-013)

Verne treats it as a real wonder rather than an invention, a benchmark cavern set beside Fingal's Cave and the grottoes of the Balearics when the scale of a fictional cave must be conveyed. (MI-057-108)

## Connections

Kentucky's Mammoth Cave is the real-world touchstone against which Verne measures his imagined underworlds — the Back Cup cavern of *Facing the Flag* (FF-009-019) and the subterranean vaults of *Journey to the Centre of the Earth* (JCE-030-013) alike are gauged by it.

Through the Terror's passage near Frankfort it also joins the roster of American states that furnish the setting of *The Master of the World*. (MW-004-013)
