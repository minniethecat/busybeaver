# Captain Spade

## Overview

He is introduced as the master of the pirate yacht — the visitor to Healthful House arrives "accompanied by Captain Spade, commander of the schooner Ebba" (FF-001-007).

His station is plain to any observer: asked "the name of the captain of the Ebba, he would have replied, Spade" (FF-002-002).

He attends his master on the fateful visit, when "the Count d'Artigas presented himself in the afternoon at the door of Healthful House, accompanied by Captain Spade, the commander of the Ebba" (FF-002-012).

## Role in the Story

While his master talks, Spade does the scouting, "minutely examining the immediate surroundings of the pavilion and the end of the park in which it was situated" (FF-002-040).

He plans the raid with cold economy: "five men will do... even if the keeper is aroused and it becomes necessary to put him out of the way" (FF-003-027).

Under cover of night "the boat was lowered, and Captain Spade and five sailors took their places in it" (FF-003-046), and at the wall he "drew the key from his pocket, inserted it in the lock and turned it noiselessly" (FF-003-058).

With the warder overpowered, "two of the men then kept guard over him, while Captain Spade and the others entered the house" to seize the inventor (FF-003-082).

## Character & Traits

He is a silent subordinate, for "Captain Spade was not in the habit of addressing him without being first spoken to" (FF-003-003).

His ruthlessness is instant and practical — faced with the doctor, "Spade decided that the best thing to be done was to spring upon him as he passed and stifle his cries" (FF-003-080).

Aboard the Ebba he is every inch the master, "strolling quietly about giving orders" to the crew (FF-004-003).

## Relationships

- [[Ker Karraje]] — the pirate chief, known ashore as Count d'Artigas, whose commands Spade obeys without question.
- [[Engineer Serko]] — his fellow confederate, who sends him off on the abduction with "Good luck, Spade" (FF-003-047).
- [[Simon Hart]] — the disguised warder Gaydon whom Spade's raiders carry off along with their true quarry.
