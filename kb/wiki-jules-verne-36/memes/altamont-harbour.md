# Altamont Harbour

## Overview

Altamont Harbour is the fictional port on the coast of New America, named for the American Altamont by Hatteras's expedition. "His proposition to that effect met with unanimous approval, and the port was forthwith dignified by the title of Altamont Harbour." (ACH-052-057)

## In the Narrative

The harbour is the far-north staging point of the final dash: "They had gone over two hundred miles from Victoria Bay to Altamont Harbour, and were in latitude 87° 5' and longitude 118° 35'." (ACH-052-058)

From it the little sloop sets out toward the Pole: "A quarter of an hour afterwards the little sloop sailed out of Altamont Harbour, and commenced her voyage of discovery." (ACH-053-019)

## Geography & Features

The harbour sits on an open stretch of the New America coast, reachable by sea: "On the 15th they sighted Altamont Harbour, but as the sea was open all along the coast, they determined to go round to Victoria Bay by water." (ACH-057-016)

Its name outlasts the voyage, entered onto the map with the expedition's other discoveries: "The Government confirmed the names of 'Queen's Island,' 'Mount Hatteras,' and 'Altamont Harbour.'" (ACH-058-023)
