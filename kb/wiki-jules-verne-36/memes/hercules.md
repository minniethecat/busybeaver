# Hercules

> **Note:** Inline PN citations for this page use a 4-character work code (`DSCF`); they render as plain text pending RFC-0003. The underlying PN data is valid.

## Overview

Hercules is one of the freed black companions of the castaways in *[[Dick Sand: A Captain at Fifteen]]*, a man of prodigious strength who becomes the little colony's protector. His power is such that to say the word "strong" to Hercules was, perhaps, imprudent (DSCF-010-023).

Yet the giant's gentleness matches his might: he hoists little Jack up in his arms as if the child were only a cork baby, to the boy's cries of joy (DSCF-005-008).

## Role in the Story

Aboard the *Pilgrim*, Hercules is the steady hand that keeps order. When the treacherous [[Negoro]] threatens the young captain, it is Hercules who simply lays his heavy hand upon the cook's shoulder (DSCF-012-031). He carries out his orders with a military preciseness that brooks no delay (DSCF-016-105).

After the wreck and the party's captivity, his strength turns to escape and rescue: with an irresistible bound he springs to the ground and vanishes under cover of the trees amid a shower of balls (DSCF-024-113).

## Character & Traits

Hercules is defined by his tireless devotion. Disguised as a magician, he ventures alone into Kazounde, having days earlier seized Cousin Benedict to learn where Mrs. Weldon was held (DSCF-035-013). Above all, he risks his own life to save [[Dick Sand]] (DSCF-035-029): when the half-drowned novice comes to himself, it is Hercules who kneels beside him, lavishing his care upon him (DSCF-035-018).

Resourceful as well as strong, it is Hercules who, roaming the riverbank at night, is fortunate enough to find the drifting boat that carries the survivors to safety (DSCF-035-042).

## Relationships

- [[Dick Sand]] — the young novice captain whom Hercules serves and, at the risk of his own life, saves from death (DSCF-035-029).
- [[Negoro]] — the treacherous cook whom Hercules subdues with a single hand aboard the *Pilgrim* (DSCF-012-031).
- Little Jack, Mrs. Weldon, and Cousin Benedict — the companions Hercules guards and carries through the African wilderness.

## Appearances

- [[Dick Sand: A Captain at Fifteen]] — as the giant, devoted protector of the *Pilgrim*'s castaways.
