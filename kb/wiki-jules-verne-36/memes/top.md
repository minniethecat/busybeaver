# Top

## Overview

Top is the dog of [[cyrus-harding|Cyrus Harding]] and one of the loyal companions of the [[lincoln-island|Lincoln Island]] castaways. Introduced as a favorite of the engineer who leaps into the balloon car with the party (MI-002-046), Top is a constant presence through the colony's ordeals, and his animal instincts more than once alert the men to unseen dangers.

## Role in the Story

Though a dog, Top functions in the narrative as a genuine member of the party. His senses repeatedly serve the castaways — when he detects a hidden presence the cry goes up that "Top has seen something" (MI-001-028), and the settlers learn to trust that they will be warned should the barking of the dog Top sound in the night (MI-003-011). His disappearances and returns mark turning points in the mystery of the island.

## Character & Traits

Top embodies uncomplicated loyalty. He is bound above all to his master, remaining the favorite of the engineer (MI-002-046) throughout, and his devotion never wavers across shipwreck, exploration, and siege. Where the human characters reason and calculate, Top supplies instinct and fidelity, and the settlers' affection for him — "If only we had had the dog Top!" (MI-006-032) — measures how fully he belongs among them.
