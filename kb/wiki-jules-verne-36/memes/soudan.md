## Overview

The Soudan is a broad region of central Africa which recurs throughout the Vernean corpus as a frontier of exploration and adventure. In *Five Weeks in a Balloon* the aeronaut Dr. Ferguson recites the roll of travellers who ventured into it, and the balloon *Victoria* itself crosses its border on the great transcontinental voyage.

Elsewhere it figures as a theatre of military service in *Off on a Comet* and as a field of geographical research in *The Waif of the "Cynthia"*.

## In the Narrative

In *Five Weeks in a Balloon*, the Soudan runs through Dr. Ferguson's survey of African discovery. He recalls that one traveller "was charged with a mission in the Soudan," (FWB-004-005) that in 1855 Brun-Rollet was appointed consul in Eastern Soudan, (FWB-004-017) and that Vogel was sent into the Soudan in 1853. (FWB-005-063)

The doctor's atlas records the Soudan according to Dr. Barth, (FWB-012-067) and he remembers how Captain Clapperton and Dr. Oudney "had pushed their way through the Soudan country as far as Sackatoo." (FWB-030-015) At length the *Victoria* itself, passing the country of the Tibbous, crosses "the border of the Soudan." (FWB-034-022)

In *Off on a Comet*, Captain Servadac counts the Soudan among his two campaigns before his Algerian posting, (OC-003-014) and it is in the Soudan that his orderly Ben Zoof repaid the debt of a life saved in Japan. (OC-003-018)

In *The Waif of the "Cynthia"*, the geographer Mr. Durrien is renowned for his travels and researches "in Asia Minor and the Soudan." (WC-013-004)

## Geography & Features

Verne treats the Soudan as a vast interior belt reached from the north across desert margins: the *Victoria* attains it only after crossing the Belad el Djerid, "a desert of briers that forms the border of the Soudan." (FWB-034-022) Its expanses are the ground traversed by Clapperton and Oudney on their march toward Sackatoo. (FWB-030-015)

For Ferguson's generation the region is still being mapped, its cartography set down "according to Dr. Barth" in the atlas that guides the balloon. (FWB-012-067)

## Connections

The Soudan is bound above all to *Five Weeks in a Balloon*, whose *Victoria* crosses the border of the Soudan on its passage over central Africa. (FWB-034-022) Its exploration is embodied by the mission charged in the Soudan that Dr. Ferguson recalls. (FWB-004-005)

It links to *Off on a Comet* through Captain Servadac, who numbers a campaign in the Soudan among his soldier's service, (OC-003-014) and to *The Waif of the "Cynthia"* through the geographer Durrien and his researches in the Soudan. (WC-013-004)
