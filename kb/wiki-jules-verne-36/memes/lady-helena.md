# Lady Helena Glenarvan

## Overview

She is the young wife of Lord Glenarvan, sailing aboard the Duncan, for "Lord Edward Glenarvan was on board with his young wife, Lady Helena, and one of his cousins, Major McNabbs" (SC-001-002).

She is Scotch rather than noble, and prized the more for it, being "a charming, high-souled, religious young woman" (SC-003-003).

## Role in the Story

She shares in unravelling the sea-worn document, pressing the search with "well, come, we have made out a good deal already" (SC-002-012), and her husband credits her with the castaways' hope, telling her "if those poor creatures ever see their native land again, it is you they will have to thank for it" (SC-002-080).

When Captain Grant's orphaned children come to Malcolm Castle she receives them tenderly, "drawing the young girl toward her, and taking both her hands and kissing the boy's rosy cheeks" (SC-003-021), yet she will not deceive them, answering "Heaven forbid that I should answer you lightly such a question; I would not delude you with vain hopes" (SC-003-023).

Her compassion sets the voyage in motion, for "Lady Helena was a brave, generous woman, and what she had just done proved it indisputably" (SC-005-001), and she carries the children with her, since "Lady Helena could not refuse Mary's request to accompany her" (SC-005-007).

## Character & Traits

She is gentle with the timid, relieving the shy girl's embarrassment "with an encouraging smile" (SC-003-011).

She is selfless toward her husband's duty, "too much concerned herself about them to grudge her husband's temporary absence" (SC-003-005).

She is capable of deep anxiety for those she loves, and once "passed the night in great anxiety, and could not close her eyes" awaiting Glenarvan's return (SC-004-018).

## Relationships

- [[Lord Glenarvan]] — her husband, toward whom "Lady Helena flew... the moment he alighted" on his return from the Admiralty (SC-004-020).

- [[Mary Grant]] — the orphaned daughter of Captain Grant whom she welcomes and takes aboard the Duncan (SC-004-027).

- [[Major McNabbs]] — the cousin who escorts her to Malcolm Castle and reassures her through her fears (SC-001-002).

## Appearances

- [[In Search of the Castaways]] — the search for Captain Grant, which her compassion first sets in motion (SC-005-001).
