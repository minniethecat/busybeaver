# The Abduction of Thomas Roch

## Overview

The Abduction of Thomas Roch is the night-time seizure of the mad inventor and his keeper from [[Healthful House]], the raid that delivers the secret of the fulgurator into the hands of the pirate Ker Karraje.

The raiders lay their plan beneath the asylum's outer wall. When they stood beneath the rear wall behind which Pavilion No. 17 was situated, the Count knew his quarry was near (FF-003-004).

The scheme is to leave no trace. Captain Spade is charged to arrange matters so that no one will for a minute suspect that Thomas Roch and his keeper have been brought on board the Ebba (FF-003-043).

## What Happens

Under cover of darkness the pavilion is breached. After assuring himself that nobody happened to be in the neighborhood of the pavilion, the captain entered, followed by his men (FF-003-060).

The keeper is overpowered first. As he raised his foot to mount the steps the four sailors sprang upon him, bore him backwards to the ground, and had gagged him, securely bound him hand and foot, and bandaged his eyes before he began to realize what had happened (FF-003-081).

The inventor is taken the same way. Gaydon was carried off in the same manner as Thomas Roch had been (FF-003-085), and the party made for the boat, where the boatswain had made all ready to receive them (FF-003-088).

## Significance

By dawn both men have vanished from the asylum. A few hours later, Thomas Roch and his keeper had been carried off (FF-004-043), spirited aboard the schooner Ebba and out to sea.

The authorities can only record the crime after the fact: Healthful House has been broken into and two persons kidnapped (FF-004-071). The abduction thus places the world's most dangerous weapon at the disposal of Ker Karraje's outlaws, setting in motion the novel's central peril.

## Participants & Setting

- The night seizure of the inventor Thomas Roch and his keeper Gaydon from Pavilion No. 17 at [[Healthful House]].
- Executed by Count d'Artigas, Captain Spade, and the crew of the schooner Ebba, agents of the pirate Ker Karraje.
- Chronicled in *[[facing-the-flag|Facing the Flag]]*.
