## Overview

Maryland is a state of the United States, home to the city of Baltimore. In the Vernean corpus it is the seat of the Gun Club, founded at Baltimore during the Federal war, and the setting of the great North Pole auction in *Topsy-Turvy*.

Its tobacco is prized as far as Scotland, and its people are drawn as a mixed and clamorous population gathered about the Gun Club's exploits.

## In the Narrative

In *Topsy-Turvy* the sale of the polar regions is announced "in the Auction Hall at Baltimore, Maryland, United States of America." (TT-001-040) When the great work begins, Barbicane "had left the city, and the State of Maryland as well, in company with Capt. Nicholl." (TT-010-007)

The decisive instant is timed for "that great city of Maryland." (TT-018-003) The club's origin lies here too: in *From the Earth to the Moon* a new and influential club "was established in the city of Baltimore, Maryland." (FEM-001-002)

## Geography & Features

The state is peopled by a mixed and boisterous population. When the Gun Club celebrates, "all the heterogeneous individuals that compose the population of Maryland" — Irish, Germans, Frenchmen, Scotchmen — cheer in their maternal tongues. (FEM-003-005)

Maryland is also a tobacco land: enterprising merchants "founded the first Glasgow warehouse for dealing in tobacco from Virginia and Maryland." (BR-001-014)

## Connections

Maryland is bound above all to the Gun Club of Baltimore, which appears in *From the Earth to the Moon* and returns in *Topsy-Turvy* to auction the North Pole from the same city. (TT-001-040)

It links to *The Blockade Runners* through its tobacco, exported from Virginia and Maryland to the warehouses of Glasgow. (BR-001-014)
