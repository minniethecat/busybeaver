# Flotsam Point

## Overview

Flotsam Point is a headland on the south-eastern shore of Lincoln Island in *The Mysterious Island*, named for the mysterious chest the colonists recover from its beach. As Pencroft and Neb work the boat around the headland, "the boat soon began to double the point, to which the name of Flotsam Point was given." (MI-024-042)

The point lies about three miles from Granite House, and its beach is where "the stranding had taken place... at high tide." (MI-032-039)

## In the Narrative

The chest cast ashore here is a turning point for the colony, stocking it with tools and instruments. The settlers reach "Flotsam Point, where they had found the precious chest" (MI-027-094), and the case proves valuable to its lining: "the case found at Flotsam Point was lined with this metal." (MI-040-038)

The point later figures in the colonists' war with the convict brig. Under the brig's guns they advance "to within five cables-lengths of that part of the shore terminated by Flotsam Point" (MI-045-028), and later the attackers flee "in the direction of Flotsam Point, out of range of the balls." (MI-045-041)

## Geography & Features

Flotsam Point stands on the route between Granite House and the southern coast. "The distance between Flotsam Point and Granite House was another four miles" by way of the mouth of the Mercy. (MI-027-095) When the *Bonadventure* sails the coast, the captain doubles "Flotsam Point and Claw Cape" before running along the southern shore. (MI-034-081)

It is one of the fixed landmarks of the island's hunting-grounds: the hunters reach them "by turning the rocks from Flotsam Point," never distant from home "more than two or three miles." (MI-042-075) It features in the reporter's photographs of "the curious development of Claw Cape, Flotsam Point, etc." (MI-040-057)
