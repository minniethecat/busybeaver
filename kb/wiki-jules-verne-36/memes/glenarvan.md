# Lord Glenarvan

## Overview

Lord Edward Glenarvan is the Scottish nobleman and owner of the yacht *Duncan* whose voyage drives *[[In Search of the Castaways]]*. When a bottle is recovered from a captured shark, it is Glenarvan who insists it be examined, knowing that bottles found in the sea often contain precious documents (SC-001-021).

The *Duncan* is his own pleasure yacht, the center of attraction wherever she lies (SC-005-014). From the recovered message he draws his purpose: the statement is too explicit for England to hesitate about going to the aid of her three sons cast away on a desert coast (SC-002-081).

## Role in the Story

Glenarvan is a man of decisive resolve. Having deciphered the document, he bids adieu to his young wife and jumps into the express train for Glasgow to make the *Duncan* ready (SC-002-084). Across the pampas he keeps faith with the search, declaring, "Yes, we'll find him — Thalcave has set us on the track, and I have great confidence in him" (SC-018-042).

As a leader he holds his party together through peril. Marooned in a flood-bound tree, he opens a grave discussion of their plight (SC-023-068); on the Australian journey it is he who settles who is to be their messenger (SC-042-091).

## Character & Traits

Glenarvan's courage is bound up with his devotion to his wife. Among the Maori, to spare Lady Helena the attacks of the witches, he walks straight up to the chief Kai-Koumou (SC-055-011).

His one great error springs from his generous trust: he places his confidence in the quartermaster [[Ayrton]], telling him simply, "I will trust to you, Ayrton" (SC-063-036). Even when that trust is betrayed, he remains a man of his word — "you have kept your word, and I will keep mine" (SC-063-071).

## Relationships

- [[Ayrton]] — the treacherous quartermaster whom Glenarvan trusts, to the expedition's cost (SC-063-036).
- [[Paganel]] — the geographer who at last cries that they are looking for Captain Grant where he is not to be found (SC-023-109).
- Lady Helena, Mary Grant, and Major McNabbs — the wife, ward, and cousin who sail with him aboard the *Duncan*.

## Appearances
