## Overview

New Archangel is the principal settlement and capital of Russian America. A few degrees south of New Georgia, on the island of Sitka, it rises as head-quarters of the Russian Fur Company, whose jurisdiction once extended over the whole of the [[Aleutian Islands]]. (FC-030-054)

## In the Narrative

For Hobson's stranded party the town is a hope of refuge. Thanks to a ship's arrival they might reach Fort Michael on Norton Sound, perhaps even New Archangel, a good deal farther south, where they could pass the rest of the winter. (FC-034-007) Yet the lieutenant doubts a messenger could get there: even suppose he reached New Archangel, how could he come to their rescue? (FC-036-035)

## Geography & Features

New Archangel lies far to the south of the drifting ice. A large three-master, evidently a whaler from New Archangel, was seen making for [[Behring Strait]] after having doubled the peninsula of Alaska. (FC-045-033) The settlement stands on the island of Sitka, on the shores of Russian America. (FC-030-054)

## Connections

Six days after their rescue the shipwrecked mariners arrived at New Archangel, the capital of Russian America. (FC-047-004) It is the far southern haven imagined by the castaways of [[The Fur Country]] as their drifting [[Victoria Island]] threatened to break up. (FC-036-035)
