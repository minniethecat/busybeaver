# Mary Grant

## Overview

Mary Grant is the young daughter of the lost Captain Grant. Left an orphan at fourteen, she "resolved to face her situation bravely, and to devote herself entirely to her little brother, who was still a mere child" (SC-004-007).

When she recounts her sorrowful story, she does so "in so simple and unaffected a manner, that it was evident she never thought her conduct had been that of a heroine through those long trying years" (SC-004-011).

Received at Malcolm Castle, she throws herself at the feet of [[Lord Glenarvan]] (SC-004-025), whom Lady Helena presents with "this is Miss Mary Grant and her brother, the two children condemned to orphanage" (SC-004-027).

## Role in the Story

Offered the Glenarvans' help, Mary answers for herself and Robert: "we will thank this noble lord and lady for what they have done for us... and then we'll both go together" (SC-004-033). Aboard the *Duncan* she pours out "her heart to God in prayer for her benefactors," while grateful tears stream down her cheeks (SC-005-015).

Through the long search she bears the suspense with fortitude, and at the crisis of the voyage she "nerved herself to the resolution never to utter the name of her father" (SC-046-002).

## Character & Traits

Her courage is remarked from the first: "That Mary Grant must be a brave girl," says the Major (SC-004-015). Her devotion to her brother never falters — when Robert is carried into danger she watches "in an agony of terror, speechless and almost unable even to see," stretching out her arms toward him (SC-040-055).

## Relationships

- [[Lord Glenarvan]] — the nobleman who takes up her cause and leads the expedition to find her father (SC-004-025).
- John Mangles — the young captain of the *Duncan*, who grows "particularly attentive to Mary Grant" over the voyage (SC-027-024, SC-028-007).
- Captain Grant — her lost father, the object of the whole search (SC-034-020).
- Robert Grant — her younger brother, to whom she devotes herself after their orphaning (SC-004-007).

## Appearances

- [[In Search of the Castaways]] — as Captain Grant's daughter, who sails aboard the *Duncan* in search of him.
