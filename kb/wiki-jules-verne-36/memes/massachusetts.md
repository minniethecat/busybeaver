## Overview

Massachusetts is a New England state of the United States. In the Vernean corpus it is the home state of Cyrus Smith and his companion Neb in *The Mysterious Island*, and the seat of the Cambridge observatory to which Verne's astronomers turn in the lunar novels.

It also supplies the granite of Fort Sumter, the calico of Salem, and a stretch of coast haunted by the Terror in *The Master of the World*.

## In the Narrative

In *The Mysterious Island*, the engineer Cyrus Smith is "a native of Massachusetts," a first-class engineer entrusted during the war with the direction of the railways. (MI-002-004) His devoted servant Neb, hearing of his master's capture, "left Massachusetts without hesitating an instant" to reach him before Richmond. (MI-002-011)

In *From the Earth to the Moon*, the Gun Club's questions are addressed "to the observatory of Cambridge in Massachusetts," (FEM-004-003) and in *Robur the Conqueror* the observatory of Cambridge in Massachusetts again weighs in on a much-disputed astronomical question. (RC-001-034)

In *The Blockade Runners*, Fort Sumter near Charleston is described as "built on an artificial island of Massachusetts granite." (BR-006-031) In *Dick Sand, A Captain at Fifteen*, the unbleached "Mericani" calico traded in Africa is noted as coming "from Salem, in Massachusetts." (DSCF-028-023)

In *The Master of the World*, the Terror is seen scurrying "along the shores of Connecticut and Massachusetts." (MW-006-025)

## Geography & Features

Massachusetts is presented as a New England state of industry and learning: Salem supplies the calico that reaches distant African markets, (DSCF-028-023) and Cambridge houses the observatory that Verne treats as a leading American authority on the heavens. (FEM-004-003)

Its granite is quarried for construction as far afield as Charleston, where it forms the artificial island beneath Fort Sumter. (BR-006-031)

## Connections

Massachusetts is the native state of Cyrus Smith, the first-class engineer whose skill sustains the castaway colony of *The Mysterious Island*. (MI-002-004)

Through the observatory of Cambridge in Massachusetts it links the astronomical debates of *From the Earth to the Moon*. (FEM-004-003) And as one of the shores along which the Terror goes scurrying, it joins the New England coast of *The Master of the World*. (MW-006-025)
