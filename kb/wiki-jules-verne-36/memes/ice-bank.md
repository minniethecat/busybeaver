# Ice Bank

## Overview

The Ice Bank is the vast wall of polar ice that bars the *Nautilus* at the southern limit of the world in *Twenty Thousand Leagues Under the Sea*. To the harpooner Ned Land it is the certain end of the voyage: Nemo "will surely have to stop, if only at the Ice Bank, and he'll return to the seas of civilization!" (TTLU-036-032)

## In the Narrative

The barrier is the crux of the running argument over Nemo's designs. Ned Land insists "nobody can clear the Ice Bank," (TTLU-037-032) while Aronnax burns to know "what's behind this Ice Bank!" (TTLU-037-033)

The Canadian holds the barrier absolute: "you've made it to the Ice Bank, which is already far enough, but you won't get any farther, neither your Captain Nemo or his Nautilus." (TTLU-037-039) Aronnax half concedes: "until ships are built to navigate over tracts of ice, they'll have to stop at the Ice Bank." (TTLU-037-040)

## Geography & Features

The bank is not reached all at once; it is heralded by looser floes: "it wasn't the Ice Bank as yet, just huge ice fields cemented together by the cold." (TTLU-037-020)

Confronted with the seemingly impassable wall, Nemo resolves to break through rather than turn back — "let's shatter this Ice Bank!" (TTLU-037-057) — driving the submarine on toward the pole beyond.
