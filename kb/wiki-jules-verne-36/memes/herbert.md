# Herbert

## Overview

Herbert Brown is the youngest of the five castaways who found the colony on Lincoln Island in *[[The Mysterious Island]]*. A boy of fifteen from New Jersey, the orphan son of a former captain, he travels with the sailor Pencroft, who loves him as if he had been his own child (MI-002-021).

Gallant and already remarkably well informed in the natural sciences, Herbert renders great service to the common cause (MI-013-006); the sailor can always rely on the young boy, who is well up in natural history and has long had a passion for the science (MI-004-014).

## Role in the Story

Constant to his favorite science, Natural History, Herbert names and explains the island's plants and animals for his companions (MI-012-053). His knowledge proves useful again and again, and he grows into a capable marksman — very proud of his first shot with a gun (MI-047-037).

He shares fully in the colony's dangers, showing great courage and much of that presence of mind which may justly be called "the reasoning of bravery" (MI-023-036). In the war against the escaped convicts he is gravely wounded by a bullet (MI-049-082).

Afterward he is stricken by an intermittent marsh fever, at whose bedside Gideon Spilett never leaves him (MI-052-028). Nursed devotedly by his companions, Herbert returns to life as the fever at last abates (MI-050-009).

## Character & Traits

Herbert combines a scholar's curiosity with a boy's agility and nerve. Well up in natural history and passionate about the science (MI-004-014), he serves as the colony's young naturalist, while his steadiness under fire earns him a place among the grown men (MI-023-036).

## Relationships

- Pencroft — the sailor who raised the orphan and loves him as his own child (MI-002-021), and who can barely suppress his emotion when he embraces "Herbert, his boy" (MI-045-011).
- Gideon Spilett — the reporter and the colony's physician, who never leaves Herbert's side through his fever (MI-052-028).
- Cyrus Harding and Neb — fellow founding colonists of Lincoln Island.

## Appearances

- [[The Mysterious Island]] — as the youngest of the five founding colonists of Lincoln Island and the settlement's naturalist.
