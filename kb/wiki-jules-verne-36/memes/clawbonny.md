# Dr Clawbonny

## Overview

He is the ship's doctor of the brig Forward, for "Dr. Clawbonny was a doctor, and a good one, though practising little" (ACH-003-054).

He is wholly in his element aboard the vessel, having "taken possession of his cabin on the 6th of February, the day after the Forward was launched" (ACH-004-004), a cabin "situated at the end of the poop, and occupied all the stern of the vessel" (ACH-004-003).

## Role in the Story

He joins the cruise on the mysterious captain's terms, invited that "if Dr. Clawbonny wishes to embark on board the Forward for a long cruise, he may introduce himself to the commander, Richard Shandon" (ACH-003-042), and it was upon that occasion that he "entered into correspondence with John Hatteras, whom he did not know, requesting to join the expedition" (ACH-012-058).

He frets for the voyage to begin, marching "up and down in agitation, looking through his telescope, gesticulating, impatient for the sea" (ACH-004-031).

When the polar dash is made overland, the party numbers four, "Hatteras, Clawbonny, Bell, and Simpson, and seven dogs" (ACH-028-013), and on that march his life is narrowly saved from the cold, for as Johnson tells him, "your nose was quite white when I looked at you, and without my bit of rubbing you would be minus nose" (ACH-030-015).

## Character & Traits

He is an encyclopaedic mind, forever explaining the Arctic's wonders, so that "scientific men, and Dr. Clawbonny amongst them, explain the fact in the following way" (ACH-024-002), and the boatswain begs him, "can you explain to me the cause of that phenomenon?" (ACH-008-083).

He is defined by an untiring optimism, for "In the long run depicts Dr. Clawbonny in a single phrase" (ACH-010-007).

He is boundlessly kind, a man "whose kindness and caresses would have tamed a tiger" (ACH-004-007), and he gathers knowledge eagerly, "getting information from the old sailor, whose numerous voyages had given him an interesting and sensible education" (ACH-007-003).

## Relationships

- [[Captain Hatteras]] — the unknown captain he wrote to join, and whose cause he defends, crying "you are mistaken, Shandon... the captain will allow me to tell you" (ACH-013-021).

- Richard Shandon — the Forward's commander to whom he first introduces himself on the captain's orders (ACH-003-042).

- Johnson — the old boatswain who instructs him in Arctic lore and saves his frostbitten nose (ACH-030-015).

## Appearances

- [[The Adventures of Captain Hatteras]] — the voyage of the Forward toward the Pole, on which he serves as doctor and savant (ACH-028-013).
