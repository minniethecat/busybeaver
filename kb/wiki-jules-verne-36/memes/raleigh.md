## Overview

Raleigh is the capital of North Carolina, one of the states of the Union, and stands as a rather important town about one hundred and fifty miles in the interior of the province. (FF-001-006) It was to this inland capital that the government inspector Strock travelled while investigating the strange occurrences of the region.

## In the Narrative

Having left Washington the night before, Strock arrived at Raleigh, the capital of the State of North Carolina, on the twenty-seventh of April. (MW-002-002) After receiving his orders he returned at once to his house to make ready, and the next evening again found him in Raleigh, from which he set out toward the mountains. (MW-002-038)

## Geography & Features

The town lies well away from the coast, deep within the province rather than upon its seaboard. When the mysterious flying machine passed over the country, it was later learned that the craft had come down in the neighborhood of Raleigh, the capital of North Carolina. (MW-001-014)

## Connections

Raleigh served as a hub of communication for the wider region and its coastal towns. When word of a disappearance spread, the news was telegraphed to [[New-Berne]], and thence onward to Raleigh, the seat of authority for the whole of [[North Carolina]]. (FF-004-016)
