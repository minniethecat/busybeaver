## Overview

Florida is the American peninsula that the Gun Club selects as the ground for its great lunar experiment in *[[From the Earth to the Moon]]*. When the choice of casting-ground is debated, it is decided that "the Columbiad should be cast either on the soil of Texas or on that of Florida" (FEM-011-016), and Florida ultimately wins the honour.

The peninsula's geography settles the matter. "The 28th parallel, when it touches the American coast, crosses the peninsula of Florida, and divides it into two nearly equal portions" (FEM-011-017), placing it at exactly the latitude the astronomers require for the shot.

## In the Narrative

Florida is framed as a rival to Texas for the enterprise. The narrator recalls that "the soil of Florida, though not so rich, offered better conditions for the casting of the Columbiad, as it was composed of sand and clay-ground" (FEM-011-026), and the two states quarrel bitterly over which shall host the cannon.

Once chosen, the whole peninsula is transformed. When the great gun is finally fired, "the pyramid of flame rose to a prodigious height in the air it lighted up the whole of Florida" (FEM-027-002), and the land "was shaken to its very depths" (FEM-027-003) by the launch.

## Geography & Features

The narrative supplies Florida's political geography in detail. "Florida is divided into two parts; the one to the north, more populous and less abandoned, has Tallahassee for capital, and Pensacola, one of the principal marine arsenals of the United States" (FEM-013-020), while the southern part remains wild and thinly settled.

Its history is given too: "Florida, discovered by Juan Ponce de Leon in 1512, on Palm Sunday, was first of all named *Pascha Florida*" (FEM-013-021). The casting works themselves are set up in the south, for the manufactory pledges "to send to Tampa Town, in South Florida, the necessary materials for the casting of the Columbiad" (FEM-012-034). It is also invoked as a geodetic reference point, "the radius of the earth under the latitude of Florida" (RM-004-097) entering the calculations of the shot.

## Connections

Florida is the launch ground of the [[Gun Club]] and the site of [[Tampa Town]] and [[Stony Hill]], where the [[Columbiad]] is cast and fired in *[[From the Earth to the Moon]]* and *[[Round the Moon]]*. Its warm channels also feed the Gulf Stream, which "after emerging from channels off Florida, it heads toward Spitzbergen" (TTLU-035-003).
