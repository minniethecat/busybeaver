# The Sea Monster Hunt

> **Note:** Inline PN citations for this page use a 4-character work code (`TTLU`); they render as plain text pending RFC-0003. The underlying PN data is valid.

## Overview

The Sea Monster Hunt is the pursuit that opens *[[twenty-thousand-leagues|Twenty Thousand Leagues Under the Sea]]s*. After ships are struck by an unknown object, public opinion splits into two camps: those favouring a monster of colossal strength, and those favouring an underwater boat of tremendous motor power (TTLU-002-006).

## What Happens

The mystery resists explanation, and an expedition is fitted out to settle it — a hazardous undertaking whose purpose was to hunt an animal that could sink a frigate as easily as a walnut shell (TTLU-003-013). Professor Aronnax is invited to join, and he accepts.

He and his companions embark: "We're leaving on the Abraham Lincoln" (TTLU-003-029), the frigate that will carry them in search of the creature under the command of Commander Farragut (TTLU-003-031). The vessel steams into the Pacific to run the monster down.

## Significance

The hunt is the doorway to the entire adventure: the chase ends when the "monster" rams the frigate and Aronnax, [[conseil|Conseil]], and [[ned-land|Ned Land]] are cast into the sea, only to be taken aboard the [[nautilus|Nautilus]]. That the enterprise was framed as hunting an animal that could sink a frigate as easily as a walnut shell (TTLU-003-013) proves truer than intended, and the early debate over a monster of colossal strength or an underwater boat (TTLU-002-006) is resolved when the vessel is revealed as [[captain-nemo|Captain Nemo]]'s submarine.

## Participants & Setting

- Undertaken aboard the frigate Abraham Lincoln under Commander Farragut.
- Draws [[Ned Land]] into the voyage.
- Ends at the [[Nautilus]], the true identity of the monster.
- Chronicled in [[Twenty Thousand Leagues Under the Seas]].
