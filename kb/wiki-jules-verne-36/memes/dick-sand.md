# Dick Sand

> **Note:** Inline PN citations for this page use a 4-character work code (`DSCF`); they render as plain text pending RFC-0003. The underlying PN data is valid.

## Overview

Dick Sand is the young hero of *[[dick-sand-a-captain-at-fifteen|Dick Sand, A Captain at Fifteen]]*. He sails aboard the *Pilgrim* as its sole novice among a crew of five sailors (DSCF-002-014), a foundling of fifteen born to an unknown father and mother (DSCF-002-015).

Originally from the State of New York (DSCF-002-016), he is a resolute youth who, even at fifteen, would carry to the end whatever his spirit had decided upon (DSCF-002-024).

## Role in the Story

Dick Sand's promise is recognized early: Captain Hull predicts that the young sailor will one day do honor to the American marine (DSCF-005-025), and entrusts him with duties at the helm during the voyage (DSCF-007-072).

When Captain Hull and the whale-boat crew are lost in a disastrous hunt, disaster leaves no true seaman aboard the *Pilgrim* but Dick Sand — only a beginner, a young man of fifteen (DSCF-009-010).

He stands motionless at the rail, watching the place where Captain Hull was swallowed up (DSCF-009-012), then takes up the burden of command, going into the captain's cabin for the chart that marks the ship's position (DSCF-009-052). From that moment the survival of the passengers rests on the shoulders of the boy-captain.

## Character & Traits

Verne sums up Dick Sand in a single Latin word — *audens*, "daring" (DSCF-002-023). Nimble, well built, and self-possessed beyond his years, he combines a sailor's practical skill with a moral seriousness that older men trust: Captain Hull stakes the boy's whole future on the sea when he foretells his honor to the American marine (DSCF-005-025).

## Relationships

- Captain Hull — master of the *Pilgrim*, who mentors Dick Sand and whose death thrusts him into command.
- Mrs. Weldon — the passenger whose safety becomes Dick Sand's chief responsibility after the catastrophe.
- Sails aboard the *Pilgrim*, the ship he is forced to captain at fifteen.
