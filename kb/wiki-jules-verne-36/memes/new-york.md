## Overview

New York is the American Atlantic port linked to Europe by transatlantic steamer, the eastern terminus of the railway from San Francisco. (AWED-024-010) Five main lines connect Omaha with New York, making it the continental gateway. (AWED-026-002)

## In the Narrative

The doctor's search for the lost "Cynthia" turned on New York, whence the vessel had sailed on the 3rd of November for Hamburg. (WC-008-003) It was in New York that he must first make his investigations. (WC-008-004) In September the three friends and Erik embarked at Christiana for New York to pursue the inquiry. (WC-008-021)

## Geography & Features

Broadway is to New York what Regent Street is to London and the Boulevard des Italiens to Paris, lined with splendid stores. (AWED-025-006) The port's shipping was underwritten in the city; the "Cynthia" was insured with the General Steam Navigation Company of New York for eight hundred thousand dollars. (WC-006-043)

## Connections

Fogg reckoned to reach [[San Francisco]] and then New York by the 11th of December, whence a steamer would carry him to [[London]].
