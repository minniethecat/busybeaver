## Overview

Semipolatinsk is one of the Siberian governments named when the Tartar insurrection severs communications across the province, listed alongside Yeniseisk, Omsk, and Tobolsk. (MS-002-021)

It lies on the Irtish, along the line of Cossack military stations that guarded the southern frontier of the empire.

## In the Narrative

When the rising begins, the line of Cossack posts ranged in echelon from Omsk to Semipolatinsk is broken in several places. (MS-003-061) Feofar-Khan invades the government of Semipolatinsk, and the small Cossack force there is obliged to retire before him. (MS-003-065)

The Tartars, with Feofar-Khan at their head, pass Semipolatinsk and descend the Irtish. (MS-009-030) Within days the invaders have overrun the whole province, descending the river by forced marches. (MS-012-075)

## Geography & Features

The town stands on the Irtish in southern Siberia, a government of the Russian steppe frontier. (MS-002-021)

Its Cossack posts formed one end of the military echelon stretching back to Omsk, a chain meant to hold the line against incursions from the south. (MS-003-061)
