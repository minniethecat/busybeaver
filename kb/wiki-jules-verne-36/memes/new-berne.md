## Overview

New-Berne is a port town of North Carolina standing on the left bank of the winding Neuse, near the broad waters of [[Pamlico Sound]]. It was off New-Berne that the schooner Ebba lay anchored. (FF-001-005) It was the first time the Ebba had dropped anchor in the port of New-Berne, brought there, it seemed, by a mere whim of her owner to the mouth of the Neuse. (FF-002-009)

## In the Narrative

As twilight came on, the outline of New-Berne on the left bank of the sinuous Neuse grew more and more indistinct until it vanished into the deepening shades of night. (FF-003-036) No breath of air stirred, and in half an hour the tide would set in again, rising strongly and rapidly for several miles above New-Berne. (FF-003-052)

When the two captives were carried off from [[Healthful House]] near New-Berne, the news reached the town and its authorities were at first puzzled to know whether the missing inventor and his keeper had fled or been abducted. (FF-004-023) They ordered the commander of the Falcon to intercept the schooner and search her from stem to stern. (FF-004-045)

## Geography & Features

The town watched its river closely, and from the extremity of the New-Berne quay the crew of a vessel might be seen holystoning the deck and clearing the halyards. (FF-004-002) Twenty miles below the town the estuary curves abruptly toward the north-west, gradually widening until it empties itself into [[Pamlico Sound]]. (FF-004-028)

## Connections

New-Berne was the nearest seat of authority to [[Healthful House]], and its officers governed the search along the coast. The presence of the Ebba in proximity to the asylum could not fail to excite suspicion and might draw a visit from the New-Berne authorities. (FF-003-103)

It was from this town in [[North Carolina]] that word went out that Thomas Roch and his keeper, kept at Pavilion No. 17 of Healthful House, had been kidnapped and carried off by sea. (FF-013-022)
