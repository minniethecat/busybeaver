# Richard Shandon

## Overview

He is the first mate of the brig Forward, announced in the newspaper notice that "the brig Forward, Captain K. Z----, Richard Shandon mate, will start from New Prince's Docks for an unknown destination" (ACH-001-002).

He is a seasoned mariner, for "Richard Shandon was a good sailor; he had been commander of whalers in the Arctic seas for many years, and had a wide reputation for skill" (ACH-003-002).

## Role in the Story

His part begins with a mysterious summons, "the letter received by Richard Shandon, eight months before" (ACH-002-002), on whose terms "Shandon set about recruiting his crew upon the conditions of family and health exacted by the captain" (ACH-003-006).

So indispensable is he that "if it hadn't been for that, Richard Shandon wouldn't have found a soul to go with him" (ACH-001-018), and until the hidden master appears "the captain is Richard Shandon till another comes" (ACH-003-021).

He takes the doctor aboard on the captain's written order that Clawbonny "may introduce himself to the commander, Richard Shandon, who has received orders concerning him" (ACH-003-042).

Yet the true captain's tokens undo his hopes, for their arrival "vexed Shandon, and took away all chance of the chief command" (ACH-004-004), leaving him "from the top of the poop... looking at the crowd with uneasy eyes, trying to read the secret of his destiny" (ACH-004-030).

## Character & Traits

He is in the prime of his vigour, "a bachelor of forty, robust, energetic, and brave, three sailor-like qualities, giving their possessor confidence, vigour, and sang-froid" (ACH-003-005).

He nurses a private ambition, for "Richard Shandon, in his secret heart, hoped that the command would remain with him" (ACH-003-022).

He can be gainsaid by wiser heads, as when the doctor cries "you are mistaken, Shandon... the captain will allow me to tell you" (ACH-013-021).

## Relationships

- [[Captain Hatteras]] — the concealed true captain whose emerging tokens strip Shandon of the chief command he coveted (ACH-004-004).

- [[Dr Clawbonny]] — the doctor he receives aboard on the captain's order, and who openly corrects him (ACH-013-021).

- Johnson — the trusted old sailor he engages as boatswain and to whom he refers the crew's prying questions (ACH-003-006).

## Appearances

- [[The Adventures of Captain Hatteras]] — the voyage of the Forward, which he fits out and provisionally commands for the unknown captain (ACH-003-021).
