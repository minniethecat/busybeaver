# The Abduction by the Albatross

## Overview

The Abduction by the Albatross is the seizure of the two leaders of the [[Weldon Institute]] by [[Robur]] and his men, the act that carries them aloft aboard his flying machine.

The trap is laid in a lonely spot outside the city. Beyond the trees was a very large clearing--an oval field, a complete amphitheater (RC-005-024).

The ground had been prepared for an ambush. Had the two colleagues not been so deep in their dispute and used their eyes as they were accustomed to, they would have found the clearing was not in its usual state (RC-005-025).

## What Happens

A signal opens the assault. A flash of electric light shot across the clearing (RC-005-029), and Robur's men close in from the shadows.

The seizure is swift and total. In a second they were rendered speechless by a gag, blind by a bandage, thrown down, pinioned and carried bodily off across the clearing (RC-005-031).

It is over almost before it begins. Between the moment they were seized a few steps out into the clearing and the moment they were thrown into confinement only two minutes elapsed (RC-006-030).

The captives wake bound and helpless. A bandage over the eyes, a gag in the mouth, a cord round the wrists, a cord round the ankles, Uncle Prudent, Phil Evans, and Frycollin were anything but pleased with their position (RC-006-002).

## Significance

Only once free of their bonds do they grasp their plight. One of them slipped off the bandage which bound his eyes, took the gag out of his mouth, and cut the cords round his ankles with his knife (RC-006-006).

They quickly name their captor and vow revenge, resolving that now is the time to get away, and to return later to settle with this Robur (RC-006-034). The abduction thus binds the Weldon Institute's balloonists to the aeronef of the very man whose flying machine they had scorned.

## Participants & Setting

- The kidnapping of Uncle Prudent and Phil Evans of the [[Weldon Institute]], together with the valet Frycollin, from a clearing near Philadelphia.
- Carried out by [[Robur]] and his crew as the opening stroke of their aerial voyage.
- Chronicled in *[[robur-the-conqueror|Robur the Conqueror]]*.
