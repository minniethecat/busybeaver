# The Missionary's Rescue

## Overview

The Missionary's Rescue is the great humanitarian episode of *[[five-weeks-in-a-balloon|Five Weeks in a Balloon]]*, in which Dr. Ferguson's balloon [[victoria-balloon|Victoria]] plucks a captive priest from a hostile tribe over [[africa|Africa]].

Drifting through the night above a village, the travellers grasp the horror below: some unfortunate Frenchman has fallen into the hands of these savages (FWB-021-060), and they resolve to attempt a rescue at daylight.

The sight beneath them is grim. A hundred feet below the balloon stood a large post, or stake, and at its foot lay a human being--a young man of thirty years or more, with long black hair, half naked, wasted and wan, bleeding, covered with wounds (FWB-022-004).

## What Happens

At dawn Ferguson eases the Victoria down. An almost imperceptible breath of air impelled the balloon directly over the prisoner, at the same time that it gently lowered with the contraction of the gas (FWB-022-013).

The snatch is the work of an instant. At the instant when the car was close to the ground, the brawny Scot, laying aside his rifle, and seizing the priest around the waist, lifted him into the car, while, at the same moment, Joe tossed over the two hundred pounds of ballast (FWB-022-015).

Lightened all at once, the craft shoots skyward. The balloon, thus suddenly lightened, made a leap of three hundred feet into the air, amid the howlings of the tribe whose prisoner thus escaped them in a blaze of dazzling light (FWB-022-021).

## Significance

Aloft and safe, the rescued man learns who his deliverers are. We are trying to cross Africa in a balloon, and, on our way, we have had the good fortune to rescue you (FWB-022-045), Ferguson tells him.

Yet the rescue cannot undo the ordeal already suffered. The weakness of the young missionary became so extreme that they had to lay him again on the bed, where a prostration, lasting for several hours, held him like a dead man under the eye of Dr. Ferguson (FWB-022-054).

For Ferguson the deed needs no defence: it was in our power to save that unfortunate missionary from a horrible death (FWB-024-032).

## Participants & Setting

- The aerial rescue of a young French missionary from a hostile tribe, high over [[africa|Africa]].
- Effected by Dr. Samuel Ferguson, the hunter Kennedy, and the servant Joe aboard the balloon [[victoria-balloon|Victoria]].
- Chronicled in *[[five-weeks-in-a-balloon|Five Weeks in a Balloon]]*.
