# Chatham Islands

## Overview

The Chatham Islands are a remote South Pacific group east of New Zealand where Robur's airship *Albatross* comes to rest in *Robur the Conqueror*. Reading their position off the map, the captives find "this point on the map answered to the position of the Chatham Islands, and particularly of Pitt Island, one of the group." (RC-019-013)

## In the Narrative

The islands are chosen as a landing place for repairs: "Hence Robur's resolve to anchor on the Chatham Islands." (RC-019-024) Their isolation makes them a prison as much as a haven — the *Albatross* rises again before dawn, so that "she would be out of sight of the Chatham Islands before sunrise." (RC-020-008)

## Geography & Features

Seldom touched by shipping, the group offers little hope of rescue: "The Chatham Islands are not much visited by navigators, and all August passed without sign of a ship." (RC-021-036) Deliverance comes only by chance: "At last a ship came to water at the Chatham Islands." (RC-021-037)
