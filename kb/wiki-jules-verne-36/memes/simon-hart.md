# Simon Hart

## Overview

Behind the warder's uniform stands a spy: "the alleged Gaydon was a French engineer named Simon Hart, who for several years past had been connected with a manufactory of chemical products in New Jersey" (FF-001-041).

Under that disguise "Simon Hart, alias Gaydon, had been an attendant at Healthful House for fifteen months" (FF-001-042).

His purpose is patriotic — "this the mission to which he had wholly devoted himself in the interest of his native country" (FF-001-044).

## Role in the Story

The bulk of the tale is his own record, headed "(Notes by Simon Hart, the Engineer.)" (FF-005-002).

Seized along with the man he guards, he is carried off when "Thomas Roch and his keeper Gaydon, or rather Simon Hart, the French engineer who occupied Pavilion No. 17, at Healthful House... were kidnapped" (FF-013-022).

His one secret advantage he guards jealously: "he must at any rate never know that I am aware of the position of Back Cup Island" (FF-011-010).

At the rescue his identity is at last confirmed, for aboard the Tonnant "it was learned from the note-book that he was Simon Hart" (FF-018-026).

## Character & Traits

His look betrays his intelligence — "a large head, high, wide forehead, strongly-cut features, iron-gray hair and moustache, eyes generally haggard, but which became piercing and imperious when illuminated by his dominant idea" (FF-001-045).

He keeps his cover flawlessly: "None suspected that I was Simon Hart, the engineer, nor could they have suspected my nationality" (FF-005-006).

Even among the pirates he trusts his anonymity — "No one could have had any idea that I was Simon Hart, the engineer" (FF-006-027).

## Relationships

- [[Ker Karraje]] — the pirate chief who unmasks him, declaring that "Warder Gaydon is Engineer Simon Hart; and Ker Karraje will never restore to liberty Engineer Simon Hart, who knows his secrets" (FF-010-093).
- [[Engineer Serko]] — the accomplice who penetrated his disguise, whom he presses to learn "how you came to find out that Gaydon, the warder, was Simon Hart, the engineer" (FF-012-024).
- [[Thomas Roch]] — the captive inventor he is charged to watch, and whose fatal secret he strives to keep from the pirates.

## Appearances

- [[Facing the Flag]] — as narrator and hidden engineer, chronicling from within Back Cup the schemes of Ker Karraje and the tragedy of Thomas Roch.
