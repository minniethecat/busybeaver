# Pencroft

## Overview

Pencroft is a sailor and one of the five castaways who found a colony on Lincoln Island in *[[The Mysterious Island]]*. A man of about thirty-five or forty, strongly built and sunburnt with a pair of bright sparkling eyes, he is an American from the North who has sailed all the ocean over and gone through every possible adventure (MI-002-021).

Bold and dashing, ready to dare anything and astonished at nothing, he becomes the practical mainstay of the settlement — the honest sailor whose good humor sustains his companions through long years of exile (MI-009-001) (MI-019-016).

## Role in the Story

Having sailed over every sea — a carpenter in the Brooklyn dockyards, an assistant tailor aboard state vessels, and a gardener during his holidays — Pencroft is, like all seamen, fit for anything and knows how to do everything (MI-013-008). On Lincoln Island this versatility makes him indispensable.

When the colonists resolve to reach neighboring land, the engineer decrees that instead of a house they will build a boat, with Master Pencroft put in command (MI-014-059). True to his promise, Pencroft frames a light craft in five days (MI-024-001).

Later, radiant with joy, he sees the fully rigged vessel floated on the rising tide amid the cheers of the colonists (MI-034-077). At his insistence the boat is christened the *Bonadventure*, and Captain Pencroft takes her to sea.

## Character & Traits

Pencroft works with confidence and even gaiety, always with some joke to crack — by turns carpenter, rope-maker, and mason — communicating his good humor to the whole little world of the colony (MI-019-016).

Having once served aboard a whaling-ship, he can methodically direct even the disagreeable three-day business of cutting up a captured whale, from which the settlers do not flinch (MI-032-052). Practical skill and cheerful nerve define him throughout the ordeal.

## Relationships

- Herbert Brown — the orphaned boy of fifteen whom Pencroft loves as if he had been his own child (MI-002-021), and with whom he lodges before the escape (MI-002-039).
- Cyrus Harding — the engineer and leader of the colony, who lays a hand on the sailor's shoulder and calls Herbert the lad chance has made their child (MI-033-021).
- Gideon Spilett and Neb — fellow castaways and founding colonists of Lincoln Island.

## Appearances

- [[The Mysterious Island]] — as one of the five founding colonists of Lincoln Island and builder of the *Bonadventure*.
