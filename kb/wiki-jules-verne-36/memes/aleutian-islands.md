# Aleutian Islands

## Overview

The Aleutian Islands are the real chain forming the southern rim of Behring Sea, the last landfall the castaways of *The Fur Country* dare hope for. As Victoria Island drifts south, the group pins its survival on reaching "the Aleutian group."

Yet the distance is cruel: the islands lie "more than eight hundred miles away, and which they might never reach." (FC-042-020)

## In the Narrative

Lieutenant Hobson and his companions "often discussed these matters, and came to the conclusion that the island could certainly never reach the Aleutian group" without accident. (FC-042-027) Mac-Nab builds his raft the stronger, for "it would have a considerable distance to go to reach the Aleutian Islands, unless they were fortunate enough to meet with a whaler." (FC-043-004)

Hobson "pointed out the Aleutian Islands on the map to his comrades" (FC-043-018) as their goal, even as he fears "the current seemed likely to drag the island away from all land, even out of sight of the Aleutian Islands." (FC-043-022)

## Geography & Features

The chain closes the southern edge of Behring Sea, extending "as far as the hundred and seventieth meridian." (FC-043-022) When at its widest drift, Victoria Island floats "six hundred miles from the nearest of the Aleutian Islands." (FC-043-002)

The islands share the cold latitudes where blue foxes are found — "in Jan Mayen's Land, the Aleutian Isles, Kamtchatka, Russian America, and Iceland." (FC-015-025)
