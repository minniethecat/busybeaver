# Gideon Spilett

## Overview

Gideon Spilett is one of the five castaways who found the colony on Lincoln Island in *[[The Mysterious Island]]*, and its chronicler. He belongs to that race of indomitable English and American chroniclers, like Stanley, who stop at nothing to obtain exact information (MI-002-005).

For ten years he had been the reporter of the New York Herald, which he enriched by his letters and drawings, being as skilful with the pencil as with the pen (MI-002-008).

Thrown together with the engineer [[Cyrus Harding]] as a prisoner at Richmond, he joins the desperate balloon escape without a moment's hesitation, for he was not a man to draw back (MI-002-036).

## Role in the Story

Spilett is the colony's recorder and its coolest head. When the survivors name the features of their island, it is he who writes them down, so that the geographical nomenclature of the island might be definitely adopted (MI-011-062).

Even as a storm gathers, he stands motionless on the shore, his arms crossed, gazing over the sea (MI-007-001), and after Harding is cast up half-drowned it is Spilett who rises, at last, after a long and attentive examination of his friend (MI-008-007).

His journalist's instinct never leaves him: watching the seabirds pass, he wonders whether it was to one of them that he had confided his last letter addressed to the New York Herald (MI-035-031).

## Character & Traits

Spilett is a man of many competences. A good marksman, he is armed with one of the two rifles that carry nearly a mile (MI-045-004).

He is also the colony's physician: he had not passed through the many incidents of his checkered life without acquiring some slight knowledge of medicine (MI-049-014), and when [[Herbert]] is gravely wounded, he and Cyrus Harding act as the best surgeon would have done (MI-049-048).

Tireless and vigilant, he follows the dog Top into the woods, encouraging him, his gun ready to fire while sheltering behind the trees (MI-051-033).

## Relationships

- [[Cyrus Harding]] — the engineer and colony leader, Spilett's fellow prisoner at Richmond and closest companion (MI-002-036).
- [[Herbert]] — the young naturalist whose grave wounds Spilett tends as physician (MI-049-014).
- [[Pencroft]], [[Neb]], and Cyrus Harding — fellow founding colonists of Lincoln Island.

## Appearances

- [[The Mysterious Island]] — as war correspondent, chronicler, marksman, and physician among the five founding colonists of Lincoln Island.
