# Ayrton

## Overview

Ayrton is a repentant castaway and former traitor who becomes the sixth colonist of [[lincoln-island|Lincoln Island]] in *[[the-mysterious-island|The Mysterious Island]]*. Discovered alone and half-savage on Tabor Island, he is identified as a Scotchman who had been one of Captain Grant's crew (MI-039-050).

His story reaches back to an earlier crime, for it is revealed that Ayrton had once been a traitor (MI-039-054).

## Role in the Story

In *[[in-search-of-the-castaways|In Search of the Castaways]]*, Ayrton first appears as Tom Ayrton, engaged as quartermaster on board the three-masted *Britannia* (SC-033-052). Confronted by Lord Glenarvan, he confirms his identity — "Yes, it was I" — claiming to be a Scotchman like his questioner (SC-033-004), and gives an account of the *Britannia's* voyage across the Pacific (SC-033-041).

In truth he was the convict Ben Joyce, who guided a band of outlaws against the expedition (MI-039-057).

As punishment, Glenarvan marooned him on Tabor Island, promising to return and take him off only when he considered that his crimes were expiated (MI-041-016).

Years later the colonists of [[lincoln-island|Lincoln Island]] find him there, sunk into savagery, and restore him to reason and honesty. He recognizes the yacht *Duncan*, aboard which he had once sailed (MI-043-018), when it finally arrives to rescue the castaways.

## Character & Traits

Ayrton's arc is one of guilt and expiation. Introduced flatly as a traitor (MI-039-054), he is the same man known among criminals as Ben Joyce (MI-039-057). His long, solitary penance on Tabor Island — Glenarvan's condition that his crimes be expiated before rescue (MI-041-016) — transforms him from villain into the loyal, self-effacing companion of the Lincoln Island colonists.

## Relationships

- [[cyrus-harding|Cyrus Harding]] — leader of the [[lincoln-island|Lincoln Island]] colonists, who restore Ayrton to reason and welcome him as one of their own.
- Lord Glenarvan — commander of the *Duncan*, who marooned Ayrton and set the terms of his redemption.
- Captain Grant — of the *Britannia*, whose crew Ayrton claimed to belong to.
