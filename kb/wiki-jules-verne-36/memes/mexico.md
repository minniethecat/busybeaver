## Overview

Mexico is the republic beyond the southern frontier of the United States, the neighbouring country the Gun Club must reach for the twenty-eighth parallel — a legitimate *casus belli*, as one member demands. (FEM-011-010) The projected line plunges into the Gulf of Mexico, skirts Texas, and continues its direction over Mexico, crossing the Sonora and Old California. (FEM-011-017)

## In the Narrative

Even this distant land takes part in the Columbiad's great subscription: Mexico sent the widow's mite, 86 piastres, for empires in course of formation are always in embarrassed circumstances. (FEM-012-025)

## Geography & Features

The republic lends its name to a gulf that recurs across the voyages. The airship *Terror* seeks, to the south, the waters of the Gulf of Mexico (MW-017-043), and the steamer *Ottawa* is met in the Gulf of Mexico, headed for the port of New Orleans. (MW-018-004)

It is there the great current is born: attaining a white heat in the Gulf of Mexico, the Gulf Stream heads north up the American coast. (TTLU-043-008) Its warmth barely differs from blood temperature as it leaves the Gulf of Mexico (TTLU-043-012), and the sea beyond is plowed by steamers between the Gulf of Mexico and New York or Boston. (TTLU-043-017)
