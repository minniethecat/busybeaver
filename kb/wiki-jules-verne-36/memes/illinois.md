## Overview

Illinois is a Midwestern state of the United States. In the Vernean corpus it is chiefly the retreat of Dirk Peters, the half-breed survivor of *An Antarctic Mystery*, who settles at Vandalia, and the state to which Captain Len Guy travels in search of him.

It figures too in the Mormon chapter of *Around the World in Eighty Days*, in the war record of Cyrus Smith, and beneath the *Albatross* of *Robur the Conqueror*.

## In the Narrative

In *An Antarctic Mystery*, Dirk Peters is said to have lived "at Illinois," (AM-004-046) and it is there that Captain Len Guy has gone to seek out the sailing-master. (AM-005-026) His home is fixed precisely: "At Vandalia, State of Illinois." (AM-017-062)

In *Around the World in Eighty Days*, Passepartout learns the Mormon history of the state — how Joseph Smith "reappeared in Illinois, and in 1839 founded a community at Nauvoo, on the Mississippi," (AWED-027-013) before the sect were "driven from Illinois in 1845" to colonise the western deserts. (AWED-026-003)

In *The Mysterious Island*, the engineer Cyrus Smith is recalled as "having begun as a volunteer at Illinois, under Ulysses Grant." (MI-002-004)

In *Robur the Conqueror*, "the State of Illinois was left by its northern frontier in less than two hours and a half" as the *Albatross* crossed the Mississippi. (RC-009-014)

## Geography & Features

Verne places Illinois on the Mississippi, at the threshold of the American West: it is by the great river, at Nauvoo, that the Mormons raise their community before their westward exodus. (AWED-027-013) The *Albatross* leaves the state by its northern frontier, crossing the Father of Waters that bounds it. (RC-009-014)

Within the state lies the town of Vandalia, remote enough to shelter Dirk Peters through his obscure later years. (AM-017-062)

## Connections

Illinois is bound above all to *An Antarctic Mystery*, for it is at Vandalia, in Illinois, that Dirk Peters withdraws and Captain Len Guy comes to seek him. (AM-005-026) It joins *Around the World in Eighty Days* through the Mormons, who founded Nauvoo in Illinois before being driven out. (AWED-026-003)

And it links to *The Mysterious Island* through Cyrus Smith, who began his soldier's service as a volunteer at Illinois. (MI-002-004)
