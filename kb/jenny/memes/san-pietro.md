San Pietro appears as a setting in Sigrid Undset's novel *[[jenny-winge|Jenny]]* (1911). The novel's expatriate artist community inhabits these spaces, and Undset's detailed descriptions of place ground the narrative in early 20th-century [[rome|Rome]] and Norway.
