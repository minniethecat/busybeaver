Marriage is a significant thematic element in Sigrid Undset's novel *[[jenny-winge|Jenny]]* (1911).

## Manifestation in the Novel

The theme of Marriage runs through the narrative, appearing in key scenes across the novel's three parts. Undset's treatment reflects her early modernist sensibility and her deep engagement with the social questions of her era.
