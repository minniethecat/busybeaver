[034-001]![The Standard Ebooks logo.](images/logo.png)

[034-002]*[[jenny-winge|Jenny]]*

[034-003]was published in 1911 by

[034-004]**Sigrid Undset**.

[034-005]It was translated from Norwegian in 1921 by

[034-006]**W. Emmé**.

[034-007]This ebook was produced for

[034-008][Standard Ebooks](<https://standardebooks.org/>)

[034-009]by

[034-010]**Liam Delaney** ,

[034-011]and is based on a transcription produced in 2019 by

[034-012]**Tim Lindell** and [Distributed Proofreaders](<https://www.pgdp.net/>)

[034-013]for

[034-014][Project Gutenberg](<https://www.gutenberg.org/ebooks/60741>)

[034-015]and on digital scans from the

[034-016][Internet Archive](<https://archive.org/details/jenny01unds>).

[034-017]The cover page is adapted from

[034-018]*Rest* ,

[034-019]a painting completed in 1905 by

[034-020][Vilhelm Hammershøi](<https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Vilhelm_Hammersh%C3%B8i>).

[034-021]The cover and title pages feature the

[034-022]**League Spartan** and **Sorts Mill Goudy**

[034-023]typefaces created in 2014 and 2009 by

[034-024][The League of Moveable Type](<https://www.theleagueofmoveabletype.com/>).

[034-025]This edition was released on

[034-026]**June 30, 2026, 2:48 a.m.**

[034-027]and is based on

[034-028]**revision ae4706f**.

[034-029]The first edition of this ebook was released on

[034-030]March 24, 2026, 6:28 p.m.
