# Characters

This page lists all characters that appear in *Wuthering Heights* (1847) by Emily Brontë.
The roster spans two generations across the Earnshaw, Linton, and Heathcliff families,
from central figures like Heathcliff and Catherine to minor servants and villagers.

## Notes

- Character entries are drawn from the full text and supplemented by scholarly consensus on the novel's dramatis personae.
