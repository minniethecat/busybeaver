# Death Register

Death permeates *Wuthering Heights* (1847) from its opening pages — Lockwood encounters
Catherine's ghost at the window — to its closing scene of three graves on the moor.
Characters die young, in childbirth, from illness, or by self-imposed starvation.
This register indexes every wiki page tagged `death`, spanning character deaths,
fatal events, and thematic explorations of mortality.

## Notes

The death tag is applied broadly — to character pages whose subject dies in the
narrative, to event pages centered on a death, and to concept pages exploring
death as a theme. The register therefore provides a multi-faceted view of mortality
in Brontë's novel rather than a simple obituary list.
