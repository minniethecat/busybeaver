# Efraín's Mother

Efraín's mother is the novel's principal maternal figure, presiding over the domestic sphere of the hacienda with quiet warmth. Where Efraín's father represents authority, decision, and the world beyond the home, his mother represents comfort, continuity, and the emotional bonds that hold the family together. Her arms around Efraín at his departures and her tears at his returns are among the novel's most consistent emotional markers (001-003) (022-008).
