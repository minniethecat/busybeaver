(060-003) was published in 1867 by

(060-004) [Jorge Isaacs](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Jorge_Isaacs).

(060-005) It was translated from Spanish in 1890 by

(060-006) [Rollo Ogden](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Rollo_Ogden).

(060-007) This ebook was produced for

(060-008) [Standard Ebooks](https://standardebooks.org/)

(060-009) by

(060-010) [Alex Cabal](https://alexcabal.com/),

(060-011) and is based on a digital scans from the

(060-012) [Internet Archive](https://archive.org/details/unset0000unse_v7o4).

(060-013) The cover page is adapted from

(060-014) *Two Houses in Barranquilla, Colombia*,

(060-015) a painting completed in 1853 by

(060-016) [Frederic Edwin Church](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Frederic_Edwin_Church).

(060-017) The cover and title pages feature the

(060-018) **League Spartan** and **Sorts Mill Goudy**

(060-019) typefaces created in 2014 and 2009 by

(060-020) [The League of Moveable Type](https://www.theleagueofmoveabletype.com/).
