# Juan Ángel

Juan Ángel is a young boy on Efraín's family hacienda — the son of [[Feliciana|Feliciana]], a slave woman who serves the household. His innocence, devotion, and attachment to both Efraín and María make him a memorable minor character. His presence in the novel humanizes the hacienda's enslaved population, giving individuality and pathos to what might otherwise remain an undifferentiated backdrop. His mother Feliciana's death is one of the novel's most emotionally charged moments, with the entire hacienda gathering for prayers (038-006) (020-001).
