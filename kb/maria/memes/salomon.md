# Salomón

Salomón is María's father, a Jewish merchant from Jamaica who was a close friend of Efraín's father. His death before the novel's action begins is the originating event of the plot: it is why María comes to live with Efraín's family, setting the stage for the love story that follows. Though absent, Salomón's presence is felt throughout the novel — in María's orphanhood, in the dowry papers that represent his posthumous care, and in the Jamaican connections that tie the novel to a wider world (007-002) (007-007).
