# Society

## Definition

**Society** (*Gesellschaft*) is a significant thematic dimension in
Goethe's *Elective Affinities*. Through the four central characters and their
estate at Wahlverwandtschaften, the novel explores how society operates as
both backdrop and active force in human affairs. (001-013), (004-006), (004-013), (004-015)

## In the Novel

The theme of society manifests throughout the thirty-six chapters, from
the estate improvements at the novel's opening to the final resolution.
[[Chapter-4]]'s chemical discussion provides an intellectual framework,
while later events test the characters' relationship to society in
concrete situations.
