# The Pension

## Overview

**The Pension** (*Pensionat*) is the boarding school where Ottilie received
her education before coming to the estate. It represents the institutional
world that shaped her character and abilities.

## In the Novel

The Pension is referenced primarily in connection with Ottilie's background.
Her education there — emphasizing order, diligence, and self-effacement —
shapes her conduct at the estate and explains her quiet competence.

## Significance

As an organization, the Pension represents the structured, rule-bound world
that contrasts with the emotional fluidity of life at the estate. Ottilie's
movement from the Pension to the estate mirrors her transition from a
regulated childhood to the unpredictable realm of adult affinities.
