# Death

## Definition

**Death** (*Tod*) is a significant thematic dimension in
Goethe's *Elective Affinities*. Through the four central characters and their
estate at Wahlverwandtschaften, the novel explores how death operates as
both backdrop and active force in human affairs. (002-001), (010-018), (018-033), (019-011)

## In the Novel

The theme of death manifests throughout the thirty-six chapters, from
the estate improvements at the novel's opening to the final resolution.
[[Chapter-4]]'s chemical discussion provides an intellectual framework,
while later events test the characters' relationship to death in
concrete situations.
