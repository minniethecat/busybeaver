# Freedom

## Definition

**Freedom** (*Freiheit*) is a significant thematic dimension in
Goethe's *Elective Affinities*. Through the four central characters and their
estate at Wahlverwandtschaften, the novel explores how freedom operates as
both backdrop and active force in human affairs. (002-010), (003-022), (005-001), (008-012)

## In the Novel

The theme of freedom manifests throughout the thirty-six chapters, from
the estate improvements at the novel's opening to the final resolution.
[[Chapter-4]]'s chemical discussion provides an intellectual framework,
while later events test the characters' relationship to freedom in
concrete situations.
