# Art

## Definition

**Art** (*Kunst*) is a significant thematic dimension in
Goethe's *Elective Affinities*. Through the four central characters and their
estate at Wahlverwandtschaften, the novel explores how art operates as
both backdrop and active force in human affairs. (003-002), (003-028), (004-022), (004-037)

## In the Novel

The theme of art manifests throughout the thirty-six chapters, from
the estate improvements at the novel's opening to the final resolution.
[[Chapter-4]]'s chemical discussion provides an intellectual framework,
while later events test the characters' relationship to art in
concrete situations.
