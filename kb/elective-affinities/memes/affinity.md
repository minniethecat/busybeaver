# Affinity

## Definition

**Affinity** (*Verwandtschaft*) is explored in Goethe's *Elective Affinities*
through the interactions of the four central characters at the estate. The novel
examines affinity as both an abstract principle and a lived reality. (004-016), (004-019), (004-036), (004-037)

## In the Novel

The treatment of affinity in the novel reflects the Enlightenment-era tension
between rational principle and emotional impulse. [[Chapter-4]] provides
the theoretical framework, while subsequent chapters test these ideas against
the complexities of human relationships.
