# Nature

## Definition

**Nature** (*Natur*) is a significant thematic dimension in
Goethe's *Elective Affinities*. Through the four central characters and their
estate at Wahlverwandtschaften, the novel explores how nature operates as
both backdrop and active force in human affairs. (002-010), (002-013), (003-018), (003-020)

## In the Novel

The theme of nature manifests throughout the thirty-six chapters, from
the estate improvements at the novel's opening to the final resolution.
[[Chapter-4]]'s chemical discussion provides an intellectual framework,
while later events test the characters' relationship to nature in
concrete situations.
