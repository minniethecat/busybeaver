# Education

## Definition

**Education** (*Erziehung*) is explored in Goethe's *Elective Affinities*
through the interactions of the four central characters at the estate. The novel
examines education as both an abstract principle and a lived reality. (005-005), (010-044), (016-003), (023-052)

## In the Novel

The treatment of education in the novel reflects the Enlightenment-era tension
between rational principle and emotional impulse. [[Chapter-4]] provides
the theoretical framework, while subsequent chapters test these ideas against
the complexities of human relationships.
