The **Red Stockings** were the [[national-game|baseball]] club of [[galena|Galena]], Illinois, and the [[catalpa-nine|Catalpa Nine]]'s opponent in the [[championship-baseball|championship series]] for Northern Illinois. They represented the third and culminating stop on the [[catalpa-nine|Catalpa club]]'s three-city [[catalpa-first-road-trip|road trip]], following the [[baseball-training|practice]] game against the [[black-hawks|Black Hawks]] of [[sandy-key|Sandy Key]] and the exhibition [[baseball-match|match]] against the [[zoo-zoo-nine|Zoo-Zoo Nine of Bluford]].

## The Road Trip Context

The [[catalpa-nine|Catalpa Nine]]'s first pilgrimage was a carefully planned three-city tour:

> From Sandy Key the Nine were to go to Bluford to play the famous "Zoo-zoo Nine," as they called themselves, of that city, and then they were to begin a struggle for the championship of Northern Illinois with the Red Stockings of Galena. (007-001)

The Red Stockings [[baseball-match|match]] was the most consequential of the tour — a [[championship-baseball|championship]] [[baseball-match|contest]] that would determine the supremacy of Northern Illinois [[national-game|baseball]].

## Championship Opposition

The Red Stockings represented the highest level of competition the [[catalpa-nine|Catalpas]] had faced. As the [[championship-baseball|championship series]] opponent, they were the benchmark against which the [[catalpa-nine|Catalpa club]]'s season would ultimately be measured. How much depended on the result — as the narrative observes — "you who have followed the career of a [[baseball-positions|baseball nine]] can best reckon" (007-001).

## Galena Connection

The Red Stockings were based in [[galena|Galena]], where the [[catalpa-nine|Catalpa]] players lodged at the [[quapaw-house|Quapaw House]] while awaiting the [[state-championship-catalpa-calumets|championship game]]s. The connection between club and town was central to the identity of the Red Stockings, much as the [[catalpa-nine|Catalpa Nine]] represented their own community.
