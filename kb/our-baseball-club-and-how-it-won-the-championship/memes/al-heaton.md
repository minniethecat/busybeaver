# Al Heaton

> "Chiefly on Al Heaton, the stalwart catcher, did the eyes of the multitude rest with favor." (002-008)

## Role in the Club

Al Heaton was [[charlie-webb|the catcher]] and premier player of the [[catalpa-nine|Catalpa Nine]]. His physical presence — "a tall, shapely young fellow, with a ruddy and oval face, bright brown eyes, a keen glance, and a sinewy length of limb" (002-008) — marked him as a natural athlete. As [[catcher-baseball|catcher]], he was the team's leader on the field, directing the [[fielding-baseball|defense]] and managing the [[pitcher-baseball|pitcher]]s.

Heaton [[baseball-equipment|bat]]ted at the top of the [[catalpa-nine|Catalpa]] order, leading off innings (002-049), which reflected his status as the team's best all-around player. A supporter declared him "a first-rate [[catcher-baseball|catcher]]" with "nobody like him" in the region (004-007).

## Key Appearances

In the pivotal Chapter 2 [[baseball-match|match]] against [[jonesville-nine|Jonesville]], Heaton's performance was central. He opened the [[catalpa-nine|Catalpas]]' innings and reached base, contributing to their scoring. His catching skill was tested by the [[jonesville-nine|Jonesville]] [[baseball-equipment|bat]]ters, and a "[[foul-ball|foul tip]] to Al Heaton, [[catcher-baseball|catcher]]" (002-066) demonstrated his alertness behind the plate.

[[dr-selby|Dr. Selby]] praised Heaton's ability, arguing that the [[dean-county-nine|Dean County Nine]]'s [[catcher-baseball|catcher]] [[bill-van-orman|Bill Van Orman]] was the only comparable talent: "Pity 'tis, too, for Al is a first-rate [[catcher-baseball|catcher]]. Nobody like him, unless it is [[bill-van-orman|Bill Van Orman]]" (004-007).

## Reputation

Heaton's reputation extended beyond [[catalpa-nine|Catalpa]]. Even in the downriver clubs, he was recognized as a premier [[catcher-baseball|catcher]]. His status was not merely athletic — as a member of the north-side [[catalpa-nine|Catalpa Nine]] (the "Aristocrats"), his social position reinforced his athletic prominence.
