# Alexander

> "That's Sam Alexander at the bat now" (002-046)

## Role in the Club

Sam Alexander played shortstop for the [[jonesville-nine|Jonesville Nine]] and [[baseball-equipment|bat]]ted at the top of the order. He was the first player announced in the [[jonesville-nine|Jonesville]] lineup and was an active presence both at the plate and in the field during the opening [[baseball-match|match]] against the [[catalpa-nine|Catalpas]].

## Key Appearances

Alexander's entire known performance comes from Chapter 2. He came to [[baseball-equipment|bat]] with a runner on, and the narrative announced, "That's Sam Alexander at the [[baseball-equipment|bat]] now" (002-046). He reached base on a hit and later "came home" to [[scoring-baseball|score]] (002-047), one of the early [[scoring-baseball|runs]] that put [[jonesville-nine|Jonesville]] ahead.

In the third inning, the [[catalpa-nine|Catalpas]] "ended the third inning by sending Alexander to the [[baseball-equipment|bat]]. He was sent to first" (002-050), though his [[base-running|baserunning]] was cut short. Later, as the leadoff man in a subsequent inning, Alexander "was put out by sending the [[baseball-equipment|ball]]" to an infielder (002-055).

Defensively, Alexander had a mixed day at shortstop. He committed an error when a [[baseball-equipment|ball]] "to shortstop which Alexander overthrew to first base" (002-051). But he also recorded an assist, getting a runner at "first base by a [[baseball-equipment|ball]] sent by Alexander to [[sam-morrison|Morrison]]" (002-061). In the late innings, Alexander reached "first base and brought home Alexander and [[studley|Studley]], who were on the second and third bases" — actually here Alexander was the hitter driving in [[studley|Studley]] and another runner (002-062).
