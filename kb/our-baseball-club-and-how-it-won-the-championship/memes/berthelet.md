# Berthelet

> "Berthelet was caught out on a foul fly" (002-066)

## Role in the Club

Berthelet played right field for the [[jonesville-nine|Jonesville Nine]], the visiting club that faced the [[catalpa-nine|Catalpas]] in the novel's opening [[national-game|baseball]] [[baseball-match|match]]. He [[baseball-equipment|bat]]ted in the lower portion of the order and had a difficult day at the plate.

## Key Appearances

Berthelet's entire known performance comes from Chapter 2, and it was a challenging outing. His first at-[[baseball-equipment|bat]] resulted in a strikeout: "Berthelet went out on three [[strikeout-baseball|strikes]]" (002-043). He followed this with another unsuccessful plate appearance, going "out on a [[foul-ball|foul tip]]" (002-049).

His difficulties continued. In a later inning, Berthelet "was neatly caught out on a [[foul-ball|foul fly]]" by [[ben-burton|Ben Burton]], a play described as partially redeeming [[ben-burton|Burton]]'s own earlier errors (002-050). In his final at-[[baseball-equipment|bat]], "Berthelet was caught out on a [[foul-ball|foul fly]]" again (002-066), capping a forgettable offensive performance in which he failed to reach base in any of his appearances.

Despite his struggles at the plate, Berthelet remained in the game defensively in right field. The [[jonesville-nine|Jonesville Nine]]'s outfield alignment of [[martin|Martin]] (LF), [[wheeler|Wheeler]] (CF), and Berthelet (RF) covered the full field.
