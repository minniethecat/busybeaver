# Mike Costigan

> "Mike Costigan, the telegraph messenger, had a holiday." (017-027)

## A Pivotal Role

Mike Costigan was the telegraph messenger in [[catalpa-nine|Catalpa]] whose discovery gives Chapter 15 its title: "Mike Costigan's Discovery." His role in the narrative is that of an unexpected hero — a minor figure in the community whose actions have significant consequences for the [[national-game|baseball]] club's fortunes.

## The Discovery

Costigan's discovery (detailed in Chapter 15) concerns the conspiracy against the [[catalpa-nine|Catalpa club]]. As telegraph messenger, Costigan had access to communications passing through [[catalpa-nine|Catalpa]], and his alertness brought critical information to light. The discovery laid open the scheme that threatened the [[catalpa-nine|Catalpa Nine]]'s [[championship-baseball|championship]] campaign, earning Costigan a place in the community's gratitude.

## Afterward

By the novel's conclusion, Costigan appears as a "faithful satellite" (017-027) granted a holiday, suggesting his service to the club and community was recognized and rewarded. His trajectory from telegraph messenger to trusted ally illustrates how the [[national-game|baseball]] club's story drew in people from all walks of [[catalpa-nine|Catalpa]] life.
