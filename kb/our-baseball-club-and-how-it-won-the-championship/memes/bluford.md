The town of **Bluford** was the home of the "[[zoo-zoo-nine|Zoo-Zoo Nine]]," a formidable [[national-game|baseball]] club whose name descended from a company of Illinois Zouaves renowned in the Civil War. It was here that the [[catalpa-nine|Catalpa Nine]] suffered their most disheartening defeat of the early season.

## The Zoo-Zoo Nine

The [[zoo-zoo-nine|Zoo-Zoo Nine of Bluford]] were an elite club with a distinguished lineage:

> The "Zoo-Zoos" were picked players, the lineal descendants of a company of Illinois Zouaves renowned in the Civil War for their bravery, dash, and skill as skirmishers. The original founders of the club had long since disappeared from the field of action, but their successors bore up the banner of their illustrious namesakes with infinite credit. (008-001)

## The Defeat

The [[catalpa-nine|Catalpa Nine]] traveled from [[sandy-key|Sandy Key]] to Bluford for their second road game, but the result was a crushing disappointment:

> Defeat, utter and overwhelming, followed the Catalpas to Bluford, where they played the "Zoo-Zoo Nine" of that city. (008-001)

No [[catalpa-nine|Catalpa]] supporters witnessed the game in person — [[al-heaton|Al Heaton]] was sick at home and the other friends of the Nine were too busy with their farms and merchandise (008-001). The news arrived indirectly through Keokuk evening papers and a dispatch from [[charlie-king|Charlie King]]:

> Next day, when The Leaf came out, and fuller particulars of the game were made known in a despatch from Charlie King, there was nothing to mitigate the gloom of the friends of the Catalpas. (008-005)

The [[zoo-zoo-nine|Zoo-Zoo Nine]] had beaten the [[catalpa-nine|Catalpas]] by a [[scoring-baseball|score]] of eleven to one (008-001).

## Aftermath

The defeat at Bluford had lasting psychological effects on the [[catalpa-nine|Catalpa club]] and its supporters:

> The defeat at Bluford had unnerved all but a few faithful friends of the nine. (009-022)

Some of the [[dean-county-nine|Dean County Nine]], who had been the most enthusiastic boosters of the [[catalpa-nine|Catalpa Nine]], now adopted a discouraging attitude, predicting defeat "all along the line" (008-005). The loss at Bluford stood in stark contrast to the triumph at [[sandy-key|Sandy Key]], and tested the resolve of the young club as they prepared for the [[championship-baseball|championship series]] at [[galena|Galena]].
