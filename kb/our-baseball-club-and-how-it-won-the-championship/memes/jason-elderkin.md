**Jason "Jase" Elderkin** was the proprietor of a dry-goods store in [[catalpa-nine|Catalpa]] and a recognizable figure in the town's daily life. His store served as an informal meeting place where news of the [[national-game|baseball]] club was discussed, opinions were exchanged, and the pulse of the community could be taken.

## Identity

Elderkin was the town's dry-goods merchant, operating a store on one of [[catalpa-nine|Catalpa]]'s main streets. He used the same system as other storekeepers — hanging a little blue flag at his door to summon an errand boy when needed (009-001). As a local businessman, he was part of the fabric of [[catalpa-nine|Catalpa]]'s commercial life.

## In the Story

Elderkin appears in several contexts that reveal his role in the community. When the new [[baseball-positions|baseball nine]] was being organized, opinions formed in his store carried weight:

> "I was in Jase Elderkin's store, just now, and he allowed that he didn't believe there was enough first-class material in the two nines to make one good club." (004-026)

This skepticism, voiced from behind his counter, represented the doubts that the [[catalpa-nine|Catalpa Nine]] had to overcome. Yet Elderkin was not merely a critic — he followed the club's fortunes with the same interest as the rest of the town. After a [[catalpa-nine|Catalpa]] victory, he remarked with satisfaction:

> "The boys have covered themselves all over with glory," remarked Jason Elderkin. "How was that?" (007-043)

## Role in the Community

As a storekeeper, Elderkin was positioned at the intersection of [[catalpa-nine|Catalpa]]'s commercial and social life. His dry-goods store was a place where farmers, townspeople, and [[national-game|baseball]] enthusiasts crossed paths. When [[rough-and-ready|Rough and Ready]] was summoned to run an errand from Elderkin's store, the old man's banter about the [[national-game|baseball]] club reflected the way [[baseball-positions|the nine]]'s fortunes had become woven into the everyday transactions of the town (009-003).
