# Gunner Kronborg

## Overview

Gunner Kronborg is one of Thea Kronborg's brothers—a member of the large Kronborg household in Moonstone, Colorado. Like his siblings, Gunner represents the ordinary family life from which Thea's extraordinary artistic destiny diverges.

## Family Role

Gunner is part of the bustling Kronborg family, growing up in the Swedish Lutheran minister's household. His life follows the conventional patterns of Moonstone—school, church, and the limited horizons of a small railroad town—while Thea's path takes her far beyond.
