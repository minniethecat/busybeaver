# Metropolitan Opera House

## Overview

The Metropolitan Opera House in New York City—the institutional pinnacle of American musical culture where Thea Kronborg achieves her greatest professional triumphs as a Wagnerian soprano.

## Location & Geography

Located in New York City, this venue represents the highest level of American musical culture at the turn of the twentieth century. Its stage is the destination toward which Thea Kronborg's entire artistic journey has been directed.

## Role in the Novel

This location plays an important role in Thea Kronborg's artistic development, serving as one of the settings where her musical identity is formed and tested. Each location in the novel carries symbolic as well as narrative weight, representing stages in Thea's journey from provincial obscurity to international acclaim.
