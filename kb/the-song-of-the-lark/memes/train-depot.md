# Moonstone Train Depot

## Overview

The railroad station where trains arrive from and depart for the wider world—the physical threshold between Thea Kronborg's provincial childhood and her artistic destiny.

## Location & Geography

Colorado is an important setting in the geography of Thea Kronborg's life. Each location in *The Song of the Lark* serves both narrative and symbolic functions, marking stages in the protagonist's journey from provincial origins to international artistic acclaim.

## Role in the Novel

This place represents one of the thresholds Thea must cross—geographical, psychological, or professional—in her development as an artist. Cather's locations are never mere backdrop; they embody the tensions, possibilities, and costs of the artistic life.
