# Moonstone Swedish Lutheran Church

## Overview

The country church where Reverend Kronborg ministers to the Swedish immigrant community—the religious and moral center of Thea's childhood world.

## Location & Geography

A significant location in the geography of Thea Kronborg's life, situated within or near Moonstone, Colorado. Its physical character and social function contribute to the richly textured world of Thea's early years.

## Role in the Novel

This location plays an important role in Thea Kronborg's artistic development, serving as one of the settings where her musical identity is formed and tested. Each location in the novel carries symbolic as well as narrative weight, representing stages in Thea's journey from provincial obscurity to international acclaim.
