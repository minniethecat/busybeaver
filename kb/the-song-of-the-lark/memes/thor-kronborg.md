# Thor Kronborg

## Overview

Thor Kronborg is one of Thea's brothers—a member of the bustling Kronborg household whose life remains rooted in Moonstone while Thea's takes her far beyond the town's horizons. He is part of the family background that Thea carries with her into the wider world, a living connection to the provincial origins she transcends but never entirely leaves behind.

## Family Context

The Kronborg household includes several children, of whom Thea is the most remarkable. Thor and his siblings represent the ordinary life of the family—the life Thea might have lived had she not possessed her extraordinary gift. Their presence in the novel serves to normalize Thea's background, grounding her exceptional story in the familiar context of a large immigrant family.

## Significance

Thor, like his siblings, functions primarily as a representative of the world Thea comes from—the world of small-town family life, church activities, and the limited horizons of Moonstone. His very ordinariness underscores Thea's exceptionality.
