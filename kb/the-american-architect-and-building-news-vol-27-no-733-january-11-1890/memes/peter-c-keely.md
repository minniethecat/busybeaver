# Peter C. Keely

American architect known for designing numerous Catholic churches across the United States. He was the father of [[Charles Keely]], with whom he practiced in [[Brooklyn, New York]].

## In the Journal

Peter C. Keely is mentioned in Section 1 of the journal in the obituary notice for his son, Charles Keely:

> "The profession in Brooklyn, N.Y., has to mourn the loss of Mr. Charles Keely, son of Mr. Peter C. Keely, the architect of so many Catholic churches all over the country, and associated with his father in business." (001-012)

## Key Facts

- Designed Catholic churches across the United States
- Practiced architecture in Brooklyn, New York
- Father and business partner of Charles Keely
- His son Charles died at approximately thirty-five years of age, predeceasing him
