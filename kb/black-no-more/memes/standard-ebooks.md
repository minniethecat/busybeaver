## Overview

Standard Ebooks is the volunteer-driven project that produced the source edition of *[[black-no-more|Black No More]]* used for this wiki. The [[imprint|imprint]] describes it as "a volunteer-driven project that produces ebook editions of public domain literature using modern typography, technology, and editorial standards, and distributes them free of cost." The edition is based on a transcription from Project Gutenberg and digital scans from the HathiTrust Digital Library (Imprint).

## Role in the Narrative

Standard Ebooks is not a fictional entity but the real-world publisher of the source text. Its presence in this wiki acknowledges the digital infrastructure that makes public domain literature accessible — the contemporary equivalent of the Black newspapers and publishing networks that distributed [[george-schuyler|Schuyler]]'s work in 1931.

## Satirical Function

The inclusion of Standard Ebooks as a "page" in the wiki is meta-textual: it connects the novel's concern with the economics of racial media to the contemporary economics of digital publishing, where public domain texts circulate outside commercial markets entirely.
