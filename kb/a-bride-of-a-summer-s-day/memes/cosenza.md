# Cosenza

Cosenza is a city in the Calabria region of southern Italy. In the novel, it serves as the critical junction where the railway ends and the mountain journey to [[alta-lauria|Alta Lauria]] begins. (020-007)

## Role in the Plot

When [[ida-culvers|Ida Culvers]] and [[francesca-xardez|Francesca Xardez]] travelled from Naples toward Alta Lauria to find the dying [[violante|Violante]], they went direct to Cosenza and from there diverged through the mountain passes. A doctor was later hastily summoned from Cosenza when Violante's condition worsened. (020-007)(020-020)

[[sefton-culvers|Sefton Culvers]] and [[clive-redway|Clive Redway]] later followed the same route — changing trains for Cosenza, and at Cosenza changing again for the smaller mountain railways leading into La Sila. (022-002)

## Geography

Cosenza lies at the foot of the La Sila mountain range and functions as the gateway between the coastal railway from Naples and the interior highlands where Alta Lauria is situated. The earthquake-damaged village of [[careno|Caréno]] lies further along this mountain route. (022-002)
