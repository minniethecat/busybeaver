# Salvation Army

The Salvation Army is a Protestant Christian charitable organization, referenced in *A Bride of a Summer's Day* as the religious order to which [[martha-skinner|Martha "Mattie" Skinner]] belongs.

A sister of the Salvation Army is first mentioned in Chapter XIV: Clive Redway encounters a Salvation Army sister outside a hotel in [[paris|Paris]]. (014-049)

Later, in Chapter XV, the distinctive Salvation Army uniform identifies Mattie to Clive: her "straight black gown, and poke-bonnet" evokes "a vision of a Salvation Army poke-bonnet and a sweet, careworn face beneath it." (015-028)

## Key Quotations

> Clive had his attention arrested by a sister of the Salvation Army, who came out of the hotel and passed down the steps close to his elbow.
>
> (014-049)

> The "chapeau comme ça" at once conjured up to Clive's fancy a vision of a Salvation Army poke-bonnet and a sweet, careworn face beneath it.
