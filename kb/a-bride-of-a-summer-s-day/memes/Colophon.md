(025-004)[Catherine Louisa Pirkis](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Catherine_Louisa_Pirkis).

(025-005)This ebook was produced for  

(025-006)[Standard Ebooks](https://standardebooks.org/)  

(025-007)by  

(025-008)[Robin Whittleton](https://www.robinwhittleton.com/),  

(025-009)and is based on digital scans from the  

(025-010)[Internet Archive](https://archive.org/details/sim_all-the-year-round_1891-06-27_5_130).

(025-012)*In the Conservatory*,  

(025-013)a painting completed in 1879 by  

(025-014)[Édouard Manet](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/%C3%89douard_Manet).

(025-016)**League Spartan** and **Sorts Mill Goudy**  

(025-017)typefaces created in 2014 and 2009 by  

(025-018)[The League of Moveable Type](https://www.theleagueofmoveabletype.com/).
