# Royal Hussars

The Royal Hussars is the British Army cavalry regiment in which [[sefton-culvers|Sefton Culvers]] served. He is introduced in Chapter I as "Sefton Culvers, late of the Royal Hussars." (001-004)

Sefton later resigned his commission at Horse Guards before departing for Italy, a decision connected to the personal and financial difficulties that form the backstory of the novel.

## Key Quotations

> This was Ida, elder daughter of George, fifth Baron Culvers, on the day that she was married to her cousin, Captain Sefton Culvers, late of the Royal Hussars.
>
> (001-004)
