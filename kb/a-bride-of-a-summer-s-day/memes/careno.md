# Caréno

Caréno was one of a cluster of little villages in the approach to the [[la-sila|La Sila]] mountains in Calabria. It was "a tiny place that had been half-wrecked by the earthquake of 1870." (022-001)(022-002)

## Role in the Plot

News of [[violante-death|Violante's death]] met [[sefton-culvers|Sefton Culvers]] and [[clive-redway|Clive Redway]] at Caréno as they journeyed toward [[alta-lauria|Alta Lauria]]. The village was the last point accessible by railway — beyond it, travellers had to continue by mule through the mountain passes. (022-001)(022-002)
