# George Johnson

George Johnson is a minor character in *A Bride of a Summer's Day*, mentioned only in Chapter XVI as the associate of [[john-skinner|John Skinner]].

When Skinner is questioned about his involvement with [[marie-schira|Marie Schira]] and the missing diamond brooch, he offers Johnson as an alibi: "Only my chum, George Johnson, sir, who'll vouch for the truth of what I've been saying." (016-036)

## Key Quotations

> "Only my chum, George Johnson, sir, who'll vouch for the truth of what I say. Marie went into raptures over the brooch, and asked the Captain where he had got such a pretty thing from."
>
> (016-036)
