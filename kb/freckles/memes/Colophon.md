## Colophon

[021-001] ![The Standard Ebooks logo.](images/logo.png)

[021-002] *[[freckles|Freckles]]*

[021-003] was published in 1904 by

[021-004] [Gene Stratton-Porter](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Gene_Stratton-Porter).

[021-005] This ebook was produced for

[021-006] [Standard Ebooks](https://standardebooks.org/)

[021-007] by

[021-008] [Brian Evans](https://sloum.colorfield.space/),

[021-009] and is based on a transcription produced in 2006 by

[021-010] **An Anonymous Volunteer** and **David Widger**

[021-011] for

[021-012] [Project Gutenberg](https://www.gutenberg.org/ebooks/111)

[021-013] and on digital scans from the

[021-014] [Internet Archive](https://archive.org/details/freckles0000unse_q2v8).

[021-015] The cover page is adapted from

[021-016] *By the Pool*,

[021-017] a painting completed in 1892 by

[021-018] [Isaac Levitan](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Isaac_Levitan).

[021-019] The cover and title pages feature the

[021-020] **League Spartan** and **Sorts Mill Goudy**

[021-021] typefaces created in 2014 and 2009 by

[021-022] [The League of Moveable Type](https://www.theleagueofmoveabletype.com/).

[021-023] This edition was released on

[021-024] **June 30, 2026, 2:48 a.m.**

[021-025] and is based on

[021-026] **revision 612ec00**.

[021-027] The first edition of this ebook was released on

[021-028] February 20, 2026, 5:29 p.m.

[021-030] [standardebooks.org/ebooks/gene-stratton-porter/freckles](https://standardebooks.org/ebooks/gene-stratton-porter/freckles).
