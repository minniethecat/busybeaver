# Standard Quality Pages

Standard-quality entries form the backbone of the wiki. Each page at this tier includes
solid prose coverage, multiple citations from James Stephens' *Irish Fairy Tales*, and
complete frontmatter. These entries are reliable reference points for readers exploring
Irish mythology.

## Path to Featured

Standard pages with the highest reference counts and richest source-text coverage are
the strongest candidates for featured status. The [Most Cited Pages](/most-cited-pages)
index identifies pages whose narrative centrality justifies the additional investment
of a featured-tier enrichment pass.
