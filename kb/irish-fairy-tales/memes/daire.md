# Daire Degamra

Daire Degamra was the king of a wondrous island that lay beyond the shores of Ireland, visited by High King [[conn-of-the-hundred-battles|Conn of the Hundred Battles]] during his search for a new queen. His wife was the daughter of Lodan, and their son [[segda|Segda]] — whose name means "Sweet Speech" — became the key to resolving the geasa crisis that arose during Conn's visit. [^1]
