# Tuatha De Danann

The Tuatha De Danann — the divine folk of the goddess Danu — are the supernatural race at the
centre of the Mythological Cycle. In James Stephens' *Irish Fairy Tales*, they appear as the
Sidhe, the fairy folk who retreated into the hollow hills, wielding magic and prophecy. This
index gathers all pages tagged with their name.
