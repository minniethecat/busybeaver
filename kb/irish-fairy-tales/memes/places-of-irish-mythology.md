# Places of Irish Mythology

This index catalogs all locations mentioned in James Stephens' *Irish Fairy Tales*,
from royal seats and battlefields to sidhe mounds and Otherworld paradises.
The collection spans 20 place entries across all four provinces of Ireland and beyond.
