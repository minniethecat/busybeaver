# Basic Quality Pages

The pages listed below have reached basic quality — they include a description, core
sections, and at least one citation — but have not yet been enriched to the standard tier.
Each entry is a candidate for deeper coverage: additional blockquotes from the source text,
expanded prose sections, or further wikilinks to related entries.

## Enrichment Priority

Pages with higher reference counts are typically more central to the narrative and
should be prioritized for enrichment. The top candidates — pages with many inbound
wikilinks but only basic coverage — offer the greatest return on editing effort.
