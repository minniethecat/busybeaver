# Sceolan

Sceolan was one of the two legendary wolfhounds of [[fionn-mac-uail|Fionn mac Uail]], brother to [[bran|Bran]]. Like Bran, he was born of a woman of the [[tuatha-de-danann|Sidhe]] transformed into a hound, and became Fionn's faithful hunting companion throughout the Fenian tales. [^1]
