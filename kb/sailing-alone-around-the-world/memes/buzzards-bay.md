# Buzzard's Bay

Buzzard's Bay is a bay on the [[atlantic-ocean|Atlantic]] coast of [[united-states|Massachusetts]], south of [[boston|Boston]] and west of [[cape-cod|Cape Cod]]. It was here that [[joshua-slocum|Joshua Slocum]] and his friend [[eben-pierce|Captain Pierce]] conducted the *[[spray|Spray]]*'s first trial sail.

## In the Book

[[sail|Sail]]s were bent, and away she flew with [[joshua-slocum|Slocum]]'s friend [[eben-pierce|Captain Pierce]] and [[joshua-slocum|Slocum]] himself, across Buzzard's Bay on a trial trip — "all [[rigging|rig]]ht," [[joshua-slocum|Slocum]] recorded (001-015). This trial run confirmed the *[[spray|Spray]]*'s seaworthiness before her departure on the great voyage. [[eben-pierce|Captain Pierce]], an experienced skipper, was among the friends who helped [[joshua-slocum|Slocum]] prepare the vessel.

The bay lies within the waters of [[joshua-slocum|Slocum]]'s old cruising grounds. Before the [[voyage|circumnavigation]], [[joshua-slocum|Slocum]] had spent years [[sail|sailing]] these familiar New England waters, and Buzzard's Bay was well known to him from his earlier voyages as a merchant captain and fisherman.

> Sails were bent, and away she flew with my friend Captain Pierce and me, across Buzzard's Bay on a trial-trip — all right. The only thing that now worried my friends along the beach was, "Will she pay?"
>
> (001-015)

## Role in the Voyage

The trial trip across Buzzard's Bay was a critical milestone — the first time the rebuilt *[[spray|Spray]]* had been put under sail. Its success gave [[joshua-slocum|Slocum]] the confidence to proceed with final preparations for the [[voyage|circumnavigation]]. The bay thus marks the very beginning of the *[[spray|Spray]]*'s seagoing life.
