# Gloucester

Gloucester, Massachusetts — the old fishing port on [[cape-ann|Cape Ann]] — was the *[[spray|Spray]]*'s home port and the point from which [[joshua-slocum|Joshua Slocum]] departed on his solo [[voyage|circumnavigation]]. The town's maritime character, its shipyards and wharves and fishing fleets, provided the [[current|set]]ting for the *[[spray|Spray]]*'s rebuilding and launching, and its citizens gave the captain a send-off he never forgot.

## Geography

Gloucester occupies the southern shore of [[cape-ann|Cape Ann]], about 30 miles northeast of [[boston|Boston]]. Its deep-water harbor, sheltered by Eastern Point and Thatcher's Island, has been a center of North Atlantic fishing since the 17th century. The harbor entrance, marked by Thatcher's Island [[beacon|lighthouse]]s, was [[joshua-slocum|Slocum]]'s last sight of American shores as he shaped his course east. The surrounding waters — Cashes Ledge, the Amen Rocks, [[bay-of-fundy|the Bay]] of [[bay-of-fundy|Fundy]] — were [[joshua-slocum|Slocum]]'s home ground, waters he had fished and sailed since boyhood.

## In the Voyage

The *[[spray|Spray]]* was rebuilt at [[fairhaven|Fairhaven]], across the bay from Gloucester, but Gloucester was her true home. [[joshua-slocum|Slocum]] described his departure with unusual warmth. "The weather was mild on the day of my departure from Gloucester. On the point ahead, as the *[[spray|Spray]]* stood out of the cove, was a lively picture, for the front of a tall factory was a flutter of handkerchiefs and caps. Pretty faces peered out of the windows from the top to the bottom of the building, all smiling bon voyage" (002-011). The send-off — handkerchiefs, cheers, a salute from the shore — was the last human contact [[joshua-slocum|Slocum]] would have with his home port for three years.

From Gloucester, [[joshua-slocum|Slocum]] "shaping her course east, by compass, to go north of Cashes Ledge and the Amen Rocks" (002-011), began the voyage that would take him [[voyage|around the world]]. The town faded astern, but its memory — the pretty faces at the factory windows, the hearty salute — stayed with him.

## Significance

Gloucester is the alpha point of the [[voyage|circumnavigation]]. Every departure needs a place, and [[joshua-slocum|Slocum]]'s was this old fishing port, whose sailors had been working the North Atlantic for generations. The *[[spray|Spray]]* was a Gloucester vessel by adoption, and the town's send-off — "the last of many kindnesses to her at Gloucester" (002-011) — testifies to the affection between the captain and his port.

> I made for the cove, a lovely branch of Gloucester's fine harbor, again to look the Spray over and again to weigh the voyage, and my feelings, and all that. The bay was feather-white as my little vessel tore in, smothered in foam.
>
> (002-006)
