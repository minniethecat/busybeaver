## Passing By

Early one morning during his southward passage along the Australian coast, Slocum "passed Twofold Bay and later Cape Bundooro in a driving gale" (013-015). The bay was noted as a navigational waypoint rather than an anchorage for the Spray.

> The Spray early in the morning passed Twofold Bay and later Cape Bundooro in a smooth sea with land close aboard. The lighthouse on the cape dipped a flag to the Spray's flag, and children on the balconies of a cottage near the shore waved handkerchiefs as she passed by.
>
> (013-015)
