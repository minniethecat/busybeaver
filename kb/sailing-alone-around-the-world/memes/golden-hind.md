# Golden Hind

The *Golden Hind* was the flagship of [[francis-drake|Sir Francis Drake]]'s [[voyage|circumnavigation]] of 1577–1580 — the second expedition to circle the globe and the first commanded by a single man throughout. For [[joshua-slocum|Slocum]], navigating the same southern waters that [[francis-drake|Drake]] had sailed, the *Golden Hind* was a historical benchmark: a small ship, a determined captain, and the world's oceans.

## Design and Construction

The *Golden Hind* was a galleon — a type developed in the 16th century combining cargo capacity with fighting ability. She was originally named *Pelican*; [[francis-drake|Drake]] renamed her mid-voyage in honor of his patron, Sir Christopher Hat[[tonnage|ton]], whose coat of arms featured a golden hind. She was about 100–150 [[tonnage|tons]], roughly 70–80 feet [[deck|on deck]] — small by modern standards but adequate for her purpose.

## In Slocum's Narrative

[[joshua-slocum|Slocum]] does not mention the *Golden Hind* by name, but [[francis-drake|Drake]]'s presence — and by extension his ship — looms over the southern chapters of the narrative. The [[strait-of-magellan|Strait of Magellan]], through which [[francis-drake|Drake]] had passed in 1578, was the supreme test of both voyages. [[francis-drake|Drake]] had a crew; [[joshua-slocum|Slocum]] had only himself. The *Golden Hind* carried cannon to fight the Spanish; the *[[spray|Spray]]* carried a rifle to deter [[tierra-del-fuego|Fuegia]]ns. But the fundamental challenge — navigating an unknown and hostile coast — was identical.

> One of the old circumnavigators (Sir Francis Drake, I think), when he first saw this magnificent pile, sang, " 'Tis the fairest thing and the grandest cape I've seen in the whole circumference of the earth."
>
> (018-014)

## Legacy

The *Golden Hind* represents the Elizabethan roots of the [[voyage|circumnavigation]] tradition. [[ferdinand-magellan|Magellan]]'s *[[victoria|Victoria]]* was the first; [[francis-drake|Drake]]'s *Golden Hind* was the second; [[joshua-slocum|Slocum]]'s *[[spray|Spray]]* was the first to do it alone. The vessels grew smaller, the crews fewer, but the achievement remained.
