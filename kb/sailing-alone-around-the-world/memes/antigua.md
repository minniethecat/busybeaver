**Antigua** is an island in the Leeward Islands of the West Indies, visited by [[joshua-slocum|Joshua Slocum]] near the end of his solo [[voyage|circumnavigation]]. It was one of his last ports of call in the Caribbean before the final passage home to [[newport|Newport]].

## Arrival

[[joshua-slocum|Slocum]] arrived at St. John's, Antigua, on June 1, having sailed from [[grenada|Grenada]] with "great caution in mid-channel all the way" (020-012). His passage through the Antilles had been eventful: at [[dominica|Dominica]] he had been ordered to move his anchorage by a officious deputy harbormaster, and had left in exasperation after finding no charts available on the island.

Upon entering Antigua's harbor, the *[[spray|Spray]]* was met by the port officers' steam-launch carrying [[francis-drake|Sir Francis]] Fleming, governor of the Leeward Islands, who "to the delight of 'all hands,' gave the officer in charge instructions to tow my ship into port" (020-013). The following day, His Excellency and Lady Fleming, along with Captain Burr, R.N., paid [[joshua-slocum|Slocum]] a visit on board the *[[spray|Spray]]*.

## The Lecture

As at [[grenada|Grenada]], [[joshua-slocum|Slocum]] was offered the courthouse for a public lecture: "The courthouse was tendered free to me at Antigua, as was done also at [[grenada|Grenada]], and at each place a highly intelligent audience filled the hall to listen to a talk about the seas the *[[spray|Spray]]* had crossed, and the countries she had visited" (020-013).

> The *Spray*, always in good company, now fell in with the port officers' steam-launch at the harbor entrance, having on board Sir Francis Fleming, governor of the Leeward Islands, who, to the delight of "all hands," gave the officer in charge instructions to tow my ship into port. On the following day his Excellency and Lady Fleming, along with Captain Burr, R.N., paid me a visit. The courthouse was tendered free to me at Antigua, as was done also at Grenada, and at each place a highly intelligent audience filled the hall to listen to a talk about the seas the *Spray* had crossed, and the countries she had visited.

## A Culinary Milestone

[[joshua-slocum|Slocum]] recorded a curious personal landmark at Antigua. A large fruitcake, sent by [[governor-sterndale|Lady Sterndale]] from [[st-helena|St. Helena]], had been carefully rationed across [[atlantic-ocean|the Atlantic]]. "I ate the last of it along with my first cup of coffee at Antigua, West Indies, which, after all, was quite a record" (019-006). The cake had lasted forty-two days — the same duration as the one his sister had made for him at the voyage's beginning in the [[bay-of-fundy|Bay of Fundy]].
