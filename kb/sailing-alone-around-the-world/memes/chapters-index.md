## About the Chapter Headings

[[joshua-slocum|Slocum]]'s chapter [[course|heading]]s are notable for their length and detail — each effectively a prose abstract of the chapter's contents. They follow the nineteenth-century convention of descriptive titling, allowing a reader to locate specific episodes without an index.
