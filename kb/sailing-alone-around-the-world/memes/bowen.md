**Bowen** is a coastal town in [[australia|Queensland]], situated at [[port-denison|Port Denison]]. [[joshua-slocum|Joshua Slocum]] anchored the *[[spray|Spray]]* there in 1897 and found it a quiet, pleasant community where even a lone circumnavigator became the latest event in town.

## Arrival

On the morning of May 26, 1897, with [[gloucester|Gloucester]] Island close aboard, the *[[spray|Spray]]* "anchored in the evening at [[port-denison|Port Denison]], where rests, on a hill, the sweet little town of Bowen, the future watering place and health-resort of Queensland" (015-002). [[joshua-slocum|Slocum]] remarked that "the country all about here had a healthful appearance" (015-002) and noted the harbor was "easy of approach, spacious and safe, and afforded excellent holding-ground" (015-003).

## The Lecture

Bowen was quiet when the *[[spray|Spray]]* arrived, and on the second evening the townspeople "came down to the School of Arts to talk about the voyage, it being the latest event" (015-003). The occasion was advertised in the town's two newspapers — "in the one the day before the affair came off, and in the other the day after, which was all the same to the editor, and, for that matter, it was the same to me" (015-003).

[[joshua-slocum|Slocum]] was less amused by the town crier hired to promote the event. "I could have keelhauled the wretch, bell and all, when he came to the door of the little hotel where my prospective audience and I were dining, and with his clattering bell and fiendish yell made noises that would awake the dead" (015-004).

The local paper, the *[[boom|Boom]]erang*, afterward described the *[[spray|Spray]]*'s voyage as running from "[[boston|Boston]] to Bowen, the two Hubs in the cartwheels of creation" (015-004) — a phrase that delighted [[joshua-slocum|Slocum]].

> On the morning of the 26th Gloucester Island was close aboard, and the *Spray* anchored in the evening at Port Denison, where rests, on a hill, the sweet little town of Bowen, the future watering place and health-resort of Queensland. The country all about here had a healthful appearance.
>
> (015-002)
