## Geography and Location

The Tamar River, located in [[tasmania|Tasmania]], Australia, flows northward to the sea. Slocum anchored the [[spray|Spray]] at Port Tamar for shelter, with [[cape-pillar|Cape Pillar]] in sight (007-028). The Spray later sailed from Port Tamar direct for Cape Pillar (008-002).

Near the head of the Tamar lay a settlement Slocum noted for its position (013-028). Another small settlement stood near the mouth of the river Tamar (014-007).

## Slocum's Visit

Port Tamar served as a sheltered anchorage during the Spray's passage along the Tasmanian coast. The harbor provided a secure resting point before Slocum ventured around Cape Pillar.
> It was the 3rd of March when the Spray sailed from Port Tamar direct for Cape Pillar, with the wind from the northeast, which I fervently hoped might hold till she cleared the land; but there was no such good luck in store. It soon began to rain and thicken in the northwest, boding no good. The Spray reared Cape Pillar rapidly, and, nothing loath, plunged into the Pacific Ocean at once, taking her first bath of it in the gathering storm. There was no turning back even had I wished to do so, for the land was now shut out by the darkness of night.
>
> (008-002)
