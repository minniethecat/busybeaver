# Java

The *Java* was a steamship encountered by [[joshua-slocum|Slocum]] in [[atlantic-ocean|the Atlantic]]. Her captain, in a gesture of professional courtesy, provided [[joshua-slocum|Slocum]] with a position fix — a kindness that filled the gap left by the chronometer the *[[spray|Spray]]* lacked.

## The Encounter

The *Java*'s captain "gave me the longitude and the time. I admired the businesslike manner in which he took his sights" (003-014). For [[joshua-slocum|Slocum]], navigating without a chronometer, such encounters were not merely pleasant — they were vital confirmations of his dead-reckoning position. The *Java*, like other passing vessels, represented the professional maritime community reaching out to the solitary sailor.

> "It lacks now three minutes of the half-hour," shouted the captain, as he gave me the longitude and the time. I admired the businesslike air of the Olympia.
>
> (003-014)
