## Early Voyages

Brazil featured at several critical points in Slocum's seafaring life. The captain of the [[aquidneck|Aquidneck]] was lost overboard on the coast of Brazil, where the ship was wrecked (001-004). Slocum later sailed a ship successfully to Brazil with an ordinary crew (002-015).

## Slocum's Passage

During the [[spray|Spray]]'s circumnavigation, Slocum made for the coast of Brazil, arriving on October 5 just north of Pernambuco (005-023). The Brazilian coast formed the western boundary of his South Atlantic passage.
> On October 24, 1895, a fine day even as days go in Brazil, the Spray sailed, having had abundant good cheer. Making about one hundred miles a day along the coast, I arrived at [[rio-de-janeiro|Rio de Janeiro]] November 5, without any event worth mentioning, and about noon cast anchor near Villaganon, to await the official port visit.
>
> (005-029)


After crossing the Atlantic from Africa, Slocum approached Brazil near Pernambuco. The Brazilian government became the subject of a formal complaint (a "bill") recorded in his narrative (005-001).
