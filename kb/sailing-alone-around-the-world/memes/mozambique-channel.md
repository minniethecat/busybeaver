## A Hard Gale

During the passage between November 8 and 9, "in the Mozambique Channel, she experienced a hard gale" (017-014). The Spray, as always, proved seaworthy in heavy weather, riding out the storm as Slocum continued his westward passage toward the [[cape-of-good-hope|Cape of Good Hope]] and the South African coast.

> On the 6th, 7th, 8th, and 9th of November, in the Mozambique Channel, she experienced a hard gale of wind from the southwest. Here the Spray suffered as much as she did anywhere, except off Cape Horn. The thunder and lightning preceding this gale were very heavy.
>
> (017-014)
