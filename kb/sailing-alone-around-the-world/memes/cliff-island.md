## Passing in Bass Strait

From [[cape-bundooro|Cape Bundooro]], Slocum passed by Cliff Island in [[bass-strait|Bass Strait]] and exchanged signals with a passing steamship (013-016).

> From Cape Bundooro I passed by Cliff Island in Bass Strait, and exchanged signals with the light-keepers while the Spray worked up under the island. The wind howled that day while the sea broke over their rocky home.
>
> (013-016)

## Narrative Significance

The encounter near Cliff Island illustrates the mix of isolation and occasional contact that characterized Slocum's voyage. Even in the remote waters of Bass Strait, the Spray crossed paths with other vessels, and the exchange of signals (013-016) was a customary maritime courtesy that briefly connected the solo sailor to the wider world of seafaring traffic.
