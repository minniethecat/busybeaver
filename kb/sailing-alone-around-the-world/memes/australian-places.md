## Slocum in Australia

[[joshua-slocum|Slocum]]'s [[australia|Australia]]n sojourn was among the most socially active periods of his [[voyage|circumnavigation]]. He was feted by yacht clubs, presented with new [[sail|sails]], and delivered lectures on his voyage. The [[australia|Australia]]n leg also marked a strategic decision: to take the inner route through the [[great-barrier-reef|Great Barrier Reef]] and [[torres-strait|Torres Strait]] rather than face the [[gale|storm]]y southern capes.
