## Definition & Context

Conditional Necessity is a concept that plays a supporting role in [[boethius|Boethius]]'s philosophical argument in the *Consolation of Philosophy*. It contributes to [[lady-philosophy|Philosophy]]'s systematic exposition and therapeutic programme.

## Role in the Consolation

This concept functions within the broader Neoplatonic and Christian framework that structures the *Consolation*. It illuminates aspects of Philosophy's argument about [[providence|Providence]], [[fate|Fate]], [[free-will|free will]], or [[happiness|true happiness]].
