# George Finch

## Overview

George Finch is the protagonist of *The Small Bachelor*. A young [[art-and-artists|painter]] living in [[new-york|New York]]'s [[art-and-artists|Bohemian]] [[Greenwich-Village|Greenwich Village]], George falls in [[love-and-romance|love]] [[humor-and-comedy|wit]]h [[Molly-Waddington|Molly Waddington]] at first sight and must overcome the opposition of her [[class-and-society|social]]ly ambitious mother to win her hand. His identity as an [[art-and-artists|artist]] — and the [[class-and-society|social]] prejudices that attach to it — drives much of the novel's conflict.
