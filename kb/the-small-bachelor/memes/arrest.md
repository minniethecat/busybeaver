# The Arrest

## In The Small Bachelor

It is difficult to arrest the progress of a millionaire who is starting off in the direction of caviar, but Mr. Waddington, with a frenzied clutch at the other’s coat-sleeve, succeeded in doing so for

“I did not know her socially. I was arresting her.”
