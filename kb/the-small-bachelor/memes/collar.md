# Collars

## In The Small Bachelor

“May!” George Finch placed a finger inside his collar, as if trying to loosen it. “May! I—I’ve just been down to the station to meet you.”

The policeman seemed taken aback. His face became as red as his wrists. But for the collar, which held him in a grip of iron, his jaw would no doubt have fallen.
