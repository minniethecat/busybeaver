The major Navajo ceremonial cycle that Laughing Boy aspires to learn — a multi-night ritual combining songs, sand-paintings, prayers, and healing practices

## Summary

This event occurs in the early chapters of *Laughing Boy*, depicted in Ch.I,III. It brings together the Navajo community for ceremony, social interaction, and competition.
