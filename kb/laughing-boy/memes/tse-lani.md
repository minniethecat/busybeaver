# Tsé Lani

The location of the great dance and horse-racing gathering in the opening chapters of Laughing Boy — a ceremonial and social center of Navajo life

## Geography

Ceremonial gathering place in the Navajo country, described in the novel's opening chapters.

## In the Narrative

This location is central to the events of *Laughing Boy*, serving as the setting for key scenes in Ch.I,II,III.

## Cultural Significance

This place holds significance within the Navajo world depicted in the novel, connected to the ceremonial and social life of the characters.
