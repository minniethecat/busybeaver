# The Hidden Hogan

The secret hogan where Laughing Boy and Slim Girl establish their married life — a secluded dwelling symbolizing Slim Girl's attempt to build a protected Navajo life away from American influence

## Geography

Isolated Navajo dwelling; site of Slim Girl and Laughing Boy's marriage, described in the novel's opening chapters.

## In the Narrative

This location is central to the events of *Laughing Boy*, serving as the setting for key scenes in Ch.VIII,IX,X.

## Cultural Significance

This place holds significance within the Navajo world depicted in the novel, connected to the ceremonial and social life of the characters.
