# The Riverbank

The river near [[blankville|Blankville]] whose waters conceal secrets — a symbolic landscape of hidden crimes and eventual revelation.

## Description

A location in the world of *The [[dead-letter|Dead Letter]]*, contributing to the novel's geographic and social landscape.

## Significance

Part of the setting against which [[murder-of-henry-moreland|the murder]] mystery unfolds — the physical and institutional spaces that [[honor-and-reputation|character]]s inhabit, traverse, and investigate.
