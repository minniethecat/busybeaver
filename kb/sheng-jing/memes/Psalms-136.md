# Psalms 136

[019-136-001] 136:1 O give thanks unto the LORD; for he is good: for his mercy endureth for

[019-136-002] 136:2 O give thanks unto the God of gods: for his mercy endureth for ever.

[019-136-003] 136:3 O give thanks to the Lord of lords: for his mercy endureth for ever.

[019-136-004] 136:4 To him who alone doeth great wonders: for his mercy endureth for ever.

[019-136-005] 136:5 To him that by wisdom made the heavens: for his mercy endureth for ever.

[019-136-006] 136:6 To him that stretched out the earth above the waters: for his mercy

[019-136-007] 136:7 To him that made great lights: for his mercy endureth for ever:

[019-136-008] 136:8 The sun to rule by day: for his mercy endureth for ever:

[019-136-009] 136:9 The moon and stars to rule by night: for his mercy endureth for ever.

[019-136-010] 136:10 To him that smote [[埃及|Egypt]] in their firstborn: for his mercy endureth for

[019-136-011] 136:11 And brought out Israel from among them: for his mercy endureth for ever:

[019-136-012] 136:12 With a strong hand, and with a stretched out arm: for his mercy endureth

[019-136-013] 136:13 To him which divided the Red sea into parts: for his mercy endureth for

[019-136-014] 136:14 And made Israel to pass through the midst of it: for his mercy endureth

[019-136-015] 136:15 But overthrew Pharaoh and his host in the Red sea: for his mercy

[019-136-016] 136:16 To him which led his people through the wilderness: for his mercy

[019-136-017] 136:17 To him which smote great kings: for his mercy endureth for ever:

[019-136-018] 136:18 And slew famous kings: for his mercy endureth for ever:

[019-136-019] 136:19 Sihon king of the Amorites: for his mercy endureth for ever:

[019-136-020] 136:20 And Og the king of Bashan: for his mercy endureth for ever:

[019-136-021] 136:21 And gave their land for an heritage: for his mercy endureth for ever:

[019-136-022] 136:22 Even an heritage unto Israel his servant: for his mercy endureth for

[019-136-023] 136:23 Who remembered us in our low estate: for his mercy endureth for ever:

[019-136-024] 136:24 And hath redeemed us from our enemies: for his mercy endureth for ever.

[019-136-025] 136:25 Who giveth food to all flesh: for his mercy endureth for ever.

[019-136-026] 136:26 O give thanks unto the God of heaven: for his mercy endureth for ever.
