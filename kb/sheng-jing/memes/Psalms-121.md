# Psalms 121

[019-121-001] 121:1 I will lift up mine eyes unto the hills, from whence cometh my help.

[019-121-002] 121:2 My help cometh from the LORD, which made heaven and earth.

[019-121-003] 121:3 He will not suffer thy foot to be moved: he that keepeth thee will not

[019-121-004] 121:4 Behold, he that keepeth Israel shall neither slumber nor sleep.

[019-121-005] 121:5 The LORD is thy keeper: the LORD is thy shade upon thy right hand.

[019-121-006] 121:6 The sun shall not smite thee by day, nor the moon by night.

[019-121-007] 121:7 The LORD shall preserve thee from all evil: he shall preserve thy soul.

[019-121-008] 121:8 The LORD shall preserve thy going out and thy coming in from this time
