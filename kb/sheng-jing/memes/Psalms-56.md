# Psalms 56

[019-056-001] 56:1 Be merciful unto me, O God: for man would swallow me up; he fighting daily

[019-056-002] 56:2 Mine enemies would daily swallow me up: for they be many that fight

[019-056-003] 56:3 What time I am afraid, I will trust in thee.

[019-056-004] 56:4 In God I will praise his word, in God I have put my trust; I will not fear

[019-056-005] 56:5 Every day they wrest my words: all their thoughts are against me for evil.

[019-056-006] 56:6 They gather themselves together, they hide themselves, they mark my steps,

[019-056-007] 56:7 Shall they escape by iniquity? in thine anger cast down the people, O God.

[019-056-008] 56:8 Thou tellest my wanderings: put thou my tears into thy bottle: are they

[019-056-009] 56:9 When I cry unto thee, then shall mine enemies turn back: this I know; for

[019-056-010] 56:10 In God will I praise his word: in the LORD will I praise his word.

[019-056-011] 56:11 In God have I put my trust: I will not be afraid what man can do unto me.

[019-056-012] 56:12 Thy vows are upon me, O God: I will render praises unto thee.

[019-056-013] 56:13 For thou hast delivered my soul from death: wilt not thou deliver my feet
