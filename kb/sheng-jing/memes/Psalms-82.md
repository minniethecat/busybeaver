# Psalms 82

[019-082-001] 82:1 God standeth in the congregation of the mighty; he judgeth among the gods.

[019-082-002] 82:2 How long will ye judge unjustly, and accept the persons of the wicked?

[019-082-003] 82:3 Defend the poor and fatherless: do justice to the afflicted and needy.

[019-082-004] 82:4 Deliver the poor and needy: rid them out of the hand of the wicked.

[019-082-005] 82:5 They know not, neither will they understand; they walk on in darkness: all

[019-082-006] 82:6 I have said, Ye are gods; and all of you are children of the most High.

[019-082-007] 82:7 But ye shall die like men, and fall like one of the princes.

[019-082-008] 82:8 Arise, O God, judge the earth: for thou shalt inherit all nations.
