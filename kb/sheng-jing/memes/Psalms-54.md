# Psalms 54

[019-054-001] 54:1 Save me, O God, by thy name, and judge me by thy strength.

[019-054-002] 54:2 Hear my prayer, O God; give ear to the words of my mouth.

[019-054-003] 54:3 For strangers are risen up against me, and oppressors seek after my soul:

[019-054-004] 54:4 Behold, God is mine helper: the Lord is with them that uphold my soul.

[019-054-005] 54:5 He shall reward evil unto mine enemies: cut them off in thy truth.

[019-054-006] 54:6 I will freely sacrifice unto thee: I will praise thy name, O LORD; for it

[019-054-007] 54:7 For he hath delivered me out of all trouble: and mine eye hath seen his
