# Psalms 3

[019-003-001] 3:1 Lord, how are they increased that trouble me! many are they that rise up

[019-003-002] 3:2 Many there be which say of my soul, There is no help for him in God.

[019-003-003] 3:3 But thou, O LORD, art a shield for me; my glory, and the lifter up of mine

[019-003-004] 3:4 I cried unto the LORD with my voice, and he heard me out of his holy hill.

[019-003-005] 3:5 I laid me down and slept; I awaked; for the LORD sustained me.

[019-003-006] 3:6 I will not be afraid of ten thousands of people, that have set themselves

[019-003-007] 3:7 Arise, O LORD; save me, O my God: for thou hast smitten all mine enemies

[019-003-008] 3:8 Salvation belongeth unto the LORD: thy blessing is upon thy people.
