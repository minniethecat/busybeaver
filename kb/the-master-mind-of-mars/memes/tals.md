## Overview

The tal (plural: tals) is a Barsoomian unit of time approximately equal to one Earth second. It is the smallest commonly referenced unit of time on Barsoom and appears frequently in moments of urgency or when precise short durations must be communicated. Like other Barsoomian measurements, it reflects the distinct cultural and scientific framework of Martian civilization.

## Description

The tal is used in the novel to convey brief, critical time spans. When Ras Thavas's heart nearly stops, he tells Vad Varo it "was like to have stopped a few tals ago" — a narrow window that underscores the urgency of the brain transplant procedure [004-001]. Similarly, when Vad Varo threatens Ras Thavas during the confrontation over the suspended animation chamber, he demands that Ras Thavas "in five tals" make a decision, or the button will be pressed — emphasizing the immediate, life-or-death nature of the ultimatum [004-002].

The brevity of the tal is confirmed by the dwar of the Jeddara's Guard, who reports that it has been "scarce twenty tals" since an event — roughly twenty seconds, an appropriately short time for a patrol responding to an alarm [012-001]. This is consistent with the tal being the Barsoomian equivalent of the second.

Barsoomian timekeeping extends beyond the tal to larger units. The novel references longer durations in other Barsoomian terms (such as the "zode" for roughly 2.5 hours), but the tal is the fundamental building block. The existence of such precise, named time units reflects the advanced scientific culture of Barsoom, where Ras Thavas can time a four-hour brain transplant operation with precision.

## Significance

The tal demonstrates the worldbuilding depth of the Barsoom setting, where even units of time have been given distinct names. In narrative terms, the tal serves as a pacing device — references to "five tals" or "twenty tals" create a sense of urgency and immediacy that the generic "seconds" would not, precisely because the unfamiliar term draws the reader's attention to the short duration involved.
