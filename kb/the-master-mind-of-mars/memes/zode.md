## Zode

A **zode** is a Barsoomian unit of time measurement, roughly analogous to an Earth hour. The term is used throughout Martian society as part of their standard temporal reckoning system.

In the Temple of Tur, the narrator demanded that the Phundahlians "clear the whole palace within a quarter *zode*" to allow an unobstructed field of action for the rescue of Valla Dia (012-063). This suggests a zode is sufficiently long that a quarter of one provides adequate time to evacuate a large building.

When priests interrogated a pursuing *dwar*, they reported "we have been here for a full *zode* and no other has entered the temple during that time" (011-117). The context indicates the priests had been stationed in the temple long enough to credibly assert that no fugitives could have slipped past them—a span comparable to roughly one Earth hour.

### Usage in Barsoomian Timekeeping

The zode appears alongside other Barsoomian temporal units, including the *tal* (a shorter span). That the pursuing warrior described the fugitives' departure as "scarce twenty *tals* since" while the priests reported their vigil at "a full *zode*" establishes a hierarchical relationship between these units, with the zode being the larger measure.
