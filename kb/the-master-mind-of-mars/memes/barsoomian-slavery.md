# Barsoomian Slavery

**Barsoomian slavery** is the institution of chattel bondage practiced across the various nations of Barsoom (Mars). Slaves serve as laborers, household attendants, and in Ras Thavas's medical institution at Toonol, as assistants who perform routine tasks under the master surgeon's direction.

## The Role of Slaves at Ras Thavas's Institution

In the Tower of Thavas at Toonol, slaves function as the primary workforce. They carry bodies, prepare patients for surgery, and attend to the daily needs of the establishment. Ras Thavas, the master surgeon, relies on slaves exclusively for manual labor while his brain alone directs the complex medical procedures.

As Ulysses Paxton (Vad Varo) observes: "the slaves and attendants that served him were but hewers of wood and carriers of water. It was his brain alone and his skill that directed the sometimes beneficent, the sometimes malevolent, but always marvelous activities of his life's work."[002-016]

A slave was dispatched to summon Paxton to Ras Thavas's bedside when the old surgeon's health failed: "One morning I was summoned to his sleeping apartment by a slave. I found the old surgeon lying, a shrunken, pitiful heap of withered skin and bones."[004-100]

## Body Resurgence as a Source of Slave Labor

Ras Thavas's technology of brain transplantation and body resurgence enables the creation or repurposing of slaves. A body designated "378-J-493811-P" was selected for resurgence "because of an existing need for another male slave" — the previous personality and identity of the body immaterial to its new utility.[002-038]

## Acquisition Through the Auction Block

The slave trade on Barsoom operates through public auctions. Dar Tarus, the Duhorian noble, relates how after being captured by a Phundahlian fleet, "We were taken to Phundahl where we were put upon the auction block and I fell to the bid of one of Ras Thavas' agents."[004-074] This system treats persons of any rank as commodities transferable through commercial transactions.

## Social Status of Slaves

Slaves occupy the lowest rung of Barsoomian society. The overseer at Ras Thavas's institution rebuked Vad Varo with the declaration: "You are naught but a slave. Cease your impudence or I shall erase you—erase you. It will be as though you never had existed."[ch??p602] The threat of erasure — the complete destruction of identity and existence — reveals the absolute power held over slaves, who possess no legal personhood.
