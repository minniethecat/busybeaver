## Overview

Tars Tarkas is the Jeddak of the Tharks, the towering Green Martian warrior who became [[John Carter]]'s closest friend and ally in the early chronicles of [[Barsoom]]. Though he does not appear in the events of this novel, his name was among those that fired the imagination of [[Ulysses Paxton|vad-varo]] and set him on the path to Mars.

## In the Narrative

Ulysses Paxton first encountered Tars Tarkas through the pages of the same novel that introduced him to John Carter and [[Dejah Thoris]]. He writes of dreaming of "John Carter, of Dejah Thoris, of Tars Tarkas and of Woola as if they had been entities of my own experience rather than the figments of your imagination" [000-003].

This literary encounter places Tars Tarkas in the company of Barsoom's most iconic figures. Unlike many of the novel's characters — who are products of [[Ras Thavas]]'s surgical experiments or the political machinations of [[Phundahl]] — Tars Tarkas belongs to the world of legend that existed before and beyond the novel's immediate concerns. He represents the broader Barsoomian universe into which Paxton has been drawn, a reminder that the events unfolding in [[Toonol]] and Phundahl are but one chapter in a much larger history.

## Significance

Tars Tarkas embodies the Green Martian warrior tradition — a culture of honor and combat that stands in contrast to the decadent courts of the Red Martian cities. His friendship with John Carter, a bond that transcended the ancient enmity between Green and Red Martians, set the template for the cross-species alliances that Paxton himself would later form with [[Gor Hajus]], [[Dar Tarus]], and [[Hovan Du]].
