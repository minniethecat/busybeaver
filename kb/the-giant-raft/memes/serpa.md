# Serpa

## Overview

Serpa is a village on the lower [[Amazon River]] in Brazil. Historically a Portuguese settlement, it was successively relocated from its original site — a practice not uncommon among riverside communities subject to the [[Amazon]]'s shifting banks. The modern city of Itacoatiara occupies the general area.

## In the Novel

The [[Jangada]] passed before the village of Serpa during the final stages of its journey down the [[Amazon]]. The settlement had been successively transported from its earlier location along the river [040-003].
