# Jahuma Island

## Overview

Jahuma Island is a large island in the middle [[Amazon River]], encountered by the crew of the [[Giant Raft]] during their downstream journey. The island is notable in the narrative for its striking visual appearance — tall vegetation silhouetted against the evening sky.

## In the Novel

The [[Jangada]] passed "alongside the large island of Jahuma" as it continued its voyage down the [[Amazon]] [011-024]. The island made a strong visual impression: "the trees on the left bank and on the island of Jahuma stood up in sharp black outline against the sky" — a memorable image of the Amazonian landscape at dusk [011-026].
