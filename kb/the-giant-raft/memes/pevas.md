# Pevas

## Overview

Pevas (also spelled Pebas) is a small Peruvian settlement on the upper [[Amazon River]], with a population of about 260 inhabitants at the time of the novel's events. It was established as a [[Mission]] settlement and lies along the journey route from [[Iquitos]] toward the Brazilian frontier.

## In the Novel

The [[Jangada]] passed through Pevas as it traveled down the [[Amazon]]. Leaving the island of [[Iquitos]] and passing through several tributary channels, the raft arrived at Pevas on the evening of the 11th, where the travelers made a brief stop [010-053]. The village, with its population of 260, consisted mainly of [[Mission]]-established dwellings [010-054].

The journey segment from [[Iquitos]] to Pevas took the raft through the narrowing channels of the [[Upper Amazon]] [010-001]. From Pevas, the [[Jangada]] continued its voyage toward the Brazilian frontier [011-001].
