# Fonteboa

## Overview

Fonteboa is a small [[Mission]] village on the middle [[Amazon River]] in Brazil, with approximately 1,000 inhabitants at the time of the novel's events. Like many settlements along the [[Amazon]], it was established as a mission outpost. The village is situated near the mouth of the Cayaratu River and is noted for its [[Church]], the Church of Notre Dame de Guadaloupe, and its [[manatee]] hunting tradition.

## In the Novel

The [[Jangada]] arrived before the village of Fonteboa during its journey down the [[Amazon]] [015-034]. Fonteboa, described as a typical [[Mission]] village of the region, lay along the river route [015-036].

Near the village, the Cayaratu River joins the [[Amazon]]. At this confluence, six manatees were spotted moving in the water [015-037]. The natives of Fonteboa were not deceived by the floating wreckage camouflage that concealed the fugitives, demonstrating their familiarity with the river's dangers [015-038].

At sunrise, the [[Jangada]] departed Fonteboa and entered the channel between the two banks of the [[Amazon]] to continue its journey [015-046].
