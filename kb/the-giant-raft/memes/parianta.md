# Parianta Island

## Overview

Parianta is an island in the upper [[Amazon River]] near [[Iquitos]], in present-day Peru.
In *The [[Giant Raft]]*, the [[Jangada]] passes between the islands of [[Iquitos]] and Parianta,
keeping to the right bank of the river [007-006]. Parianta appears again as a
river landmark on the continuing upriver journey; both [[Iquitos]] and Parianta islands
are passed on the right as the raft makes its way toward the Brazilian frontier
[010-005]. The island marks one of the last Peruvian landmarks before the river
enters Brazilian territory.
