# Vila Bela

## Overview

Vila Bela (Vila Bela da Imperatriz) is a Brazilian town on the [[Lower Amazon]],
mentioned in *The [[Giant Raft]]* as the principal market for guaraná in the province
[040-004]. Goods destined for the [[Upper Amazon]] basin passed through Vila Bela,
which served as a commercial waypoint connecting the interior settlements with
the river trade [009-010]. The town appears as the [[Jangada]] nears the end of its
long voyage, marking the transition from the sparsely populated upper river to
the more developed [[Lower Amazon]] region.
