# Coary

## Overview

Coary (modern-day Coari) is a small Brazilian settlement on the middle [[Amazon River]], situated at the entrance to Lake Coary. At the time of the novel's events, the village consisted of about a dozen houses and served as a minor stopping point along the [[Amazon]] waterway.

## In the Novel

The [[Jangada]] stopped at the entrance into Lake Coary during its descent of the [[Amazon]] [016-085]. The travelers came in sight of the village of Coary, which at the time comprised a dozen houses, considerably smaller than many of the other settlements along the river [016-088].
