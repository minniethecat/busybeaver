# Cabello-Cocha

## Overview

Cabello-Cocha is a lake on the right bank of the [[Amazon River]] in the Peruvian section of the waterway. In *The [[Giant Raft]]*, the [[Jangada]] passes this lake as it navigates through a cluster of islands in the [[Upper Amazon]] region, not far downstream from the Peruvian frontier settlements.

## In the Novel

The [[Jangada]] "communicates with the lake of Cabello-Cocha on the right bank" during its passage, putting in near this lakeside area [011-007]. The raft continued "passing between the islands of Cabello-Cocha, Tarapote, and Cacao," navigating through the island-dotted stretch of the [[Upper Amazon]] [011-011]. Many small craft were observed in the vicinity, indicating the area was a populated waterway junction.
