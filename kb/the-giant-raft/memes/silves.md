# Silves

## Overview

Silves is a village situated on the left bank of the [[Amazon River]] in Brazil, located in the [[Lower Amazon]] region. It appears among the succession of riverside settlements encountered during the final stages of the journey down the great river.

## In the Novel

The village of Silves, built on the left bank of the [[Amazon]], was passed by the [[Jangada]] as it navigated the lower reaches of the river toward its destination [040-004].
