# Amazon River

## Overview

Not to be confused with concept page — this is the geographical entity page describing the river as a physical location

## In the Novel

Detailed location information will be enriched in subsequent butler rounds.

## Geography

The [[Amazon River]] and its tributaries define the geography of this location. Characters navigate between settlements by river, with each town and [[Fazenda]] positioned along the waterway.

## Journey Role

This location forms part of the 800-league journey from [[Iquitos]] to Belém aboard the [[Jangada]], as described in Jules Verne's "The [[Giant Raft]]" (1881).

## Historical Context

By 1881, the [[Amazon]] had been explored but remained a frontier of Western geographical knowledge. Verne's detailed descriptions draw on contemporary travel literature and scientific expeditions.
