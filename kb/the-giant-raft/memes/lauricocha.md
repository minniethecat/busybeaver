# Lake Lauricocha

## Overview

Lake Lauricocha is identified in *The [[Giant Raft]]* as the source of the [[Amazon River]]. Situated high in the Peruvian Andes between the eleventh and twelfth degrees of south latitude, this glacial lake feeds the youthful river that will become, over thousands of miles, the greatest waterway on Earth.

## In the Novel

The novel's geographical exposition traces the [[Amazon]] to its origin: "it starts from the Lake of Lauricocha, which is situated between the eleventh and twelfth degree of south latitude" [005-021]. From this high-altitude source, "the youthful river starts towards the northeast" before receiving its first tributaries and growing into the [[Marañón]] [005-023]. The lake represents the ultimate headwater of the entire Amazon system that the [[Jangada]] will navigate thousands of miles downstream.
