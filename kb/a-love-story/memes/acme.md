# Acmé

## Overview

Acmé Frascati is the female lead of *A Love Story* — "the youthful Greek" (009-001) whose love for Sir Henry Delmé and her profound devotion make her the emotional center of the novel. She is introduced in a moment of intimate tenderness, her name whispered as "Acmé, carissima mia!" (008-001). The novel presents her as the embodiment of "woman's first love" (009-004), a devotion so complete that it transforms everyone who witnesses it.

## Biography

Acmé is introduced as a Greek woman living in Malta, where she becomes connected to the Delmé family through George. Her first appearance is rendered with striking intimacy — a whispered endearment, a "silken cheek" (008-001) — immediately establishing her as someone of profound emotional significance. She "presided at the breakfast table" (008-020) with natural grace, and her presence animates every scene she enters.

Her relationship with Sir Henry Delmé unfolds through the middle chapters of the novel. The narrative dwells on "the warm devotion of woman's first love" (009-004) that she embodies, comparing it to "an eastern sun" in its intensity. She is alone with Sir Henry in moments of profound emotional exchange (009-004), and her influence on him is unmistakable.

George Delmé's narrative reveals that he "had met Acmé" (008-022) before Henry, and that his sense of obligation to her — "a wish to act nobly by Acmé" (008-023) — created an internal struggle. This triangular dimension adds complexity to her role in the family.

## Relationships

- **Sir Henry Delmé** — Her beloved, the object of her "first love" and complete devotion (009-004).
- **George Delmé** — Friend and confidant; their connection predates Henry's involvement (008-022).

## Key Scenes

- **First appearance** (008-001): "Acmé, carissima mia!" — the whisper that introduces her to the reader.
- **Presiding at breakfast** (008-020): A scene of domestic grace that reveals her character.
- **Alone with Sir Henry** (009-004): The moment of confessed devotion that defines her emotional role.
- **With George** (008-022–008-023): The triangular tension that complicates the family dynamics.
