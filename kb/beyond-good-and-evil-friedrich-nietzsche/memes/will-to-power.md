# Will to Power

The **Will to Power** (German: *Wille zur Macht*) is a central concept in Nietzsche's philosophy, introduced and developed throughout *Beyond Good and Evil*.

## Overview

Nietzsche proposes that the fundamental driving force in humans and all living beings is not mere survival (as Darwin suggested) or the pursuit of pleasure (as utilitarians argued), but rather the will to power — the drive to expand one's influence, to overcome resistance, and to assert one's strength.
