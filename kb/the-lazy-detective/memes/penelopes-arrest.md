# Penelope's Arrest

The arrest of Penelope Noelson ([[penelope-noelson]]) represents a calculated risk by Labar. He has accumulated evidence of her attempts to bribe him, her involvement with the forged cheque, and her connection to the Gertstein household. Yet the arrest is as much strategic as punitive — it removes her from the influence of Larry Hughes ([[larry-hughes]]) and creates the conditions for her eventual cooperation.

## Strategic Purpose

Labar's goal is not merely to detain Penelope but to turn her. His confrontation catalogs her offenses:

> "She only tried to bribe me yesterday. She only changed a forged cheque on the Midland Bank. She only tried to sandbag me last night." [[005-034]]

Each charge is delivered not as an accusation but as a lever — a reason for Penelope to recognize that her only path forward is through cooperation.
