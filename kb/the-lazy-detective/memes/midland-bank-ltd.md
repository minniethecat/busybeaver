# Midland Bank Ltd

Midland Bank Ltd is the financial institution drawn into the investigation when a cheque bearing Solly Gertstein's forged signature ([[forgery]]) is presented at one of its branches. The bank's cautious manager becomes an obstacle to evidence-gathering:

> "He's a cautious Scot. You see if it is a forgery the bank will be liable." [[004-038]]

The bank's primary concern is not justice but liability — an institutional priority that Labar must work around. The episode illustrates how criminal investigation must navigate the self-protective instincts of the organizations it encounters.
