# Old Bailey

The Old Bailey, London's Central Criminal Court, is the culminating setting of *The Lazy Detective*. It is here that Detective Inspector Labar takes the witness stand and his entire investigation is subjected to the formal procedures of criminal justice:

> Labar took his stand in the witness box for examination at the hands of Treasury Counsel. [[030-073]]

The building carries symbolic weight — centuries of criminal justice, the most serious cases, the highest stakes. For Labar, it is the arena where everything he has discovered must be proved.
