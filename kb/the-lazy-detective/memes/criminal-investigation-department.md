# Criminal Investigation Department

The Criminal Investigation Department (CID) at Scotland Yard ([[scotland-yard]]) is the organizational framework within which Detective Inspector Labar operates. It is the branch of the Metropolitan Police responsible for investigating serious crimes, and its Chief Constable, Winter ([[chief-constable-winter]]), is the man with the power to end Labar's career.

## In the Novel

The CID is not described in administrative detail but is felt as an institutional presence — the source of pressure, resources, and judgment. Labar is "a protégé" of Winter's, which gives him latitude but also scrutiny. The Department's expectations shape every decision he makes in the Streetly House investigation.
