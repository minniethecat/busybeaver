# Midland Bank

The Midland Bank is a crucial node in the investigation of *The Lazy Detective* — the institution where Mrs. Gertstein's ([[mrs-gertstein]]) forged cheque was presented, and where the cautious bank manager's reluctance to release evidence complicates Labar's work:

> "He's a cautious Scot. You see if it is a forgery the bank will be liable." [[004-038]]

The bank's liability for honoring a forged cheque adds a financial dimension to the criminal investigation, and the manager's institutional caution becomes another obstacle Labar must navigate.
