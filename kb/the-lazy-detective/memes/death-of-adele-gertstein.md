The death of Adèle Gertstein is a pivotal event that alters the dynamics of the criminal conspiracy. As the wife of Solly Gertstein and a figure connected to several key characters, her death sends shockwaves through both the criminal underworld and the investigation.

## Adèle Gertstein

Adèle Gertstein reclines with feline luxuriousness in a deck chair on one of the wide terraces of "Maid's Retreat," appearing almost asleep to any casual observer [011-001]. Her position at the heart of the Gertstein household gives her intimate knowledge of her husband's operations — knowledge that becomes dangerous as the investigation closes in.

## The Death

The circumstances of Adèle Gertstein's death represent a critical turning point. It deals a blow to Labar's investigation, disrupting the chain of intelligence he had been building [012-001]. The event also destabilises Solly Gertstein, who, chewing a cold cigar and striding about with rolling gait, cannot conceal how deeply the disaster has affected him [017-001].

## Impact on the Investigation

Adèle's death forces Labar to recalibrate his approach. The information she possessed is lost, and the criminal network reacts to the event by tightening its defences. For the conspirators, her death is both a warning and a catalyst — the stakes have risen, and the circle is closing.
