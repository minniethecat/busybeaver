# Metropolitan Police

The Metropolitan Police Service — "the Met" — is the territorial police force within which the events of *The Lazy Detective* unfold. It is the institution that employs Labar, Malone, Winter, and the entire apparatus of criminal investigation that pursues the Streetly House thieves.

The Met is treated as background rather than subject — a given of the novel's world. Its presence is felt in the chain of command, the bureaucratic procedures, and the institutional culture that shapes how detectives work and how justice is pursued.
