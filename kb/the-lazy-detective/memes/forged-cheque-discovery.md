# Forged Cheque Discovery

The discovery of a forged cheque at the Midland Bank ([[midland-bank]]) is the investigation's turning point — the moment when Labar realizes that the jewel theft is connected to a financial fraud within Gertstein's own household. Mrs. Gertstein ([[mrs-gertstein]]) has forged her husband's signature on a cheque form missing from his book:

> Gertstein had been able to throw no light on the forgery, save that a cheque form was missing from his book. [[009-002]]

The forgery ([[forgery]]) links the world of professional jewel thieves to the domestic secrets of the Gertstein household, and Labar's investigation pivots from the crime scene to the family's hidden financial pressures.
