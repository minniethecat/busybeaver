# Insurance Companies

The insurance companies appear in *The Lazy Detective* only as a reference — a reminder that behind every major theft lies a financial apparatus of risk and compensation. Solly Gertstein ([[solly-gertstein]]) dismisses them with contempt:

> "Insured! What is insurance to me? Do you think that I — Gertstein — want the money? That — poof — a fleabite. The insurance companies will pay, but will that help me to get back all my beautiful things?" [[002-005]]

For Gertstein, the insurance payout is irrelevant — his collection represents years of work, not monetary value. The insurance companies represent the cold calculus of compensation, inadequate to the emotional reality of loss.
