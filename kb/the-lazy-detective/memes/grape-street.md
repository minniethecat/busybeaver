# Grape Street

Grape Street houses the divisional detective force in *The Lazy Detective* — the headquarters from which Labar directs investigations and manages the administrative machinery of police work. It is described with characteristic understatement as "the stiffly furnished room that formed the headquarters of the divisional detective force" [[001-027]].

## Function

Grape Street is where Labar assigns tasks to his subordinates, receives reports, and deals with the "official routine correspondence that was the bane of his life" [[001-028]]. It represents the mundane reality of police work — the paperwork, the telephone calls, the bureaucratic obligations — that the lazy detective must navigate between moments of brilliant insight.
