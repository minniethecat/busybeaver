The arrest of the conspirators is the decisive law enforcement action that brings down Larry Hughes's criminal network. Following the rescue of Sophie Lengholm, the police move swiftly to round up everyone involved in the conspiracy.

## The Round-Up

After the captives are freed from the underground hideout, half a dozen constables, each armed with a shotgun, take the prisoners into custody [027-001]. The retired military officer who heads the Kent County Constabulary personally enters the scene to oversee the arrests [027-011].

## The Manhunt

The immediate question to be solved is whether Larry Hughes is still hiding on the marsh, or whether he has eluded the cordon [028-001]. The police spread out across the Romney Marshes, searching every possible hiding place. The manhunt extends across the territory of three police forces — the Metropolitan Police, the Kent Constabulary, and the Sussex Police [024-022].

## Resolution

The conspirators are taken into custody and transported for processing. The arrests mark the effective end of Hughes's criminal enterprise. Those apprehended face charges ranging from abduction and conspiracy to accessory to forgery and theft. The arrests clear the path for the trial at the Old Bailey [030-001].
