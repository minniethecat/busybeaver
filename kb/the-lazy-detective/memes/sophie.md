# Sophie

Sophie is a supporting figure in *The Lazy Detective* — a servant whose long service and steadfast loyalty place her at the intersection of the household's secrets and the detective's investigation. Labar recognizes her value immediately:

> "We have known each other a long time, Sophie and I." [[010-008]]

Her role is modest but crucial: she knows the rhythms of the household, the relationships between its members, and — perhaps — more than she is willing to say directly. Her presence reminds the reader that the investigation touches everyone, from the financier in his study to the servant in the hallway.
