# Milagrosa

## Overview

The *Milagrosa* (Nuestra Señora de la Milagrosa — "Our Lady of Miracles") is a Spanish vessel captured by Peter Blood during his buccaneering career. As one of Blood's named prizes, the *Milagrosa* represents the wealth and naval dominance that Blood achieves through his operations against Spanish shipping in the Caribbean.

## In the Novel

The *Milagrosa* is referenced as one of Blood's captures — a Spanish ship taken as a prize of war. Its capture contributes to the escalating conflict between Blood and the Spanish authorities, particularly Don Diego de Espinosa and his kinsman Don Miguel.
