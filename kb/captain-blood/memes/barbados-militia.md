# Barbados Militia

## Overview

The Barbados Militia is the colonial military force commanded by Colonel William Bishop, the Deputy Governor of Barbados. As the armed instrument of plantation authority, the Militia represents the physical power that enforces the slave system. Blood's escape from Barbados requires evading the Militia's patrols and outwitting Bishop's military command.

## In the Novel

Colonel Bishop appears as commander of "the Barbados Militia" ((004-139)) — a role that combines military authority with plantation economics. The Militia's function is both defensive (protecting the island from Spanish raids) and repressive (maintaining control over the slave population). Blood's escape humiliates Bishop not just personally but professionally — the Militia commander could not prevent slaves from seizing a Spanish ship under his nose.
