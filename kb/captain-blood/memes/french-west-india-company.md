# French West India Company

## In the Novel

The French West India Company is a French colonial enterprise operating in the Caribbean during the period of *Captain Blood*. It appears through its representative, Monsieur de Cussy, who negotiates with Peter Blood on behalf of French interests.

> "in representing the French West India Company seemed to represent France itself" ([013-083])

De Cussy acts as an intermediary between Blood and the French colonial administration, with the Company's commercial interests often aligning with, or at least not conflicting with, Blood's buccaneering activities. The Company's presence highlights the complex web of colonial powers — English, French, Spanish — competing for influence in the Caribbean, with Blood navigating between them as an outlaw with no fixed national allegiance.
