# Port Royal

## Overview

Port Royal, Jamaica, was the principal English port in the Caribbean and the historical center of English buccaneering in the late 17th century. In the novel, Port Royal represents the seat of legitimate English authority in the Caribbean — and the place where Blood's transformation from outlaw to governor is consummated.

## In the Novel

Port Royal appears primarily at the novel's conclusion, when Blood is appointed Governor of Jamaica. The port, historically notorious for its pirate economy, becomes the symbol of Blood's restoration — the pirate who once preyed on Caribbean shipping now governs from its principal English stronghold.

## Significance

- Seat of English authority in Jamaica
- Site of Blood's governorship
