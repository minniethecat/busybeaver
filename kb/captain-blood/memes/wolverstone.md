# Wolverstone

## Overview

Wolverstone is a member of Peter Blood's crew of escaped slaves and rebels-convict. As one of the named buccaneers who appears regularly in the narrative, Wolverstone represents the collective character of Blood's company: men who have suffered injustice, who fight with desperation born of having nothing to lose, and whose loyalty to Blood is absolute because he has earned it through competence and fair treatment.

## In the Novel

Wolverstone serves as part of Blood's inner circle of crew members, present at councils of war and participating in key actions. His presence contributes to the novel's texture — the sense that Blood commands not a faceless crew but a company of individuals, each with their own history and motivations.
