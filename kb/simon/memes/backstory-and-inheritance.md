# Backstory & Inheritance

Much of the tension in *Simon* is rooted in the past: the Rattar family history, the Cromarty inheritance, unexplained deaths, hidden relationships, and events that occurred years before the novel opens. J. Storer Clouston weaves these backstory threads through the investigation, making the past as important as the present. This page indexes all entries dealing with family history, inheritance disputes, and the backstory that drives the plot.
