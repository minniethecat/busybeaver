# Legal & Procedure

*Simon* is, at its core, a legal mystery — the investigation of Sir Reginald Cromarty's murder drives the plot from the Procurator Fiscal's arrival through to the final revelations. This page indexes all entries touching on legal institutions, investigative procedure, evidence, and the Scottish criminal justice system as portrayed in J. Storer Clouston's 1919 novel.
