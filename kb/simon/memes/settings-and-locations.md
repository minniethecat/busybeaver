# Settings & Locations

The world of *Simon* unfolds across a network of interconnected locations in early-20th-century Scotland: the grand country house of Keldale, the seaside estate of Stanesland, the Kings Arms hotel, and the various rooms, roads, and landscapes that frame J. Storer Clouston's mystery. This page gathers all entries tied to the novel's physical settings.
