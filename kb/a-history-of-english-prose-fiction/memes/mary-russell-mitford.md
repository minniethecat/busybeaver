# Mary Russell Mitford

Mary Russell Mitford (1787–1855) was an [[novel|English novel]]ist best known for her sketches of rural life in "Our Village."

## Life and Works

Miss Mitford was a prolific author whose writings included poems, plays, and prose sketches. Her most enduring work, *Our Village* (1824–1832), consists of a series of literary sketches depicting English country life—a form that would influence later Victorian domestic fiction. (042-010)

Tuckerman notes Mitford alongside other contributors to the English domestic novel tradition, including Mrs. Crowe and Mrs. Marsh. (044-013) Together with [[mrs-alexander|Mrs. Alexander]], Mitford "have written many entertaining and popular novels." (042-010)
