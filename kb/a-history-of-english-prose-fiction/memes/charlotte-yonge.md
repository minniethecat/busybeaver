## Literary Significance

Miss Yonge, author of the "Heir of Redclyffe," added largely to the department of English domestic fiction, alongside [[mrs-henry-wood|Mrs. Henry Wood]], author of "East Lynne," and Mrs. Lynn Linton (042-010). The domestic novel was the most considerable subdivision of the [[novel|English novel]], a department for which women were particularly fitted and in which they were eminently successful (042-008).
