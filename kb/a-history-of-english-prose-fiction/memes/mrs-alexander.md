## Works

Mrs. Alexander, the pseudonym of Annie French Hector, was the author of many popular domestic novels including *The Wooing O't* (1873) and *Her Dearest Foe* (1876). She wrote over forty novels during her career, specializing in stories of English domestic life.

## Reception in Tuckerman

Tuckerman mentions Mrs. Alexander briefly, noting that she and [[Mary Russell Mitford]] "have written many entertaining novels dealing with various phases of English life" (042-010). She is also listed among the contributors to domestic fiction in a footnote cataloguing the English domestic novel tradition (044-013).
