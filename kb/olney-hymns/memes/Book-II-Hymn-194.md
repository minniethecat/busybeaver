[194-001] This is the feast of heav’nly wine,
    And God invites to sup;
The juices of the living vine
    Were press’d to fill the cup.

[194-002] O bless the Saviour, ye that eat,
    With royal dainties fed;
Not heaven affords a costlier treat,
    For Jesus is the bread.

[194-003] The vile, the lost, he calls to them,
    Ye trembling souls, appear!
The righteous in their own esteem
    Have no acceptance here.

[194-004] Approach, ye poor, nor dare refuse
    The banquet spread for you:
Dear Saviour, this is welcome news,
    Then I may venture too.

[194-005] If guilt and sin afford a plea,
    And may obtain a place,
Surely the Lord will welcome me.
    And I shall see his face.
