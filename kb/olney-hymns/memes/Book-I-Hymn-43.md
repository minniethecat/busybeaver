[043-001] Sweet was the time when first I felt
    The Saviour’s pard’ning blood
Applied, to cleanse my soul from guilt,
    And bring me home to God.

[043-002] Soon as the morn the light reveal’d,
    His praises tuned my tongue;
And when the evening shades prevail’d,
    His love was all my song:

[043-003] In vain the tempter spread his wiles,
    The world no more could charm;
I lived upon my Saviour’s smiles,
    And lean’d upon his arm.

[043-004] In pray’r my soul drew near the Lord,
    And saw his glory shine;
And when I read his holy word
    I call’d each promise mine.

[043-005] Then to his saints I often spoke
    Of what his love hath done:
But now my heart is almost broke,
    For all my joys are gone.

[043-006] Now when the evening shade prevails,
    My soul in darkness mourns;
And when the morn the light reveals,
    No light to me returns.

[043-007] My pray’rs are now a chatt’ring noise,
    For Jesus hides his face;
I read, the promise meets my eyes,
    But will not reach my case.

[043-008] Now Satan threatens to prevail,
    And make my soul his prey;
Yet, Lord, thy mercies cannot fail:
    O come without delay!
