[212-001] As the sun’s enliv’ning eye
Shines on every place the same;
So the Lord is always nigh
To the souls that love his name.

[212-002] When they move at duty’s call,
He is with them by the way;
He is ever near them all,
Those who go, and those who stay.

[212-003] From his holy mercy-seat
Nothing can their souls confine;
Still in spirit they may meet,
And in sweet communion join.

[212-004] For a season call’d to part,
Let us then ourselves commend
To the gracious eye and heart
Of our ever-present Friend.

[212-005] Jesus, hear our humble pray’r!
Tender Shepherd of thy sheep,
Let thy mercy and thy care
All our souls in safety keep.

[212-006] In thy strength may we be strong,
Sweeten every cross and pain;
Give us, if we live, ere long,
Here to meet in peace again.

[212-007] Then if thou thy help afford,
Ebenezers shall be rear’d;
And our souls shall praise the Lord,
Who our poor petitions heard.
