[291-001] Honour and happiness unite
To make the Christian’s name a praise;
How fair the scene, how clear the light,
That fill the remnant of his days!

[291-002] A kingly character he bears,
No change his priestly office knows;
Unfading is the crown he wears,
His joys can never reach a close.

[291-003] Adorn’d with glory from on high,
Salvation shines upon his face;
His robe is of th’ethereal dye,
His steps are dignity and grace.

[291-004] Inferior honours he disdains,
Nor stoops to take applause from earth:
The King of kings himself maintains
Th expenses of his heavenly birth.

[291-005] The noblest creature seen below,
Ordain’d to fill a throne above;
God gives him all he can bestow
His kingdom of eternal love!

[291-006] My soul is ravish’d at the thought!
Methinks from earth I see him rise!
Angels congratulate his lot,
And shout him welcome to the skies!
