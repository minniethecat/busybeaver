[033-001] Behold the throne of grace!
    The promise calls me near;
There Jesus shows a smiling face,
    And waits to answer pray’r.

[033-002] That rich atoning blood
    Which sprinkled round I see
Provides for those who come to God
    An all-prevailing plea.

[033-003] My soul, ask what thou wilt﻿—
    Thou canst not be too bold;
Since his own blood for thee he spilt,
    What else can he withhold?

[033-004] Beyond thy utmost wants
    His love and power can bless;
To praying souls he always grants
    More than they can express.

[033-005] Since ’tis the Lord’s command,
    My mouth I open wide;
Lord, open thou thy bounteous hand,
    That I may be supplied.

[033-006] Thine image, Lord, bestow,
    Thy presence and thy love;
I ask to serve thee here below,
    And reign with thee above.

[033-007] Teach me to live by faith,
    Conform my will to thine;
Let me victorious be in death,
    And then in glory shine.

[033-008] If thou these blessings give,
    And wilt my portion be,
Cheerful the world’s poor toys I leave
    To them who know not thee.
