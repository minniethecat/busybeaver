[003-001] Oh for a closer walk with God,
    A calm and heavenly frame,
A light to shine upon the road
    That leads me to the Lamb!

[003-002] Where is the blessedness I knew
    When first I saw the Lord?
Where is the soul-refreshing view
    Of Jesus and his word?

[003-003] What peaceful hours I once enjoy’d!
    How sweet their mem’ry still!
But they have left an aching void
    The world can never fill.

[003-004] Return, O holy Dove! return,
    Sweet messenger of rest;
I hate the sins that made thee mourn,
    And drove thee from my breast.

[003-005] The dearest idol I have known,
    Whate’er that idol be,
Help me to tear it from thy throne,
    And worship only thee.

[003-006] So shall my walk be close with God,
    Calm and serene my frame;
So purer light shall mark the road
    That leads me to the Lamb.
