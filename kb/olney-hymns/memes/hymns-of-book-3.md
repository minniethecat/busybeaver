# Hymns of Book III

Book III of the Olney Hymns contains hymns on the interior life of faith. Newton's subtitle reads: "On the Rise, Progress, Changes, and Comforts of the Spiritual Life." These are the most introspective and experiential hymns in the collection.

## About Book III

Book III is the devotional heart of the Olney Hymns. Its subjects trace
the soul's pilgrimage: conviction of sin, conversion, conflict with
indwelling sin, desire for growth, seasons of darkness and comfort, and
the longing for heaven. Cowper's most famous hymn, "Light Shining Out
of Darkness," appears in this book.
