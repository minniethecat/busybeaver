[148-001] Now, gracious Lord, thine arm reveal,
    And make thy glory known;
Now let us all thy presence feel,
    And soften hearts of stone!

[148-002] Help us to venture near thy throne,
    And plead a Saviour’s name;
For all that we can call our own
    Is vanity and shame.

[148-003] From all the guilt of former sin
    May mercy set us free;
And let the year we now begin
    Begin and end with thee.

[148-004] Send down thy Spirit from above,
    That saints may love thee more;
And sinners now may learn to love
    Who never loved before.

[148-005] And when before thee we appear,
    In our eternal home,
May growing numbers worship here,
    And praise thee in our room.
