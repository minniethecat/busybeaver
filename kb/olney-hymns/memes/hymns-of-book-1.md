# Hymns of Book I

## About Book I

Book I is the only book of the Olney Hymns organized around specific
biblical texts. Each hymn takes a particular passage as its starting
point and develops it devotionally. The scriptural references span
from Genesis to Revelation, though the Old Testament predominates.
