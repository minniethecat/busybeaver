## About This Index

While the Olney Hymns draw more frequently from Old Testament types,
the New Testament events — particularly the Passion and Resurrection —
are the gravitational center toward which all the types point. Hymns on
the crucifixion and resurrection appear across all three books, and the
gospel narratives provide the framework for Newton's evangelical
theology.
