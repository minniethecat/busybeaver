[116-001] “I am,” saith Christ, “your glorious head,”
    (May we attention give,)
“The resurrection of the dead,
    The life of all that live.

[116-002] “By faith in me the soul receives
    New life, though dead before;
And he that in my name believes,
    Shall live to die no more.

[116-003] “The sinner, sleeping in his grave,
    Shall at my voice awake;
And when I once begin to save,
    My work I ne’er forsake.”

[116-004] Fulfill thy promise, gracious Lord,
    On us assembled here;
Put forth thy Spirit with the word,
    And cause the dead to hear.

[116-005] Preserve the power of faith alive
    In those who love thy name;
For sin and Satan daily strive
    To quench the sacred flame.

[116-006] Thy power and mercy first prevail’d
    From death to set us free;
And often since our life had fail’d,
    If not renew’d by thee.

[116-007] To thee we look, to thee we bow,
    To thee for help we call;
Our life and resurrection thou,
    Our hope, our joy, our all.
