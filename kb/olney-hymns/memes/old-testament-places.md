## About This Index

Geography in the Olney Hymns is never merely geographical. Canaan is the
promised land of rest; Egypt is the house of bondage; Zion is the city
of God; Eden is paradise lost and regained. Each place name carries a
spiritual meaning earned through centuries of devotional reading, and
Newton and Cowper deploy this vocabulary with the fluency of lifelong
students of Scripture.
