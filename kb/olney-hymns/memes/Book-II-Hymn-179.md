[179-001] My song shall bless the Lord of all,
My praise shall climb to his abode.
Thee, Saviour, by that name I call﻿—
The great supreme, the mighty God.

[179-002] Without beginning or decline,
Object of faith and not of sense﻿—
Eternal ages saw him shine,
He shines eternal ages hence.

[179-003] As much, when in the manger laid,
Almighty Ruler of the sky,
As when the six days work he made
Fill’d all the morning-stars with joy.

[179-004] Of all the crowns Jehovah bears,
Salvation is his dearest claim:
That gracious sound well pleased he hears,
And owns Emmanuel for his name.

[179-005] A cheerful confidence I feel,
My well-placed hopes with joy I see;
My bosom glows with heav’nly zeal
To worship Him who died for me.

[179-006] As man he pities my complaint,
His power and truth are all divine﻿—
He will not fail, he cannot faint,
Salvation’s sure, and must be mine.
