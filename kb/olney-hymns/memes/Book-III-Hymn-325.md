[325-001] Rejoice, believer, in the Lord,
    Who makes your cause his own;
The hope that’s built upon his word
    Can ne’er be overthrown.

[325-002] Though many foes beset your road,
    And feeble is your arm,
Your life is hid with Christan God,
    Beyond the reach of harm.

[325-003] Weak as you are, you shall not faint,
    Or fainting shall not die;
Jesus, the strength of every saint,
    Will aid you from on high.

[325-004] Though sometimes unperceived by sense,
    Faith sees him always near,
A guide, a glory, a defence;
    Then what have you to fear?

[325-005] As surely as he overcame
    And triumph’d once for you;
So surely you that love his name
    Shall triumph in him too.
