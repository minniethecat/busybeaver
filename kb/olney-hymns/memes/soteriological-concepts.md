## About This Index

No theme is more central to the Olney Hymns than salvation. From
*Amazing Grace* to the closing hymns of Book III, Newton and Cowper
return repeatedly to the doctrines of grace, atonement, repentance,
and the assurance of redemption. This index gathers the key
soteriological concepts into one view.
