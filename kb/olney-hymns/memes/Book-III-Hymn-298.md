[298-001] When Hagar found the bottle spent,
    And wept o’er Ishmael,
A message from the Lord was sent
    To guide her to a well.

[298-002] Should not Elijah’s cake and cruse
    Convince us at this day,
A gracious God will not refuse
    Provisions by the way?

[298-003] His saints and servants shall be fed,
    The promise is secure:
“Bread shall be given them,” as he said,
    “Their water shall be sure.”

[298-004] Repasts far richer they shall prove,
    Than all earth’s dainties are;
’Tis sweet to taste a Saviour’s love,
    Though in the meanest fare.

[298-005] To Jesus then your trouble bring,
    Nor murmur at your lot;
While you are poor, and he is King,
    You shall not be forgot.
