## About This Index

Old Testament history provided Newton and Cowper with a rich vocabulary
of types and shadows. The Flood becomes a picture of salvation through
judgment; the Exodus maps onto the believer's deliverance from sin;
Eden and the Fall frame the human condition that grace addresses. This
index gathers the events that appear most prominently in the hymns.
