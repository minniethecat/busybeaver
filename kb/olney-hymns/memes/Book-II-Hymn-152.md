[152-001] Sin has undone our wretched race.
    But Jesus has restored
And brought the sinner face to face
    With his forgiving Lord.

[152-002] This we repeat, from year to year,
    And press upon our youth;
Lord, give them an attentive ear;
    Lord, save them by thy truth.

[152-003] Blessings upon the rising race!
    Make this a happy hour,
According to thy richest grace
    And thine almighty power.

[152-004] We feel for your unhappy state,
    (May you regard it too!)
And would awhile ourselves forget,
    To pour out prayer for you.

[152-005] We see, though you perceive it not,
    Th’ approaching awful doom;
O tremble at the solemn thought,
    And flee the wrath to come!

[152-006] Dear Saviour, let this new-born year
    Spread an alarm abroad;
And cry in every careless ear
    “Prepare to meet thy God!”
