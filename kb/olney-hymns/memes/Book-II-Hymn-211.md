[211-001] Kindred in Christ, for his dear sake,
A hearty welcome here receive;
May we together now partake
The joys which only he can give!

[211-002] To you and us by grace ’tis given
To know the Saviour’s precious name,
And shortly we shall meet in heaven,
Our hope, our way, our end, the same.

[211-003] May he, by whose kind care we meet,
Send his good Spirit from above,
Make our communications sweet,
And cause our hearts to burn with love!

[211-004] Forgotten be each worldly theme,
When Christians see each other thus:
We only wish to speak of Him
Who lived and died and reigns for us.

[211-005] We’ll talk of all He did and said,
And suffer’d for us here below;
The path He mark’d for us to tread,
And what He’s doing for us now.

[211-006] Thus, as the moments pass away,
We’ll love and wonder and adore,
And hasten on the glorious day
When we shall meet to part no more.
