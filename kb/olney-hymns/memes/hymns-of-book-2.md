# Hymns of Book II

Book II of the Olney Hymns contains hymns on occasional subjects. Newton described them as "Hymns upon occasional Subjects and Dispensations" — covering seasons, ordinances, providences, and the Christian life as it unfolds in time.

## About Book II

Book II moves from the scriptural expositions of Book I to the rhythms
of the Christian year and life. Its hymns address topics like the new
year, prayer meetings, sacraments, sickness, recovery, and thanksgiving
for providences — the "occasional subjects" that mark a believer's
journey through time.
