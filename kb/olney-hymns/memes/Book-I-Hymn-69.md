[069-001] From pole to pole let others roam,
    And search in vain for bliss;
My soul is satisfied at home,
    The Lord my portion is.

[069-002] Jesus, who on his glorious throne,
    Rules heaven and earth and sea,
Is pleased to claim me for his own,
    And give himself to me.

[069-003] His person fixes all my love,
    His blood removes my fear;
And while he pleads for me above,
    His arm preserves me here.

[069-004] His word of promise is my food,
    His Spirit is my guide;
Thus daily is my strength renewed,
    And all my wants supplied.8

[069-005] For him I count as gain each loss,
    Disgrace for him renown;
Well may I glory in his cross,
    While he prepares my crown!

[069-006] Let worldlings then indulge their boast,
    How much they gain or spend;
Their joys must soon give up the ghost,
    But mine shall know no end.
