[260-001] God of my life, to thee I call,
Afflicted at thy feet I fall;
When the great water-floods prevail,
Leave not my trembling heart to fail!

[260-002] Friend of the friendless and the faint,
Where should I lodge my deep complaint?
Where but with thee, whose open door
Invites the helpless and the poor?

[260-003] Did ever mourner plead with thee,
And thou refuse that mourner’s plea?
Does not the word still fix’d remain
That none shall seek thy face in vain?

[260-004] That were a grief I could not bear,
Didst thou not hear and answer pray’r;
But a pray’r-hearing, answ’ring God,
Supports me under every load.

[260-005] Fair is the lot that’s cast for me,
I have an Advocate with thee:
They whom the world caresses most
Have no such privilege to boast.

[260-006] Poor though I am, despised, forgot,
Yet God, my God, forgets me not;
And he is safe and must succeed,
For whom the Lord vouchsafes to plead.
