## Character Overview

[[minnie-may-barry|Minnie May Barry]] is the younger sister of [[diana-barry|Diana Barry]], living [[humor|wit]]h the Barry family at [[orchard-slope|Orchard Slope]]. She is presented as a curious and sometimes precocious child who asks pointed questions and shares observations about the people around her [017-118].

## Role in Story

Minnie May appears in the narrative primarily as a source of childhood candor. She asks [[anne-shirley|Anne]] direct questions about [[love|romance]] [026-135] and passes along gossip overheard from adults [028-056]. Her unguarded remarks sometimes reveal truths that older characters would prefer left unspoken.
