## Character Overview

Miss Stacy is the [[avonlea|Avonlea]] schoolteacher who succeeds Mr. Phillips. She represents a modern, progressive approach to [[teaching|education]], notable for her refusal to use corporal punishment — a fact that [[anne-shirley|Anne Shirley]] cites approvingly [005-044]. She is a valued member of [[avonlea|Avonlea]]'s educated class and forms friendships [[humor|wit]]h the Allans.

## Role in Story

Miss Stacy's influence extends beyond the classroom. She is invited to social gatherings alongside the Allans, reflecting her status in the [[neighbor|community]] [022-069]. At [[anne-shirley|Anne]]'s dinner party, she is among the honored guests [023-068], and her presence at social events underscores the respect she commands [023-076].

Her teaching philosophy aligns [[humor|wit]]h [[anne-shirley|Anne]]'s own values — emphasizing encouragement over punishment and fostering intellectual curiosity rather than rote discipline.
