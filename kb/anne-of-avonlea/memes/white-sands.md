White Sands is situated in the rural landscape of [[prince-edward-island|Prince Edward Island]], part of the network of communities — [[avonlea|Avonlea]], [[carmody|Carmody]], White Sands, [[newbridge|Newbridge]], [[grafton|East Grafton]] — that form the geographic world of *[[anne-shirley|Anne]] of [[avonlea|Avonlea]]*. ## Role in the Story

White Sands serves as a significant setting in the novel, contributing to the richly textured rural world that shapes the characters' experiences and provides the backdrop for [[anne-shirley|Anne Shirley]]'s young adulthood.
