## Character Overview

Fred Wright is a student at [[avonlea|Avonlea]] school who holds the position of vice-president in the [[avonlea-village-improvement-society|Village Improvement Society]] under [[gilbert-blythe|Gilbert Blythe]]'s presidency [007-080]. He is one of the young men in [[anne-shirley|Anne]]'s social circle, participating in [[neighbor|community]] activities and escorting the girls to events.

## Role in Story

Fred Wright appears as a minor figure in [[avonlea|Avonlea]]'s youthful social life. He serves on the Improvement Society committee alongside [[anne-shirley|Anne]] and [[gilbert-blythe|Gilbert]] [007-116]. He escorts [[anne-shirley|Anne]] on social occasions, including walking her part of the way to events [012-010]. His absence from town becomes notable to [[anne-shirley|Anne]], who uses it as an excuse when declining other invitations [012-132].
