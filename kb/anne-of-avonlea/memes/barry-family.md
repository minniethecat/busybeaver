The **Barry family** is one of the most prominent families in [[avonlea|Avonlea]],
residing at [[orchard-slope|Orchard Slope]], the farm adjacent to [[green-gables|Green Gables]].
The Barrys are close friends of [[marilla-cuthbert|Marilla Cuthbert]] and [[anne-shirley|Anne Shirley]],
and their property includes the celebrated [[lake-of-shining-waters|Lake of Shining Waters]] (originally "Barry's pond" [020-006]).

## The Barry Property

Orchard Slope includes the Barry garden with its large willow tree, under which Anne and Diana
spend many afternoons in conversation [022-078]. The property also encompasses the pond that Anne
rechristened the [[lake-of-shining-waters|Lake of Shining Waters]] [020-006].

## Role in the Community

The Barrys are active participants in Avonlea society:

- Diana serves as treasurer of the [[avonlea-village-improvement-society|Avonlea Village Improvement Society]] [007-081]
- The Barry parlor hosts meetings of the Improvement Society [007-107]
- Mr. Barry serves as a reliable neighbor, called upon in emergencies [013-002]
- Miss Josephine Barry's [[charlottetown|Charlottetown]] connections link Avonlea to the wider world [023-079–080]

## Key Events

- **The Well Rescue** [013-003–008]: Mr. Barry assists in hauling Davy out of the well
- **The Willowware Platter Incident** [024-002–062]: Miss Barry's cherished platter is broken; Anne takes responsibility
- **Minnie May's Croup**: Anne's nursing skills save Minnie May, cementing the Barry-Shirley bond
