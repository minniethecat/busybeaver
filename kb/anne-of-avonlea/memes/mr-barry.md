# Mr. Barry

Mr. Barry is the father of [[diana-barry|Diana Barry]] and proprietor of [[orchard-slope|Orchard Slope]], a steady and practical [[avonlea|Avonlea]] farmer who anchors his family [[humor|wit]]h quiet competence [002-023].

## Character Overview

A respected figure in [[avonlea|Avonlea]], Mr. Barry is the head of the Barry household at [[orchard-slope|Orchard Slope]]. Unlike his wife, he is rarely the subject of village gossip — he goes about his farming duties [[humor|wit]]h a quiet reliability that makes him a [[faith|trust]]ed neighbor. His practical [[nature-appreciation|nature]] is evident when "Mr. Barry came back [[humor|wit]]h [[anne-shirley|Anne]], carrying his long well-rope" to rescue [[davy-keith|Davy Keith]] from a misadventure [013-003].

## Role in the Story

Mr. Barry's most significant narrative contribution comes during a crisis involving young [[davy-keith|Davy Keith]], when he is summoned to [[green-gables|Green Gables]] to drag the well [013-006]. His steady presence in an emergency — "Finally Mr. Barry shook his head, [[humor|wit]]h a relieved smile" [013-008] — demonstrates the quiet competence that makes him valued in the [[neighbor|community]]. His judgment carries weight: "Mr. Barry laughed and advised that [[davy-keith|Davy]] be kept away from the well for a time" [013-050].

## Relationships

Mr. Barry is the father of [[diana-barry|Diana Barry]], [[anne-shirley|Anne Shirley]]'s [[kindred-spirits|bosom friend]], and by extension a benevolent presence in [[anne-shirley|Anne]]'s life. His relationship [[humor|wit]]h his wife, Mrs. Barry, forms a stable household at [[orchard-slope|Orchard Slope]]. He is regarded as a reliable neighbor by the Cuthberts, called upon when practical help is needed [013-002].

## Character Analysis

Mr. Barry represents the steady, unremarkable goodness that forms the backbone of [[avonlea|Avonlea]] [[community|society]]. He is not a character of great drama or memorable pronouncements, but his quiet dependability makes him essential. In a novel where the domestic sphere dominates, Mr. Barry exemplifies the capable father — present in crisis, generous [[humor|wit]]h advice, and content to let others occupy the foreground.
