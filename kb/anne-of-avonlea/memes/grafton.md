# Grafton

Grafton is a rural [[neighbor|community]] near [[avonlea|Avonlea]], divided into East Grafton, West Grafton, and Middle Grafton. It serves as the [[belonging|home]] of several significant characters — including [[miss-lavendar-lewis|Miss Lavendar Lewis]] and [[paul-irving|Paul Irving]] — and features prominently in the social and geographical fabric of the novel.

## Description

Grafton is not described as a unified settlement but rather as a dispersed rural district [[humor|wit]]h distinct sections. East Grafton appears as the [[belonging|home]] of [[miss-lavendar-lewis|Miss Lavendar Lewis]] and, briefly, of [[davy-keith|Davy Keith]]'s grandmother. West Grafton lies along wooded roads connecting to [[avonlea|Avonlea]]. Middle Grafton contains the baseline road that [[anne-shirley|Anne]] navigates on her travels. The Grafton River runs through the district, providing a scenic backdrop — "the blue loop of the Grafton River" visible from vantage points along the road. [030-044]

## Location

Grafton is situated near [[avonlea|Avonlea]], accessible by road. East Grafton lies in one direction from [[avonlea|Avonlea]]; West Grafton is reached through the woods from [[anne-shirley|Anne]]'s walking routes. The West Grafton road connects near the Kimball property, and the baseline road runs through Middle Grafton. The distance is manageable on foot or by buggy, making Grafton a frequent destination for [[avonlea|Avonlea]] residents. [029-139] [029-167] [029-168]

## Significance

Grafton plays a key role as the [[belonging|home]] of [[miss-lavendar-lewis|Miss Lavendar Lewis]], one of the novel's most be[[romance|love]]d characters. Her house, [[echo-lodge|Echo Lodge]], is in East Grafton, and her romantic history — being "jilted by her [[romance|love]]r" — is associated [[humor|wit]]h the [[neighbor|community]]. [002-083]

The area also serves as a [[belonging|place]] of origin or connection for several other characters: [[mrs-irving|Paul Irving's grandmother]] lives in East Grafton, and [[davy-keith|Davy]] and [[dora-keith|Dora Keith]] come from a farm on the East Grafton road. The Grafton district thus functions as a secondary center of [[neighbor|community]] life, providing characters whose stories intersect [[humor|wit]]h [[avonlea|Avonlea]]'s. [010-003] [006-038]
