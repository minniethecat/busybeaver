## Colophon

[050-001]*[[said|Saïd]] the [[fisherman|Fisherman]]*

[050-002]was published in 1903 by

[050-003][Marmaduke Pickthall](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Marmaduke_Pickthall).

[050-004]This ebook was produced for

[050-005][Standard Ebooks](https://standardebooks.org/)

[050-006]by

[050-007][Jason Livermore](https://jasonlivermore.wordpress.com/ebooks),

[050-008]and is based on a transcription produced in 2025 by

[050-009]**Hendrik Kaiber** and [Distributed Proofreaders](https://www.pgdp.net/)

[050-010]for

[050-011][Project Gutenberg](https://www.gutenberg.org/ebooks/77078)

[050-012]and on digital scans from the

[050-013][Internet Archive](https://archive.org/details/saidfisherman0000marm).

[050-014]The cover page is adapted from

[050-015]*View of Medinet El-Fayoum*,

[050-016]a painting completed circa 1870 by

[050-017][Jean-Léon Gérôme](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Jean-L%C3%A9on_G%C3%A9r%C3%B4me).

[050-018]The cover and [[effendi|title]] pages feature the

[050-019]**League Spartan** and **Sorts Mill Goudy**

[050-020]typefaces created in 2014 and 2009 by

[050-021][The League of Moveable Type](https://www.theleagueofmoveabletype.com/).

[050-022]This edition was released on

[050-023]**June 30, 2026, 2:48 a.m.**

[050-024]and is based on

[050-025]**revision 4bc8bea**.

[050-026]The first edition of this ebook was released on

[050-027]May 14, 2026, 6:16 p.m.

[050-029][standardebooks.org/ebooks/marmaduke-pickthall/said-the-fisherman](https://standardebooks.org/ebooks/marmaduke-pickthall/said-the-fisherman).
