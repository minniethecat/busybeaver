[173-001]Formerly when the men went to a hunt the mistress of the house would not bathe all the time they were away and when the hunters returned she met them at the front door and washed their feet and welcomed them home. The wife of the _dehri_ used to put a dish of water under her bed at night and if the water turned red like blood they believed that it was a sign that game had been killed.
