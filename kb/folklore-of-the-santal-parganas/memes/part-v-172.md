[172-001]Santals are very much afraid of burial grounds; for dead men become _bongas_ and _bongas_ eat men. If a man meet such a _[[bongas|bonga]]_ in a burial ground it is of little use to fight for the _bonga_ keeps on changing his shape. He may first appear as a man and then change into a [[tigers|leopard]] or a bear or a pig or a cat: very few escape when attacked by such a being.

[172-002]It is said that the spirits of young children become _bhuts_ and those of grown-up people _bongas_ and those of pregnant women _churins_.
