[[arthur|Arthur]] (the patient), [[tom-brown|Tom Brown]] (the anxious friend), [[the-doctor|Dr. Arnold]] (the pastor), [[the-great-school|the school]] doctor

## Significance

This event shaped the moral and social world of [[rugby-school|Rugby School]] as depicted in the novel, contributing to the formation of character that was [[the-great-school|the school]]'s central mission (016-092).
