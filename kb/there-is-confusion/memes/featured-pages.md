## About Featured Pages

Featured and premium designations reflect depth of coverage: comprehensive biographical or thematic treatment, multiple analytical perspectives, rich PN citations, and strong cross-referencing within the wiki. The total is capped at 5% of all entry pages for each tier, per wiki quality guidelines.
