## About This Index

Characters are drawn from the novel's primary and secondary casts, including the Marshall, Bye, and related families, as well as Philadelphia and New York social circles. Quality ratings reflect the depth of each character page: `featured` pages offer comprehensive analysis with multiple perspectives, while `standard` pages provide core biographical and narrative coverage.
