## Front vs. Rear

The front tenement was the street-facing building on a tenement lot, built to the property line. While it had access to street light and air — unlike the [[rear-tenement|rear tenement]], which was cut off from both — the front tenement was still a product of profit-maximizing logic. Rooms at the rear of the front building received no direct light, and the narrow lot dimensions meant that even "good" rooms were cramped and poorly ventilated.
