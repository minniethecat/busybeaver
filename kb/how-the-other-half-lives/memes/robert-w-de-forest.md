## Biography

Robert W. De Forest was a lawyer, philanthropist, and reformer who served as counsel to the [[charity-organization-society|Charity Organization Society]] and chair of the Tenement House Committee. His legal expertise was instrumental in drafting and passing the Tenement House Act of 1901.
