# Spartan Gerousia

The Spartan Gerousia, the council of elders in ancient Sparta that formed part of the mixed constitution Rousseau admired — an aristocratic element balancing the popular assembly and the kings

## Historical Context

Spartan Gerousia was a key institution of the classical republican world that Rousseau studied as a source of political wisdom.

## Role in Rousseau's Theory

Rousseau references Spartan Gerousia in his analysis of political institutions, using it as an example of the institutional forms that government — as distinct from sovereignty — can take. The institution illustrates his broader argument that the forms of political organization must be suited to the character and size of the State.
