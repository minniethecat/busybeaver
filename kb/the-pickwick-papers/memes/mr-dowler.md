## Overview

Mr. Dowler bursts into the narrative on the coach to [[bath|Bath]], a retired military gentleman of emphatic opinions and still more emphatic manner, who travels with a wife he describes as "a fine woman" and a temper that proves to be the most combustible element in the fashionable spa town. (007-039–040) He is the novel's most concentrated study in the comedy of masculine honour: a man who talks continually of blows and vengeance, yet whose courage, when the moment of confrontation finally arrives, melts into meekness with a speed that is almost beautiful to behold. (010-075–092)

## Role in the Novel

Dowler's role is that of the comic foil — the blustering man of action who is, in fact, a man of pure posture. He introduces [[Mr. Pickwick]] to [[bath|Bath]] society through [[Angelo Cyrus Bantam]], Master of the Ceremonies, and thus serves a genuine plot function as social facilitator. (007-068–080) But his principal contribution to the novel is the nocturnal farce of Chapters 7–10: the midnight tangle of rooms at the [[white-hart-hotel|White Hart]] that leads Dowler to believe his wife has been insulted by [[nathaniel-winkle|Winkle]], precipitating a threatened duel that dissolves, with perfect comic timing, into Dowler's declaration that "A blow! Never!" (010-076) Through Dowler, Dickens anatomises the gap between the code of gentlemanly honour and the reality of gentlemanly conduct.

## Relationships

- **[[mrs-dowler|Mrs. Dowler]]** — Dowler's wife, whom he describes with proprietary pride. Their relationship is one of the novel's comic portraits of marriage: Dowler talks of her as a possession, yet it is her shriek that sends him into action. (007-040, 008-080)
- **[[Mr. Pickwick]]** — Dowler takes to [[mr-pickwick|Pickwick]] immediately, recognising a fellow gentleman and arranging his introduction to [[bath|Bath]] society. (007-068)
- **[[Nathaniel Winkle]]** — The unfortunate object of Dowler's wrath. Their near-duel is resolved not by courage but by mutual recognition of shared cowardice. (010-075–092)
- **[[Angelo Cyrus Bantam]]** — Dowler's friend and the Master of Ceremonies at [[bath|Bath]], through whom [[mr-pickwick|Pickwick]] gains entrée to the Assembly Rooms. (007-068)
