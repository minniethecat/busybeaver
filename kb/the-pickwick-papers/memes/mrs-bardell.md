## Overview

Mrs. Martha Bardell is the widowed landlady of [[mr-pickwick|Mr. Pickwick]]'s lodgings in Goswell Street, whose fateful misunderstanding of a conversation about a servant — "My dear Mrs. Bardell, you're a good creature" (006-143) — sets in motion the [[breach-of-promise]] action that dominates the novel's middle chapters. Encouraged by the unscrupulous lawyers [[Dodson and Fogg]], Mrs. Bardell becomes the instrument through which Dickens satirises the [[legal-system]]'s capacity to transform domestic trivialities into ruinous litigation. After the trial, she is herself imprisoned in the [[Fleet Prison]] for non-payment of costs — a twist that exposes the law's indifference to plaintiff and defendant alike.

## Role in the Novel

Mrs. Bardell's role is at once comic and cautionary. As plaintiff in _Bardell v. [[mr-pickwick|Pickwick]]_, she is the unwitting engine of the novel's legal plot: her action, once filed by [[Dodson and Fogg]], cannot be withdrawn, and the damages awarded against [[mr-pickwick|Pickwick]] lead directly to his principled imprisonment. (006-039–225) Yet Dickens never treats her as a villain; she is a "weak and tender" woman (006-062) whose sentimental self-deception is exploited by sharper minds. Her own incarceration in the [[Fleet Prison]] (018-036–045) — imprisoned by the very lawyers who promised her victory — completes the novel's indictment of a [[legal-system]] that consumes everyone it touches.

## Relationships

- **[[Mr. Pickwick]]** — Landlord and tenant; the innocent conversation that Mrs. Bardell misreads as a proposal creates the novel's central conflict. (006-143)
- **[[Dodson and Fogg]]** — The attorneys who encourage her suit and later imprison her for costs. Her fate exposes their predatory practice. (017-119)
- **[[mrs-cluppins|Mrs. Cluppins]]** — Mrs. Bardell's loyal friend and courtroom supporter, who testifies on her behalf. (006-084)
- **[[Serjeant Buzfuz]]** — Her barrister, whose florid rhetoric transforms her domestic disappointments into high drama. (006-060)
