# Winnemac

Winnemac is the fictional Midwestern state that Sinclair Lewis created as the setting for several of his novels, including *Dodsworth*. It contains the city of Zenith and the University of Winnemac, and it represents the regional American identity — prosperous, provincial, self-confident — that the novel's European journey both tests and ultimately complicates.

## Description

Winnemac is defined in the novel through its institutions rather than its landscape: Winnemac University, whose banners hang alongside those of Yale and Harvard at Hurd's London dinner (021-005); the Centaur State Bank, of which Tub Pearson is president; the Zenith Chamber of Commerce, whose four-sheet poster of the Revelation car appears at the same dinner. It is a state of banks and factories and civic organizations — the institutional framework of American prosperity.

Lewis gave Winnemac a consistency across his novels that makes it one of American literature's most fully realized fictional geographies. In *Dodsworth*, it functions as synecdoche: Winnemac is America in miniature, and Sam's relationship to it — pride, defensiveness, eventual detachment — is a measure of his growth.

## Significance in the Novel

Winnemac is what Sam carries with him to Europe. Its values — industry, sincerity, democratic openness — are the standard against which he measures everything he encounters. The novel does not ask Sam to repudiate Winnemac but to see it more clearly: to recognize both its virtues and its limitations, and to understand that its values are not universal.

The state's fictional status is significant. Lewis could have set his novel in Ohio or Illinois, but the invented Winnemac allows him to make it representative — not one particular Midwestern state but the idea of the Midwestern state, the regional identity that is both a strength and a confinement.

## Key Events

**Hurd's London dinner (Chapter XXI)**: The banners of Winnemac University, hung alongside Yale and Harvard in a Soho restaurant, embody the provincial identity that follows Sam to Europe.

## Characters Associated

Sam Dodsworth is Winnemac made flesh — industrious, sincere, emotionally inarticulate. Tub Pearson, as president of the Centaur State Bank and holder of an honorary LL.D. from Winnemac University, is the state's most distinguished citizen.
