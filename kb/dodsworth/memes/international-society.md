# International Society

The floating world of international society — the hotels, steamers, resorts, and salons that form the Dodsworths' social environment in Europe — is the novel's most characteristic social formation. It is a society defined by mobility rather than place, by surface rather than depth, by the performance of cultivation among people who belong nowhere in particular and everywhere temporarily.

## Description

Lewis's international society is rendered through its venues: the Claridge's dining room, the Ultima's promenade deck, the Paris restaurants "reeking with cigarette smoke and expensive perfume and smart scandal" (028-001). It is a world of hotel lobbies and steamer chairs, of people who recognize each other without knowing each other, of conversations that are sophisticated without being substantial.

This society has its own codes and hierarchies, as rigid in their way as those of the English aristocracy. Fran masters them instinctively; Sam, who "didn't want to enter a throne-room" (008-006), observes them with the detachment of a man who has built things and knows what it costs to build them. International society builds nothing; it is a society of consumers, not producers, and Sam — the industrialist who built a company from nothing — is fundamentally alien to it.

## Role in the Novel

International society is the medium through which the Dodsworths move and the force that dissolves their marriage. Its values — surface, performance, the endless comparison of one's own sophistication to others' provincialism — are Fran's values, and the novel makes clear that they are corrosive. Sam's resistance to international society is not American philistinism but moral discrimination: he recognizes that a world organized around appearances cannot sustain genuine intimacy.

The novel's resolution — Sam at the Ercole estate with Edith Cortright — represents an escape from international society into a genuine community. The Ercole expatriates are not tourists; they have made homes. International society offers only hotels.
