# Anti-Semites

## Overview

In [[friedrich-nietzsche|Nietzsche]]'s *[[genealogy|Genealogy]] of Morality*, the **anti-Semites** of his own time appear not as a marginal phenomenon but as a diagnostic specimen of modern [[ressentiment]]. Their hatred of the [[Jews]], far from being a return to [[Master Morality|aristocratic]] values, is itself a symptom of the very [[slave-morality|slave morality]] they imagine themselves to oppose. [[friedrich-nietzsche|Nietzsche]]'s treatment of anti-Semitism is one of the text's most pointed demonstrations of how [[ressentiment]] operates in the modern world.

## Nietzsche's Characterization

### Anti-Semitism as Ressentiment

[[friedrich-nietzsche|Nietzsche]] places anti-Semites in the same category as anarchists — both are movements driven by reactive hatred rather than spontaneous self-affirmation. The anti-Semite does not affirm himself; he negates the [[Jews|Jew]], and this negation is the only content of his "value."

> Among Anarchists and anti-Semites, a hidden flower, as it has ever been, of ressentiment. (027-001)

This is the [[genealogy|genealogical method]] applied to the present. The anti-Semite imagines himself to be a defender of tradition, of nation, of authentic values — but genealogically, he is indistinguishable from the anarchist he despises. Both are creatures of [[ressentiment]].

### Anti-Semites and the "Moral Refuse"

[[friedrich-nietzsche|Nietzsche]]'s most withering characterization groups anti-Semites with what he calls "moral refuse" (*Moral-Abfall*). In the context of Essay 3, he discusses [[eugen-duhring|Eugen Dühring]] — a contemporary anti-Semitic philosopher — as the archetype of the [[ressentiment]]-driven intellectual.

> They are all men of ressentiment, these physiologically botched and worm-eaten creatures, a whole quivering kingdom of underground revenge. (055-002)

The anti-Semite, in [[friedrich-nietzsche|Nietzsche]]'s diagnosis, is not strong but weak — not a master but the most degraded form of slave, whose "[[ideals|ideal]]ism" is merely the rationalization of a [[health|physiological]] incapacity for genuine power.

### The "Speculators in Idealism"

In a late passage, [[friedrich-nietzsche|Nietzsche]] extends his critique to what he calls "speculators in [[ideals|ideal]]ism" — those who traffic in moral indignation as a form of self-aggrandizement. Anti-Semites are the prime example: their moral posturing conceals a deeper emptiness.

> The speculators in idealism, the Anti-Semites, who nowadays roll their eyes in Christian-Aryan-bourgeois-pathos. (067-001)

The phrase "roll their eyes" (*die Augen verdrehen*) captures [[friedrich-nietzsche|Nietzsche]]'s contempt: anti-Semitic "[[ideals|ideal]]ism" is performance, a theatrical display of virtue that masks the absence of any genuine value.

## Role in Moral History

In the genealogical narrative, the anti-Semites occupy a uniquely instructive position: they are the modern inheritors of the very [[slave-morality|slave morality]] they claim to reject. Their hatred of the [[Jews]] is not a return to [[Master Morality|master morality]] but a mutation of [[ressentiment]] — the same psychological mechanism that produced [[Christianity]] now turned against one of its own sources.

This makes the anti-Semites a crucial test case for [[friedrich-nietzsche|Nietzsche]]'s method. If even the enemies of the "Jewish" value system are driven by [[ressentiment]], then [[ressentiment]] is not a specifically Jewish phenomenon — it is a general human possibility that has simply found its most effective historical vehicle in the [[Jews|Jewish]]-[[Christianity|Christian]] tradition.

## Key Passages

> Among Anarchists and anti-Semites, a hidden flower, as it has ever been, of ressentiment — only in a more virulent form. (027-001)

> They are all men of ressentiment, these physiologically botched and worm-eaten creatures, a whole quivering kingdom of underground revenge, inexhaustible and insatiable in its outbursts against the fortunate. (055-002)

> The speculators in idealism, the Anti-Semites, who nowadays roll their eyes in Christian-Aryan-bourgeois-pathos, and by means of an insufferable abuse of the cheapest means of propaganda, try to work up a "national" movement. (067-001)
