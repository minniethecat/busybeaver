#### PEOPLES AND COUNTRIES.

##### Translated by J. M. KENNEDY.

[076-001]The Germans are a dangerous people: they are experts at inventing intoxicants. Gothic, rococo (according to Semper), the historical sense and exoticism, Hegel, Richard Wagner—Leibniz, too (dangerous at the present day)—(they even idealised the serving soul as the virtue of scholars and soldiers, also as the simple mind). The Germans may well be the most composite people on earth.

[076-002]"The people of the Middle," the inventors of porcelain, and of a kind of Chinese breed of Privy Councillor.
