#### PEOPLES AND COUNTRIES.

##### Translated by J. M. KENNEDY.

[080-001]As Frenchmen reflect the politeness and _esprit_ of French society, so do Germans reflect something of the deep, pensive earnestness of their mystics and musicians, and also of their silly childishness. The Italian exhibits a great deal of republican distinction and art, and can show himself to be noble and proud without vanity.
