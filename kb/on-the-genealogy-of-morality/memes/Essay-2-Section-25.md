##### SECOND ESSAY.

#### "GUILT," "BAD CONSCIENCE," AND THE LIKE.

[041-001]But what am I talking of? Enough! Enough? At this juncture I have only one proper course, silence: otherwise tresspass on a domain open alone to one who is younger than I, one stronger, more "_future_ " than I—open alone to _Zarathustra, Zarathustra the godless._

* * *

[041-002][[1]](<8766172926465922021_52319-h-1.htm.html#FNanchor_1_1>) The German is: "_Sittlichkeit der Sitte_." H. B. S.

[041-003][[2]](<8766172926465922021_52319-h-1.htm.html#FNanchor_2_2>) The German world "_schuld_ " means both debt and guilt. Cp. the English "owe" and "ought," by which I occasionally render the double meaning.—H. B. S.

[041-004][[3]](<8766172926465922021_52319-h-1.htm.html#FNanchor_3_3>) German: "_Verbrecher_."—H.B.S.

[041-005][[4]](<8766172926465922021_52319-h-1.htm.html#FNanchor_4_4>) An allusion to _Der Zweck im Recht_ , by the great German jurist, Professor Ihering.
