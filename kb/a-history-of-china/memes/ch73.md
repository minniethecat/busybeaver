[073-011] the civil war, see L.M. Chassin, _The Communist Conquest of China: A

[073-012] History of the Civil War, 1945-1949_, Cambridge, Mass., 1965. For brief

[073-013] information on communist society, see Franz Schurmann and Orville

[073-014] Schell, _The China Reader_, vol. 3, _Communist China_, New York 1967.

[073-016] Organization in Communist China_, Berkeley 1966. For cultural and

[073-017] political problems, see Ho Ping-ti, _China in Crisis_, vol. 1, _China's

[073-018] Heritage and the Communist Political System_, Chicago 1968. For a

[073-020] _Report from a Chinese Village_, New York 1966; for Taiwanese village

[073-022] Change_, Berkeley 1966.
