# Index of Historical Eras

Human history unfolds across vast epochs, each defined by the rise and fall of distinct species and civilizations. This index catalogues the major eras that structure Stapledon's two-billion-year chronicle.

## About This Index

Eras are ordered alphabetically by name. Each era page covers the defining events, dominant species, and cultural achievements of its period. For a chronological sequence, see the [[first-men|First Men]] through [[eighteenth-men|Eighteenth Men]] species pages.
