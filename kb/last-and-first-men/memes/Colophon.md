[017-002]*Last and [[first-men|First Men]]*

[017-003]was published in 1930 by

[017-004][[[olaf-stapledon|Olaf Stapledon]]](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Olaf_Stapledon).

[017-005]This ebook was produced for

[017-006][Standard Ebooks](https://standardebooks.org/)

[017-007]by

[017-008][David Grigg](https://thegriggs.org/david/),

[017-009]and is based on a transcription produced by **An Anonymous Volunteer** for

[017-010][Faded Page](https://www.fadedpage.com/showbook.php?pid=20200531)

[017-011]and on digital scans from the

[017-012][Internet Archive](https://archive.org/details/bwb_ks-[384-312].

[017-013]The cover page is adapted from

[017-014]*The Man of the Sea*,

[017-015]a painting completed in 1927 by

[017-016][René Magritte](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/René_Magritte).

[017-017]The cover and title pages feature the

[017-018]**League Spartan** and **Sorts Mill Goudy**

[017-019]typefaces created in 2014 and 2009 by

[017-020][[[league-of-nations|The League]] of Moveable Type](https://www.theleagueofmoveabletype.com/).

[017-021]This edition was released on

[017-022]**June 30, 2026, 2:48 a.m.**

[017-023]and is based on

[017-024]**revision 91e1d77**.

[017-025]The first edition of this ebook was released on

[017-026]March 31, 2026, 5:26 p.m.
