# Index of Organizations

Across the long human story, organizations rose and fell — world states, scientific orders, religious bodies, financial councils. These institutions shaped the collective life of each human species, embodying both the highest aspirations and the deepest flaws of their age. This index catalogues every named organization in the chronicle.

Organizations are listed alphabetically. Each entry links to a detailed page describing the institution's founding, structure, historical role, and ultimate fate.
