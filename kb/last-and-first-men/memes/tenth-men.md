# Tenth Men

The Tenth Men were the first fully human species to emerge on [[neptune-place|Neptune]] — descended from rabbit-like creatures, they developed an upright gait, tool-making hands, and a new brain structure that overlaid the ancient human cerebrum. They spread across the northern continents for more than a million years (013-Neptune).

## Biology

The Tenth Men evolved from rabbit-like ancestors, adopting an almost upright gait and growing in stature and brain size. Crucially, their new brain was not a mere revival of the atrophied human cerebrum but a novel organ that overlaid the ancient structure (013-Neptune). They were long-armed and hairless, well-adapted to the cold Neptunian environment.

## Culture

The Tenth Men crafted bone implements and built wicker villages across the northern continents. They held powwows and developed a social life organized around communal gatherings (013-Neptune). Their culture remained stable for millions of years with little further advancement.

## Historical Role

### Origin

The Tenth Men evolved on [[neptune-place|Neptune]] from rabbit-like organisms that, through manipulation and environmental necessity, developed versatility of behavior, suppleness of mind, and a truly human cognitive apparatus (013-Neptune).

### Fate

After a million years of occupation, the Tenth Men were destroyed by a microorganism. From their ruins several primitive human species emerged, leading eventually to the next stages of Neptunian evolution (013-Neptune).
