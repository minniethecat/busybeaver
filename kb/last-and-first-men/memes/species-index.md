# Index of Human Species

The eighteen human species chronicled in Olaf Stapledon's visionary history span two billion years of evolution across multiple worlds. Each species represents a distinct biological and psychological type, shaped by the environments of Earth, Venus, Neptune, and beyond.

## About This Index

The species are listed in evolutionary sequence, from the [[first-men|First Men]] (contemporary humanity) through the [[eighteenth-men|Eighteenth Men]], the final and most advanced form of humanity. Each entry links to a detailed page covering that species' biology, psychology, civilization, and ultimate fate.
