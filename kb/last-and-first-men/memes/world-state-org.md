# World State Government

The World State Government was the administrative and political apparatus of the [[first-world-state|First World State]] — the first institution in human history to govern the entire planet. It represented the culmination of the [[first-men|First Men]]'s long struggle toward unity, achieved after centuries of war, diplomacy, and ideological transformation.

## Foundation & Structure

The World State was established in the aftermath of the [[european-war|European War]] and the [[russo-german-war|Russo-German War]] — [[war|conflict]]s that had demonstrated the intolerable cost of national division in an age of industrial [[war|warfare]] [001-008]. The government was presided over by a [[president-of-the-world|World President]] who exercised executive authority over the entire planet. The Council of national representatives served as a deliberative body, though real power increasingly concentrated in the administrative apparatus that managed the global energy system.

## Functions & Powers

The World State Government's most critical function was the management of the planetary energy supply. The power needed to keep civilization running was drawn from the burning of Antarctica's vast coal deposits, and the government maintained exclusive control over this resource [004-025]. This energy monopoly gave the government effective control over all aspects of civilization: industry, transportation, communication, and food production. The operations of civilization had become so intricately steered by the fourth millennium of the World State that any disruption to central administration threatened total collapse [004-039].

## Science & Religion

The Government maintained itself as a stronghold of both science and religion [004-015]. It established the formal union of science and religion that became the ideological foundation of the state, registering the unity of all previous religions and subordinating them to the worship of [[gordelpus|Gordelpus]], the Prime Mover [004-017]. The government's fusion of cosmic physics with state worship was one of the most sophisticated systems of social control ever devised by the [[first-men|First Men]].

## Decline

The World State Government proved unable to manage the fundamental contradiction of its civilization: the dependence of a global technological society on a finite energy resource. When the Antarctic fuel deposits failed, the intricate machinery of world-civilization seized up, and the government that had seemed eternal collapsed within a generation. The downfall of the World State Government and the downfall of [[gordelpus|Gordelpus]] were symbolically united in the ruined monuments that haunted the subsequent Dark Age [005-006].
