# Fifth Men

The Fifth Men were the apex of human achievement on [[earth-place|Earth]]. Combining telepathy with lifespans of thousands of years, they developed a culture of unparalleled artistic and philosophical depth. Their "extremely subtle brain-structure" enabled each individual to form intimate connections across the entire species [011-043].

## Biology

The Fifth Men possessed the most refined brain structure of any terrestrial human species. Combined with their extreme longevity, this enabled [[telepathy|telepathic]] communication on "even the highest planes of experience" [011-044].


## Culture

Art was central to Fifth Men civilization. "Since art seemed to the Fifth Men to be in some sense basic to the cosmos, they were naturally very much preoccupied with artistic creation." Their society was "fashioned in relation to its best culture" [011-045].


## Fate

The Fifth Men ultimately migrated to [[venus-place|Venus]] to escape planetary catastrophe on [[earth-place|Earth]]. On [[venus-place|Venus]], environmental stress caused them to lose their [[telepathy|telepathic]] faculty and decline into barbarism [013-008].
