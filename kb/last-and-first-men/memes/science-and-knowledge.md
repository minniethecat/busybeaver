# Science and Knowledge

Each human species in *Last and First Men* attained unique scientific
understandings — the First Men mastered electricity and atomic energy,
the Great Brains achieved near-omniscient observational capacity, and
the Last Men developed a cosmic philosophy blending science with
aesthetic contemplation. This page indexes the scientific and technological
concepts that appear across the narrative.
