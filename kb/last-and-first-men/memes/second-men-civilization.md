# Second Men Civilization

The **Second Men Civilization** arose from the ruins of the [[First World State]], after the [[First Dark Age]] had extinguished the achievements of the [[first-men|First Men]] [006-001]. The [[second-men|Second Men]] emerged as a biologically improved species, with superior tissue repair, delayed maturity (fifty years to adulthood), and lifespans extending to nearly two hundred years [007-003].

## Overview

What distinguished the Second Men Civilization was its foundation in innate cosmopolitanism. Unlike the [[first-men|First Men]], who required arduous schooling to achieve social cohesion, the Second Men possessed a natural aptitude for world-community [007-004]. Their most serious loyalty was instinctively directed toward the race as a whole, and wars — when they occurred — degenerated into athletic contests followed by fraternization [007-005].

## Political Structure

The civilization was organized as a loose confederation of tribes and nations rather than a unified world state. Governance was minimal, relying on the species' natural altruism and capacity for dispassionate cognition [007-007]. Individuals were motivated more by social loyalty than private ambition, which slowed material progress but ensured enduring peace [007-006].

## Culture

The Second Men possessed a remarkable innate interest in science, mathematics, and philosophy. Children delighted in mathematical formulae and philosophical puzzles as naturally as earlier species enjoyed physical games [007-006]. Their religion centered on love and admiration of fate, though this created tension between compassionate action and fatalistic acceptance [007-008].

## Decline

The civilization's very excellence contained its flaw: lacking the selfish drive that propelled the First Men forward, the Second Men preferred peace to enterprise. Progress toward full self-conscious mastery of the planet was curiously slow, and eventually the species fell into a racial trance lasting thirty million years, from which the [[third-men|Third Men]] would later emerge [010-003].

## Legacy

The Second Men Civilization demonstrated that a species could be "natural Christians" — loving their neighbors without reservation [007-008]. Their biological and psychological improvements became the foundation for all subsequent human species.
