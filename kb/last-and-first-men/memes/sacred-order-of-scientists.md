# Sacred Order of Scientists

The **Sacred Order of Scientists** (often abbreviated as **S.O.S.**) was the elite religious-scientific body of the [[first-world-state|First World State]], formed by the merger of the [[international-college-of-science|International College of Science]] with the unified world religions. The Order was tasked with safeguarding the alleged "supreme secret" of [[gordelpus|Gordelpus]] — the key to subatomic energy — and with conducting research into the most scientific manner of divine worship.

## Foundation

The Order was established approximately two centuries after the formation of the First World State, during the great conference of science and religion called by the [[president-of-the-world|President of the World]]:

> "He, therefore, called upon the International College of Science to nominate from amongst themselves a select body, which should be sanctified by the Church, and called the Sacred Order of Scientists. These custodians of the supreme secret were to be kept at public expense. They were to devote themselves wholly to the service of science, and in particular to research into the most scientific manner of worshipping the Divine Gordelpus." [004-017]

The conference unified [[buddhism|Buddhism]], Mohammedanism, Hinduism, the [[regenerate-christian-brotherhood|Regenerate Christian Brotherhood]], and the Modern Catholic Church under the shared worship of Divine Energy. The [[energists-sect|Energists]] sect had prepared the ideological ground by preaching the sanctity of action and movement.

## Role and Privileges

Members of the S.O.S. were maintained at public expense and devoted themselves exclusively to science and the worship of Gordelpus. They held a uniquely privileged position:

> "In the hierarchy of industry three occupations were honoured almost as much as the Sacred Order of Scientists, namely flying, dancing, and athletics." [004-040]

The Order's primary work was the mathematical description of all ritual movements, industrial machinery, and natural phenomena — records filed in sacred archives as a means of passing the evanescent into the eternal being of Gordelpus.

## The Gordelpus Deception

Despite their exalted status, the S.O.S. did not actually possess the supreme secret of subatomic energy. The "mystery of Gordelpus" had been discovered by a Chinese physicist centuries earlier but subsequently lost. The Order maintained the fiction of their knowledge to preserve their authority:

> "All knew, said the President, that some of the scientists were in possession of the supreme secret, though, wisely, they would not definitely admit it." [004-017]

When the energy crisis became acute and the Order could not deliver, their deception unraveled catastrophically. The revelation that "the whole hoary legend of the divine secret was a lie" triggered global uprisings, civil wars, and ultimately the [[fall-of-the-first-world-state|fall of the First World State]].
