# Index of Civilizations

Across two billion years, humanity built successive civilizations — world communities, empires, and planetary cultures — each reflecting the biology and psychology of its dominant species. This index gathers them all.

## About This Index

Civilizations are listed alphabetically. Each entry details the political structures, cultural achievements, and ultimate fate of that world community. For the broader historical context, see the [[era-index|Index of Historical Eras]].
