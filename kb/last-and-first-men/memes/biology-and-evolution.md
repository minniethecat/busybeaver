# Biology and Evolution

The central arc of *Last and First Men* is biological: eighteen distinct
human species arising across two billion years, each shaped by planetary
conditions and selective pressures. This page indexes biological and
evolutionary concepts, alongside the species themselves, to orient the
reader within Stapledon's vast phylogenetic vision.
