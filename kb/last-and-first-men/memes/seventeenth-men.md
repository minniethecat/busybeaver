# Seventeenth Men

The Seventeenth Men were an engineered human species on [[neptune-place|Neptune]], created by the [[sixteenth-men|Sixteenth Men]] who believed they had achieved their hearts' desire. These beings were outwardly glorious but inwardly tortured by subtle imperfections beyond their makers' understanding (016-Neptune).

## Biology

The Seventeenth Men were biologically engineered by their predecessors. Their bodies were designed for perfection, but they carried hidden flaws that would ultimately compel them to seek their own replacement.

## Culture

The Seventeenth Men attained full cultural stature rapidly. Their civilization was crowded with both splendour and agony — achievements of great beauty alongside profound suffering arising from their intrinsic imperfections (016-Neptune).

## Historical Role

### Origin

The Seventeenth Men were deliberately created by the [[sixteenth-men|Sixteenth Men]] after a hundred million years of effort, intended as the perfected human form (016-Neptune).

### Fate

Recognizing their imperfections, the Seventeenth Men bent all their strength to producing a new type — essentially like their own but perfected. After a brief career of a few hundred thousand years, they gave place to the [[eighteenth-men|Eighteenth Men]], the Last human species (016-Neptune).
