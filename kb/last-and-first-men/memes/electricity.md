## Overview

Electricity is a foundational energy technology that evolves across the ages in "Last and First Men." The First Men's civilization depended on electrical power for everything from transportation to communication, while later species developed far more sophisticated energy systems that rendered First Men technology primitive by comparison.

## First Men's Electrical Infrastructure

The First Men built their global civilization on electricity. They learned "to drive their aeroplanes by electricity generated from coal" [004-025], making electrical power the backbone of transportation. However, this dependence on coal created vulnerabilities — as coal reserves depleted, the energy foundation of their civilization began to crumble.

The transition away from coal marked a turning point. Wind and water power were "converted into electricity to supplement human and animal labor" [005-037], representing a renewable energy revolution that the Second Men accelerated. "Vast mining operations were undertaken" [005-037] in the Antarctic, where the Second Men secretly developed their superior energy technology.

## Martian Electrical Warfare

The Martians weaponized electricity in ways the First Men never imagined. During the Martian invasion, they "charged the Martian clouds with high-tension electricity in the form of artificial lightning" [009-008], using electrical discharges as a weapon of mass destruction against human populations. This represented a terrifying application of electrical science far beyond human capability.

## Later Energy Systems

Subsequent human species moved beyond the First Men's crude electrical systems. The Great Brains and later species developed energy technologies based on subatomic processes and biological systems rather than electromechanical generation. Electricity remained important but became one component of a vastly more sophisticated energy ecology.
