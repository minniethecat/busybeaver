# Index of Events

The chronicle of humanity is punctuated by pivotal events: devastating wars, cosmic catastrophes, epochal discoveries, and transformative transitions. This index catalogues the major events that redirected the course of human history, from the First Men's world wars to the final transcendence of the Eighteenth Men.

## About This Index

Events are listed alphabetically. Each entry links to a detailed page describing the event's context, participants, consequences, and place in the larger narrative. For the eras in which these events occurred, see the [[era-index|Index of Historical Eras]]; for the species and civilizations involved, see the [[species-index|Index of Human Species]] and the [[civilization-index|Index of Civilizations]].
