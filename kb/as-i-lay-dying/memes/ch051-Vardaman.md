# Vardaman

## Vardaman

[047-001][[darl-bundren|Darl]] and [[jewel-bundren|Jewel]] and Dewey Dell and I are walking up the hill behind the wagon. Jewel came back. He came up the [[the-journey|road]] and got into the wagon. He was walking. Jewel hasn’t got a horse any more. Jewel is my brother. [[cash-bundren|Cash]] is my brother. Cash has a broken leg. We fixed Cash’s leg so it doesn’t hurt. Cash is my brother. Jewel is my brother too, but he hasn’t got a broken leg.

[047-002]Now there are five of them, tall in little tall black circles.

[047-003]“Where do they stay at night, Darl?” I say. “When we stop at night in the barn, where do they stay?”

[047-004]The hill goes off into the sky. Then the sun comes up from behind the hill and the mules and the wagon and [[anse-bundren|pa]] walk on the sun. You cannot watch them, walking slow on the sun. In [[jefferson|Jefferson]] it is red on the track behind the glass. The track goes shining round and round. Dewey Dell says so.

[047-005]Tonight I am going to see where they stay while we are in the barn.
