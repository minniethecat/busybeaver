# Places

This page indexes all significant locations in *As I Lay Dying*. The novel traces the Bundren family's journey across Yoknapatawpha County — from their farm on a rural hill, across a flooded river, through the town of Mottson, to the county seat of Jefferson. Each place carries both literal geography and symbolic weight.
