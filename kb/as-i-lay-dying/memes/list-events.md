# Events

This page indexes all significant events in *As I Lay Dying*. The narrative follows a simple plot — a family transporting their mother's body to Jefferson for burial — but Faulkner fractures this journey across fifty-nine chapters and fifteen narrators, transforming each event into a kaleidoscope of private experience.

## Notes

Event entries capture both plot-level occurrences (deaths, river crossings, fires) and their subjective refraction through individual consciousness. The same event often appears described by multiple narrators across different chapters.
