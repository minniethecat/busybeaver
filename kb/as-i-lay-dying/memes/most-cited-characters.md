# Most Cited Characters

This page ranks all characters in *As I Lay Dying* by their total reference count across the wiki — a proxy for narrative prominence in Faulkner's multi-narrator novel. Characters who appear across many chapters and through multiple narrators' perspectives accumulate more citations.

::: query

type: person

sort: total_refs desc

display: table

fields: [label, quality, total_refs, description]

:::

## Reading the Rankings

Reference counts reflect both diegetic presence (how often a character physically appears) and narrative presence (how often they are thought about, spoken of, or perceived by other characters). In Faulkner's polyphonic structure, a character like Addie Bundren — dead for most of the novel — may accumulate high reference counts through the memories and perceptions of her family.
