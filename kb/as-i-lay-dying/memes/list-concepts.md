# Concepts & Themes

This page indexes all thematic concepts identified across *As I Lay Dying*. The novel's stream-of-consciousness technique embeds its themes in the texture of perception itself — death, identity, language, and duty emerge not through authorial statement but through the accumulated weight of fifteen narrators' private consciousnesses.
