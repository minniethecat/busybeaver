The Queen's Stone is a covert meeting point and message drop located at Chitor, used by operatives of [[the-great-game|the Great Game]]. The spy [[e23|E23]] buried a sensitive document there: "I buried it under the Queen's Stone, at Chitor, in the place known to us." (011-145) The location is also invoked as a secret oath—E23 curses a policeman "by the curse of the Queen's Stone, by the writing under the Queen's Stone." (012-023) The Stone represents the clandestine infrastructure of British intelligence in India: hidden in plain sight, known only to initiates.

E23 reveals the location of the hidden document:

> "I buried it under the Queen's Stone, at Chitor, in the place known to us all."
>
> (011-145)
