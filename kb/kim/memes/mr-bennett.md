# Mr. Bennett

**Mr. Bennett** is a character in Rudyard Kipling's *[[kimball-ohara|Kim]]*.

Descriptive content for mr-bennett generated during Phase 9-B r3 pilot batch.
PN citations: (007-033), (012-135), (011-055).

Bennett brings the found boy to Father Victor:

> "I want your advice, Father Victor," said Bennett. "I found this boy in the dark outside the Mess-tent. Ordinarily, I should have chastised him and let him go, because I believe him to be a thief. But it seems he talks English, and he attaches some sort of value to a charm round his neck."
>
> (005-073)
