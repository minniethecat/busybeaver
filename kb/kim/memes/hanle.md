Hanlé is a settlement on the Indus River route through Ladakh, in the high Himalayas. In *Kim*, Hurree Babu mentions it while discussing the movements of the Russian and French agents: "...and down the Indus to Hanlé (I know that road), and then, I think, they will make a big rush." (013-024)

## On the Intelligence Route

The Babu's familiarity with Hanlé — "I know that road" — underscores his deep knowledge of Himalayan geography, a professional asset that makes him invaluable to the British intelligence network. The route through Hanlé represents one thread in the web of paths and passes that the Great Game's players must master. (013-024)
