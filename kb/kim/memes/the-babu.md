# The Babu

**The Babu** is a minor character in Rudyard Kipling's *[[kimball-ohara|Kim]]*, representing the class of English-educated Bengali clerks who served as intermediaries in the British Indian administration.

His presence in the novel reflects the colonial system's reliance on Indian intermediaries. PN citations: (007-033), (012-135), (011-055).
