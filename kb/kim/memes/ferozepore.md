## Overview

Ferozepore was a town in the Punjab region of British India, situated on the railway line where Kimball O'Hara Sr., Kim's father, worked after leaving the Mavericks regiment. It holds a sombre place in Kim's family history. (001-003)(001-004)

## Role in Kim's Story

After Kimball O'Hara Sr. left the Mavericks and took a civilian post on the Sind, Punjab, and Delhi Railway, the family moved to Ferozepore. It was here that Kim's mother, an Irish nursemaid who had married O'Hara, died of cholera. Following her death, O'Hara "fell to drink and loafing up and down the line with the keen-eyed three-year-old baby." He later became gang-foreman on the Ferozepore line, and in his opium-hours would weep bitterly in a broken rush chair on the veranda, speaking of the grand future he believed awaited his son. (001-003)(001-004)

The tragedy that set Kim's father adrift:
