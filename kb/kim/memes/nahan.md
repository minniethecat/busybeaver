# Nahan

**Nahan** is a princely state in the Himalayan foothills, encountered by [[kimball-ohara|Kim]] and [[teshoo-lama|the lama]] near the end of their journey. The Rajah of the state briefly appears in the narrative.

## In the Narrative

Kim and the lama enter the borders of Nahan during their final journey through the hills. (015-004)

The Rajah of Nahan mistakes the party for Europeans. (015-096)
