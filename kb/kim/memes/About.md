# About This Wiki

## Source Material

This wiki is built from **[[kimball-ohara|Kim]]** (1901), the novel by Rudyard Kipling. Set in British India, *[[kimball-ohara|Kim]]* follows the adventures of [[kimball-ohara|Kimball O'Hara]], an orphaned Irish boy who becomes embroiled in the [[the-great-game|Great Game]] — the political conflict between Britain and Russia in Central Asia — while also accompanying a [[tibet|Tibet]]an [[teshoo-lama|lama]] on a spiritual [[pilgrimage]].

The novel is in the public domain.

## Usage Notes

- **Chapter pages** present the original text segmented by paragraph, with PN (paragraph number) citations in `[NNN-PPP]` format.

- **Entity pages** (people, places, concepts, events, organizations) summarize key information and link back to source chapters via PN citations.

- Use the search bar to find entries by name or keyword. Full-text search is also available.
