# The Old Soldier

**The Old Soldier** is a character in Rudyard Kipling's *[[kimball-ohara|Kim]]*.

Descriptive content for the-old-soldier generated during Phase 9-B r3 pilot batch.
PN citations: (007-033), (012-135), (011-055).

The old soldier demands proof of Kim's claims:

> "A sign. Give me a sign," thundered the old soldier suddenly. "If there were war my sons would have told me."
>
> (003-075)
