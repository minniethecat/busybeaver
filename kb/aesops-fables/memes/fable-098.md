## THE BEAR AND THE FOX

::: image pn=098-001
![THE BEAR AND THE FOX](images/2977816755627561192_108t.jpg)
:::

[098-002]A Bear was once bragging about his generous feelings, and saying how refined he was compared with other animals. (There is, in fact, a tradition that a Bear will never touch a dead body.) A Fox, who heard him talking in this strain, smiled and said, "My friend, when you are hungry, I only wish you _would_ confine your attention to the dead and leave the living alone."

[098-003]A hypocrite deceives no one but himself.
