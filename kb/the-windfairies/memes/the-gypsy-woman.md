## Overview

**This page is a spelling variant (alias) for [[the-gipsy-woman]].** The Gipsy Woman is the mother of the Gipsy in "The Gipsy's Cup," a poor widow who raised her son and daughter with wisdom, teaching them kindness to animals and the power of helping others. She is healed by the magical cup at the story's climax.

Please see [[the-gipsy-woman]] for the full page.
