# The Raven

The Raven is a supernatural guide in the latter half of "Dumb Othmar" (Chapter 7), a "big black raven" who rises from the ground and leads Hulda on her quest to find the witch who can restore Othmar's voice. The raven is a creature of ancient knowledge — the old man in the woods confirms it is a "fairy" in bird form (007-048).

## Nature

The Raven is more than a bird — it is a fairy or spirit in avian form. It "rose from the ground" with a croak (007-058) and leads Hulda unerringly toward her destination, finding food and shelter for her along the way. An old man tells Hulda: "I am sure that the old man was a fairy, and if so, no living man can harm him" — but it is the raven, not the old man, that he is speaking of (007-048). The raven thus shares the ambiguous supernatural nature common to the dwarf and other beings in this story.

## Appearance

The Raven is "big" and "black," casting "a long shadow" (007-025). It is described with the gravity associated with ravens in folklore — bird of omen, bird of knowledge, death's attendant — and its croak punctuates critical moments in Hulda's journey.

## Powers & Abilities

- **Guidance** — The raven flies ahead of Hulda, showing her the way and preventing her from getting lost (007-062, 007-063)
- **Provision** — It finds food for Hulda: "the raven had found a long worm" (007-065)
- **Croaking** — Its voice communicates urgency and direction, though it does not speak human language directly. Hulda instinctively understands its meaning (007-064)
- **Flight** — As a bird-spirit, it navigates the landscape with ease, transforming the wilderness from an obstacle into a path

## Role in the Story

The Raven appears after Othmar returns mute from the underground kingdom. When Hulda resolves to find help, the raven becomes her guide. It leads her through dangerous wilderness to the dwelling of the witch who — the story implies — may hold the key to reversing the dwarf's curse. The raven is the counter-force to the dwarf: where the dwarf takes away voice and leads into darkness underground, the raven guides toward restoration and leads through the open air.
