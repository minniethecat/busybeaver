# Concepts by Theme

This page presents all abstract concepts and moral themes that recur across
the tales of *The Windfairies*. Concepts are sorted by how frequently they
are referenced by other pages, surfacing the thematic backbone of the collection.

Themes such as faith, beauty, cruelty, and truth-telling weave through multiple
stories, connecting characters and events across chapters.

## All Concepts

::: query
type: concept
display: table
fields: [label, quality, total_refs]
field_labels:
  label: Concept
  quality: Quality
  total_refs: Citations
sort: total_refs
order: desc
title: Concepts by Citation Count
:::
