# List of Creatures

This page provides a complete, dynamically generated index of all supernatural beings
who inhabit the world of *The Windfairies*. From the windfairies themselves to gnomes,
talking trees, rain maidens, and other elemental spirits, each entry links to a dedicated page
describing the creature's nature, realm, powers, and story appearances.

The index is sorted alphabetically by name.
