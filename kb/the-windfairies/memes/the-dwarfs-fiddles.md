# The Dwarf's Fiddles

The Dwarf's Fiddles are the enchanted musical instruments in "Dumb Othmar," the tools through which the dwarf musician attracts and tests human musicians. They are symbols of supernatural artistry — beautiful, seductive, and ultimately dangerous.

## Description

The instruments are described as "all manner of musical instruments" — fiddles, pipes, and other noise-makers fastened to a mule (007-002). When the dwarf unpacks them, village musicians gather, unable to resist the chance to play instruments of supernatural craftsmanship.

## Properties

- **Seduction** — The instruments draw musicians to them. Even Othmar, with his extraordinary natural gift, is overwhelmed by their sound
- **Test and trap** — Playing the instruments is the first step in the dwarf's seduction of human musicians. The instruments are not merely tools but lures

## Owner / Creator

- [[the-dwarf-musician]] — Uses the instruments to identify and capture talented humans
