## Overview

**This page is a reference alias for [[the-king|the King]].** In "The Rain Maiden" (Chapter 8), the old King is the father of the Prince who falls in love with the shepherd's daughter. Though initially wrathful at his son's choice of a poor bride, he relented and welcomed her as a daughter. He ordered great preparations for the wedding feast, which was famously disrupted by the torrential rain summoned by the [[rain-maiden|Rain Maiden]].
