[010-004]Mary De Morgan**.

[010-006][Standard Ebooks](https://standardebooks.org/)

[010-007]by

[010-008]Hendrik Kaiber**,**

[010-009]and is based on a transcription produced in 2023 by

[010-010]Brian Wilsden**, **Tim Lindell**, and [Distributed Proofreaders](https://www.pgdp.net/)**

[010-012][Project Gutenberg](https://www.gutenberg.org/ebooks/69875)

[010-013]and on digital scans from the

[010-014][Internet Archive](https://archive.org/details/windfairiesother00demo).

[010-016]*Fairy Land*,

[010-018][Gustave Doré](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Gustave_Dor%C3%A9).

[010-020]League Spartan** and **Sorts Mill Goudy****

[010-021]typefaces created in 2014 and 2009 by

[010-022][The League of Moveable Type](https://www.theleagueofmoveabletype.com/).
