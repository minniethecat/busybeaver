# The Cottage by the Sea

The Cottage by the Sea is Lucilla's married home in "The Windfairies," a small dwelling on the beach where she lives with the Sailor. It represents the ordinary human happiness that stands between her enchanted childhood and her later engagement with royal courts and fairy powers.

## Description

The cottage is "little" — small enough for a fisherman's family — and sits directly on the beach (001-022). It is humble but happy: "For some time the sailor and Lucilla lived together very happily" here (001-024). It represents the dream of simple domestic contentment: Lucilla tends to her father and children here, looking out at the sea and waiting for her husband's return.

## Location

The cottage is "by the sea, many miles away from the mill where Lucilla had lived as a child" (001-023). It is part of a small community of "fisher folk who lived in little cottages on the beach" (001-020).

## Events

1. **Married life** — Lucilla and the Sailor live happily here, raising their children (001-023, 001-024).

2. **The Windfairies' return** — While dancing on the shore near the cottage, Lucilla is reclaimed by the Windfairies (001-026).

3. **The final return** — After the palace's destruction, Lucilla returns "to her little cottage and her babies by the sea" (001-068, 001-069).

## Inhabitants / Visitors

- [[the-sailor]] — Her husband, who provides for the family through fishing
