# Standard Pages

The **standard** tier forms the bulk of the wiki's content — pages that meet
the baseline requirements of adequate prose, at least one PN citation, and
functional wikilinks, but have not yet been elevated to featured status.

This page is the third panel in the quality triptych: pair it with the
[Upgrade Queue](/upgrade-queue) (basic tier) and [Featured Pages](/featured-pages)
(premium tier) for a complete quality audit.
