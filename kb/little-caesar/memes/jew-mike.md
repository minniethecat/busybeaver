# Jew Mike

Jew Mike. A lieutenant in Little Arnie's Chicago gambling operation, described as a tougher and more violent version of his boss. His joint is demolished on Rico Bandello's orders during the gang war for control of Chicago's gambling territory.

## Role in the Novel

Jew Mike serves as one of Little Arnie's three key lieutenants during the gang war with Rico Bandello. His presence illustrates the structure of Arnie's organisation and the domino effect of Arnie's defeat — when the leader falls, his subordinates scatter. Rico correctly assesses Jew Mike as "yellow" and unwilling to put up a fight, making him a soft target in the territorial campaign ([033-131], [035-006]).

## Biography

Jew Mike operated as one of Little Arnie's lieutenants during the period when Arnie controlled Chicago's gambling scene. He ran a joint that served as part of Arnie's territory. His reputation within the underworld was that of "a tougher and more violent replica of his chief" ([035-006]).

When Rico Bandello launched his campaign to take over Arnie's operations, Jew Mike's establishment was one of the first targets. Rico ordered Bat Carillo: "I want you to take your gang and smash up Jew Mike's. Run everybody out and then smash the place" ([033-129]). Rico understood his enemy: "Jew Mike's yellow and he won't put up no fight" ([033-131]).

The assessment proved correct. Bat Carillo's gang demolished Jew Mike's joint without significant resistance. Following Arnie's defeat and departure from Chicago, "Jew Mike, whose joint Bat Carillo and his gang had demolished, fled to the South Side, where he opened a couple of vice-joints" ([035-008]).

Jew Mike's flight was part of a broader collapse: Arnie's three lieutenants fell in rapid succession, and "the last vestiges of opposition were wiped out" ([035-008]). The Little Italy gang under Rico now controlled territory it had not held since the era of Monk De Angelo ([035-005]).

## Key Scenes

**The Demolition Order** ([033-129]–[033-131]): Rico assigns Bat Carillo to destroy Jew Mike's joint as part of the systematic dismantling of Arnie's operations. Rico's prediction that Jew Mike will not fight proves accurate.

**Flight to the South Side** ([035-008]): In the aftermath of Arnie's fall, Jew Mike relocates to the South Side and opens new vice-joints. His departure marks the end of organised resistance to Rico's expansion.

## Significance

Jew Mike exemplifies the mid-level gangster who prospers under a powerful boss but cannot survive independently when that protection collapses. His flight to the South Side mirrors the fate of other Arnie affiliates (Kid Burg, Joseph Pavlovsky) who scatter after their leader's defeat. The ease with which Rico's forces dismantle his operation validates Rico's strategic insight — identifying which enemies will fold and which will fight.
