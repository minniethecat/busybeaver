# Spike Rieger

Spike Rieger. A Chicago police detective involved in the investigation and arrests related to the Courriere holdup and Rico Bandello's gang.

## Role in the Novel

Spike Rieger appears as one of the police detectives pursuing Rico's gang. He works alongside Flaherty in key arrest scenes and serves as a representative of the methodical, persistent law enforcement presence that eventually closes in on the gang ([022-022], [080-001]).

## Character Profile

- **Full Name**: Spike Rieger
- **Aliases**: Spike
- **Affiliation**: Chicago Police Department
- **Role**: Detective
- **First Appearance**: Chapter 1 ([022-022])

## Biography

Spike Rieger is introduced indirectly through a story about the Courriere holdup aftermath. When the manager of the Wood jewelry store mistakenly identified the robbers as Poles, Rieger arrested Steve Gollancz and his gang — only to discover Gollancz had just robbed a bank, not the jewelry store ([022-022]).

Rieger participates in the arrest of Joe Massara, arriving at Joe's apartment with Flaherty. He handles the procedural details — producing the warrant and handcuffing Joe ([080-001]–[081-001]). During Joe's interrogation, Rieger takes turns questioning him alongside McClure and Flaherty ([106-001]).

In the novel's climax, Rieger leads the police raid on Sam Vettori's hideout, arresting Sam and his remaining men ([176-001]).

## Key Scenes

**Arresting Joe Massara** ([080-001]): Rieger accompanies Flaherty to Joe's apartment, produces the warrant, and handcuffs Joe while Olga struggles against DeVoss.

**Interrogation** ([106-001]): Rieger participates in the grueling two-hour interrogation of Joe under a blazing light, taking turns with McClure and Flaherty.

**Hideout raid** ([176-001]): Rieger leads the police into Sam Vettori's hideout, putting his head through the door before entering with two officers to make the final arrests.

## Significance

Spike Rieger represents the procedural, institutional face of law enforcement in the novel. Unlike Flaherty, who has personal motives and moments of moral ambiguity, Rieger is a professional detective executing his duties. His presence across multiple arrest scenes underscores the tightening net around Rico's world.
