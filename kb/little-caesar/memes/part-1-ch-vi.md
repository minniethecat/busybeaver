### VI

(007-001)[[rico-bandello|Rico]] was standing in front of his mirror, combing his hair with a little ivory pocket comb. Rico was vain of his hair. It was black and lustrous, combed straight back from his low forehead and arranged in three symmetrical waves.

(007-002)Rico was a simple man. He loved but three things: himself, his hair and his gun. He took excellent care of all three.
