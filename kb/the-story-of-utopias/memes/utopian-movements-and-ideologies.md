## About This Section

Mumford was both sympathetic to and critical of utopian socialism. He admired its constructive impulse while warning against the *idolum* of the perfect blueprint. The entries here reflect the range of movements — from Fourier's phalanxes to the Fabian Society — that populate the original text.
