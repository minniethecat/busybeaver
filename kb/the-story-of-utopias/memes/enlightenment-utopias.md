The Enlightenment brought a new rationalist spirit to utopian thinking. Rather than imagining fantastical islands, Enlightenment thinkers projected utopias grounded in reason, natural law, and the perfectibility of human institutions. This page gathers the Enlightenment-era figures and ideas covered in the wiki.
