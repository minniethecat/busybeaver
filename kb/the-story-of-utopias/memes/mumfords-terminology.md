# Mumford's Terminology

Lewis Mumford developed a distinctive critical vocabulary for analyzing utopian thought. Terms like *idolum*, *eutopia*, and the *utopia of reconstruction* are not merely descriptive labels — they constitute an analytical framework for distinguishing genuine social ideals from compensatory fantasies. This page gathers all entries tagged with Mumford's conceptual apparatus.

## About This Lexicon

Mumford's terms often work in pairs or triads — *eutopia* versus *idolum*, the *utopia of escape* versus the *utopia of reconstruction*, the *country-house* versus *coketown*. Reading across these entries reveals the systematic architecture of his critique.
