## About This Index

The persons listed here span the full history of utopian speculation — from Plato and Thomas More to nineteenth-century utopian socialists and twentieth-century critics. Lewis Mumford's original text engages with philosophers, economists, novelists, social reformers, and religious thinkers, each contributing to the long conversation about ideal societies.
