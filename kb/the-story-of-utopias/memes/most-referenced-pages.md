## Reading This List

A high reference count does not mean Mumford devoted the most pages to a topic. It means that many other entries invoke that concept or figure in their own explanations. *Idolum*, for instance, appears throughout the wiki not because Mumford wrote a single long chapter on it, but because it is the critical lens through which he evaluates nearly every utopia he discusses. Similarly, Plato and More anchor the tradition — nearly every later thinker is measured against them.
