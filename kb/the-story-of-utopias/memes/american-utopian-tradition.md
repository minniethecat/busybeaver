# American Utopian Tradition

The United States served as both laboratory and subject for utopian thought: its open land invited intentional communities, its democratic rhetoric inspired social visions, and its industrial transformation provoked sharp critique. This page gathers the American thinkers, communities, and works that Mumford engages with in *The Story of Utopias*.
