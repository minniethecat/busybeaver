# Literary Utopias and Dystopias

The utopian imagination finds its freest expression in literature — where ideal cities can be built with words alone, unfettered by the compromises of politics or the friction of materials. Mumford reads utopian fiction not as escapism but as a diagnostic tool: each imagined society reveals what its author found intolerable in the real one. This page surveys the literary works that populate *The Story of Utopias*.
