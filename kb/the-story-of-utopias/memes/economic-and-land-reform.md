# Economic and Land Reform Thinkers

For many of the figures in *The Story of Utopias*, the path to the ideal society ran through the redistribution of land and the reorganization of economic life. This page gathers the economists, land reformers, and cooperative thinkers who viewed property relations — rather than political constitutions or technological advance — as the decisive lever of social transformation.
