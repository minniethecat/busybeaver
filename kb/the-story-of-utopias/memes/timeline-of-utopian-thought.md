## About This Timeline

This page does not aim for exhaustive chronological coverage. It reflects the periods that Lewis Mumford engaged with most extensively in *The Story of Utopias*, and the thinkers, works, and concepts for which dedicated entries exist in this wiki. Entries tagged with multiple eras appear in each relevant section.
