# The Blimber Establishment

## Overview

The institutional apparatus of Victorian [[education|education]] — pedantic, oppressive, and ultimately futile.

## Role in the Novel

Operating in [[Brighton|brighton]], this education appears from Section 27 to Section 34, involving key characters in its orbit.

## Key Characters

- [[Dr. Blimber]]
- [[Mrs. Blimber]]

## Significance

This education contributes to Dickens's panoramic portrait of Victorian society and its institutions.
