## About This List

The novel's educational episodes, centered on Paul Dombey's brief and unhappy schooling, serve as a sharp indictment of utilitarian and classical pedagogies that prioritize cramming over care. The entries collected here range from the institutional (Doctor Blimber's establishment) to the personal (Paul's tutors and schoolfellows), together tracing Dickens's argument for an education of the heart.
