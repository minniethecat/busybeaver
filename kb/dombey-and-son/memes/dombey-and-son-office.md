# Dombey and Son's Offices

## Overview

The headquarters of the mercantile firm — a temple of [[commerce|money-and-commerce]] where human values are subordinated to business.

A significant setting in *[[Dombey and Son|dombey-and-son-firm]]*, associated with ["[[Paul Dombey|[[paul|paul-dombey-jr]]-dombey]] Sr.", "[[James Carker|james-carker]]", "[[Walter Gay|walter-gay]]"].
