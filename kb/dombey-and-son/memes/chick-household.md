# The Chick Household

## Overview

The middle-[[class|class-and-society]] domestic circle that provides comic relief and social commentary.

## Role in the Novel

Operating in London, this domestic appears from Section 1 to Section 174, involving key characters in its orbit.

## Significance

This domestic contributes to Dickens's panoramic portrait of Victorian society and its institutions.
