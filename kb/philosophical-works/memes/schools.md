# Index of Philosophical Schools

This page indexes the philosophical schools and traditions that shaped early modern
thought. From ancient Platonism and Aristotelianism through medieval Scholasticism
to the early modern rationalist and empiricist movements — each school represents
a coherent tradition of inquiry.

## About This Index

Schools capture the collective dimension of philosophy — traditions of thought,
pedagogical lineages, and communities of inquiry that persist across generations.
Descartes positioned himself against Scholasticism while laying the groundwork
for what would become Cartesianism and continental rationalism.
