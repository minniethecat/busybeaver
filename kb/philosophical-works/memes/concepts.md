## About This Index

Concepts are the core building blocks of philosophical discourse. This index covers
terms from Cartesian philosophy (e.g. *cogito*, *dualism*, *clear and distinct ideas*)
as well as concepts from the broader early modern and medieval traditions that inform
the Meditations, the Discourse, and the Principles.
