(098-001) That it is likewise certain that [[god|God]] has foreordained all things.

(098-002) But because what we have already discovered of God, gives us the assurance that his power is so immense that we would sin in thinking ourselves capable of ever doing anything which he had not ordained beforehand, we should soon be embarrassed in great difficulties if we undertook to harmonise the preordination of God with the [[will|freedom]] of our will, and endeavoured to comprehend both truths at once.
