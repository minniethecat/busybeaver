(053-001) Of sight.

(053-002) Finally, the extremities of the optic nerves, composing the coat in the eyes called the retina, are not moved by the air nor by any terrestrial object, but only by the globules of the second element,38 whence we have the sense of light and colours: as I have already at sufficient length explained in the Dioptrics and treatise of Meteors.39
