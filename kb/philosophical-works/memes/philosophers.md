## About This Index

The philosophical tradition represented here spans from ancient Greece through the
seventeenth century and beyond. Many figures were direct interlocutors of Descartes
(e.g. Hobbes, Gassendi, Princess Elisabeth); others shaped the intellectual landscape
he inherited (e.g. Aristotle, Aquinas, Augustine).
