(094-001) That our [[error|errors]] cannot be imputed to [[god|God]].

(094-002) But although God has not given us an omniscient understanding, he is not on this account to be considered in any wise the author of our errors, for it is of the nature of created intellect to be finite, and of finite intellect not to embrace all things.
