# Most Referenced Pages

This page ranks wiki entries by the number of cross-references they receive from
other pages, providing a view of which concepts, persons, and works form the
intellectual backbone of the Descartes corpus and its surrounding scholarship.


display: table
fields: [label, type, total_refs]
field_labels:
  label: Page
  type: Type
  total_refs: Citations
sort: total_refs

title: Top 50 Most Referenced Pages

## About This Ranking

Citation counts reflect how often each entry is linked from other wiki pages.
Highly referenced pages typically represent central concepts (e.g. *substance*,
*cogito*), major figures (e.g. Descartes, Aristotle), or foundational works — the
nodes around which philosophical discourse in this wiki is organised.
