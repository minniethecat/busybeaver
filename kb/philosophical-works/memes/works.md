## About This Index

The works indexed here include Descartes' own corpus (the *Meditations*, *Discourse*,
*Principles*, *Passions of the Soul*) alongside works by predecessors (Aristotle's
*Metaphysics*) and contemporaries (Hobbes' *Leviathan*, Spinoza's *Ethics*).
