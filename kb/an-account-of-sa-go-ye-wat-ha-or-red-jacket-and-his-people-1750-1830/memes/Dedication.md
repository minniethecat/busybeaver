[P02-001]*To the Hon. Henry G. [[john-niles-hubbard|Hubbard]], of Middletown, Conn.*

[P02-002]DEAR SIR: Your name, associated with many pleasant memories in the past,

[P02-003]and in later years with substantial tokens of esteem, is held in grateful

[P02-004]recollection; and the hope that these pages may serve to interest an

[P02-005]occasional leisure hour, has led to their being inscribed to you, by your

[P02-006]friend and relative.

##### THE AUTHOR.
