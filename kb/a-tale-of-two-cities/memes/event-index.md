## The Arc of Revolution

The events catalogued here trace the arc of the French Revolution as Dickens imagined it: secret injustices, public upheavals, show trials, and private redemptions. Each dedicated event page situates its subject within the novel's chapter structure, with inline citations linking narrative moments to their source passages.
