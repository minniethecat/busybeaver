# Top Referenced Pages

This page ranks the fifty most-cited entries in the wiki, ordered by total backlink count. Pages with high citation counts are typically central characters, pivotal events, or major themes.

## Interpreting Citation Counts

Citation counts reflect the number of other wiki pages that reference a given entry through inline source citations (PN references) and wikilinks. A high count signals a page deeply woven into the novel's fabric.

This page includes all entry types: characters, events, places, themes, glossary terms, and works. Chapter and overview pages are excluded.
