## Thematic Reading

Dickens's novel operates on multiple thematic registers simultaneously: the personal (love, loyalty, revenge), the social (class, justice, mob rule), and the metaphysical (fate, resurrection, the sweep of history). Each theme page traces its motif across chapters, with quoted passages that show how Dickens returned to these ideas in ever-deepening variations.
