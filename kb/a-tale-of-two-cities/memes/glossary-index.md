## Why a Glossary

Dickens wrote for a Victorian audience familiar with eighteenth-century France, but modern readers encounter a world of obsolete objects, political institutions, and cultural practices that require explication. Each glossary page defines its term within the novel's context, supported by cited passages and, where helpful, historical background that Dickens's original readers would have taken for granted.
