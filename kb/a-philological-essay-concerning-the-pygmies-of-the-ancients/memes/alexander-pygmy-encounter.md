# Alexander's Alleged Pygmy Encounter

## Overview

Alexander's Alleged Pygmy Encounter is discussed in Edward Tyson's _A Philological Essay Concerning the Pygmies of the Ancients_ (1699). Tyson references this as part of his comprehensive examination of ancient accounts concerning pygmies.

## Ancient Sources

The primary sources for this event come from classical authors whose works Tyson systematically analyzes.

## Tyson's Analysis

Tyson examines this event within his broader framework of reconciling ancient accounts with natural-historical observation. His treatment reflects the methodological approach of early modern philology.
