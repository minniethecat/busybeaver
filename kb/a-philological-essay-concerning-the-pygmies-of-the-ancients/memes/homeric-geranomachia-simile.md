# Homeric Geranomachia Simile

## Overview

Homeric Geranomachia Simile is discussed in Edward Tyson's _A Philological Essay Concerning the Pygmies of the Ancients_ (1699). Tyson references this as part of his comprehensive examination of ancient accounts concerning pygmies.

The primary sources for this event come from classical authors whose works Tyson systematically analyzes.
