# Troglodytes

## Overview

An edge-case concept in Edward Tyson's _A Philological Essay Concerning the Pygmies of the Ancients_ (1699). This entity has limited direct corpus coverage and represents a boundary case for the wiki's knowledge representation.

## Relation to Tyson's Thesis

This concept relates to Tyson's broader argument about the natural-historical basis of ancient pygmy accounts.
