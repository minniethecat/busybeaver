## The Novel's Structure

*Hard Times* is divided into three books, each containing multiple chapters:

- **Book the First: Sowing** — Introduces the characters and the philosophy of Fact that dominates Coketown.
- **Book the Second: Reaping** — The consequences of the Gradgrind philosophy unfold.
- **Book the Third: Garnering** — Resolution and the harvesting of what was sown.

Each chapter page contains a summary and linked character and concept references.
