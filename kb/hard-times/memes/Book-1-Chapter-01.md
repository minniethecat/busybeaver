[001-001] THE ONE THING NEEDFUL

[001-002] ‘NOW, what I want is, [[facts|Facts]]. Teach these boys and girls nothing but

[001-003] Facts. Facts alone are wanted in life. Plant nothing else, and root out

[001-004] everything else. You can only form the minds of [[reason|reasoning]] animals upon

[001-005] Facts: nothing else will ever be of any service to them. This is the

[001-006] principle on which I bring up my own [[childhood|children]], and this is the principle

[001-007] on which I bring up these children. Stick to Facts, sir!’

[001-008] The scene was a plain, bare, monotonous vault of a school-room, and the

[001-009] speaker’s square forefinger emphasized his observations by underscoring

[001-010] every sentence with a line on [[mchoakumchild|the schoolmaster]]’s sleeve. The emphasis

[001-011] was helped by the speaker’s square wall of a forehead, which had his

[001-012] eyebrows for its base, while his eyes found commodious cellarage in two

[001-013] dark caves, overshadowed by the wall. The emphasis was helped by the

[001-014] speaker’s mouth, which was wide, thin, and hard set. The emphasis was

[001-015] helped by the speaker’s voice, which was inflexible, dry, and

[001-016] dictatorial. The emphasis was helped by the speaker’s hair, which

[001-017] bristled on the skirts of his bald head, a plantation of firs to keep the

[001-018] wind from its shining surface, all covered with knobs, like the crust of

[001-019] a plum pie, as if the head had scarcely warehouse-room for the hard facts

[001-020] stored inside. The speaker’s obstinate carriage, square coat, square

[001-021] legs, square shoulders,—nay, his very neckcloth, trained to take him by

[001-022] the throat with an unaccommodating grasp, like a stubborn fact, as it

[001-023] was,—all helped the emphasis.

[001-024] ‘In this life, we want nothing but Facts, sir; nothing but Facts!’

[001-025] The speaker, and the schoolmaster, and the third grown person present,

[001-026] all backed a little, and swept with their eyes the inclined plane of

[001-027] little vessels then and there arranged in order, ready to have imperial

[001-028] gallons of facts poured into them until they were full to the brim.
