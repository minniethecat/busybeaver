# Coketown and Its People

Coketown is the fictional industrial city that serves as the primary setting of *Hard Times*. Dickens' description of its "interminable serpents of smoke," its identical red-brick buildings, and its machine-like rhythms stands as one of the most powerful critiques of industrial capitalism in English literature.
