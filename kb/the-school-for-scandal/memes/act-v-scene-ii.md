# Act V, Scene II — At Sir Peter's House

**Setting**: At Sir Peter Teazle's House  
**Characters present**: [[lady-teazle]], [[sir-peter-teazle]], [[mrs-candour]], [[sir-oliver-surface]], [[rowley]]  
**Dramatic function**: resolution

## Summary

The aftermath of the screen scene plays out in the Teazle household. Lady Teazle, humbled and repentant, reconciles with Sir Peter. She admits the folly of her flirtation with fashionable scandal and acknowledges Sir Peter's genuine care for her. Mrs. Candour arrives with fresh gossip — the scandal of the screen scene is already spreading — but is dismissed. Sir Oliver arrives and begins to orchestrate the final revelations. The reconciliation of the Teazles restores one moral axis of the play, preparing for the final judgment on the Surface brothers.
