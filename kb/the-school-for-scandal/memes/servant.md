# Servant

The Servant is a minor functionary in The School for Scandal, appearing at Joseph Surface's house to announce visitors and carry messages. His role is purely mechanical — he delivers the news of Sir Peter's arrival in the screen scene (004-190) and appears again in Act V (005-001) — but his presence is essential to the play's comic timing. His announcements trigger the entrances and exits that structure Joseph's exposure.

## Function

The Servant's primary function is to precipitate the screen scene's central event: his announcement of Sir Peter's arrival forces Lady Teazle to hide, setting the comic machinery in motion. Without the Servant, there is no screen scene — he is the trigger for the play's most famous moment.
