# About This Wiki

## Source Material

*The Absolute at Large* (Czech: *Továrna na absolutno*) is a science fiction novel

by Karel Čapek, first published in 1922. This wiki is built from the English

translation of the novel.

## Usage Notes

This wiki serves as a structured knowledge base for exploring the characters,

concepts, places, and events in Čapek's novel. Browse by chapter or search for

specific topics.
