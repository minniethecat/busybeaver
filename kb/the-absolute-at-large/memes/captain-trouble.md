## Overview

Captain Trouble is a minor character in Karel Čapek's *The Absolute at Large*, a rugged sea captain who protects the industrialist G.H. Bondy on his coral island refuge of Hereheretua in the Pacific during the height of the Greatest War. He is a man of decisive action, quick with a rifle, and possessed of colourful tales of cannibalism and danger in the South Seas (027-001).

## Role & Significance

Captain Trouble represents the frontier masculinity and pragmatic violence that stands in sharp contrast to Bondy's urban, commercial sensibility. While Bondy frets and philosophizes about the nature of the Absolute, Captain Trouble simply aims his rifle and shoots into the mangrove thicket. His presence underscores the novel's theme that civilization, when stripped of its veneer, reverts to the most basic calculus of survival — and that some men are far better equipped for that reality than others (027-003).

## Appearances

Captain Trouble appears in Chapter XXVII ("A Coral Island in the Pacific"), where he serves as Bondy's protector on the island of Hereheretua.

He is first introduced with characteristic bluntness, identifying a potential threat:

> "Well, I'll go to blazes," said Captain Trouble, "if that lanky fellow over there isn't their leader!" (027-001)

He regales Bondy with tales of South Seas dangers, referencing real-world incidents of cannibalism:

> "I should say so," boomed Captain Trouble. "Not far off, on Rawaiwai, Captain Barker and his whole crew were eaten. And on Mangai they had a banquet on three millionaires like yourself." (027-002)

Throughout the chapter, he maintains vigilance over the bungalow:

> Captain Trouble was glowering suspiciously at the thicket of mangroves and bananas which surrounded the bungalow. (027-003)

When Bondy engages in philosophical reflection on the nature of the Absolute — "He is infinite. That's just where the trouble lies" — Captain Trouble interrupts with characteristic directness:

> "Wait a minute," interrupted Captain Trouble, and aimed a shot into the mangrove thicket. "There, I think that's one less of them." (027-004)

## Relationships

- **[[g-h-bondy|G.H. Bondy]]** — Bondy is Captain Trouble's employer and charge on Hereheretua. Trouble protects him from perceived threats while Bondy provides the philosophical musings that Trouble has no patience for.
