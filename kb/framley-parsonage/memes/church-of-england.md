# The Church of England

The Church of England is the established national church of England, the institution whose hierarchy, patronage, and preferment form the ecclesiastical framework of _Framley Parsonage_. Its bishops sit in the House of Lords; its parishes cover every corner of the land; its clergy — from the Archbishop of Canterbury to the humblest [[curate]] — constitute the professional world within which [[mark-robarts|Mark Robarts]] and his fellow clergymen live and work.
