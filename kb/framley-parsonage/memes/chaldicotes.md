# Chaldicotes

Chaldicotes is the country seat of [[sowerby|Mr. Sowerby]] and the centre of the "fast" political set that entangles [[mark-robarts|Mark Robarts]] in debt and social complication. Its atmosphere of masculine conviviality and political calculation stands in deliberate contrast to the domestic propriety of [[framley-court|Framley Court]].
