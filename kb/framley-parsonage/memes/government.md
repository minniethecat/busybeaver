# Her Majesty's Government

Her Majesty's Government is the executive authority of the United Kingdom, formed from the majority party in [[parliament|Parliament]] and responsible for the conduct of national affairs. Its composition and policies form the backdrop to the political drama of the [[election|parliamentary election]].
