# Helen Hudson Meets Bobby Merrick

## Overview

The first meeting between [[helen-hudson|Helen Hudson]] and [[bobby-merrick|Bobby Merrick]], initiating the relationship that becomes central to the novel.

## Event Details

Part of the world of Lloyd C. Douglas's *Magnificent Obsession* (1929).

## Narrative Significance

Contributes to the novel's exploration of the "Magnificent Obsession" philosophy — [[secrecy|secret]] service to others as the path to expanded personality.

## Key Passages

Appears in the novel across multiple chapters, woven into the narrative of [[bobby-merrick|Bobby Merrick]]'s transformation.
