The civic organization that programmed a discussion of Birth Control, at which Dr. [[doctor-mclaren|McLaren]] was asked to offer the in[[profession|vocation]].

Part of the institutional landscape of Lloyd C. Douglas's *Magnificent Obsession* (1929).
