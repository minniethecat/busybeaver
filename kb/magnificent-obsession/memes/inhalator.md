# Inhalator

The **inhalator** is a medical device of the "super-type" used by life-saving crews,
owned by [[wayne-hudson|Doctor Hudson]] and kept at the [[hudson-cottage|Hudson's cottage on the lake]].
It is an advanced piece of respiratory equipment for its time — described as "elaborate but not very decorative" (001-126).

## Description

The device belongs to a class of respirators described as a "super-type used by life-saving crews" (001-126),
indicating it is a professional-grade piece of emergency medical equipment rather than a household appliance.

## Role in the Story

Doctor Hudson brought the inhalator to the cottage on the lake and explained its use to the staff (001-131).
When a medical emergency occurred — the boating accident involving several young people on the lake —
a messenger was dispatched to the nearby village to summon aid.

By a "singular coincidence," the inhalator owned by Doctor Hudson was at the cottage,
and those at the scene, informed of its presence, rushed to retrieve it (001-146, 001-149).
However, the dispatch rider reported that had the inhalator been immediately available,
an important medical intervention might have succeeded (001-151).
The delay in accessing the device — or the delay in learning of its availability —
was a contributing factor in the tragic outcome.

## Significance

The inhalator serves as a narrative device that underscores the themes of proximity,
readiness, and the gap between available resources and timely action.
Its presence at the cottage — unknown to those at the accident scene until it was too late —
mirrors the novel's broader exploration of help that exists but goes unrecognized or unaccessed.
