# Carron Company

The Carron Company, founded in 1759, was one of the largest ironworks in Europe. Its carronades (short naval cannon) were used by the [[admiralty|Royal Navy]], and its works pioneered the steam blowing engine with [[john-smeaton|John Smeaton]].

## History

Founded in 1759 in [[falkirk|Falkirk]], [[scotland|Scotland]], Carron Company played an important role in the industrial development of Britain.

## Contributions

The organisation contributed to technological and industrial progress during the [[industrial-revolution|Industrial Revolution]].
