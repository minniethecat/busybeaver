# Boulton and Watt

The partnership of [[matthew-boulton|Matthew Boulton]] and [[james-watt|James Watt]], formed in 1775, produced steam engines that powered the [[industrial-revolution|Industrial Revolution]]. Their [[soho-manufactory|Soho Manufactory]] was a model of industrial organisation, combining Watt's technical genius with Boulton's business acumen.

## History

Founded in 1775 in [[birmingham|Birmingham]], [[england|England]], Boulton and Watt played an important role in the industrial development of Britain.

## Contributions

The organisation contributed to technological and industrial progress during the Industrial Revolution.
