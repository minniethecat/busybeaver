# Maudslay Sons and Field

[[henry-maudslay|Henry Maudslay]]'s [[lambeth|Lambeth]] works, later Maudslay Sons and Field, was the leading precision engineering establishment of its age. It produced machine tools, marine engines, and trained Whitworth, Nasmyth, and Muir.

## History

Founded in 1798 in Lambeth, [[london|London]], Maudslay Sons and Field played an important role in the industrial development of Britain.

## Contributions

The organisation contributed to technological and industrial progress during the [[industrial-revolution|Industrial Revolution]].
