# [[joseph-whitworth|Joseph Whitworth]] and Co

Joseph Whitworth and Co in [[manchester|Manchester]] was the leading precision engineering firm of its era. Whitworth's standard screw thread system, true surface plates, and measuring machines set the standards for British engineering.

## History

Founded in 1833 in [[manchester|Manchester]], [[england|England]], Joseph Whitworth and Co played an important role in the industrial development of Britain.

## Contributions

The organisation contributed to technological and industrial progress during the [[industrial-revolution|Industrial Revolution]].
