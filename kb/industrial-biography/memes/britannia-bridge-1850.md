# [[tubular-bridge|Britannia Bridge]] 1850

In 1850, [[robert-stephenson|Robert Stephenson]] completed the Britannia [[tubular-bridge|Tubular Bridge]] across the Menai Strait. Its wrought-iron box sections, through which trains ran, represented a completely new structural concept in bridge engineering. (054-037)

## Context

This event occurred during the [[industrial-revolution|Industrial Revolution]] and contributed to the development of iron and engineering technology.
