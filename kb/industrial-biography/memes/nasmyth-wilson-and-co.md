# Nasmyth Wilson and Co

Nasmyth Wilson and Co, formerly the Bridgewater [[foundry|Foundry]] at Patricroft, manufactured Nasmyth's steam hammers along with machine tools and locomotives. The company exported worldwide.

## History

Founded in 1836 in Patricroft, [[manchester|Manchester]], Nasmyth Wilson and Co played an important role in the industrial development of Britain.

## Contributions

The organisation contributed to technological and industrial progress during the [[industrial-revolution|Industrial Revolution]].
