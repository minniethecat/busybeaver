# [[james-watt|James Watt]] and Company

James Watt and Company was formed after [[matthew-boulton|Matthew Boulton]]'s retirement, with Watt's sons taking over management. The company continued producing steam engines at the Soho [[foundry|Foundry]] into the 19th century.

## History

Founded in 1795 in Soho, [[birmingham|Birmingham]], James Watt and Company played an important role in the industrial development of Britain.

## Contributions

The organisation contributed to technological and industrial progress during the [[industrial-revolution|Industrial Revolution]].
