# Carron Iron Works

The Carron Iron Works on the River Carron near [[falkirk|Falkirk]] was founded in 1759 and became one of the largest ironworks in Europe. [[john-smeaton|John Smeaton]] installed the first steam-powered blowing engine here. Carron became famous for its naval cannon, known as carronades. (016-071)

## Industrial Significance

Carron Iron Works contributed to the industrial development of Britain's iron and engineering industries.
