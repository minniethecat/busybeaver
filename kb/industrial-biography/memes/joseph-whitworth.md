# Joseph Whitworth

Joseph Whitworth was apprenticed to [[henry-maudslay|Henry Maudslay]] and became the leading precision engineer of his generation. His British Standard Whitworth thread system (1841) made nationwide interchangeability possible. He invented measuring machines capable of detecting one-millionth of an inch and pioneered the use of true surface plates. (002-126)
