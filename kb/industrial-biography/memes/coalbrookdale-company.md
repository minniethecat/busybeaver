# [[coalbrookdale|Coalbrookdale]] Company

The Coalbrookdale Company was founded by [[abraham-darby-i|Abraham Darby I]] in 1709 and remained in the Darby family for generations. It pioneered coke smelting, produced the castings for the world's first iron bridge, and was synonymous with innovation in iron-making throughout the 18th century.

## History

Founded in 1709 in [[coalbrookdale|Coalbrookdale]], [[shropshire|Shropshire]], Coalbrookdale Company played an important role in the industrial development of Britain.

## Contributions

The organisation contributed to technological and industrial progress during the [[industrial-revolution|Industrial Revolution]].
