# Maudslay [[slide-rest|Slide Rest]] c.1798

Around 1798, [[henry-maudslay|Henry Maudslay]] invented the slide rest, a device that mechanically constrained the cutting tool to move with geometric precision. This was the foundational invention of the machine tool industry. (037-158)

## Context

This event occurred during the transformative era of the [[industrial-revolution|Industrial Revolution]] in Britain.

## Significance

The event contributed to the advancement of iron-making and engineering technology.
