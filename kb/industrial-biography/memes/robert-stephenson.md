# Robert Stephenson

Son of [[george-stephenson|George Stephenson]], Robert Stephenson became one of the greatest engineers of the Victorian age. His [[tubular-bridge|tubular iron bridge]]s across the Menai Strait were revolutionary applications of wrought iron in civil engineering. He also designed locomotives including the famous Rocket.
