# [[coalbrookdale|Coalbrookdale]] [[coke-smelting|Coke Smelting]] 1709

In 1709, [[abraham-darby-i|Abraham Darby I]] successfully smelted iron using coke at [[coalbrookdale|Coalbrookdale]], breaking the charcoal constraint that had limited iron production. This was the foundational event of the [[industrial-revolution|Industrial Revolution]] in iron. (021-122)

## Context

This event occurred during the transformative era of the Industrial Revolution in Britain.

## Significance

The event contributed to the advancement of iron-making and engineering technology.
