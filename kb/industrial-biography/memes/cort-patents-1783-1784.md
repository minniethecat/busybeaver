# Cort [[patents|Patents]] 1783-1784

[[henry-cort|Henry Cort]] published his patents in 1783 and 1784, covering the [[puddling-process|puddling]] process for converting pig iron and grooved rollers for shaping [[wrought-iron|bar iron]]. These patents established the integrated coke-based iron production system that revolutionised the industry. (021-156)

## Context

This event occurred during the transformative era of the [[industrial-revolution|Industrial Revolution]] in Britain.

## Significance

The event contributed to the advancement of iron-making and engineering technology.
