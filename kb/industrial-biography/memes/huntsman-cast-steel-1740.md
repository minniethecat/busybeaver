# Huntsman [[cast-steel|Cast Steel]] c.1740

Around 1740, [[benjamin-huntsman|Benjamin Huntsman]] perfected the cast steel process at [[attercliffe|Attercliffe]] near [[sheffield|Sheffield]], melting blister steel in sealed crucibles to produce the world's finest steel. (019-107)

## Context

This event occurred during the transformative era of the [[industrial-revolution|Industrial Revolution]] in Britain.

## Significance

The event contributed to the advancement of iron-making and engineering technology.
