# Whitworth Thread Standard 1841

In 1841, [[joseph-whitworth|Joseph Whitworth]] published his standard screw thread system, the British Standard Whitworth (BSW). For the first time, bolts and nuts could be interchangeable nationwide, a fundamental advance in standardised manufacturing.

## Context

This event occurred during the [[industrial-revolution|Industrial Revolution]] and contributed to the development of iron and engineering technology.
