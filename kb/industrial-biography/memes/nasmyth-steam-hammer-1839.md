# Nasmyth [[steam-hammer|Steam Hammer]] 1839

In 1839, [[james-nasmyth|James Nasmyth]] invented the steam hammer to solve Brunel's problem of forging the massive paddle shaft for the SS Great Britain. The hammer could deliver blows from a tap to five tons with perfect control.

## Context

This event occurred during the transformative era of the [[industrial-revolution|Industrial Revolution]] in Britain.

## Significance

The event contributed to the advancement of iron-making and engineering technology.
