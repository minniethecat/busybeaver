# Merthyr Tydfil

Merthyr Tydfil in [[south-wales|South Wales]] became one of the world's greatest iron-producing districts in the late 18th century. [[peter-onions|Peter Onions]] patented his [[puddling-process|[[puddling-process|puddling]] furnace]] here in 1783. The Cyfarthfa and [[dowlais-ironworks|Dowlais]] ironworks were among the largest in the world. (021-136)

## Industrial Significance

Merthyr Tydfil played a significant role in the industrial development of Britain during the 18th and 19th centuries.
