# First [[iron-bridge|Iron Bridge]] 1779

In 1779, the world's first iron bridge was erected across the [[severn|River Severn]] at [[coalbrookdale|Coalbrookdale]]. Cast by [[abraham-darby-i|Abraham Darby I]]II at the family's [[coalbrookdale|Coalbrookdale]] Iron Works, the bridge demonstrated that iron could be used for major structural engineering projects. (001-180)

## Context

This event occurred during the transformative era of the [[industrial-revolution|Industrial Revolution]] in Britain.

## Significance

The event contributed to the advancement of iron-making and engineering technology.
