# James Nasmyth

James Nasmyth was born in [[edinburgh|Edinburgh]] and apprenticed to [[henry-maudslay|Henry Maudslay]] in [[london|London]]. His steam hammer (1839) solved the problem of forging the massive paddle shafts for Brunel's SS Great Britain. He established the [[patricroft-foundry|Patricroft Foundry]] near [[manchester|Manchester]] and was also an accomplished astronomer.
