# Peter Onions

Peter Onions of [[merthyr-tydfil|Merthyr Tydfil]] patented a [[puddling-process|[[puddling-process|puddling]] furnace]] in 1783 that used a blast to maintain combustion. His design was the first to clearly describe the manual [[puddling-process|puddling]] operation. Though Cort is more famous, he was anticipated by Onions in the process of [[puddling-process|puddling]]. (021-143)
