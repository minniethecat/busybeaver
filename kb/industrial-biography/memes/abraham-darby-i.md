# Abraham Darby I

Abraham Darby I was a [[bristol|Bristol]] brass-founder who established the [[coalbrookdale|Coalbrookdale]] Iron Works in 1709. His crucial innovation was using coke from local low-sulphur coal to smelt iron ore, making coke smelting commercially successful where [[dud-dudley|Dud Dudley]] had failed. His initial production focused on cast-iron cooking pots and hollow-ware. (001-170)

## Early Life and Career

Born in 1678, Abraham Darby I entered the industrial world during a period of rapid change in British manufacturing.

## Major Contributions

Abraham Darby I's principal contributions established new methods that influenced subsequent generations of engineers and industrial workers.
