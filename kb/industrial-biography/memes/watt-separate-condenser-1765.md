# Watt [[watt-separate-condenser|Separate Condenser]] 1765

In 1765, [[james-watt|James Watt]] conceived the separate condenser while walking on [[glasgow|Glasgow]] Green. This insight, transforming the Newcomen engine from an inefficient pump into a universal prime mover, was the critical invention that enabled the steam-powered [[industrial-revolution|Industrial Revolution]].

## Context

This event occurred during the transformative era of the Industrial Revolution in Britain.

## Significance

The event contributed to the advancement of iron-making and engineering technology.
