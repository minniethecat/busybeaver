# Glasgow

Glasgow was at the centre of Scottish industrial innovation. [[james-watt|James Watt]] was an instrument-maker at Glasgow University when he conceived the separate condenser. [[james-beaumont-neilson|James Beaumont Neilson]] managed the Glasgow Gas-works before inventing the hot blast. The [[clyde|Clyde]] Valley became [[scotland|Scotland]]'s iron-making heartland. (027-070)

## Industrial Significance

Glasgow played a significant role in the industrial development of Britain during the 18th and 19th centuries.
