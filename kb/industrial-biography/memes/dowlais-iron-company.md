# [[dowlais-ironworks|Dowlais]] Iron Company

The [[dowlais-ironworks|Dowlais Ironworks]], later the Dowlais Iron Company, was one of the great Welsh iron enterprises. It was among the first to install Neilson's hot blast, dramatically improving its blast furnace efficiency.

## History

Founded in 1759 in [[merthyr-tydfil|Merthyr Tydfil]], Wales, Dowlais Iron Company played an important role in the industrial development of Britain.

## Contributions

The organisation contributed to technological and industrial progress during the [[industrial-revolution|Industrial Revolution]].
