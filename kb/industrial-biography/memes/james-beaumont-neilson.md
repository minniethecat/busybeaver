# James Beaumont Neilson

James Beaumont Neilson was born near [[glasgow|Glasgow]] and became manager of the [[glasgow|Glasgow]] Gas-works. Convinced that preheating blast air could improve furnace efficiency, he successfully trialled the hot blast at [[clyde-iron-works|[[clyde|Clyde]] Iron Works]] in 1828. Though his patent was heavily litigated, Neilson prevailed and the hot blast spread rapidly. (027-070)
