# About This Wiki

## Source Material

This wiki is built from *Industrial Biography: Iron Workers and Tool Makers* (1863) by **[[samuel-smiles|Samuel Smiles]]** — a pioneering work of industrial history that chronicles the lives and inventions of the engineers, ironmasters, and tool makers who drove the [[industrial-revolution|Industrial Revolution]].

The book presents detailed biographical accounts of key figures such as [[henry-maudslay|Henry Maudslay]], [[joseph-clement|Joseph Clement]], [[james-nasmyth|James Nasmyth]], and [[henry-bessemer|Henry Bessemer]], interwoven with the technological developments they pioneered.

## Scope

- Biographies of iron workers, engineers, and inventors

- Key inventions and technological innovations (steam hammer, slide rest, Bessemer converter, etc.)

- Industrial processes and concepts ([[puddling-process|puddling]], rolling, casting)

- Historical context of the Industrial Revolution in Britain

## Usage Notes

This wiki is designed for researchers, students, and enthusiasts of industrial history. Pages are linked via paragraph-number (PN) citations back to the source chapters, enabling traceable references.
