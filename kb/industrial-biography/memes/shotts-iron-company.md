# Shotts Iron Company

The Shotts Iron Company in Lanarkshire was one of the earliest adopters of Neilson's hot blast process, which transformed the economics of Scottish iron-making.

## History

Founded in 1802 in Lanarkshire, [[scotland|Scotland]], Shotts Iron Company played an important role in the industrial development of Britain.

## Contributions

The organisation contributed to technological and industrial progress during the [[industrial-revolution|Industrial Revolution]].
