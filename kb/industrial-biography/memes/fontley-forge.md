# Fontley Forge

Fontley Forge near Fareham in Hampshire was the site of [[henry-cort|Henry Cort]]'s experimental and commercial ironworks. Here Cort developed and demonstrated his [[puddling-process|puddling]] process and grooved rollers, the inventions that would revolutionise British iron production. (021-156)

## Industrial Significance

Fontley Forge contributed to the industrial development of Britain's iron and engineering industries.
