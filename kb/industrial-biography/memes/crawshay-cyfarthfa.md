# Crawshay Cyfarthfa

Under [[richard-crawshay|Richard Crawshay]], the [[cyfarthfa-ironworks|Cyfarthfa Ironworks]] became one of the world's largest. Crawshay adopted Cort's [[puddling-process|puddling]] process early and produced [[wrought-iron|bar iron]] on an unprecedented scale for export and domestic markets.

## History

Founded in 1786 in [[merthyr-tydfil|Merthyr Tydfil]], Wales, Crawshay Cyfarthfa played an important role in the industrial development of Britain.

## Contributions

The organisation contributed to technological and industrial progress during the [[industrial-revolution|Industrial Revolution]].
