# Matthew Boulton

Matthew Boulton was a leading figure of the [[industrial-revolution|Industrial Revolution]] in [[birmingham|Birmingham]]. His [[soho-manufactory|Soho Manufactory]] was a model of industrial organisation, and his partnership with [[james-watt|James Watt]] from 1775 produced the steam engines that powered the age. Boulton provided the business acumen that complemented Watt's technical genius.
