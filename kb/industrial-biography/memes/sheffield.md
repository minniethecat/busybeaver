# Sheffield

Sheffield's rise as the global capital of steel began with [[benjamin-huntsman|Benjamin Huntsman]]'s [[cast-steel|crucible steel]] process in the 1740s. The city's cutlers, tool-makers, and steel-makers produced the finest edge tools, cutlery, and later armour plate in the world. By the 19th century, Sheffield steel was a global brand. (019-092)

## Industrial Significance

Sheffield played a significant role in the industrial development of Britain during the 18th and 19th centuries.
