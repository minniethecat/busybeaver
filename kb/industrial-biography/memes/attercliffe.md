# Attercliffe

Attercliffe near [[sheffield|Sheffield]] was the location of [[benjamin-huntsman|Benjamin Huntsman]]'s [[cast-steel|crucible steel]] works from around 1740. Here, in secrecy, Huntsman perfected the process of melting blister steel in sealed crucibles to produce the homogeneous cast steel that made [[sheffield|Sheffield]] famous. (019-107)

## Industrial Significance

Attercliffe contributed to the industrial development of Britain's iron and engineering industries.
