# Smiles Industrial Biography 1863

In 1863, [[samuel-smiles|Samuel Smiles]] published Industrial Biography: Iron Workers and Tool Makers, chronicling the lives and inventions of the engineers, ironmasters, and tool-makers who built the [[industrial-revolution|Industrial Revolution]]. The book is the primary source for this wiki.

## Context

This event occurred during the Industrial Revolution and contributed to the development of iron and engineering technology.
