# The Chinese Administration in Mei-tan-fu

## Overview

The Chinese administration, represented by [[colonel-yu|Colonel Yü]] and other local officials, is the indigenous governance structure that exists alongside the British colonial presence. The novel acknowledges it only briefly, but its presence is significant: it reminds the reader that the British story is not the only story, and that Chinese society has its own structures of authority independent of colonial power.
