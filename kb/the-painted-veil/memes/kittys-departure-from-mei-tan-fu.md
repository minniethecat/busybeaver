# Kitty's Departure from Mei-tan-fu

## Overview

After [[death|Walter's death]], [[mother-superior|the Mother Superior]] urges [[kitty-fane|Kitty]] to leave: "It is your duty, my dear child, to do everything in your power for the welfare of the being that God has entrusted to your care" (068-008). [[waddington|Waddington]] arranges an escort through [[colonel-yu|Colonel Yü]], and the nuns prepare for her departure at dawn (068-015). [[kitty-fane|Kitty]]'s departure from [[mei-tan-fu|Mei-tan-fu]] is bittersweet — she leaves the place where she learned to be useful, but she carries within her the person she has become. The soldiers of the escort shuffle along as she makes her way out of the city that changed her (069-002).

> "There is nothing for you to stay for. You came to be with your husband. Your husband is dead. You are in a condition in which you will shortly need a care and attention which it is impossible for you to get here. It is your duty, my dear child, to do everything in your power for the welfare of the being that God has entrusted to your care."
>
> (068-008)
