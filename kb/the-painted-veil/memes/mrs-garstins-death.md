# Mrs. Garstin's Death

## Overview

[[mrs-garstin|Mrs. Garstin]]'s death, reported in the novel's final chapters, is a narrative release (080-001). The woman whose ambition drove [[kitty-fane|Kitty]] into [[walter-fane|Walter]]'s arms and whose values shaped [[kitty-fane|Kitty]]'s entire sense of self is gone. Her death frees [[kitty-fane|Kitty]] — not from grief but from judgment (080-029) — and enables the novel's final, hopeful scene of [[forgiveness|reconciliation]] with her father (080-021).

> Deeply regret to inform you that your mother died this morning. Father.
