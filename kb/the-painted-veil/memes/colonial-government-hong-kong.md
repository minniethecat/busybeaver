# Colonial Government (Hong Kong)

## Overview

The colonial government is the institutional backdrop of the entire novel. It employs [[walter-fane|Walter]] as Government Bacteriologist (004-001), [[charles-townsend|Townsend]] as Assistant Colonial Secretary, and [[waddington|Waddington]] as Deputy Commissioner. It is the system that determines social status, professional advancement, and — through its hierarchy — the relative value of different kinds of work (035-012). The novel treats it with quiet skepticism: a system that rewards charm over competence and that sustains the "painted veil" of colonial prestige.

> The owner of the shop knew who Charlie was and he was not such a fool as to put up the back of the Assistant Colonial Secretary. What did anything matter really but that Charlie loved her?
>
> (005-002)
