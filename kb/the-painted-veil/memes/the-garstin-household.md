# The Garstin Household

## Overview

The Garstin household is the novel's primary domestic institution — a family organized as a strategic enterprise, with [[mrs-garstin|Mrs. Garstin]] as CEO, [[mr-garstin|Mr. Garstin]] as silent partner, and the daughters as assets to be invested in the marriage market. It is the institution that formed [[kitty-fane|Kitty]], and her liberation from its values is the novel's central arc (008-001). After [[mrs-garstins-death|Mrs. Garstin's death]], only Kitty and her father remain — the household reduced to its final, quiet form, as Mr. Garstin prepares to leave for a new post abroad (080-001).
