# Standard Ebooks

## Overview

Standard Ebooks is the publisher of the source edition used for this wiki. A volunteer-driven project, it produces carefully formatted, professionally proofread digital editions of public domain works. Its edition of The Painted Veil (2025-08-23) provided the clean, well-structured source text from which this wiki's chapter corpus was extracted.
