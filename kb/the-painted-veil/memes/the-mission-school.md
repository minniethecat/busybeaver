# The Mission School

## Overview

The school within the convent is where the nuns educate Chinese orphans (022-020) — teaching them language, skills, and the Catholic faith. When funds ran out, Waddington himself came to the nuns' rescue so they could feed their orphans (041-010). The children are orphans only in the sense that their parents cannot afford to keep them, abandoned at the convent gates by families with nothing left to give (042-009). [[kitty-fane|Kitty]]'s involvement with the school children, particularly her [[love|affection]] for a little girl, is one of the channels through which she discovers her capacity for love that is not about herself.
