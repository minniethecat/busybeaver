# The Edwardian Marriage Market

## Overview

The marriage market is the social institution that shaped [[kitty-fane|Kitty]]'s values and constrained her choices. It is a system in which young women are displayed, assessed, and selected — a market in which beauty is currency and personality is secondary. [[mrs-garstin|Mrs. Garstin]] is its expert practitioner; [[kitty-fane|Kitty]] is its product; and the novel is, in part, its indictment.
