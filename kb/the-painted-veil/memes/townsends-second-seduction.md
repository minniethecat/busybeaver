# Townsend's Second Seduction

## Overview

Staying at the [[charles-townsend|Townsend]]s' house after her return from [[mei-tan-fu|Mei-tan-fu]], [[kitty-fane|Kitty]] is once again seduced by [[charles-townsend|Townsend]] — not because she still loves him (she now sees him clearly) but from a complex of weariness, self-contempt, and the gravitational pull of old patterns (076-006). The morning after fills her with "a panic of [[humiliation|shame]]" (076-008) that precipitates her final departure for [[london|England]] (076-015).
