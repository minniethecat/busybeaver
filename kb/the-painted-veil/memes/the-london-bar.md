# The London Bar

## Overview

The London Bar is the professional institution that employed — and disappointed — [[mr-garstin|Bernard Garstin]]. His failure to rise to the High Court (008-002), and his wife's [[bitterness|resentment]] of that failure, shapes the family dynamics that drive the novel's opening situation. The Bar represents the professional hierarchy that values certain kinds of success and is indifferent to quieter virtues (008-001).
