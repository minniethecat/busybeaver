# Doris's Engagement

## Overview

[[doris-dennison|Doris]], at eighteen, secures "quite a good marriage" to a baronet's son (010-017). The announcement is devastating to [[kitty-fane|Kitty]], who at twenty-five is still single and watching her younger sister succeed where she has failed. The pressure — from her mother, from society, from her own [[fear|terror]] of being left "on the shelf" — drives her to accept [[walter-fane|Walter Fane]]'s proposal in desperation (011-032).
