# The French Catholic Mission

## Overview

The French Catholic mission is the larger organization that established and sustains the [[mei-tan-fu|Mei-tan-fu]] convent (022-020). The [[mother-superior|Mother Superior]] mentions having served in Hanoi before coming to [[mei-tan-fu|Mei-tan-fu]]. Unlike the Protestant missionaries who take regular leave, these French nuns come to China knowing they have left their homes forever — theirs is a vocation of permanent exile (039-018). The mission represents an institutional alternative to the colonial government — organized around faith and service rather than administration and power.
