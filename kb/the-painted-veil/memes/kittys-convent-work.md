# Kitty's Work at the Convent

## Overview

[[kitty-fane|Kitty]] begins visiting the convent out of boredom and curiosity (039-001), but the work — caring for cholera orphans, helping with the sewing, assisting in [[the-hospital|the infirmary]] — gradually becomes the center of her life in [[mei-tan-fu|Mei-tan-fu]]. For the first time, she is valued for what she does rather than how she looks (044-002). This experience of useful work is the foundation of her moral transformation (044-001).

> "You see, you and I are the only people here who walk quite quietly and peaceably on solid ground. The nuns walk in heaven and your husband—in darkness."
>
> (037-002)
