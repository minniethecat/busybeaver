# British Army Garrison

## Overview

The British Army maintains a garrison in [[hong-kong|Hong Kong]] and a presence in outlying posts including [[mei-tan-fu|Mei-tan-fu]]. It appears in the novel only at the margins — mentioned in passing, its officers attending the same social functions as the civilian administrators. Its presence is the unremarked foundation on which colonial society rests.
