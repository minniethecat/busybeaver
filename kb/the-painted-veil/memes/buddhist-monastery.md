# Buddhist Monastery

A Buddhist monastery located ten miles from Mei-tan-fu, described as a place of pilgrimage with some reputation. Kitty Fane and Waddington visited it on a Sunday excursion during her time at the convent. (053-001)

The monastery consisted of straggling low buildings by the side of the river, shaded by trees. The monks led visitors through empty courtyards to temples with grimacing gods, and in the sanctuary sat a remote, wistful Buddha. The site carried an atmosphere of decay and resignation, the magnificence shoddy and ruined, the gods dusty, the faith that made them dying. (053-002)
