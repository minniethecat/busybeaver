# Walter's Cholera Work

## Overview

In [[mei-tan-fu|Mei-tan-fu]], [[walter-fane|Walter]] throws himself into the cholera response with a dedication that impresses everyone. The epidemic had already killed the missionary doctor whom Walter was sent to replace — the place was notorious for cholera (022-019). A French convent and the missionary's empty house were what awaited him (022-020). Walter organizes sanitation measures, treats the sick, and conducts bacteriological research to understand the outbreak. The nuns "swear by him" (038-037). His work saves lives and earns him the respect that his personality never could. It also, ultimately, kills him — he contracts cholera, presumably through a laboratory accident.
