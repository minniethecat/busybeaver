# Chapter II

[002-001]He did as she bade and in five minutes she joined him. He was smoking a cigarette.

[002-002]“I say, could I have a brandy and soda?”

[002-003]“Yes, I’ll ring.”

[002-004]“I don’t think it would hurt *you* by the look of things.”

[002-005]They waited in silence for the boy to answer. She gave the order.

[002-006]“Ring up the laboratory and ask if [[walter-fane|Walter]] is there,” she said then. “They won’t know your voice.”

[002-007]He took up the receiver and asked for the number. He inquired whether Dr. Fane was in. He put down the receiver.

[002-008]“He hasn’t been in since tiffin,” he told her. “Ask the boy whether he has been here.”

[002-009]“I daren’t. It’ll look so funny if he has and I didn’t see him.”

[002-010]The boy brought the drinks and [[charles-townsend|Townsend]] helped himself. When he offered her some she shook her head.

[002-011]“What’s to be done if it was Walter?” she asked.

[002-012]“Perhaps he wouldn’t care.”

[002-013]“Walter?”

[002-014]Her tone was incredulous.

[002-015]“It’s always struck me he was rather shy. Some men can’t bear scenes, you know. He’s got sense enough to know that there’s nothing to be gained by making a scandal. I don’t believe for a minute it was Walter, but even if it was my impression is that he’ll do nothing. I think he’ll ignore it.”

[002-016]She reflected for a moment.

[002-017]“He’s awfully in love with me.”

[002-018]“Well, that’s all to the good. You’ll get round him.”

[002-019]He gave her that charming smile of his which she had always found so irresistible. It was a slow smile which started in his clear blue eyes and travelled by perceptible degrees to his shapely mouth. He had small white even teeth. It was a very sensual smile and it made her heart melt in her body.

[002-020]“I don’t very much care,” she said, with a flash of gaiety. “It was worth it.”

[002-021]“It was my fault.”

[002-022]“Why did you come? I was amazed to see you.”

[002-023]“I couldn’t resist it.”

[002-024]“You dear.”

[002-025]She leaned a little towards him, her dark and shining eyes gazing passionately into his, her mouth a little open with [[passion|desire]], and he put his arms round her. She abandoned herself with a sigh of ecstasy to their shelter.

[002-026]“You know you can always count on me,” he said.

[002-027]“I’m so happy with you. I wish I could make you as happy as you make me.”

[002-028]“You’re not frightened any more?”

[002-029]“I hate Walter,” she answered.

[002-030]He did not quite know what to say to this, so he kissed her. Her face was very soft against his.

[002-031]But he took her wrist on which was a little gold watch and looked at the time.

[002-032]“Do you know what I must do now?”

[002-033]“Bolt?” she smiled.

[002-034]He nodded. For one instant she clung to him more closely, but she felt his desire to go, and she released him.

[002-035]“It’s shameful the way you neglect your work. Be off with you.”

[002-036]He could never resist the temptation to flirt.

[002-037]“You seem in a devil of a hurry to get rid of me,” he said lightly.

[002-038]“You know that I hate to let you go.”

[002-039]Her answer was low and deep and serious. He gave a flattered laugh.

[002-040]“Don’t worry your pretty little head about our mysterious visitor. I’m quite sure it was the amah. And if there’s any trouble I guarantee to get you out of it.”

[002-041]“Have you had a lot of experience?”

[002-042]His smile was amused and complacent.

[002-043]“No, but I flatter myself that I’ve got a head screwed on my shoulders.”
