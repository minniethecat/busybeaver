# Moral Universe

George MacDonald was both a novelist and a Christian minister. *At the Back of [[north-wind|the North Wind]]* weaves a rich tapestry of moral and spiritual ideas — suffering and redemption, trust and courage, wonder and love. This index organizes the concepts and figures that carry the novel's moral vision.
