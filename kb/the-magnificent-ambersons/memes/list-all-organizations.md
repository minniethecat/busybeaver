# All Organizations

Beyond the Amberson family itself, the world of the novel is populated by organizations that reflect Midland's social and economic life: the Amberson Motor Company embodies both Eugene Morgan's inventive genius and the family's failed attempt to enter the modern economy; the Women's Tennyson Club and the F.O.T.A. reveal the town's cultural aspirations and its youth's secret societies.
