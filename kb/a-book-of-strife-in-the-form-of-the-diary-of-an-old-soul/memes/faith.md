# Faith

## Definition

In [[george-macdonald|MacDonald]]'s [[worship|devotion]]al poetry, faith is both a gift from [[god|God]] and
a [[strife|struggle]] of the human will. It is not mere [[mind|intellect]]ual assent but
a lived trust in the [[presence|divine presence]] through darkness and doubt.

## Key Passages

From January 9 (001-009):

> I search my heart--I search, and find no faith.
> Hidden He may be in its many folds--
> I see him not revealed in all the world
> Duty's firm shape thins to a misty wraith.
> No good seems likely. To and fro I am hurled.
> I have no stay. Only obedience holds:--
> I haste, I rise, I do the thing he saith.
