**Need** in MacDonald's devotional poetry is the soul's recognition of its absolute dependence on God. It is not a deficiency to be ashamed of but the very condition for receiving divine help.

## Present Need

The speaker lives in the reality of present spiritual need, not past sufficiency or future hope:

> Only for the present spiritual need. (008-007)

## Need as Openness

Spiritual need creates the opening through which God's grace enters. The [[soul]] that knows its need is ready to receive. MacDonald presents need not as poverty but as the authentic posture of the creature before the Creator.

Human need stands in contrast to [[self-sufficiency]] and [[pride]]. It is aligned with [[humility]] and [[dependence]].
