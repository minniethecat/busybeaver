**Conscience** in MacDonald's poetry is the inner moral faculty — God's voice within the [[soul]] that guides, warns, and judges. It is not merely human psychology but a channel of divine communication.

## Conscience as Guide

The conscience acts as an internal compass, directing the [[soul]] through the stormy seas of life:

> My conscience bears me through the breasting seas. (001-013)

## Conscience and Obedience

Conscience is intimately linked to [[obedience]]. To heed conscience is to obey [[god|God]]; to ignore it is to walk in [[darkness]]. The conscience is the proximate voice of [[law|divine law]] within the human [[heart]].

## Conscience and Spiritual Growth

A sensitive conscience is a sign of spiritual health. As the [[soul]] draws nearer to God, conscience becomes more acute, detecting subtler forms of [[sin]] and [[self-deception]].
