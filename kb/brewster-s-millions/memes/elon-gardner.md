# Elon Gardner
## Character Overview
Elon Gardner is Brewsters friend and stockbroker, the man through whom Brewster attempts to lose money in the stock market — with famously perverse results. Gardner "stealthily watched the stock market and plied his friends with questions" (010-006), providing the financial expertise that Brewster hopes will accelerate his losses.
## Role in the Novel
Gardner is an unintentional antagonist. His market advice leads Brewster into investments that, contrary to all expectation, prosper. When Lumber and Fuel rises dramatically, it is Gardner who delivers the unwelcome news. His character illustrates the novels theme of perverse luck: even expert speculation cannot guarantee the losses Brewster needs.
