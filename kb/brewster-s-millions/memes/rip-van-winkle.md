# "Rip" Van Winkle
## Character Overview
"Rip" Van Winkle is a steadfast member of Brewsters inner circle, appearing alongside "Subway" Smith throughout the novel. His nickname evokes the Washington Irving character, suggesting a genial, easygoing temperament. When conflicts arise in Brewsters social orbit, it is often Van Winkle who intervenes: "Rip Van Winkle interfered and peace was restored" (023-008).
## Role in the Novel
Van Winkle serves as a stabilizing influence. Brewster confides in him and Smith as his most trusted friends (023-006). His loyalty, like that of the other friends, does not require understanding — it survives Brewsters inexplicable spending year intact.
