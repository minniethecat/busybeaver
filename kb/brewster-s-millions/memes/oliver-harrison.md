## Overview

Oliver Harrison, affectionately nicknamed 「Nopper」, is one of Montgomery Brewster's closest friends and a member of the 「Little Sons of the Rich」. He later becomes Brewster's personal secretary and superintendent of affairs during the million-dollar spending campaign, managing the practical logistics of Brewster's extravagant lifestyle.

## Role in the Story

Harrison first appears at Brewster's twenty-fifth birthday dinner at Pettingill's studio, where he is among the nine friends celebrating the occasion (001-015). His lively personality is evident from his singing and joking throughout the evening (001-029). When Ellis brings news of Edwin Peter Brewster's death, Harrison rushes to offer him a nightcap, displaying his characteristic humor even in serious moments (001-034).

Two days after the grandfather's death, Harrison stirs Brewster out early, and Brewster confides in him about his new fortune — it was Harrison who would have described the windfall as having 「realized」(002-002, 002-006).

When Brewster embarks on his spending campaign, Harrison is employed as 「superintendent of affairs」 alongside Elon Gardner, who serves as financial secretary (005-071). Harrison leases offices, manages day-to-day expenses, and handles the practical arrangements for Brewster's increasingly extravagant lifestyle (006-002). At the end of the challenge, Harrison travels to Europe to meet Brewster at Holland House in London, bringing news of the successful completion of the spending campaign (034-039).

## Relationships

Harrison is part of Brewster's innermost circle, alongside 「Rip」 Van Winkle, Subway Smith, and Elon Gardner. His loyalty to Brewster is demonstrated when he travels to London to deliver the final accounting. He works closely with Elon Gardner, the financial secretary, dividing the administrative labour of Brewster's campaign between operations and finance (005-071).

## Significance

Harrison represents the loyal friend who becomes indispensable to Brewster's scheme — he is both a confidant and a facilitator. His nickname 「Nopper」 humanises the character, suggesting a familiar warmth among the 「Little Sons」. Through Harrison, the novel depicts the practical machinery behind Brewster's seemingly whimsical spending: leases, office rentals, and the day-to-day management of excess.
