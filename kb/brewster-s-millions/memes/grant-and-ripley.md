# Grant & Ripley
## Overview
Grant & Ripley is the law firm that serves as intermediary between [[swearengen-jones|the executor]] [[swearengen-jones|Swearengen Jones]] in [[montana|Montana]] and the heir [[montgomery-brewster|Montgomery Brewster]] in [[new-york-city|New York]]. [[mr-grant|Mr. Grant]] delivers the news of Sedgwicks death and the terms of his will, and the firm continues to advise Brewster throughout his spending year.
## In the Novel
[[mr-grant|Mr. Grant]] is the bearer of extraordinary news. He reveals that Sedgwicks [[inheritance|estate]] is "valued at almost seven million dollars" (004-006) and explains the conditions that govern its disposition. The firms role is both legal and dramatic — they are the channel through which the dead Sedgwick communicates his challenge to the living Brewster.
