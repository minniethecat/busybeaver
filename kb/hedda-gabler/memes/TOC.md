## Overview


> "Hedda Gabler is a play written by Norwegian playwright Henrik Ibsen, first published in 1890."
>
> (001-003)

*Hedda Gabler* is a four-act play by Henrik Ibsen, completed in 1890 and first published in Copenhagen the same year. (001-003)(001-017) The play unfolds over approximately twenty-four hours in the Tesman villa in the west end of Christiania (now Oslo). (002-139) Ibsen structured the work with tight dramatic compression, concentrating the action into a series of psychological confrontations that drive the narrative toward its tragic conclusion. (002-095)
