Copenhagen was the city of publication and early performance for *Hedda Gabler*. After completing the manuscript in Munich, Ibsen sent it to Copenhagen for publication. (001-017) The play was published there on December 16, 1890. (001-079)

## Early Performances

The play was seen in Copenhagen on February 25, 1891, with Fru Hennings in the role of Hedda. (001-086) This Copenhagen production occurred in the same month as the Christiania premiere, establishing the play rapidly across Scandinavia.
