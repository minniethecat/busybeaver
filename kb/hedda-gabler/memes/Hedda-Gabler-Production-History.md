# Hedda Gabler: Production History

_Hedda Gabler_ premiered at the Residenz Theater, Munich, on January 31, 1891, with Frau Conrad-Ramlo in the title role (001-083).


> "The first performance took place at the Residenz Theater, Munich, on the last day of January 1891."
>
> (001-083)

The first Norwegian Hedda was Froken Constance Bruun (001-088); the first French Hedda was Mlle. Marthe Brandes (001-105).

Notable English-language Heddas include Elizabeth Robins (London, 1891), Eleonora Duse, Mrs. Patrick Campbell, and Alla Nazimova — making the role a benchmark for tragic actresses worldwide (001-098).

Robins' performance was particularly significant: it established the role's reputation in the English-speaking world. Each generation of actresses has reinvented the character according to its own understanding of female psychology, ensuring the play's continuing relevance on stage.
