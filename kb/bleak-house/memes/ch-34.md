[034-001] “Now, what,” says [[george-rouncewell|Mr. George]], “may this be? Is it blank cartridge or

[034-002] ball? A flash in the pan or a shot?”

[034-003] An open letter is the subject of the trooper’s speculations, and it

[034-004] seems to perplex him mightily. He looks at it at arm’s length, brings

[034-005] it close to him, holds it in his right hand, holds it in his left

[034-006] hand, reads it with his head on this side, with his head on that

[034-007] side, contracts his eyebrows, elevates them, still cannot satisfy

[034-008] himself. He smooths it out upon the table with his heavy palm, and
