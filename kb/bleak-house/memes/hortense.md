# Hortense

> **Stub.** This page will be expanded during [[GROW]] Phase 2.1-B.

Hortense is [[lady-dedlock|Lady Dedlock]]'s French maid, dismissed from her service at [[chesney-wold|Chesney Wold]]. Her simmering resentment at this dismissal leads her to murder [[mr-tulkinghorn|Mr. Tulkinghorn]] in his [[lincolns-inn|Lincoln's Inn Fields]] chambers — a crime for which Lady Dedlock is initially suspected. Her arrest by [[inspector-bucket|Inspector Bucket]] in Chapter 54 reveals the novel's central irony: Tulkinghorn, who accumulated the secrets of the powerful, was killed by the resentment of the powerless.
