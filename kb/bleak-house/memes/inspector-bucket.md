# Inspector Bucket

> **Stub.** This page will be expanded during [[GROW]] Phase 2.1-B.

Inspector Bucket is a detective from the Metropolitan Police and one of the earliest professional detectives in English fiction. He investigates the murder of [[mr-tulkinghorn|Mr. Tulkinghorn]] and leads the pursuit of [[lady-dedlock|Lady Dedlock]] across the winter landscape. Though methodical and officious, he demonstrates genuine compassion in his treatment of the dying [[jo-crossing-sweeper|Jo]].
