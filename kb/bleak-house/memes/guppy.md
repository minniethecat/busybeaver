# Mr. Guppy

> **Stub.** This page will be expanded during [[GROW]] Phase 2.1-B.

Mr. Guppy is a junior clerk at [[kenge-and-carboy|Kenge and Carboy]], the firm of solicitors handling the [[jarndyce-and-jarndyce|Jarndyce and Jarndyce]] suit. His comic courtship of [[esther-summerson|Esther Summerson]] — proposed in Chapter 9 and withdrawn in Chapter 35 after her disfigurement by smallpox — provides some of the novel's most sharply observed social comedy. Beneath the comedy, however, Guppy represents the social mobility and moral compromise that the Chancery system enables: a young man on the make, learning to navigate a world where professional courtesy masks institutional predation.
