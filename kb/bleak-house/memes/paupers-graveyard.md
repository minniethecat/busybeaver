# Paupers' Graveyard
## Overview
The burial ground where Captain Hawdon (Nemo) lies in a pestilential pit — the site of Lady Dedlock's final destination, where she dies at the gates, joining her lover in death what she could not in life.
## In the Novel
Paupers' Graveyard is significant in the novel's topography of Victorian England.
