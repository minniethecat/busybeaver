[049-001] A great annual occasion has come round in the establishment of Mr.

[049-002] Matthew Bagnet, otherwise Lignum Vitae, ex-artilleryman and present

[049-003] bassoon-player. An occasion of feasting and festival. The celebration

[049-004] of a birthday in the family.

[049-005] It is not Mr. Bagnet’s birthday. Mr. Bagnet merely distinguishes that

[049-006] epoch in the musical instrument business by kissing the children with

[049-007] an extra smack before breakfast, smoking an additional pipe after

[049-008] dinner, and wondering towards evening what his poor old mother is

[049-009] thinking about it—a subject of infinite speculation, and rendered so

[049-010] by his mother having departed this life twenty years. Some men rarely

[049-011] revert to their father, but seem, in the bank-books of their

[049-012] remembrance, to have transferred all the stock of filial affection

[049-013] into their mother’s name. Mr. Bagnet is one of these. Perhaps his

[049-014] exalted appreciation of the merits of the old girl causes him usually

[049-015] to make the noun-substantive “goodness” of the feminine gender.

[049-016] It is not the birthday of one of the three children. Those occasions

[049-017] are kept with some marks of distinction, but they rarely overleap the

[049-018] bounds of happy returns and a pudding. On young Woolwich’s last

[049-019] birthday, Mr. Bagnet certainly did, after observing on his growth and

[049-020] general advancement, proceed, in a moment of profound reflection on

[049-021] the changes wrought by time, to examine him in the catechism,

[049-022] accomplishing with extreme accuracy the questions number one and two,

[049-023] “What is your name?” and “Who gave you that name?” but there failing

[049-024] in the exact precision of his memory and substituting for number

[049-025] three the question “And how do you like that name?” which he

[049-026] propounded with a sense of its importance, in itself so edifying and

[049-027] improving as to give it quite an orthodox air. This, however, was a

[049-028] speciality on that particular birthday, and not a general solemnity.

[049-037] inhabitants of any coop in Europe. Returning with these triumphs of

[049-046] illustrious for his cookery, this may be supposed to be a matter of

[049-047] state rather than enjoyment on the old girl’s part, but she keeps her

[049-048] state with all imaginable cheerfulness.
