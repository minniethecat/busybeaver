# Cook's Court
## Overview
The small street near Chancery Lane where Mr. Snagsby, the law-stationer, keeps his shop — a site of modest domesticity shadowed by the legal district's moral murk.
## In the Novel
Cook's Court is significant in the novel's topography of Victorian England.
