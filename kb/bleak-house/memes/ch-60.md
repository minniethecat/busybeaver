[060-001] I proceed to other passages of my narrative. From the goodness of all

[060-002] about me I derived such consolation as I can never think of unmoved.

[060-005] not a long one; and I would avoid even this mention of it if I could

[060-006] quite keep down the recollection of their sympathy.
