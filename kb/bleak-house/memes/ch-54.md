[054-001] Refreshed by sleep, Mr. Bucket rises betimes in the morning and

[054-002] prepares for a field-day. Smartened up by the aid of a clean shirt

[054-003] and a wet hairbrush, with which instrument, on occasions of ceremony,

[054-005] severe study, Mr. Bucket lays in a breakfast of two mutton chops as a

[054-008] strengthening matters and having held subtle conference with his

[054-010] quietly to [[sir-leicester-dedlock|Sir Leicester Dedlock]], Baronet, that whenever he’s ready

[054-011] for me, I’m ready for him.” A gracious message being returned that

[054-013] library within ten minutes, Mr. Bucket repairs to that apartment and

[054-014] stands before the fire with his finger on his chin, looking at the

[054-017] but composed, sure, confident. From the expression of his face he
