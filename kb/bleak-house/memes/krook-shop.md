# Krook's Shop
## Overview
Krook's rag-and-bone shop on Chancery Lane — a chaos of waste paper, legal documents, bones, and bottles that is explicitly described as a parody of the Court of Chancery, with Krook as its 'Lord Chancellor.'
## In the Novel
Krook's Shop is significant in the novel's topography of Victorian England.
