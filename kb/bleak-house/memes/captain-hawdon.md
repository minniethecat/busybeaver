# Captain Hawdon

> **Stub.** This page will be expanded during [[GROW]] Phase 2.1-B.

Captain Hawdon is the former lover of [[lady-dedlock|Lady Dedlock]] and the father of [[esther-summerson|Esther Summerson]]. Living under the alias "Nemo" (Latin: "no one"), he works as a law-writer in [[krook-shop|Krook's shop]] and dies of an opium overdose before the novel's midpoint. His handwriting — recognized by Lady Dedlock on a legal affidavit — is the catalyst for the entire detective plot of *Bleak House*.
