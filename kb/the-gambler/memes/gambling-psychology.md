## About This Index

The gambling-psychology intersection is the novel's central thematic axis. Alexei Ivanovitch's narration moves between the cold logic of betting systems and the fever-dream of compulsion — these pages trace that arc. See also the [Psychological Themes](psychological-themes) and [Roulette Mechanics](roulette-mechanics) indexes for each dimension separately.
