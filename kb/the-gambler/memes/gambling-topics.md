# Gambling Topics

This page aggregates all gambling-related entries in *The Gambler*. Dostoyevsky wrote the novel in part to pay off his own gambling debts, and its depiction of roulette's grip on the human mind remains one of literature's most unflinching portraits of compulsion. The entries span game mechanics, psychological states, social rituals, and the financial calculus that drives — and destroys — the players.
