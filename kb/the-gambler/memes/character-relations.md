# Character Relations

The social world of *The Gambler* is a tight web of dependence, rivalry, and desire. Alexei Ivanovitch, the tutor-narrator, sits at the centre of this web — loved by Polina, employed by the General, confided in by Mr. Astley, and despised by the Marquis. This page indexes wiki entries mapped to character types and relationship dynamics.

Pages listed here carry tags that describe character roles and relational positions: `love`, `schemer`, `supporting`, `minor`, and `aristocrat`.
