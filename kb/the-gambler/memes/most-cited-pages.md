# Most Cited Pages

This page ranks wiki entries by how frequently they are referenced from other pages — a measure of their centrality to the novel's world. A high citation count indicates an entity or concept that other pages consistently link to, reflecting its importance in *The Gambler*.

The table below updates automatically as pages gain new references through wikification and enrichment.

::: query
display: table
fields: [label, type, total_refs, quality]
field_labels:
  label: Page
  type: Type
  total_refs: Citations
  quality: Quality Tier
sort: total_refs
order: desc
limit: 30
:::

## About Citation Counts

`total_refs` is a Wiki engine statistic — the number of distinct pages that contain a wikilink to this entry. It is recomputed each time the backlinks index is rebuilt. Pages with the highest citation counts tend to be major characters, central concepts (roulette, gambling), and the primary setting (Roulettenberg).
