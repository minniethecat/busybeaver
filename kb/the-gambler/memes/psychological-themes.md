# Psychological Themes

*The Gambler* is as much a psychological study as a gambling novel. Dostoyevsky maps the interior landscape of compulsion with clinical precision: the fever of anticipation, the trance at the table, the self-loathing that follows loss, and the irrational certainty that the next spin will redeem everything.
