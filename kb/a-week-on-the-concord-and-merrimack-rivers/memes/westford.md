## Description

Westford is a town in Middlesex County, Massachusetts, located west of the [[merrimack-river|Merrimack River]]. During [[henry-david-thoreau|Thoreau]]'s era, Westford was known for its granite quarries, which supplied the distinctive [[chelmsford|Chelmsford]] granite used in construction throughout the region. (003-1828)

## On the River Route

In the Sunday chapter, [[henry-david-thoreau|Thoreau]] observes the geological character of the river valley, noting the [[chelmsford|Chelmsford]] granite quarried in Westford and neighboring towns. This observation reflects his interest in the material foundations of the [[nature|landscape]] — the stone that shaped the built environment along the [[merrimack-river|Merrimack]]. (003-1828)
