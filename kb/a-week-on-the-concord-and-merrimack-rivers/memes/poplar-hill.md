## Description

Poplar Hill is an elevation near the [[concord-river-mass|Concord River]] in Massachusetts, situated between Ponkawtasset and the [[great-meadows|Great Meadows]]. It served as a local landmark along the [[concord-river-mass|Concord River]] route. (002-123)

## On the River Route

[[henry-david-thoreau|Thoreau]] mentions Poplar Hill on Saturday, the first day of the journey, as the brothers rowed [[history|past]] this landmark on their way from [[concord-mass|Concord]] toward the [[merrimack-river|Merrimack River]]. The hill marked a point along the river where the [[nature|landscape]] opened into the [[great-meadows|Great Meadows]]. (002-123)
