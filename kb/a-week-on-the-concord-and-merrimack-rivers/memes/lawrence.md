## Description

Lawrence is a city in Essex County, Massachusetts, located on the [[merrimack-river|Merrimack River]]. During [[henry-david-thoreau|Thoreau]]'s time, Lawrence was emerging as a major industrial center for textile manufacturing, powered by the [[merrimack-river|Merrimack River]]'s falls. (003-1213)

## On the River Route

[[henry-david-thoreau|Thoreau]] mentions Lawrence in the Sunday chapter as one of the industrial cities along the [[merrimack-river|Merrimack River]], listed alongside [[newburyport|Newburyport]], [[haverhill|Haverhill]], [[lowell-mass|Lowell]], and [[nashua|Nashua]]. These cities exemplified the transformation of the river from a natural waterway into an engine of American industry. (003-1213)
