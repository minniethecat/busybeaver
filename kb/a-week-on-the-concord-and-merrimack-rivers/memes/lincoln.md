## Description

Lincoln is a town in Middlesex County, Massachusetts, located west of Boston. The town's boundary extends to the [[concord-river-mass|Concord River]], where it meets three other towns at a notable landmark called Bound Rock. (001-051)

## On the River Route

In the [[concord-river-mass|Concord River]] chapter, [[henry-david-thoreau|Thoreau]] describes the remarkable point where four towns — Lincoln, [[wayland|Wayland]], [[sudbury|Sudbury]], and [[concord-mass|Concord]] — converge at a single rock in the river. This geographic curiosity marks a distinctive boundary intersection on the [[concord-river-mass|Concord River]], observed early in the brothers' journey. (001-051)
