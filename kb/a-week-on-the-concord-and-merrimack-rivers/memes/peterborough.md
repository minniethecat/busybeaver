## Description

Peterborough is a town in Hillsborough County, southern New Hampshire, situated in the [[monadnock|Monadnock]] Region. The town is known for the Peterborough Hills, a range of elevations that form part of the New England upland [[nature|landscape]]. (004-1219)

## On the River Route

[[henry-david-thoreau|Thoreau]] mentions the Peterborough Hills in the Monday chapter, during a poetic [[thought|reflection]] on the distant sources of rivers and the hills that feed them. The hills are invoked as part of a broader meditation on the [[nature|landscape]] that surrounds the [[concord-mass|Concord]] and [[merrimack-river|Merrimack]] river system. (004-1219)
