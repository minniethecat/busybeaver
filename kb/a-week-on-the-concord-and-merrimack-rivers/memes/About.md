# About This Wiki

## Source Material

This wiki is built from *A Week on the [[concord-mass|Concord]] and [[merrimack-river|Merrimack River]]s* by [[henry-david-thoreau|Henry David Thoreau]], first published in 1849 by James Munroe and Company, Boston. The book recounts a boat trip [[henry-david-thoreau|Thoreau]] took with his brother John in 1839, traveling from [[concord-massachusetts|Concord, Massachusetts]], up the [[concord-river-mass|Concord River]] to the [[middlesex-canal|Middlesex Canal]], then up the [[merrimack-river|Merrimack River]] and back.

## Usage Notes

This wiki serves as a structured knowledge base for readers, scholars, and enthusiasts of [[henry-david-thoreau|Thoreau]]'s work. Each chapter is broken into individually referenceable paragraphs using PN (paragraph number) citations. Cross-referenced entity pages for concepts, people, places, and events mentioned in the text enrich the reading experience.
