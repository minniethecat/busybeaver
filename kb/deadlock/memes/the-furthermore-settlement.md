## Introduction

The Furthermore Settlement is a cultural and educational institution that hosts [[dr-mchibbert|Dr. McHibbert]]'s lecture in Chapter VII of _Deadlock_. It is described as having a mission "to bring culture among the people" (007-003).

## The Lecture

Dr. McHibbert's lecture at the Furthermore Settlement is the occasion for Miriam Henderson's meeting with [[lucie-duclaux|Lucie Duclaux]]. The Settlement's cultural mission — bringing intellectual life to a broader public — provides the setting for one of the novel's key encounters and an extended philosophical discussion.

## The Settlement Movement

The Furthermore Settlement reflects the historical Settlement movement of late Victorian and Edwardian Britain, which established cultural and educational institutions in working-class areas. It represents the intersection of social reform, education, and cultural outreach that characterized progressive circles of the period.
