# Religion and Spirituality

Religious faith and spiritual questioning permeate *Deadlock*. Miriam Henderson encounters Judaism through the Shatov family, contemplates Christianity as a cultural inheritance, and navigates her own evolving relationship to religious practice. These pages explore the spiritual and theological dimensions of the novel.
