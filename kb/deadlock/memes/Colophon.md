[014-002]_Deadlock_

[014-003]was published in 1921 by

[014-004][Dorothy M. Richardson](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Dorothy_M._Richardson).

[014-005]This ebook was produced for

[014-006][Standard Ebooks](https://standardebooks.org/)

[014-007]by

[014-008][Ray Ryan](http://rjrjr.com/),

[014-009]and is based on a transcription produced in 2018 by

[014-010]**Mary Glenn Krause** , **An Anonymous Volunteer** , **Jens Sadowski** , and [Distributed Proofreaders](https://www.pgdp.net/)

[014-011]for

[014-012][Project Gutenberg](https://www.gutenberg.org/ebooks/56925)

[014-013]and on digital scans from the

[014-014][Internet Archive](https://archive.org/details/deadlock00rich).

[014-015]The cover page is adapted from

[014-016]_Chez le père Lathuille_ ,

[014-017]a painting completed in 1879 by

[014-018][Édouard Manet](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/%C3%89douard_Manet).

[014-019]The cover and title pages feature the

[014-020]**League Spartan** and **Sorts Mill Goudy**

[014-021]typefaces created in 2014 and 2009 by

[014-022][The League of Moveable Type](https://www.theleagueofmoveabletype.com/).
