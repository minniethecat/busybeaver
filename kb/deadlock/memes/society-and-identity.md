# Society and Identity

The social world of *Deadlock* encompasses Edwardian class structures, gender
roles, national identities, and the intimate negotiations of boardinghouse life.
Miriam Henderson navigates these overlapping social spheres with acute
observation, questioning the constraints they impose while seeking her own
path.
