# Art and Culture

Art in its many forms — literature, music, sculpture, architecture — runs
through *Deadlock* as a persistent preoccupation. Miriam Henderson's
sensibility is shaped by her encounters with art: the Elgin Marbles at the
British Museum, the music she hears at concerts and gatherings, the literature
she reads and discusses, and the architectural spaces she inhabits.
