# Psychology and Inner Life

Dorothy Richardson's *Deadlock* is a landmark of psychological fiction. Its stream-of-consciousness method renders the flow of Miriam Henderson's inner life with unprecedented fidelity. These pages explore the psychological themes that structure the novel's representation of mind and experience.
