# Language and Communication

Language in *Deadlock* is never merely instrumental. It is a site of cultural encounter, philosophical debate, and intimate connection — but also of misunderstanding, inadequacy, and strategic silence. These pages trace the novel's sustained meditation on what language can and cannot do.
