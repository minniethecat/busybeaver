# Gender and Relationships

Miriam Henderson's experience as a working woman in Edwardian London places gender at the centre of *Deadlock*. The novel explores the constraints and possibilities of women's lives — work, marriage, independence — through Miriam's consciousness and her relationships with the men and women around her.
