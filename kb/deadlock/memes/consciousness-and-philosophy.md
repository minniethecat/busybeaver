# Consciousness and Philosophy

*Deadlock* is celebrated for its pioneering stream-of-consciousness technique,
but the novel is equally a work of philosophical inquiry. Through Miriam
Henderson's reflections, Richardson explores questions of epistemology,
personal identity, the nature of meaning, and the relationship between inner
experience and external reality.
