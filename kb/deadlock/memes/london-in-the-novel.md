# London in the Novel

London is more than a setting in *Deadlock* — it is a presence that shapes
Miriam Henderson's consciousness. From the boardinghouse on Tansley Street to
the British Museum, from Wimpole Street's dental practices to Hyde Park's open
spaces, the city's geography maps onto the protagonist's inner journey.
