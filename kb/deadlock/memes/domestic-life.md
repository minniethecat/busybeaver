# Domestic Life

The boardinghouse at Tansley Street is the novel's domestic heart. Within its shabby rooms, the rhythms of meals, conversation, and solitude structure the characters' lives. These pages explore the domestic world of *Deadlock* — its spaces, rituals, and relationships.
