# Women

The experience and position of **women** is a pervasive theme in [[deadlock|Deadlock]], inflected through [[miriam-henderson|Miriam Henderson]]'s sharp observations of gender dynamics in Edwardian [[london|London]]. The novel attends closely to the lives of working women — typists, landladies, daughters-in-service — and to the unspoken assumptions that govern relations between men and women.
