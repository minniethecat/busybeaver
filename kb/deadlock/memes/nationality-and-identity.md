# Nationality and Identity

*Deadlock* is a novel of cultural encounter. Through Miriam Henderson and the international cast of characters who pass through the Tansley Street boardinghouse, Richardson explores what it means to be English, Russian, Jewish, or German — and what happens when these identities meet across a dining table.
