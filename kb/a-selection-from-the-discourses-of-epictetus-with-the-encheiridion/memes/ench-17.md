[085-001]Remember that thou art an actor in a play, of such a kind as the teacher

[085-002](author) may choose; if short, of a short one; if long, of a long one: if he

[085-003]wishes you to act the part of a poor man, see that you act the part naturally;

[085-004]if the part of a lame man, of a magistrate, of a private person, (do the same).

[085-005]For this is your duty, to act well the part that is given to you; but to select

[085-006]the part, belongs to another.
