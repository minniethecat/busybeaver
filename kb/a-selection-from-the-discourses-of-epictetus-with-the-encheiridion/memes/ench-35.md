[103-001]When you have decided that a thing ought to be done, and are doing it, never

[103-002]avoid being seen doing it, though the many shall form an unfavorable opinion

[103-003]about it. For if it is not right to do it, avoid doing the thing; but if it is

[103-004]right, why are you afraid of those who shall find fault wrongly?
