[104-001]As the proposition, it is either day, or it is night, is of great importance

[104-002]for the disjunctive argument, but for the conjunctive, is of no value, so in a

[104-003]symposium (entertainment) to select the larger share is of great value for the

[104-004]body, but for the maintenance of the social feeling is worth nothing. When,

[104-005]then, you are eating with another, remember, to look not only to the value for

[104-006]the body of the things set before you, but also to the value of the behavior

[104-007]towards the host which ought to be observed.
