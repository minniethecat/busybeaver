[086-001]When a raven has croaked inauspiciously, let not the appearance hurry you away

[086-002]with it; but straightway make a distinction in your mind and say, None of these

[086-003]things is signified to me, but either to my poor body, or to my small property,

[086-004]or to my reputation, or to my children, or to my wife: but to me all

[086-005]significations are auspicious if I choose. For whatever of these things

[086-006]results, it is in my power to derive benefit from it.
