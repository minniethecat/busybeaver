[113-001]Does a man bathe quickly (early)? do not say that he bathes badly, but that he

[113-002]bathes quickly. Does a man drink much wine? do not say that he does this badly,

[113-003]but say that he drinks much. For before you shall have determined the opinion

[113-004]how do you know whether he is acting wrong? Thus it will not happen to you to

[113-005]comprehend some appearances which are capable of being comprehended, but to

[113-006][[assent]] to others.
