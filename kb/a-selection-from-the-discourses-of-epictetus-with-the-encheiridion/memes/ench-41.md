[109-001]It is a mark of a mean capacity to spend much time on the things which concern

[109-002]the body, such as much exercise, much eating, much drinking, much easing of the

[109-003]body, much copulation. But these things should be done as subordinate things;

[109-004]and let all your care be directed to the mind.
