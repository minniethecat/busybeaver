[074-001]Be not elated at any advantage (excellence) which belongs to another. If a

[074-002]horse when he is elated should say, I am beautiful, one might endure it. But

[074-003]when you are elated, and say, I have a beautiful horse, you must know that you

[074-004]are elated at having a [[good]] horse. What then is your own? The use of

[074-005]appearances. Consequently when in the use of appearances you are conformable to

[074-006][[nature]], then be elated, for then you will be elated at something good which is

[074-007]your own.
