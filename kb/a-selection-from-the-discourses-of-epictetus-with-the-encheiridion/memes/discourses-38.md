[038-001]When one of those who were present said, Persuade me

[038-002]that logic is necessary, he replied, Do you wish me to prove this to you? The

[038-003]answer was, Yes. Then I must use a demonstrative form of speech. This was

[038-004]granted. How then will you know if I am cheating you by my argument? The man

[038-005]was silent. Do you see, said [[epictetus|Epictetus]], that you yourself are admitting that

[038-006]logic is necessary, if without it you cannot know so much as this, whether

[038-007]logic is necessary or not necessary?
