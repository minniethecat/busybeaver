[015-001]It is circumstances (difficulties)

[015-002]which show what men are. Therefore when a difficulty falls upon you, remember

[015-003]that God, like a trainer of wrestlers, has matched you with a rough young man.

[015-004]For what purpose? you may say. Why, that you may become an Olympic conqueror;

[015-005]but it is not accomplished without sweat. In my opinion no man has had a more

[015-006]profitable difficulty than you have had, if you choose to make use of it as an

[015-007]athlete would deal with a young antagonist. We are now sending a scout to [[rome|Rome]];

[015-008]but no man sends a cowardly scout, who, if he only hears a noise and sees a

[015-009]shadow anywhere, comes running back in terror and reports that the enemy is

[015-010]close at hand. So now if you should come and tell us: “Fearful is the state of

[015-011]affairs at Rome; terrible is death; terrible is exile; terrible is calumny;

[015-012]terrible is poverty; fly, my friends, the enemy is near,” we shall answer:

[015-013]“Begone, prophesy for yourself; we have committed only one fault, that we sent

[015-014]such a scout.”

[015-015]Diogenes, who was sent as a scout before you, made a different report to us. He

[015-016]says that death is no evil, for neither is it base; he says that fame

[015-017](reputation) is the noise of madmen. And what has this spy said about pain,

[015-018]about pleasure, and about poverty? He says that to be naked is better than any

[015-019]purple robe, and to sleep on the bare ground is the softest bed; and he gives

[015-020]as a proof of each thing that he affirms his own courage, his tranquillity, his

[015-021]freedom, and the healthy appearance and compactness of his body. There is no

[015-022]enemy near, he says; all is peace. How so, Diogenes? “See,” he replies, “if I

[015-023]am struck, if I have been wounded, if I have fled from any man.” This is what a

[015-024]scout ought to be. But you come to us and tell us one thing after another. Will

[015-025]you not go back, and you will see clearer when you have laid aside fear?
