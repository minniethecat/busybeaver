[107-001]The measure of possession (property) is to every man the body, as the foot is

[107-002]of the shoe. If then you stand on this rule (the demands of the body), you will

[107-003]maintain the measure; but if you pass beyond it, you must then of necessity be

[107-004]hurried as it were down a precipice. As also in the matter of the shoe, if you

[107-005]go beyond the (necessities of the) foot, the shoe is gilded, then of a purple

[107-006]color, then embroidered; for there is no limit to that which has once passed

[107-007]the true measure.
