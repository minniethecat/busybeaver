[078-001]On the occasion of every accident (event) that befalls you, remember to turn to

[078-002]yourself and inquire what power you have for turning it to use. If you see a

[078-003]fair man or a fair woman, you will find that the power to resist is temperance

[078-004](continence). If labor (pain) be presented to you, you will find that it is

[078-005]endurance. If it be abusive words, you will find it to be patience. And if you

[078-006]have been thus formed to the (proper) habit, the appearances will not carry you

[078-007]along with them.
