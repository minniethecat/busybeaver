[108-001]Women forthwith from the age of fourteen are called by the men mistresses

[108-002]([Greek: churiai], dominæ). Therefore, since they see that there is nothing

[108-003]else that they can obtain, but only the power of lying with men, they begin to

[108-004]decorate themselves, and to place all their hopes in this. It is worth our

[108-005]while then to take care that they may know that they are valued (by men) for

[108-006]nothing else than appearing (being) decent and modest and discreet.
