[077-001]Disease is an impediment to the body, but not to the will, unless the will

[077-002]itself chooses. Lameness is an impediment to the leg, but not to the will. And

[077-003]add this reflection on the occasion of everything that happens; for you will

[077-004]find it an impediment to something else, but not to yourself.
