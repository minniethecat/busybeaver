[081-001]If you would improve, submit to be considered without sense and foolish with

[081-002]respect to [[externals]]. Wish to be considered to know nothing; and if you shall

[081-003]seem to some to be a person of importance, distrust yourself. For you should

[081-004]know that it is not easy both to keep your will in a condition conformable to

[081-005][[nature]] and (to secure) external things: but if a man is careful about the one,

[081-006]it is an absolute necessity that he will neglect the other.
