[072-001]When you are going to take in hand any act remind yourself what kind of an act

[072-002]it is. If you are going to bathe, place before yourself what happens in the

[072-003]bath; some splashing the water, others pushing against one another, others

[072-004]abusing one another, and some stealing; and thus with more safety you will

[072-005]undertake the matter, if you say to yourself, I now intend to bathe, and to

[072-006]maintain my will in a manner conformable to [[nature]]. And so you will do in every

[072-007]act; for thus if any hindrance to bathing shall happen let this thought be

[072-008]ready. It was not this only that I intended, but I intended also to maintain my

[072-009]will in a way conformable to nature; but I shall not maintain it so if I am

[072-010]vexed at what happens.
