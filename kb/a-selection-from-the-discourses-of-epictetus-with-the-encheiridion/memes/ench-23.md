[091-001]If it should ever happen to you to be turned to [[externals]] in order to please

[091-002]some person, you must know that you have lost your purpose in life. Be

[091-003]satisfied then in everything with being a philosopher; and if you wish to seem

[091-004]also to any person to be a philosopher, appear so to yourself, and you will be

[091-005]able to do this.
