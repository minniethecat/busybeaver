[096-001]If any person was intending to put your body in the power of any man whom you

[096-002]fell in with on the way, you would be vexed; but that you put your

[096-003]understanding in the power of any man whom you meet, so that if he should

[096-004]revile you, it is disturbed and troubled, are you not ashamed at this?
