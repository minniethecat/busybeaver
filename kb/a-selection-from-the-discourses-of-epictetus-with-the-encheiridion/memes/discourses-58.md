[058-001]To this matter before all you must attend, that you be

[058-002]never so closely connected with any of your former intimates or friends as to

[058-003]come down to the same acts as he does. If you do not observe this rule, you

[058-004]will ruin yourself. But if the thought arises in your mind, “I shall seem

[058-005]disobliging to him and he will not have the same feeling towards me,” remember

[058-006]that nothing is done without cost, nor is it possible for a man if he does not

[058-007]do the same things to be the same man that he was. Choose then which of the two

[058-008]you will have, to be equally loved by those by whom you were formerly loved,

[058-009]being the same with your former self; or, being superior, not to obtain from

[058-010]your friends the same that you did before.
