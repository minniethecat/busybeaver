[088-001]Remember that it is not he who reviles you or strikes you, who insults you, but

[088-002]it is your opinion about these things as being insulting. When then a man

[088-003]irritates you, you must know that it is your own opinion which has irritated

[088-004]you. Therefore especially try not to be carried away by the appearance. For if

[088-005]you once gain time and delay, you will more easily master yourself.
