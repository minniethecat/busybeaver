[075-001]As on a voyage when the vessel has reached a port, if you go out to get water

[075-002]it is an amusement by the way to pick up a shellfish or some bulb, but your

[075-003]thoughts ought to be directed to the ship, and you ought to be constantly

[075-004]watching if the captain should call, and then you must throw away all those

[075-005]things, that you may not be bound and pitched into the ship like sheep. So in

[075-006]life also, if there be given to you instead of a little bulb and a shell a wife

[075-007]and child, there will be nothing to prevent (you from taking them). But if the

[075-008]captain should call, run to the ship and leave all those things without regard

[075-009]to them. But if you are old, do not even go far from the ship, lest when you

[075-010]are called you make default.
