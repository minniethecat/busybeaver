[071-001]In everything which pleases the soul, or supplies a want, or is loved, remember

[071-002]to add this to the (description, notion): What is the [[nature]] of each thing,

[071-003]beginning from the smallest? If you love an earthen vessel, say it is an

[071-004]earthen vessel which you love; for when it has been broken you will not be

[071-005]disturbed. If you are kissing your child or wife, say that it is a human being

[071-006]whom you are kissing, for when the wife or child dies you will not be

[071-007]disturbed.
