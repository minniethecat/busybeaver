[112-001]These reasonings do not cohere: I am richer than you, therefore I am better

[112-002]than you; I am more eloquent than you, therefore I am better than you. On the

[112-003]contrary, these rather cohere: I am richer than you, therefore my possessions

[112-004]are greater than yours; I am more eloquent than you, therefore my speech is

[112-005]superior to yours. But you are neither possession nor speech.
