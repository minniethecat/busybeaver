[111-001]Everything has two handles, the one by which it may be borne, the other by

[111-002]which it may not. If your brother acts unjustly, do not lay hold of the act by

[111-003]that handle wherein he acts unjustly, for this is the handle which cannot be

[111-004]borne; but lay hold of the other, that he is your brother, that he was nurtured

[111-005]with you, and you will lay hold of the thing by that handle by which it can be

[111-006]borne.
