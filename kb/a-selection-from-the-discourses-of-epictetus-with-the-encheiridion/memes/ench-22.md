[090-001]If you [[desire]] philosophy, prepare yourself from the beginning to be ridiculed,

[090-002]to expect that many will sneer at you, and say, He has all at once returned to

[090-003]us as a philosopher; and whence does he get this supercilious look for us? Do

[090-004]you not show a supercilious look; but hold on to the things which seem to you

[090-005]best as one appointed by God to this station. And remember that if you abide in

[090-006]the same principles, these men who first ridiculed will afterwards admire you;

[090-007]but if you shall have been overpowered by them, you will bring on yourself

[090-008]double ridicule.
