[064-001]When you see

[064-002]another man in the possession of power (magistracy), set against this the fact

[064-003]that you have not the want ([[desire]]) of power; when you see another rich, see

[064-004]what you possess in place of riches: for if you possess nothing in place of

[064-005]them, you are miserable; but if you have not the want of riches, know that you

[064-006]possess more than this man possesses and what is worth much more.
