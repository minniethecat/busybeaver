[079-001]Never say about anything, I have lost it, but say I have restored it. Is your

[079-002]child dead? It has been restored. Is your wife dead? She has been restored. Has

[079-003]your estate been taken from you? Has not then this also been restored? But he

[079-004]who has taken it from me is a bad man. But what is it to you, by whose hands

[079-005]the giver demanded it back? So long as he may allow you, take care of it as a

[079-006]thing which belongs to another, as travellers do with their inn.
