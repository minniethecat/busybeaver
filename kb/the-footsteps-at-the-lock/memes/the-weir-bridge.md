# The Weir Bridge

The footbridge at Shipcote Lock from which Nigel photographed the lock and watched the canoe drift away (002-007).

## Description

A functional stone and iron structure. Its framework later held the camera: "the camera itself had obviously been held in position by the framework of the bridge" (007-035).

## Role in the Story

The bridge serves as Nigel's alibi platform, the camera's resting place, and Bredon's reconstruction site (004-043).
