# Indescribable House

The London headquarters of the Indescribable Company. "Neither you, reader, nor I are ever likely to be shown up beyond the second floor" (004-003).

## Location

London, precise address undisclosed — fitting for a company whose name is never spoken.

## Description

Bredon is shown to the third floor. "Somewhere in the vast labyrinth of the third floor Bredon disappeared from sight" (004-003).
The narrator imagines "gold ashtrays, real oak panelling, and one or two Rubenses on the walls" (004-003).

## Role in the Story

Indescribable House is where Bredon is briefed by Sholto and Dr. Tremayne, and where the terms of the investigation are established (004-004).
