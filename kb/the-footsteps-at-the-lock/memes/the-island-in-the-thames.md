# The Island in the Thames

A small eyot in the Thames between Shipcote Lock and Eaton Bridge, searched by Bredon and Inspector Leyland (012-001).

## Location

In the main channel, accessible only by boat. A drifting canoe would pass close by.

## Description

Small, overgrown with willow and alder, fringed with reeds. Ample cover for concealment.

## Role in the Story

Bredon and Leyland conduct a thorough search, examining banks for footprints and undergrowth for discarded items. What they find — or fail to find — shapes the subsequent investigation (012-001).
