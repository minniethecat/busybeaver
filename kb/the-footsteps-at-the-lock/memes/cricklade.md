# Cricklade

The navigable limit of the Upper Thames and intended destination of Derek and Nigel's expedition (001-021). They never reached it.

## Significance

Cricklade exists in the novel only as intention, not as arrival. The journey was interrupted; the destination remained hypothetical. This mirrors the novel's deeper structure — truth is sought but never fully attained.
