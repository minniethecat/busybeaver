# Wigpole Street

The consulting-room of Dr. Simmonds, where Derek was examined and the fateful canoe trip prescribed (001-018).

## Location

In London's medical district, modeled on Wigmore Street or Wimpole Street.

## Role in the Story

Here Dr. Simmonds examined Derek, assessed his acrasia, and prescribed "open air combined with exercise" — specifically, the river: "The river, now; ever go on the river?" (001-020).
