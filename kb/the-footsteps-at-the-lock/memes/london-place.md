# London

The metropolitan counterpoint to the Thames Valley setting. London houses the Indescribable (004-003), the solicitors (015-028), the moneylenders (001-012), and Dr. Simmonds's consulting room (001-018).

## Significance

London and the Thames Valley form a spatial dialectic: London is power, money, and institutional abstraction; the river is where those abstractions become concrete.
Bredon moves between them, translating corporate imperatives into riverbank investigations.
