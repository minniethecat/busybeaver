# Bredon's Cottage

The country home of Miles and Angela Bredon: "his country cottage, and the unstated companionship of his really admirable wife" (004-002).

## Significance

The cottage is the starting point of every investigation and the place Bredon longs to return to. His detection work is an interruption of his preferred life, not its substance.
