# Section 92

[091-001]‘For rich people.’

[091-002]‘Yes, for rich people. How I pity the poor devils!--Play anything.

[091-003]Better still if you will sing, my nightingale!’

[091-004]So [[amy-reardon|Amy]] first played and then sang, and [[jasper-milvain|Jasper]] lay back in dreamy bliss.
