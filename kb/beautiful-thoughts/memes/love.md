# Love

## Definition

In Henry Drummond's thought, Love is not merely an emotion or sentiment—it is the supreme law of the spiritual world, the essence of the Christian life, and the one thing that lasts into eternity. Drummond's most famous work, *The Greatest Thing in the World*, is entirely devoted to this theme.

## Key Passages

> The greatest thing a man can do for his Heavenly Father is to be kind to some of His other children. [001-001]

Drummond argues that Love is practical, not theoretical. It manifests in specific virtues: patience, kindness, generosity, humility, courtesy, unselfishness, good temper, guilelessness, and sincerity.

> God is Love. Covet therefore that everlasting gift. [365-001]

Love is the only immortal thing. Faith and hope may pass away, but Love endures.
