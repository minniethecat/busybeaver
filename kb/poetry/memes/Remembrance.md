# Remembrance

[008-001]Remembering thee, I search out these faint flowers   

[008-002]Of rhyme; remembering thee, this crescent night   

[008-003]While o’er the buds and o’er the grass-blades, bright   

[008-004]And clinging with the dew of odorous showers,   

[008-005]With purple sandals sweep the grave-eyed hours﻿—   

[008-006]Remembering thee, I muse, while fades in flight   

[008-007]The honey-hearted leisure of the [[shadow-and-light|light]],   

[008-008]And hanging o’er the hush of willow bowers,

[008-009]Of ceaseless loneliness and high regret   

[008-010]Sings the young wistful [[soul-in-yeats|spirit]] of a star   

[008-011]Enfolden in the shadows of the East,   

[008-012]And silence holding revelry and feast;   

[008-013]Just now my soul rose up and touched it, far   

[008-014]In space, made equal with a sigh, we met.
