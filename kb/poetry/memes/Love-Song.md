# Love Song

[009-001]From the Gaelic

[009-002]My love, we will go, we will go, I and you,   

[009-003]And away in the woods we will scatter the dew;   

[009-004]And the salmon behold, and the ousel too,   

[009-005]My love, we will hear, I and you, we will hear,   

[009-006]The calling afar of the doe and the deer.   

[009-007]And the bird in the branches will cry for us clear,   

[009-008]And the cuckoo unseen in his festival mood;   

[009-009]And death, oh my fair one, will never come near   

[009-010]In the bosom afar of the fragrant wood.
