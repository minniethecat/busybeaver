# A Vision Hub

*A Vision* (1925, revised 1937) is Yeats's most elaborate prose work — a cosmological, historical, and psychological system revealed through the automatic writing of his wife George. The concepts it introduces — gyres, lunar phases, masks, the *Anima Mundi* — permeate his later poetry. This page gathers all wiki entries that engage with the Vision system.
