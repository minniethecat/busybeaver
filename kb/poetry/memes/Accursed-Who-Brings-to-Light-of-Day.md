# Accursed Who Brings to Light of Day

[036-001]Accursed who brings to [[shadow-and-light|light]] of day   

[036-002]The writings I have cast away!   

[036-003]But blessed he that stirs them not   

[036-004]And lets the kind worm take the lot!
