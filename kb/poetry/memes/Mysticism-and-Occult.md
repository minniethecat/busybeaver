# Mysticism and Occult

Yeats was a lifelong student of esoteric traditions. A member of the Hermetic Order of the Golden Dawn and a reader of Swedenborg, Boehme, and Plotinus, he saw poetry as a form of revelation and the imagination as a gateway to spiritual reality. This page indexes wiki entries on the mystical and occult dimensions of his work.
