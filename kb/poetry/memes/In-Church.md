# In Church

[010-001]She prays for father, mother dear,   

[010-002]To Him with thunder shod,   

[010-003]She prays for every falling tear   

[010-004]In the holy church of God.

[010-005]For all good men now fallen ill,   

[010-006]For merry men that weep,   

[010-007]For holiest teachers of His will,   

[010-008]And common men that sleep.

[010-009]The sunlight flickering on the pews,   

[010-010]The sunlight in the air,   

[010-011]The flies that dance in threes, in twos,   

[010-012]They seem to join her prayer﻿—

[010-013]Her prayer for father, mother dear,   

[010-014]To Him with thunder shod,   

[010-015]A prayer for every falling tear   

[010-016]In the holy church of God.
