# When You Are Sad

[030-001]When you are sad,   

[030-002]The mother of the stars weeps too,   

[030-003]And all her starlight is with sorrow mad,   

[030-004]And tears of fire fall gently in the dew.

[030-005]When you are sad,   

[030-006]The mother of the wind mourns too,   

[030-007]And her old wind that no mirth ever had,   

[030-008]Wanders and wails before my heart most true

[030-009]When you are sad,   

[030-010]The mother of the wave sighs too,   

[030-011]And her dim wave bids man be no more glad,   

[030-012]And then the whole world’s trouble weeps with you.
