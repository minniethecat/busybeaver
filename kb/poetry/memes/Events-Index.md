## About Events in Yeats

Yeats's poetry is deeply rooted in historical events — the Irish Literary Revival, the Easter Rising, the Irish War of Independence, and the Anglo-Irish Treaty all shaped his vision. This index will grow as more event pages are created during the GROW expansion.
