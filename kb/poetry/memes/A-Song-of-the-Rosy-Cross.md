# A Song of the Rosy Cross

[034-001]He who measures gain and loss,   

[034-002]When he gave to thee the [[rose-symbol|Rose]],   

[034-003]Gave to me alone the Cross;   

[034-004]Where the blood-red blossom blows   

[034-005]In a wood of dew and moss,   

[034-006]There thy wandering pathway goes,   

[034-007]Mine where waters brood and toss;   

[034-008]Yet one joy have I, hid close,   

[034-009]He who measures gain and loss,   

[034-010]When he gave to thee the Rose,   

[034-011]Gave to me alone the Cross.
