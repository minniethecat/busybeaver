# Wisdom and Dreams

[017-001]I pray that I ever be weaving   

[017-002]An intellectual tune,   

[017-003]But weaving it out of threads   

[017-004]From the distaff of the moon.

[017-005][[wisdom-in-yeats|Wisdom]] and dreams are one,   

[017-006]For dreams are the flowers ablow,   

[017-007]And Wisdom the fruit of the garden:   

[017-008]God planted him long ago.
