# Where My Books Go

[013-001]All the words that I gather,   

[013-002]And all the words that I write,   

[013-003]Must spread out their wings untiring,   

[013-004]And never rest in their flight,   

[013-005]Till they come where your sad, sad heart is,   

[013-006]And sing to you in the night,   

[013-007]Beyond where the waters are moving,   

[013-008]Storm darkened or starry bright.
