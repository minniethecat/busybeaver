# Irish Mythology

Yeats drew deeply on Irish myth and folklore throughout his career, from the early narrative poems like "The Wanderings of Usheen" to the Cúchulainn plays of his middle period. The mythological figures and cycles of ancient Ireland provided him with a national symbolic vocabulary distinct from classical and biblical traditions.
