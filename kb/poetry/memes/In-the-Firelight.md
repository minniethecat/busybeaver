# In the Firelight

[012-001]Come and dream of kings and kingdoms,   

[012-002]Cooking chestnuts on the bars﻿—   

[012-003]Round us the white roads are endless,   

[012-004]Mournful under mournful stars.

[012-005]Whisper lest we too may sadden,   

[012-006]Round us herds of shadows steal﻿—   

[012-007]Care not if beyond the shadows   

[012-008]Flieth Fortune’s furious wheel.

[012-009]Kingdoms rising, kingdoms falling,   

[012-010]Bowing nations, plumèd wars﻿—   

[012-011]Weigh them in an hour of [[dream-and-vision|dreaming]],   

[012-012]Cooking chestnuts on the bars.
