# In a Drawing-Room

[004-001]Around the twitter of the lips of dust   

[004-002]A tossing laugh between their redness ’bides﻿—   

[004-003]With patient beauty yonder Attic bust   

[004-004]In the deep alcove’s dimness smiles and hides.

[004-005]Two [[soul-in-yeats|spirit]] things a man hath for his friends:   

[004-006]Sorrow that gives for guerdon liberty,   

[004-007]And joy, the touching of whose finger lends   

[004-008]To lightest of [[shadow-and-light|light]] things all sanctity.
