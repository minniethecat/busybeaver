[050-003]was published in 1925 by

[050-004][[[andre-gide|André Gide]]](<https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Andr%C3%A9_Gide>).

[050-005]It was translated from French in 1927 by

[050-006][Dorothy Bussy](<https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Dorothy_Bussy>).

[050-007]This ebook was produced for

[050-008][Standard Ebooks](<https://standardebooks.org/>)

[050-009]by

[050-010][Alex Cabal](<https://alexcabal.com/>),

[050-011]and is based on a transcription produced in 2025 by

[050-012]**Hannah Wilson** , **Laura Natal** , and [Distributed Proofreaders](<https://www.pgdp.net/>)

[050-013]for

[050-014][Project Gutenberg](<https://www.gutenberg.org/ebooks/76965>)

[050-015]and on digital scans from

[050-016][Google Books](<https://www.google.com/books/edition/The_Counterfeiters/ElYp2GGsHv8C>).

[050-018]_Tour Eiffel_ ,

[050-020][Robert Delaunay](<https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Robert_Delaunay>).
