## Overview

[[standard-ebooks|Standard Ebooks]] is a volunteer-driven project that produces high-quality ebook editions of public domain literature. This edition of Walter White's *Flight* is a product of their work, building on a transcription from Crisis and Opportunity Books and digital scans from the HathiTrust Digital Library (P01-003). The project represents the ongoing effort to preserve and disseminate literary works in the public domain.
