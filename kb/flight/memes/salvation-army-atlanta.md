## Overview

The Salvation Army maintained a presence in [[atlanta-georgia|Atlanta]] during the period of the novel, and its workers would have been among those providing aid to the victims of the [[atlanta-race-riot-1906|1906 race riot]]. The organization's mission of service to the poor and dispossessed made it one of the few institutions that crossed the color line in its charitable work, though it operated within the constraints of the segregation era.
