## Overview

This page provides a complete alphabetical index of all event entries in the Flight wiki. Events range from major historical turning points — the Atlanta race riot of 1906, the Great Migration, World War I — to intimate personal turning points in the lives of Mimi Daquin, Jean Daquin, and the communities they inhabit. Each entry is listed with its quality tier, reference count, and chapter spread. The index is dynamically generated and stays current as new event pages are created or existing ones upgraded.

## Index

field_labels:

  total_refs: References
  total_chapters: Chapters
