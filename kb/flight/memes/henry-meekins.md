Henry Meekins is a young, enthusiastic professor invited to dine at the home of [[jimmie-forrester|Jimmie Forrester]] and [[mimi-daquin|Mimi Daquin]]. His forceful critique of Western industrialism and racial hierarchies serves as an intellectual catalyst for Mimi, confirming and giving shape to her own vague dissatisfactions.

## In the Story

### Dinner Debate

Meekins is introduced at a dinner party at the Forrester home [021-025]. He launches into a critique of industrial labor, describing the Ford factory worker who "tightening a bolt or inserting a plug over and over again, thousands of times a day" loses all creativity and individuality [021-026]. When challenged, he cheerfully agrees that the West has brought sanitation and railways to colonized peoples, but presses harder: "Do we teach them or, better, prove to them that our religion is better than theirs?" [021-031].

### Intellectual Catalyst

Meekins, "young, eager, enthusiastic, was thoroughly aroused" by the debate [021-033]. He challenges the assumption of Western superiority, asserting that other civilizations had "art and literature, ethics and philosophies of life and codes of conduct many of them much better than anything we've got" [021-038].

Mimi, who had been silent through much of the discussion, found herself "glad to hear Meekins confirm and make more concrete some of her own vague dissatisfaction" [021-039]. When Jimmie attempts to invoke a racist pseudoscientific text, Meekins "snorted him into silence" [021-042] and half-shouted his rebuttal, "forgetting social amenities in his anger" [021-043].

### Aftermath

Despite his fierce debating style, Meekins proved to be "an excellent dancer and a jovial companion" after dinner, even making Jimmie laugh reluctantly at his witticisms [021-046]. Mimi later reflected on Meekins' opinions when considering her own growing racial consciousness, remembering "his reception of the night before of Meekins' opinions" and recognizing that Jimmie would not be receptive to her own emerging views [022-039].
