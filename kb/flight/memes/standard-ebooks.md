## Overview

Standard Ebooks is a volunteer-driven project that produces high-quality, carefully formatted ebook editions of public-domain literary works. The project takes texts from sources such as Project Gutenberg and the Internet Archive, then corrects transcription errors, modernizes typography, and produces editions that meet professional publishing standards.

The edition of Walter White's *Flight* used as the source corpus for this wiki was produced by Standard Ebooks. The project's commitment to accurate transcription and careful formatting made the text available in a clean, machine-readable format suitable for the structured analysis performed by this wiki's build pipeline. The Standard Ebooks edition preserves the novel's chapter structure and paragraph divisions, enabling the PN (paragraph number) citation system used throughout these pages.

## In the Novel

Standard Ebooks does not appear in the novel itself. It is a present-day organization whose work enables the scholarly access to *Flight* that this wiki represents. The project's volunteer contributors transcribed the text, corrected errors, and produced a clean digital edition in 2024, making the novel freely available to readers and researchers.

The edition was sourced from a transcription originally prepared by [[crisis-opportunity-books|Crisis & Opportunity Books]], which itself drew upon the novel's first edition. This chain of preservation — from the original 1926 publication through the transcription to the Standard Ebooks digital edition — exemplifies the ongoing effort to keep historically significant works of African American literature in circulation.
