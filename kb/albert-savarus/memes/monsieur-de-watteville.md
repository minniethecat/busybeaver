# Monsieur de Watteville

**Monsieur de Watteville** is the common form of address for the [[baron-de-watteville|Baron de Watteville]], the passive husband of the Baronne and father of [[rosalie-de-watteville|Rosalie de Watteville]]. A gentleman of limited intelligence whose chief occupation is wood-turning, he is the unwitting instrument of Rosalie's scheme to build the [[belvedere-watteville|Belvedere]].
