# Revue de l'Est

The **Revue de l'Est** (_Eastern Review_) is the fortnightly periodical founded by [[albert-savarus|Albert Savarus]] in [[besancon|Besançon]], financed by forty shares of five hundred francs each from his first ten merchant clients. Ostensibly dedicated to promoting the economic and cultural interests of the Franche-Comté region, the journal also serves as a vehicle for Albert's political ambitions — and, secretly, as a channel of communication with [[francesca|Francesca Colonna]] in Italy.
