# Willie

The young grandson of Farmer Thoroughgood, whose gentle handling and persistent optimism help restore Black Beauty to health and dignity.

## 角色概述

Willie is the grandson of Farmer Thoroughgood, the kind farmer who buys Beauty at a horse fair when the old horse is in pitiful condition. Willie becomes Beauty's primary caretaker during the critical months of rehabilitation, demonstrating that kindness to animals is not limited by age or experience. His bond with Beauty is one of the most tender relationships in the novel's closing chapters. (052-003) (052-023)

## 故事经历

### 早期经历

Willie is introduced when his grandfather brings the worn-out Beauty home from the fair. Farmer Thoroughgood points out the horse to Willie, noting that Beauty "has known better days." From this first encounter, Willie takes a personal interest in the horse's welfare. (052-003)

### 关键转折

Under his grandfather's guidance, Willie takes on the daily responsibility of caring for Beauty. He is tasked with overseeing Beauty's gentle exercise regime, leading the horse out during the day and ensuring he gets small amounts of restorative work. Willie takes this responsibility seriously and becomes emotionally invested in Beauty's recovery. (052-023) (052-025)

As Beauty gradually regains his strength, Willie's joy is evident. He reports progress to his grandfather with enthusiasm, noting improvements in Beauty's legs and overall condition. The partnership between the old farmer and his young grandson creates the perfect environment for Beauty's rehabilitation. (052-026) (052-027)

### 结局

When Beauty is sufficiently recovered to be sold to a new home, Willie prepares him with extra care, polishing the harness and presenting the horse at his best. He accompanies his grandfather to deliver Beauty to Miss Ellen and Miss Blomefield's residence, and rings the bell to announce their arrival. Even after Beauty is settled in his final home, Willie continues to speak to the horse whenever he can, maintaining the bond they formed during those months of recovery. (053-001) (053-003) (053-022)

## 性格特征

- Gentle and patient with animals, demonstrating maturity beyond his years
- Diligent and responsible in carrying out his grandfather's instructions
- Optimistic and encouraging, celebrating each small sign of Beauty's improvement
- Emotionally invested in the animals under his care

## 主题意义

Willie embodies the novel's belief that compassion can and should be cultivated in the young. His role in Beauty's recovery shows that kindness to animals is not just the province of experienced adults but can be practiced meaningfully by children. The partnership between Willie and his grandfather also illustrates the importance of passing down humane values across generations — a theme that runs throughout the novel's closing chapters.
