# Organization Index

An index of philosophical schools, intellectual movements, and organizations that appear in William James's *Pragmatism: A New Name for Some Old Ways of Thinking* (1907). James structures much of his argument around contrasts between competing philosophical traditions.

## About This Index

Each entry describes a philosophical tradition or school that James engages with, either as an ally or an opponent in his argument for pragmatism. The **Citations** count reflects how often the school is referenced across the wiki.
