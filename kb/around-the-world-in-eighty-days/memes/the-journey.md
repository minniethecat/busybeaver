# The Journey

A collection of pages tracing Phileas Fogg's journey around the world — the route itself, the locations visited, the modes of transport, and the concept of circumnavigation that gives *Around the World in Eighty Days* its name. From the Reform Club wager to the triumphant return to London, these pages map the eighty-day odyssey across four continents, three oceans, and the full span of the 19th-century world.
