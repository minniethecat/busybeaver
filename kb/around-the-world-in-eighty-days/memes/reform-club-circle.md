# The Reform Club Circle

The Reform Club in Pall Mall, London, is the starting point and finishing line of Phileas Fogg's journey. It is here that he accepts the £20,000 wager from his fellow whist players, setting the entire adventure in motion. This page collects all pages relating to the Reform Club, its members, and the famous wager.
