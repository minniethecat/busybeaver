# Steamships and Vessels

A collection of the steamships and vessels that transport Phileas Fogg and his companions across the oceans and seas. From the Peninsular and Oriental Company's Mongolia (Suez to Bombay) and Rangoon (Calcutta to Hong Kong), to the chartered Tankadere that chases the Carnatic to Shanghai, and the Henrietta — commandeered for the desperate Atlantic dash to Liverpool. Each vessel is more than a mode of transport; each carries its own drama of schedules, storms, and human ingenuity pushed to its limits.
