# Transportation Methods

Phileas Fogg's journey around the world relied on an astonishing variety of conveyances: steamers, railways, sailing ships, and even an elephant. The rapid transit technologies of the late nineteenth century are central to the novel's theme of time and movement. This page aggregates all pages related to transportation methods and their operators.
