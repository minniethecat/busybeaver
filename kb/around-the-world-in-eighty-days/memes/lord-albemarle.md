# Lord Albemarle

An elderly, paralytic gentleman who is one of the five Reform Club members to accept Phileas Fogg's wager, betting five thousand pounds that Fogg will succeed in his circumnavigation.

## Role in the Novel

Lord Albemarle is a minor character who appears only in the Reform Club scenes at the beginning and end of the novel. He is one of the five whist partners who engage with Fogg during the famous wager scene, and his bet of five thousand pounds represents one of the largest individual stakes in the outcome. ([004-070])

## Biography / Background

Lord Albemarle is an elderly member of the British aristocracy, described as "paralytic" — suggesting physical infirmity that confines him to the Reform Club. Despite his condition, he remains an active participant in the club's social life, particularly its card games. ([004-070])

## Key Actions

- **Joining the wager**: Lord Albemarle is among the five Reform Club members who take up Fogg's £20,000 wager, committing £5,000 of his own money on the outcome. ([004-070])
- **Following the journey**: Along with the other whist partners, Lord Albemarle tracks Fogg's progress through the newspaper reports and telegraph dispatches that chronicle the journey. ([031-022])
- **The final tally**: At the novel's conclusion, Lord Albemarle's stake is settled: Fogg's success means Albemarle comes out behind financially, losing "a cool five thousand." ([031-063])

## Character Significance

Lord Albemarle represents the aristocratic milieu of the Reform Club — a world of gentlemen who treat a £20,000 wager as a matter of honour and sporting interest. His willingness to bet, despite his physical infirmity, underscores the confidence that Fogg's methodical character inspires among his peers. He also embodies the novel's theme of precision and calculation: the Reform Club gentlemen treat the circumnavigation as a mathematical problem to be wagered upon, not a romantic adventure.

> Lord Albemarle, an elderly paralytic gentleman, was now the only one who had not spoken. ([004-070])
