# The Burning of the Henrietta

## Summary

A key incident in Phileas Fogg's eighty-day journey around the world — one of the episodes that tests his timetable, challenges his principles, and ultimately reveals his character.

This event represents a critical junction in the novel's plot. Each incident in Fogg's journey either advances his progress, threatens his schedule, or deepens his relationships — and often does all three simultaneously.
