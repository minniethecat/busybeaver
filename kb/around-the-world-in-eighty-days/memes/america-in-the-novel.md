# America in the Novel

A collection of pages related to the American portion of Phileas Fogg's journey. Landing at San Francisco after crossing the Pacific, Fogg and his companions traverse the continent by the newly completed transcontinental railway — past Salt Lake City, across the Great Plains, through Chicago and on to New York. The American leg features some of the novel's most dramatic episodes: the Sioux attack on the train, the improvised sledge journey across the prairie, and the desperate charter of the Henrietta for the Atlantic dash to Liverpool.
