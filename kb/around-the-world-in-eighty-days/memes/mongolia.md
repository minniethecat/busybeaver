# Mongolia (Steamship)

The Mongolia is a [[p-and-o-company|P&O]] screw steamer of two thousand eight hundred tons burden, plying regularly between Brindisi and [[bombay|Bombay]] via the [[the-suez-canal|Suez Canal]]. It carries [[phileas-fogg|Phileas Fogg]] and [[passepartout|Passepartout]] on the first major sea leg of the [[around-the-world-route|circumnavigation]], and it is aboard the Mongolia that [[detective-fix|Detective Fix]] first begins his pursuit of Fogg.

## In the Novel

Fogg and Passepartout board the Mongolia at [[brindisi|Brindisi]], having crossed Europe by rail. The steamer is due at [[suez|Suez]] on the 9th of October, where Detective Fix — convinced that Fogg is the bank robber who has stolen fifty-five thousand pounds from the [[bank-of-england|Bank of England]] — waits to intercept him. (PN: 005-015)

Fix boards the Mongolia at Suez under the guise of a fellow passenger and begins his surveillance of Fogg. The steamer continues through the Red Sea and across the Indian Ocean, arriving at Bombay two days ahead of schedule — a stroke of fortune that Fogg records in his meticulous travel journal. (PN: 007-012)

> "The Mongolia, of the Peninsular and Oriental Company, was a screw steamer of two thousand eight hundred tons burden, and could accomplish a speed of ten knots an hour." (005-015)

## Aboard the Mongolia

Life aboard the Mongolia is a microcosm of Victorian steamer travel. Fogg passes his time with measured regularity — taking his meals, playing whist, and studying the ship's progress on the chart — while Passepartout explores the vessel and marvels at the machinery of steam navigation. Fix, meanwhile, cultivates Passepartout's acquaintance in the hope of gathering intelligence about his master. (PN: 005-075)

The Mongolia encounters rough weather in the Indian Ocean, but Fogg remains unperturbed. His only concern is the ship's schedule; a delay would threaten the precise timetable upon which the [[the-wager|wager]] depends. The steamer pushes forward rapidly, reaching Bombay on the 20th of October. (PN: 008-075)
