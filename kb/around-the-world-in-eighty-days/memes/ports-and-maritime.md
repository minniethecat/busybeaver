# Ports and Maritime Routes

A collection of port cities and maritime routes that feature in Phileas Fogg's circumnavigation of the globe. The sea journey forms the backbone of the route — from the steamer Mongolia departing Suez, through the Indian Ocean to Bombay, across the China Sea to Hong Kong and Yokohama, and finally the Atlantic crossing to Liverpool. These ports and the vessels that connect them are among the most vividly described settings in *Around the World in Eighty Days*.
