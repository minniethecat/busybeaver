[002-001] But only three in all [[religious-faith|God]]’s universe

[002-002] Have heard this word thou hast said—Himself, beside

[002-003] Thee speaking, and me listening! and replied

[002-004] One of us—*that* was [[god|God]]!—and laid the curse

[002-005] So darkly on my eyelids, as to amerce

[002-006] My sight from seeing thee—that if I had died,

[002-007] The deathweights placed there would have signified

[002-008] Less absolute exclusion. “Nay” is worse

[002-009] From God than from all others, O my friend!

[002-010] Men could not part us with their worldly jars,

[002-011] Nor the seas change us, nor the tempests bend:

[002-012] Our hands would touch for all the mountain-bars—

[002-013] And, heaven being rolled between us at the end,

[002-014] We should but vow the faster for the stars.
