[034-001] With the same [[heart-imagery|heart]], I said, I’ll answer thee

[034-002] As those, when thou shalt call me by my name—

[034-003] Lo, the vain promise! Is the same, the same,

[034-004] Perplexed and ruffled by life’s strategy?

[034-005] When called before, I told how hastily

[034-006] I dropped my flowers or brake off from a game.

[034-007] To run and answer with the smile that came

[034-008] At play last moment, and went on with me

[034-009] Through my obedience. When I answer now,

[034-010] I drop a grave thought—break from solitude;—

[034-011] Yet still my heart goes to thee—ponder how—

[034-012] Not as to a single good, but all my good!

[034-013] Lay thy hand on it, best one, and allow

[034-014] That no child’s foot could run fast as this blood.
