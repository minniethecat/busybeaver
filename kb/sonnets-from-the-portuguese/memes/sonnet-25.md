[025-001] A heavy [[heart-imagery|heart]], Beloved, have I borne

[025-002] From year to year until I saw thy face,

[025-003] And [[grief-theme|sorrow]] after sorrow took the place

[025-004] Of all those natural joys as lightly worn

[025-005] As the stringed pearls, each lifted in its turn

[025-006] By a beating heart at dance-time. Hopes apace

[025-007] Were changed to long despairs, till [[religious-faith|God]]’s own grace

[025-008] Could scarcely lift above the world forlorn

[025-009] My heavy heart. Then *thou* didst bid me bring

[025-010] And let it drop adown thy calmly great

[025-011] Deep being! Fast it sinketh, as a thing

[025-012] Which its own nature does precipitate,

[025-013] While thine doth close above it, mediating

[025-014] Betwixt the stars and the unaccomplished fate.
