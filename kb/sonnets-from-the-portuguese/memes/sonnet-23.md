[023-001] Is it indeed so? If I lay here dead,

[023-002] Wouldst thou miss any life in losing mine?

[023-003] And would the sun for thee more coldly shine,

[023-004] Because of grave-damps falling round my head?

[023-005] I marvelled, my Beloved, when I read

[023-006] Thy thought so in the letter. I am thine—

[023-007] But—*so* much to thee? Can I pour thy wine

[023-008] While my hands tremble? Then my [[soul-theme|soul]], instead

[023-009] Of [[dream|dreams]] of [[death-theme|death]], resumes life’s lower range.

[023-010] Then, [[love-theme|love]] me, Love! look on me—breathe on me!

[023-011] As brighter ladies do not count it strange,

[023-012] For love, to give up acres and degree,

[023-013] I yield the grave for thy sake, and exchange

[023-014] My near sweet view of Heaven for earth with thee!
