[039-001] Because thou hast the power and own’st the grace

[039-002] To look through and behind this mask of me,

[039-003] (Against which, years have beat thus blanchingly,

[039-004] With their rains), and behold my [[soul-theme|soul]]’s true face,

[039-005] The dim and weary witness of life’s race—

[039-006] Because thou hast the [[religious-faith|faith]] and [[love-theme|love]] to see,

[039-007] Through that same soul’s distracting lethargy,

[039-008] The patient [[angel-imagery|angel]] waiting for a place

[039-009] In the new Heavens—because nor sin nor woe,

[039-010] Nor [[god|God]]’s infliction, nor [[death-theme|death]]’s neighbourhood,

[039-011] Nor all which others viewing, turn to go,

[039-012] Nor all which makes me tired of all, self-viewed—

[039-013] Nothing repels thee—Dearest, teach me so

[039-014] To pour out gratitude, as thou dost, good!
