[033-001] Yes, call me by my pet name! let me hear

[033-002] The name I used to run at, when a child,

[033-003] From innocent play, and leave the cowslips plied,

[033-004] To glance up in some face that proved me dear

[033-005] With the look of its eyes. I miss the clear

[033-006] Fond voices, which, being drawn and reconciled

[033-007] Into the [[music-theme|music]] of Heaven’s undefiled,

[033-008] Call me no longer. Silence on the bier

[033-009] While I call [[religious-faith|God]]—call [[god|God]]!—so let thy mouth

[033-010] Be heir to those who are now exanimate;

[033-011] Gather the north flowers to complete the south,

[033-012] And catch the early [[love-theme|love]] up in the late!

[033-013] Yes, call me by that name—and I, in truth,

[033-014] With the same [[heart-imagery|heart]], will answer and not wait.
