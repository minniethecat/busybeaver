[022-001] When our two souls stand up erect and strong,

[022-002] Face to face, [[silence|silent]], drawing nigh and nigher,

[022-003] Until the lengthening wings break into fire

[022-004] At either curved point—what bitter wrong

[022-005] Can the earth do to us, that we should not long

[022-006] Be here contented? Think. In mounting higher,

[022-007] The [[angel-imagery|angels]] would press on us, and aspire

[022-008] To drop some golden orb of perfect [[music-theme|song]]

[022-009] Into our deep, dear silence. Let us stay

[022-010] Rather on earth, Beloved—where the unfit

[022-011] Contrarious moods of men recoil away

[022-012] And isolate pure spirits, and permit

[022-013] A place to stand and [[love-theme|love]] in for a day,

[022-014] With darkness and the [[death-theme|death]]-hour rounding it.
