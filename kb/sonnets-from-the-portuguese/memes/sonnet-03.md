[003-001] Unlike are we, unlike, O princely Heart!

[003-002] Unlike our uses and our destinies.

[003-003] Our ministering two [[angel-imagery|angels]] look surprise

[003-004] On one another, as they strike athwart

[003-005] Their wings in passing. Thou, bethink thee, art

[003-006] A guest for queens to social pageantries,

[003-007] With gazes from a hundred brighter eyes

[003-008] Than [[tear-imagery|tears]] even can make mine, to play thy part

[003-009] Of chief [[music-theme|musician]]. What hast *thou* to do

[003-010] With looking from the lattice-lights at me,

[003-011] A poor, tired, wandering singer, singing through

[003-012] The dark, and leaning up a cypress tree?

[003-013] The chrism is on thine head—on mine, the dew—

[003-014] And Death must dig the level where these agree.
