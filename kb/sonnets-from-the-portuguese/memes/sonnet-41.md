[041-001] I thank all who have loved me in their hearts,

[041-002] With thanks and [[love-theme|love]] from mine. Deep thanks to all

[041-003] Who paused a little near the prison-wall

[041-004] To hear my [[music-theme|music]] in its louder parts,

[041-005] Ere they went onward, each one to the mart’s

[041-006] Or temple’s occupation, beyond call.

[041-007] But thou, who, in my voice’s sink and fall

[041-008] When the sob took it, thy divinest Art’s

[041-009] Own instrument didst drop down at thy foot

[041-010] To harken what I said between my [[tear-imagery|tears]]—

[041-011] Instruct me how to thank thee! Oh, to shoot

[041-012] My [[soul-theme|soul]]’s full meaning into future years,

[041-013] That *they* should lend it utterance, and salute

[041-014] Love that endures, from Life that disappears!
