[038-001] First time he kissed me, he but only kissed

[038-002] The fingers of this hand wherewith I write,

[038-003] And ever since, it grew more clean and white—

[038-004] Slow to world greetings, quick with its “Oh, list,”

[038-005] When the [[angel-imagery|angels]] speak. A ring of amethyst

[038-006] I could not wear here, plainer to my sight

[038-007] Than that first [[kiss-imagery|kiss]]. The second passed in height

[038-008] The first, and sought the forehead, and half missed,

[038-009] Half falling on the hair. O beyond meed!

[038-010] That was the chrism of [[love-theme|love]], which love’s own crown,

[038-011] With sanctifying sweetness, did precede

[038-012] The third upon my lips was folded down

[038-013] In perfect, purple state; since when, indeed,

[038-014] I have been proud and said: “My love, my own.”
