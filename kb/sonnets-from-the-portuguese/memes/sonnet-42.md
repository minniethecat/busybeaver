[042-001] *My future will not copy fair my past*—

[042-002] I wrote that once; and thinking at my side

[042-003] My ministering life-[[angel-imagery|angel]] justified

[042-004] The word by his appealing look upcast

[042-005] To the white throne of [[religious-faith|God]], I turned at last,

[042-006] And there, instead, saw thee, not unallied

[042-007] To angels in thy [[soul-theme|soul]]! Then I, long tried

[042-008] By natural ills, received the comfort fast,

[042-009] While budding, at thy sight, my pilgrim’s staff

[042-010] Gave out green leaves with morning dews impearled.

[042-011] I seek no copy now of life’s first half:

[042-012] Leave here the pages with long musing curled,

[042-013] And write me new my future’s epigraph,

[042-014] New angel mine, unhoped for in the world!
