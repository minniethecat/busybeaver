[009-001] Can it be right to give what I can give?

[009-002] To let thee sit beneath the fall of [[tear-imagery|tears]]

[009-003] As salt as mine, and hear the sighing years

[009-004] Re-sighing on my lips renunciative

[009-005] Through those infrequent smiles which fail to live

[009-006] For all thy adjurations? O my fears,

[009-007] That this can scarce be right! We are not peers,

[009-008] So to be lovers; and I own and grieve

[009-009] That givers of such gifts as mine are, must

[009-010] Be counted with the ungenerous. Out, alas!

[009-011] I will not soil thy purple with my dust,

[009-012] Nor breathe my poison on thy Venice-glass,

[009-013] Nor give thee any [[love-theme|love]]—which were unjust.

[009-014] Beloved, I only love thee! let it pass.
