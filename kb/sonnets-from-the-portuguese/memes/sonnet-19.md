[019-001] The [[soul-theme|soul]]’s Rialto hath its merchandise;

[019-002] I barter curl for curl upon that mart,

[019-003] And from my poet’s forehead to my [[heart-imagery|heart]]

[019-004] Receive this lock which outweighs argosies—

[019-005] As purply black, as erst to Pindar’s eyes

[019-006] The dim purpureal tresses gloomed athwart

[019-007] The nine white Muse-brows. For this counterpart,

[019-008] The bay-crown’s [[shadow|shade]], Beloved, I surmise,

[019-009] Still lingers on thy curl, it is so black!

[019-010] Thus, with a fillet of smooth-kissing breath,

[019-011] I tie the shadows safe from gliding back,

[019-012] And lay the gift where nothing hindereth,

[019-013] Here on my heart as on thy brow, to lack

[019-014] No natural heat till mine grows cold in [[death-theme|death]].
