# About This Wiki

## Source Material

This wiki is based on **Sonnets from the Portuguese** by Elizabeth Barrett Browning (1806–1861), first published in 1850. The collection contains 44 sonnets chronicling the poet's love for her husband, Robert Browning. The title was chosen to disguise the deeply personal nature of the poems, pretending they were translations from Portuguese originals.

## Usage Notes

This wiki serves as a literary companion to the sonnet sequence:

- Each sonnet has its own page with full text and analysis.

- Theme pages explore recurring motifs across the collection.

- Person, concept, and event pages provide historical and biographical context.

Target readers include students of Victorian poetry, literary scholars, and lovers of the sonnet form.
