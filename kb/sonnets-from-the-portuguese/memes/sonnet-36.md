[036-001] When we met first and loved, I did not build

[036-002] Upon the event with marble. Could it mean

[036-003] To last, a [[love-theme|love]] set pendulous between

[036-004] Sorrow and [[grief-theme|sorrow]]? Nay, I rather thrilled,

[036-005] Distrusting every [[light-imagery|light]] that seemed to gild

[036-006] The onward path, and feared to overlean

[036-007] A finger even. And, though I have grown serene

[036-008] And strong since then, I think that [[religious-faith|God]] has willed

[036-009] A still renewable fear—O love, O troth—

[036-010] Lest these enclasped hands should never hold,

[036-011] This mutual [[kiss-imagery|kiss]] drop down between us both

[036-012] As an unowned thing, once the lips being cold.

[036-013] And Love, be false! if *he*, to keep one oath,

[036-014] Must lose one joy, by his life’s star foretold.
