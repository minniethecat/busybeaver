[007-001] The face of all the world is changed, I think,

[007-002] Since first I heard the footsteps of thy [[soul-theme|soul]]

[007-003] Move still, oh, still, beside me, as they stole

[007-004] Betwixt me and the dreadful outer brink

[007-005] Of obvious [[death-theme|death]], where I, who thought to sink

[007-006] Was caught up into [[love-theme|love]], and taught the whole

[007-007] Of life in a new rhythm. The cup of dole

[007-008] [[religious-faith|God]] gave for baptism, I am fain to drink,

[007-009] And praise its sweetness, Sweet, with thee anear.

[007-010] The names of country, heaven, are changed away

[007-011] For where thou art or shalt be, there or here;

[007-012] And this—this lute and [[music-theme|song]]—loved yesterday,

[007-013] (The singing [[angel-imagery|angels]] know) are only dear,

[007-014] Because thy name moves right in what they say.
