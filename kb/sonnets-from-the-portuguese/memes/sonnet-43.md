[043-001] How do I [[love-theme|love]] thee? [[sonnet-xliii|Let me count the ways]].

[043-002] I love thee to the depth and breadth and height

[043-003] My [[soul-theme|soul]] can reach, when feeling out of sight

[043-004] For the ends of Being and ideal Grace.

[043-005] I love thee to the level of everyday’s

[043-006] Most quiet need, by sun and candlelight.

[043-007] I love thee freely, as men strive for Right;

[043-008] I love thee purely, as they turn from Praise.

[043-009] I love thee with the passion put to use

[043-010] In my old griefs, and with my childhood’s [[religious-faith|faith]].

[043-011] I love thee with a love I seemed to lose

[043-012] With my lost saints—I love thee with the breath,

[043-013] Smiles, [[tear-imagery|tears]], of all my life!—and, if [[god|God]] choose,

[043-014] I shall but love thee better after [[death-theme|death]].
