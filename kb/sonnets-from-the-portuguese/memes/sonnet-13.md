[013-001] And wilt thou have me fashion into speech

[013-002] The [[love-theme|love]] I bear thee, finding words enough,

[013-003] And hold the torch out, while the winds are rough,

[013-004] Between our faces, to cast [[light-imagery|light]] on each?—

[013-005] I drop it at thy feet. I cannot teach

[013-006] My hand to hold my spirits so far off

[013-007] From myself—me—that I should bring thee proof

[013-008] In words, of love hid in me out of reach.

[013-009] Nay, let the [[silence]] of my womanhood

[013-010] Commend my woman-love to thy belief—

[013-011] Seeing that I stand unwon, however wooed,

[013-012] And rend the garment of my life, in brief,

[013-013] By a most dauntless, voiceless fortitude,

[013-014] Lest one touch of this [[heart-imagery|heart]] convey its [[grief-theme|grief]].
