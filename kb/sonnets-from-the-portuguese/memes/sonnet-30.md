[030-001] I see thine image through my [[tear-imagery|tears]] tonight,

[030-002] And yet today I saw thee smiling. How

[030-003] Refer the cause?—Beloved, is it thou

[030-004] Or I who makes me sad? The acolyte

[030-005] Amid the chanted joy and thankful rite

[030-006] May so fall flat, with pale insensate brow,

[030-007] On the altar-stair. I hear thy voice and vow

[030-008] Perplexed, uncertain, since thou art out of sight,

[030-009] As he, in his swooning ears, the choir’s amen.

[030-010] Beloved, dost thou [[love-theme|love]]? or did I see all

[030-011] The glory as I dreamed, and fainted when

[030-012] Too vehement [[light-imagery|light]] dilated my ideal

[030-013] For my [[soul-theme|soul]]’s eyes? Will that light come again

[030-014] As now these tears come—falling hot and real?
