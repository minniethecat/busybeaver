[006-001] Go from me. Yet I feel that I shall stand

[006-002] Henceforth in thy [[shadow]]. Nevermore

[006-003] Alone upon the threshold of my door

[006-004] Of individual life, I shall command

[006-005] The uses of my [[soul-theme|soul]], nor lift my hand

[006-006] Serenely in the sunshine as before.

[006-007] Without the sense of that which I forbore

[006-008] Thy touch upon the palm. The widest land

[006-009] Doom takes to part us, leaves thy [[heart-imagery|heart]] in mine

[006-010] With pulses that beat double. What I do

[006-011] And what I [[dream]] include thee, as the wine

[006-012] Must taste of its own grapes. And when I sue

[006-013] [[religious-faith|God]] for myself, He hears that name of thine,

[006-014] And sees within my eyes the [[tear-imagery|tears]] of two.
