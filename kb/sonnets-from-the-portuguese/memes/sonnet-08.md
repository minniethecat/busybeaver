[008-001] What can I give thee back, O liberal

[008-002] And princely giver—who hast brought the gold

[008-003] And purple of thine [[heart-imagery|heart]], unstained, untold,

[008-004] And laid them on the outside of the wall

[008-005] For such as I to take or leave withal,

[008-006] In unexpected largesse? Am I cold,

[008-007] Ungrateful, that for these most manifold

[008-008] High gifts, I render nothing back at all?

[008-009] Not so; not cold—but very poor instead!

[008-010] Ask [[religious-faith|God]] who knows! For frequent [[tear-imagery|tears]] have run

[008-011] The colours from my life, and left so dead

[008-012] And pale a stuff, it were not fitly done

[008-013] To give the same as pillow to thy head.

[008-014] Go farther! let it serve to trample on.
