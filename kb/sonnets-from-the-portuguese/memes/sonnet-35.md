[035-001] If I leave all for thee, wilt thou exchange

[035-002] And be all to me? Shall I never miss

[035-003] Home-talk and blessing and the common [[kiss-imagery|kiss]]

[035-004] That comes to each in turn, nor count it strange,

[035-005] When I look up, to drop on a new range

[035-006] Of walls and floors, another home than this?

[035-007] Nay, wilt thou fill that place by me which is

[035-008] Filled by dead eyes too tender to know change?

[035-009] That’s hardest! If to conquer [[love-theme|love]] has tried,

[035-010] To conquer [[grief-theme|grief]] tries more, as all things prove;

[035-011] For grief indeed is love and grief beside.

[035-012] Alas, I have grieved so I am hard to love—

[035-013] Yet love me—wilt thou? Open thy [[heart-imagery|heart]] wide

[035-014] And fold within, the wet wings of thy dove.
