## Historical Context

The Browning courtship is the defining biographical event that produced _Sonnets from the Portuguese_. Elizabeth Barrett and Robert Browning met in 1845, exchanged nearly 600 letters, and married secretly in 1846 before moving to Italy. The 44 sonnets were written during this courtship and presented to Robert after their marriage.
