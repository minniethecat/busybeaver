[010-001] Yet, [[love-theme|love]], mere love, is beautiful indeed

[010-002] And worthy of acceptation. Fire is bright,

[010-003] Let temple burn, or flax; an equal [[light-imagery|light]]

[010-004] Leaps in the flame from cedar-plank or weed:

[010-005] And love is fire. And when I say at need

[010-006] *I love thee*—mark!—*I love thee*—in thy sight

[010-007] I stand transfigured, glorified aright,

[010-008] With conscience of the new rays that proceed

[010-009] Out of my face toward thine. There’s nothing low

[010-010] In love, when love the lowest: meanest creatures

[010-011] Who love [[religious-faith|God]], [[god|God]] accepts while loving so.

[010-012] And what I *feel*, across the inferior features

[010-013] Of what I *am*, doth flash itself, and show

[010-014] How that great work of Love enhances Nature’s.
