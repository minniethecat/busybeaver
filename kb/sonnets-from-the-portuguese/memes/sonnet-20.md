[020-001] Beloved, my Beloved, when I think

[020-002] That thou wast in the world a year ago,

[020-003] What time I sate alone here in the snow

[020-004] And saw no footprint, heard the [[silence]] sink

[020-005] No moment at thy voice, but link by link

[020-006] Went counting all my chains as if that so

[020-007] They never could fall off at any blow

[020-008] Struck by thy possible hand—why, thus I drink

[020-009] Of life’s great cup of wonder. Wonderful,

[020-010] Never to feel thee thrill the day or night

[020-011] With personal act or speech—nor ever cull

[020-012] Some prescience of thee with the blossoms white

[020-013] Thou sawest growing! Atheists are as dull,

[020-014] Who cannot guess [[religious-faith|God]]’s presence out of sight.
