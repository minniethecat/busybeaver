[026-001] I lived with [[dream|visions]] for my company

[026-002] Instead of men and women, years ago,

[026-003] And found them gentle mates, nor thought to know

[026-004] A sweeter [[music-theme|music]] than they played to me.

[026-005] But soon their trailing purple was not free

[026-006] Of this world’s dust, their lutes did [[silence|silent]] grow,

[026-007] And I myself grew faint and blind below

[026-008] Their vanishing eyes. Then *thou* didst come—to *be*,

[026-009] Beloved, what they *seemed*. Their shining fronts,

[026-010] Their songs, their splendours—better, yet the same,

[026-011] As river-water hallowed into fonts—

[026-012] Met in thee, and from out thee overcame

[026-013] My [[soul-theme|soul]] with satisfaction of all wants—

[026-014] Because [[religious-faith|God]]’s gifts put man’s best dreams to shame.
