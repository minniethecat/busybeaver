[016-001] And yet, because thou overcomest so,

[016-002] Because thou art more noble and like a king,

[016-003] Thou canst prevail against my fears and fling

[016-004] Thy purple round me, till my [[heart-imagery|heart]] shall grow

[016-005] Too close against thine heart henceforth to know

[016-006] How it shook when alone. Why, conquering

[016-007] May prove as lordly and complete a thing

[016-008] In lifting upward as in crushing low!

[016-009] And as a vanquished soldier yields his sword

[016-010] To one who lifts him from the bloody earth,

[016-011] Even so, Beloved, I at last record,

[016-012] Here ends my strife. If *thou* invite me forth,

[016-013] I rise above abasement at the word.

[016-014] Make thy [[love-theme|love]] larger to enlarge my worth.
