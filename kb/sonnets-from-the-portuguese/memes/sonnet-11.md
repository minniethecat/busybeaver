[011-001] And therefore if to [[love-theme|love]] can be desert,

[011-002] I am not all unworthy. Cheeks as pale

[011-003] As these you see, and trembling knees that fail

[011-004] To bear the burden of a heavy [[heart-imagery|heart]],

[011-005] This weary minstrel-life that once was girt

[011-006] To climb Aornus, and can scarce avail

[011-007] To pipe now ’gainst the valley nightingale

[011-008] A melancholy [[music-theme|music]]—why advert

[011-009] To these things? O Beloved, it is plain

[011-010] I am not of thy worth nor for thy place!

[011-011] And yet, because I love thee, I obtain

[011-012] From that same love this vindicating grace—

[011-013] To live on still in love, and yet in vain,

[011-014] To bless thee, yet renounce thee to thy face.
