[040-001] Oh, yes! they [[love-theme|love]] through all this world of ours!

[040-002] I will not gainsay love, called love forsooth.

[040-003] I have heard love talked in my early youth,

[040-004] And since, not so long back but that the flowers

[040-005] Then gathered, smell still. Mussulmans and Giaours

[040-006] Throw kerchiefs at a smile, and have no ruth

[040-007] For any [[tear-imagery|weeping]]. Polypheme’s white tooth

[040-008] Slips on the nut, if after frequent showers,

[040-009] The shell is over-smooth; and not so much

[040-010] Will turn the thing called love, aside to hate,

[040-011] Or else to oblivion. But thou art not such

[040-012] A lover, my Beloved! thou canst wait

[040-013] Through [[grief-theme|sorrow]] and sickness, to bring souls to touch,

[040-014] And think it soon when others cry “Too late.”
