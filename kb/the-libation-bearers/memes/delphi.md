# Delphi

[[apollo|Apollo]]'s oracle at Delphi is a constant presence in the Choephoroe, though geographically distant from [[argos|Argos]]. It is from Delphi that the command to kill comes, and it is to Delphi that [[orestes|Orestes]] will flee after the [[killing-of-clytemnestra|matricide]], seeking [[catharsis|purification]].
