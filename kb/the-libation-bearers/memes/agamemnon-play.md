# Agamemnon (Play)

Greek|The [[agamemnon|Agamemnon]] is the first play of the [[oresteia|Oresteia]]. Its murder of the [[exile-and-return|return]]ing king sets in motion the [[divine-vengeance|vengeance]] that the Choephoroe must complete and the Eumenides must resolve.
