# Agamemnon's Tomb

The tomb of [[agamemnon|Agamemnon]] is the Choephoroe's most important physical space. 'The grave is their only home, / The beggared and outcast' (001-002). It is simultaneously a place of mourning, a locus of supernatural power, and the threshold between the living and the dead. Here the [[libation-ritual|libations]] are poured, the [[recognition-scene|recognition]] occurs, and the great [[kommos]] summons the dead king to wakefulness.
