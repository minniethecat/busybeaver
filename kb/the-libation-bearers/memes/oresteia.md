# Oresteia

Greek|The Oresteia is 'the only [[the-trilogy-form|trilogy]] preserved to us from the Athenian stage' (P01-001). It traces 'the great problem which lies at the centre of Greek religion — Hubris, [[justice-and-vengeance|Dike]], Soteria or Crime, Punishment and Deliverance' (P01-002).
