# Alice

## Character Overview

Alice is [[mrs-arbuthnot|Mrs. Arbuthnot]]'s maid at [[wrockley|Wrockley]], appearing in Act IV (004-040) as the only servant character affiliated [[wit-and-epigram|wit]]h the play's moral center rather than the aristocratic world of [[hunstanton-chase|Hunstanton Chase]]. Her presence — quiet, loyal, discreet — mirrors her mistress's own situation: a woman whose life is defined by service and silence, yet who possesses a dignity that the drawing-room [[wit-and-epigram|wit]]s lack. She is mentioned in Act II when [[mrs-arbuthnot|Mrs. Arbuthnot]] refers to her household, and she appears briefly in Act IV to attend [[mrs-arbuthnot|Mrs. Arbuthnot]].

## Role in the Play

Alice's most significant dramatic function occurs in Act IV. She serves as the quiet [[wit-and-epigram|wit]]ness to [[mrs-arbuthnot|Mrs. Arbuthnot]]'s private world at [[wrockley|Wrockley]] — the modest household where [[mrs-arbuthnot|Rachel]] has raised [[gerald-arbuthnot|Gerald]] alone. She enters to announce the arrival of [[lady-hunstanton|Lady Hunstanton]] and [[mrs-allonby|Mrs. Allonby]] (004-040), and later announces [[lord-illingworth|Lord Illingworth]]'s arrival (004-070).

Her simple presence emphasizes the contrast between the two worlds of the play: the glittering, amoral sphere of [[hunstanton-chase|Hunstanton Chase]] and the modest, morally-grounded home [[mrs-arbuthnot|Mrs. Arbuthnot]] has built. When [[lord-illingworth|Lord Illingworth]] arrives at [[wrockley|Wrockley]] seeking reconciliation, Alice's presence as the loyal servant underscores what he has never understood — that [[mrs-arbuthnot|Mrs. Arbuthnot]] has created a life of her own, complete in its quiet way, that has no room for him.

## Relationships

- **[[mrs-arbuthnot|Mrs. Arbuthnot]]**: Her employer and the play's moral center. Alice's loyalty to [[mrs-arbuthnot|Mrs. Arbuthnot]] reflects the respect [[mrs-arbuthnot|Rachel]] commands from those who know her — a respect [[lord-illingworth|Lord Illingworth]] cannot comprehend.
- **[[gerald-arbuthnot|Gerald Arbuthnot]]**: [[mrs-arbuthnot|Mrs. Arbuthnot]]'s son, whom Alice has known since his childhood.
