# "God's law is only Love."

> God's law is only Love.
> (Spoken by Hester Worsley)

## Context

Hester's simplest and most powerful statement — the Puritan moralist reduces moral law to a single principle.

## Analysis

This line exemplifies Wilde's ability to make dialogue do dramatic work. It is at once a piece of characterization — revealing something essential about Hester Worsley — and a contribution to the play's thematic architecture.
