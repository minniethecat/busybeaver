# Marvel

The **[[miracle|marvel]]** (or marvellous) is a fun[[lady|dame]]ntal mode of experience in Thomas Malory's *Le Morte d'[[king-arthur|Arthur]]*. It is the encounter with phenomena that exceed natural explanation — miraculous [[sword|swords]], prophetic [[dream|dreams]], supernatural creatures, and divine interventions. Marvels mark the boundary between the mundane world of chivalric [[battle|combat]] and the larger cosmic drama in which [[king-arthur|Arthur]]'s [[realm|kingdom]] is embedded.

## The World of Marvels

Caxton's preface establishes [[miracle|marvel]] as a key term of the work. He notes "marvellous works of iron lying under the ground" (002-059) at [[king-arthur|Arthur]]'s supposed tomb, and wonders "[[miracle|marvel]] why he is no more [[honour|renown]]ed" (002-061). The Morte is filled with "[[adventure|marvellous adventure]]s" (002-111, 002-117) — the word "marvellous" is Caxton's preferred adjective for the stories he presents. Marvel is not ornamental but essential: the [[king-arthur|Arthur]]ian world is one where the supernatural regularly breaks into the natural.

## Marvel as Narrative Signal

When characters "[[miracle|marvel]]led who that might be that lay there" (005-031), their wonder signals to the reader that something significant is occurring. The chapter "Of the wonders and [[miracle|marvel]]s of a sword taken out of a stone" (008-001) uses [[miracle|marvel]] as a narrative frame — the [[miracle|marvel]] is the story, and the story is the [[miracle|marvel]]. Marvel in Malory is both the content of the narrative and its emotional register.

## Marvel and the Grail

The Holy Grail quest represents the apotheosis of [[miracle|marvel]] in Malory. The Grail itself is the supreme [[miracle|marvel]] — a sacred object whose appearance is accompanied by supernatural signs, and whose achievement requ[[wrath|ire]]s not [[courage|prowess]] but purity. The Grail quest moves the narrative from the [[miracle|marvel]]s of [[enchantment|magic]] ([[merlin|Merlin]]'s enchantments, [[enchantment|magic]]al [[sword|swords]]) to the [[miracle|marvel]]s of [[grace|divine grace]], mar[[kinsman|kin]]g a transition from chivalric to spiritual understanding.
