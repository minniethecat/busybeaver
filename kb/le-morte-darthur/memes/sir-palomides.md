# Sir Palomides
**Sir Palomides** the Saracen is a knight of the [[round-table|Round Table]] in Thomas Malory's *Le Morte d'[[king-arthur|Arthur]]*, distinguished by his non-Christian origin and his obsessive pursuit of the [[questing-beast|Questing Beast]]. His presence in the fellowship expands the chivalric ideal beyond its Christian framework.

## The Saracen Knight
Palomides is explicitly identified as a Saracen (Muslim), ma[[kinsman|kin]]g him unique among the [[round-table|Round Table]] knights. His [[courage|prowess]] earns him a place among [[king-arthur|Arthur]]'s fellowship, and his [[christen|baptism]] as a Christian later in the narrative represents one of Malory's most extended treatments of religious conversion and inclusion.

## The Questing Beast
Palomides is defined by his pursuit of the [[questing-beast|Questing Beast]] — a [[marvel|supernatural]] creature whose bizarre appearance and unceasing noise mark it as one of the Morte's strangest [[miracle|marvel]]s. His obsessive hunt mirrors the larger quest structure of [[king-arthur|Arthur]]ian narrative while remaining uniquely his own.
