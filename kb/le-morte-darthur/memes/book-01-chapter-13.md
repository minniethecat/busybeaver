[016-001]Hundred Knights.

[016-002]So by [[merlin|Merlin]]’s advice there were sent fore-riders to skim the country,

[016-003]and they met with the fore-riders of the north, and made them to tell which way

[016-004]the host came, and then they told it to [[king-arthur|Arthur]], and by King Ban and [[sir-bors|Bors]]’

[016-005]council they let burn and destroy all the country afore them, there they should

[016-006]ride.

[016-007]The King with the Hundred Knights met a [[marvel|wonder]] dream two nights afore the

[016-008]battle, that there blew a great wind, and blew down their castles and their

[016-009]towns, and after that came a water and bare it all away. All that heard of the

[016-010]sweven said it was a token of great battle. Then by counsel of Merlin, when

[016-011]they wist which way the eleven kings would ride and lodge that night, at

[016-012]midnight they set upon them, as they were in their pavilions. But the

[016-013]scout-watch by their host cried, Lords! at arms! for here be your enemies at

[016-014]your hand!
