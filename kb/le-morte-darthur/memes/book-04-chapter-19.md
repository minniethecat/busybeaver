[085-001]Uwaine met three damosels, and each of them took one.

[085-002]Now shall everych of us choose a damosel. I shall tell you, said Sir Uwaine, I

[085-003]am the youngest and most weakest of you both, therefore I will have the eldest

[085-004]damosel, for she hath seen much, and can best help me when I have need, for I

[085-005]have most need of help of you both. Now, said Sir Marhaus, I will have the

[085-006]damosel of thirty winter age, for she falleth best to me. Well, said Sir

[085-007][[sir-gawaine|Gawaine]], I thank you, for ye have left me the youngest and the fairest, and she

[085-008]is most liefest to me. Then every damosel took her knight by the reins of his

[085-009]bridle, and brought him to the three ways, and there was their oath made to

[085-010]meet at the fountain that day twelvemonth an they were living, and so they

[085-011]kissed and departed, and each knight set his lady behind him. And Sir Uwaine

[085-012]took the way that lay west, and Sir Marhaus took the way that lay south, and

[085-013]Sir Gawaine took the way that lay north. Now will we begin at Sir Gawaine, that

[085-014]held that way till that he came unto a fair manor, where dwelled an old knight

[085-015]and a good householder, and there Sir Gawaine asked the knight if he knew any

[085-016]adventures in that country. I shall show you some to-morn, said the old knight,

[085-017]and that [[marvel|marvellous]]. So, on the morn they rode into the forest of adventures to

[085-018]a laund, and thereby they found a cross, and as they stood and hoved there came

[085-019]by them the fairest knight and the seemliest man that ever they saw, making the

[085-020]greatest dole that ever man made. And then he was ware of Sir Gawaine, and

[085-021]saluted him, and prayed God to send him much [[honour|worship]]. As to that, said Sir

[085-022]Gawaine, [[mercy|gramercy]]; also I pray to God that he send you honour and worship. Ah,

[085-023]said the knight, I may lay that aside, for sorrow and shame cometh to me after

[085-024]worship.
