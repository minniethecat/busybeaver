Elijah Pogram is an [[america|America]]n politician and Member of Congress whom [[martin-chuzzlewit-jr|Martin]] Chuzzlewit meets aboard a steamboat during his travels in the [[america|United States]]. Pogram is a caricature of [[america|America]]n political bombast and cultural chauvinism, known for his pompous oratory and his "Defiance" of the rest of the world (021-001).

## Overview

The "Honourable Elijah Pogram" is introduced to [[martin-chuzzlewit-jr|Martin]] as "one of the masterminds of our country" by a fellow passenger on the steamboat. Dickens uses Pogram to satirize [[america|America]]n political rhetoric, particularly the self-aggrandizing speeches and patriotic bluster common among public figures of the era (021-002).

## Biography

### Political Career

Pogram is a Member of Congress who, when Congress is in recess, tours the [[america|United States]] as its "gifted son." He is preparing a great speech on foreign relations for the next session and is actively gathering information—or inventing it—from European travelers like [[martin-chuzzlewit-jr|Martin]] (021-009).

### Encounter with Martin

On the steamboat, Pogram engages [[martin-chuzzlewit-jr|Martin]] in conversation about British antipathy toward [[america|America]]n institutions. He declares that this hatred is "partly [[envy|jealousy]] and pre‑judice" and stems from "the nat'ral unfitness of the British people to appreciate the ex‑alted Institutions of our native land." Pogram is also known for delivering the "Pogram Defiance," which "defied the world in general to com‑pete with our country upon any hook" (021-005).

## Character Analysis

Pogram embodies the type of [[america|America]]n politician whom Dickens portrays as verbose, self-important, and addicted to empty patriotic rhetoric. His speeches are filled with grandiose metaphors about "Natur'," "Freedom," and "the Settin Sun," yet he is fundamentally lazy—[[martin-chuzzlewit-jr|Martin]] observes that Pogram could have stayed home and sent his shoes on the tour for all the good he was doing (021-007).
