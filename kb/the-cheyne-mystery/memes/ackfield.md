# Mr. Ackfield

**Mr. Ackfield** is a lawyer based in [[plymouth|Plymouth]] who handles legal matters for the group involved in the Cheyne mystery. He is first mentioned early in the story as the family's solicitor in Plymouth.

## Role in the Story

Ackfield serves as the legal representative for the conspirators, based in Plymouth. His involvement is revealed when [[maxwell-cheyne|Maxwell Cheyne]]'s mother mentions receiving a telegram about him:

> "Why, your telegram about Mr. Ackfield, of course," his mother answered. [002-057]

The connection to Ackfield as the family lawyer is confirmed:

> …of the fact that Mr. Ackfield of Plymouth was their lawyer… [002-069]

## The Plymouth Connection

Ackfield's office in Plymouth is a meeting point for those involved in the affair. When Maxwell Cheyne returns to Plymouth, he expects to find both Ackfield and his associates waiting:

> …when there was no sign of you or Mr. Ackfield, so we sat in the waiting-room… [002-073]
