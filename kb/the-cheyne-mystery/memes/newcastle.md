## Description

Newcastle upon Tyne is a major port city in northeast England. In the novel, it appears as the destination of two coasters listed among the vessels that departed Antwerp on the day the gang fled with the stolen cipher tracing.

> There were four coasters, two for Newcastle, one for Goole, and one for Belfast, a 6,000 tonner for Singapore and the Dutch Islands... [017-008]

## Role in the Story

Newcastle is mentioned only in passing as [[inspector-french|Inspector French]] examines the list of ships that left Antwerp on the day in question. It is one of multiple destinations of departing vessels, none of which prove to be the gang's means of escape — French's attention is instead drawn to a five-masted sailing ship bound for Buenos Aires.
