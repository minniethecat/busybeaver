# Paignton

Paignton is a coastal town on Tor Bay in South Devon, situated between Brixham to the east and Torquay to the north. In *The Cheyne Mystery*, it is one of three possible landing places searched by Maxwell Cheyne as he pursued the conspirators aboard the motor launch *Enid* [015-012].

## Geography

The town lies on the railway branch line from Newton Abbot through Torquay and Paignton to Kingswear [019-022]. It is approximately ten miles from Inner Froward Point by sea and about five miles from Brixham by road [015-012].

## Cheyne's Search

During the pursuit, Cheyne rode from Brixham to Paignton "at something well above the legal limit" [015-012], hoping to intercept the *Enid*. Inquiries at the pier produced no result, but a chance meeting with a coastguard proved crucial:

> The coastguard told Cheyne that "though no launch had come into Paignton that morning, he had about three-quarters of an hour earlier seen one crossing the bay from the south and evidently making for Torquay" [015-012].

This information redirected Cheyne's pursuit to Torquay, where he found the *Enid* at anchor in the inner harbor. Paignton was one of the three possible landing sites — along with Brixham and Torquay — that the conspirators could have reached before Cheyne could alert the authorities [019-022].
