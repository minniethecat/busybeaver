# Alwyn Road

**Alwyn Road** is a street that intersects with Hopefield Avenue in the area where Maxwell Cheyne conducted his investigation.

## Role in the Story

During his surveillance of the house at Hopefield Avenue, Cheyne observed that Alwyn Road served as a crossroad [005-056]. He began his reconnaissance by crossing Alwyn Road and walking along Hopefield Avenue [005-058].

This allowed him to survey the area and the suspicious house from multiple angles.

Cheyne also walked down Alwyn Road itself, noting the layout of the neighborhood and the vacant lots along the road [005-059].

Between the first vacant lot and the next, he was able to get a clearer view of the activities at the house that interested him.

## Significance

Alwyn Road was part of the surveillance circuit that Cheyne established around the gang's suspected hideout.

His careful observation of the streets, including Alwyn Road, demonstrated his methodical approach to gathering intelligence about the criminals he was tracking.
