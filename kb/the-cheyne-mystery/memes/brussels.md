# Brussels

Brussels, the capital of Belgium, appears in *The Cheyne Mystery* as a key transit point in Inspector French's continental investigation. French passed through Brussels while traveling between England and Antwerp, using it as a base for research and connections [016-014].

## French's Investigation

After discovering a hotel bill fragment suggesting the conspirators had crossed to the Continent, French narrowed the possible location to "the whole of Belgium and the northwest of France" [016-014]. He determined that the town he sought was unlikely to be east of Brussels and Paris, reasoning that a capital city would include a street address, whereas the bill gave only a hotel and town name [016-014].

## Transit Hub

> French arrived at Brussels-Nord station after a "great through express from Ostend to Brussels, Aix-la-Chapelle, Strasbourg, and, it seemed to him, the whole of the rest of Europe" [016-014]. From there he took an express to Antwerp, arriving at "a few minutes after three o'clock."

French used his brief stop in Brussels to purchase a map of Antwerp at a bookstall before continuing his journey [016-014]. The city served as his gateway to the Belgian investigation that would ultimately lead him to the Merkel & Lowenthal shipping office and the trail of the *L'Escaut*.
