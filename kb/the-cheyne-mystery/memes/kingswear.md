# Kingswear

**Kingswear** is a village on the eastern bank of the River Dart in Devon, England, directly opposite [[dartmouth]]. It serves as a transit and observation point during the Dart estuary section of *The Cheyne Mystery*, where [[maxwell-cheyne]] and [[joan-merrill]] pursue leads related to the [[enid|Enid]] launch.

## In the Story

Kingswear features in [005-006] when Cheyne and Joan Merrill urgently cross the river to reach the village with "the minimum of delay possible." They are pursuing information about the mysterious launch *Enid*, which had been sighted in the Dart estuary.

The geography of Kingswear and its surroundings is described in [005-007], which notes that the land east of Dartmouth forms a peninsula, with Dartmouth and Kingswear situated to the southwest. The road network connects Kingswear in the southwest corner to [[brixham]] in the northeast.

In [005-008], the narrative details that the main road from Kingswear to Brixham runs along high ground, providing extensive views of the surrounding countryside and waterways — a detail that becomes relevant to the pursuit.

A practical detail appears in [005-021]: the railway line from [[paignton]] has its terminus at Kingswear, from which a ferry connects across the river to Dartmouth. This makes Kingswear an important transit hub in the region.

## Geography

Kingswear occupies a strategic position on the east bank of the River Dart, opposite Dartmouth. The area forms part of a peninsula surrounded by the river's estuary. The road network links Kingswear northward to Brixham and Paignton, and the railway terminus provides a connection to the broader Devon rail network.
