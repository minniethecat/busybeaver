## Role in the Story

Sime is a key member of the criminal gang led by [[dangle|James Dangle]]. Together with Dangle and [[blessington|Blessington]], Sime forms the core of the conspiracy to obtain the [[the-tracing|linen tracing]] and unlock [[schulzs-secret|Otto Schulz's secret]]. While Dangle provides the strategic direction — "We supply the brains, you and Sime only rise to the muscles" [006-021] — Sime executes the physical aspects of their operations, including the [[warren-lodge-burglary|burglary at Warren Lodge]].

## Background

Sime's background is revealed through his association with Dangle. He is described as the motorman of [[the-enid|the Enid]], the yacht used by the conspirators [006-024]. This role suggests technical and maritime skills that make him useful to Dangle's operations. Sime is also involved in photography, operating the camera during the conspirators' documentation of evidence [006-015].

Speedwell later described Sime's profession to Cheyne: "Sime is another of the same kidney. He does the night club end and brings likely mugs on to the gaming rooms. A plausible ruffian, Sime. A man without scruple and bad to be up against. He has no ostensible business, either."

> "Sime is another of the same kidney. He does the night club end and brings likely mugs on to the gaming rooms. A plausible ruffian, Sime. A man without scruple and bad to be up against." [009-075]

## Key Actions

### The Burglary at Warren Lodge

Sime participates directly in the burglary at [[warren-lodge|Warren Lodge]], working under Dangle's direction to search for the tracing [006-010]. Dangle directs Sime during the operation, demonstrating the hierarchical nature of their partnership.

> "Sime is right," Price decided. [006-010]

### The Photographic Documentation

During the conspirators' activities, Sime operates the camera equipment used to photograph documents. While Dangle handles the lens, Sime burns magnesium wire to provide illumination for the photographs [006-016]. This suggests a degree of technical competence beyond mere physical enforcement.

> At the same time Sime burned a length of magnesium wire to provide the flash. [006-016]

### The Enid Operations

As the motorman of [[the-enid|the Enid]], Sime plays a crucial role in the conspirators' maritime operations. The yacht serves as a mobile base for the gang, and Sime's ability to operate it is essential to their mobility and escape capabilities [006-024].

### The Discovery of the Ladder

During the events at Hopefield Avenue, it was Sime who discovered the ladder outside the window, alerting the gang to Cheyne's presence.

> Then Sime's voice came, grumbling and muffled: "Where the blazes do you keep the darned stuff? How can I find it in the dark?" There was a moment's pause, then in a changed voice a sudden sharp call of "Here, James! Look here quickly! What's this?" [006-039]
