# Species Index

Throughout the *Journal of a Disappointed Man*, Barbellion records his encounters with a wide range of animal and plant species — from the birds of the Devon countryside to marine invertebrates collected along the coast, from insects caught under the microscope to mammals tracked by their signs in the field. This index brings together all species pages in a single, dynamically updated view, with scientific names, taxonomic groups, habitats, and observation details drawn directly from each page's frontmatter.

## About This Index

The species listed here represent Barbellion's direct, first-hand observations — not merely creatures mentioned in passing from his reading. His taxonomic interests ranged from the common (house sparrows, moles) to the meticulous (sea urchins requiring dissection, microscopic pond life). The index reflects both the breadth of his naturalist curiosity and the depth of his observational patience.

Species pages in this wiki are classified under `type: species` and include key frontmatter fields such as `scientific_name` (binomial nomenclature), `taxonomic_group` (Bird, Fish, Insect, Mammal, Plant, Marine-Invertebrate), `habitat`, `observation_place`, and `observation_date`.
