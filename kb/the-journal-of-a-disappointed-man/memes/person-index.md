# Person Index

The *Journal of a Disappointed Man* is peopled with a wide cast — fellow naturalists at the British Museum and Zoological Society, literary correspondents, physicians attending to his multiple sclerosis, family members, and chance acquaintances met on field excursions. This index brings together every person who appears in the Journal as a named individual with a dedicated wiki page, with key biographical details drawn from each page's frontmatter.

## About This Index

Personnel pages are classified under `type: person` and include fields for `nationality`, `affiliation`, `relation` (colleague, literary-influence, family, physician, acquaintance), and `born`/`died` dates. The index is dynamically maintained — any new person page automatically appears here.
