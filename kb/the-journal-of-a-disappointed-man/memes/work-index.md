# Work Index

Barbellion was a voracious reader and a keen cultural consumer. His Journal is threaded with references to books he read, music he heard, plays he attended, scientific papers he studied, and periodicals that shaped his intellectual world. This index collects every work that has a dedicated wiki page, with its creator, medium, year, and — perhaps most revealing — Barbellion's own recorded response to it.

:::

## About This Index

Work pages use `type: work` with fields for `creator`, `medium` (Book, Music, Article, Play, Scientific-Paper, Periodical), `year`, `genre`, `language`, and `barbellion_response` — which captures whether Barbellion admired, criticized, felt ambivalence toward, or drew inspiration from the work.
