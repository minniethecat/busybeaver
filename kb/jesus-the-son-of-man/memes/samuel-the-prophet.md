> The prophet who anointed the first kings of Israel, standing at the threshold between the age of judges and the monarchy.

Gibran invokes Samuel among the lineage of Hebrew prophets whose voices echo through the spiritual landscape of his Jesus narrative. Samuel's role as anointer of kings prefigures the messianic themes that Gibran explores in his portrait of Jesus.
