## Nathaniel

[020-001]They say that [[jesus-of-nazareth|Jesus of Nazareth]] was humble and meek.

[020-002]They say that though He was a just man and righteous, He was a weakling, and was often confounded by the strong and the powerful; and that when He stood before men of authority He was but a lamb among lions.

[020-003]But I say Jesus had authority over men, and that He knew His power and proclaimed it among the hills of [[galilee|Galilee]], and in the cities of [[judea|Judea]] and Phoenicia.

[020-004]What man yielding and soft would say: “I am life, and I am the way to truth”?

[020-005]What man meek and lowly would say: “I am in God, our Father; and our God, the Father, is in me”?

[020-006]What man unmindful of His own strength would say: “He who believes not in me believes not in this life nor in the life everlasting”?

[020-007]What man uncertain of tomorrow would proclaim: “Your world shall pass away and be naught but scattered ashes ere my words shall pass away”?

[020-008]Was He doubtful of Himself when He said to those who would confound Him with a harlot: “He who is without sin, let him cast a stone”?

[020-009]Did He fear authority when He drove the money-changers from the court of the temple, though they were licensed by the priests?

[020-010]Were His wings shorn when He cried aloud: “My kingdom is above your earthly kingdoms”?

[020-011]Was He seeking shelter in words when He repeated again and yet again: “Destroy this temple and I will rebuild it in three days”?

[020-012]Was it a coward who shook His hand in the face of the authorities and pronounced them “liars, low, filthy, and degenerate”?

[020-013]Shall a man bold enough to say these things to those who ruled Judea be deemed meek and humble?

[020-014]Nay. The eagle builds not his nest in the weeping willow. And the lion seeks not his den among the ferns.

[020-015]I am sickened and the bowels within me stir and rise when I hear the fainthearted call Jesus humble and meek, that they may justify their own faintheartedness; and when the downtrodden, for comfort and companionship, speak of Jesus as a worm shining by their side.

[020-016]Yea, my heart is sickened by such men. It is the mighty hunter I would preach, and the mountainous spirit unconquerable.
