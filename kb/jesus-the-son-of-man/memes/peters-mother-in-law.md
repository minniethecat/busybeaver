> The mother of Simon Peter's wife, whom Jesus healed of a fever in Capernaum.

Gibran includes this healing episode among the quiet miracles that reveal Jesus's compassion for ordinary domestic life. The woman's restoration to health and her immediate service afterward illustrate the wholeness that Jesus's touch brings to both body and community.
