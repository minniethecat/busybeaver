## A Cobbler in Jerusalem

[061-001]I loved Him not, yet I did not hate Him. I listened to Him not to hear His words but rather the sound of His voice; for His voice pleased me.

[061-002]All that He said was vague to my mind, but the music thereof was clear to my ear.

[061-003]Indeed were it not for what others have said to me of His teaching, I should not have known even so much as whether He was with [[judea|Judea]] or against it.
