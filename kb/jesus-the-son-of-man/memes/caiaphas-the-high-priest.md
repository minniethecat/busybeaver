## Caiaphas the High Priest

[007-001]In speaking of that man [[jesus-of-nazareth|Jesus]] and of His death let us consider two salient facts: the Torah must needs be held in safety by us, and this kingdom must needs be protected by [[romans|Rome]].

[007-002]Now that man was defiant to us and to [[rome|Rome]]. He poisoned the mind of the simple people, and He led them as if by magic against us and against Caesar.

[007-003]My own slaves, both men and women, after hearing him speak in the marketplace, turned sullen and rebellious. Some of them left my house and escaped to the desert whence they came.

[007-004]Forget not that the Torah is our foundation and our tower of strength. No man shall undermine us while we have this power to restrain his hand, and no man shall overthrow [[jerusalem|Jerusalem]] so long as its walls stand upon the ancient stone that David laid.

[007-005]If the seed of Abraham is indeed to live and thrive this soil must remain undefiled.

[007-006]And that man Jesus was a defiler and a corrupter. We slew Him with a conscience both deliberate and clean. And we shall slay all those who would debase the laws of Moses or seek to befoul our sacred heritage.

[007-007]We and [[pontius-pilate|Pontius Pilatus]] knew the danger in that man, and that it was wise to bring Him to an end.

[007-008]I shall see that His followers come to the same end, and the echo of His words to the same silence.

[007-009]If [[judea|Judea]] is to live all men who oppose her must be brought down to the dust. And ere Judea shall die I will cover my grey head with ashes even as did Samuel the prophet, and I will tear off this garment of Aaron and clothe me in sackcloth until I go hence forever.
