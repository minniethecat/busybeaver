## Barabbas

[072-001]They released me and chose Him. Then He rose and I fell down.

[072-002]And they held Him a victim and a sacrifice for the Passover.

[072-003]I was freed from my chains, and walked with the throng behind Him, but I was a living man going to my own grave.

[072-004]I should have fled to the desert, where shame is burned out by the sun.

[072-005]Yet I walked with those who had chosen Him to bear my crime.

[072-006]When they nailed Him on His cross I stood there.

[072-007]I saw and I heard but I seemed outside of my body.

[072-008]The thief who was crucified on His right said to Him: “Are you bleeding with me, even you, [[jesus-of-nazareth|Jesus of Nazareth]]?”

[072-009]And Jesus answered and said: “Were it not for this nail that stays my hand I would reach forth and clasp your hand.

[072-010]“We are crucified together. Would they had raised your cross nearer to mine.”

[072-011]Then He looked down and gazed upon His mother and a young man who stood beside her.

[072-012]He said: “Mother, behold your son standing beside you.

[072-013]“Woman, behold a man who shall carry these drops of my blood to the North Country.”

[072-014]And when he heard the wailing of the women of [[galilee|Galilee]] He said: “Behold, they weep and I thirst.

[072-015]“I am held too high to reach their tears.

[072-016]“I will not take vinegar and gall to quench this thirst.”

[072-017]Then His eyes opened wide to the sky, and He said: “Father, why hast Thou forsaken us?”

[072-018]And then He said in compassion: “Father, forgive them, for they know not what they do.”

[072-019]When He uttered those words methought I saw all men prostrated before God beseeching forgiveness for the crucifixion of this one man.

[072-020]Then again He said with a great voice: “Father, into Thy hand I yield back my spirit.”

[072-021]And at last He lifted up His head and said: “Now it is finished, but only upon this hill.”

[072-022]And He closed His eyes.

[072-023]Then lightning cracked the dark skies, and there was a great thunder.

---

[072-024]I know now that those who slew Him in my stead achieved my endless torment.

[072-025]His crucifixion endured but for an hour.

[072-026]But I shall be crucified unto the end of my years.
