> Rafca the Bride of Cana speaks among the seventy-nine voices in Gibran's polyphonic portrait of Jesus.

Rafca the Bride of Cana testifies in a single chapter [009-001–009-027] of *[[titlepage|Jesus the Son of Man]]*.

## Testimony

## Significance

Each of the seventy-nine witnesses in Gibran's book brings a distinct angle of vision. Rafca the Bride of Cana represents one such perspective — the accumulation of these partial views creates the book's unique, prismatic portrait of [[jesus-of-nazareth|Jesus]].
