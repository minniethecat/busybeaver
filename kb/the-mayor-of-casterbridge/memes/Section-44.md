# Section 44

(043-001) “Ah, therein lies a grand secret,” said [[joshua-jopp|Jopp]]. “It is the passion of

(043-002) love. To think that a woman should love one man so well, and hate

(043-121) Such was the state of things when the current affairs of Casterbridge

(043-129) further west, to inaugurate an immense engineering work out that way.

(043-135) farming on a more scientific footing.
