# Joshua Jopp

A minor character with major consequences. Joshua Jopp is [[michael-henchard|Henchard]]'s former manager, discharged to make room for [[donald-farfrae|Farfrae]]. His resentment at this treatment makes him the vector through which [[lucetta-templeman|Lucetta]]'s letters reach the public sphere — a small act of spite with lethal consequences.

## Role in the Novel

Jopp's function is mechanical but crucial: he is the man who delivers [[lucetta-templeman|Lucetta]]'s letters to the [[mixen-lane|Mixen Lane]] crowd, enabling the [[the-skimmity-ride|skimmity-ride]]. Hardy uses him to show how personal grievances, however petty, can produce catastrophic outcomes when they intersect with larger social forces. Jopp does not intend to destroy [[lucetta-templeman|Lucetta]] — but his resentment is the match that lights the fire.
