# Alderman Vatt

An alderman of [[casterbridge|Casterbridge]] who appears in the council proceedings, most notably as the bearer of the news of Dr. Chalkfield's death — a moment that underscores the quiet turnover of [[casterbridge|the town]]'s civic life.

## Appearances

Vatt is introduced as a neighbour and council member:

> "Their neighbour Alderman Vatt" [040-064]

He announces the death of Dr. Chalkfield, a fellow councilman:

> "This afternoon at five," said Mr. Vatt. Chalkfield was the Councilman. [040-067]

Vatt's brief role is that of messenger — [[casterbridge|the town]]'s institutional life continues through such small, procedural moments, even as the personal dramas of [[michael-henchard|Henchard]] and [[lucetta-templeman|Lucetta]] consume the novel's foreground.
