(021-001) These precincts embodied the mournful phases of [[casterbridge|Casterbridge]] life, as

(021-002) the south avenues embodied its cheerful moods. The whole way along here

(021-003) was sunless, even in summer time; in spring, white frosts lingered here

(021-006) year. The Casterbridge doctors must have pined away for want of

(021-007) sufficient nourishment but for the configuration of the landscape on

(021-008) the north-eastern side.

(021-009) The river—slow, noiseless, and dark—the Schwarzwasser of

(021-010) Casterbridge—ran beneath a low cliff, the two together forming a

(021-011) defence which had rendered walls and artificial earthworks on this side

(021-013) attached to the same, the water of which roared down a back-hatch like

(021-014) the voice of desolation. Above the cliff, and behind [[the-river|the river]], rose a

(021-015) pile of buildings, and in the front of the pile a square mass cut into

(021-016) the sky. It was like a pedestal lacking its statue. This missing

(021-017) feature, without which the design remained incomplete, was, in [[appearance-and-reality|truth]],

(021-018) the corpse of a man, for the square mass formed the base of the

(021-019) gallows, the extensive buildings at the back being the county gaol. In

(021-020) the meadow where [[michael-henchard|Henchard]] now walked the mob were wont to gather

(021-021) whenever an execution took place, and there to the tune of the roaring

(021-022) weir they stood and watched the spectacle.

(021-023) The exaggeration which darkness imparted to the glooms of this region

(021-024) impressed Henchard more than he had expected. The lugubrious harmony of

(021-030) single gentleman; and climbed up by a steep back lane into the town.

(021-042) asserted her to be, and his daughter she should always think herself,

(021-043) no matter what hyprocrisy it involved.

(021-044) But he was ill-prepared for the first step in this new situation. The

(021-064) Of all the enigmas which ever confronted a girl there can have been

(021-065) seldom one like that which followed Henchard’s announcement of himself

(021-068) behold, from the next morning onwards his manner was constrained as she

(021-069) had never seen it before.

(021-070) The coldness soon broke out into open chiding. One grievous failing of

(021-071) Elizabeth’s was her occasional pretty and picturesque use of dialect

(021-072) words—those terrible marks of the beast to the truly genteel.

(021-073) It was dinner-time—they never met except at meals—and she happened to

(021-074) say when he was rising from table, wishing to show him something, “If

(021-075) you’ll bide where you be a minute, father, I’ll get it.”

(021-076) “‘Bide where you be,’” he echoed sharply, “Good God, are you only fit

(021-077) to carry wash to a pig-trough, that ye use such words as those?”

(021-079) “I meant ‘Stay where you are,’ father,” she said, in a low, humble
