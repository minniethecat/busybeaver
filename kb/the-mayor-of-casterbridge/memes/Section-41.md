# Section 41

(040-001) “Is that so—ah, is that so?” said [[donald-farfrae|Farfrae]], looking down. “Why should he

(040-002) do it?” added the young man bitterly; “what harm have I done him that

(040-005) long-[[regret-and-suffering|suffering]] in you to put up with him, and keep him in your employ.”

(040-007) I forget that when I came here ’twas he enabled me to make a footing

(040-011) more about it.”

(040-013) thrown over it by these and other voices in the air, he went and

(040-014) countermanded his orders. The then occupier of the shop was in it when

(040-015) Farfrae spoke to him and feeling it necessary to give some explanation

(040-016) of his withdrawal from the negotiation Donald mentioned [[michael-henchard|Henchard]]’s

(040-017) name, and stated that the intentions of the Council had been changed.

(040-019) as soon as he saw him, that a scheme of the Council for setting him up

(040-020) in a shop had been knocked on the head by Farfrae. And thus out of
