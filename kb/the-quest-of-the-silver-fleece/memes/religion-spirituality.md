# Religion and Spirituality

Faith runs through *The Quest of the Silver Fleece* as both a communal institution and a private
conscience. The church anchors the Black community of Tooms County; individual characters grapple
with moral questions that Du Bois frames in language shaped by the Black church tradition. This page
collects entries at the intersection of religion, morality, and spiritual experience.
