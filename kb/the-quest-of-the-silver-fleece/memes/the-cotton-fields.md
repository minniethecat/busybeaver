# The Cotton Fields

The vast planted acreage of [[tooms-county|Tooms County]] where black laborers cultivate the crop that drives the Southern [[economics|economy]]—a landscape of both beauty and [[greed|exploitation]].

## Description

The cotton [[field|fields]] are the novel's most pervasive physical setting. They are where [[bles-alwyn|Bles]] studies, where [[zora|Zora]] labors, where the community gathers, and where the brutal arithmetic of the cotton [[economics|economy]] is written into the landscape itself (003-013)(004-007)(006-001).
