# Places and Settings

The world of *The Quest of the Silver Fleece* moves between two poles: the Alabama Black Belt,
with its cotton fields and swamplands, and Washington, D.C., with its parlors and political offices.
This page organizes entries by the physical and social spaces that structure the novel's geography.
