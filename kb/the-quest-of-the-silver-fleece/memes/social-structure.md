# Social Structure

Du Bois's *The Quest of the Silver Fleece* renders the layered social world of the post-Reconstruction South
and early-twentieth-century Washington, D.C. This page organizes wiki entries by the forces that shape
social position—class, community bonds, institutional authority, and the structures of power that surround
the novel's characters.
