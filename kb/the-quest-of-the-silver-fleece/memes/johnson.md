## Key Relationships

A preacher and community leader whose role in the black [[the-church|church]] represents indigenous black leadership—complex, compromised, and essential.

## Key Scenes

Brother Johnson serves as [[preacher|pastor]] of the black [[the-church|church]] in [[tooms-county|Tooms County]]. He is a figure of [[power|authority]] within the black community, his [[power|influence]] deriving from the pulpit rather than the plantation—a source of power that the white establishment cannot fully [[power|control]] (022-110).

## Character Arc

- With the Congregation: [[john-taylor|John]]son leads the spiritual life of the community, his sermons shaping how his flock understands their condition.
