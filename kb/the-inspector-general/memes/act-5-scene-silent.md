[058-001]The **[[anton-antonovich|Governor]]** stands in the center rigid as a [[post-office|post]], with outstretched hands and head thrown backward. On his right are his **wife** and **daughter** straining toward him. Back of them the **[[ivan-shpekin|Postmaster]]**, turned toward the audience, metamorphosed into a question mark. Next to him, at the edge of the group, three lady guests leaning on each other, with a most satirical expression on their faces directed straight at the **Governor’s family**. To the left of the **Governor** is [[artemy-zemlianika|Artemy Zemlianika]], his head to one side as if listening. Behind him is the **[[ammos-liapkin-tiapkin|Judge]]** with outspread hands almost crouching on the ground and pursing his lips as if to whistle or say: “A nice pickle we’re in!” Next to him is **Korobkin**, turned toward the audience, with eyes screwed up and making a venomous gesture at the **Governor**. Next to him, at the edge of the group, are [[piotr-dobchinsky|Piotr Dobchinsky]] and [[piotr-bobchinsky|Piotr Bobchinsky]], gesticulating at each other, open-mouthed and wide-eyed. The other guests remain standing stiff. The whole group retain the same position of rigidity for almost a minute and a half. The curtain falls.
