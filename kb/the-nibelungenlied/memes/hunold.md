## Introduction

**Hunold** is the chamberlain (Kammerer) at the [[burgundian-court|Burgundian Court]] in [[worms|Worms]]. He serves under King [[gunther|Gunther]] and is responsible for managing the royal household and its ceremonial functions (001-038, 001-044).

## Role at Court

Hunold appears primarily in the early adventures of the Nibelungenlied as part of the Burgundian court establishment. He is listed alongside other court officials, including [[sindold|Sindold]] the cupbearer, as one of the noble lords who maintain the honor and splendor of the Burgundian royal household (001-038).

Hunold's role as chamberlain places him in charge of the king's chambers, household staff, and ceremonial hospitality — important functions in the elaborate court culture depicted in the poem (001-044).

## Military Service

Hunold also participates in military campaigns. During the **Saxon Invasion** (Adventure 4), he fights alongside the Burgundian forces against the Saxons and Danes, proving himself a capable warrior as well as a court official (004-137, 004-245).
