## Introduction

**Wolfbrand** is one of the warriors serving under [[dietrich|Dietrich von Bern]] at [[etzels-court|Etzel's Court]]. He appears in the final adventures of the Nibelungenlied as part of Dietrich's retinue and participates in the climactic battle in the great hall (038-105).

## Role in the Final Battle

Wolfbrand first appears in **Adventure 38** alongside other warriors in Dietrich's company, including Helferich, Helmnot, Wolfwin, and Sigestab (038-105). When the battle erupts in Etzel's hall between the Burgundians and the Huns, Wolfbrand is among Dietrich's men who enter the fray.

The poem describes Wolfbrand fighting fiercely in the storm of battle, moving through the hall with martial prowess (038-188). He fights alongside the other warriors of Dietrich's retinue until he is ultimately slain in the carnage (038-355).

## Dietrich's Retinue

Wolfbrand belongs to the group of warriors who follow Dietrich von Bern, the exiled king living at Etzel's court. Dietrich's men are drawn into the conflict reluctantly, as Dietrich himself attempts to remain neutral in the feud between [[kriemhild|Kriemhild]] and [[hagen|Hagen]].
