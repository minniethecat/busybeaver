## Introduction

**Else** is a Bavarian margrave and the brother of [[gelfrat|Gelfrat]]. Together they rule the Bavarian March, the territory along the [[danube|Danube River]] that the Burgundians must cross on their journey to [[hunland|Hunland]] (025-176).

## Role in the Narrative

Else is first mentioned in **Adventure 25** as the lord of the Bavarian March, alongside his brother Gelfrat. When [[hagen|Hagen]] kills Else's ferryman for refusing to transport the Burgundian army across the Danube, Else loses one of his men (026-022). Else and Gelfrat then gather forces to pursue the Burgundians (026-027).

Else fights alongside his brother in the skirmish against the Burgundian army. The confrontation leads to Gelfrat's death at the hands of [[dankwart|Dankwart]].

## The Bavarian March

As margrave of the Bavarian borderlands, Else held authority over the Danube crossing point, including the ferryman who was slain by Hagen. The Bavarian March served as a territorial buffer between the Burgundian kingdom and the lands to the east.
