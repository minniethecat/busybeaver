## Introduction

**Sindold** is the cupbearer (Schenke) at the [[burgundian-court|Burgundian Court]] in [[worms|Worms]]. He serves under King [[gunther|Gunther]] and holds the important ceremonial office of wine service at royal feasts and gatherings (001-038, 001-043).

## Role at Court

Sindold appears among the court officials listed in the opening adventure of the Nibelungenlied. As cupbearer (*Schenke*), he is responsible for overseeing the wine service — a prestigious position in medieval court culture that involves managing the royal cellars and directing the ceremonial distribution of drink at feasts (001-043).

He serves alongside [[hunold|Hunold]] the chamberlain, together forming part of the noble household that maintains the splendor of Gunther's court. In the elaborate world of the Burgundian court, the cupbearer's role carries both practical and symbolic weight (001-038).

## Military Participation

Sindold also fights in battle. During the **Saxon Invasion** (Adventure 4), he rides out with the Burgundian army alongside Hunold, [[gernot|Gernot]], and other warriors, demonstrating that court officials were expected to be capable fighters as well (004-094, 004-137, 004-245).
