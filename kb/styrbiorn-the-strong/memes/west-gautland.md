# West Gautland

**West Gautland** (modern Västergötland) was a western province of Sweden, part of the realm over which [[king-eric|King Eric the Victorious]] held dominion. It was the ancestral home of the Earls of West Gautland, from whom [[gudrid|Gudrid]] — mother of [[sigrid-the-queen|Sigrid the Queen]] — was descended, and a source of noble warriors in Styrbiorn's host.

## King Eric's Dominion

West Gautland was among the many lands and kingdoms ruled by [[king-eric|King Eric]], alongside the Up-Swedes, Nether-realm, Southmanland, and [[east-gautland|East Gautland]], and all countries eastward from the Elf to the main sea (001-002). Together with [[east-gautland|East Gautland]] (Östergötland), it formed the heartland of the Geatish [[all-persons|people]], distinct from the Swedes of Uppland but united under Eric's crown.

> Eric the Victorious was in that time King in Upsala... and had dominion over the Up-Swedes and over the folk of Nether-realm and Southmanland and East and West Gautland and over all countries and kingdoms eastaway from the Elf to the main sea. (001-002)

The two Gautlands — West and East — were historically separate from the Swedish kingdom proper and retained a distinct identity even under Eric's rule. Their inclusion in Eric's list of dominions signalled the extent of his power and the unification of the Geatish and Swedish [[all-persons|people]]s under a single crown.

## Sigrid's Kinship

[[gudrid|Gudrid]], the wife of [[skogul-tosti|Skogul-Tosti]] and mother of [[sigrid-the-queen|Sigrid the Queen]], was of the kin of the Earls of West Gautland. She was described as fair to look on but proud, grasping, and somewhat hard of heart (003-002).

> His wife was named Gudrid. She was a stirring woman, of the kin of the Earls of West Gautland. She was fair to look on, but folk deemed her over proud and grasping and somewhat hard of heart. (003-002)

This lineage meant that [[queen-sigrid|Sigrid the Queen]] — who would later become Styrbiorn's wife, albeit in a marriage that remained unconsummated — carried the blood of the West Gautland earls through her mother. The connection between the Swedish royal house and the West Gautland nobility was thus both political and familial.

## Warriors in Styrbiorn's Host

When Styrbiorn marshalled his forces for the invasion of Sweden, West Gautland contributed notable warriors to his cause. Bodvar Auzurson of Leikberg in [[east-gautland|East Gautland]] was among the named captains, alongside men from [[tenthland|Tenthland]], [[skaney|Skane]]y, and other territories (014-007). The Gautland provinces, with their tradition of producing hardy fighting men, were an important source of military strength.

> Bodvar Auzurson of Leikberg in East Gautland (014-007)
