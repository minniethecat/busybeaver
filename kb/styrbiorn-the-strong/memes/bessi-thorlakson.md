## Biography

Bessi Thorlakson was a veteran [[jomsburg|Jomsburg]] warrior and one of "the chiefest among Styrbiorn's following" (007-070). He had been "many years vikings in Jom" alongside other seasoned warriors such as Thorolf Attercop, Howard the Hewer, and Alf Braggart (014-055).

Bessi is most remembered for a single violent act during a sea battle: when Eystein came pitching forward across the gunwale of [[palnatoki|Palnatoki]]'s ship, Bessi "fetched him with an axe-hammer so mighty a blow on the head that all the brains were beaten out of it" (011-045). Men said there was an old blood-feud between the two, and that Bessi cried out as he struck: "So knock we the harns out of Foxes" (011-045). This killing rallied the [[jomsvikings|Jomsburgers]], and [[palnatoki|Palnatoki]] "egged on his men right fiercely" in its wake.

When Styrbiorn sailed for Sweden with his fleet to claim the throne, Bessi was among those who accompanied him. He fared with ten ships alongside [[gunnstein-lowry|Gunnstein Lowry]] and others of lesser note, while Biorn Asbrandson remained behind due to sickness (014-085).

## Role in the Story

Bessi Thorlakson represents the veteran [[jomsburg|Jomsburg]] warrior class — hardened men of long service whose violent skills serve Styrbiorn's ambition. His killing of Eystein, motivated by an old blood-feud, illustrates the personal vendettas that ran beneath the surface of [[jomsburg|Jomsburg]]'s warrior fellowship. His distinctive battle-cry — "So knock we the harns out of Foxes" — is one of the few individual war-cries recorded in the novel's battle scenes.

## Historical Basis

Bessi Thorlakson appears to be a fictional character created by E.R. Eddison. His patronymic "Thorlakson" follows Norse naming conventions, but no historical Bessi matching this description appears in the known Styrbiorn sources. His role as a veteran [[jomsburg|Jomsburg]] warrior and his distinctive axe-hammer kill contribute to Eddison's vivid battle-tableau style.
