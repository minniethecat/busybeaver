## Geography

East Gautland (modern Östergötland) was an eastern province of Sweden, mentioned alongside [[helsingland|Helsingland]], [[tenthland|Tenthland]], and [[west-gautland|West Gautland]] as lands owing allegiance to the Swedish crown (001-001). In the hosting of [[king-eric|King Eric]]'s forces, Bodvar Auzurson of Leikberg is listed among those called from East Gautland (014-007).
