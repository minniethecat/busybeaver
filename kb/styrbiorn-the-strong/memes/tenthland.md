# Tenthland

**Tenthland** was a province of Sweden, notable as the home of [[lambi-the-white|Lambi the White]] from [[stocksound|Stocksound]], the man proclaimed as a rival king during the disputed [[thing-stead|Thing]] at [[upsala|Upsala]].

## The Proclamation of Lambi the White

During [[king-eric|King Eric]]'s absence, a [[thing-stead|Thing]] was summoned at [[upsala|Upsala]] against all law. At this assembly, fierce disputes erupted between the bonders and [[styrbiorn-the-strong|Styrbiorn]]'s supporters. Styrbiorn declared that, by his father's right, he was King of half the realm of Sweden. In response, the bonders proclaimed [[lambi-the-white|Lambi the White]] of [[stocksound|Stocksound]] in Tenthland as King of that same half, in defiance of Styrbiorn's claim (002-016).

> They feared not to choose and proclaim one Lambi the White, a man from Stocksound in Tenthland, King in Styrbiorn's despite of that half of the kingdom claimed by him (002-016)

## Muster for Fyrisfield

Tenthland sent its Earl among the many lords who gathered at [[upsala|Upsala]] when [[king-eric|King Eric]] summoned the realm's forces to face Styrbiorn's invasion (016-014).
