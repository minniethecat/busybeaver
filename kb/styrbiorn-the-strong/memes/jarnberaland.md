# Jarnberaland

**Jarnberaland** (modern Järnbäraland) was a northern province of Sweden, one of the Swedish lands that contributed warriors to the host mustered before the [[battle-of-fyrisfield|Battle of Fyrisfield]].

## Geography

Jarnberaland lay in the northern coastal region of Sweden, visited by [[king-eric|King Eric]] during his spring progress through [[helsingland|Helsingland]] and Jarnberaland (002-014).

## Muster for Fyrisfield

When [[king-eric|King Eric]] called the holy [[thing-stead|Thing]] at [[upsala|Upsala]] to rally the Swedish forces against Styrbiorn's invasion, An the Black came from Jarnberaland with his company to answer the King's summons (016-014).

> And thither were gathered... An the Black from Jarnberaland (016-014)
