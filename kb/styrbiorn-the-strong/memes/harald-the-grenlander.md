## Identity

Harald the Grenlander was one of the many suitors who sought the hand of [[queen-sigrid|Sigrid Skogul-Tostisdottir]] before she became Queen of Sweden. According to [[gudrid|Gudrid]], Sigrid's mother, Sigrid had "said no to a dozen ere now, and not one of them but had been a great match" — Harald among them, and he had since become a king (003-014). No further details of his life or lordship appear in the saga.
