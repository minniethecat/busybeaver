## Biography

Tofa was the daughter of [[strut-harald|Strut-Harald]], the powerful Earl of [[skaney|Skane]]y, and the sister of three famous [[jomsburg|Jomsburg]] lords: [[sigvaldi|Sigvaldi]], [[heming|Heming]], and [[thorkell-the-high|Thorkell the High]] (004-002). Her marriage to [[sigurd-cape|Sigurd Cape]] of [[borgundholm|Borgundholm]] further cemented the dense web of kinship ties that bound together the leadership of the [[jomsburg|Jomsburg]] viking stronghold.

Through her marriage, Tofa also became sister-in-law to [[bui-digri|Bui Digri]] (also called Bui the Thick), [[sigurd-cape|Sigurd]]'s brother, making her a connecting figure between three of the most prominent families in the [[jomsburg|Jomsburg]] confederation: the Strut-Harald lineage of [[skaney|Skane]]y, the brothers of [[borgundholm|Borgundholm]], and the wider network of [[jomsburg|Jomsburg]] lords.


> Those three sons of Strut-Harald that was in that time a famous Earl in Skaney, Sigvaldi namely and Heming and Thorkell the High; and these three were brothers-in-law to Bui and Sigurd, since Sigurd had to wife Tofa the daughter of Strut-Harald.
>
> (004-002)

## Role in the Story

Tofa is mentioned only once in the narrative, in the roll-call of [[jomsburg|Jomsburg]] lords given when [[styrbiorn|Styrbiorn]] first arrives at [[jomsburg|Jomsburg]] (004-002). Her significance is genealogical rather than dramatic: she represents the marriage alliances that gave the [[jomsburg|Jomsburg]] vikings their formidable cohesion. The fact that [[sigurd-cape|Sigurd Cape]] was brother-in-law to Sigvaldi, [[heming|Heming]], and [[thorkell-the-high|Thorkell]] — through his marriage to Tofa — helps explain the political dynamics within the stronghold and the complex loyalties that would later play out in the saga's climactic [[all-events|events]].

As with many noblewomen in the saga, Tofa herself remains a background figure, her name preserved in the record primarily as a kinship link between powerful men.

## Relationships

- **Strut-Harald**: Her father, the magnificent Earl of [[skaney|Skane]]y
- **Sigvaldi, [[heming|Heming]], [[thorkell-the-high|Thorkell the High]]**: Her three brothers, all [[jomsburg|Jomsburg]] lords
- **[[sigurd-cape|Sigurd Cape]]**: Her husband, one of the [[jomsburg|Jomsburg]] lords of [[borgundholm|Borgundholm]]
- **[[bui-digri|Bui Digri]]**: Her brother-in-law through marriage to [[sigurd-cape|Sigurd]]
