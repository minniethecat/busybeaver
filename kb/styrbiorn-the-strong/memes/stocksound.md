## Location

Stocksound was a settlement in [[tenthland|Tenthland]], one of the Swedish provinces under [[king-eric|King Eric]]'s dominion (002-004). Its exact location is not specified beyond its association with the [[tenthland|Tenthland]] region, which lay within the Swedish realm that Eric ruled from [[upsala|Upsala]].


> They feared not to choose and proclaim one Lambi the White, a man from Stocksound in Tenthland, King in Styrbiorn's despite of that half of the kingdom claimed by him.
>
> (002-004)

## Significance in the Novel

Stocksound is notable as the home of [[lambi-the-white|Lambi the White]], the bonder who was proclaimed king by the rebellious bonder-folk at an unlawful [[thing-stead|Thing]] in [[upsala|Upsala]], in defiance of [[styrbiorn|Styrbiorn]]'s claim to joint kingship (002-004). Lambi's selection as a rival king — a man "from Stocksound in [[tenthland|Tenthland]]" — represented the bonder-folk's resistance to Styrbiorn's lawless and overbearing character.

The choice of a man from Stocksound, rather than from the more central regions of Sweden, may suggest that the resistance to Styrbiorn found its strongest support in the outlying provinces where the King's direct authority was weaker.
