# Characters

This page provides a complete index of all characters in Tolstoy's *Anna Karenina*. The novel's ensemble spans the Russian social hierarchy — from the aristocratic Oblonskys and Karenins to the peasants and servants who work their estates. Each entry links to a dedicated page with analysis, quotations from the Garnett translation, and narrative context.

## Notes

Character entries are drawn from across all eight parts of the novel. Reference counts reflect cross-page wikilink citations within this wiki and serve as a proxy for the character's narrative prominence. Quality ratings follow the wiki's five-tier scale (stub / basic / standard / featured / premium).
