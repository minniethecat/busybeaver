# Satyagraha Sabha

The Satyagraha Sabha was a dedicated organization established by Gandhi in Bombay in 1919 to coordinate the Rowlatt Satyagraha — the first all-India campaign of civil disobedience. "A separate body called the Satyagraha Sabha was established at my instance. Its principal members were drawn from Bombay" (153-014). Thousands signed the satyagraha pledge, committing to nonviolent defiance of the Rowlatt Acts.

## Gandhi's Relationship

The Sabha was Gandhi's creation — a dedicated organization for satyagraha distinct from the Congress. It represented his recognition that nonviolent resistance required a specialized body with its own discipline, pledge, and training, rather than ad hoc political mobilization.
