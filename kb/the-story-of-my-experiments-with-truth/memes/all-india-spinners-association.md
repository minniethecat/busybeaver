# All-India Spinners' Association

The All-India Spinners' Association (AISA) was founded to organize and promote hand-spinning and the production of khadi throughout India. It was the institutional expression of Gandhi's conviction that the spinning wheel was both an economic tool (providing supplementary income to millions of villagers) and a spiritual discipline (cultivating patience, concentration, and identification with the poor).

## Gandhi's Relationship

Gandhi was the driving force behind AISA. He required Congress members to spin daily and to wear khadi produced by the Association. AISA organized production, set quality standards, marketed khadi, and educated the public about swadeshi. It was one of the several organizations through which Gandhi sought to build the economic foundations of swaraj independently of the state.
