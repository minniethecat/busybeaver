## Overview

"Sun and Saddle Leather" is a collection of cowboy poetry by [[charles-badger-clark|Charles Badger Clark]], Jr. (1883–1957), who served as a [[colorado|Colorado]] [[puncher|cowpuncher]]. The anthology's biographical notes reference this work: 'See "Sun and Saddle Leather," by [[charles-badger-clark|Charles Badger Clark]], Jr.' (077-001).

> See "Sun and Saddle Leather," by Charles Badger Clark, Jr...
>
> (077-001)
