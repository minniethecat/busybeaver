# Lasso

The lasso (also called lariat or reata) is the cowboy's rope with a running noose, an essential tool for roping cattle on the open range. In the anthology, it appears as part of the working cowboy's everyday equipment.

In "[[A-Ranger]]" the cowboy rides with his "lasso at his side" as he roams the plains (073-001), while in another poem the speaker exclaims "dog blast my lasso" as an oath of surprise upon recognizing a fellow rider (063-001).
