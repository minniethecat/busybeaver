# Sonora

Sonora is a Mexican state referenced in two poems from the collection as part of the borderlands cattle culture.

In "[[To-Hear-Him-Tell-It]]," cattle come "from down across the line—[[chihuahua|Chihuahua]] an' Sonora stuff" (014-001), placing Sonora as a source region for cross-border livestock. In "[[A-Border-Affair]]," a narrator describes learning a song from a girl "livin' down Sonora way" (024-001), associating the region with the romantic balladry of the border.
