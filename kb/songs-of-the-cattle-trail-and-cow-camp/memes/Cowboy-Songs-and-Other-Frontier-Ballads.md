## Overview

[[john-a-lomax|John A. Lomax]]'s first major collection of [[singing-and-songmaking|cowboy songs]] and [[the-west|frontier]] ballads, published in 1910. As the Introduction notes, the present volume is a "product of my earlier collection, Cowboy Songs and Other Frontier Ballads" (P03-002). The earlier book was a comprehensive gathering, while this anthology focuses more narrowly on songs of the cattle trail and cow camp.
