# Index of Poets

The poets and authors collected in [[john-a-lomax|John A. Lomax]]'s "[[Songs-of-the-Cattle-Trail-and-Cow-Camp]]" (1916) range from celebrated cowboy poets like [[Charles-Badger-Clark]] to newspaper versifiers, working cowboys who composed around the campfire, and anonymous contributors whose names have been lost. This index lists every poet with a dedicated page in the wiki, ranked alphabetically by name.
