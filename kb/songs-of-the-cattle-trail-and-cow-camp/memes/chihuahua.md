# Chihuahua

Chihuahua is a Mexican state referenced in two poems from the collection, appearing in the context of border cattle trade and historical communication routes.

In "[[To-Hear-Him-Tell-It]]," cattle are described as "Chihuahua an' [[sonora|Sonora]] stuff" (014-001), marking the region as a source of livestock driven north across the border. In "[[The-Bull-Fight]]," "the couriers from Chihuahua go to distant Cusi and Santavo" (027-001), evoking the historical relay networks of northern Mexico.
