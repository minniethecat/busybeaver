# Costock

## Overview

Costock is a minor character connected to the Whistlefield Maze investigation. While not central to the novel's main narrative, their presence contributes to the social fabric of the case — the network of relationships, suspicions, and potential motives that Sir Clinton Driffield must navigate.

## Role in the Plot

Costock's involvement in the case adds to the complexity of the suspect pool. Sir Clinton's systematic approach to elimination considers every person with potential access, motive, or opportunity — and Costock is among those whose circumstances must be evaluated and either cleared or pursued.

## Relationships

Connected to the Shandon family and Whistlefield household through the circumstances of the investigation.
