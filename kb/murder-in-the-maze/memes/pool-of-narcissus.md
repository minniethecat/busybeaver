# Pool of Narcissus

## Overview

Pool of Narcissus is a location in the world of *Murder in the Maze*. The second centre of the Whistlefield Maze where Neville Shandon's body is discovered

The physical characteristics of Pool of Narcissus are described through the narrative, contributing to the atmosphere and plot mechanics of the novel.

Pool of Narcissus plays a significant role in the events of the Maze murders, serving as the setting for key scenes and providing the spatial context within which the investigation unfolds.
