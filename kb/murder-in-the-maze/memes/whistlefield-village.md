# Whistlefield Village

## Overview

Whistlefield Village is a location in the world of *Murder in the Maze*. The village near the Shandon estate where the inquest is held and suspects are watched

## Description

The physical characteristics of Whistlefield Village are described through the narrative, contributing to the atmosphere and plot mechanics of the novel.

## Role in the Plot

Whistlefield Village plays a significant role in the events of the Maze murders, serving as the setting for key scenes and providing the spatial context within which the investigation unfolds.
