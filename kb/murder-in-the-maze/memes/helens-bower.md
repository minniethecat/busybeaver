# Helens Bower

## Overview

Helens Bower is a location in the world of *Murder in the Maze*. One of the two centres of the Whistlefield Maze where Roger Shandon's body is found

## Description

The physical characteristics of Helens Bower are described through the narrative, contributing to the atmosphere and plot mechanics of the novel.

## Role in the Plot

Helens Bower plays a significant role in the events of the Maze murders, serving as the setting for key scenes and providing the spatial context within which the investigation unfolds.
