# Mr. Manisty

**Mr. Manisty** is a gentleman of Vinings, a property in the countryside near [[morfe|Morfe]]. He is the father of Hubert Manisty and another tall son, both of whom appear as young subalterns in the community.

## Family and Social Standing

Mr. Manisty belongs to the local gentry of the district surrounding [[morfe|Morfe]]. He is described as riding along Ley Street with his "two tall sons."[009-032]

His son Hubert Manisty becomes engaged to [[dora-draper|Dora Draper]], the daughter of [[mrs-draper|Mrs. Draper]] and [[dr-draper|Dr. Draper]].[028-079] This engagement connects the Manisty family to the Draper social circle, which is closely tied to the [[caroline-olivier|Olivier family]] through [[mrs-draper|Mrs. Draper]]'s close friendship with [[mary-olivier|Mary Olivier]]'s mother.

## In the Community

The two Manisty sons are noted at [[church-and-chapel|church]] services, described as "looking taller and leaner than ever" among the congregation.[017-049] They are part of the social gatherings where [[mary-olivier|Mary Olivier]] observes the local families, alongside the [[mr-propart|Propart]]s and [[norman-waugh|Norman Waugh]].[028-204]
