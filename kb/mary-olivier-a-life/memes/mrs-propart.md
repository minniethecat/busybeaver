# Mrs. Propart

**Mrs. Propart** is the wife of [[mr-propart|Mr. Propart]] and a member of the [[morfe|Morfe]] community. She is associated with the Chapel, where she plays the organ.

## At the Chapel

Mrs. Propart serves as the organist at the Chapel, playing after Evening Service. [[mary-olivier|Mary Olivier]] recalls the "nice feeling that she had when Mrs. Propart played the March in Scipio"[011-021] — one of the sensory experiences tied to [[mary-olivier|Mary]]'s childhood attendance at [[church-and-chapel|church services]].

## In the Community

Within the tight-knit [[morfe|Morfe]] social circle, Mrs. Propart participates in the local discourse. She is noted whispering alongside [[mrs-farmer|Mrs. Farmer]] that "[[mary-olivier|Mary]] is an Infidel,"[016-005] reflecting the community's awareness of [[mary-olivier|Mary]]'s unconventional religious views.

Mrs. Propart also provides a point of reference for local [[school|education]]: her nieces attended what had once been "a wonderful school,"[022-006] a detail offered when [[mary-olivier|Mary]]'s schooling is being discussed.
