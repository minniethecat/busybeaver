# The Home Counties

## Overview

The region where Vernon's Bank's reputation extends — 'known through all the Home Counties' (001-002), representing the bank's reach beyond Redborough itself.

## Role in the Novel

The Home Counties represents a location referenced rather than directly depicted — part of the wider world that impinges on Redborough and shapes the possibilities available to its inhabitants.

## Significance

Mentioned or implied locations contribute to *Hester*'s sense of a larger world beyond the provincial setting — the financial markets, the fashion capitals, the historical institutions that frame the novel's local concerns.
