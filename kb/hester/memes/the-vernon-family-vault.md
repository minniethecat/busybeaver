# The Vernon Family Vault

## Overview

The physical and symbolic repository of the Vernon family's accumulated wealth and history — the material foundation on which the family's status rests.

## Role in the Novel

The Vernon Family Vault represents a location referenced rather than directly depicted — part of the wider world that impinges on Redborough and shapes the possibilities available to its inhabitants.

## Significance

Mentioned or implied locations contribute to *Hester*'s sense of a larger world beyond the provincial setting — the financial markets, the fashion capitals, the historical institutions that frame the novel's local concerns.
