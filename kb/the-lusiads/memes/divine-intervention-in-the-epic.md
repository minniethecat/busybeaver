## About This Index

The `divine-intervention` tag identifies episodes and entities where supernatural agency directly alters the course of events. Camões uses this classical epic convention to frame Portuguese history within a cosmic struggle between favorable and hostile powers — Venus representing divine providence, Bacchus representing the forces that oppose Portugal's ordained destiny.
