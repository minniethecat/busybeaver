## To My Master Camões

[P03-001](*Tu se’ lo mio maestro, e ’l mio autore*).

[P03-002]Great Pilgrim-poet of the Sea and Land;

[P03-003] Thou life-long sport of [[fortune|Fortune]]’s ficklest will;

[P03-004] Doomed to all human and inhuman ill,

[P03-005]Despite thy lover-heart, thy hero-hand:

[P03-006]Enrollèd by thy pen what marv’ellous band

[P03-007] Of god-like Forms thy golden pages fill;

[P03-008] Love, Honour, Justice, Valour, Glory thrill

[P03-009]The Soul, obedient to thy strong command:

[P03-010]Amid the Prophets highest sits the Bard,

[P03-011] At once Revealer of the Heav’en and Earth,

[P03-012]To Heav’en the guide, of Earth the noblest guard;

[P03-013]And, ’mid the Poets thine the peerless worth,

[P03-014] Whose glorious song, thy Genius’ sole reward,

[P03-015]Bids all the Ages, Camões! bless thy birth.

[P03-016]R. F. B.
