# Golden Age Myth

## Definition
The classical myth of a lost Golden Age, adapted by Camões to frame Portuguese maritime expansion as both fulfillment and loss of pastoral innocence.

## In The Lusiads
This concept appears across multiple cantos, where Camões develops it through the poem's action, divine machinery, and the narrator's reflective passages.

## Historical/Cultural Context
Rooted in classical and Renaissance intellectual traditions, this concept reflects the syncretic worldview of sixteenth-century Portuguese humanism.

The concept is illustrated in several passages drawn from across the poem's narrative arc.
