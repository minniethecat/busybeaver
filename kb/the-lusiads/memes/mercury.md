# Mercury

## Overview
messenger of the gods — appears to the Portuguese fleet in Canto II

## Role in The Lusiads
A significant divine figure in Camões' epic, this deity participates in the mythological framework that structures the poem's action and elevates Portuguese maritime achievement to cosmic significance.

## Key Passages
Key passages in the poem feature this deity in action, illustrating Camões' syncretic use of classical mythology within a Christian providential framework.

## Classical Background
Rooted in Greco-Roman mythology as transmitted through Ovid, Virgil, and Homer to Renaissance readers.

## Notes
Camões adapts the classical characterization to serve his poem's Portuguese nationalist themes.
