# Portuguese Empire

The Portuguese Empire — or more precisely, the idea of empire — is the grand theme of *Os Lusíadas*. Camões writes to celebrate a nation that, in his telling, was divinely ordained to rule the seas and spread both Christianity and Portuguese dominion across the globe. ## About This Index

The `empire` and `medieval-portugal` tags identify the persons, kingdoms, and concepts that form the historical and ideological foundations of Portuguese imperial power. Camões traces the empire's lineage from the legendary founding of Lusitania through the medieval Portuguese monarchy to the global maritime empire of his own day, presenting imperial expansion as the fulfillment of Portugal's providential destiny.
