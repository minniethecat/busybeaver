# Council of the Gods

## Definition
The divine council on Olympus in Canto I of The Lusiads — modeled on Homer and Virgil — where [[venus|Venus]] and [[bacchus|Bacchus]] debate the fate of the Portuguese expedition.

## In The Lusiads
This concept plays a significant role in the poem's narrative and thematic architecture, appearing in key passages across multiple cantos.

Camões draws on both classical epic convention and Renaissance literary theory in his treatment of this concept, adapting inherited forms to Portuguese national subject matter.
